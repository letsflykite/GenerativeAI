Saka has spent his entire first-team club career with Arsenal, where he has won an [[FA Cup]] and an [[FA Community Shield]], progressively becoming one of Arsenal's most prolific and influential players.<ref>{{Cite web |date=26 December 2021 |title=Opinion: Bukayo Saka and Emile Smith Rowe have taken their game to the next level as Arsenal continue rise |url=https://www.eurosport.com/football/premier-league/2021-2022/opinion-bukayo-saka-and-emile-smith-rowe-have-taken-their-game-to-the-next-level-as-arsenal-continue_sto8684604/story.shtml |access-date=3 January 2022 |website=Eurosport |archive-date=29 June 2023 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230629094728/https://www.eurosport.com/geoblocking.shtml |url-status=live }}</ref> Following the [[2020‚Äì21 Arsenal F.C. season|2020‚Äì21]] and [[2021‚Äì22 Arsenal F.C. season|2021‚Äì22]] seasons, he was named as [[Arsenal Player of the Season|Arsenal's player of the season]].

Saka represented England at various youth levels before making his debut for the senior team in October 2020. He was part of England's squad at the [[UEFA Euro 2020]], and represented the nation at the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].
