'''Victor Miguel de Freitas Gomes''' (born 15 December 1982) is a South African [[Association football|football]] [[Referee (association football)|referee]].

A PSL referee since 2008, Gomes was voted [[PSL Referee of the Season]] in 2012–13 and 2017–18 and has been an international referee since 2011.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.iol.co.za/sport/soccer/psl/khune-scoops-four-psl-awards-1522037#.UijQaiDwDDc|title=Khune scoops four PSL awards|publisher=Independent Online|date=27 May 2013|access-date=28 May 2018}}</ref>

In 2018, he was hailed by the [[South African Football Association]] after he rejected and reported an attempted bribe of over R300{{nbsp}}000. Gomes had been approached and offered the sum of money to fix a [[CAF Confederation Cup]] match between Nigerian side [[Plateau United F.C.|Plateau United]] and Algerian side [[USM Alger]].<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.goal.com/en-za/news/safa-lavish-praise-on-referee-victor-gomes-for-turning-down/1kb1visv4icz91mzxp4vbkhkos|title=Safa lavish praise on referee Victor Gomes for turning down lucrative bribe|publisher=Goal|first=Phumzile|last=Ngcatshe|date=12 April 2018|access-date=28 May 2018}}</ref> He was again nominated for the PSL Referee of the Season award upon the conclusion of the domestic campaign.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.sport24.co.za/Soccer/PSL/victor-gomes-nominated-for-psl-referee-of-the-season-20180524|title=Victor Gomes nominated for PSL Referee of the Season|publisher=News 24|last=Siwela|first=Zakhele|date=24 May 2018|access-date=28 May 2018}}</ref>

He officiated the [[2019 Africa Cup of Nations|2019]] and [[2021 Africa Cup of Nations|2021]] edition of [[Africa Cup of Nations]]. He refereed the [[2021 Africa Cup of Nations Final]] between [[Senegal national football team|Senegal]] and [[Egypt national football team|Egypt]].
