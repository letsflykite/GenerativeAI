"'''Dreamers'''" is a 2022 song by South Korean singer [[Jungkook]], a member of the [[boy band]] [[BTS]]. Part of the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] [[FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022 Official Soundtrack|official soundtrack]], the song was released on November 20 to coincide with the first match of that year's FIFA World Cup and its opening ceremony.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.fifa.com/fifaplus/en/articles/bts-jung-kooks-dreamers-unveiled-to-celebrate-the-start-of-fifa-world-cup-2022|title=BTS Jung Kook's hit single Dreamers unveiled to celebrate the start of FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022™|publisher=[[FIFA]]|access-date=November 20, 2022}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last=Carras |first=Christi |date=November 21, 2022 |title=Jungkook of BTS debuts World Cup song 'Dreamers' at opening ceremony in Qatar |url=https://www.latimes.com/entertainment-arts/story/2022-11-20/jungkook-bts-world-cup-song-dreamers-qatar |access-date=November 21, 2022 |website=[[Los Angeles Times]]}}</ref>

== Live performances and release ==
On November 19, Jungkook's agency [[Big Hit Music]] announced via [[Weverse]] his participation in the opening ceremony.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web |date=November 20, 2022 |title=Jungkook drops FIFA World Cup 2022 official track Dreamers, BTS ARMY says: 'It's so good I'm crying' |url=https://www.hindustantimes.com/entertainment/music/jungkook-drops-fifa-2022-world-cup-official-song-dreamers-bts-army-reacts-101668930107351.html |access-date=November 21, 2022 |website=[[Hindustan Times]]}}</ref> The next day, [[FIFA]]'s official [[Twitter]] account shared a video of the singer teasing his upcoming performance.<ref>{{Cite magazine |last=Benjamin |first=Jeff |date=November 20, 2022 |title=BTS' Jung Kook Drops World Cup Song 'Dreamers' Featuring Qatari Singer Fahad Al Kubaisi: Listen |url=https://www.billboard.com/music/pop/bts-jung-kook-dreamers-fahad-al-kubaisi-world-cup-song-listen-stream-1235174263/ |access-date=November 21, 2022 |magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] }}</ref> The performance featured Jungkook in an all black suit surrounded by background dancers, being joined by [[Fahad Al Kubaisi]] wearing Qatari clothing midway through the performance.<ref>{{Cite magazine |last=Peters |first=Mitchell |date=November 20, 2022 |title=BTS' Jung Kook Mesmerizes With Live Debut of 'Dreamers' at 2022 World Cup Opening Ceremony |url=https://www.billboard.com/music/music-news/bts-jungkook-2022-fifa-world-cup-opening-ceremony-dreamers-performance-video-1235174395/ |access-date=November 21, 2022 |magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]}}</ref><ref>{{Cite magazine |date=November 20, 2022 |title=See BTS' Jung Kook Debut New Single 'Dreamers' at FIFA World Cup Opening Ceremony |url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-news/see-jung-kook-dreamers-fifa-world-cup-opening-ceremony-1234633953/ |access-date=November 21, 2022 |magazine=[[Rolling Stone]]}}</ref>

== Music video ==
A music video for the Fahad Al Kubaisi version of the song was announced, with a scheduled release date of November 22 with the video being released on FIFA's [[YouTube]] channel.<ref name=":0" />
