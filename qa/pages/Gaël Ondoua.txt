'''Gaël Nesterovich Bella Ondoua''' (born 4 November 1995), more known as '''Gaël Ondoua,''' is a [[Cameroon]]ian professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[midfielder]] for the [[Cameroon national football team|Cameroon national team]].<ref name="soccerway">{{Soccerway|376527}}</ref><ref name=":0">{{Cite news|title=«Прыгаю по дому как ребёнок!» Единственный россиянин, который сыграет на чемпионате мира|author=Полина Куимова|url=https://www.championat.com/football/article-4886069-chempionat-mira-2022-edinstvennyj-rossiyanin-kotoryj-sygraet-na-chempionate-mira-gael-ondua-v-sostave-sbornoj-kameruna.html}}</ref>

==Career==
===Club===
In April 2014, Ondoua moved from [[FC Lokomotiv Moscow|Lokomotiv Moscow]] to [[PFC CSKA Moscow|CSKA Moscow]].<ref>{{cite web|title=18-летний полузащитник Ондуа Гаэль перешел из "Локомотива" в ЦСКА|url=https://www.sports.ru/football/159558741.html|website=sports.ru|publisher=Sports RU|accessdate=18 May 2018|language=Russian|date=20 April 2014}}</ref> He made his professional debut on 24 September 2014 for CSKA Moscow in a [[Russian Cup (football)|Russian Cup]] game against [[FC Khimik Dzerzhinsk|Khimik Dzerzhinsk]].<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[Russian Premier League]]|url=http://www.rfpl.org/match/match_10611.html|title=Khimik-CSKA Match Report|date=24 September 2014}}</ref> In January 2015, Ondoua went on trial with [[FC Sakhalin Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk|Sakhalin Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk]] of the [[Russian Football National League]].<ref name="Two on trial">{{cite web|title=На просмотре у "Сахалина" бывший и действующий игроки ПФК ЦСКА|url=http://fc-sakhalin.ru/news.php?id=1743|publisher=[[FC Sakhalin Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk]]|accessdate=28 January 2015|language=Russian|date=28 January 2015}}</ref>

At the end of December 2017, it was reported that Ondoua had signed with [[FC Zorya Luhansk|Zorya Luhansk]].<ref>{{cite web|title=ОФИЦИАЛЬНО: Гаэль Ондуа — игрок Зари|url=https://sport.ua/news/366351-ofitsialno-gael-ondua-igrok-zari|website=sport.ua|publisher=Sport UA|accessdate=18 May 2018|language=Russian|date=22 December 2017}}</ref> On 12 March, however, he posted on his [[Instagram]] account that due circumstances beyond his contract he would not be a Zorya Luhansk player.<ref>{{cite web|title=Ондуа не станет игроком Зари|url=https://sport.ua/news/375142-ondua-ne-stanet-igrokom-zari|website=sport.ua|publisher=Sport UA|accessdate=18 May 2018|language=Russian|date=13 March 2018}}</ref>

On 27 July 2018, Ondoua signed with the [[Russian Premier League]] club [[FC Anzhi Makhachkala|Anzhi Makhachkala]].<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[FC Anzhi Makhachkala]]|url=http://fc-anji.ru/news/ru/club_news/v_anji_pereshel_gajel_ondua270718/|title=В "Анжи" перешел Гаэль Ондуа|date=27 July 2018|language=Russian}}</ref>

On 2 July 2019, he signed with Swiss club [[Servette FC|Servette]] on a two-year contract plus an option year.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[Servette FC]]|url=https://www.servettefc.ch/index.php/saison/actualites/1166-gael-ondoua-signe-au-servette-fc|title=GAËL ONDOUA SIGNE AU SERVETTE FC|trans-title=Gaël Ondoua signs with Servette FC|date=2 July 2019|language=French}}</ref>

On 26 August 2021, Ondoua joined German club [[Hannover 96]], signing a two-year contract.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.kicker.de/hannover-96-verstaerkt-sich-mit-ondoua-869565/artikel |title=Hannover 96 verstärkt sich mit Ondoua |website=kicker.de |publisher=[[Kicker (sports magazine)|kicker]] |date=26 August 2021 |access-date=26 August 2021 |language=de}}</ref>

==International career==
Ondoua debuted with the [[Cameroon national football team|Cameroon national team]] in a 1–0 [[2022 World Cup qualification]] loss to [[Algeria national football team|Algeria]] on 25 March 2022.<ref name="How things stand in FIFA World Cup qualifying 2021">{{cite web | title=FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022™ | website=How things stand in FIFA World Cup qualifying | date=8 June 2021 | url=origin1904-p.cxm.fifa.com/tournaments/mens/worldcup/qatar2022/qualifiers/caf/match-center/400228711 | access-date=26 March 2022}}</ref> He was part of Cameroon's squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] in [[Qatar]] where he made headlines after wearing the [[Flag of Russia|Russian flag]] on his boots amidst the [[2022 Russian invasion of Ukraine|Russian invasion of Ukraine]].<ref>{{Cite web |date=2022-11-24 |title=Ондуа: «Я всегда играю в бутсах с российским флагом. Почему я должен отказаться от своего дома?» |url=https://www.sport-express.ru/football/world/2022/news/futbolist-sbornoy-kameruna-ondua-obyasnil-pochemu-sygral-v-butsah-s-rossiyskim-flagom-na-chm-2022-2002686/ |access-date=2022-11-27 |website=sport-express.ru |language=ru}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |date=2022-11-25 |title=Cameroonian Footballer Gael Ondoua Wears Boots With Russian Flag at World Cup 2022 |url=https://www.telecomasia.net/blog/cameroonian-footballer-gael-ondoua-wears-boots-with-russian-flag-at-world-cup-2022/ |access-date=2022-11-27 |website=www.telecomasia.net |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news |date=2022-11-26 |title=World Cup: Gael Ondua played in boots with the flag of Russia for Cameroon, reaction in Europe, scandal that threatens |url=https://news.russia.postsen.com/sports/81973.html |access-date=2022-11-27 |website=Russia Posts English |language=en-us}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Ondoua grew up in Russia, where his father was a diplomat. He is fluent in [[Russian language|Russian]] and holds [[Russian citizenship law|Russian citizenship]].<ref name=":0" /><ref>{{Cite web |date=2022-02-23 |title=Exclusive {{!}} Gaël Ondoua: "I'm hungry for success whatever the field & I work hard, no matter how difficult it is.
