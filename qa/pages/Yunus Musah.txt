'''Yunus Dimoara Musah''' (born November 29, 2002) is an American professional [[Association football|soccer]] player who plays as a [[Midfielder#Central midfielder|midfielder]] for [[La Liga]] club [[Valencia CF|Valencia]] and the [[United States men's national soccer team|United States national team]].

Born in the United States to Ghanaian parents, he grew up mostly in Italy and England. He was a youth international for England before committing to the United States in 2021.

==Club career==
===Early career===
Musah was born in [[New York City]] while his Ghanaian mother was on vacation in the United States.<ref>{{Cite magazine|last=Straus|first=Brian|title=USMNT Makes Its Case to Another Rising Star With Options|url=https://www.si.com/soccer/2020/11/04/yunus-musah-usmnt-camp-valencia-england-ghana-italy-berhalter|access-date=January 3, 2021|magazine=Sports Illustrated|language=en-us}}</ref> His father is also Ghanaian.<ref name=":0" /> He moved to Italy after his birth, living in [[Castelfranco Veneto]] and later starting his career at [[A.S.D. Giorgione Calcio 2000|Giorgione Calcio 2000]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://storiedieccellenza.it/yunus-una-bella-favola-che-diviene-realta-dal-giorgione-al-valencia-passando-per-arsenal-e-nazionale-inglese/|title=Yunus, una Bella Favola che diviene Realtà: Dal Giorgione al Valencia, passando per Arsenal e Nazionale inglese!|trans-title=Yunus, a beautiful tale that becomes reality: from Giorgione to Valencia, passing through Arsenal and the English national team!|publisher=Stori di Eccellenza|language=it|date=August 24, 2020|access-date=September 13, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Player Analysis: Yunus Musah|url=https://breakingthelines.com/player-analysis/player-analysis-yunus-musah/|access-date=January 3, 2021|website=Breaking The Lines|language=en-GB}}</ref> In 2012, at age nine, he moved to [[London]] and joined [[Arsenal F.C.|Arsenal]]'s Academy.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.lasprovincias.es/valenciacf/talento-ingles-enamoro-20200830184754-nt.html|title=El talento inglés que se enamoró del fútbol del sur|trans-title=The English talent who fell in love for the southern football|publisher=Las Provincias|language=es|date=August 31, 2020|access-date=September 13, 2020}}</ref><ref name=":0" /> [[Arsenal F.C. Under-23s and Academy|Arsenal U16]] coach [[Trevor Bumstead]] stated Musah made an immediate impression; "As a player, he was ‘wow’. He’s got fantastic physical attributes and the drive and determination to go with that. He would play anywhere to get in the team but his favourite was as a central attacking midfield player."<ref>{{cite web|last=Hartley|first=George|title=The inside story of Yunus Musah: Through the chaos|url=https://firsttimefinish.co.uk/2021/04/18/the-inside-story-of-yunus-musah-through-the-chaos|date=August 31, 2020|access-date=November 22, 2022|website=First Time Finish}}</ref>

===Valencia===
In the summer of 2019, Musah joined [[Valencia CF|Valencia]], at the age of 16,<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/60418715|title=Musah the blueprint for Valencia's future|work=BBC Sport }}</ref> and was assigned to [[Valencia CF Mestalla|the reserves]] in [[Segunda División B]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://golsmedia.com/comunidad-valenciana/futbol/valencia-cf-mestalla/2019/08/09/valencia-cf-mestalla-ficha-dos-talentos-internacionales/|title=El Valencia CF Mestalla ficha a dos talentos internacionales|trans-title=Valencia CF Mestalla sign two international talents|publisher=Golsmedia|language=es|date=August 9, 2019|access-date=September 13, 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ghanaweb.com/GhanaHomePage/SportsArchive/Valencia-sign-Ghanaian-teenager-Yunus-Musah-from-Arsenal-778866|title=Valencia sign Ghanaian teenager Yunus Musah from Arsenal|publisher=Ghana Web|date=September 6, 2019|access-date=September 13, 2020}}</ref> He made his debut with the B-team at the age of 16 on September 15, 2019, starting in a 0–0 home draw against [[CF La Nucía]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://eldesmarque.com/valencia/valencia-cf/209189-valencia-cf-mestalla-la-nucia|title=Al Mestalla se le escapa el triunfo con uno más (0–0)|trans-title=Mestalla let the win slip away with one more man (0–0)|publisher=El Desmarque|language=es|date=September 14, 2019|access-date=September 13, 2020}}</ref> He scored his first senior goal on March 1, 2020, netting his team's only goal in a 2–1 loss at [[Gimnàstic de Tarragona]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://golsmedia.com/comunidad-valenciana/futbol/valencia-cf-mestalla/2020/03/01/derrota-amarga-valencia-cf-mestalla-casa-nastic-tarragona/|title=Derrota amarga del Valencia CF Mestalla en casa del Nàstic de Tarragona|trans-title=Bittersweet defeat of Valencia CF Mestalla at the home of ''Nàstic'' de Tarragona|publisher=Golsmedia|language=es|date=March 1, 2020|access-date=September 13, 2020}}</ref>

After the arrival of new manager [[Javi Gracia]] in Valencia's first team, Musah spent the 2020 pre-season with the main squad.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://golsmedia.com/comunidad-valenciana/futbol/valencia-cf/2020/08/23/yunus-musah-enamora-valencianismo-europa/|title=Yunus Musah enamora al valencianismo y a Europa|trans-title=Yunus Musah charms the ''valencianismo'' and Europe|publisher=Golsmedia|language=es|date=August 23, 2020|access-date=September 13, 2020}}</ref> At age 17 years and eight months, he made his first team—and [[La Liga]]—debut on September 13 of that year, starting in a 4–2 home win against [[Levante UD]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.marca.com/futbol/primera-division/valencia-vs-levante/cronica/2020/09/13/5f5e6a3b46163f20208b45c8.html|title=El Valencia supera el pánico|trans-title=Valencia overcome the panic|publisher=[[Marca (newspaper)|Marca]]|language=es|date=September 13, 2020|access-date=September 13, 2020}}</ref> In doing so, he became the first Englishman and American to debut for the club.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://as.com/futbol/2020/09/13/primera/1600024191_615544.html|title=Yunus Musah, la última perla de la cantera che|trans-title=Yunus Musah, the last pearl of the ''che'' youth setup|publisher=[[Diario AS]]|language=es|date=September 13, 2020|access-date=September 13, 2020}}</ref>

On November 1, 2020, aged 17 years and 338 days, Musah scored a goal in a 2–2 draw against [[Getafe CF]], becoming the youngest non-Spanish player to score for Valencia, breaking the previous record held by [[Lee Kang-in]], aged 18 years and 219 days.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.superdeporte.es/valencia/2020/11/01/musah-gol-record/477165.html |title=Musah, gol y récord |trans-title=Musah, goal and record |website=[[Super Deporte]] |language=es |date=November 1, 2020 }}</ref> The following month, he extended his contract with Valencia until 2026.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.si.com/soccer/2020/12/04/yunus-musah-valencia-extension-2026-usmnt |title=U.S.-Eligible Yunus Musah Signs Valencia Extension Through 2026 |website=si.com |date=December 4, 2020 }}</ref> On December 16, in the [[2020–21 Copa del Rey|first round]] of the [[Copa del Rey]], he came on with four minutes remaining away to [[Tercera División]] club [[Terrassa FC]] and scored an equalizing goal as his team won 4–2 after [[Overtime (sports)|extra time]].<ref>{{cite news |title=Yunus Musah pokes home late equalizer as Valencia survive Terrassa scare in Copa del Rey |url=https://ghanasoccernet.com/yunus-musah-pokes-home-late-equalizer-as-valencia-survive-terrassa-scare-in-copa-del-rey |access-date=April 25, 2022 |publisher=Ghana Soccer Net |date=December 16, 2020}}</ref>

In the [[2021–22 Copa del Rey]], Musah scored in wins at [[CD Utrillas]] and [[CD Arenteiro]] in the early rounds.<ref>{{cite magazine |last1=Creditor |first1=Avi |title=USMNT's Yunus Musah Has Goal, Assist in Valencia's Copa Del Rey Win |url=https://www.si.com/soccer/2021/12/02/yunus-musah-valencia-goal-assist-copa-del-rey-usmnt |access-date=April 25, 2022 |magazine=[[Sports Illustrated]] |date=December 2, 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Valencia's Musah scores 2nd goal of season, in Copa del Rey |url=https://apnews.com/article/soccer-sports-la-liga-yunus-musah-manu-vallejo-f24742b727ce6732a8fe0526521843a6 |access-date=April 25, 2022 |publisher=[[Associated Press]] |date=December 16, 2021}}</ref> In the [[2022 Copa del Rey Final|final]] on April 23, he replaced [[Dimitri Foulquier]] after 100 minutes of a 1–1 draw against [[Real Betis]] and was the only player to miss in the [[Penalty shoot-out (association football)|penalty shootout]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/61202911|title=Real Betis 1–1 Valencia|publisher=BBC Sport|date=April 23, 2022|access-date=April 23, 2022}}</ref>

==International career==
As a youth, Musah was eligible to play for the [[United States men's national soccer team|United States]], [[Ghana national football team|Ghana]], [[Italy national football team|Italy]], and [[England national football team|England]].<ref>{{cite news |last1=Cannon |first1=George |title=Yunus Musah: Things to Know About the Midfielder |url=https://www.90min.com/posts/yunus-musah-things-to-know |access-date=December 28, 2020 |work=90min |date=November 2, 2020}}</ref><ref name=":1">{{Cite web|last=Feehely|first=Alan|date=December 1, 2020|title=Kieran Trippier advises Yunus Musah on England future|url=https://www.football-espana.net/2020/12/01/kieran-trippier-advises-yunus-musah-on-england-future|access-date=January 3, 2021|website=Football Espana|language=en}}</ref> 

===England youth teams===
Musah made his international debut with England's [[England national under-15 football team|under-15s]] in 2016 and subsequently represented England up to the [[England national under-18 football team|under-18]] level. He was also called up to the [[England under-19 football team|under-19]] squad in October 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|last=The Football Association|date=October 2, 2020|title=YOUNG LIONS SQUAD NAMED FOR DOUBLE-HEADER WITH SCOTLAND AT ST. GEORGE'S PARK|url=https://www.thefa.com/news/2020/oct/02/england-mu19s-squad-named-for-scotland-games-021020|website=The FA}}</ref> A Musah penalty earned England's under-18s a draw against Brazil's [[Brazil national under-17 football team|under-17s]] on September 8, 2019,<ref>{{Cite web|last=Association|first=The Football|title=England MU18s 1-1 Brazil|url=http://www.thefa.com/news/2019/sep/08/england-mu18s-1-1-brazil-080919|access-date=January 4, 2021|website=www.thefa.com|language=en}}</ref> and he went on to score a crucial goal against Austria's [[Austria national under-18 football team|under-18s]] on October 16, 2019, with England winning 3–2.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Association|first=The Football|title=Young Lions end Euro trip with three wins from three after victory in Austria|url=http://www.thefa.com/news/2019/oct/16/england-u18s-austria-report-161019|access-date=January 4, 2021|website=www.thefa.com|language=en}}</ref> In total, Musah played more than 30 times for England at youth level.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Sousa|first=Justin|date=November 9, 2020|title=Yunus Musah: Journey to the USMNT|url=https://www.scoutedftbl.com/post/yunus-musah-journey-usmnt|access-date=January 4, 2021|website=Scouted Football|language=en}}</ref>

===U.S. men’s team===
Musah accepted a call-up to the United States senior squad on November 2, 2020, to play in friendlies against [[Wales national football team|Wales]] and [[Panama national football team|Panama]] later that month.<ref>{{cite web|last=Tenorio|first=Paul|title=Valencia's Yunus Musah to get USMNT call-up in roster for friendly vs. Wales|url=https://theathletic.com/2176719/2020/11/02/yunus-musah-usmnt-valencia/|access-date=November 3, 2020|website=The Athletic}}</ref> He was part of a largely Europe-based squad which included 10 first-time selections and had an average age of 22.<ref>{{Cite web|title=England Under-18 Star Yunus Musah Called Up to USA Squad|url=https://www.90min.com/posts/england-under-18-star-yunus-musah-called-up-to-senior-usa-squad|access-date=January 4, 2021|website=90min.com|date=November 3, 2020 |language=en-GB}}</ref> Musah had been contacted by the [[United States Soccer Federation]] through [[Nico Estévez]], a United States assistant manager who had connections with Valencia CF having previously managed their youth and reserve teams for eight years.<ref>{{cite magazine|last=Straus|first=Brian|title=USMNT Makes Its Case to Another Rising Teen Star With Options|url=https://www.si.com/soccer/2020/11/04/yunus-musah-usmnt-camp-valencia-england-ghana-italy-berhalter|access-date=November 4, 2020|magazine=Sports Illustrated}}</ref> Musah made his senior international debut on November 11, starting in a 0–0 draw against Wales at the [[Liberty Stadium]] in [[Swansea]].<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.marca.com/en/football/international-football/2020/11/13/5fae9e49268e3e84668b4593.html|title=England and the United States fighting to secure Yunus Musah's services|newspaper=Marca|date=November 12, 2020|access-date=November 12, 2020}}</ref> He then started the following 6–2 win over Panama on November 16.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ussoccer.com/stories/2020/11/usmnt-6-panama-2-match-report-stats|title=Goal-Scoring Outbursts Fuel Young USMNT Past Panama 6–2 in Austria|website=ussoccer.com|date=November 16, 2020}}</ref>

Despite Musah having played for the United States at senior level, England senior manager [[Gareth Southgate]] attempted to persuade Musah to play for England, stating: "We're monitoring him. He's been with us in the last couple of months and we'd very much like his future to be with us."<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|title=The 'adaptability and hunger' driving Yunus Musah's journey to stardom with USMNT and Valencia|url=https://www.cbssports.com/soccer/news/the-adaptability-and-hunger-driving-yunus-musahs-journey-to-stardom-with-usmnt-and-valencia/|access-date=January 3, 2021|website=CBSSports.com|language=en}}</ref> [[England national under-21 football team|England under-21]] manager [[Aidy Boothroyd]] also stated: "I am hopeful we will see him in the U21s at some point. I don’t know [if he has made his decision]. I hope he hasn’t because I think if he came here and saw what we are all about that he would really enjoy it."<ref>{{Cite web|title='I hope Musah hasn't chosen the USMNT' - Boothroyd admits England still hope to win race for Valencia wonderkid {{!}} Goal.com|url=https://www.goal.com/en-gb/news/hope-musah-hasnt-chosen-usmnt-england-still-hope-win-race/qb2ahsuhwsng1jlm7l55vhs3t|access-date=January 3, 2021|website=www.goal.com}}</ref> Meanwhile, United States management continued to connect with Musah after the November USMNT camp, with assistant manager Nico Estévez in day-to-day contact and also manager [[Gregg Berhalter]] holding conversations with Musah and his family.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ussoccer.com/stories/2021/03/five-things-to-know-usmnt-midfielder-yunus-musah|title=GFIVE THINGS TO KNOW: USMNT MIDFIELDER YUNUS MUSAH|website=ussoccer.com|date=March 15, 2021}}</ref>

In late 2020, as he approached his 18th birthday, Musah was reportedly undecided on the question of which nation to represent permanently.<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1" /> On March 15, 2021, however, he officially committed to represent the United States internationally.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ussoccer.com/stories/2021/03/yunus-musah-commits-to-us-mens-national-team|title=Yunus Musah commits to U.S. Men's National Team|website=US Soccer|date=March 15, 2021|accessdate=March 15, 2021}}</ref> Musah played for the U.S. at the [[2022 FIFA World Cup|2022 World Cup]] in Qatar, being eliminated in the round of 16 against the Netherlands.
