'''Andrés Matías Matonte Cabrera''' (born 30 March 1988) is a [[Uruguay]]an professional [[Association football|football]] [[Referee (association football)|referee]]. On 19 May 2022 FIFA announced the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]]'s referee list, where Matonte was chosen.

==Career==
Matonte is a physical education teacher. He started his career as a referee in 2008. He made his debut in the [[Uruguayan Primera División]] in the match between [[Club Atlético Fénix|Fénix]] and [[River Plate F.C.|River Plate]] (0-0) in 2017.<ref>{{cite news |title=Andrés Matonte: "El mejor árbitro es el que se equivoca menos, no existe un arbitraje perfecto" |url=https://www.espn.com.ar/futbol/mundial/nota/_/id/10394222/uruguay-la-celeste-qatar-2022-andres-matonte-mundial-arbitraje |access-date=15 November 2022 |agency=ESPN |date=15 May 2022}}</ref>

He is FIFA referee since 2019 and was part of the referee list of [[2021 FIFA Arab Cup]], where he as he was summoned to officiate the match between [[United Arab Emirates national football team|United Arab Emirates]] and [[Qatar national football team|Qatar]], and [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification (CONMEBOL)]].<ref>{{cite web|title=List of appointed Match Officials|url=https://digitalhub.fifa.com/m/c692b0b51863b43/original/FIFA-REFEREES-LIST-Fifa-Arab-Cup-2021-QAT_081021.pdf|website=FIFA.com|publisher=Fédération Internationale de Football Association}}</ref>
