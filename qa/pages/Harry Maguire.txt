'''Jacob Harry Maguire''' (born 5 March 1993) is an English professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[centre-back]] for {{English football updater|ManchesU}} club [[Manchester United F.C.|Manchester United]] and the [[England national football team|England national team]].

Maguire came through the youth system at [[Sheffield United F.C.|Sheffield United]] before graduating to the [[First team (association football)|first team]] in 2011. He made 166  appearances for United and was the club's [[Sheffield United F.C. Player of the Year|Player of the Year]] three seasons in succession, also featuring in the [[PFA Team of the Year]] for [[EFL League One|League One]] as many times. In 2014, he transferred to [[Hull City A.F.C.|Hull City]] for ¬£2.5&nbsp;million, before being [[Loan (association football)|loaned]] to [[Wigan Athletic F.C.|Wigan Athletic]] in 2015. He joined [[Leicester City F.C.|Leicester City]] in 2017 for an initial fee of ¬£12&nbsp;million and in the [[2017‚Äì18 Leicester City F.C. season|2017‚Äì18 season]] he played in every minute and was named Player of the season. Maguire moved to Manchester United in 2019 for a fee believed to be ¬£80&nbsp;million, a [[List of most expensive association football transfers|world-record amount]] for a defender, and within six months was appointed [[club captain]].

Maguire played one match for the [[England national under-21 football team|England national under-21 team]] in 2012. In 2017, he made his senior debut and he was chosen for the England's squads for the [[2018 FIFA World Cup|2018]] and [[2022 FIFA World Cup]]s, and [[UEFA Euro 2020]], earning a place in the [[UEFA European Championship awards|Team of the Tournament]] in the latter.

Despite winning approximately twice as many duels as he lost in the 2020‚Äì21 season,<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.premierleague.com/players/9566/Harry-Maguire/stats |title= Harry Maguire: Premier League |website=Premier League}}</ref> Maguire admitted enduring a "tough" time in which Manchester United finished sixth with their lowest points tally since the formation of the Premier League. Maguire also came under intense criticism for his performances as many believed he had failed to show consistency.<ref>{{cite web |url= https://www.goal.com/en-in/news/compared-how-harry-maguire-has-fared-in-premier-league-in/blt653d2aebf525dfba |title= How Harry Maguire has fared in the Premier League in comparison to Van Dijk, Rudiger, and Ruben Dias. |website=goal.com |date=8 January 2022 }}</ref>

===Senior===
[[File:Harry Maguire 2018-07-11 1.jpg|thumb|upright|Maguire playing for [[England national football team|England]] at the [[2018 FIFA World Cup]]]]

On 24 August 2017, [[England national football team|England]] manager [[Gareth Southgate]] included Maguire in his squad for the [[2018 FIFA World Cup qualification]] matches against [[Malta national football team|Malta]] and [[Slovakia national football team|Slovakia]].<ref>{{cite news |title=England: Nathaniel Chalobah and Harry Maguire in squad for World Cup qualifiers |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/41039802 |access-date=24 August 2017 |website=BBC Sport |date=24 August 2017}}</ref> He made his debut when starting in England's 1‚Äì0 away win over [[Lithuania national football team|Lithuania]], which was the team's final match in their successful World Cup qualification campaign.<ref>{{cite news |first=Phil |last=McNulty |title=Lithuania 0‚Äì1 England |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/41457291 |website=BBC Sport |date=8 October 2017 |access-date=8 October 2017}}</ref> Maguire was named in the England squad for the [[2018 FIFA World Cup|2018 World Cup]].<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/44138600 |title=England World Cup squad: Trent Alexander-Arnold in 23-man squad |website=BBC Sport |date=16 May 2018 |access-date=17 May 2018}}</ref> He assisted [[Harry Kane]]'s winner against [[Tunisia national football team|Tunisia]] on 18 June 2018 in England's World Cup opener. England won 2‚Äì1.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://englandfootball.org/2018/06/18/review-tunisia-1-2-england-18-06-18/ |title=Review ‚Äì Tunisia 1‚Äì2 England ‚Äì 18‚Äì06‚Äì18 |date=18 June 2018 |website=englandfootball.org}}</ref> Maguire scored his first England goal on 7 July with a 30th-minute header from Ashley Young's cross in a 2‚Äì0 win over [[Sweden national football team|Sweden]] in the quarter-finals.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/44665445 |title=Sweden 0‚Äì2 England |first=Phil |last=McNulty |website=BBC Sport |date=7 July 2018 |access-date=7 July 2018}}</ref>

At [[UEFA Euro 2020]], Maguire played his first match against [[Czech Republic national football team|Czech Republic]] in the last fixture of the group stage.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://theathletic.com/news/harry-maguire-england-team-news-euro-2020/fkpwjxYJc7sL |title=England set to hand Harry Maguire start against Czech Republic tonight |website=The Athletic |date=22 June 2021|first=David|last=Ornstein}}</ref> Afterwards, he featured in the round of 16, when England won 2‚Äì0 against [[Germany national football team|Germany]], for which he was awarded the Star of the Match.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.uefa.com/uefaeuro-2020/match/2024484--england-vs-germany/ |title=England 2‚Äì0 Germany |publisher=UEFA |date=29 June 2021}}</ref> On 3 July 2021, Maguire scored the second goal for England in a 4‚Äì0 win over [[Ukraine national football team|Ukraine]] in the quarter-finals.<ref>{{Cite news|title=Ukraine 0‚Äì4 England: Ruthless England set up Denmark semi-final|first=Phil|last=McNulty|website=BBC Sport|url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/51198691|date=3 July 2021|access-date=14 July 2021}}</ref> Maguire started in the semi-finals against [[Denmark national football team|Denmark]] and in the [[UEFA Euro 2020 final|final]] against [[Italy national football team|Italy]]. England drew 1‚Äì1 after 120 minutes; in the resulting [[Penalty shoot-out (association football)|penalty shoot-out]] Maguire scored England's second penalty, which is often considered to be the best penalty of the year 2021.<ref>{{cite news|url= https://www.givemesport.com/1807934-the-greatest-penalty-in-2021-harry-maguire-for-england-vs-italy-in-euro-2020-final |title= The greatest penalty in 2021? On 15 November 2021, Maguire scored the opening goal in a 10‚Äì0 victory over [[San Marino national football team|San Marino]] on the final day of [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification|2022 World Cup qualification]], making him England's highest scoring defender with seven goals, surpassing [[John Terry]] and [[Jack Charlton]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Hytner|first=David|date=15 November 2021|title=England confirm World Cup 2022 spot with 10-goal demolition of San Marino|url=https://www.theguardian.com/football/2021/nov/15/san-marino-england-world-cup-2022-qualifying-match-report|url-status=live|access-date=16 November 2021|website=The Guardian}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Smith|first=George|date=16 November 2021|title=John Terry sends message to Harry Maguire after he breaks England record|url=https://www.manchestereveningnews.co.uk/sport/football/football-news/manchester-united-harry-maguire-england-22176930|url-status=live|access-date=16 November 2021|website=Manchester Evening News}}</ref> Maguire was selected for England's squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup|2022 World Cup]] in Qatar where he earned praise for a controlling performance in a 0-0 draw against the [[United States men's national soccer team|United States]] ‚Äì a game in which he made his 50th appearance for England ‚Äì as well as for consistent and successful performances in the latter stages of the tournament. Maguire was selected for the 2022 World Cup Team of the Tournament.<ref>{{Cite web|title= World Cup team of the tournament: Lionel Messi, Kylian Mbappe, Antoine Griezmann, Harry Maguire|url= https://www.skysports.com/football/news/12098/12768686/world-cup-team-of-the-tournament-lionel-messi-kylian-mbappe-antoine-griezmann-harry-maguire |url-status=live|access-date= 27 January 2023|website=[[Sky Sports]]}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title= Maguire & 2 more Man United players included in World Cup Team of the Tournament|url= https://tribuna.com/en/news/manutd-2022-12-20-maguire-2-more-man-united-players-included-in-world-cup-team-of-the-tournament/ |url-status=live|access-date= 27 January 2023|website=Tribuna|date= 20 December 2022 }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title= 'Harry Maguire is in my team of the tournament' ‚Äì Manchester United captain hailed for 'fantastic' England performances at World Cup |url= https://talksport.com/football/1267355/harry-maguire-team-of-the-tournament-england-world-cup/ |url-status=live|access-date= 27 January 2023|website=[[TalkSport]]|date= 5 December 2022 }}</ref>

===Altercation in Greece===
On 21 August 2020, Maguire was arrested on the Greek island of [[Mykonos]] after an incident involving the police. Manchester United said in a statement that Maguire was "fully co-operating with the Greek authorities."<ref>{{cite news |title=Manchester United captain Harry Maguire 'arrested' on Greek island |url=https://news.sky.com/story/manchester-united-captain-harry-maguire-arrested-on-greek-island-12053676 |access-date=21 August 2020 |website=Sky News |date=21 August 2020}}</ref> Maguire appeared in court on the island of [[Syros]] the following day, after spending two days in [[Detention (imprisonment)|detention]].<ref>{{Cite news|date=22 August 2020|title=Maguire released by Greek prosecutor, hearing next week|publisher=Reuters|url=https://uk.reuters.com/article/uk-soccer-england-mun-maguire-idUKKBN25I0F2|access-date=22 August 2020}}</ref> He was released from custody with his trial postponed until 25 August.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/uk-news/2020/aug/22/harry-maguire-walks-free-for-now-after-syros-court-appearance-greece-manchester-united|title=Harry Maguire walks free ‚Äì for now ‚Äì after Syros court appearance|last1=Jackson|first1=Jamie|last2=Smith|first2=Helena|date=22 August 2020|newspaper=The Guardian|location=Greece|access-date=22 August 2020}}</ref> On 25 August, Maguire was convicted of all charges, and found guilty of [[Assault|aggravated assault]], [[resisting arrest]] and [[Bribery|attempted bribery]] by a court on the island of Syros. Maguire was absent at the court ruling.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Harry Maguire: Man Utd captain found guilty of aggravated assault, resisting arrest and attempted bribery in Greece|url=https://news.sky.com/story/harry-maguire-found-guilty-of-aggravated-assault-resisting-arrest-and-attempted-bribery-in-greece-12056524|access-date=25 August 2020|website=Sky News}}</ref> The same day, Maguire was given a suspended [[Prison|jail term]] of 21 months and 10 days as it was a first offence and the three charges were classed as misdemeanours.<ref>{{cite web|title=Harry Maguire: Man Utd captain given suspended jail term for assault and attempted bribery in Greece|url=https://news.sky.com/story/harry-maguire-man-utd-captain-given-suspended-jail-term-for-assault-and-attempted-bribery-in-greece-12056524|access-date=25 August 2020|website=Sky News}}</ref>

On 26 August 2020, Maguire's legal team lodged an appeal against the court verdict. While Manchester United and some British media sources have asserted that his conviction is nullified, this is disputed by the prosecution.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/53922147 |title=Harry Maguire: Man Utd captain's legal team lodges appeal against guilty verdict |date=26 August 2020 |website=BBC Sport |access-date=27 August 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.theguardian.com/football/2020/aug/28/harry-maguire-disappointed-gareth-southgate-cut-him-from-england-squad|title=Harry Maguire accused of 'fantasy tales' by Greek prosecution lawyer|date=28 August 2020|website=The Guardian}}</ref> On 5 March 2021, it was reported that Maguire's appeal was unlikely to take place in 2021, and that while the prosecution are keen for the case to be resolved as quickly as possible, proceedings could theoretically be delayed until 2028.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.theguardian.com/football/2021/mar/05/harry-maguire-free-to-focus-on-united-and-euros-with-greek-hearing-delayed|title=Harry Maguire free to focus on United and Euros with Greek hearing delayed|date=5 March 2021|website=The Guardian}}</ref>
