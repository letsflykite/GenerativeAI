The '''second round''' of [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification (CAF)|CAF matches]] for '''[[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification]]''' was played over six matchdays, from 1 September to 16 November 2021.

==Format==
The second round saw the top 26 ranked CAF teams joined by the 14 winners from the [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification – CAF first round|first round]]. These teams were drawn into ten groups of four teams to play home-and-away [[round-robin tournament|round-robin]] matches. The winners of each group advanced to the [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification – CAF third round|third round]].

==Seeding==
The draw for the second round was held on 21 January 2020, 19:00 [[Central Africa Time|CAT]] ([[UTC+02:00|UTC+2]]), at the Nile Ritz-Carlton in [[Cairo]], Egypt.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.fifa.com/worldcup/news/fifa-world-cup-2022-qualifiers-draws-to-take-centre-stage-in-south-america-and-a |title=FIFA World Cup 2022 qualifiers: draws to take centre stage in South America and Africa |date=11 December 2019 |website=[[FIFA]] |access-date=11 December 2019}}</ref>

The seeding was based on the [[FIFA World Rankings]] of December 2019 (shown in parentheses below).<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.fifa.com/fifa-world-ranking/ranking-table/men/rank/id12770/#CAF |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200115181312/https://www.fifa.com/fifa-world-ranking/ranking-table/men/rank/id12770/#CAF |url-status=dead |archive-date=January 15, 2020 |title=FIFA Men's Ranking – December 2019 (CAF) |publisher=FIFA.com |date=19 December 2019}}</ref>

Note: '''Bolded''' teams qualified for the third round.
