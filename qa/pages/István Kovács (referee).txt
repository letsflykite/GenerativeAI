'''István Kovács'''<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.uefa.com/printoutfiles/competitions/uefachampionsleague/2023/e/e_2035649_pk.pdf |title=GNK Dinamo Zagreb – Chelsea FC Match press kit |date=5 September 2022 |website=UEFA.com |publisher=UEFA |page=2 |access-date=8 February 2023}}</ref> ({{IPA-hu|ˈiʃtvaːn ˈkovaːt͡ʃ}}; born 16 September 1984) is a Hungarian-Romanian [[Association football|football]] [[Referee (association football)|referee]] who officiates primarily in the [[Liga I]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.gsp.ro/fotbal/liga-1/istvan-kovacs-in-istoria-arbitrajului-din-romania-648696.html |title=Istvan Kovacs intră în istoria arbitrajului din România! Doar 3 "centrali" au mai bifat această performanță importantă |last1=Crăiţoiu|first1=Andrei |last2=Manolache |first2=Liviu |date=6 December 2021 |website=[[Gazeta Sporturilor]] |language=ro |trans-title=Istvan Kovacs enters Romanian refereeing history! Only 3 "central" referees have ever achieved this important performance |access-date=24 February 2023}}</ref> He has been a full international for [[FIFA]] since 2018 and a [[UEFA]] elite referee since 2019.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://digitalhub.fifa.com/m/357d1bbadb3ef112/original/2023-FIFA-Refereeing-International-Lists.pdf |title=2023 Refereeing International Lists |date=January 2023 |website=FIFA Digital Assets Hub |publisher=FIFA |page=161 |access-date=8 February 2023}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.frf.ro/recomandate/istvan-kovacs-promovare-in-elite-class/ |title=Istvan Kovacs, promovare în Elite Class |date=14 December 2019 |website=[[Romanian Football Federation]] |language=ro |trans-title=Istvan Kovacs, promotion to Elite Class |access-date=6 February 2023}}</ref>

==Career==
Kovács was selected for [[UEFA Euro 2020|Euro 2020]] alongside his compatriot [[Ovidiu Hațegan]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://ziare.com/ovidiu-hategan/arbitri-romani-la-euro-1674626 |title=Doi romani alesi de UEFA sa arbitreze meciuri la EURO. Cine sunt acestia |last=Serban |first=Teodor |date=21 April 2021 |website=Ziare.com |language=ro |trans-title=Two Romanians chosen by UEFA to referee matches at the EURO. Who they are |access-date=24 February 2023}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.uefa.com/insideuefa/mediaservices/mediareleases/news/0268-12182cf1475f-825d23faf300-1000--referee-teams-for-uefa-euro-2020-appointed/ |title=Referee teams for UEFA EURO 2020 appointed |date=21 April 2021 |website=UEFA.com |access-date=24 February 2023}}</ref> He was in charge of one group stage game between the [[Netherlands national football team|Netherlands]] and [[North Macedonia national football team|North Macedonia]].<ref name="UEFA">{{cite web |url=https://www.uefa.com/insideuefa/protecting-the-game/news/0275-153851958c83-3a1b008ba484-1000--heady-times-for-tirana-final-referee-kovacs/ |title=Heady times for Tirana final referee Kovacs |date=24 May 2022 |website=UEFA.com |access-date=24 February 2023}}</ref>

On 26 April 2022, Kovács officiated the first leg of the [[UEFA Champions League]] semi-final between [[Manchester City F.C.|Manchester City]] and [[Real Madrid CF|Real Madrid]], which the English side won 4–3.<ref name="Guardian">{{cite news |url=https://www.theguardian.com/football/2022/apr/26/manchester-city-real-madrid-champions-league-semi-final-first-leg-match-report |title=Manchester City edge Real Madrid in 4-3 thriller but Benzema keeps tie alive |last=Hytner |first=David |date=26 April 2022 |website=[[The Guardian]] |location=Manchester |issn=1756-3224 |access-date=11 February 2023}}</ref> After a good performance, Kovács was praised by the media especially for the advantage let before [[Bernardo Silva]]'s goal.<ref name="Guardian"/><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.caughtoffside.com/2022/04/27/man-city-silva-goal-advantage-praised/ |title="Fantastic" decision to give advantage for Man City goal against Real Madrid, says Mark Halsey |last=Brus |first=Mark |date=27 April 2022 |website=CaughtOffside |access-date=11 February 2023}}</ref> As a reward, the UEFA Referees Committee chose him to officiate the [[2022 UEFA Europa Conference League final]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://romapress.net/istvan-kovacs-appointed-referee-for-conference-league-final-between-roma-and-feyenoord/ |title=Istvan Kovacs appointed referee for Conference League final between Roma and Feyenoord |date=13 May 2022 |website=RomaPress |access-date=24 February 2023}}</ref>

On 25 May 2022, Kovács was in charge of the [[2022 UEFA Europa Conference League final]] between [[A.S. Roma]] and [[Feyenoord]], the first final in this competition.<ref name="UEFA"/>

István Kovács was appointed for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] along with his compatriot assistant referees Vasile Marinescu and Mihai Artene.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://digitalhub.fifa.com/m/1da4b811328add8f/original/MEDIA-Alphabetical-order-List-of-Match-Officials-FWC-2022-Qatar.pdf |title=List of appointed FIFA Match Officials |website=FIFA Digital Assets Hub |publisher=FIFA |access-date=24 February 2023}}</ref> He was the [[Assistant referee (association football)#Fourth official|fourth official]] in eight matches, but did not referee any games at the tournament.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.digisport.ro/fotbal/cm-2022-qatar/de-ce-istvan-kovas-nu-a-arbitrat-la-cm-2022-si-a-fost-de-8-ori-rezerva-i-au-zis-sa-stea-pe-margine-pentru-ca-e-prea-bun-2107509 |title=De ce Istvan Kovacs nu a arbitrat la CM 2022 și a fost de 8 ori rezervă: "I-au zis să stea pe margine pentru că e prea bun" |date=16 December 2022 |website=[[Digi Sport (Romania)|Digi Sport]] |language=ro |trans-title=Why Istvan Kovacs did not referee at World Cup 2022 and was a reserve 8 times: "They told him to stay on the sidelines because he is too good" |access-date=24 February 2023}}</ref>

On 19 January 2023, Kovács refereed the final of the [[25th Arabian Gulf Cup]] between [[Iraq national football team|Iraq]] and [[Oman national football team|Oman]].<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.iraqinews.com/iraq/iraq-oman-get-ready-for-25th-gulf-cup-final/ |title=Iraq, Oman get ready for 25th Gulf Cup final |last=Salem |first=Amr |date=18 January 2023 |website=Iraqi News |location=Baghdad |access-date=24 February 2023}}</ref>

In January 2023, he was one of six match officials appointed for the [[2022 FIFA Club World Cup]] in Morocco.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://en.as.com/soccer/referees-named-ahead-of-fifa-club-world-cup-n/ |title=Referees named ahead of FIFA Club World Cup |last=Reidy |first=Paul |date=21 January 2023 |website=[[Diario AS]] |access-date=6 February 2023}}</ref> On 7 February, he oversaw the semi-final between [[CR Flamengo|Flamengo]] and [[Al Hilal SFC|Al Hilal]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.marca.com/claro-mx/futbol/mundial-de-clubes/2023/02/07/63e2b91722601d36688b45c6.html |title=Flamengo y Al Hilal protagonizan un polémico cierre de primer tiempo: ¡VAR, penalti y expulsión! |last=Osornio |first=Alan |date=7 February 2023 |website=Marca Claro México |language=es-MX |trans-title=Flamengo and Al Hilal star in a controversial closing of the first half: VAR, penalty and sending off! |access-date=7 February 2023}}</ref>

In June 2023, Kovács was appointed as the fourth official for the [[2023 UEFA Champions League final|UEFA Champions League final]].<ref>{{cite news |title=Referee teams appointed for 2023 UEFA club competition finals |url=https://www.uefa.com/insideuefa/news/0281-1812baf4488c-70b9f3974d92-1000/ |access-date=1 June 2023 |agency=UEFA |date=22 May 2023}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Born in [[Carei]] to a Hungarian father and a [[Satu Mare Swabians|Swabian]] mother, Kovács is a dual citizen,<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.hungarianfootball.com/2021/07/12/an-interview-with-fifa-referee-istvan-kovacs/ |title=An interview with elite FIFA referee István Kovács. |date=12 July 2021 |website=Hungarian Football |access-date=15 February 2023}}</ref> having acquired Hungarian citizenship in 2011.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.nemzetisport.hu/minden_mas_foci/kovacs-istvan-semmifele-hatranyom-nem-szarmazott-a-szarmazasombol-2741861 |title=Kovács István: Semmiféle hátrányom nem származott a származásomból |last=Somogyi |first=Zsolt |date=13 January 2020 |website=[[Nemzeti Sport]] |language=hu |trans-title=István Kovács: I had no disadvantages because of my origin |access-date=25 February 2023 |quote=Anyai ágon sváb a családunk, apai ágon magyar, így kétezertizenegyben kettős állampolgár lettem, és erre nagyon büszke vagyok. |trans-quote=My family is Swabian on my mother's side and Hungarian on my father's side, so I became a dual citizen in 2011, and I'm very proud of that.}}</ref> His younger brother, Szabolcs, is also a referee in the [[Liga I]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.prosport.ro/fotbal-intern/szabolcs-fratele-lui-istvan-kovacs-a-debutat-ca-arbitru-de-centru-in-liga-1-19169483 |title=Szabolcs, fratele lui Istvan Kovacs, a debutat ca arbitru de centru în Liga 1. Au mai existat astfel de cazuri în fotbalul românesc? |last1=Nazare |first1=Daniel |last2=Toma |first2=Răzvan |date=22 December 2020 |website=[[Prosport]] |language=ro |trans-title=Szabolcs, Istvan Kovacs' brother, made his debut as a central referee in Liga 1.
