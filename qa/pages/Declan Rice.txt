'''Declan Rice''' (born 14 January 1999) is an English professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[defensive midfielder]] for {{English football updater|WestHamU}} club [[West Ham United F.C.|West Ham United]] and the [[England national football team|England national team]].

Born in England, Rice has paternal Irish grandparents and previously represented the [[Republic of Ireland national football team|Republic of Ireland]] internationally at both youth and senior levels, before switching his allegiance to England in 2019.
