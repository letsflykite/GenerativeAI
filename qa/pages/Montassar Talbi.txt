'''Montassar Omar Talbi''' ({{lang-ar|مُنتَصَّر عُمَر الطَّالِبِيّ}}; born 26 May 1998) is a professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[centre-back]] for {{French football updater|Lorient}} club [[FC Lorient|Lorient]]. Born in France, he plays for the [[Tunisia national football team|Tunisia national team]].

==Club career==
Born in France, Talbi began playing football with the youth academies of [[Paris FC]] and [[FC Les Lilas]]. His family had to move back to Tunisia, and at the age of 12 Talbi moved to the academy of [[Espérance Sportive de Tunis|ES Tunis]], and moved up through the youth levels.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.football365.fr/turquie-rizespor-montassar-talbi-viser-plus-haut-niveau-europeen-exclu365-7671784.html|title=Turquie - Rizespor / Montassar Talbi : " Viser le plus haut niveau européen " [exclu365]|date=1 September 2018|publisher=}}</ref> Talbi made his professional debut with ES Tunis in a 0–0 [[Tunisian Ligue Professionnelle 1]] tie with [[CS Sfaxien]] on 2 April 2017.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2017/04/02/tunisia/ligue-1/club-sportif-sfaxien/esperance-sportive-de-tunis/2417636/|title=CS Sfaxien vs. ES Tunis - 2 April 2017 - Soccerway|website=Soccerway}}</ref> On 20 June 2018, Talbi signed with [[Çaykur Rizespor]] in the Turkish [[Süper Lig]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mosaiquefm.net/fr/actualites-sport-tunisie/362361/le-defenseur-de-l-est-signe-un-contrat-avec-le-club-turc-rizerpor|title=Le défenseur de l'EST signe un contrat avec le club Turc Rizerpor|website=MosaiqueFM}}</ref>

On 9 August 2021, he signed a four-year contract with [[Russian Premier League]] club [[FC Rubin Kazan]].<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[FC Rubin Kazan]]|url=https://www.rubin-kazan.ru/posts/montassar-talbi-v-rubine|title=МОНТАССАР ТАЛЬБИ – В "РУБИНЕ"|date=9 August 2021|language=ru}}</ref>

On 18 July 2022, Talbi signed a five-year contract with [[FC Lorient|Lorient]] in France.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=Lorient|url=https://fclweb.fr/montassar-talbi-rejoint-le-fc-lorient/|title=MONTASSAR TALBI REJOINT LE FC LORIENT|date=18 July 2022|access-date=18 July 2022|language=fr}}</ref>

==International career==
Talbi is a youth international for the [[Tunisia national under-23 football team|Tunisia 23]]s and made his debut in a 2–0 friendly loss to the [[Italy national under-21 football team|Italy 23]]s on 15 October 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.skysports.com/football/italy-u21-vs-tunisia-u21/402248|title=Football (Sky Sports)|website=SkySports}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.letemps.com.tn/article/110529/eno-italie-%E2%80%93-tunisie-lundi-15-octobre-en-v%C3%A9n%C3%A9tie|title=ENO: Italie – Tunisie, lundi 15 octobre en Vénétie|website=Le Temps}}</ref> He debuted with the senior [[Tunisia national football team|Tunisia national team]] in a 2–1 [[2021 Africa Cup of Nations qualification]] win over [[Equatorial Guinea national football team|Equatorial Guinea]] on 28 March 2021.<ref name="Global Sports Archive 2021">{{cite web | title=Match Report of Tunisia vs Equatorial Guinea - 2021-03-28 - Total Africa Cup of Nations Qualification | website=Global Sports Archive | date=28 March 2021 | url=https://globalsportsarchive.com/match/soccer/2021-03-28/tunisia-vs-equatorial-guinea/1784957/ | access-date=29 March 2021}}</ref>

He was later included for the squad of the [[2021 FIFA Arab Cup]], despite not being released by the Russian club. He reunited with the team following Rubin's 2–0 away defeat to [[PFC Krylia Sovetov Samara|Krylia Sovetov Samara]] and made his debut in the cup in the semi-finals against Egypt, which was won by Tunisia 1–0.{{fact|date=March 2022}}
