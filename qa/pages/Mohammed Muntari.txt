'''Mohammed Muntari''' ({{lang-ar|محمد مونتاري}}; born 20 December 1993) is a [[Ghana]]ian-born naturalised [[Qatar]]i [[association football|footballer]] who currently plays as a [[Forward (association football)#Striker|striker]] for [[Al-Duhail SC|Al-Duhail]] and the [[Qatar national football team|Qatar national team]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://int.soccerway.com/players/mohammed-muntari/286019/|title=Player profile|publisher=soccerway.com|accessdate=27 December 2014}}</ref>

==Career==
Muntari started his career in the Golden Lions Soccer Academy, owned by former Ghanaian international [[Nii Lamptey]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ghanaweb.com/GhanaHomePage/SportsArchive/artikel.php?ID=340205|title=Ghana loses hugely talented striker Mohammed Muntari to Qatar|publisher=ghanaweb.com|date=23 December 2014|accessdate=27 December 2014}}</ref> He joined El Jaish in 2012.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://asia.eurosport.com/football/mohammed-muntari_prs347414/person.shtml|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141227194349/http://asia.eurosport.com/football/mohammed-muntari_prs347414/person.shtml|url-status=dead|archive-date=27 December 2014|title=Player profile|publisher=eurosport.com|accessdate=27 December 2014}}</ref>

In July 2015, he signed a five-year deal with [[Lekhwiya SC|Lekhwiya]].<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.qsl.qa/en/muntari-signs-lekhwiya | title=Muntari signs for Lekhwiya | date= | accessdate=2022-11-29}}</ref>

==International career==
Muntari was born and raised in Ghana, but early in his career moved to Qatar and became a naturalized citizen. He was called up to the [[Qatar national football team|Qatar national team]] in December 2014.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.globalpost.com/dispatch/news/xinhua-news-agency/141222/qatar-squad-asian-cup|title=Qatar squad for Asian Cup|publisher=Xinhua News Agency|date=22 December 2014|accessdate=22 December 2014}}</ref> He made his debut on 27 December 2014 in a friendly against [[Estonia national football team|Estonia]], scoring his first goal.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://cdn.alkass.net/news_details.aspx?news_id=81504|title=منتخبنا الوطني يفوز على استونيا بثلاثية|publisher=alkass.net|date=27 December 2014|accessdate=27 December 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141227200320/http://cdn.alkass.net/news_details.aspx?news_id=81504|archive-date=27 December 2014|url-status=dead}}</ref>

Muntari scored Qatar's first-ever, and only, World Cup goal during the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] against [[Senegal national football team|Senegal]] on 25 November 2022.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Qatar 1-3 Senegal: World Cup hosts on brink of elimination after defeat |url=https://www.skysports.com/football/news/11095/12754582/qatar-1-3-senegal-world-cup-hosts-on-brink-of-elimination-after-defeat |access-date=2022-11-25 |website=Sky Sports |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |date=2022-11-25 |title=World Cup 2022: Ghana-born Mohammed Muntari becomes first-ever player to score for Qatar |url=https://ghanasoccernet.com/world-cup-2022-ghana-born-mohammed-muntari-becomes-first-ever-player-to-score-for-qatar |access-date=2022-11-25 |website=GhanaSoccernet |language=en}}</ref>
