'''Ibrahim Danlad''' (born 2 December 2002) is a Ghanaian professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Goalkeeper (association football)|goalkeeper]] for [[Ghana Premier League]] club [[Asante Kotoko S.C.|Asante Kotoko]] and the [[Ghana national football team|Ghana national team]].

==Club career==
Born in [[Accra]], Danlad joined [[Asante Kotoko]] in 2016–17 season.<ref name="goal">{{cite web | url=https://www.goal.com/en-gh/player/ibrahim-danlad/e764x78r4n8xqjhq1r0v76elm | title=Ibrahim Danlad  | publisher=goal.com | accessdate=7 February 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.worldfootball.net/player_summary/ibrahim-danlad/|title=Ibrahim Danlad}}</ref> In December 2019, Danlad joined [[Ghana Premier League]] club [[Berekum Chelsea F.C.|Berekum Chelsea]] for the rest of the 2019–20 season.<ref name="joyonline">{{cite web | url=https://www.myjoyonline.com/sports/2019/December-10th/kotoko-goalkeeper-danlad-ibrahim-joins-berekum-chelsea-on-loan.php | title=Kotoko goalkeeper Danlad Ibrahim joins Berekum Chelsea on loan   | publisher=myjoyonline.com | accessdate=7 February 2020}}</ref><ref name="ghsoccernet">{{cite web | url=https://ghanasoccernet.com/official-berekum-chelsea-sign-goalkeeper-ibrahim-danlad-on-loan-from-kotoko | title=OFFICIAL: Berekum Chelsea sign goalkeeper Ibrahim Danlad on loan from Kotoko    | date=21 December 2019 | publisher=ghanasoccernet.com | accessdate=7 February 2020}}</ref>

==International career==
Danlad has played for [[Ghana national football team|Ghana]] at all levels, having represented the nation at [[Ghana national under-17 football team|under-17]] to the [[Ghana national football team|senior national team]] (Black Stars) level.<ref name="kasapa">{{cite web | url=https://kasapafmonline.com/2019/01/u-20-afcon-ghana-submit-final-21-man-squad/ | title=U- 20 AFCON: Ghana submit final 21-man squad | publisher=kasapafmonline.com | accessdate=7 February 2020 | archive-date=7 February 2020 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200207110557/https://kasapafmonline.com/2019/01/u-20-afcon-ghana-submit-final-21-man-squad/ | url-status=dead }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.ghanaweb.com/GhanaHomePage/SportsArchive/Ghana-U-20-goalkeeper-Ibrahim-Danlad-eyes-gold-at-All-Africa-games-772306 |title=Ghana U-20 goalkeeper Ibrahim Danlad eyes gold at All Africa games |date=15 August 2019 |publisher=ghanaweb.com |accessdate=27 February 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |date=15 August 2019 |url=https://footballghana.com/2019-africa-games-goalkeeper-ibrahim-dan-lad-targets-winning-gold-with-ghana-u-20 |title= 2019 Africa games: Goalkeeper Ibrahim Danlad targets winning gold with Ghana u-20 |publisher=footballghana.com |accessdate=27 February 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ghanasportsonline.com/2019/10/kotoko-confirm-ghana-u23-call-up-for-goalkeepers-kwame-baah-and-danlad-ibrahim/|title=Kotoko confirm Ghana U23 call up for goalkeepers Kwame Baah and Danlad Ibrahim — Ghana Sports Online|date=11 October 2019}}</ref>

In 2017, he was the first choice goalkeeper for the Ghana squad which participated at the [[2017 FIFA U-17 World Cup]] in India,<ref name="ussoccer">{{cite web | url=https://www.ussoccer.com/stories/2017/10/five-things-to-know-ghana-u17s | title=Five Things to Know: Ghana U-17s | publisher=ussoccer.com | accessdate=7 February 2020}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://scores24.live/en/soccer/p-danlad-ibrahim|title=Danlad Ibrahim - Goalkeeper of Asante Kotoko}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sofascore.com/player/danlad-ibrahim/905154|title=Ibrahim Danlad Asante Kotoko SC video, transfer geçmişi ve istatistik - SofaScore}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.ghanaweb.com/GhanaHomePage/SportsArchive/Meet-Ghana-s-Ibrahim-Danlad-the-youngest-player-at-the-U-17-World-Cup-592301|title=Meet Ghana's Ibrahim Danlad, the youngest player at the U-17 World Cup|date=19 October 2017}}</ref> playing all the matches.
