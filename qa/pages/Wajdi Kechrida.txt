'''Wajdi Kechrida''' ({{lang-ar|وجدي كشريدة}}; born 5 November 1995) is a professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[right-back]] for [[Super League Greece]] club [[Atromitos F.C.|Atromitos]]. Born in France, he plays for the [[Tunisia national football team|Tunisia national team]].

==Club career==
Kechrida made his professional debut for [[Étoile Sportive du Sahel]] in a 3–0 [[Tunisian Ligue Professionnelle 1]] win over [[ES Zarzis]] on 10 September 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2016/09/10/tunisia/ligue-1/esperance-sportive-de-zarzis/etoile-sportive-du-sahel/2339489/|title=Zarzis vs. Étoile Sahel - 10 septembre 2016 - Soccerway|website=int.soccerway.com}}</ref>

On 31 July 2021 he was purchased by [[U.S. Salernitana 1919]],<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.ussalernitana1919.it/lassana-coulibaly-e-wajdi-kechrida-sono-giocatori-della-salernitana/ | title=Lassana Coulibaly e Wajdi Kechrida sono giocatori della Salernitana – US Salernitana 1919 &#124; Sito ufficiale della U.S. Salernitana 1919 }}</ref> strongly desired by Salernitana's manager Angelo Mariano Fabiani. He made his first appearance in the [[Coppa Italia]] match against [[Reggina 1914|Reggina]], won 2-0 by Salernitana, and his debut in [[Serie A]] in the first day of the season against [[Bologna F.C. 1909|Bologna]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Bologna-Salernitana 3-2: vittoria in rimonta per Mihajlovic |url=https://tuttosport.com/news/calcio/serie-a/2021/08/22-84710218/bologna-salernitana_3-2_vittoria_in_rimonta_per_mihajlovic |access-date=2022-11-30 |website=tuttosport.com |language=it}}</ref>

On 29 August 2022, Kechrida signed a two-year contract with [[Atromitos F.C.|Atromitos]] in Greece.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=Atromitos|url=https://www.atromitosfc.gr/transfers/enarksi-synergasias-me-ton-ouaznti-kesxrinta/|title=Έναρξη συνεργασίας με τον Ουαζντί Κεσχρίντα|date=29 August 2022|access-date=29 August 2022|language=el}}</ref>

==International career==
Kechrida made his debut for the [[Tunisia national football team|Tunisia]] in a [[2019 Africa Cup of Nations qualification]] 4–0 win over [[Eswatini national football team|Eswatini]] on 22 March 2019.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cafonline.com/en-us/competitions/32ndeditionoftotalafricacupofnations/MatchDetails?MatchId=34XPQaH06nqiUoCNfk0j2Cr3K5+hfqDSHFGfl5zGwvFlSynONLPmoQNexRyQrghs|title=CAF - Competitions - 32nd Edition of Total Africa Cup of Nations - Match Details|website=www.cafonline.com}}</ref>
