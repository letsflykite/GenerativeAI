'''Ricardo Jorge da Luz Horta''' ({{IPA-pt|ʁiˈkaɾðuˈɔɾtɐ}}; born 15 September 1994) is a Portuguese professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Midfielder#Winger|right winger]] for [[Primeira Liga]] club [[S.C. Braga|Braga]] and the [[Portugal national football team|Portugal national team]].

He spent most of his career with [[S.C. Braga|Braga]] after coming through [[S.L. Benfica|Benfica]]'s youth academy, playing more than 300 games for the former and scoring a club record 110 goals while winning the [[2020–21 Taça de Portugal]] and the [[2019–20 Taça da Liga]]. In the [[Primeira Liga]], he also represented [[Vitória F.C.|Vitória de Setúbal]], and spent two years with [[Málaga CF|Málaga]] in [[La Liga]].

Horta finished second with [[Portugal national under-21 football team|Portugal]] at the [[2015 UEFA European Under-21 Championship|2015 European Under-21 Championship]], also being an international [[Portugal national youth football team|at various youth levels]]. He made his debut with [[Portugal national football team|the full side]] in 2014, being selected for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup|2022 World Cup]].

On 9 December 2013, Horta scored his first professional goal, the game's only against [[Associação Académica de Coimbra – O.A.F.|Académica de Coimbra]].<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.dn.pt/desporto/futebol-nacional/interior/ricardo-horta-da-triunfo-ao-vitoria-de-setubal-3578404.html|title=Ricardo Horta dá triunfo ao Vitória de Setúbal|trans-title=Ricardo Horta gives win to Vitória de Setúbal|newspaper=[[Diário de Notícias]]|first=Tiago|last=Silva Pires|language=pt|date=9 December 2013|access-date=6 August 2014|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140808054741/https://www.dn.pt/desporto/futebol-nacional/interior/ricardo-horta-da-triunfo-ao-vitoria-de-setubal-3578404.html|archive-date=8 August 2014}}</ref> He was a regular starter in his [[2013–14 Primeira Liga|second season]], playing 28 matches and scoring seven times.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.dn.pt/desporto/futebol-internacional/malaga-contrata-o-jovem-portugues-ricardo-horta-4023718.html|title=Málaga contrata o jovem português Ricardo Horta|trans-title=Málaga sign Portuguese youngster Ricardo Horta|newspaper=Diário de Notícias|first=André|last=Rodrigues|language=pt|date=12 July 2014|access-date=21 January 2020}}</ref>

===Málaga===
On 12 July 2014, Horta signed a five-year deal with [[La Liga]] side [[Málaga CF|Málaga]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.malagacf.com/en/new/first-team/portugueseborn-ricardo-horta-joins-malaga-cf/241/71574|title=Portuguese-born Ricardo Horta joins Málaga CF|publisher=Málaga CF|date=12 July 2014|access-date=6 August 2014}}</ref> He made his debut in the competition on 23 August, starting in a 1–0 home win over [[Athletic Bilbao]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.espnfc.com/spanish-primera-divisi%C3%B3n/match/402619/malaga-athletic-bilbao/report|title=Malaga 1–0 Athletic Bilbao|publisher=[[ESPN FC]]|date=23 August 2014|access-date=3 September 2014}}</ref>

Horta scored his first goal for on 6 January 2015, in a 2–0 defeat of [[Levante UD|Levante]] in the [[2014–15 Copa del Rey|round of 16]] of the [[Copa del Rey]] also at [[La Rosaleda Stadium]].<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.marca.com/eventos/marcador/futbol/2014_15/copa/octavos/ida/mga_lev/|title=El Málaga cree más en la Copa|trans-title=Málaga believe more in Cup|newspaper=[[Marca (newspaper)|Marca]]|first=Pablo|last=Egea|language=es|date=6 January 2015|access-date=29 November 2016}}</ref> He finished his spell with 56 appearances in all competitions.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.marca.com/futbol/liga-portuguesa/2020/01/27/5e2ddb2b268e3e57538b4572.html|title=Ricardo Horta: de ser uno más en Málaga... a campeón y 'jugador de moda' en Portugal|trans-title=Ricardo Horta: from just one of the guys in Málaga... to champion and 'hip player' in Portugal|newspaper=Marca|first=Alberto|last=Rubio|language=es|date=27 January 2020|access-date=2 February 2021}}</ref>

In May 2022, after almost eight years in the international wilderness, he was recalled by [[Fernando Santos (footballer, born 1954)|Fernando Santos]] for upcoming [[2022–23 UEFA Nations League]] games,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://maisfutebol.iol.pt/videos/6287894e0cf2ea367d3ce69e/o-ricardo-horta-sempre-fez-parte-de-um-lote-de-jogadores-que-estavam-a-ser-observados|title="O Ricardo Horta sempre fez parte de um lote de jogadores que estavam a ser observados"|trans-title="Ricardo Horta was always part of a group of players we were watching"|publisher=Mais Futebol|language=pt|date=20 May 2022|access-date=21 May 2022}}</ref> playing 20 minutes in the 1–1 draw with [[Spain national football team|Spain]] in [[Seville]] on 2 June and scoring his team's goal.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://portugoal.net/selecao/2618-spain-vs-portugal-uefa-nations-league-2|title=Portugal steal a point in Sevilla|publisher=PortuGOAL|first=Nathan|last=Motz|date=2 June 2022|access-date=3 June 2022}}</ref> In November, he was named in the final squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] in Qatar.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://desporto.sapo.pt/futebol/mundial/artigos/os-26-convocados-de-portugal-para-o-mundial2022|title=Os 26 convocados de Portugal: Gonçalo Ramos, António Silva, Otávio e Matheus Nunes chamados|trans-title=Portugal select 26: Gonçalo Ramos, António Silva, Otávio and Matheus Nunes called|publisher=SAPO|language=pt|date=10 November 2022|access-date=10 November 2022}}</ref> As the nation had already secured qualification from their group, he started in the last fixture, opening an eventual 2–1 loss to [[South Korea national football team|South Korea]] in [[Al Rayyan]] following a [[Cross (association football)|cross]] from [[Diogo Dalot]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/63752148|title=South Korea 2–1 Portugal|publisher=[[BBC Sport]]|first=Chris|last=Bevan|date=2 December 2022|access-date=2 December 2022}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Horta's younger brother, [[André Horta|André]], is also a footballer. He was a teammate at Benfica, Vitória de Setúbal and Braga.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.record.xl.pt/Futebol/Nacional/1a_liga/vitoria_setubal/interior.aspx?content_id=930982|title=André Horta acredita numa surpresa na Luz|trans-title=André Horta believes in surprise at the Luz|newspaper=Record|first=Ricardo|last=Lopes Pereira|language=pt|date=13 February 2015|access-date=25 August 2015|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150706054057/http://www.record.xl.pt/Futebol/Nacional/1a_liga/vitoria_setubal/interior.aspx?content_id=930982|archive-date=6 July 2015}}</ref>
