'''Facundo Pellistri Rebollo''' ({{IPA-es|faˈkundo peˈlistɾi}}; born 20 December 2001) is a Uruguayan professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Midfielder#Winger|winger]] for [[Premier League]] club [[Manchester United F.C.|Manchester United]] and the [[Uruguay national football team|Uruguay national team]].

Born in [[Montevideo]], Pellistri is a graduate of [[Peñarol]]'s youth system. He made 37 first-team appearances for the club and was named in the [[Uruguayan Primera División]] Team of the Season in 2019. He transferred to Manchester United in October 2020, for a fee of £9 million. Since joining United, he has had two loan spells at Spanish club [[Deportivo Alavés|Alavés]], where he made 35 appearances in total.

Pellistri represented Uruguay at under-16 level, before making his senior international debut in January 2022. He played for Uruguay at the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].

==Early life and education==
Pellistri was born in [[Montevideo]] to a family of [[Spanish Uruguayans|Spanish]] and [[Italian Uruguayans|Italian]] origin. He also holds a [[Spanish passport]] since his father's grandmother was from [[A Coruña]] in [[Galicia (Spain)|Galicia]].<ref>{{Cite web|date=28 April 2020|title=Pellistri, comunitario y en la mira del Real Madrid|url=https://www.tenfield.com.uy/pellistri-comunitario-y-en-la-mira-de-real-madrid/|access-date=7 October 2020|website=Tenfield.com|language=es}}</ref> The lead singer of Uruguayan rock band [[La Vela Puerca]], Sebastián Teysera, is Pellistri's [[Godparent|godfather]].<ref name="godfather">{{cite web|title=Facundo Pellistri: Hacer un gol clásico es único|date=29 September 2017 |url=http://www.padreydecano.com/cms/formativas/facundo-pellistri-hacer-un-gol-clasico-es-unico/|access-date=7 October 2020}}</ref>

Pellistri is the son of an accountant.<ref name="theathletic">{{cite web |last1=Mitten |first1=Andy |title=Facundo Pellistri: 'My baby-like signature almost cost me my move to Manchester United' |url=https://theathletic.com/2880125/2021/10/10/facundo-pellistri-my-baby-like-signature-almost-cost-me-my-move-to-manchester-united/ |website=[[The Athletic]] |access-date=24 November 2022 |language=en |date=10 October 2021}}</ref> He was privately educated at two institutions in the [[Pocitos]] neighbourhood: St Brendan's School for his early years, where he first learned English, and then [[Ivy Thomas Memorial School]] through the fourth grade of high school.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Inzaurralde |first1=Luis Eduardo |title=Pellistri en su camino, entre Forlán y el Chino Recoba |url=https://www.elobservador.com.uy/nota/pellistri-en-su-camino-entre-forlan-y-el-chino-202010172260 |website=[[El Observador (Uruguay)|El Observador]] |access-date=24 November 2022 |language=Spanish |date=18 October 2020}}</ref> His strongest subject at school was [[Mathematics]].<ref name=theathletic /> In 2019, Pellistri graduated from a pre-architectural study program.<ref name="ovacion">{{Cite web|title=Facundo Pellistri: "Xisco me agarró y me dijo: 'el gol es tuyo'"|url=https://www.ovaciondigital.com.uy/futbol/facundo-pellistri-xisco-me-agarro-me-dijo-gol.html|date=25 November 2019|access-date=7 October 2020|website=ovaciondigital.com.uy|language=es}}</ref>

==Club career==
===Peñarol===
Pellistri is a youth academy graduate of [[Peñarol]], who he joined in 2012,<ref>{{cite news |title=Pellistri viajaría a Manchester |url=https://www.tenfield.com.uy/pellistri-viajaria-el-lunes-a-manchester/ |website=tenfield.com.uy |date=4 October 2020 |access-date=7 December 2022}}</ref> having previously played youth football for La Picada and [[Club Atlético River Plate (Montevideo)|River Plate Montevideo]].<ref>{{cite news |title=Facundo Pellistri: "Me gustaría poder luchar por un lugar" |url=https://www.padreydecano.com/cms/noticias/facundo-pellistri-me-gustaria-poder-luchar-por-un-lugar/ |website=padreydecano.com |date=21 June 2019 |access-date=7 December 2022}}</ref> He made his professional debut on 11 August 2019, in Peñarol's 2–2 draw against [[Defensor Sporting]].<ref name="debynt">{{cite web|title=Debutantes de la 4a fecha del Torneo Intermedio 2019|url=https://www.auf.org.uy/debutantes-de-la-4a-fecha-del-torneo-intermedio-2019/|date=14 August 2019|access-date=3 July 2021}}</ref> He scored his first goal on 6 November 2019, in a 3–1 win against [[C.A. Cerro|Cerro]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Cerro vs. Peñarol - 6 November 2019 |url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2019/11/06/uruguay/primera-division/club-atletico-cerro/club-atletico-penarol/3163292/ |access-date=10 August 2020 }}</ref> For his performances in his debut season in the [[Uruguayan Primera División]], he was named in the Equipo Ideal del Campeonato Uruguayo (Ideal Team of the Uruguayan Championship) in December 2019.<ref name="2019award">{{cite news |title=Equipo Ideal del Campeonato Uruguayo 2019 |url=https://www.auf.org.uy/equipo-ideal-del-campeonato-uruguayo-2019/ |website=auf.org.uy |date=27 December 2019 |access-date=7 December 2022}}</ref>

===Manchester United===
Pellistri joined Manchester United on 5 October 2020, for a reported transfer fee of £9 million, on a five-year contract.<ref>{{cite news |title=Alex Telles: Man Utd sign Porto left-back and agree Amad Diallo move |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/54425891 |website=BBC Sport |date=5 October 2020 |access-date=7 December 2022}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Man Utd complete €10m signing of Uruguayan star Pellistri |url=https://www.goal.com/en-qa/news/man-utd-complete-10-million-signing-of-uruguayan-star/10at0l08wyrl61ni73unr44oya |website=Goal.com |date=5 October 2020 |access-date=5 October 2020 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Pellistri becomes a Red |url=https://www.manutd.com/en/news/detail/man-utd-sign-facundo-pellistri-from-club-atletico-penarol |website=ManUtd.com |publisher=Manchester United |date=5 October 2020 |access-date=5 October 2020 }}</ref> [[Diego Forlan]], who was then managing Peñarol, endorsed Pellistri in a conversation with Manchester United manager and former teammate [[Ole Gunnar Solskjær]], comparing the young player to [[Ryan Giggs]] and emphasising his ability to speak English.<ref>{{cite web |author1=Staff Reporter |title=Diego Forlan reveals what he told Man Utd boss Solskjaer about Facundo Pellistri |url=https://metro.co.uk/2020/10/16/diego-forlan-reveals-what-he-told-ole-gunnar-solskjaer-about-manchester-new-boy-facundo-pellistri-13432417/ |website=[[Metro (British newspaper)|Metro]] |access-date=24 November 2022 |language=en |date=16 October 2020}}</ref> His first pre-season goal was on 18 July 2021, against [[Derby County F.C.|Derby County]], in a 2–1 away win at [[Pride Park]].<ref>{{cite news |title=Derby 1 Manchester United 2 |url=https://www.manutd.com/en/news/detail/derby-v-man-utd-friendly-match-report-18-july-2021 |website=ManUtd.com |publisher=Manchester United |date=18 July 2021 |access-date=4 August 2021 }}</ref> 

On 31 January 2021, Pellistri joined [[Deportivo Alavés]] on loan for the remainder of the season.<ref>{{cite news |last=Ganley |first=Joe |date=31 January 2021 |title=Facundo Pellistri joins Alaves on loan |website=ManUtd.com |publisher=Manchester United |url=https://www.manutd.com/en/amp/news/detail/man-utd-winger-facundo-pellistri-completes-loan-move-to-alaves |access-date=31 January 2021}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last=Booth |first=Dominic |date=31 January 2021 |title=Facundo Pellistri loan exit from Manchester United confirmed |url=https://www.manchestereveningnews.co.uk/sport/football/transfer-news/facundo-pellistri-man-utd-breaking-19739427 |access-date=31 January 2021 |website=Manchester Evening News}}</ref> He returned to United to play in their summer pre-season matches, before going back on loan to Alavés in August for the 2021–22 season.<ref>{{cite news |last=Partington |first=Mikey |date=5 August 2021 |title=Loan move secured for Pellistri |website=ManUtd.com |publisher=Manchester United |url=https://www.manutd.com/en/news/detail/facundo-pellistri-rejoins-deportivo-alaves-on-loan-for-the-2021-22-campaign-5-august-2021 |accessdate=5 August 2021}}</ref>

Pellistri made his competitive debut for Manchester United on 10 January 2023, coming on as a substitute and assisting the second goal in a 3–0 [[2022–23 EFL Cup|EFL Cup]] win over [[Charlton Athletic F.C.|Charlton Athletic]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Manchester United 3-0 Charlton Athletic: Old Trafford side into Carabao Cup semi-finals|first=Simon |last=Stone |language=en-GB |work=BBC Sport |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/64149053 |access-date=10 January 2023|date=10 January 2023}}</ref> Pellistri made his Premier League debut on 8 February 2023, coming on as a substitute in a 2–2 draw against [[Leeds United F.C.|Leeds United]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.manutd.com/en/amp/news/detail/man-utd-vs-leeds-united-match-report-8-february-2023|title=Manchester United 2 Leeds United 2|work=Manchester United|date=9 February 2023|access-date=4 March 2023|last=Mulholland|first=Charlie}}</ref> On 17 March 2023, Pellistri made his first senior start for United, against [[Real Betis]] in the [[2022–23 UEFA Europa League|UEFA Europa League]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.manutd.com/en/news/detail/facundo-pellistri-watch-on-his-first-senior-start-for-man-united|title=Facundo Pellistri Watch On His First Senior Start for Man United|date=17 March 2023|access-date=23 March 2023|work=Manchester United|last=Marshall|first=Adam}}</ref>

==International career==
[[File:Facundo Pellistri 2022.jpg|thumb|224x224px|Pellistri lining up for [[Uruguay national football team|Uruguay]] in 2022.]]
Pellistri is a former Uruguayan youth international, having made two appearances at under-16 level in 2017.<ref name=debynt/> He was called up to the [[Uruguay national football team|senior team]] for the first time on 7 January 2022, for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification (CONMEBOL)|World Cup qualifying]] matches against [[Paraguay national football team|Paraguay]] and [[Venezuela national football team|Venezuela]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Reservados vs. Paraguay y Venezuela|url=https://www.auf.org.uy/reservados-vs-paraguay-venezuela/|date=7 January 2022|access-date=7 January 2022}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=January's international break explained |url=https://www.manutd.com/en/amp/news/detail/a-guide-to-the-january-2022-international-break-for-manchester-united-players|website=ManUtd.com |publisher=Manchester United |date=26 January 2022|access-date=27 January 2022}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Forlan preaches patience with Pellistri|url=https://www.manutd.com/en/news/detail/diego-forlan-preaches-patience-with-loanee-facundo-pellistri|website=ManUtd.com |publisher=Manchester United |date=25 January 2022|access-date=27 January 2022}}</ref> He made his full international debut on 27 January, in a 1–0 away win against Paraguay.<ref>{{cite web|title=Facundo Pellistri makes debut for Uruguay in World Cup qualifying win |url=https://www.unitedinfocus.com/news/facundo-pellistri-makes-debut-for-uruguay-in-world-cup-qualifying-win/amp/ |website=United in Focus |date=27 January 2022|access-date=27 January 2022}}</ref>

Pellistri was included in the [[2022 FIFA World Cup squads#Uruguay|squad]] for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.auf.org.uy/se-confirmo-la-lista-de-convocados-para-la-copa-mundial-de-catar-2022/ |title=Se confirmó la lista de convocados para la Copa Mundial de Catar 2022 |trans-title=2022 Qatar World Cup squad confirmed |publisher=[[Uruguayan Football Association]] |date=10 November 2022 |access-date=10 November 2022 |language=es}}</ref> He started and played for 88 minutes in Uruguay's first game of the tournament, against [[South Korea national football team|South Korea]], before being replaced by [[Guillermo Varela]].<ref>{{cite news |first=Emma |last=Sanders|title=World Cup 2022: Uruguay 0-0 South Korea - nothing to separate teams in exciting goalless draw|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/63657469|work=BBC Sport |date=24 November 2022 |access-date=24 November 2022 }}</ref>

==Style of play==
A [[Midfielder#Winger|winger]], Pellistri has been praised highly for his agility, versatility, dribbling skills, technique, and strategic vision on the field.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Turk |first=Alex |url=https://www.si.com/soccer/manchesterunited/news/riquelme-claims-there-arent-many-like-pellistri |title=South American great claims "there aren't many" like Facundo Pellistri |date=7 October 2020 |access-date=7 October 2020 |work=Sports Illustrated}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last=Fay |first=Richard |url=https://www.manchestereveningnews.co.uk/sport/football/transfer-news/manchester-united-pellistri-latest-news-19050642 |title=What Diego Forlan has said about Man United signing Facundo Pellistri |date=5 October 2020 |access-date=7 October 2020 |publisher=Manchester Evening News}}</ref>
