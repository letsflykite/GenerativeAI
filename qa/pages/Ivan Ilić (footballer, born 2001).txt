'''Ivan Ilić''' ({{lang-sr-cyr|Иван Илић}}; born 17 March 2001) is a [[Serbia|Serbian]] professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[midfielder]] for [[Serie A]] club [[Torino F.C.|Torino]], on loan from [[Hellas Verona F.C.|Hellas Verona]], and the [[Serbia national football team|Serbia national team]].

==Club career==
===Red Star Belgrade===
Ivan started playing football with local football club Filip Filipović.<ref name="biseri">{{cite web |url=http://www.kurir.rs/sport/fudbal/zvezdini-biseri-braca-luka-i-ivan-ilic-velika-buducnost-crveno-belih-clanak-2762057|title=ZVEZDINI BISERI: Braća Luka i Ivan Ilić velika budućnost crveno-belih|website=[[Kurir]]|language=sr|date=1 April 2017|access-date=1 April 2017}}</ref> Ilić moved to [[Red Star Belgrade]] youth team from Real Niš in summer 2016.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.fss.rs/documents//fudbal/fudbal_32_2016.pdf|title="FUDBAL" 32/16 - page 2078|website=[[Football Association of Serbia]]|language=sr|date=10 August 2016|access-date=1 April 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160826133242/http://www.fss.rs/documents//fudbal/fudbal_32_2016.pdf|archive-date=22 August 2016}}</ref> Shortly after he joined the new club, he signed his first three-year professional contract.<ref name="professional">{{cite web |url=http://www.crvenazvezdafk.com/scc/vest/4976/ivan-ilic-postao-profesionalac|title=Иван Илић постао професионалац|website=Red Star Belgrade official website|language=sr|date=27 June 2016|access-date=1 April 2017}}</ref> Ilić made his senior debut for [[Red Star Belgrade]] in 28 fixture match of the [[2016–17 Serbian SuperLiga]] season against [[FK Spartak Subotica|Spartak Subotica]], replacing [[Srđan Plavšić]] in last minute of the game played on 1 April 2017. He became the youngest debutant in the club history<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.crvenazvezdafk.com/scc/vest/5756/Ivan-Ilic-najmladi-debitant-Zvezde|title=Иван Илић најмлађи дебитант Звезде!|website=Red Star Belgrade official website|language=sr|date=1 April 2017|access-date=1 April 2017}}</ref> and first player born 2001 who appeared in the [[Serbian SuperLiga]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://mondo.rs/a995967/Sport/Fudbal/Ivan-Ilic-najmladji-debitant-u-Superligi.html|title=Zvezda i Srbija dobili novog najmlađeg debitanta!|website=mondo.rs|language=sr|date=1 April 2017|access-date=1 April 2017}}</ref>

===Manchester City move & Red Star loan===
In summer 2017, Ilić had been sold to [[Manchester City F.C.|Manchester City]] along with his older brother [[Luka Ilić|Luka]], staying on loan at Red Star until his 18th birthday.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.crvenazvezdafk.com/scc/vest/6149/Terzic-Zvezdasi-budite-ponosni|title=Терзић: Звездаши, будите поносни|website=Red Star Belgrade official website|language=sr|date=7 August 2017|access-date=7 August 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last=O'Rourke |first=Peter |title=Man City to sign brothers Luka, Ivan Ilic from Red Star Belgrade - sources |url=http://www.espn.co.uk/football/soccer-transfers/story/3159149/manchester-city-to-sign-brothers-luka-ivan-ilic-from-red-star-belgrade-sources |publisher=[[ESPN]] |date=17 July 2017|access-date=2 July 2018}}</ref>  At the beginning of October 2017, [[The Guardian]] picked Ilić as the most prospective first-year scholar in the [[Manchester City F.C. EDS and Academy|academy of Manchester City]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.theguardian.com/football/ng-interactive/2017/oct/03/next-generation-2017-20-of-the-best-talents-at-premier-league-clubs|title=Next Generation 2017: 20 of the best talents at Premier League clubs|website=[[The Guardian]]|date=3 October 2017|access-date=4 October 2017}}</ref> Ilić played his first [[Full-time (sports)|full-time]] senior match in 5–0 [[Serbian Cup]] victory over [[FK Dinamo Vranje|Dinamo Vranje]] on 11 October 2017.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.mozzartsport.com/vesti/sastavi-crvena-zvezda-dinamo-vranje/181077|title=KRAJ: Crvena zvezda - Dinamo Vranje 5:0 (VIDEO)|website=mozzartsport.com|language=sr|date=11 October 2017|access-date=11 October 2017}}</ref>

=== Hellas Verona & Torino ===
In 2020, Ilić joined [[Serie A]] club [[Hellas Verona F.C.|Hellas Verona]]. He was loaned to [[Torino F.C.|Torino]] on 30 January 2023.<ref>{{Cite web |last= |date= 28 January 2023|title=Ivan Ilic to choose Torino over Marseille |url=https://www.footitalia.com/ivan-ilic-to-choose-torino-over-marseille/ |access-date=2023-01-30 |website=FootItalia.com |language=en-US}}</ref>

==Style of play==
As a left-footed player, Ivan appeared as a [[Defender (association football)#Left-back|left-back]] in early years of playing football. Later he converted his position on the field to [[Midfielder#Defensive midfielder|defensive midfielder]], and through the youth career, Ilić usually played pairing with his older brother Luka as a central midfielder.<ref name="biseri"/> He is also available to play a classic defensive role in 4–1–4–1 formation, which he mostly played in national team.{{citation needed|date=March 2018}} Like his brother, Ivan is 1.82 m tall.<ref name="ldi">{{cite web |url=http://www.zurnal.rs/fudbal/zvezda/44841/luka-ilic-sin-proslavljene-kosarkasice-odradio-cetvrte-pripreme-sa-prvim-timom-zvezde|title=Лука Илић, син прослављене кошаркашице, одрадио четврте припреме са првим тимом Звезде|website=[[Sportski žurnal]]|language=sr|date=11 February 2017|access-date=4 April 2017}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Born in [[Niš]],<ref name="crvenazvezdafk">{{cite web |url=http://www.crvenazvezdafk.com/scc/igraci/prvi-tim/365/ivan-ilic|title=Иван Илић|website=Red Star Belgrade official website|language=sr|access-date=1 April 2017}}</ref> Ilić grew up in a sports family. His father Srđan played as a [[Winger (sports)|winger]] with [[FK Radnički Niš|Radnički Niš]] and his mother [[Danijela Ilić]] played basketball professionally. Ivan's older brother, [[Luka Ilić|Luka]] is also a professional footballer.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.novosti.rs/%D0%B2%D0%B5%D1%81%D1%82%D0%B8/%D1%81%D0%BF%D0%BE%D1%80%D1%82.434.html:659292-Luka-i-Ivan-Ilic-predvode-novi-talas-talenata-Zivimo-za-fudbal-i-Zvezdin-dres|title=Лука и Иван Илић предводе нови талас талената: Живимо за фудбал и Звездин дрес!|website=[[Večernje novosti]]|language=sr|date=7 April 2017|access-date=15 August 2017}}</ref>
