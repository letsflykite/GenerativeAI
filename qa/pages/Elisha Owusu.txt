'''Elisha Owusu''' (born 7 November 1997) is a professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[midfielder]] for {{French football updater|Auxerre}} club [[AJ Auxerre|Auxerre]]. Born in France, he plays for the [[Ghana national football team|Ghana national team]].

==Club career==
Owusu is a youth product of [[Olympique Lyonnais|Lyon]], joining in 2010 and became the captain of their reserve side. On 23 February 2017, Owusu signed his first professional contract with Lyon for 3 years.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ol.fr/fr-fr/contenus/articles/2017/02/23/elisha-owusu-signe-un-premier-contrat-professionnel-de-trois-ans-avec-lol-29759|title=Elisha Owusu signe un premier contrat professionnel de trois ans avec l'OL|publisher=}}</ref> On 19 June 2018, he was loaned for the season to [[FC Sochaux-Montbéliard|Sochaux]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.ol.fr/fr-fr/contenus/articles/2018/06/19/mercato-elisha-owusu-prete-un-an-au-fc-sochaux-montbeliard-35376|title=Mercato : Elisha Owusu prêté un an au FC Sochaux-Montbéliard|publisher=}}</ref> He made his professional debut with Sochaux in a 1–0 [[Ligue 2]] loss to [[Grenoble Foot 38|Grenoble]] on 27 July 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.lfp.fr/ligue2/feuille_match/84994|title=LFP.fr - Ligue de Football Professionnel - Domino's Ligue 2 - Saison 2018/2019 - 1ère journée - Grenoble Foot 38 / FC Sochaux-Montbéliard|website=www.lfp.fr}}</ref>

On 21 June 2019, Owusu signed a four-year contract with Belgian club [[K.A.A. Gent|Gent]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.kaagent.be/nl/supporter/news/21-06-2019/bienvenue-elisha-owusu|title=BIENVENUE ELISHA OWUSU!|accessdate=25 June 2019|publisher=K.A.A. Gent}}</ref> He left the club on 16 January 2023, joining [[Ligue 1]] side [[AJ Auxerre|Auxerre]] on a contract until June 2025.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Elisha Owusu (La Gantoise) s'engage avec l'AJ Auxerre |url=https://www.lequipe.fr/Football/Actualites/Elisha-owusu-la-gantoise-s-engage-avec-l-aj-auxerre/1375053 |access-date=16 January 2023 |date=16 January 2023 |website=[[L'Équipe]] |lang=fr}}</ref> 

==International career==
Born in France, Owusu is of Ghanaian descent.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://ghanasoccernet.com/exclusive-ghanaian-youngster-elisha-owusu-completes-sochaux-loan-move|title=EXCLUSIVE: Ghanaian youngster Elisha Owusu completes Sochaux loan move|website=GhanaSoccernet}}</ref> He debuted for the [[Ghana national football team|Ghana national team]] in a 1–1 [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification]] tie with [[Nigeria national football team|Nigeria]] on 29 March 2022, a result that helped Ghana qualify for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ghanasportsonline.com/2022/03/black-stars-elisha-owusu-debuts-as-ghana-beat-nigeria-to-secure-fifa-world-cup-ticket/|title=Black Stars: Elisha Owusu debuts as Ghana beat Nigeria to secure FIFA World Cup ticket — Ghana Sports Online|date=30 March 2022}}</ref>
