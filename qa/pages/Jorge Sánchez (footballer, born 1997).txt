'''Jorge Eduardo Sánchez Ramos''' (born 10 December 1997) is a Mexican professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[right-back]] for [[Eredivisie]] club [[AFC Ajax|Ajax]] and the [[Mexico national football team|Mexico national team]].

==Club career==
===Santos Laguna===
Born in [[Torreón]], Sánchez began his career with [[Santos Laguna]], joining the team's youth academy. He made his [[Liga MX]] debut on 18 September 2016 against [[Club Universidad Nacional|Pumas UNAM]] in a 3–1 victory, playing all 90 minutes.<ref>{{cite web|title=Jorge Eduardo Sánchez Debutó con los Guerreros|url=http://www.ascensomx.net/cancha/detallenoticia/17794/jorge-eduardo-sanchez-debuto-con-los-guerreros|work=AscensoMX.net|first=Juan Pablo|last=Aguila|date=18 September 2016|language=Spanish}}{{Dead link|date=April 2023 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref>

===América===
In June 2018, Sánchez joined [[Club América]].<ref>{{cite web|title=América anuncia el fichaje de Jorge Sánchez|url=https://www.goal.com/es-mx/noticias/america-anuncia-el-fichaje-de-jorge-sanchez/1nb1s4bo3wrd21l7o455igaeav|work=Goal.com|date=6 June 2019|language=Spanish}}</ref>

===Ajax===
On 10 August 2022, Sánchez joined Dutch club [[AFC Ajax]] on a four-year contract, joining up with international teammate [[Edson Álvarez]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Oficial: Jorge Sánchez es nuevo jugador del Ajax|url=https://www.tvazteca.com/aztecadeportes/oficial-jorge-sanchez-nuevo-jugador-ajax-eredivisie|work=Tvazteca.com|first=Miguel|last=Bárcena|date=10 August 2022|language=Spanish}}</ref> He was handed jersey number 19.<ref>{{cite web|title=Este es el número que usará Jorge Sánchez con el Ajax en Países Bajos|url=https://www.tvazteca.com/aztecadeportes/numero-jorge-sanchez-ajax-eredivisie|work=Tvazteca.com|first=Pedro|last=Gálvez|date=10 August 2022|language=Spanish}}</ref> On 28 August 2022, he made his [[Eredivisie]] debut for Ajax in a 2–0 victory against [[FC Utrecht]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Así fue el debut de Jorge Sánchez con el Ajax en la Eredivisie|url=https://www.tvazteca.com/aztecadeportes/video-debut-jorge-sanchez-ajax-eredivisie-utrecht|work=Tvazteca.com|first=Luis|last=Mejía|date=28 August 2022|language=Spanish}}</ref> On 7 September 2022, Sánchez made his [[UEFA Champions League]] debut in a 4–0 home victory against Scottish club [[Rangers F.C.|Rangers]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Así fue el debut de Jorge Sánchez en la Champions League|url=https://www.tvazteca.com/aztecadeportes/video-debut-jorge-sanchez-champions-league|work=Tvazteca.com|first=Pedro|last=Gálvez|date=7 September 2022|language=Spanish}}</ref>

==International career==
===Youth===
Sánchez was included in the final roster that participated at the [[2018 Toulon Tournament]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Revelan convocatoria del Tri Sub-21 para el torneo Esperanzas de Toulon|url=http://espndeportes.espn.com/futbol/mundial/nota/_/id/4333245/revelan-convocatoria-del-tri-sub-21-para-el-torneo-esperanzas-de-toulon|work=ESPN|language=Spanish|date=22 May 2018|accessdate=26 October 2018}}</ref> He appeared in four matches and was included in the Best XI of the tournament as Mexico finished runner-up.<ref name="Toulon">{{cite web|title=L'équipe type du Festival International Espoirs 2018 |url=https://festival-foot-espoirs.com/festival/detail_actualite.php?id=3391 |date=11 June 2018 |publisher=Toulon Tournament |language=French}}</ref> That year, Sánchez also played in the [[Football at the 2018 Central American and Caribbean Games – Men's tournament|Central American and Caribbean Games]],<ref>{{cite web|title=Diego Lainez to lead team for the Central American and Caribbean Games|url=https://www.fmfstateofmind.com/2018/7/13/17566292/diego-lainez-to-lead-mexico-team-for-the-central-american-and-caribbean-games |work=FMF State of Mind|date=13 July 2018 |accessdate=30 July 2018}}</ref> appearing in two group stage matches. Mexico finished last in their group with one point.

Sánchez was in the preliminary roster for the [[2020 CONCACAF Men's Olympic Qualifying Championship]] but did not make the final squad.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://miseleccion.mx/noticias/3319/Convocatoria%2520preliminar%2520para%2520el%2520Preol%25C3%25ADmpico%2520|title=Convocatoria preliminar para el Preolímpico|publisher=[[Mexican Football Federation]]|website=miseleccion.mx/|date=23 February 2021|language=es}}</ref>

In 2021, Sánchez was included in the [[Mexico national under-23 football team|under-23 squad]] for [[Football at the 2020 Summer Olympics - Men's tournament|Summer Olympics]] in Japan. He won the bronze medal with the Olympic team.<ref>{{cite web|title=Revelan lista oficial de la Selección Sub-23 que acudirá a Tokio 2020|date=5 July 2021 |url=https://espndeportes.espn.com/futbol/selecciones/nota/_/id/8854207/mexico-sub23-convocatoria-jaime-lozano-tokio-2020}}</ref>

===Senior===
Sánchez made his [[Mexico national football team|senior national team]] debut on 26 March 2019 in a friendly against [[Paraguay national football team|Paraguay]], as a starter.<ref>{{cite web|title=Sanchez sets eyes on Gold Cup after Mexico debut|url=https://www.goal.com/en-us/news/sanchez-sets-eyes-on-gold-cup-after-mexico-debut/jbwao35yhlqb1qoep90raw9cu|work=Goal.com|first=Jon|last=Arnold|date=27 March 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[ESPN]]|url=http://www.espn.com/soccer/match?gameId=532880|title=Mexico v Paraguay game report|date=26 March 2019}}</ref> In May, he was included in [[Gerardo Martino]]'s preliminary list for the [[2019 CONCACAF Gold Cup|CONCACAF Gold Cup]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Marco Fabián vuelve y no está Tecatito; anuncian lista previa de México para Copa Oro|url=https://www.mediotiempo.com/futbol/seleccion-mayor/seleccion-mexicana-prelista-copa-oro-2019-tata-martino|publisher=Mediotiempo.com|first=Enrique|last=Martínez Villar|date=14 May 2019}}</ref> On 9 June, during a friendly match against [[Ecuador national football team|Ecuador]], he picked up an injury on his right leg causing him to miss the Gold Cup.<ref>{{cite web|title=Jorge Sánchez se pierde la Copa Oro; a Moreno lo esperarán|url=https://www.mediotiempo.com/futbol/seleccion-mayor/copa-oro-2019-jorge-sanchez-baja-uriel-antuna-reemplazara|publisher=Mediotiempo.com|first=Enrique|last=Martínez Villar|date=14 June 2019}}</ref>

In October 2022, Sánchez was named in Mexico's preliminary 31-man [[Mexico national football team|squad]] for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]], and in November, he was ultimately included in the final 26-man roster.<ref>{{cite web|title=Lista de convocados de la Selección Mexicana al Mundial de Qatar 2022|url=https://www.tvazteca.com/aztecadeportes/lista-oficial-convocados-seleccion-mexicana-mundial-qatar-2022|work=Tvazteca.com|first=Omar|last=Moro|date=14 November 2022|language=Spanish}}</ref>
