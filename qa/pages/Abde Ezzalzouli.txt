'''Abdessamad''' "'''Abde'''" '''Ezzalzouli''' ({{lang-ar|عبد الصمد الزلزولي}}; born 17 December 2001), sometimes known as '''Ez Abde''', is a Moroccan professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[winger (association football)|winger]] for [[La Liga]] club [[FC Barcelona|Barcelona]]. Born in Morocco and raised in Spain, he represents the [[Morocco national football team|Morocco national team]] at international level. He began his professional career playing for [[Hércules CF|Hércules]].

== Early life ==
Born in Morocco, Ezzalzouli moved to Spain with his family at the age of seven to live and started his football youth career in the neighborhood of Carrús, in the city of [[Elche]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.lagradaonline.com/primer-equipo/abde|title=Apuntan a un supuesto interés por Abde|date=23 November 2020|website=lagradaonline.com}}</ref>

== Club career ==

=== Hércules ===
Ezzalzouli was part of the youth academies for Peña Ilicitana Raval CF, CD Pablo Iglesias, [[Kelme CF]], Promesas Elche CF, and CD Cultural Carrús.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.elespanol.com/alicante/deporte/hercules-cf/20210831/abde-pone-rumbo-masia/608439157_0.html|title=Abde pone rumbo a La Masía|date=31 August 2021|website=El Español}}</ref> He trialled at [[Elche CF]], the main club in his hometown, but was not offered a place in their academy.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://as.com/futbol/2020/11/23/mas_futbol/1606158475_770163.html|title=Abde, el niño rebelde que tiene como referente a Neymar|date=23 November 2020|website=AS.com}}</ref>

Ezzalzouli continued to play for neighbourhood clubs in Elche until [[Hércules B]] coach Antonio Moreno Domínguez offered him a contract, and Ezzalzouli subsequently joined the main club in the neighbouring city of [[Alicante]]. He moved to [[Hércules CF|Hércules]]' reserves in 2016, where he began his senior career in 2019.

=== Barcelona ===
Ezzalzouli transferred to [[FC Barcelona B|Barcelona B]] on 31 August 2021.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.mundodeportivo.com/futbol/fc-barcelona/20211030/1001706790/abde-debuta-mano-sergi.html|title=Abde debuta de la mano de Sergi|date=30 October 2021|website=Mundo Deportivo}}</ref> He made his professional debut with [[FC Barcelona|Barcelona]] in a 1–1 [[La Liga]] draw with [[Deportivo Alavés|Alavés]] on 30 October 2021, coming on as a substitute in the 80th minute. Ezzalzouli thus became the first Moroccan-born player to feature for Barça's first team.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2021/10/30/spain/primera-division/futbol-club-barcelona/deportivo-alaves/3530336/|title=Barcelona vs. Alaves – 30 October 2021|publisher=Soccerway}}</ref> He scored his first goal for Barcelona against [[CA Osasuna|Osasuna]] in a 2–2 draw.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Nico and Ez Abde score first goals for FC Barcelona |url=https://www.fcbarcelona.com/en/football/first-team/news/2413084/nico-and-ez-abde-score-first-goals-for-fc-barcelona |access-date=15 March 2022 |website=www.fcbarcelona.com |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |date=13 December 2021 |title=Moroccan-born EZ Abde scores first goal for Barcelona |url=https://www.maghrebmagazine.com/moroccan-born-ez-abde-scores-first-goal-for-barcelona/901/ |access-date=15 March 2022 |website=Maghreb Magazine}}</ref>

In September 2022, he renewed his contract with Barcelona until 2026 and was ensuingly loaned to Osasuna.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.mundodeportivo.com/futbol/fc-barcelona/20220901/1001860569/abde-renovado-cedido-osasuna.html|website=[[Mundo Deportivo]]|date=1 September 2022|first=Jordi|last=Batalla|title=Abde, renovado y cedido a Osasuna}}</ref> On 4 September, he made his first appearance for Osasuna against [[Rayo Vallecano]] after being subbed in the 81st minute, he managed to assist the winning goal in that game, which ended in a 2–1 victory.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Sadhan |first=Srinivas |date=4 September 2022 |title=Video: Barcelona youngster grabs stunning assist on debut for loan club |url=https://barcauniversal.com/video-barcelona-youngster-grabs-stunning-assist-on-debut-for-loan-club/ |access-date=7 September 2022 |website=Barca Universal |language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |title=Debut dreamed: Abde took and gives him the triumph to the Osasuna |url=http://www.fcbarcelonanoticias.com/en/players/famous-debut-abde-attends-da-triunfo-osasuna_283980_102.html |access-date=7 September 2022 |website=FCBN |date=4 September 2022 |language=es-ES}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |title=WATCH: Ez Abde conjures up brilliant assist on Osasuna debut |url=https://www.barcablaugranes.com/2022/9/4/23336709/watch-ez-abde-conjures-up-brilliant-assist-on-osasuna-debut |access-date=7 September 2022 |website=www.barcablaugranes.com |date=4 September 2022 |language=en}}</ref>

== International career ==
