'''Piero Martín Hincapié Reyna''' (born 9 January 2002) is an Ecuadorian professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[centre-back]] for [[Bundesliga]] club [[Bayer 04 Leverkusen|Bayer Leverkusen]] and the [[Ecuador national football team|Ecuador national team]].<ref name="SW1">{{cite news|title=Ecuador - P. Hincapié|url=https://int.soccerway.com/players/piero-martin-hincapie-reyna/600454/|accessdate=31 October 2020|work=Soccerway}}</ref>

==Club career==
Hincapié began playing football at the age of seven, initially for local clubs Escuela Refinería, Emelec and Barcelona.<ref name="LH1">{{cite news|title=El crecimiento futbolístico de quien sueña jugar en Europa|url=https://www.lahora.com.ec/esmeraldas/noticia/1102179972/el-crecimiento-futbolistico-de-quien-suena-jugar-en-europa|accessdate=31 October 2020|work=La Hora|date=21 August 2018}}</ref><ref name="MAR1">{{cite news|title=Piero Hincapié, el admirador de Puyol que asombra en Ecuador|url=https://www.marca.com/futbol/america/2019/12/11/5df1123b268e3ef80b8b4632.html|accessdate=31 October 2020|work=Marca|date=11 December 2019}}</ref> Aged ten, the defender moved to [[Guayaquil]] with [[C.S. Norte América|Norte América]].<ref name="MAR1"/> After a spell with [[Deportivo Azogues]], he joined [[C.S.D. Independiente del Valle|Independiente del Valle]] in November 2016.<ref name="EU1">{{cite news|title=Piero Hincapié, de Independiente del Valle al primer equipo del Talleres de Córdoba argentino|url=https://www.eluniverso.com/deportes/2020/08/21/nota/7950292/ecuatoriano-piero-hincapie-fichaje-talleres-cordoba-argentina|accessdate=31 October 2020|work=El Universo|date=21 August 2020}}</ref> Hincapié was promoted into their first-team in August 2019 for a [[Ecuadorian Serie A|Serie A]] match with [[Mushuc Runa S.C.|Mushuc Runa]].<ref name="SW1"/> He was selected to start and played the full duration of a narrow home defeat.<ref name="SW1"/> In the subsequent [[2020 Ecuadorian Serie A|2020]] campaign, shortly after winning the [[2020 U-20 Copa Libertadores|U-20 Copa Libertadores]], Hincapié made appearances off the bench against [[C.D. Universidad Católica del Ecuador|Universidad Católica]] and [[C.S.D. Macará|Macará]].<ref name="SW1"/>

On 20 August 2020, Hincapié switched Ecuador for Argentina after agreeing a five-year contract with [[Argentine Primera División|Primera División]] side [[Talleres de Córdoba|Talleres]]; who paid [[$]]1,000,000 for 50% of his pass.<ref name="TAL1">{{cite news|title=Piero Hincapié es nuevo refuerzo de Talleres|url=https://www.clubtalleres.com.ar/piero-hincapie-es-nuevo-refuerzo-de-talleres/|accessdate=31 October 2020|work=Talleres|date=20 August 2020}}</ref><ref name="MD1">{{cite news|title=Talleres presentó por Zoom a Piero Hincapié: "Estoy ansioso por jugar"|url=https://mundod.lavoz.com.ar/futbol/talleres-presento-por-zoom-a-piero-hincapie-estoy-ansioso-por-jugar|accessdate=31 October 2020|work=Mundo D|date=1 October 2020}}</ref>

In August 2021, Hincapié signed for the German [[Bundesliga]] club [[Bayer 04 Leverkusen|Bayer Leverkusen]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Bayer Leverkusen sign Ecuador teenager Piero Hincapie|url=https://www.bundesliga.com/en/bundesliga/news/bayer-leverkusen-sign-ecuador-teenager-piero-hincapie-talleres-16927|access-date=8 October 2021|website=bundesliga.com - the official Bundesliga website|language=en}}</ref> He made his debut on 16 August, coming into the [[UEFA Europa League|Europa League]] group stage match against [[Ferencvárosi TC|Ferencváros]] as Leverkusen won 2–1.<ref>{{Cite web|last=UEFA.com|title=Leverkusen-Ferencváros {{!}} UEFA Europa League|url=https://www.uefa.com/uefaeuropaleague/match/2033182--leverkusen-vs-ferencvaros/|access-date=8 October 2021|website=UEFA.com|language=en}}</ref> He scored his first goal for the club on 30 September, scoring the opening goal against [[Celtic F.C.|Celtic]] in a 4–0 victory in the Europa League.<ref>{{Cite news|title=Ruthless Leverkusen punish sloppy Celtic|language=en-GB|work=BBC Sport|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/58654135|access-date=8 October 2021}}</ref>

==International career==
Hincapié appeared for the Ecuador U15s in 2017.<ref name="LH1"/> In 2019, Hincapié represented his country as captain at [[Ecuador national under-17 football team|U17]] level.<ref name="SW1"/><ref name="MAR1"/> He played seven times at the [[2019 South American U-17 Championship|South American U-17 Championship]] in Peru, before featuring four times at the subsequent [[2019 FIFA U-17 World Cup|FIFA U-17 World Cup]] in Brazil.<ref name="SW1"/><ref name="MAR1"/>

He made his debut for [[Ecuador national football team|Ecuador senior national team]] on 13 June 2021 in a [[2021 Copa América]] game against Colombia. He started and played a full match as Ecuador lost 1–0.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[ESPN]]|url=https://www.espn.com/soccer/lineups?gameId=560524|title=Colombia v Ecuador game report|date=13 June 2021}}</ref>
