'''Youssouf Fofana''' (born 10 January 1999) is a French professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[midfielder]] for [[Ligue 1]] club [[AS Monaco FC|Monaco]] and the [[France national football team|France national team]].

==Early life==
Youssouf Fofana<ref>{{cite web |url=https://fdp.fifa.org/assetspublic/ce44/pdf/SquadLists-English.pdf |title=Squad List: FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022: France (FRA) |publisher=FIFA |page=12 |date=18 December 2022 |access-date=10 May 2023}}</ref> was born on 10 January 1999 in [[Paris]].<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.lequipe.fr/Football/FootballFicheJoueur69798.html |title=Youssouf Fofana |newspaper=L'Équipe |location=Paris |access-date=7 November 2022 |language=fr}}</ref> He is of Malian descent.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://reference14sport.blogspot.com/2017/09/joueurs-maliens-ou-dorigines-maliennes.html |title=Joueurs maliens ou d'origines maliennes à travers le monde du 08/09/2017}}</ref>

==Club career==
Fofana began his career with various youth academies in Paris, before joining the [[RC Strasbourg Alsace Academy|youth academy]] of [[RC Strasbourg Alsace|Strasbourg]] on 21 February 2017.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.ja-drancy.com/index.php/youssouf-fofana-sengage-en-faveur-de-strasbourg/ |title=Youssouf FOFANA s'engage en faveur de STRASBOURG – JA Drancy |date=21 February 2017}}</ref> He made his professional debut with Strasbourg in a 2–0 [[Ligue 1]] loss to [[Olympique Lyonnais|Lyon]] on 24 August 2018.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://ca.soccerway.com/matches/2018/08/24/france/ligue-1/olympique-lyonnais/racing-club-de-strasbourg/2792239/ |title=Olympique Lyonnais vs. Strasbourg – 24 August 2018 |website=Soccerway |publisher=Perform Group}}</ref>

Fofana played his final game for Strasbourg on 25 January 2020, a 3–1 away win over [[AS Monaco FC|Monaco]].<ref>{{Cite web |last=Rainbot |first=Arthur |date=25 January 2020 |title=Monaco - RC Strasbourg: Le RC Strasbourg bat Monaco 1-3 à l'extérieur |url=https://m.20minutes.fr/amp/a/2703439 |access-date=30 December 2022 |website=[[20 Minutes (France)|20 Minutes]] |language=Fr}}</ref> Four days later, he was signed by Monaco for €15&nbsp;million,<ref>{{Cite web |last=Chirat |first=Antoine |date=29 January 2020 |title=AS Monaco : Youssouf Fofana signe à son tour ! |url=https://www.onzemondial.com/ligue-1/2019-2020/as-monaco-youssouf-fofana-signe-a-son-tour-officiel-208670?view=amp |access-date=30 December 2022 |website=Onze Mondial |language=Fr}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last=Borne |first=Fabien |date=30 January 2020 |title=Officiel : Youssouf Fofana signe lui aussi à Monaco |url=https://www.dailymercato.com/amp/news/officiel-youssouf-fofana-signe-lui-aussi-a-monaco |access-date=30 December 2022 |website=Dailymercato |language=Fr}}</ref> and made his debut on 1 February, playing 71 minutes of a 3–1 away defeat to [[Nîmes Olympique|Nîmes]].<ref>{{Cite web |date=1 February 2020 |title=NÎMES / AS MONACO - (3 / 1) - J22 - 2019-2020 |url=https://www.ligue1.fr/feuille-match |access-date=30 December 2022 |website= |publisher=[[Ligue 1]] |language=Fr}}</ref> On 25 November 2021, Fofana scored his first goal for Monaco, the winning goal in a 2–1 [[UEFA Europa League]] victory at home over [[Real Sociedad]].<ref>{{Cite web |date=25 November 2021 |title=Monaco vs. Real Sociedad |url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2021/11/25/europe/uefa-cup/association-sportive-monaco-fc/real-sociedad-de-futbol/3633591/ |access-date=30 December 2022 |website=Soccerway}}</ref> He scored his first league goal with the club in a 4–2 home defeat to [[ES Troyes AC|Troyes]] on 31 August 2022.<ref>{{Cite web |date=September 2022 |title=VIDÉO : Le premier but de Youssouf Fofana en Ligue 1 |url=https://fr.besoccer.com/info/amp/le-premier-but-de-youssouf-fofana-en-ligue-1-1178341 |access-date=30 December 2022 |website=BeSoccer |language=Fr}}</ref>

==International career==
Fofana was a youth international for France from [[France national under-19 football team|under-19]] to [[France national under-21 football team|under-21]] level.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.ja-drancy.com/index.php/youssouf-fofana-convoque-en-equipe-de-france-u19-2018/ |title=Youssouf FOFANA convoqué en équipe de FRANCE U19 – JA Drancy |date=15 February 2018 |access-date=25 August 2018 |archive-date=26 May 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190526232515/http://www.ja-drancy.com/index.php/youssouf-fofana-convoque-en-equipe-de-france-u19-2018/ |url-status=dead }}</ref>

On 15 September 2022, Fofana received his first call-up to the [[France national football team|France national team]] for two [[UEFA Nations League]] matches against [[Austria national football team|Austria]] and [[Denmark national football team|Denmark]].<ref>{{Cite web |date=15 September 2022 |title=France Nations League squad: Badiashile, Fofana and Kolo Muani receive first-ever call-up, Dembele and Giroud return |url=https://footballtoday.com/2022/09/15/france-nations-league-squad-badiashile-fofana-and-kolo-muani-receive-first-ever-call-up-dembele-and-giroud-return/ |access-date=15 September 2022 |website=Football Today |language=en-GB}}</ref> In November, he was called up for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] in Qatar by [[Didier Deschamps]].<ref>{{Cite web |date=11 November 2022 |title=Youssouf Fofana sur sa sélection pour la Coupe du monde : « On ne peut pas faire plus grand » |url=https://www.lequipe.fr/Football/Actualites/Youssouf-fofana-sur-sa-selection-pour-la-coupe-du-monde-on-ne-peut-pas-faire-plus-grand/1364342 |access-date=11 December 2022 |website=[[L'Équipe]] |language=Fr}}</ref> At the tournament, he played in six of France's seven matches, starting the ones against [[Tunisia national football team|Tunisia]] in the group stage (1–0 loss) and [[Morocco national football team|Morocco]] in the semi-finals (2–0 win).<ref>{{Cite web |date=14 December 2022 |title=France-Maroc : Fofana et Konaté titulaires à la place de Rabiot et Upamecano, malades |url=https://www.leparisien.fr/amp/sports/football/coupe-du-monde/france-maroc-rabiot-et-upamecano-sur-le-banc-fofana-et-konate-titulaires-14-12-2022-MF33TVASCVB2BO7OGCDDC35AEI.php |access-date=30 December 2022 |website=[[Le Parisien]] |language=Fr}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |date=14 December 2022 |title=CdM : France 2-0 Maroc (fini) |url=https://amp.maxifoot.fr/cdm/france-2-0-maroc-fini-foot-381379.htm |access-date=30 December 2022 |website=Maxifoot.fr |language=Fr}}</ref> In [[2022 FIFA World Cup final|the final]] against [[Argentina national football team|Argentina]] on 18 December, Fofana came on as a 96th-minute substitute in extra time for [[Adrien Rabiot]]. The game ended with a score of 3–3, and Argentina came out victorious on [[Penalty shoot-out (association football)|penalties]].<ref>{{Cite web |date=18 December 2022 |title=ARGENTINE - FRANCE COMPOSITIONS |url=https://www.eurosport.fr/football/coupe-du-monde/2022/live-argentine-france_mtc1287454/live-lineup.shtml |access-date=30 December 2022 |website=[[Eurosport]] |language=Fr}}</ref>
