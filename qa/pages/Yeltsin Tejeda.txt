'''Yeltsin Ignacio Tejeda Valverde''' ({{IPA-es|ˈʝeltsin teˈxeða}}; born 17 March 1992) is a Costa Rican professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Midfielder#Defensive midfielder|defensive midfielder]] for [[Liga FPD]] club [[C.S. Herediano|Herediano]], which he [[Captain (association football)|captains]], and the [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica national team]].

He is named after the first [[President of Russia]] [[Boris Yeltsin]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.kp.ru/daily/26238.7/3120109/|script-title=ru:На чемпионат мира по футболу в Бразилию поедет Ельцин |last1=Nesyn |first1=Evgeny|date=1 June 2014|website=KP.RU|publisher=Komsomolskaya Pravda|access-date=6 July 2014|language=ru}}</ref>

==Club career==
===Saprissa===
Tejeda began his career with [[Deportivo Saprissa|Saprissa]] in 2011 scoring one goal against [[A.D. San Carlos|San Carlos]]. In the 2014 Verano season, Tejeda was named the captain of the team.

===Evian===
After Costa Rica's campaign at the [[2014 FIFA World Cup]], Tejeda traveled to the United Kingdom to sign with [[Swansea City A.F.C.|Swansea City]], but the club halted the negotiations, and Tejeda instead traveled to France.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Monge |first1=Alejandro |title=Yeltsin Tejeda relata su fichaje fallido con club de la Premier League |url=https://www.espn.co.cr/futbol/costa-rica/nota/_/id/8285966/yeltsin-tejeda-fichaje-fallido-swansea-gales |access-date=24 July 2022 |work=ESPN.com.mx |date=6 March 2021 |language=es}}</ref> On 22 August 2014, then-Ligue 1 club [[Thonon Evian Grand Genève FC|Evian]] announced that Tejeda had signed a four-year contract.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.etgfc.com/2014/08/22/tejeda-sengage-pour-4-ans/|title=Tejeda sengage pour 4 ans|publisher=Evian Thonon Gaillard|access-date=22 August 2014}}</ref> He scored his first goal for the club on 19 September 2014 in a 2–1 loss to [[FC Girondins de Bordeaux|Bordeaux]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.espnfc.us/french-ligue-1/match/393654/bordeaux-evian-thonon-gaillard/report|title=Bordeaux Saprissa teammate [[David Ramírez (footballer, born 1993)|David Ramírez]].<ref>{{cite news |last1=Vargas |first1=Dinia |title=Ramírez jugará en el Evian of the season.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Masís |first1=Fiorella |title=Evian de Yeltsin Tejeda y David Ramírez firmó su descenso en Francia ===Lausanne-Sport===
In July 2016 Tejeda joined Swiss club [[FC Lausanne-Sport|Lausanne-Sport]] on a three year contract.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.lausanne-sport.ch/yeltsin-tejeda-sengage-lausanne-3-saisons/|title=YELTSIN TEJEDA S'ENGAGE AVEC LAUSANNE POUR 3 SAISONS|date=July 16, 2022}}|</ref>

===Herediano===
In January 2019 Tejeda returned to Costa Rica, signing with [[C.S. Herediano|Herediano]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fobeUxSXBAE|title=Yeltsin Tejeda jugará para Herediano|website=[[YouTube]] |date=January 24, 2019}}</ref> He signed a two-year contract extension with the club in October 2021.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sensaciondeportiva.com/yeltsin-tejeda-renovo-con-herediano-hasta-2023/|date=October 8, 2021|title=Yeltsin Tejeda renovó con Herediano hasta 2023.|first=Cristian|last=Jimenez}}</ref>

==International career==
Tejeda was selected in Costa Rica's squad for the [[2009 CONCACAF U-17 Championship]] and he was named in the [[2011 CONCACAF U-20 Championship]]. He played for his country at the [[2009 FIFA U-17 World Cup|2009 FIFA U-17]] and [[2011 FIFA U-20 World Cup]]s.<ref name=FIFA/>

He officially debuted in the senior [[Costa Rica national football team]] on 11 December 2011 in a friendly match against [[Cuba national football team|Cuba]], in [[Havana]] and has, as of November 2014, earned a total of 30 caps, scoring no goals. Tejeda was a key part during Costa Rica's successful qualification to the [[2014 FIFA World Cup]] and during the finals tournament.<ref name=FIFA2>{{FIFA player|307541}}</ref> He also played at the [[2013 Copa Centroamericana]] and [[2013 CONCACAF Gold Cup]].

In May 2018 he was named in Costa Rica's [[Costa Rica national football team|23 man squad]] for the [[2018 FIFA World Cup]] in Russia.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.goal.com/en/news/costa-rica-names-23-man-world-cup-squad-featuring-keylor/1bw2iz8pkma5614bzushqyyo7g|title=Costa Rica national team World Cup squad: Oscar Ramirez names 23-man roster featuring Keylor Navas, six MLS players|website=Goal.com|access-date=2 December 2021}}</ref>

In November 2022, Tejeda was named to Costa Rica's 26-man squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fifa.com/fifaplus/en/articles/costa-rica-squad-list-fifa-world-cup-qatar-2022|title=Suarez names Costa Rica squad for Qatar 2022}}</ref> On December 1 in Costa Rica's final group stage game, he scored his first goal for Costa Rica against [[Germany national football team|Germany]] in a 4-2 defeat.<ref>{{Cite news |title=Germany beat Costa Rica but crash out of World Cup |language=en-GB |work=BBC Sport |url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/63739248 |access-date=2022-12-01}}</ref>
