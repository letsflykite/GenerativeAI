'''Samuel Piette''' (born 12 November 1994) is a Canadian professional [[association football|soccer]] player who plays as a [[defensive midfielder]] for [[Major League Soccer]] club [[CF Montréal]] and the [[Canada men's national soccer team|Canada national team]].

==Club career==

===Early career===
Piette began playing soccer at age four with local club Lionceaux de Le Gardeur<ref name="CSA" /> and later joined Olympique de Repentigny and [[FC Boisbriand]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://canadasoccer.com/news/canada-soccer-announce-squad-for-fifa-world-cup-qatar-2022/|title=Canada Soccer announces squad for FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022|date=November 12, 2022|work=[[Canadian Soccer Association]]}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nordinfo.com/sports/samuel-piette-a-deja-porte-les-couleurs-du-fc-boisbriand/|title=Samuel Piette a déjà porté les couleurs du FC Boisbriand|language=fr|trans-title=Samuel Piette has already worn the colors of FC Boisbriand|first=Christian|last=Asselin|date=20 August 2017|work=Nord Info}}</ref> In 2009, he moved to Europe and joined the youth team of [[FC Metz|Metz]] but after French football rules, which state that a player must be 18 to sign a contract with a club, Piette joined [[Bundesliga]] youth side [[Fortuna Düsseldorf]] on 26 June 2012.<ref>{{cite web|last=Day|first=Gavin|title=Canadian Exports: Teen Piette inks at Fortuna Dusseldorf|url=http://www.mlssoccer.com/news/article/2012/06/26/canadian-exports-teen-piette-inks-fortuna-dusseldorf|publisher=MLS Soccer|access-date=7 September 2012}}</ref>

===Fortuna Düsseldorf===
After continuing to play for [[Fortuna Düsseldorf|Fortuna Düsseldorf II]] Piette made his senior debut as a second-half substitute for [[Erwin Hoffer]] on 4 April 2014 in a 2–1 victory over [[SC Paderborn 07|SC Paderborn]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2014/04/04/germany/2-bundesliga/sc-paderborn-07-ev/dusseldorfer-tus-fortuna-1895-ev/1486972/|title=Paderborn vs. Fortuna Düsseldorf – 4 April 2014|website=soccerway.com|publisher=Soccerway|date=4 April 2014|access-date=23 March 2020}}</ref>

===Deportivo La Coruña===
On 25 August 2014, Piette signed a one-year deal with [[Deportivo de La Coruña]], being assigned to [[Deportivo de La Coruña|the reserves]] in [[Tercera División]].<ref>[http://canaldeportivo.com/servlet/es.iris.servlets.Noticias?accion=4&ver=1&nid=28626&mid=8 Samuel Piette refuerza el mediocampo del Fabril (Samuel Piette bolsters midfield of ''Fabril'')]; Deportivo's official website, 25 August 2014 {{in lang|es}}.</ref> After overcoming fitness issues, Piette made his league debut against [[Bertamiráns FC|Bertamiráns]] on 30 November.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.canaldeportivo.com/noticia/29222|title=Victoria demasiado corta en Bertamiráns de un Fabril lanzado a los puestos de ascenso|date=30 November 2014}}</ref>

==== Loan to Racing de Ferrol ====
Piette was loaned to [[Racing de Ferrol]] for the 2015–16 season on 7 August 2015.<ref>{{cite web|title=Samuel Piette nueva incorporación del equipo|url=http://www.racingclubferrol.net/noticias/ver/1848|access-date=7 August 2015}}</ref>

===Izarra===
After terminating his contract with Deportivo on 15 July 2016,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.marca.com/futbol/deportivo/2016/07/15/5788ce1b268e3ede6b8b460a.html|title=Sam Piette rescinde su contrato con el Deportivo|trans-title=Sam Piette rescinded his contract with Deportivo|publisher=[[Marca (newspaper)|Marca]]|language=es|date=15 July 2016|access-date=2 October 2016}}</ref> Piette signed with [[Segunda División B]] club [[CD Izarra]] the following day.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://navarra.elespanol.com/articulo/futbol/izarra-ficha-internacional-canadiense-samuel-piette/20160716124031057936.html|title=El Izarra ficha al internacional canadiense Samuel Piette|trans-title=Izarra sign the Canadian international Samuel Piette|publisher=Navarra.com|language=es|date=16 July 2016|access-date=2 October 2016}}</ref>

==International career==
===Youth teams===
Piette played for the [[Canada men's national youth soccer teams|Canada U17 national team]], making his debut in the qualifiers for the [[2011 FIFA U-17 World Cup]]. His team would go on to qualify for the tournament. Piette was also a part of the [[Canada men's national under-23 soccer team|Canada U23 side]], that attempted to qualify for the [[Football at the 2012 Summer Olympics|2012 Olympics Football tournament]], earning three caps. The team did not qualify for [[2012 Summer Olympics|London 2012]].

===Senior team===
Piette was called up to the [[Canada men's national soccer team|Canada senior team]] for the first time for a friendly against [[Armenia national football team|Armenia]] in mid February but failed to see the pitch.<ref>{{Cite news|last=Massey|first=Benjamin|title=Canada Names Roster for Armenia Friendly: Young, Old, Mediocre and Short-Handed|url=http://www.eightysixforever.com/2012/2/23/2820331/canada-names-roster-for-armenia-friendly-young-old-mediocre-and-short|newspaper=Eighty Six Forever|location=Toronto|date=23 February 2012|access-date=6 June 2012}}</ref> He made his senior international debut on 3 June 2012, coming on as an 85th-minute substitute for [[Julian De Guzman]] in a 0–0 draw with the [[United States men's national soccer team|United States]] at [[BMO Field]].<ref>{{Cite news|last=Blair|first=Jeff|title=Canada, U.S. tie in soccer friendly|url=https://www.theglobeandmail.com/sports/soccer/canada-us-tie-in-soccer-friendly/article4227954/|newspaper=The Globe and Mail|location=Toronto|date=3 June 2012|access-date=6 June 2012}}</ref> This [[Cap (sport)|cap]] made Piette the third youngest player to ever suit up for the Canadian senior side. On 27 June 2013, he was listed as a part of the confirmed 23-man squad for [[Colin Miller (soccer, born 1964)|Colin Miller]]'s Canada squad for [[2013 CONCACAF Gold Cup]].<ref>{{Cite news|title=Canada Finalize roster for Gold Cup, announces friendly| agency =Canada Soccer|url=http://www.canadasoccer.com/canada-finalizes-roster-for-gold-cup-announces-friendly--p154281|access-date=29 June 2013}}</ref>

Piette was called up for the [[2015 CONCACAF Gold Cup]] by [[Benito Floro]] in June 2015, as well as the [[2017 CONCACAF Gold Cup]] by [[Octavio Zambrano]]. He earned rave reviews for his performance in the 2017 CONCACAF Gold Cup, particularly during group stage matches against [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica]] and [[Honduras national football team|Honduras]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.espnfc.com/concacaf-gold-cup/59/blog/post/3159409/canadian-teenage-sensation-alphonso-davies-leads-gold-cup-best-xi|website=ESPN|title=Canadian teenage sensation Davies leads Gold Cup group stage Best XI|date=17 July 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mlssoccer.com/post/2017/07/11/canada-player-ratings-milan-borjan-stands-out-draw-costa-rica|website=MLS|title=Canada Player Ratings: Milan Borjan stands out in draw with Costa Rica|author=Daniel Squizzato|date=11 July 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://ici.radio-canada.ca/nouvelle/1044984/samuel-piette-content-contribution-concacaf-costa-rica-soccer|website=CBC (French)|title=Samuel Piette fier de sa contribution contre le Costa Rica|date=12 July 2017}}</ref>

On 30 May 2019, Piette was named to the Canadian squad for the [[2019 CONCACAF Gold Cup]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.canadasoccer.com/together-we-rise-canada-soccer-announces-squad-for-the-2019-concacaf-gold-cup-p162198|date=30 May 2019|title=Together We Rise: Canada Soccer announces squad for the 2019 Concacaf Gold Cup}}</ref> On 1 July 2021, he was called up to Canada for the [[2021 CONCACAF Gold Cup]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.canadasoccer.com/news/canada-soccer-announces-2021-concacaf-gold-cup-roster/|title=CANADA SOCCER ANNOUNCES 2021 CONCACAF GOLD CUP ROSTER|date=1 July 2021}}</ref>

In November 2022, Piette was named to Canada's 26-man squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://canpl.ca/article/canada-announces-26-man-squad-for-2022-fifa-world-cup-in-qatar|date=November 13, 2022|title=Canada announces 26-man squad for 2022 FIFA World Cup in Qatar|work=[[Canadian Premier League]]|first=Charlie|last=O'Connor-Clarke}}</ref>
