'''Ismail Jakobs''' (born 17 August 1999) is a professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[left back]] or [[left winger]] for [[Ligue 1]] club [[AS Monaco FC|Monaco]].<ref name="Fix">{{Cite web|url=https://www.kicker.de/fix-koelns-jakobs-wechselt-zur-as-monaco-865308/artikel|title=Fix: Kölns Jakobs wechselt zur AS Monaco|access-date=12 July 2021|website=kicker.de|language=de}}</ref> Born in Germany, he represents the [[Senegal football team|Senegal national team]].

==Club career==
===1.FC Köln===
After starting his career at BC Bliesheim, Jakobs joined the youth academy of [[1.FC Köln]] in 2012. In 2017, having progressed through all of the club's youth sides, he was introduced into Köln's [[1. FC Köln II|U21 team]] competing in the fourth-tier [[Regionalliga West]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.kicker.de/ismail-jakobs/spieler|title=Ismail Jakobs|access-date=11 July 2021|website=kicker.de}}</ref>

Ahead of the [[2019–20 Bundesliga|2019–20 season]] Jakobs was promoted to the club's first team by then manager [[Achim Beierlorzer]]. On 8 November 2019, he made his debut appearance in a match against [[TSG Hoffenheim]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.rundschau-online.de/sport/1-fc-koeln/nach-remis-gegen-augsburg-die-spieler-des-1--fc-koeln-in-der-einzelkritik-33547744|website=Kölnische Rundschau|title=Nach Remis gegen Augsburg. Die Spieler des 1. FC Köln in der Einzelkritik|date=30 November 2019 |access-date= 13 December 2019}}</ref> On 18 December 2019, Jakobs scored his first goal as a professional in a 2–4 victory against [[Eintracht Frankfurt]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.kicker.de/4588797/spielbericht/eintracht-frankfurt-32/1-fc-koeln-16|title=Aus 0:2 mach 4:2&nbsp;– Kölner Wahnsinn in Frankfurt|website=kicker.de|access-date=18 December 2019}}</ref> In March 2020, the club announced that Jakobs' contract had been extended until 2022.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.kicker.de/4000000033032/transfermeldung/jakobs___automatische__vertragsverlängerung_in_köln_|title=Jakobs: "Automatische" Vertragsverlängerung in Köln|website=kicker.de|access-date=4 March 2020}}</ref> In October of the same year, he signed a further extension keeping him at Köln until July 2024.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.kicker.de/jakobs-verlaengert-der-fc-ist-mein-verein-786769/artikel|title=Jakobs verlängert: "Der FC ist mein Verein"|access-date=9 October 2020|website=kicker.de|language=de}}</ref>

===AS Monaco===
On 12 July 2021, Jakobs was transferred to [[Ligue 1]] side [[AS Monaco]], where he signed a contract until 2026.<ref name="Fix"/> He made his debut for the club in a 2–0 win over [[AC Sparta Prague|Sparta Prague]] in the third qualifying round of the [[2021–22 UEFA Champions League|UEFA Champions League]] on 3 August.

==International career==
On 21 August 2020, Jakobs was nominated for [[Germany national under-21 football team|Germany's under-21 national team]] by manager [[Stefan Kuntz]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.kicker.de/782489/artikel/wirtz_maina_co_kuntz_beruft_sieben_neue_in_die_u_21?fbclid=IwAR3ZGfxWeVjlL91z2YZCDwxcSRhvZC7IUCTIKNQoCkMWNiXsKlPW9uD-pbI#fb|title=Wirtz, Maina & Co.: Kuntz beruft sieben Neue in die U21|website=kicker.de|access-date=21 August 2020}}</ref>
