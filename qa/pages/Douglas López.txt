'''Douglas Andrey López Araya''' (born 21 September 1998) is a Costa Rican professional [[association football|footballer]] for [[C.S. Herediano]] in [[Liga FPD]], the Costa Rican top division, and the [[Costa Rica national football team]].

==Career==
López made his debut for [[Santos de Guapiles]]on 22 July 2018 against [[Deportivo Saprissa]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://everardoherrera.com/index.php/home/74-futbol-costa-rica-1/44419-saprissa-inicia-la-defensa-de-su-titulo-con-ajustado-triunfo-2-1-ante-santos|title=Saprissa inicia la defensa de su título con ajustado triunfo 2-1 ante Santos|website=everadoherrera.com}}</ref> On 5 September 2018 he scored his first goal as a professional, against [[A.D. Municipal Pérez Zeledón|Municipal de Pérez Zeledón]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.lateja.cr/deportes/video-douglas-lopez-se-visito-de-crack-para/IFOMDMEC7ZD5RDYU36XKYO6J4E/story/|title=Douglas López was visited as a crack to give Santos the victory against Pérez|website=lateja.cr}}</ref> In October 2021 it was revealed he had agreed to join [[C.S. Herediano]] at the end of his contract.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://everardoherrera.com/index.php/futbol-costa-rica-4/81221-herediano-llego-a-un-acuerdo-para-fichar-al-santista-douglas-lopez|title=Herediano reached an agreement to sign the saintly Douglas López|website=everadoherrera.com}}</ref> In June 2022 López officially joined C.S. Herediano from Santos de Guapiles, signing a contract until 2025.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.teletica.com/amp/club-sport-herediano/douglas-lopez-quiero-ser-campeon-con-el-herediano_314223|title=Douglas López: "Quiero ser campeón con el Herediano"|website=Teletica.com|date=25 June 2022 }}</ref>

==International career==
López made his debut as a full Costa Rican international on 30 March 2022 in the final qualifying match for the World Cup against the United States, playing the last twenty-two minutes of the 2-0 victory.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.fedefutbol.com/2022/03/30/la-sele-cierra-la-octogonal-con-paso-de-gigante/|title= La Sele closes the octagonal with a giant step|website=fedefutbol.com}}</ref> López was named in the [[Costa Rica national football team]] squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.lateja.cr/deportes/video-imposible-no-llorar-al-ver-la-reaccion-de-la/GLT42BBKL5FDDPE7RUWJ5VZX2A/story/%3FoutputType%3Damp-type|title= Imposible no llorar al ver la reacción de la familia de Douglas López al escuchar convocatoria|website=lateja.cr}}</ref>
