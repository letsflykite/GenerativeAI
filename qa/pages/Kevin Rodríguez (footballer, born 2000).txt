'''Kevin José Rodríguez Cortez''' (born 4 March 2000) is an Ecuadoran [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Forward (association football)|forward]] for [[C.S.D. Independiente del Valle|Independiente del Valle]].<ref>{{Soccerway}}</ref>

==Club career==
===Imbabura===
Born in [[Ibarra, Ecuador|Ibarra]], Rodríguez joined hometown side [[Imbabura S.C.|Imbabura]] at the age of 13. He progressed through the youth setup until making his first team debut in 2017, featuring in two matches as his club was relegated from the [[Ecuadorian Serie B|Serie B]].

Rodríguez started to feature more regularly in 2018, scoring seven times in the national stage of the [[Ecuadorian Segunda Categoria|Segunda Categoria]]. After scoring 11 goals overall during the 2019 season, he only scored once in five matches in 2020. In 2021, he only scored twice as his side returned to the second level.

Rodríguez became a regular starter for Imbabura in the [[2022 Ecuadorian Serie B|2022 Serie B]], scoring ten times as his side finished fifth.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://elnorte.ec/kevin-rodriguez-el-maximo-arponero-gardenio/|title=Kevin Rodríguez, el máximo arponero gardenio|trans-title=Kevin Rodríguez, the top scorer ''gardenio''|publisher=Diario El Norte|language=es|date=16 June 2022|accessdate=3 December 2022}}</ref> In the [[2022 Copa Ecuador]], he notably scored the winner to knock out [[L.D.U. Quito|LDU Quito]] in the round of 16.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://lanacion.com.ec/kevin-rodriguez-luego-de-eliminar-a-ldu-estoy-concentrado-en-que-quiero-comerme-al-mundo/|title=Kevin Rodríguez luego de eliminar a LDU: "Estoy concentrado en que quiero comerme al mundo"|trans-title=Kevin Rodríguez right after knocking out LDU: "I am focused on taking on the world"|publisher=La Nación|language=es|date=22 July 2022|accessdate=3 December 2022}}</ref>

==International career==
Rodríguez was called up to the [[Ecuador national football team|Ecuador national team]] for the first time in November 2022, as part of a preparation match against [[Iraq national football team|Iraq]] ahead of the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] later that month.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://elnorte.ec/kevin-rodriguez-imbabura-sc-convocado-tricolor/ |title=Kevin Rodríguez, del Imbabura SC, convocado para amistoso con la Tricolor |trans-title=Kevin Rodríguez, from Imbabura SC, called up for a friendly with the Tricolor |work=Diario El Norte |date=5 November 2022 |access-date=14 November 2022 |language=es}}</ref> He made his debut in the match, which finished as a 0–0 draw, coming on as a substitute in the 63rd minute for [[José Cifuentes]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.elcomercio.com/deportes/mundial-catar/kevin-rodriguez-seleccion-ecuador-imbabura.html |title=Kevin Rodríguez, jugador del Imbabura, debutó en la Selección de Ecuador |trans-title=Kevin Rodríguez, Imbabura player, debuted in the Ecuador National Team |work=[[El Comercio (Ecuador)|El Comercio]] |date=12 November 2022 |access-date=14 November 2022 |language=es}}</ref> Two days later, he was included by coach [[Gustavo Alfaro]] in [[2022 FIFA World Cup squads#Ecuador|Ecuador's squad for the World Cup]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.eluniverso.com/deportes/futbol/quien-es-kevin-rodriguez-el-delantero-sorpresa-en-la-nomina-de-ecuador-para-qatar-2022-nota/ |title=¿Quién es Kevin Rodríguez?
