'''Abderrazak Hamdallah''' ({{lang-ar|عبد الرزاق حمد الله}}; born 17 December 1990) is a Moroccan professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Striker (association football)|striker]] for [[Saudi Professional League]] club [[Al-Ittihad Club (Jeddah)|Al-Ittihad]] and the [[Morocco national football team|Morocco national team]]. He is nicknamed '''The Executioner''' for his goal scoring ability.<ref name="kooora.com">{{Cite web |date=9 February 2022 |title=الجلاد حمد الله يطمح لإضافة النصر إلى قائمة ضحاياه |url=https://www.kooora.com/?n=1111945 |access-date=1 March 2022 |website=كووورة}}</ref><ref name="حمد الله.. جلاد في محيط الرعب">{{Cite web |title=حمد الله.. جلاد في محيط الرعب |url=https://arriyadiyah.com/665616 |access-date=1 March 2022 |website=arriyadiyah.com |language=ar}}</ref>

He started his professional career playing for [[Olympic Club de Safi]] in Morocco, later transferring to [[Aalesunds FK|Aalesunds]] in Norway. After one season with them, he joined Chinese club [[Guangzhou F.C.|Guangzhou R&F]]. In 2015, he transferred to [[El Jaish SC|El Jaish]], where he won the [[2016 Qatar Cup]]. Soon later he signed with Qatari club [[Al-Rayyan SC|Al-Rayyan]]. He felt in terms with Al-Rayyan and joined Saudi [[Al Nassr FC|Al Nassr]]. In the season, [[2018–19 Saudi Professional League]] he scored a league record of 34 goals in one season, and was the world's leading goalscorer during the 2019 calendar year with 57 goals overall.<ref>{{Cite web |title=IFFHS |url=https://www.iffhs.com/posts/914 |access-date=27 February 2022 |website=www.iffhs.com}}</ref> He won two [[Saudi Professional League]] and three [[Saudi Super Cup]].

A full international for Morocco since 2012, Hamdallah has represented his country at the [[2013 Africa Cup of Nations]] and [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] tournaments.

==Early life==
Born in [[Safi, Morocco]], Hamdallah was the youngest of seven children of the family. Hamadallah spoke about his childhood, stating:<blockquote>I started playing football in the street with other children of the city where I was born, Safi. As a teenager, I enrolled in a club in town. I was playing almost every day after school and all weekend. My older brother always supported me. He encouraged me to work hard.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://fr.fifa.com/world-match-centre/news/newsid/252/129/6/ |title=Hamdallah sort de l'ombre chinoise |publisher=FIFA Official Website |date=6 February 2015|access-date=5 July 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150705085219/http://fr.fifa.com/world-match-centre/news/newsid/252/129/6/|archive-date=5 July 2015|url-status=dead}}</ref></blockquote>

==Club career==
Hamdallah received his initial football formation at Nejm Shabab Safi club, before moving to the first team, [[Olympic Club de Safi]], where his professional career began since the [[2010–11 Botola]], during which he played his first match in the Moroccan championship. He managed to score his first goal for the team against [[Difaâ Hassani El Jadidi]]. Hamdallah scored twice for [[Olympic Club de Safi]] in his first appearance in the [[Moroccan Throne Cup]] against [[Raja CA]], the match ended in a 3–2 victory. The [[2011–12 Botola]] marked the real start of Hamdallah at the scoring level, as he finished the season by scoring 15 goals, occupying the second row, two goals ahead of the Chadian new striker [[Karl Max Barthélémy]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.goalzz.com/?c=7607&scorers=true |title=Moroccan Pro League 2011/2012: Scorers}} goalzz.com</ref> In the 2012–13 season, Hamdallah scored 15 goals in the first two-thirds of the tournament before joining, during March 2013, to professionally with the Norwegian club Ålesund.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.goalzz.com/?c=8648&scorers=true |title=Moroccan Pro League 2012/2013 |work=goalzz}}</ref> He scored his first hat-trick for the team against [[Wydad de Fès]]. The financial value of Hamdallah's transfer to Olesund Club amounted to one million US dollars.<ref>{{Cite web |last=الشامي |first=طارق |title=غريب حمد الله هداف الدوري المغربي والنرويجي في نفس الموسم |url=https://www.nador24.com/غريب-حمد-الله-هداف-الدوري-المغربي-والنرويجي-في-نفس-الموسم_a10868.html |access-date=27 February 2022 |website=Nador أخبار الناظور المتجدّدة 24 |language=ar}}</ref>

===Aalesund===
On 14 February 2013, it was confirmed that Hamdallah had joined [[Tippeliga]] club [[Aalesunds FK|Aalesund]]. The transfer fee was in the region of €1&nbsp;million or 7.4&nbsp;million [[Norwegian Krone]] and the player signed a three-year contract, his first European contract. The transfer fee was the highest Aalesund has paid for a player ever.<ref>{{cite news |last=Lid |first=Ivar |title="Superspissen" klar for AaFK: – Klubbens dyreste spiller noensinne |url=https://www.nrk.no/sport/fotball/_superspissen_-klar-for-aafk-1.10913711|access-date=14 February 2013 |newspaper=Nrk.no |date=14 February 2013 |language=no}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last=Vågnes |first=Stig |title=Her setter Aalesund rekord på overgangsmarkedet |url=http://fotball.aftenposten.no/eliteserien/article268045.ece |access-date=14 February 2013 |newspaper=100% Fotball |date=14 February 2013 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130217003452/http://fotball.aftenposten.no/eliteserien/article268045.ece |archive-date=17 February 2013}}</ref>

Hamdallah spoke about the first time he arrived at Norway, stating: ''"It was a difficult experience because I arrived in a country that's very cold, but through an effort of will, I overcame every obstacle. I was the first player from the Moroccan championship to come to Norway, and my signing generated a lot of attention in the newspapers. In the end, I was able to silence my critics by finishing as one of the leading scorers in the league."''<ref name="HamdallahFIFA">{{cite web |url=https://www.fifa.com/world-match-centre/news/newsid/243/639/9/ |title=Hamdallah: We can be kings of Africa |publisher=FIFA Official Website |date=5 September 2014|access-date=5 July 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150705085547/http://www.fifa.com/world-match-centre/news/newsid/243/639/9/|archive-date=5 July 2015|url-status=dead}}</ref>

Hamdallah made his debut on 1 April 2013 as a substitute in the 1–0 away win of [[Sandnes Ulf]] and then scored his first goal for the club, in a 2–0 win over [[Sarpsborg 08 FF|Sarpsborg 08]] on 14 April 2013. Then on 13 May 2013, Hamdallah scored his first hat-trick for Aalesund in a 7–1 win over [[Lillestrøm SK]], which he helped the club into second place after nine games.<ref>{{cite news |title=Hamed-Allah scored a hat-trick as AaFK slaughtered LSK|trans-title=Hamed-Allah scoret hat-trick da AaFK slaktet LSK |url=http://www.vg.no/sport/fotball/aalesund/hamed-allah-scoret-hat-trick-da-aafk-slaktet-lsk/a/10103405/ |publisher=VG Sporten |language=no |date=12 May 2013|access-date=21 May 2014}}</ref> After scoring ten goals so far this season, Hamadallah scored his second hat-trick of the season, in a 3–1 win over [[Viking FK|Viking]] on 25 October 2013.<ref>{{cite news |title=Viking 1–3 Aalesund|trans-title=Viking 1–3 Aalesund |url=http://www.altomfotball.no/element.do?cmd=match&matchId=662505&tournamentId=1&seasonId=335&useFullUrl=false |publisher=Altom Football |language=no |date=25 October 2013|access-date=21 May 2014}}</ref> In his first season with the club, Hamdallah scored 15 times in 27 appearances and was included in the Team of the Year.<ref>{{cite news |title=Hangeland after voting mistake: Stefan moral winner|trans-title=Hangeland etter stemmetabbe: Stefan moralsk vinner |url=http://www.vg.no/sport/fotball/stroemsgodset/hangeland-etter-stemmetabbe-stefan-moralsk-vinner/a/10133344/ |publisher=VG Sporten |language=no |date=24 November 2013|access-date=21 May 2014}}</ref> Also, it left Hamdallah as the club's top scorer, though he was the second in the league behind [[Frode Johnsen]].<ref>{{cite news |title=Johnsen stand for this year's odds bomb in Norwegian football|trans-title=Johnsen kan stå for årets oddsbombe i norsk fotball |url=http://www.vg.no/sport/tippetips/johnsen-kan-staa-for-aarets-oddsbombe-i-norsk-fotball/a/10144857/ |publisher=VG Sporten |language=no |date=4 November 2013|access-date=21 May 2014}}</ref>

==International career==
On 22 December 2009, Hamdallah was called up to the [[Morocco national under-23 football team|Morocco national under-23 team]] for a week long training camp. On 13 December 2010, Hamdallah scored a goal against Libya's Under-23 team in the first game for Morocco in the [[2010 UNAF U-23 Tournament]], as Morocco went on to win the game 4–0. On 18 December 2010, Hamdallah scored in a 2–1 victory against [[Cameroon]]'s Under-23 team.

==Style of play==
Hamdallah is described as an "unstoppable phenomenon", and is considered a prolific goalscorer. He is a technical player, with excellent movement, heading technique, and deadly last touches. Hamdallah can score with both feet, and also capable of providing assists due to his vision. He has been criticized of his pace though.<ref>{{Cite web |last=السلمي |first=تقرير-علي |date=31 January 2019 |title=الهداف.. "حمدالله" |url=https://www.alyaum.com/articles/6075980/الميدان-الرياضي/الهداف-حمدالله |access-date=28 February 2022 |website=alyaum |language=Arabic}}</ref> He is nicknamed "the Executioner" for his goalscoring ability.<ref name="kooora.com"/><ref name="حمد الله.. جلاد في محيط الرعب"/>
