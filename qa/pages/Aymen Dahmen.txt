'''Aymen Dahmen''' ({{lang-ar|أَيْمَن دَحمَان}}; born 28 January 1997) is a Tunisian professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[goalkeeper (association football)|goalkeeper]] for Saudi Arabian club [[Al-Hazem F.C.|Al-Hazem]].

==Club career==
Dahmen made his professional debut with [[CS Sfaxien]] in a 2–0 [[Tunisian Ligue Professionnelle 1]] win over [[ES Métlaoui]] on 16 September 2018.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2018/09/16/tunisia/ligue-1/etoile-sportive-de-metlaoui/club-sportif-sfaxien/2847505/|title=Métlaoui vs. CS Sfaxien - 16 September 2018|website=Soccerway}}</ref>

On 11 June 2023, Dahmen joined Saudi Pro League club [[Al-Hazem F.C.|Al-Hazem]] on a two-year contract.<ref>{{cite web |title=رسميا.. الحزم يتعاقد مع أيمن دحمان |url=https://www.kooora.com/?n=1247327}}</ref>

==International career==
Dahmen was called up to represent the [[Tunisia national under-23 football team|Tunisia U23 national team]] for [[2019 Africa U-23 Cup of Nations qualification]] matches.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.afrik-foot.com/can-u23-tunisie-la-liste-contre-le-cameroun-avec-3-joueurs-de-la-can-2019|title=CAN U23 – Tunisie : la liste contre le Cameroun avec 3 joueurs de la CAN 2019 !|date=27 August 2019}}</ref>

He made his debut for [[Tunisia national football team|senior national team]] on 28 March 2021 in an [[2021 Africa Cup of Nations qualification Group J|AFCON 2021 qualifier]] against [[Equatorial Guinea]].<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[Confederation of African Football]]|url=https://www.cafonline.com/total-africa-cup-of-nations/2021/matches/2032569/|title=Tunisia v Equatorial Guinea game report|date=28 March 2021}}</ref>

On November 14, 2022, he was selected by Jalel Kadri to participate in the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Khazri, Talbi et Abdi dans la liste de la Tunisie pour la Coupe du monde |url=https://www.lequipe.fr/Football/Actualites/Khazri-talbi-et-abdi-dans-la-liste-de-la-tunisie-pour-la-coupe-du-monde/1364820 |access-date=2023-02-08 |website=L'Équipe |language=fr}}</ref> He participated in all Tunisia's matches in the World Cup.
