'''Bakary Papa Gassama''' (born 10 February 1979) is a [[The Gambia|Gambia]]n [[association football|football]] [[referee (association football)|referee]].<ref>[http://worldreferee.com/ World Referee]</ref> He became a [[FIFA]] referee in 2007.<ref>[[FIFA]]. [https://web.archive.org/web/20070813041227/http://www.fifa.com/associations/association=gam/footballofficials/referees/peoplekind=ref.html "Gambia: Referees"]. Retrieved on 22 April 2013.</ref> He officiated at the [[Football at the 2012 Summer Olympics - Men's tournament|2012 Olympic tournament]],<ref>FIFA. [https://web.archive.org/web/20080704050928/http://www.fifa.com/mensolympic/referees/index.html "Men's Olympic Football Tournament: Referees"]. Retrieved on 22 April 2013.</ref> in which he was the fourth official for the gold medal match between [[Mexico national football team|Mexico]] and [[Brazil national football team|Brazil]].<ref>FIFA. [https://web.archive.org/web/20120814234153/http://www.fifa.com/mensolympic/matches/round=256027/match=300197697/report.html "Men's Olympic Football Tournament: Brazil 1:2 (0:1) Mexico"]. 11 August 2012. Retrieved on 22 April 2013.</ref>
He also served as a referee at the [[2012 Africa Cup of Nations|2012]]<ref>Confederation of African Football. [http://www.cafonline.com/competition/african-cup-of-nations_2012/match1483/live/statistics "Match Gabon vs. Morocco 01-27-2012"]. 27 January 2012. Retrieved on 22 April 2013.</ref> and [[2013 Africa Cup of Nations]]<ref>Morrison, Neil. [https://www.rsssf.org/tables/2013a.html "African Nations Cup 2013"]. ''[[Rec.Sport.Soccer Statistics Foundation|RSSSF]]'', 22 February 2013. Retrieved on 22 April 2013.</ref> tournaments, as well as the [[2014 FIFA World Cup qualification – CAF second round|2014 FIFA World Cup qualifiers]].<ref>FIFA. [https://web.archive.org/web/20130709104741/http://www.fifa.com/worldcup/preliminaries/africa/matches/round=258334/match=300181923/report.html "Match Report – Congo – Gabon 1:0 (0:0)"]. 23 March 2013. Retrieved on 22 April 2013.</ref>

In March 2013, FIFA named Gassama as one of the fifty potential referees for the [[2014 FIFA World Cup|2014 World Cup]]. On 15 January 2014, FIFA announced that he would be one of the 25 referees for the tournament. His assistants were announced to be Evarist Menkouandé and Felicien Kabanda.<ref>[https://web.archive.org/web/20140116084943/http://www.fifa.com/mm/document/tournament/competition/02/26/13/55/2014fwcrefereesselected_neutral.pdf Referees & Assistant referees for the 2014 FIFA World Cup] FIFA, 14 January 2014. cited on 17 April 2014.</ref> He officiated the [[2014 FIFA World Cup Group B#Netherlands v Chile|third group stage match]] in [[2014 FIFA World Cup Group B|group B]] between the [[Netherlands national football team|Netherlands]] and [[Chile national football team|Chile]] on 23 June 2014. He officiated the [[2015 Africa Cup of Nations Final]] match on 8 February 2015 between [[Ghana national football team|Ghana]] and [[Ivory Coast national football team|Côte d'Ivoire]].

On 27 April 2017, Gassama was selected as [[Confederation of African Football|CAF]]'s sole referee for the [[2017 FIFA Confederations Cup]] in Russia. His assistants were announced to be Jean Claude Birumushahu ([[Football Federation of Burundi|Burundi]]) and [[Marwa Range]] ([[Football Kenya Federation|Kenya]]), and his video assistant referee was appointed to be [[Malang Diedhiou]] ([[Senegalese Football Federation|Senegal]]). His only match at the competition was the [[2017 FIFA Confederations Cup Group A|Group A]] match between [[Mexico national football team|Mexico]] and [[New Zealand national football team|New Zealand]] on 21 June 2017.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://resources.fifa.com/mm/Document/Tournament/Competition/02/88/05/84/ListofAppointedMatchOfficialsFIFACCRussia2017_Neutral.pdf?t=1493294430382 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170814094438/http://resources.fifa.com/mm/Document/Tournament/Competition/02/88/05/84/ListofAppointedMatchOfficialsFIFACCRussia2017_Neutral.pdf?t=1493294430382 |url-status=dead |archive-date=14 August 2017 |title=FIFA Confederations Cup 2017 - List of appointed match officials |author=<!--Not stated--> |date=27 April 2017 |website=fifa.com |access-date=19 June 2017 }}</ref>

On 29 March 2018, FIFA announced that he had been selected to officiate some matches at [[2018 FIFA World Cup]] along with Jean-Claude Birumushahu and Marwa Range as assistant referees.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://resources.fifa.com/image/upload/list-of-match-officials-for-the-2018-fifa-world-cuptm.pdf?cloudid=ygppy1zdhjaxituykarn |title=List of Match Officials at 2018 FIFA World Cup |author=<!--Not stated--> |date=29 March 2018 |website=fifa.com |access-date=31 March 2018 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180330012652/http://resources.fifa.com/image/upload/list-of-match-officials-for-the-2018-fifa-world-cuptm.pdf?cloudid=ygppy1zdhjaxituykarn |archive-date=30 March 2018 |url-status=dead}}</ref>

FIFA announced that he had been selected to officiate some matches at 2022 FIFA World Cup Qatar.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://digitalhub.fifa.com/m/1da4b811328add8f/original/MEDIA-Alphabetical-order-List-of-Match-Officials-FWC-2022-Qatar.pdf |title=FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022: List of match officials |publisher=[[FIFA]] |format=PDF |date=19 May 2022 |access-date=18 November 2022}}</ref>
