'''Andreas Skov Olsen''' ({{IPA-da|ˈskʌwˀ ˈoˀlsn̩}}; born 29 December 1999) is a Danish professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Winger (association football)|winger]] or a [[right midfielder]] for [[Belgian Pro League]] side [[Club Brugge KV|Club Brugge]] and the [[Denmark national football team|Denmark national team]].

==Youth career==
Olsen started his career at local club, ASGI from Alsønderup, outside of [[Hillerød]], but later joined the [[FC Nordsjælland]] at the age of 12.<ref>{{ cite web | title = 17-årige Olsen efter debut for FC Nordsjælland: Kræver hårdt arbejde hver dag | url = https://tonsser.com/dk/nextgen/17-aarige-olsen-efter-debut-for-fc-nordsjaelland-kraever-haardt-arbejde-hver-dag | publisher = tonsser.com | date = 17 July 2017 }}</ref>

==Club career==

===Nordsjælland===
Already at the age of 17, Olsen was called up by the coach of the [[Denmark national under-21 football team|Danish national under-21 football team]], to replace an injured [[Kasper Junker]] in June 2017. He then got his debut for the U21 national team in a game against [[Sweden national under-21 football team|Sweden U21]].<ref>{{ cite web | title = Kasper Junker EM-testes søndag | url = http://www.bold.dk/fodbold/nyheder/kasper-junker-em-testes-soendag/ | publisher = bold.dk | date = 11 June 2017 }}</ref>

The winger got his first senior call up on 17 July 2017, where he sat on the bench for the whole game against [[Odense Boldklub|OB]]. He was also with the first team squad in Sweden in the summer 2017, where he played a in a friendly match.

Olsen got his debut for [[FC Nordsjælland]] on 23 July 2017 at the age of 17. He started on the bench, but replaced [[Emiliano Marcondes]] in the 95th minute in a 3–2 victory against [[Brøndby IF]] in the [[Danish Superliga]].<ref>{{ cite web | title = NORDSJAELLAND VS. BRONDBY 3 - 2 | url = https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2017/07/23/denmark/superliga/fc-nordsjalland/brondby-if/2460321/ | publisher = soccerway.com | date = 23 July 2017 }}</ref> Few days later, he played 72 minutes in a [[Danish Cup]] game against amateur side Vejgaard B, where he both scored and assisted a goal.

On 12 July 2018, Olsen scored his first European goal in a [[2018–19 UEFA Europa League|Europa League Qualifier]] against [[Cliftonville F.C.|Cliftonville]].<ref>{{Cite web |last=UEFA.com |title=Cliftonville-Nordsjælland {{!}} UEFA Europa League 2018/19 |url=https://www.uefa.com/uefaeuropaleague/match/2024673/events/ |access-date=15 May 2022 |website=UEFA.com |language=en}}</ref>

Olsen was officially promoted to the first team squad from the summer 2018. Olsen had a turbulent first half season in the Danish Superliga scoring 11 goals despite his young age.<ref>[https://www.bold.dk/fodbold/nyheder/fcn-komet-har-indfriet-saesonmaal-det-er-stort/ FCN-komet har indfriet sæsonmål: Det er stort], bold.dk, 14 January 2019</ref> He ended the season scoring 22 league goals.

===Bologna===
On 24 July 2019, it was confirmed, that Olsen had left Denmark after two seasons in the Danish Superliga, to join Italian [[Serie A]] club [[Bologna F.C. 1909|Bologna]].<ref>[https://www.bolognafc.it/andreas-skov-olsen-al-bologna/ Andreas Skov Olsen al Bologna], bolognafc.it, 24 July 2019</ref>

===Club Brugge===
On 28 January 2022, Bologna announced Olsen's transfer to [[Club Brugge KV|Club Brugge]] in Belgium, where he signed a four-and-a-half-year contract.<ref>{{cite press release|publisher=[[Bologna F.C. 1909|Bologna]]|url=https://www.bolognafc.it/skov-olsen-al-bruges/|title=Skov Olsen al Bruges|date=28 January 2022|access-date=28 January 2022|language=it}}</ref> He made his debut on 2 February in a [[Belgian Cup]] match against [[K.A.A. Gent|Gent]], coming off the bench for [[Ruud Vormer]] at half-time and providing the decisive assist for [[Charles De Ketelaere]], securing a 1–0 win for his team.<ref>{{cite news |title=Club Brugge wil met Danish Dynamite AA Gent in de vernieling spelen |url=https://www.proximus.be/pickx/nl/2204411/club-brugge-wil-met-danish-dynamite-aa-gent-in-de-vernieling-spelen |access-date=18 July 2022 |work=Proximus |date=5 February 2022 |language=nl}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Gent vs. Club Brugge – 2 February 2022 |url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2022/02/02/belgium/beker-van-belgie/koninklijke-atletiek-associatie-gent/club-brugge-kv/3718491/ |website=Soccerway |publisher=Perform Group |access-date=18 July 2022}}</ref> On 17 July 2022, Olsen scored the winning goal for Club in the [[2022 Belgian Super Cup]] against Gent.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.clubbrugge.be/en/news/club-beats-gent-1-0-and-lifts-supercup|title=CLUB BEATS GENT 1-0 AND LIFTS THE SUPERCUP|website=clubbrugge.be|date=17 July 2022|access-date=20 July 2022}}</ref>

==International career==
Olsen was called up to the senior Denmark squad for the UEFA Nations League matches against [[Belgium national football team|Belgium]] and [[England national football team|England]] in September 2020.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.squawka.com/en/denmark-belgium-live-stream-watch-nations-league-predictions/|title=Denmark vs Belgium live stream: Watch today's Nations League game online|publisher=squawka.com |date=5 September 2020 |accessdate=8 September 2020}}</ref> He made his debut on 7 October 2020 in a friendly against [[Faroe Islands national football team|Faroe Islands]] and scored a goal in a 4–0 win.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[ESPN]]|url=https://www.espn.com/soccer/match?gameId=572285|title=Denmark v Faroe Islands game report|date=7 October 2020}}</ref>

In June 2021, he was included in the national team's bid for [[2020 UEFA Euro]], where the team reached the semi-finals.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://dbu.dk/nyheder/2021/maj/26-spillere-klar-til-em-for-danmark/ |title=26 spillere klar til EM for Danmark |trans-title=26 players ready for the European Championship for Denmark |work=[[Danish Football Association]] |date=25 May 2021 |access-date=25 May 2021 |language=da}}</ref>
