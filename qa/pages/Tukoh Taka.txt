"'''Tukoh Taka'''" is the 2022 FIFA Fan Festival anthem performed by Trinidadian-born rapper [[Nicki Minaj]], Colombian singer [[Maluma]] and Lebanese singer [[Myriam Fares]].<ref>{{Cite web |date=November 18, 2022 |title=Nicki Minaj, Maluma, Myriam Fares release World Cup anthem |url=https://abcnews.go.com/Entertainment/wireStory/nicki-minaj-maluma-myriam-fares-release-world-cup-93566751 |access-date=November 25, 2022 |website=[[ABC News]] |language=en}}</ref> It was released by [[FIFA]] for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] [[FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022 Official Soundtrack|soundtrack]] on November 18, 2022, through [[Universal Music Group|Universal Arabic Music]] and [[Republic Records]] along with an accompanying music video.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Callas |first=Brad |date=November 18, 2022 |title=Nicki Minaj Joins Forces With Maluma and Myriam Fares for World Cup Song "Tukoh Taka" |url=https://www.complex.com/music/nicki-minaj-maluma-myriam-fares-tukoh-taka |access-date=November 25, 2022 |website=[[Complex Networks]] |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite magazine |last=Mier |first=Tomás |date=November 18, 2022 |title=Maluma and Nicki Minaj Welcome the 2022 World Cup With Arab-Latin Anthem 'Tukoh Taka' |url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-news/nicki-minaj-maluma-tukoh-taka-world-cup-anthem-1234633137/ |access-date=November 25, 2022 |magazine=[[Rolling Stone]] |language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |date=November 15, 2022 |title=Wassim Salibi brings together Nicki Minaj, Maluma and Myriam Fares in 'Tukoh Taka'… behind the scenes of the expected work (video) |url=https://news.middleeast-24.com/entertainment/news/144910.html |access-date=November 16, 2022 |website=Middle East 24 Entertainment English |language=en-us}}</ref>

== Composition ==
The song features the three artists' languages in the lyrics: Minaj's English, Maluma's Spanish, and Fares' Arabic, becoming the first world championship anthem to feature all three languages.<ref>{{Cite magazine |last=Peters |first=Mitchell |date=November 20, 2022 |title=Fans Choose Nicki Minaj, Maluma & Myriam Fares' 'Tukoh Tukoh Taka' as This Week's Favorite New Music |url=https://www.billboard.com/music/music-news/nicki-minaj-maluma-myriam-fares-tukoh-taka-2022-world-cup-best-new-music-1235174444/ |access-date=November 25, 2022 |magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] |language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |date=November 18, 2022 |title=Nicki Minaj, Maluma, Myriam Fares release World Cup anthem |url=https://www.nbcnews.com/news/nbcblk/nicki-minaj-maluma-myriam-fares-release-world-cup-anthem-rcna57974 |access-date=November 25, 2022 |website=[[NBC News]] |language=en}}</ref> Fares said in a statement: "'Tukoh Taka', the song that I was honored to participate in its composition, arrangement, and choreography, made me more passionate about it, in addition to working alongside two of my favorite international artists, Nicki Minaj and Maluma. I truly wish that 'Tukoh Taka' will be transmitting the [[Eastern culture]] and [[Arabic music|Arabian music]] to the whole world.”<ref>{{Cite magazine |last=Flores |first=Griselda |date=November 11, 2022 |title=Nicki Minaj, Maluma & Myriam Fares Unleash Euphoric World Cup Song 'Tukoh Taka' |url=https://www.billboard.com/music/latin/nicki-minaj-maluma-myriam-fares-world-cup-song-tukoh-taka-2-1235173740/ |access-date=November 25, 2022 |magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] |language=en-US}}</ref>

The song has [[reggaeton]], [[Pop music|Pop]] and [[house-rap]] sounds.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Darville |first=Jordan |date=November 18, 2022 |title=Travis Scott, Skepta, and Yves Tumor to headline festival celebrating Virgil Abloh's life |url=https://www.thefader.com/2022/11/22/travis-scott-skepta-and-yves-tumor-headline-mirror-mirror-festival-celebrating-virgil-abloh |access-date=November 25, 2022 |website=[[The Fader]] |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last=Krol |first=Charlotte |date=November 18, 2022 |title=Hear Nicki Minaj, Maluma and Myriam Fares' FIFA Fan Festival song 'Tukoh Taka' |url=https://www.nme.com/news/music/hear-nicki-minaj-maluma-and-myriam-fares-fifa-fan-festival-song-tukoh-taka-3351920 |access-date=November 25, 2022 |website=[[NME]] |language=en-GB}}</ref>

The name of the song is said to be Arabic for "knock-knock", with fans and commenters also recognising it to sound like "''[[tiki-taka]]''", a style of football play.<ref>{{Cite web |title=The World Cup 2022 FIFA Fan Anthem: Mixed Reviews Lillian Mose Newstead Wood School |url=https://www.thisislocallondon.co.uk/news/23152079.fifa-fan-anthem-mixed-reviews-lillian-mose-newstead-wood-school/ |access-date=November 28, 2022 |website=This Is Local London |date=November 26, 2022 |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last=Krishnamurthy |first=Chaitra |date=November 19, 2022 |title=Meaning of Tukoh Taka as the new FIFA World Cup 2022 song has fans grooving |url=https://www.hitc.com/en-gb/2022/11/19/meaning-of-tukoh-taka-the-new-fifa-world-cup-2022-song/ |access-date=November 28, 2022 |website=HITC |language=en-GB}}</ref>

== Critical reaction ==
''[[Forbes]]''{{'}}s writer Chris Malone Méndez said the pairing of the two international artists with the [[Arabs|Arab]] singer "was a natural choice for optimal global reach" but, while appreciating the song's trilingualism, noted that "no [[Music of Qatar|Qatari artists]] are featured on any [[List of FIFA World Cup songs and anthems|World Cup songs]]".<ref>{{Cite web |last=Méndez |first=Chris Malone |date=November 18, 2022 |title=Nicki Minaj, Maluma, and Myriam Fares Team Up For FIFA World Cup Song 'Tukoh Taka' |url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/cmalone/2022/11/18/nicki-minaj-maluma-and-myriam-fares-team-up-for-fifa-world-cup-song-tukoh-taka/ |access-date=November 25, 2022 |website=[[Forbes]] |language=en}}</ref>

The song received negative reactions in the [[Arab world]]. Fans and critics accused Myriam Fares of imitating [[Shakira]]'s dancing style and overall aesthetics.<ref>{{cite web|last=Ifteqar|first=Naheed|url=https://en.vogue.me/culture/fifa-world-cup-2022-anthem-tukoh-taka-myriam-fares-nicki-minaj-maluma-criticism/|title=Here's What Fifa Fans Have to Say of Myriam Fares's Feature on the 'Tukoh Taka' Anthem|publisher=[[Vogue Arabia]]|date=November 21, 2022}}</ref> According to ''[[Haaretz]]'', numerous online critics said the song had "annoying lyrics".<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.haaretz.com/middle-east-news/2022-11-23/ty-article/.premium/cheap-imitation-of-shakira-arab-world-blasts-lebanese-singers-world-cup-anthem/00000184-a461-de09-a7d5-f5eb763c0000|title=Qatar 2022: Arab World Blasts Lebanese Singer's Anthem 'Tukoh Taka'|date=November 23, 2022|publisher=[[Haaretz]]}}</ref>

== Controversy ==
{{Main|2022 FIFA World Cup controversies#Opening ceremony and anthem}}
After the announcement of their collaboration for the World Cup anthem, the three artists were criticised for choosing to be paid to sing a song in an [[Emirate]] whose internal policies violate [[LGBT rights in Qatar|LGBT rights]] and support perceived [[slavery]].<ref>{{Cite web |last=Irvin |first=Jack |date=November 18, 2022 |title=Nicki Minaj, Maluma and Myriam Fares Drop Historic Trilingual World Cup Song amid Qatar Controversy |url=https://people.com/music/nicki-minaj-drops-first-ever-trilingual-world-cup-anthem/ |access-date=November 21, 2022 |website=[[People Magazine]] |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last=O'Connor |first=Rachael |date=November 16, 2022 |title=Nicki Minaj faces backlash for Qatar World Cup song over human rights concerns |url=https://metro.co.uk/2022/11/16/nicki-minaj-faces-backlash-for-qatar-world-cup-song-17772339/ |access-date=November 21, 2022 |website=[[Metro (British newspaper)|Metro]] |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last=Sadler |first=Armon |date=November 15, 2022 |title=Nicki Minaj Confirms 2022 Qatar World Cup Song Despite Backlash |url=https://www.vibe.com/music/music-news/nicki-minaj-qatar-world-cup-song-backlash-1234711455/ |access-date=November 21, 2022 |website=[[Vibe (magazine)|Vibe]] |language=en-US}}</ref> Singer Maluma replied to the charge by declaring: "it's something I can't resolve; [...] It's not something that I actually have to be involved with. I'm here enjoying my music and the beautiful life, playing soccer too".<ref>{{Cite web |last=Média |first=Bell |date=November 21, 2022 |title=Maluma Shuts Down Questions About Qatar's Human Rights Abuses |url=https://www.iheartradio.ca/newstalk-1290-cjbk/news/maluma-shuts-down-questions-about-qatar-s-human-rights-abuses-1.18817458 |access-date=November 21, 2022 |website=[[iHeartRadio]] |language=en-CA}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last=Richards |first=Will |date=November 21, 2022 |title=World Cup anthem singer Maluma walks out of interview after accusations of "whitewashing" Qatar human rights abuses |url=https://www.nme.com/news/music/world-cup-anthem-singer-maluma-walks-out-of-interview-after-accusations-of-whitewashing-qatar-human-rights-abuses-3352775 |access-date=November 21, 2022 |website=[[NME]] |language=en-GB}}</ref> FIFA has also been under fire online for featuring Fares considering her [[blackface]] stunt in her "[[Goumi (song)|Goumi]]" (2018) music video.<ref>{{cite magazine|url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-news/world-cup-myriam-fares-blackface-1234634934/ |title=World Cup Anthem Singer Myriam Fares Wore Blackface in 2018 |first=Tomás|last=Mier |date=November 21, 2022 |access-date=December 13, 2022 |magazine=[[Rolling Stone]]}}</ref>

== Music video ==
The music video, directed by Edgar Esteves and Juan Felipe Zuleta, features Maluma and Fares performing in the Qatari desert, while Minaj performs her verses aboard a bus, all alternating with some clips of soccer players cheering after goals.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Valdez |first=Jonah |date=November 18, 2022 |title=World Cup debuts anthem 'Tukoh Taka,' featuring Nicki Minaj, Maluma and Myriam Fares |url=https://www.latimes.com/entertainment-arts/story/2022-11-18/nicki-minaj-maluma-myriam-fares-party-in-the-desert-in-fifa-world-cup-anthem-tukoh-taka |access-date=November 15, 2022 |website=[[Los Angeles Times]] |language=en-US}}</ref>
