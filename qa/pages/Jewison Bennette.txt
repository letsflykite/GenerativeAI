'''Jewison Francisco Bennette Villegas''' (born 15 June 2004) is a Costa Rican professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Midfielder#Winger|left winger]] for [[Sunderland A.F.C.|Sunderland]] and the [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica national team]].

==Club career==
===Early career===
Born in [[Heredia, Costa Rica|Heredia]], Bennette started his career at [[C.S. Herediano|Herediano]].<ref name="NFT" /> He made his senior debut on 1 August 2021 in a 3–2 defeat to [[A.D.R. Jicaral|Jicaral]], and scored the first goal of the match after four minutes.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Brenes |first=Cristian |date=1 August 2021 |title=Técnico de Herediano cataloga como diamante a Jewison Bennett |url=https://www.nacion.com/puro-deporte/futbol-nacional/tecnico-de-herediano-cataloga-como-diamante-a/HXVJ65ZK3NEXBIRHUAZILDLBLE/story/ |access-date=17 January 2022 |website=[[La Nación (San José)|La Nación]]|language=es}}</ref>

===Sunderland===
In August 2022, [[EFL Championship]] side [[Sunderland A.F.C.|Sunderland]] announced they had signed Bennette to a four-year deal.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://safc.com/news/team-news/2022/august/safc-sign-jewison-bennette|date=25 August 2022|title=DONE DEAL: Jewison Bennette signs for SAFC|work=[[Sunderland A.F.C.|Sunderland]]}}</ref> He made his debut for his new club on 14 September, replacing [[Corry Evans]] as a substitute against [[Reading F.C.|Reading]].<ref>{{cite tweet|author=[[Sunderland A.F.C.]]|user=SunderlandAFC|number=1570150901482139649|title=Debut time...@Jewisonoficial9 #SAFC #REASUN|date=14 September 2022|access-date=14 September 2022}}</ref> In his second match for Sunderland against [[Watford F.C.|Watford]], he scored his first goal.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://rokerreport.sbnation.com/2022/9/17/23357851/andys-player-ratings-watford-2-2-sunderland-alese-bennettes-first-goals-win-a-deserved-point|date=17 September 2022|work=Roker Report|title=Andy's Player Ratings: Watford 2-2 Sunderland: Alese & Bennette's first goals win a deserved point}}</ref>

==International career==
Bennette played for the Costa Rica under-15 team in 2019.<ref>{{Cite web |title=J. Bennette: Summary |url=https://uk.soccerway.com/players/jewison-bennette/657584/ |access-date=17 January 2022 |website=Soccerway |publisher=Perform Group}}</ref> He trained with the Costa Rica national team in summer 2021 ahead of the [[2021 CONCACAF Gold Cup]],<ref>{{Cite web |last=Naranjo |first=Julio |date=1 July 2021 |title=Jewison Bennette: el jugador que a Suárez le habría gustado llevar a la Copa Oro |url=https://www.teletica.com/la-sele/jewison-bennette-el-jugador-que-a-suarez-le-habria-gustado-llevar-a-la-copa-oro_288898 |access-date=17 January 2022 |website=Teletica.com |publisher=[[Teletica]] |language=es}}</ref> and made his senior international debut for [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica]] in a 0–0 draw against [[El Salvador national football team|El Salvador]] on 21 August 2021, becoming the youngest player to represent Costa Rica internationally, at 17 years and 2 months.<ref name="auto">{{Cite web |last=Villarreal |first=Juan Diego |date=17 November 2021 |title=Jewison Bennette: El chico que impuso récord en la 'Tricolor' y tuvo un debut que lo marcará para siempre |url=https://www.nacion.com/puro-deporte/seleccion-nacional/jewison-bennette-el-chico-que-impuso-record-en-la/E4O6D6XEBFFMFD3SA5A3DGYB5Y/story/ |access-date=17 January 2022 |website=La Nación |language=es}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last=Naranjo |first=Julio |date=22 August 2021 |title=Jewison Bennette: "El objetivo es meterse en la lista de la eliminatoria" |url=https://www.teletica.com/la-sele/jewison-bennette-el-objetivo-es-meterse-en-la-lista-de-la-eliminatoria_293364 |access-date=17 January 2022 |website=Teletica.com |publisher=Teletica |language=es}}</ref>

He played in Costa Rica's [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification (CONCACAF)|2022 World Cup qualification]] fixtures against [[Canada national men's soccer team|Canada]] and [[Honduras national football team|Honduras]] in November 2021, becoming Costa Rica's youngest player to feature in World Cup qualifying.<ref name="auto" /> Bennette became crucial for the team, as he assisted [[Joel Campbell]] to score the lone goal at the [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification (CONCACAF–OFC play-off)|play-off match]] against [[New Zealand national football team|New Zealand]] to seal Costa Rica's spot at the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]]. On 23 September he scored his first two international goals in a friendly against [[South Korea national football team|South Korea]].<ref>{{cite news |last1=Herrera |first1=Walter |title=(VIDEO) ¡Oficial! Jewison Bennette ficha por el Sunderland de Inglaterra |url=https://www.larepublica.net/noticia/video-oficial-jewison-bennette-ficha-por-el-sunderland-de-inglaterra |access-date=4 September 2022 |work=La República |date=25 August 2022 |language=es}}</ref> In November 2022 Bennette was named to the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] squad for Costa Rica.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://safc.com/news/team-news/2022/november/jewison-bennette-wc-call-up|title=WORLD CUP CALL UP: Jewi heading to Qatar|date=3 November 2022|first=Matty|last=Jones|work=[[Sunderland A.F.C.|Sunderland]]}}</ref>

==Personal life==
His father, Jewisson Bennette, also played as a footballer for [[C.S. Herediano|Herediano]] and the [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica national team]], as did his uncle [[Try Bennett]].<ref name="auto1">{{Cite web |last=Fernando Areya |first=José |date=23 September 2020 |title=Hijo de Jewison Bennette buscar dejar su huella en la Sub20 |url=https://www.teletica.com/la-sele/hijo-de-jewison-bennette-buscar-dejar-su-huella-en-la-sub20_269075 |access-date=17 January 2022 |website=Teletica.com |publisher=Teletica |language=es}}</ref> He is the older brother of twins Nick and Mike.<ref name="auto1" />
