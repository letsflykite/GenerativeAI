'''Grzegorz Krychowiak''' ({{IPAc-pl|AUD|Pl-Grzegorz Krychowiak.ogg|'|g|rz|e|g|o|rz|-|k|r|y|'|ch|o|w|J|j|a|k}}; born 29 January 1990) is a Polish professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[defensive midfielder]] for the [[Poland national football team|Poland national team]].

Beginning his senior career in France with [[FC Girondins de Bordeaux|Bordeaux]], he later played for [[Stade de Reims|Reims]], before joining [[Sevilla FC|Sevilla]] for €3.5 million in 2014, winning the [[UEFA Europa League]] in both of his two seasons at the club.

Krychowiak has earned over 90 caps for Poland since his international debut in 2008, and represented the nation at the [[UEFA European Championship]] in [[UEFA Euro 2016|2016]] and [[UEFA Euro 2020|2020]] and the [[FIFA World Cup]] in [[2018 FIFA World Cup|2018]] and [[2022 FIFA World Cup|2022]].

==Club career==
===Early career===
[[File:Grzegorz Krychowiak.jpg|thumb|left|upright|Krychowiak training at [[FC Girondins de Bordeaux|Bordeaux]] in 2009]]

Although initially not a big fan of football, Krychowiak became involved in the sport at his brother's insistence and left home at the age of 12 to join a sports academy.<ref name=confirmer>{{cite news|first=Gérard|last=Kancel|url=http://www.lunion.presse.fr/article/a-la-une/stade-de-reims-pacy-pour-confirmer|title=Stade de Reims / Pacy pour confirmer|date=23 January 2010|work=L'union|access-date=23 January 2010|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120226222558/http://www.lunion.presse.fr/article/a-la-une/stade-de-reims-pacy-pour-confirmer|archive-date=26 February 2012|url-status=dead}}</ref> He then joined the academies of various clubs, including Orzeł Mrzeżyno, Żaki 94 Kołobrzeg, Stal Szczecin, and [[Arka Gdynia]].<ref name="Krychowiak Youth Club 1">{{cite news|title=Z Żaków 94 Kołobrzeg do reprezentacji|url=https://gk24.pl/z-zakow-94-kolobrzeg-do-reprezentacji/ar/4223983|publisher=GK 24|language=pl|date=3 July 2007|access-date=11 November 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Piłka nożna. Szczecińskie czasy Krychowiaka|url=https://24kurier.pl/aktualnosci/sport/pilka-nozna-szczecinskie-czasy-krychowiaka/|publisher=24 Kurier|language=pl|date=4 April 2016|access-date=11 November 2020}}</ref>

During the [[France national under-16 football team|France U16]] and [[Poland national under-16 football team|Poland U16]] in [[Strasbourg]], Krychowiak's performance attracted interests by Philippe Goubet, leading him to join [[FC Girondins de Bordeaux|Bordeaux]] in 2006.<ref>{{cite news|title=Diabeł kusi nastolatka z Arki Gdynia|url=http://www.archiwum.wyborcza.pl/Archiwum/1,0,4537109,20051214GD-DLO,Diabel_kusi_nastolatka_z_Arki_Gdynia,.html|publisher=Gazeta Wybocrcza|language=pl|date=14 December 2005|access-date=11 November 2020}}<br>{{cite news|title=Grzegorz Krychowiak, au bon souvenir de Bordeaux|url=https://www.sudouest.fr/2011/06/09/grzegorz-krychowiak-au-bon-souvenir-de-bordeaux-421247-8.php|publisher=Sudouest|language=fr|date=9 June 2011|access-date=11 November 2020}}</ref> He then progressed through the ranks of the club's youth system and signed his first professional contract with them.<ref>{{cite news|title=Reims : Grzegorz Krychowiak (Bordeaux) prêté?|url=https://www.foot-national.com/foot-reims-grzegorz-krychowiak-bordeaux-prete-13901.html|publisher=Foot National|language=fr|date=18 November 2009|access-date=11 November 2020}}<br>{{cite news|title=Krychowiak, le fruit de la patience|url=https://abonne.lunion.fr/art/517805/article/2015-07-26/krychowiak-le-fruit-de-la-patience|publisher=L'Union (subscription required)|language=fr|date=26 July 2015|access-date=11 November 2020}}</ref>

==International career==
===Youth career===
Krychowiak represented [[Poland national under-20 football team|Poland under-20]]s at the [[2007 FIFA U-20 World Cup]] and scored a free kick against [[Brazil national under-20 football team|Brazil]], the only goal of the match.<ref>{{cite news|title=Polacy się nie przestraszyli|url=http://www.archiwum.wyborcza.pl/Archiwum/1,0,4901043,20070702RP-TSP,Polacy_sie_nie_przestraszyli,.html|publisher=Gazeta Wybocrcza|language=pl|date=2 July 2007|access-date=11 November 2020}}</ref> He was the youngest outfield player named to the Poland U20 squad.<ref name=confirmer/><ref name="Krychowiak Youth Club 1"/> He helped the U20 national side progress before being eliminated by [[Argentina national under-20 football team|Argentina U20]] in the round of 16 of the tournament.<ref>{{cite news|title=Freddy Adu i Danny Szetela rozgromili Polaków|url=http://www.archiwum.wyborcza.pl/Archiwum/1,0,4901269,20070704RP-DGW,Freddy_Adu_i_Danny_Szetela_rozgromili_Polakow,.html|publisher=Gazeta Wybocrcza|language=pl|date=3 July 2007|access-date=11 November 2020}}<br>{{cite news|title=Polacy przegrali z Argentyną i odpadli|url=http://www.archiwum.wyborcza.pl/Archiwum/1,0,4907676,20070713RP-DGW,Polacy_przegrali_z_Argentyna_i_odpadli,.html|publisher=Gazeta Wybocrcza|language=pl|date=16 July 2007|access-date=11 November 2020}}</ref> Krychowiak went on to make seven appearances and scoring once for the U20 side.

In May 2009, Krychowiak was called up to the [[Poland national under-21 football team|Poland U21 team]] for the first time.<ref>{{cite news|title=Powołania do kadry U-21|url=https://sport.onet.pl/pilka-nozna/kadra/powolania-do-kadry-u-21/8h8qc|publisher=Sport Onet|language=pl|date=22 May 2009|access-date=11 November 2020}}</ref> He made his U21 debut, playing the whole game in the centre-back position, in a 2–1 loss against [[Sweden national under-21 football team|Sweden U21]] on 5 June 2009.<ref>{{cite news|title=Szwedzka młodzieżówka lepsza od polskiej|url=https://sport.onet.pl/pilka-nozna/kadra/szwedzka-mlodziezowka-lepsza-od-polskiej/xd85q|publisher=Sport Onet|language=pl|date=5 June 2009|access-date=11 November 2020}}</ref>

Two years later, Krychowiak was called up to the U21 team for the first time.<ref>{{cite news|title=Polscy juniorzy pokonali Danię w sparingu|url=https://sport.onet.pl/pilka-nozna/kadra/polscy-juniorzy-pokonali-danie-w-sparingu/tszg3|publisher=Sport Onet|language=pl|date=10 September 2011|access-date=11 November 2020}}</ref> He his first appearances in a 3–0 win against [[Albania national under-21 football team|Albania U21]] on 2 September 2011.<ref>{{cite news|title=El. ME U-21: bardzo dobry początek Polaków|url=https://sport.onet.pl/pilka-nozna/kadra/el-me-u-21-bardzo-dobry-poczatek-polakow/v7t1q|publisher=Sport Onet|language=pl|date=2 September 2011|access-date=11 November 2020}}</ref> This was followed up by captaining the U21 side in a 2–0 loss against [[Russia national under-21 football team|Russia U21]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Polska - Rosja w el. MME: ambitna walka Biało-Czerwonych|url=https://sport.onet.pl/pilka-nozna/kadra/polska-rosja-w-el-mme-ambitna-walka-bialo-czerwonych/pxnf4|publisher=Sport Onet|language=pl|date=9 September 2011|access-date=11 November 2020}}</ref> On 11 October 2011, he scored his first Poland U21 goal, in a 4–3 win against Albania U21.<ref>{{cite news|title=EL.
