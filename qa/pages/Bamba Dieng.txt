'''Cheikh Ahmadou Bamba Mbacke Dieng''' (born 23 March 2000) is a Senegalese professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Forward (association football)|forward]] for [[Ligue 1]] club [[FC Lorient|Lorient]] and the [[Senegal national football team|Senegal national team]].

==Club career==
Dieng began his career with [[Diambars FC|Diambars]] in the [[Senegal Premier League]], and had 12 goals in his first 14 games in his debut season. He finished as the top scorer of the Senegal Premier League for the 2019–20 season.{{Citation needed|date=September 2021}}

Dieng signed for [[Olympique de Marseille|Marseille]] on 5 October 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.om.fr/fr/actualites/4806/centre-de-formation/42434-targhalline-et-dieng-rejoignent-le-centre-de-formation|title=Targhalline et Dieng rejoignent le centre de formation|website=OM}}</ref> He made his debut the club in a 2–0 [[Coupe de France]] win over [[AJ Auxerre|Auxerre]] on 10 February 2021, scoring a goal in the 92nd minute of the game.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.lequipe.fr/Football/match-direct/coupe-de-france/2020-2021/auxerre-om-live/507014|title=Résultat et résumé Auxerre - Marseille, Coupe de France, 32es de finale, Mercredi 10 Février 2021|website=L'Équipe}}</ref>

On 27 January 2023, Dieng signed a two-and-a-half-year contract for [[FC Lorient|Lorient]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Transferts : accord OM-Lorient pour Bamba Dieng |url=https://www.lequipe.fr/Football/Actualites/Transferts-accord-om-lorient-pour-bamba-dieng/1377090 |access-date=2023-01-28 |website=L'Équipe |language=fr}}</ref> 

==International career==
Dieng debuted with the [[Senegal national football team|Senegal national team]] in a 4–1 [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification]] win over [[Namibia national football team|Namibia]] on 9 October 2021.<ref name="FIFA 2021">{{cite web | title=FIFA | website=FIFA | date=9 October 2021 | url=https://www.fifa.com/tournaments/mens/worldcup/qatar2022/qualifiers/caf/match-center/400204263 | access-date=10 October 2021}}</ref>

He was called up to the Senegal squad for the [[2021 Africa Cup of Nations]] in January 2022, scoring his first goal for the side in a 2–0 round of 16 victory over [[Cape Verde national football team|Cape Verde]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.beinsports.com/us/afcon/video/mane-and-dieng-lead-senegal-into-quarterfinal/1821349|title=Mane and Dieng lead Senegal into quarterfinals after beating Cape Verde|work=[[beIN Sports]]|date=25 January 2022|access-date=25 January 2022}}</ref> In the [[2021 Africa Cup of Nations Final]], Dieng scored in the [[Penalty shoot-out (association football)|penalty shootout]] to help [[Senegal national football team|Senegal]] become the champions of the tournament.<ref>{{cite web |title=Senegal vs. Egypt Match Commentary |url=https://www.cafonline.com/total-africa-cup-of-nations/matches/2021/2243301/ |publisher=Confederation of African Football}}</ref>

He was appointed a [[National Order of the Lion|Grand Officer of the National Order of the Lion]] by [[President of Senegal]] [[Macky Sall]] following the nation's victory at the 2021 Africa Cup of Nations.<ref name= "reuters">{{cite web |title=Cash prizes, real estate, and highest honours as Senegal celebrates Nations Cup heroes |url=https://www.reuters.com/lifestyle/sports/cash-prizes-real-estate-highest-honours-senegal-celebrates-nations-cup-heroes-2022-02-08/ |website=[[Reuters]] |access-date=28 March 2023 |date=8 February 2022}}</ref>
