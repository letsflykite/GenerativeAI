'''Ernesto Alexis Vega Rojas''' (born 25 November 1997) is a Mexican professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Midfielder#Winger|winger]] for [[Liga MX]] club [[C.D. Guadalajara|Guadalajara]] and the [[Mexico national football team|Mexico national team]].<ref>[http://sub20.ligamx.net/cancha/jugador/109028/ernesto-alexis-vega-rojas Ernesto Vega Liga MX Profile]</ref>

==Club career==
===Toluca===
Vega made his senior team debut on 27 February 2016 coming on as a  substitute in a home [[Liga MX|league]] match against [[C.F. Pachuca|Pachuca]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Alexis Vega, contento de debutar con Toluca|date=2 July 2021|url=https://www.vavel.com/mx/futbol-mexicano/2016/03/05/toluca/615106-alexis-vega-contento-de-debutar-con-toluca.html}}</ref> On April 5 he scored his first professional goal for Toluca in the [[Copa Libertadores]] against Ecuadorian club [[L.D.U. Quito]], the game ended 2–1 and Toluca qualified to the tournament's [[2016 Copa Libertadores final stages|Round of 16]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Alexis Vega, motivado por su gol en Copa Libertadores|url=https://www.telemundodeportes.com/mexico/alexis-vega-motivado-por-su-gol-en-copa-libertadores}}</ref> On 16 April, Vega scored his first two goals in the league against [[C.D. Veracruz|Veracruz]].

===Guadalajara===
On 8 December 2018 it was announced that Vega was joining [[C.D. Guadalajara|Guadalajara]] for a reported $9 million, making his transfer one of the most expensive in the history of the club.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.futboltotal.com.mx/futbol-mexicano/liga-mx/alexis-vega-seria-presentado-con-chivas-el-sabado/2018/12|title=Alexis Vega sería presentado con Chivas el sábado|publisher= Staff FT|website= Futboltotal.com.mx |date=5 December 2018|language=es |access-date= 16 February 2019}}</ref>

After playing six games without any goals and going three straight games without any wins on 16 February 2019 Vega managed to score 3 goals in the 3–0 victory against [[Clásico Tapatío|town rivals]] [[Atlas F.C.|Atlas]], his first career [[hat-trick]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.fmfstateofmind.com/2019/2/16/18228091/chivas-earn-emphatic-clasico-tapatio-victory-3-0-atlas-behind-alexis-vega-hat-trick-highlights|title=Chivas earn emphatic Clasico Tapatio victory behind Alexis Vega hat-trick|publisher= Rafael Díaz |website= Fmfstateofmind.com|date=16 February 2019|access-date= 16 February 2019}}</ref>

Prior to the start of the [[2021–22 Liga MX season|2021–22 season]], Vega was given the number 10 jersey.<ref>{{cite web|title=Chivas: Vega será el nuevo número 10, Zaldívar hereda el 9 de Macías|date=21 July 2021|url=https://espndeportes.espn.com/futbol/mexico/nota/_/id/8934950/chivas-angel-zaldivar-numero-nueve-macias}}</ref>

==International career==
===Youth===
Vega was called up by [[Jaime Lozano]] to participate at the [[2020 CONCACAF Men's Olympic Qualifying Championship|2020 CONCACAF Olympic Qualifying Championship]], scoring one goal in five appearances, where Mexico won the competition. He was awarded the Golden Ball for best player<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.concacaf.com/olympics-qualifying-men/article/vega-earns-2020-cmoq-best-player-award-presented-by-scotiabank/|title=Vega earns 2020 CMOQ Best Player Award, presented by Scotiabank|publisher=CONCACAF|date=31 March 2021|access-date=31 March 2021}}</ref> and included in the tournament's Best XI.<ref name=CONCACAF>{{cite web|title=Ellos son los 4 mexicanos que encabezan el 11 ideal de Concacaf del Preolímpico 2021|url=https://soyreferee.com/futbolinternacional/Ellos-son-los-4-mexicanos-que-encabezan-el-11-ideal-de-Concacaf-del-Preolimpico-2021-20210331-0016.html}}</ref> He was subsequently called up to participate in the [[2020 Summer Olympics]]. Vega won the [[bronze medal]] with the Olympic team.<ref>{{cite web|title=Revelan lista oficial de la Selección Sub-23 que acudirá a Tokio 2020|date=5 July 2021|url=https://espndeportes.espn.com/futbol/selecciones/nota/_/id/8854207/mexico-sub23-convocatoria-jaime-lozano-tokio-2020}}</ref>

===Senior===
Vega made his [[Mexico national football team|senior national team]] debut on 26 March 2019 under [[Gerardo Martino]] in a friendly against [[Paraguay national football team|Paraguay]], as a 78th-minute substitute for [[Javier Hernández]].<ref>{{cite web|work=[[ESPN]]|url=http://www.espn.com/soccer/match?gameId=532880|title=Mexico v Paraguay game report|date=26 March 2019}}</ref>

In May 2019, Vega was included in the provisional [[2019 CONCACAF Gold Cup]] roster<ref>{{cite web|title=Tata Martino Announces Mexico Preliminary Gold Cup Squad; Vela, Chicharito, Herrera, Corona left out|url=https://www.fmfstateofmind.com/2019/5/14/18534851/mexico-el-tri-concacaf-gold-cup-copa-oro-squad-final-list-preliminary-corona-vela-chicharito-herrera|work=Fmfstateofmind.com|first=Antonio|last=Tinajero|date=14 May 2019}}</ref> and was subsequently included in the final list.<ref>{{cite web|title=Selección Mexicana anuncia lista definitiva para Copa Oro 2019|url=https://www.mediotiempo.com/futbol/copa-oro/seleccion-mexicana-anuncia-lista-definitiva-copa-oro-2019|date=6 June 2019|work=MedioTiempo.com}}</ref> In the first [[2019 CONCACAF Gold Cup Group A|group stage]] match against [[Cuba national football team|Cuba]], he would score his first goal with the national team in a 7–0 thrashing.<ref>{{cite web|title=México 7-0 Cuba: goles, resumen y videos|url=https://www.goal.com/es-mx/noticias/mexico-vs-cuba-goles-resumen-y-videos/7vna2lpkd3rr15sa06pez53sm|work=[[Goal (website)|Goal.com]]|language=es|date=15 June 2019}}</ref> Mexico would go on to win the tournament.<ref>{{cite web|title=Mexico Turns Tide, Wins Gold Cup Title Again vs. Wasteful USMNT|url=https://www.si.com/soccer/2019/07/07/mexico-usa-gold-cup-title-dos-santos-pizarro-pulisic-berhalter|publisher=[[Sports Illustrated]]|first=Brian|last=Straus|date=7 July 2019}}</ref>

In October 2022, Vega was named in Mexico's preliminary 31-man [[Mexico national football team|squad]] for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]], and in November, he was ultimately included in the final 26-man roster.<ref>{{cite web|title=Lista de convocados de la Selección Mexicana al Mundial de Qatar 2022|url=https://www.tvazteca.com/aztecadeportes/lista-oficial-convocados-seleccion-mexicana-mundial-qatar-2022|work=Tvazteca.com|first=Omar|last=Moro|date=14 November 2022|language=Spanish}}</ref>
