'''Gideon Mensah''' (born 18 July 1998) is a Ghanaian professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[left-back]] for French {{French football updater|Auxerre}} club [[AJ Auxerre|Auxerre]]. He represents the [[Ghana national football team|Ghana national team]].

==Club career==

===Red Bull Salzburg===
In January 2019, Mensah joined [[SK Sturm Graz|Sturm Graz]] on loan until the end of the season.<ref>{{cite web|title=GIDEON MENSAH JOINS STURM GRAZ|url=https://www.redbullsalzburg.at/en/fc-red-bull-salzburg/news/saison_2018_19/gideon-mensah-joins-sturm-graz.html|publisher=Red Bull Salzburg|language=en}}</ref> On 29 August 2019, he was then loaned out to Belgian club [[S.V. Zulte Waregem|Zulte Waregem]] for the 2019–20 season.<ref>[https://www.goal.com/en/news/gideon-mensah-ghanaian-fullback-joins-zulte-waregem-from-red/1imbx0qvmb5t11ee0i6wzqxm8w Gideon Mensah: Ghanaian fullback joins Zulte Waregem from Red Bull Salzburg], goal.com, 29 August 2019</ref>

==== Loan to Bordeaux ====
On 3 August 2021, Mensah joined [[FC Girondins de Bordeaux|Bordeaux]] on a season-long loan with an option to buy until the end of the [[2021–22 Ligue 1]] season.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Gideon Mensah rejoint le Club|url=https://girondins.com//news/32332/gideon-mensah-rejoint-le-club|access-date=3 August 2021|website=Girondins.com}}</ref>

===Auxerre===
On 12 August 2022, Mensah signed a three-year contract with [[AJ Auxerre|Auxerre]].<ref>{{cite web|publisher=Auxerre|url=https://www.aja.fr/gideon-mensah-est-auxerrois|title=GIDEON MENSAH EST AUXERROIS !|date=12 August 2022|access-date=16 August 2022|language=fr}}</ref>

== International career ==
Mensah made his debut for the [[Ghana national football team|Ghana national team]] in the 2021 AFCON qualifier against south Africa in 2019<ref>{{Cite web|last=Sienu|first=Sheikh Tophic|date=6 September 2021|title=WCQ 2022: Gideon Mensah earns Ghana start against South Africa|url=https://footballmadeinghana.com/2021/09/06/wcq-2022-gideon-mensah-earns-ghana-start-against-south-africa/|access-date=26 January 2022|website=Football Made In Ghana|language=en-US}}</ref>

He was part of the Ghana national team in the [[2021 Africa Cup of Nations]] that was eliminated at the group stage of the competition.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Gideon Mensah returns to Bordeaux after Ghana's exit from Afcon|url=https://footballghana.com/gideon-mensah-returns-to-bordeaux-after-ghanas-exit-from-afcon|access-date=26 January 2022|website=Footballghana|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|title=Ghana dumped out of Afcon by Comoros|language=en-GB|work=BBC Sport|url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/59947164|access-date=26 January 2022}}</ref>
