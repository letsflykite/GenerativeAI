'''Rouzbeh Cheshmi''' ({{lang-fa|روزبه چشمی}}; born 24 June 1993) is an Iranian professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Midfielder#Defensive midfielder|defensive midfielder]] or a [[Defender (association football)|centre-back]] for [[Persian Gulf Pro League]] club [[Esteghlal F.C.|Esteghlal]] and the [[Iran national football team|Iran national team]].<ref>[http://www.varzesh3.com/news.do?itemid=976708&title= دعوت 27 بازيکن به اردوي تيم فوتبال جوانان] {{in lang|fa}}</ref>

==Early life==
Cheshmi was born in [[Tehran]] to a family originally from [[Chesham, Iran|Chesham]] in [[Sabzevar]].<ref name= "Interview">{{cite web|url=http://persianv.com/maharat/روزبه-چشمی-،-پدیده-ای-تازه-در-فوتبال-،-ا.html|title=روزبه چشمی، پدیده‌ای تازه در فوتبال، از زندگی شخصی و کاری اش می‌گوید|trans-title=Rouzbeh Cheshmi talks about his personal life|language=fa}}</ref>

==Club career==
===Early career===
Cheshmi began his career with [[Paykan F.C.|Paykan]], before moving to [[Persepolis F.C.|Persepolis]] in 2008. In 2011, he was loaned to [[Moghavemat Tehran F.C.|Moghavemat Tehran]] to complete his mandatory military service.<ref name= "Bio">{{cite web|url=https://www.tarafdari.com/خبر/404885/چشمی-الگویم-پوگباست؛-بخاطر-استقلال-قید-بوندسلیگا-را-زدم|title=چشمی: الگویم پوگباست؛ بخاطر استقلال قید بوندسلیگا را زدم|trans-title=Cheshmi: Pogba is my idol; Gave up playing in Bundesliga to play for Esteghlal|language=fa}}</ref> In 2013, Cheshmi handed a transfer request after [[Ali Daei]] asked him to have a trial with Persepolis' senior team and moved to [[Saba Qom F.C.|Saba Qom]].<ref name="Bio"/> In his first season with Saba, Cheshmi made 25 appearances and scored one goal.

===Esteghlal===
Cheshmi signed a two-year deal with [[Esteghlal F.C.|Esteghlal]] on 30 June 2015.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.tarafdari.com/خبر/350655/روزبه-چشمی-رسما-به-آبی-پوشان-پیوست-عکس|title=روزبه چشمی رسما به آبی پوشان پیوست (عکس)|trans-title=Rouzbeh Cheshmi officially joined the blues|language=fa}}</ref> He later revealed that he down offers from a [[Bundesliga]] club to join Esteghlal.<ref name="Bio"/><ref name="Interview"/> Upon his arrival, he chose to wear number 4 which was previously worn by [[Amir Hossein Sadeghi]].<ref name="Arrival">{{cite web|url=https://www.tarafdari.com/خبر/359961/چشمی-از-حضور-در-استقلال-خوشحالم؛-شماره-چهار-را-می-پوشم|title=چشمی: از حضور در استقلال خوشحالم؛ شماره چهار را می پوشم|trans-title=Cheshmi: Happy to join Esteghlal; I will wear number four|language=fa}}</ref>

[[File:Rozbeh Cheshmi vs Pars.jpg|thumb|220px|left|Cheshmi (left) playing against [[F.C. Pars Jonoubi Jam|Pars Jonoubi Jam]] in 2017.]]
Cheshmi made his debut for Esteghlal on 30 July 2015 against [[Siah Jamegan F.C.|Siah Jamegan]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.tarafdari.com/خبر/367762/سیاه-جامگان-مشهد-1-2-استقلال-تهران؛-افتتاحیه-سه-امتیازی-آبی-ها|title=سیاه جامگان مشهد 1 - 2 استقلال تهران؛ افتتاحیه سه امتیازی آبی ها|language=fa}}</ref> On 3 March 2015, he torn his Cruciate ligament in a match against [[Padideh F.C.|Padideh]] which caused him to miss the rest of the season.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.tarafdari.com/خبر/453518/رسمی؛-روزبه-چشمی-رباط-صلیبی-پاره-کرد|title=رسمی؛ روزبه چشمی رباط صلیبی پاره کرد|trans-title= official; Cheshmi torn his Cruciate ligament|language=fa}}</ref> It was also reported that he will miss the first half of [[2016–17 Esteghlal F.C. season|2016–17 season]] and the team coach [[Alireza Mansourian]] decided to exclude his name from Esteghlal's list for the first half of the season and was reported that he would return to the squad in January.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://11news.ir/fa/news/4823/خارج-شدن-روزبه-چشمی-از-لیست-استقلال|title=خارج شدن روزبه چشمی از لیست استقلال!|language=fa}}</ref> On 29 June 2016, he signed a 3-year contract extension with Esteghlal.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.tarafdari.com/خبر/504992/چشمی-سه-فصل-دیگر-با-استقلال-تمدید-کرد|title=چشمی سه فصل دیگر با استقلال تمدید کرد|trans-title=Cheshmi extended his contract for another 3 years|language=fa}}</ref> He returned to training on 30 December 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.varzesh3.com/news/1383637/بازگشت-چشمی-به-تمرین-استقلال|title=بازگشت چشمی به تمرین استقلال|trans-title=Cheshmi's return to training|publisher=Varzesh3|language=fa|date= 30 December 2016|access-date= 21 March 2018}}</ref> However, due to the club's transfer ban which did not allow it to register any new players, Esteghlal could not include his name in their squad for the rest of season and meant that he would not be able to play for Esteghlal until the end of the season.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.tarafdari.com/خبر/634722/استقلال-به-یک-شرط-می%E2%80%8Cتواند-3-بازیکن-آزاد-جذب-کند؛-چشمی-هم-قادر-به-ثبت-قرارداد-با-استقلال|title=استقلال به یک شرط می‌تواند 3 بازیکن آزاد جذب کند؛ چشمی هم قادر به ثبت قرارداد با استقلال نیست|publisher=Tarafdari|language=fa|access-date=21 March 2018}}</ref>

Cheshmi scored his first goal for the club against [[Zob Ahan Esfahan F.C.|Zob Ahan]] in a 1–1 draw on 20 September 2017.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.tarafdari.com/خبر/803608/استقلال-1-1-ذوب-آهن؛-تساوی-با-طعم-شکست-برای-منصوریان|title=استقلال 1-1 ذوب آهن؛ تساوی با طعم شکست برای منصوریان|trans-title=Esteghlal 1–1 Zob Ahan|language=fa|date=20 September 2017|access-date=19 November 2017}}</ref> On 13 February 2018, Cheshmi made his [[AFC Champions League]] debut in a 2–2 group stage draw with Qatari club [[Al-Rayyan SC|Al-Rayyan]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.varzesh3.com/news/1504890/اولین-تجربه-آسیایی-چشمی-و-شجاعیان-برابر-الریان|title=اولین تجربه آسیایی چشمی و شجاعیان برابر الریان|trans-title=First Asian experience for Cheshmi and Shojaeian|publisher=Varzesh3|language=fa|date=13 February 2018|access-date=14 February 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.varzesh3.com/news/1504921/گزارش-بازی-الریان-و-استقلال-تاریخ-1396-11-24|title=الریان 2 - استقلال 2؛ برد از دستمان پر زد|trans-title=Al-Rayyan 2 - Esteghlal 2|publisher=Varzesh3|language=fa|date=13 February 2018|access-date=14 February 2018}}</ref>

==International career==
===U17===
He played 3 games at the 2010 Asian U16 Championships.

===U20===
He is part of [[Iran national under-20 football team|Iran U–20]] during [[2012 AFC U-19 Championship qualification]], [[2012 CIS Cup]], [[2012 AFF U-19 Youth Championship]] and [[2012 AFC U-19 Championship]].

===U23===
He invited to [[Iran national under-23 football team|Iran U-23]] training camp by [[Nelo Vingada]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://isport.ir/fa/content/47518/%D9%84%DB%8C%D8%B3%D8%AA-%DB%B3%DB%B1-%D9%86%D9%81%D8%B1%D9%87-%D9%88%DB%8C%D9%86%DA%AF%D8%A7%D8%AF%D8%A7-%D8%A8%D8%B1%D8%A7%DB%8C-%D8%AA%DB%8C%D9%85-%D9%85%D9%84%DB%8C-%D8%A7%D9%85%DB%8C%D8%AF|title = لیست ۳۱ نفره وینگادا برای تیم ملی امید}}</ref> to preparation for [[Football at the 2014 Asian Games|Incheon 2014]] and 2016 AFC U-22 Championship (Summer Olympic qualification). He named in [[Iran national under-23 football team|Iran U23]] final list for [[Football at the 2014 Asian Games|Incheon 2014]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://ffiri.ir/news/detail/%D8%A8%D8%A7%D8%B2%DB%8C%DA%A9%D9%86%D8%A7%D9%86-%D8%A7%D8%B9%D8%B2%D8%A7%D9%85%DB%8C-%D8%AA%DB%8C%D9%85-%D9%85%D9%84%DB%8C-%D8%A7%D9%85%DB%8C%D8%AF-%D8%A8%D9%87-%D8%A7%DB%8C%D9%86%DA%86%D8%A6%D9%88%D9%86-%D9%85%D8%B4%D8%AE%D8%B5-%D8%B4%D8%AF/117027/news.aspx|title=بازیکنان اعزامی تیم ملی امید به اینچئون مشخص شد|publisher=ffiri.ir|access-date=12 August 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140819231500/http://ffiri.ir/news/detail/%D8%A8%D8%A7%D8%B2%DB%8C%DA%A9%D9%86%D8%A7%D9%86-%D8%A7%D8%B9%D8%B2%D8%A7%D9%85%DB%8C-%D8%AA%DB%8C%D9%85-%D9%85%D9%84%DB%8C-%D8%A7%D9%85%DB%8C%D8%AF-%D8%A8%D9%87-%D8%A7%DB%8C%D9%86%DA%86%D8%A6%D9%88%D9%86-%D9%85%D8%B4%D8%AE%D8%B5-%D8%B4%D8%AF/117027/news.aspx|archive-date=19 August 2014|url-status=dead}}</ref>

===Senior===
[[File:2018 FIFA World Cup Group B march IRN-MAR 25.jpg|thumbnail|Cheshmi (right) playing for Iran, against [[Morocco national football team|Morocco]] in the [[2018 FIFA World Cup]].]]
Cheshmi was called up for the first time for the [[Iran national football team|Iran senior team]] in August 2017 by coach [[Carlos Queiroz]] for Iran's [[2018 FIFA World Cup qualifying]] matches against [[South Korea national football team|South Korea]] and [[Syria national football team|Syria]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.varzesh3.com/news/1457050/اعلام-اسامی-بازیکنان-تیم-ملی-برای-دیدار-با-کره-و-سوریه|title=اعلام اسامی بازیکنان تیم ملی برای دیدار با کره و سوریه|language=fa}}</ref> He made his official debut as a substitute in 0–0 draw against [[South Korea national football team|South Korea]] on 31 August 2017.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.tarafdari.com/خبر/787476/کره-جنوبی-0-0-ایران؛-یوزها-همچنان-حریفی-در-آسیا-ندارند|title=کره جنوبی 0-0 ایران؛ یوزها همچنان حریفی در آسیا ندارند|trans-title=South Korea 0–0 Iran; Cheetas have no competitor in Asia|language=fa|date=31 August 2017|access-date=10 November 2017}}</ref>

In May 2018, Cheshmi was named in Iran's preliminary [[Iran national football team|squad]] for the [[2018 FIFA World Cup]] in Russia.<ref>{{cite web|title= World Cup 2018: All the confirmed squads for this summer's finals in Russia|url= https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/440833652018}} {{Dead link|date=June 2018}}</ref> He made his debut in the competition on 15 June, playing the whole 90 minutes of the 1–0 group stage win against [[Morocco national football team|Morocco]], helping his team to win their first World Cup match since 1998.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/44408322|title= Iran won their first match at a World Cup finals since 1998 as Morocco substitute Aziz Bouhaddouz scored an own goal in injury time.|work=BBC Sport|date=15 June 2018|access-date=16 June 2018}}</ref> After helping Iran to keep a clean sheet in their opening win over Morocco, the World Cup turned out to be a personal disappointment for him, as he suffered an injury during training session prior the game against the [[Spain national football team|Spain]], which ruled him out of the competition.<ref>{{Cite web|url = https://en.mehrnews.com/news/134909/Iran-s-Cheshmi-misses-2018-World-Cup|title = Iran's Cheshmi misses 2018 World Cup|date = 18 June 2018|access-date = 19 June 2018|publisher = [[Mehr News Agency|MNA]]}}</ref>

Cheshmi was also selected for [[2019 AFC Asian Cup]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.ffiri.ir/en/news/detail/-FINAL-REGISTRATION-CALL-UP-LIST/186009/news.aspx|title=FINAL REGISTRATION CALL UP LIST|publisher=[[Football Federation Islamic Republic of Iran|FFIRI]]|date=26 December 2018|access-date=27 December 2018}}</ref>
He was in the initial lineup during the match of the 2022 Qatar World Cup against England. He scored the winning goal in the match against Wales in injury time, before [[Ramin Rezaeian]] doubled the lead minutes later, thus giving Iran their first victory against a European team at the World Cup.
