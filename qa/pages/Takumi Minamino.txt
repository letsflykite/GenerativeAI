{{Nihongo|'''Takumi Minamino'''|南野 拓実|Minamino Takumi|born 16 January 1995}} is a Japanese professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as an [[attacking midfielder]] or [[Winger (association football)|winger]] for [[Ligue 1]] club [[AS Monaco FC|Monaco]] and the [[Japan national football team|Japan national team]].

Minamino began his club career at [[J.League Division 1]] club [[Cerezo Osaka]] in 2012 and in his first season he was named [[J.League Rookie of the Year]]. In 2014 he moved to Austrian club [[FC Red Bull Salzburg|Red Bull Salzburg]] with whom he spent four successful seasons. Following impressive displays for the club he joined Liverpool on 1 January 2020, winning the [[2019–20 Premier League|Premier League]] in his inaugural season.

Making his senior international debut for Japan in 2015, Minamino starred in the team that reached the [[2019 AFC Asian Cup Final|final]] of the [[2019 AFC Asian Cup]], also playing at the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].

==Early life==
Minamino was born in [[Izumisano]], [[Osaka Prefecture]].<ref>{{cite news |url=https://en.as.com/en/2020/01/05/football/1578226929_849019.html |title=Liverpool newboy Takumi Minamino survived the Great Hashin earthquake as a new-born baby in Japan |first=Alex |last=Del Río |newspaper=Diario AS |location=Madrid |date=5 January 2020 |access-date=21 August 2020}}</ref> While at elementary school, he played for the local club Sessel Kumatori.<ref name="Youth"/><ref>{{cite news |title=生粋のサッカー好きにして負けず嫌い 幼なじみ・室屋成が語る「南野拓実の素顔」 |url=https://news.livedoor.com/article/detail/15614558/ |website=News Live Door |language=ja |date=18 November 2018 |access-date=22 December 2019}}</ref> Growing up, he was inspired by the finishing and dribbling of Brazilian striker [[Ronaldo (Brazilian footballer)|Ronaldo]], who was the top scorer at the [[2002 FIFA World Cup]] which Japan co-hosted when Minamino was seven.<ref name="Youth">{{cite news |title=Takumi Minamino: Liverpool new signing is 'one of a kind' inspired by Ronaldo |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/50850594 |access-date=19 December 2019 |website=BBC Sport}}</ref> He would watch videos of Ronaldo's feints, and would go outside to practice. He was also inspired by his older brother Kenta, and their father would put cones down in a car park for them to dribble in and out of at speed.<ref name="Youth"/> He attended Osaka-Kokoku High School.<ref>{{cite news |title=ＭＦ南野、高校時代からみていた「世界」 |url=http://www.nikkansports.com/soccer/japan/news/1673883.html |website=Nikkan Sports |language=ja |date=6 July 2016 |access-date=22 December 2019}}</ref> Minamino described himself as an "active child" growing up.<ref>{{cite news |title=幼稚園時代に出会った南野拓実選手と室屋成選手。"幼馴染"のふたりが築いた最高のライバル関係 |url=https://jr-soccer.jp/2016/07/29/post51285/ |website=JR Soccer |language=ja |date=29 July 2016 |access-date=22 December 2019}}</ref>

==Style of play==
[[File:FC Red Bull Salzburg versus FK Austria Wien (11. August 2018) 03.jpg|thumb|upright|Minamino playing for Red Bull Salzburg 2018]]

At Salzburg, Minamino was played in a fluid attacking role that allowed him to move freely and 'unlock doors, pick a pass and move with an elegant gait.'<ref>{{cite web |last1=Green |first1=Ben |title=Takumi Minamino: Liverpool target "archetypal Klopp player" in bargain deal |url=https://www.japantimes.co.jp/sports/2020/11/14/soccer/japan-panama-takumi-minamino/ |website=Squawka |date=14 November 2020 |publisher=Catena Media |access-date=18 November 2020}}</ref>

His comparatively slight frame has been described as an 'advantage' as his low centre-of-gravity means he can 'run past players with ease.'<ref>{{cite web |last1=Abdullah |first1=Abdullah |title=Tactical Analysis: Red Bull Salzburg's Takumi Minamino |url=https://totalfootballanalysis.com/player-analysis/takumi-minamino-austrian-bundesliga-fc-salzburg-tactical-analysis |website=totalfootballanalysis.com |date=14 December 2018 |publisher=Total Football Analysis |access-date=18 November 2020}}</ref>

Liverpool manager [[Jürgen Klopp]] has heaped praise on him, saying, prior to him signing for Liverpool, 'I'd like to have Minamino in Liverpool. He's classy. The best talent.'<ref>{{cite web |title=Klopp downplays Liverpool's interest in Haland |url=https://www.besoccer.com/new/klopp-downplays-liverpool-s-interest-in-haland-724011 |website=BeSoccer |publisher=BeSoccer.com |access-date=18 November 2020}}</ref> After a game against Ajax in the Champions League, Klopp said 'He's everywhere, he defends like a devil and then offensively he's really involved.'<ref>{{cite web |title=Jurgen Klopp praises 'machine' Takumi Minamino after Liverpool's win over Ajax |url=https://metro.co.uk/2020/10/22/jurgen-klopp-praises-machine-takumi-minamino-after-liverpools-win-over-ajax-13461859/ |website=Metro |location=UK |date=22 October 2020 |access-date=18 November 2020}}</ref>
