'''Anthony William Hernández González''' (born 11 October 2001) is a Costa Rican professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[forward (association football)|forward]] for [[Liga FPD]] club [[Puntarenas F.C.|Puntarenas]] and the [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica national team]].

==Club career==
Hernández started playing for Puntarenas in 2020. In May 2022, Puntarenas beat Carmelita 3–0 on aggregate to earn promotion from [[Segunda División de Costa Rica]] to Costa Rica’s top flight for the first time since 2014. Hernández made his debut in the top flight on 20 July 2022 as his side won 3–0 against [[A.D. San Carlos]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.infobae.com/noticias/2022/07/20/puntarenas-y-ad-san-carlos-se-encuentran-en-la-fecha-1/|title=Puntarenas beat AD San Carlos 3-0|website=infobae.com}}</ref> He scored his first professional goal on 11 August 2022, in a 3–1 win against [[Municipal Grecia]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hoyeneldeportecr.com/puntarenas-vencio-a-grecia-y-sigue-firmo-como-lider-del-grupo-b/|title=Puntarenas venció a Grecia y sigue firme como lider del Grupo B|website=hoyeneldeportecr.com|date=11 August 2022 }}</ref>

==International career==
Born in Costa Rica, Hernández is of Jamaican descent.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rsqNSuRu2rQ|title=How many players of Jamaican Heritage will be at the World Cup?|via=www.youtube.com}}</ref> On 23 August 2022 he made his debut for the [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica national team]] against [[South Korea national football team|South Korea]], coming on in the 65th minute for [[Jewison Bennette]] in a 2–2 draw.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://everardoherrera.com/index.php/futbol-costa-rica-5/94094-costa-rica-iguala-2-2-frente-a-una-exigente-corea-del-sur|title= Costa Rica draws 2-2 against a demanding South Korea|website=everadoherrera.com}}</ref> Hernández scored his first goal for Costa Rica on 27 September 2022 against Uzbekistan.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ticotimes.net/2022/10/11/costa-rica-wins-2-1-in-pre-world-cup-match-with-uzbekistan|title=Costa Rica Wins 2-1 in Pre-World Cup Match with Uzbekistan|website=Ticotimes.net|date=11 October 2022 }}</ref> He was named in the Costa Rica squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.espn.com.mx/futbol/costa-rica/nota/_/id/11184697/anthony-hernandez-incredulo-joel-campbell-cuando-dijo-estaba-mundial%3Fplatform%3D|title=Anthony Hernández fue incrédulo hasta de Joel Campbell cuando le dijo que estaba en Mundial|website=ESPN.com|date=5 November 2022 }}</ref>
