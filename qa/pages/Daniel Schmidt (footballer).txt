{{nihongo|'''Daniel Schmidt'''|シュミット・ダニエル|Shumitto Danieru|born 3 February 1992}} is a Japanese professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Goalkeeper (association football)|goalkeeper]] in the [[Belgian Pro League]] for club  [[Sint-Truidense V.V.|Sint-Truiden]].

==Early life==
Born in the [[United States]] to a [[German Americans|German]] father and Japanese mother, Schmidt moved to [[Sendai]], [[Japan]] at the age of two. He attended [[Chuo University]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.nikkansports.com/soccer/news/p-sc-tp1-20131217-1232540.html|title = Ｊ最長身ダニエル仙台で定位置奪取だ - Ｊ１ニュース}}</ref> <ref>{{Cite web|url= https://www.eurosport.de/fussball/wm/2022/japan-torwart-daniel-schmidt-im-interview_sto9227692/story.shtml|title = WM 2022 - JAPAN-TORWART DANIEL SCHMIDT IM INTERVIEW: "WIR HABEN EINE CHANCE GEGEN DEUTSCHLAND"}}</ref>

==Style of play==
Schmidt is known for his ability with long balls to forwards and his frequency of coming off his line in precise timing to deal with threats early. He is also credited as a good penalty saver.{{Citation needed|date=November 2022}}

==Club career==
Schmidt played three consecutive seasons as a [[J.League designated special players|Designated Special Player]] for [[Kawasaki Frontale]] while at the [[Chuo University]], before entering the professional ranks in 2013.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.frontale.co.jp/info/2012/0323_1.html|title = 川崎フロンターレ オフィシャルWebサイト}}</ref>

He later signed for [[Vegalta Sendai]],<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.vegalta.co.jp/news/press_release/2013/11/post-1195.html|title=バックナンバー2013 &#124; 中央大学 シュミット ダニエル選手 2014シーズン新加入内定のお知らせ}}</ref> but Schmidt played his first pro match during a one-month loan to [[Roasso Kumamoto]] in April 2014.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://roasso-k.com/news/news_view.htm?id=1027|title = トップページ｜ロアッソ熊本 公式サイト｜Roasso Kumamoto Official Website}}</ref> After playing his first ever game with Vegalta in [[J. League Cup]], he was again loaned to Roasso, this time until the end of [[2015 J2 League|2015 season]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://roasso-k.com/news/news_view.htm?id=1926|title = トップページ｜ロアッソ熊本 公式サイト｜Roasso Kumamoto Official Website}}</ref> A third loan was established in [[2016 J2 League|2016]], this time to [[Matsumoto Yamaga]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.yamaga-fc.com/archives/43393|title=シュミット ダニエル選手 ベガルタ仙台から 期限付移籍加入のお知らせ|date=24 December 2015}}</ref>

In 2019, he finally transferred overseas to Sint-Truiden in Belgium.

On 4 February 2023, he made his 100th appearance in the Jupiler Pro League against [[K.V. Kortrijk]].

==International career==
On 30 August 2018, Schmidt received his first international callup from the [[Japan national football team]] for the [[Kirin Cup|Kirin Challenge Cup]] 2018.<ref>{{cite web|title=SAMURAI BLUE (Japan National Team) Squad, Schedule - KIRIN CHALLENGE CUP 2018【9/7＠Hokkaido vs Chile】【9/11＠Osaka vs Costa Rica】|url=http://www.jfa.jp/eng/samuraiblue/news/00018623/|date=30 August 2018|work=jfa.jp|accessdate=30 August 2018}}</ref> He made his debut appearance in a friendly match against [[Venezuela national football team|Venezuela]] on 16 November 2018 and played his first competitive match for Japan on 17 January 2019 in the 2-1 win against [[Uzbekistan national football team|Uzbekistan]] during the [[2019 AFC Asian Cup]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://stats.the-afc.com/match_report/13289|title = AFCS}}</ref>

In September 2022, he saved the team from a desperate pinch by stopping the penalty against [[Ecuador national football team|Ecuador]] and was selected as MOM of the media.<ref>{{Cite web |date=28 September 2022 |title=Scoring by everyone!! Ecuador and Moriyasu J who scored a scoreless draw, the highest evaluation was... |url=https://news.yahoo.co.jp/articles/853cf945040577021f4d654c18d7b19453f2411e}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |date=27 September 2022 |title=[Japan 0-0 Ecuador {{!}} Scoring & Commentary] |url=https://news.yahoo.co.jp/articles/e842951a1972ac110ccde68b771e828e6e632ca3}}</ref> It was also the number one trend on Twitter.<ref>{{Cite web |date=28 September 2022 |title="Schmidt" acclaimed as No. 1 in SNS trends! |url=https://www.nikkansports.com/soccer/japan/news/202209270001743.html}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |date=28 September 2022 |title=[Japan National Team] "Schmidt" tops Twitter trend |url=https://www.football-zone.net/archives/405646}}</ref>

In November 2022, he was selected for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] team for the first time.<ref>{{Cite web |date=2022-12-08 |title=SAMURAI BLUE (Japan National Team) squad - FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022™ |url=http://www.jfa.jp/eng/news/00030869/}}</ref>
