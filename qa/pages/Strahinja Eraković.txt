'''Strahinja Eraković''' ({{lang-sr-Cyrl|Страхиња Ераковић}}; born 22 January 2001) is a [[Serbia]]n [[Association football|football]]er who plays as a 
[[centre back]] for Serbian club [[Red Star Belgrade]].

==Club career==
===Red Star Belgrade===
Strahinja Eraković joined Red Star Belgrade in 2010 as a nine-year-old. He played for every youth category for the Belgrade club<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AHX1pmQEcMo |title=Meet Strahinja Erakovic |publisher=FK Crvena zvezda|access-date=4 February 2018}}</ref> and was the captain of the Under-19 side. He appeared for the club in the [[UEFA Youth League]] in matches against [[S.S.C. Napoli|Napoli]]<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.uefa.com/uefayouthleague/match/2025557--napoli-vs-crvena-zvezda/ |title=UEFA Youth League vs. Napoli |publisher=UEFA Youth League|access-date=28 November 2018}}</ref> and [[Paris Saint-Germain F.C.|Paris Saint-Germain]].

He made his first-team debut for Red Star Belgrade on 1 August 2020 in a [[Serbian SuperLiga|Superliga]] match against [[FK Novi Pazar|Novi Pazar]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://superliga.rs/sezone/2020-21/match/2004 |title=Eraković makes his Crvena zvezda debut |publisher=Serbian SuperLiga|access-date=1 August 2020}}</ref> Despite being just 19, he quickly became a first-team regular for the champions of Serbia. In his first season with the senior side, he played against [[TSG 1899 Hoffenheim|Hoffenheim]],<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.uefa.com/uefaeuropaleague/match/2029727--hoffenheim-vs-crvena-zvezda/ |title=UEFA Europa League vs. Hoffenheim|publisher=UEFA Europa League|access-date=22 October 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.uefa.com/uefaeuropaleague/match/2029811--crvena-zvezda-vs-hoffenheim/ |title=UEFA Europa League vs. Hoffenheim|publisher=UEFA Europa League|access-date=3 December 2021}}</ref> [[K.A.A. Gent|Gent]]<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.uefa.com/uefaeuropaleague/match/2029799--gent-vs-crvena-zvezda/ |title=UEFA Europa League vs. Gent|publisher=UEFA Europa League|access-date=26 November 2021}}</ref> and [[FC Slovan Liberec|Slovan Liberec]]<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.uefa.com/uefaeuropaleague/match/2029848--liberec-vs-crvena-zvezda/ |title=UEFA Europa League vs. Liberec|publisher=UEFA Europa League|access-date=10 December 2021}}</ref> in the [[UEFA Europa League]]. He was a part of the Red Star Belgrade side that won the first league<ref>{{cite web |url=http://crvenazvezdafk.com/en/vest/11378/crvena-zvezda-vojvodina-1-0-1-0 |title=Red Star wins the title|publisher=Red Star Belgrade |access-date=29 April 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://superliga.rs/vesti/2845-ceremonija-dodele-pehara-i-medalja-na-stadionu-rajko-mitic |title=Red Star wins the title |publisher=Serbian SuperLiga |access-date=19 May 2021}}</ref> and cup double<ref>{{cite web |url=http://crvenazvezdafk.com/en/vest/11528/crvena-zvezda-je-osvojila-kup-srbije |title=Crvena zvezda wins the league and cup double |publisher=Red Star Belgrade |access-date=25 May 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://fss.rs/penal-rulet-stao-na-crveno-crvena-zvezda-posle-devet-godina-osvojila-kup-srbije/ |title=Crvena zvezda wins the league and cup double |publisher=Football Association of Serbia |access-date=25 May 2021}}</ref> after a 14-year wait.

In the 2021/22 campaign, he became one of the most important players<ref>{{cite web |url=https://sportal.blic.rs/fudbal/domaci-fudbal/ocene-zvezde-erakovic-je-igrac-za-a-tim-srbije-izgubljena-najjaca-zvezdina-osovina-i-italijanski-popravni-stankovica/2022050822344340314 |title=Eraković named man of the match in the Eternal Derby|publisher=Blic|access-date=16 April 2022}}</ref> for the team and ranked among the players with most minutes spent on the pitch. He was a first-team regular in European competition as well, playing against [[S.C. Braga|Braga]],<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.uefa.com/uefaeuropaleague/match/2033261--braga-vs-crvena-zvezda/ |title=UEFA Europa League vs. SC Braga|publisher=UEFA Europa League|access-date=9 December 2021}}</ref> [[FC Midtjylland|Midtjylland]]<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.uefa.com/uefaeuropaleague/match/2033229--crvena-zvezda-vs-midtjylland/ |title=UEFA Europa League vs. Midtjylland |publisher=UEFA Europa League|access-date=4 November 2021}}</ref> and [[PFC Ludogorets Razgrad|Ludogorets]]<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.uefa.com/uefaeuropaleague/match/2033200--ludogorets-vs-crvena-zvezda/ |title=UEFA Europa League vs. Ludogorets |publisher=UEFA Europa League|access-date=30 September 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.uefa.com/uefaeuropaleague/match/2033249--crvena-zvezda-vs-ludogorets/ |title=UEFA Europa League vs. Ludogorets |publisher=UEFA Europa League|access-date=25 November 2021}}</ref> in the Europa League group stage. He captained the side in the away match at Ludogorets.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.republika.rs/sport/fudbal/311929/drugi-u-ovom-veku-zvezdin-kapiten-na-listi-gde-su-sve-same-crveno-bele-legende |title=Eraković captains the side in Europa League|date=2 October 2021 |publisher=Republika |access-date=2 October 2021}}</ref> Red Star Belgrade qualified for the competition's knockout stage and Eraković played in both legs as Red Star Belgrade bowed out of the competition with a 4-2 aggregate defeat against [[Rangers F.C.|Rangers FC]] of [[Scotland]]. Eraković played as Red Star Belgrade secured back-to-back Superliga<ref>{{cite web |url=http://crvenazvezdafk.com/en/vest/13211/crvena-zvezda-vozdovac-3-1-1-0 |title=Five in a row |publisher=Red Star Belgrade |access-date=22 May 2022}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://superliga.rs/vesti/3576-sampionsko-vece-na-stadionu-rajko-mitic-i-peta-titula-u-nizu |title=Five in a row |publisher=Serbian SuperLiga |access-date=22 May 2022}}</ref> and [[Serbian Cup|Kup Srbije]] doubles.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://crvenazvezdafk.com/en/vest/13250/pobeda-u-vecitom-derbiju |title=Zvezda retain the double! |publisher=Red Star Belgrade |access-date=26 May 2022}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://fss.rs/crvena-zvezda-osvojila-kup-srbije/ |title=Zvezda retain the double! |publisher=Football Association of Serbia |access-date=26 May 2022}}</ref>

At the start of the 2022/23 season, Eraković signed a new contract to remain at Red Star Belgrade until June 2026.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://crvenazvezdafk.com/news/details/index/13636 |title=Eraković signs new contract to stay at Zvezda until 2026|publisher=Red Star Belgrade|access-date=12 August 2022}}</ref>

===Loan to Grafičar===
Eraković spent the 2019/20 season playing for [[RFK Grafičar Beograd|RFK Grafičar]],<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.crvenazvezdafk.com/news/details/index/8636|title=Zvezda's development side begins preparations |date=27 June 2019|accessdate=6 January 2020|publisher=Red Star Belgrade}}</ref> Red Star Belgrade's affiliate club in the [[Serbian First League|Prva liga Srbije]], the second tier of Serbian football. Eraković appeared in 21 games and scored two goals for Grafičar.

==International career==
Eraković was selected for all youth national teams of Serbia and was made captain. He was called up for the [[Serbia national football team|senior national team]] by [[Dragan Stojković]] and made his debut in a [[UEFA Nations League]] match against Norway<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.mozzartsport.com/fudbal/vesti/piksi-o-debitantima-erakovic-je-dokaz-ispravnosti-bonus-pravila-odlucio-sam-se-za-njega-pre-finala-kupa/421393 |title=Eraković makes Serbia NT bow |publisher=MozzartSport |access-date=2 June 2022}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.uefa.com/uefanationsleague/match/2034421--serbia-vs-norway/ |title=UEFA Nations League vs. Norway |publisher=UEFA Nations League |access-date=2 June 2022}}</ref> at [[Red Star Stadium|Rajko Mitić Stadium]]. He was a part of the Serbia team that gained promotion to the Nations League Division A.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.rts.rs/page/sport/sr/story/36/fudbal/4965932/norveska-srbija-b-liga-nacija-izvestaj.html |title=Serbia wins promotion to the UEFA Nations League Division A |publisher=Radio Television of Serbia|access-date=27 September 2022}}</ref>

In November 2022, he was selected in Serbia's squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] in Qatar,<ref>{{cite web |url=https://fss.rs/en/head-coach-dragan-stojkovic-announces-the-list-of-players-for-the-fifa-world-cup-2022-in-qatar/ |title=Head-coach Dragan Stojković announces the list of players for the FIFA World Cup 2022 in Qatar |publisher=[[Football Association of Serbia]] |date=11 November 2022 |access-date=11 November 2022}}</ref> but he didn't make any appearance there.
