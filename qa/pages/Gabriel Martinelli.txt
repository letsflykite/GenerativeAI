'''Gabriel Teodoro Martinelli Silva''' (born 18 June 2001), known as '''Gabriel Martinelli''', is a Brazilian professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Forward (association football)|forward]] for {{English football updater|Arsenal}} club [[Arsenal F.C.|Arsenal]] and the [[Brazil national football team|Brazil national team]].

Martinelli began his senior club career playing for [[Ituano FC|Ituano]] and signed for Arsenal in July 2019, aged 18. He won the [[FA Cup]] in his debut season.

At international level, Martinelli represents the [[Brazil national under-23 football team|Brazil under-23]]s. He helped Brazil win an Olympic Gold in [[Football at the 2020 Summer Olympics ‚Äì Men's tournament|men's football]] at the [[2020 Summer Olympics]]. He made his senior debut in 2022 and was part of Brazil's squad at the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].

== Style of play ==
A dynamic and explosive wide forward known for his agile movement, Martinelli can dribble past multiple opponents to create or score goals.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Martinelli's 'all-round game exceptional' - Arsenal boss Arteta |url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/articles/c72lky4pvdeo |access-date=1 July 2022 |website=BBC Sport |language=en-GB}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last=pmanalytics |date=5 August 2020 |title=Gabriel Martinelli ‚Äì Player Analysis |url=https://pmanalytics.wordpress.com/2020/08/05/gabriel-martinelli-player-analysis/ |access-date=1 July 2022 |website=PM Analytics |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last1=Bil |first1=Toni |last2=zic |title=Gabriel Martinelli: A spark of hope for Arsenal |url=https://totalfootballanalysis.com/article/gabriel-martinelli-scout-report-tactical-analysis-tactics |access-date=1 July 2022 |website=Total Football Analysis Magazine |language=en-GB}}</ref> Martinelli models himself on [[Cristiano Ronaldo]], considered a natural template for any goalscoring wide forward.<ref name=":1">{{Cite news |last=Cox |first=Michael |date=31 December 2022 |title=Gabriel Martinelli might be the Premier League's pivotal player of 2023 |work=[[The Athletic]] |url=https://theathletic.com/4043186/2022/12/30/gabriel-martinelli-could-keep-arsenal-top/?source=weeklyemail&campaign=602288 |url-access=subscription |access-date=1 January 2023}}</ref>
