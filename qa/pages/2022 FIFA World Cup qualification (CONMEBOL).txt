The South American section of the [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification]]<!-- DO NOT BOLD, see [[WP:SBE]] for further guidance --> acted as qualifiers for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]], to be held in Qatar, for national teams which are members of the South American Football Confederation ([[CONMEBOL]]). A total of 4.5 slots (4 direct slots and 1 inter-confederation play-off slot) in the final tournament were available for CONMEBOL teams.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.fifa.com/worldcup/news/y=2015/m=5/news=current-allocation-of-fifa-world-cuptm-confederation-slots-maintained-2610611.html |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150530211217/http://www.fifa.com/worldcup/news/y=2015/m=5/news=current-allocation-of-fifa-world-cuptm-confederation-slots-maintained-2610611.html |url-status=dead |archive-date=30 May 2015 |title=Current allocation of FIFA World Cup confederation slots maintained |publisher=FIFA |date=30 May 2015}}</ref>

The qualification process began on 8 October 2020 and ended on 29 March 2022. Uruguay's [[Luis Suárez]] scored the first goal of the round-robin. This was the third time Suárez had opened scoring in the group (after [[2010 FIFA World Cup qualification (CONMEBOL)|2010]] and [[2014 FIFA World Cup qualification (CONMEBOL)|2014]]), as well as the fourth consecutive time a Uruguayan player had done so ([[Martín Cáceres]] scored the first goal of the [[2018 FIFA World Cup qualification|2018]] process).<ref>{{cite news |title=Clasificatorias Sudamericanas: Selección que anotó el primer gol siempre fue al Mundial |url=https://www.golperu.pe/selecciones/otras-selecciones/la-seleccion-que-anoto-el-primer-gol-en-clasificatorias-sudamericanas-siempre-fue-al-mundial-26901 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220203073226/https://www.golperu.pe/selecciones/otras-selecciones/la-seleccion-que-anoto-el-primer-gol-en-clasificatorias-sudamericanas-siempre-fue-al-mundial-26901 |url-status=dead |archive-date=3 February 2022 |access-date=9 October 2020 |work=Gol Perú |date=8 October 2020 |language=es }}</ref>

==Format==
On 24 January 2019, the CONMEBOL Council decided to maintain the same qualification structure used for the previous six tournaments.<ref name="dates">{{cite web |url=http://www.conmebol.com/es/clasificatorio-sudamericano-al-mundial-de-qatar-arrancara-en-marzo-del-2020 |title=Clasificatorio sudamericano al Mundial de Qatar arrancará en marzo del 2020 |publisher=Conmebol.com |date=24 January 2019 |language=Spanish}}</ref> The ten teams play in a league of home-and-away [[round-robin tournament|round-robin]] matches.

The fixtures were determined by a draw which was held on 17 December 2019, 10:00 [[Time in Paraguay|PYST]] ([[UTC−03:00|UTC−3]]), at the Bourbon Asunción Convention Hotel in [[Luque]], Paraguay.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.fifa.com/worldcup/news/fifa-world-cup-2022-qualifiers-draws-to-take-centre-stage-in-south-america-and-a |title=FIFA World Cup 2022 qualifiers: draws to take centre stage in South America and Africa |publisher=FIFA |date=11 December 2019 |access-date=11 December 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Se viene el sorteo oficial de las Eliminatorias Sudamericanas para la Copa Mundial de la FIFA QATAR 2022 |url=http://www.conmebol.com/es/se-viene-el-sorteo-oficial-de-las-eliminatorias-sudamericanas-para-la-copa-mundial-de-la-fifa-qatar |publisher=CONMEBOL |date=19 November 2019 |language=Spanish}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Eliminatorias sudamericanas 2022: cuándo empiezan, fechas y partidos |url=https://www.goal.com/es/noticias/eliminatorias-sudamericanas-2022-mundial-qatar-2022/icw0hksmcn0w1uiv9bhw4dnne |work=Goal (website) |date=29 November 2019 |language=Spanish}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.fifa.com/worldcup/news/watch-live-preliminary-draw-for-conmebol-qatar-2022-qualifiers |title=RELIVE: Preliminary Draw for CONMEBOL Qatar 2022 qualifiers |publisher=FIFA |date=17 December 2019 |access-date=17 December 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=http://conmebol.com/es/el-calendario-de-partidos-de-las-eliminatorias-mas-emocionantes-del-mundo |title=El calendario de partidos de las Eliminatorias más emocionantes del mundo |trans-title=The calendar of matches of the most exciting qualifiers in the world |publisher=CONMEBOL |date=17 December 2019 |access-date=17 December 2019 |language=Spanish}}</ref>

Originally, Brazil and Argentina were both to be drawn into either position 4 or 5 in the draw, thus ensuring that no team has to play both of them on any double matchday.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.conmebol.com/es/se-sortean-las-eliminatorias-sudamericanas-al-mundial-de-la-fifa-qatar-2022 |title=Se sortean las Eliminatorias Sudamericanas al Mundial de la FIFA – Qatar 2022 |trans-title=FIFA World Cup – Qatar 2022 South American Qualifiers to be drawn |publisher=CONMEBOL.com |language=Spanish |date=11 December 2019 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191214025929/http://www.conmebol.com/es/se-sortean-las-eliminatorias-sudamericanas-al-mundial-de-la-fifa-qatar-2022 |archive-date=14 December 2019}}</ref> However, the decision was later reversed on 16 November 2019 by the CONMEBOL Council, making the draw completely open.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://conmebol.com/es/el-sorteo-de-eliminatorias-conmebol-para-el-mundial-fifa-qatar-2022-contara-con-bolillas |title=El Sorteo de Eliminatorias CONMEBOL para el Mundial FIFA Qatar 2022 contará con bolillas indiferenciadas para todas las selecciones |trans-title=The CONMEBOL qualifying draw for the FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022 will feature undifferentiated balls for all teams |publisher=CONMEBOL |date=16 December 2019 |access-date=17 December 2019 |language=Spanish}}</ref>

The CONMEBOL Council approved the use of the [[video assistant referee]] system for the qualifiers.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.conmebol.com/es/se-aplicara-el-var-en-todos-los-encuentros-clasificatorios-para-el-mundial-fifa-qatar-2022|title=Se aplicará el VAR en todos los encuentros clasificatorios para el Mundial FIFA Qatar 2022|publisher=CONMEBOL.com|date=14 February 2020|language=Spanish}}</ref>

==Schedule==
The qualifying matches are played on dates that fall within the [[FIFA International Match Calendar]].<ref name="dates" /><ref>{{cite web|url=https://digitalhub.fifa.com/m/11f7fa1dc502361/original/Enclosure_Circular-1766_Updates-to-the-Men-s-International-Match-Calendar_EN.pdf|title=INTERNATIONAL MATCH CALENDAR 2020-2024|publisher=FIFA|access-date=1 November 2021|archive-date=3 January 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210103155126/https://img.fifa.com/image/upload/wt4ycd2y7awqfslrf8wi.pdf|url-status=live}}</ref> There are a total of 18 matchdays. Originally eight matchdays would be in 2020 and ten would be in 2021.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.conmebol.com/es/clasificatorio-de-selecciones-conmebol-al-mundial-de-qatar-arranca-en-marzo-del-2020 |title=Clasificatorio de selecciones CONMEBOL al Mundial de Qatar arranca en marzo del 2020 |publisher=Conmebol.com |date=18 May 2019 |language=Spanish}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://conmebol.com/sites/default/files/fwc_q22_conmebol_draw_schedule-3.pdf |title=Preliminary draw (CONMEBOL) |publisher=CONMEBOL |date=17 December 2019 |access-date=17 December 2019}}</ref>

On 12 March 2020, FIFA announced that matches on matchdays 1–2 due to take place in March 2020 were postponed due to the [[COVID-19 pandemic]], with the new dates to be confirmed.<ref name="covid19fifa">{{cite web |url=https://www.fifa.com/worldcup/news/update-on-upcoming-fifa-world-cup-qualifiers-in-south-america |title=Update on upcoming FIFA World Cup qualifiers in South America |publisher=FIFA |date=12 March 2020|access-date=13 March 2020}}</ref>

On 25 June 2020, FIFA announced that the [[#Inter-confederation play-offs|inter-confederation play-offs]], originally scheduled to be played in March 2022, were moved to June 2022.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fifa.com/who-we-are/news/fifa-council-unanimously-approves-covid-19-relief-plan|title=FIFA Council unanimously approves COVID-19 Relief Plan|publisher=FIFA|date=25 June 2020}}</ref>

On 10 July 2020, FIFA announced that the CONMEBOL qualifiers in September 2020 were postponed, with the qualifiers starting in October 2020. CONMEBOL also requested FIFA to include a replacement international window in January 2022 in order to complete the qualifiers in March 2022.<ref>{{cite ==Inter-confederation play-off==
{{main|2022 FIFA World Cup qualification (AFC–CONMEBOL play-off)}}
The inter-confederation play-off was determined by a draw held on 26 November 2021. The fifth-placed team from CONMEBOL was drawn against the [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification – AFC fourth round|AFC fourth round]] winners.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.fifa.com/news/european-and-intercontinental-play-off-draw-results-and-reaction |title=European and intercontinental play-off draw results and reaction |publisher=FIFA |date=26 November 2021 |access-date=26 November 2021}}</ref> The play-off was played as a single match in Qatar on 13 June 2022.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://en.as.com/en/2021/11/26/soccer/1637955253_226019.html |title=2022 World Cup inter-confederation play-off draw: fixtures and format |last=Allen |first=William |work=as.com |date=26 November 2021 |access-date=30 November 2021}}</ref>
