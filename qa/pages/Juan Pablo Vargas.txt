'''Juan Pablo Vargas Campos''' (born 6 June 1995) is a Costa Rican professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Defender (association football)|defender]] for [[Categoría Primera A]] club [[Millonarios FC|Millonarios]] and the [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica national team]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mlssoccer.com/players/juan-pablo-vargas/|title=Juan Pablo Vargas|website=Major League Soccer}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://int.soccerway.com/players/juan-pablo-vargas/394586/|title=J. Vargas|website=Soccerway}}</ref>

==Club career==
Vargas came through the academy at [[Liga Deportiva Alajuelense]] and was promoted to the main squad by coach [[Óscar Ramírez (footballer, born 1964)|Óscar Ramírez]] on 30 May 2013, along with the other youth [[Ronald Matarrita]] and [[Steve Garita]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.aldia.cr/futbol-costa-rica/alajuelense/Liga-Steve_Garita-Ronald_Matarrita-Juan_Pablo_Vargas_1_144595549.html|title=Steve Garita, Ronald Matarrita and Juan Pablo Vargas arrive at the Alajuelense first team|website=Aldia.cr}}</ref>

Vargas has achieved great form playing for Millonarios and has been called the best defender playing in the Colombian league, with some [[tongue-in-cheek]] comparisons with [[Lionel Messi]] after an assist in a 4–1 win against [[Cortuluá]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://futbolcentroamerica.com/costarica/Juan-Pablo-Vargas-se-vistio-de-Messi-y-repartio-una-extraordinaria-asistencia-en-Colombia-VIDEO-20220830-0019.html|title=Juan Pablo Vargas "dressed up as Messi" and distributed an extraordinary assistance in Colombia|website=futbolcentoamerica.com}}</ref> Vargas played as Millionarios reached the final of the [[Copa Colombia]] in September 2022.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://futbolcentroamerica.com/costarica/Costa-Rica-Juan-Pablo-Vargas-a-un-paso-de-hacer-historia-con-Millonarios-en-Colombia--20220908-0005.html|title=Costa Rica: Juan Pablo Vargas one step away from making history with Millionaires in Colombia|website=futbolcentroamerica.com}}</ref>

==International career==
Vargas made his senior [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica]] debut at the [[2017 Copa Centroamericana]], playing in a 3–0 win over [[Belize national football team|Belize]] on January 15, 2017.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.worldfootball.com/m/528036/2017-01-15/copa-centroamericana/belize/costa-rica|title=Copa Centroamericano Belize vs. Costa Rica|website=worldfootball.com}}</ref> He scored his first goal for the national team in a [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification]] match against the [[United States men's national soccer team|United States]], netting the opener in a 2–0 victory on March 30, 2022.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://gladiadoresdelfutbol.com/2022/03/30/video-el-tremendo-golazo-de-cabeza-de-juan-pablo-vargas-que-tiene-a-costa-rica-ganandole-a-usa/|date=March 30, 2022|title=VIDEO: El tremendo golazo de cabeza de Juan Pablo Vargas que tiene a Costa Rica ganándole a USA|first=Julian|last=Sierra}}</ref> In November 2022, Vargas was named to Costa Rica's squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fifa.com/fifaplus/en/articles/costa-rica-squad-list-fifa-world-cup-qatar-2022|title=Suarez names Costa Rica squad for Qatar 2022}}</ref>
