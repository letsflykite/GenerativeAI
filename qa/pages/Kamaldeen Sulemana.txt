'''Kamaldeen Sulemana''' (born 15 February 2002), also known simply as '''Kamaldeen''', is a Ghanaian professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[winger (association football)|left winger]] or a [[forward (association football)|forward]] for [[EFL Championship]] club [[Southampton F.C.|Southampton]] and the [[Ghana national football team|Ghana national team]].<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|title=Kamaldeen Sulemana, Biography|url=https://www.ghanaweb.com/person/Kamaldeen-Sulemana-5410|access-date=7 February 2022|website=www.ghanaweb.com}}</ref>

==Club career==

===Nordsjælland===
Born in [[Techiman]], [[Bono East Region]],<ref>{{cite web |title=THE JOURNEY: Kamaldeen Sulemana |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8q4OrQwWrqg |website=www.youtube.com |publisher=[[FC Nordsjælland]] |accessdate=8 October 2020 |date=24 February 2020}}</ref> Kamaldeen was a part of the [[Right to Dream Academy]] before joining its cooperative club in Denmark, [[FC Nordsjælland]], in January 2020.<ref name=":0" /> He signed a five-year deal with the club on his 18th birthday and made his debut for the club a week later, 22 February 2020, against [[SønderjyskE Fodbold|SønderjyskE]] in the [[Danish Superliga]].<ref>[https://jyllands-posten.dk/sport/fodbold/superliga/fcn/ECE11960869/fcn-har-lavet-femaarig-aftale-med-debuterende-stortalent/ FCN har lavet femårig aftale med debuterende stortalent], jyllands-posten.dk, 23 February 2020</ref> Kamaldeen started on the bench, but replaced [[Mohammed Diomande]] in the 61st minute.

In August 2020, he was handed shirt number 10, after the departure of [[Mohammed Kudus]] to [[AFC Ajax|Ajax]].<ref>{{cite news |last1=Okine |first1=Sammy Heywood |title=FC Nordsjaelland unveils Kamaldeen Sulemana as new playmaker |url=https://newsghana.com.gh/fc-nordsjaelland-unveils-kamaldeen-sulemana-as-new-playmaker/ |work=News Ghana |date=31 August 2020}}</ref> He has been considered as one of the most promising young talents of the Danish Superliga, regarded for his technical ability, his dribbling and his speed, making him one of the most creative players in the league.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Bruijn |first1=Thijs de |title=Even voorstellen: Ajax-target Kamaldeen Sulemana |url=https://www.elfvoetbal.nl/binnenland/280160/even-voorstellen-ajax-target-kamaldeen-sulemana |work=Elfvoetbal.nl |date=31 January 2021 |language=nl}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last1=Risager |first1=Victor |title=FCN-træner om hypet talent: Han er stadig ved at finde sine ben |url=https://www.tipsbladet.dk/nyhed/superliga/fcn-traener-om-hypet-talent-han-er-stadig-ved-finde-sine-ben |work=Tipsbladet.dk |date=6 July 2020 |language=da}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last1=Sauermilch |first1=Jonas |title=Fire Superliga-spillere på fornem talentliste - TV 2 |url=https://sport.tv2.dk/fodbold/2021-01-07-fire-superliga-spillere-paa-fornem-talentliste |work=sport.tv2.dk |date=7 January 2021 |language=da-DK}}</ref> During the [[List of Danish football transfers winter 2020–21|2020–21 winter transfer window]], his name was linked with several European clubs interested in him, in particular Ajax and [[Bayer 04 Leverkusen|Bayer Leverkusen]], without this amounting to a move.<ref>{{cite web |title=Ajax eye FC Nordsjælland talent as Promes replacement |url=http://www.football-oranje.com/ajax-eye-fc-nordsjaelland-talent-as-promes-replacement/ |website=Football Oranje |ref=30 January 2021 |date=30 January 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last1=Ketterl |first1=Jonny |title=Bayer Leverkusen: Kamaldeen Sulemana im Winter noch zu teuer? |url=https://wetten.com/aktuelles/fussball/2021/0119/bayer-leverkusen-kamaldeen-sulemana-im-winter-noch-zu-teuer/ |work=Wetten.com DE |date=19 January 2021 |language=de-DE}}</ref>

After scoring five goals in five games, Kamaldeen was voted the [[Danish Superliga|Superliga Player of the Month]] for April 2021.<ref name="potm1" />

===Rennes===
On 16 July 2021, Kamaldeen signed a five-year contract with [[Ligue 1]] club [[Stade Rennais F.C.|Rennes]],<ref>{{cite web |title=Kamaldeen Sulemana est Rennais ! |url=https://www.staderennais.com/kamaldeen-sulemana-est-rennais |website=STADE RENNAIS F.C. |language=fr |date=16 July 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last1=Risager |first1=Victor |title=Officielt: Kamaldeen forlader FC Nordsjælland |url=https://www.tipsbladet.dk/nyhed/superliga/officielt-kamaldeen-forlader-fc-nordsjaelland |work=Tipsbladet.dk |date=16 July 2021 |language=da}}</ref> for a reported €20 million.<ref name="rekord">{{cite news |last1=Abolhosseini |first1=Farzam |title=B.T. afslører: FC Nordsjælland har solgt Kamaldeen Sulemana for rekordbeløb |url=https://www.bt.dk/superligaen/bt-afsloerer-fc-nordsjaelland-har-solgt-kamaldeen-sulemana-for-rekordbeloeb |date=16 July 2021 |language=da}}</ref> Thereby, he surpassed the former Danish leagues transfer record holder,  [[Alexander Sørloth]].<ref name="rekord" />

Kamaldeen made his full debut for Rennes on 8 August 2021 against [[RC Lens|Lens]] in the first game of the [[2021–22 Ligue 1|Ligue 1]] season. He scored Rennes' only goal after 14 minutes in a game that ended 1–1.<ref>[https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2021/08/08/france/ligue-1/stade-rennais-fc/racing-club-de-lens/3525908/ RENNES VS. LENS  1 - 1], soccerway.com, 8 August 2021</ref>

===Southampton===
On 1 February 2023, Sulemana signed a four-and-a-half-year contract with [[Southampton F.C.|Southampton]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Sulemana is a Saint |url=https://www.southamptonfc.com/news/2023-02-01/announcement-kamaldeen-sulemana-southampton-rennes-transfer |access-date=2023-02-01 |website=Southampton FC |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Kamaldeen Sulemana: Southampton pay club-record £22m for Rennes winger |work=BBC Sport |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/64469266 |access-date=1 February 2023}}</ref> On 4 February 2023, Sulemana made his first Premier League appearance in a 3–0 defeat against [[Brentford F.C.|Brentford]], replacing [[Ibrahima Diallo (footballer, born 1999)|Ibrahima Diallo]] at half-time.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Rindl |first=Joe |date=4 February 2023 |title=Brentford 3–0 Southampton |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/64436130 |access-date=5 March 2023 |website=BBC Sport}}</ref> On 28 May 2023, Sulemana scored his first [[Premier League]] goals in a 4–4 draw with [[Liverpool F.C.|Liverpool]].<ref>{{Cite web |last=Rindl |first=Joe |date=28 May 2023 |title=Southampton 4–4 Liverpool |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/65661819 |access-date=28 May 2023 |website=BBC Sport}}</ref>

==International career==
Kamaldeen received his first call-up for the [[Ghana national football team|Ghana national team]] on 25 September 2020 ahead of the game against [[Mali national football team|Mali]].<ref>{{cite web |title=18-årig FCN-komet jubler over landsholdsudtagelse |url=https://www.berlingske.dk/sport/18-aarig-fcn-komet-jubler-over-landsholdsudtagelse |publisher=[[Berlingske]] |accessdate=8 October 2020 |language=Danish |date=28 September 2020}}</ref><ref name=":0" /> He debuted with Ghana in a 3–0 friendly loss to [[Mali national football team|Mali]] on 9 October 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://uk.soccerway.com/matches/2020/10/09/world/friendlies/mali/ghana/3396568/|title=Mali vs. Ghana - 9 October 2020 - Soccerway|website=uk.soccerway.com}}</ref> Kamaldeen was also used in the friendly game against [[Qatar national football team|Qatar]] three days later, when he came on as a substitute for the last 30 minutes.<ref>[https://www.bold.dk/fodbold/nyheder/fcn-komet-blev-indskiftet-i-ghanesisk-storsejr/ FCN-komet blev indskiftet i ghanesisk storsejr], bold.dk, 12 October 2020</ref>

Kamaldeen is part of the Ghana U-23 football team that will be participating at 2023 [[U-23 Africa Cup of Nations]] (AFCON) in [[Morocco|Moroco]].<ref>{{Cite web |date=2023-06-08 |title=2023 U23 AFCON: Kamaldeen, Nuamah headline Black Meteors squad - MyJoyOnline.com |url=https://www.myjoyonline.com/2023-u23-afcon-kamaldeen-nuamah-headline-black-meteors-squad/ |access-date=2023-06-09 |website=www.myjoyonline.com |language=en-US}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Sulemana's father is [[Gonja people|Gonja]] and his mother is [[Dagomba people|Dagomba]].<ref name="swg">{{cite news |last1=Raubil |first1=Gariba |title=Kamaldeen Sulemana talks about his origin and how it all started for him in Techiman |url=https://sportsworldghana.com/kamaldeen-sulemana-talks-about-his-origin-and-how-it-all-started-for-him-in-techiman/ |work=Sportsworld Ghana |date=6 November 2021}}</ref> He is a Muslim.<ref name="swg" />
