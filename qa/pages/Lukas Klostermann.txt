===Senior team===
[[File:2019-06-11 Fußball, Männer, Länderspiel, Deutschland-Estland StP 2110 LR10 by Stepro.jpg|thumb|right|Klostermann with [[Germany national football team|Germany]] in 2019]]
Klostermann received his first call up to the [[Germany national football team|senior national team]] in March 2019 for the friendly match against [[Serbia national football team|Serbia]] and [[UEFA Euro 2020 qualifying]] match against the [[Netherlands national football team|Netherlands]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Löw beruft Stark, Klostermann und Eggestein|trans-title=Löw calls Stark, Klostermann and Eggestein|url=http://www.kicker.de/news/fussball/nationalelf/744640/artikel_loew-beruft-stark-klostermann-und-eggestein.html|publisher=kicker|date=15 March 2019|access-date=20 March 2019 |language=de}}</ref> He made his Germany debut, playing the whole game, in a 1–1 draw against Serbia on 20 March 2019.<ref>{{cite news|title=VIDEO: REMIS NACH RÜCKSTAND GEGEN SERBIEN|url=https://www.dfb.de/news/detail/video-remis-nach-rueckstand-gegen-serbien-199929/?no_cache=1&cHash=d4e25a287dd7b6b7139b407e88e6b727|publisher=The DFB Website|language=de|date=20 March 2019|access-date=4 August 2019}}</ref> After the match, Manager [[Joachim Löw]] praised his debut performance.<ref>{{cite news|title=LÖW: "KLOSTERMANN HAT RICHTIG GUT GESPIELT"|url=https://www.dfb.de/news/detail/loew-klostermann-hat-richtig-gut-gespielt-199951/?no_cache=1&cHash=997aaf91847acd07dbe397c335dd68d9|publisher=The DFB Website|language=de|date=21 March 2019|access-date=4 August 2019}}</ref> On 19 May 2021, he was selected to the squad for the [[UEFA Euro 2020]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.kicker.de/em-kader-offiziell-loew-beruft-mueller-hummels-und-volland-804642/artikel|title=EM-Kader offiziell: Löw beruft Müller, Hummels und Volland|date=19 May 2021|website=kicker.de|language=de|access-date=19 May 2021}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Klostermann's parents were both athletes: his father ran 110m hurdles and his mother ran all-round.<ref>{{cite news |date=17 August 2016 |title="Den linken Fuß nicht nur zum Stehen": Klostermanns gute Gene |language=nl |publisher=kicker.de |url=http://www.kicker.de/news/olympia/startseite/658291/artikel_den-linken-fuss-nicht-nur-zum-stehen_klostermanns-gute-gene.html |access-date=29 December 2017}}</ref> His sister, Lisa, is also a footballer and plays as a goalkeeper.<ref>{{cite news |date=1 July 2016 |title=Nationalelf ist hier Familiensach |language=de |publisher=Bild.de |url=http://www.bild.de/sport/fussball/rb-leipzig/nationalelf-ist-hier-familiensache-46583162.bild.html |access-date=29 December 2017}}</ref> He attended a school in Gevelsberg to get his high school diploma and graduated in May 2014.<ref name="Klostermann Bochum" /><ref>{{cite web |date=16 April 2014 |title=Klostermann: "Ein überragendes Gefühl" |url=http://www.vfl-bochum.de/site/_home/aktuelles/14298_klostermanneinueberragendesgefuehlp.htm |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171226203232/http://www.vfl-bochum.de/site/_home/aktuelles/14298_klostermanneinueberragendesgefuehlp.htm |archive-date=26 December 2017 |access-date=26 December 2017 |publisher=VfL Bochum |language=de}}<br />{{cite news |date=30 June 2014 |title=Fix: Terrazzino kommt zum VfL Bochum |language=de |publisher=kicker.de |url=http://www.kicker.de/news/fussball/2bundesliga/startseite/606613/artikel_fix_terrazzino-kommt-zum-vfl.html |access-date=29 December 2017}}</ref> Klostermann attended the [[University of Hagen]], studying economics.<ref>{{cite news |date=25 August 2017 |title=Lukas Klostermann im Goal-Interview: "Glaube schon, dass ich eine Ausnahme bin" |language=de |publisher=Goal.com |url=https://www.goal.com/de/meldungen/lukas-klostermann-rb-leipzig-goal-interview-glaube-schon/59rkrumzs1v219ci3e5idptrm |access-date=4 August 2019}}</ref>
