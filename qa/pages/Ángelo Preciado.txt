'''Ángelo Smit Preciado Quiñónez''' (born 18 February 1998) is an Ecuadorian professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Defender (association football)#Full-back|right-back]] for [[Belgian First Division A|Belgian Pro League]] club [[KRC Genk|Genk]] and the [[Ecuador national football team|Ecuador national team]].<ref name="SW"/>

==Club career==
Born in [[Shushufindi Canton|Shushufindi]], [[Sucumbíos Province|Sucumbíos]], Preciado joined [[C.S.D. Independiente del Valle|Independiente del Valle]]'s youth setup in 2015, from [[América de Quito]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.eltelegrafo.com.ec/noticias/futbol/1/preciado-le-dedicara-el-mundial-a-su-padre|title=Preciado le dedicará el mundial a su padre|trans-title=Preciado will dedicate the World Cup to his father|publisher=[[El Telégrafo (Ecuador)|El Telégrafo]]|language=es|date=16 May 2017|accessdate=4 December 2019}}</ref> In February 2018, during the semifinals of the [[2018 U-20 Copa Libertadores]], he went viral after using the [[corner flag]] to defend himself from a fight with the [[Club Atlético River Plate (Montevideo)|River Plate Montevideo]] players.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.benditofutbol.com/independiente-del-valle/angelo-preciado-copalibertadores-sub20-banderin.html|title=¿Quién es el futbolista que se defendió con el banderín en la Libertadores Sub 20?|trans-title=Who is the footballer who defended himself with the corner flag in the U-20 Libertadores?|publisher=Bendito Fútbol|language=es|date=22 February 2018|accessdate=4 December 2019}}</ref>

Promoted to the first team after playing a short period in the [[reserve team]], [[Alianza Cotopaxi S.C.|Alianza Cotopaxi]], Preciado made his senior – and [[Ecuadorian Serie A|Serie A]] – debut on 9 July 2018, starting in a 3–1 away loss against [[S.D. Aucas|Aucas]]. He soon became a regular starter, and scored his first goals on 25 August by netting a brace in a 4–2 home defeat of [[C.D. El Nacional|El Nacional]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.elcomercio.com/deportes/independientedelvalle-elnacional-estadioruminahui-campeonatoecuatoriano-segundaetapa.html|title=Independiente remontó y ganó 4–2 a El Nacional en Sangolquí|trans-title=Independiente completed a comeback and defeated El Nacional by 4–2 in Sangolquí|publisher=[[El Comercio (Ecuador)|El Comercio]]|language=es|date=25 August 2018|accessdate=4 December 2019}}</ref>

==International career==
On 4 May 2017, Preciado was called up to [[Ecuador national under-20 football team|Ecuador under-20s]] for [[2017 FIFA U-20 World Cup|the year]]'s [[FIFA U-20 World Cup]],<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.andes.info.ec/es/noticias/ecuador-elige-juveniles-participaran-mundial-sub-20-corea-sur-2017.html|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170505014521/http://www.andes.info.ec/es/noticias/ecuador-elige-juveniles-participaran-mundial-sub-20-corea-sur-2017.html|title=Ecuador elige a los juveniles que participarán en el Mundial Sub 20 Corea del Sur 2017|trans-title=Ecuador choose the youngsters who will take part of U-20 World Cup South Korea 2017|publisher=[[Public News Agency of Ecuador and South America|ANDES]]|language=es|date=4 May 2017|archivedate=5 May 2017|accessdate=4 December 2019}}</ref> On 30 August of the following year, he was called up to [[Ecuador national football team|the full side]] by manager [[Hernán Darío Gómez]], for two [[Exhibition match|friendlies]] against [[Jamaica national football team|Jamaica]] and [[Guatemala national football team|Guatemala]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.eluniverso.com/deportes/2018/08/30/nota/6930152/22-convocados-hernan-dario-gomez-partidos-amistosos-ante-jamaica|title=Los 22 convocados de Hernán Darío Gómez para los partidos amistosos ante Jamaica y Guatemala|trans-title=The 22 called up by Hernán Darío Gómez for the friendly matches against Jamaica and Guatemala|publisher=[[El Universo]]|language=es|date=30 August 2019|accessdate=4 December 2019}}</ref>

Preciado made his full international debut on 12 October 2018, replacing Independiente teammate [[Stiven Plaza]] in a 4–3 loss against [[Qatar national football team|Qatar]] at the [[Jassim bin Hamad Stadium]] in [[Doha]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.elcomercio.com/deportes/ecuador-experiencia-juventud-catar-amistoso.html|title=Goles y errores en la derrota 4–3 de Ecuador ante Catar en Doha|trans-title=Goals and errors in Ecuador's 4–3 defeat to Qatar in Doha|publisher=El Comercio|language=es|date=12 October 2018|accessdate=4 December 2019}}</ref>
