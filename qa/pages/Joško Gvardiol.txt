'''Joško Gvardiol''' ({{IPA-sh|jôʃko ɡʋârdioːl|hr}};<ref>{{Cite web|title=Jȍško|url=http://hjp.znanje.hr/index.php?show=search_by_id&id=el9nXxI=&keyword=Jo%C5%A1ko|access-date=29 August 2020|website=Hrvatski jezični portal|language=hr}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=gvȁrdijān|url=http://hjp.znanje.hr/index.php?show=search_by_id&id=fV5nXxM=&keyword=gvardijan|access-date=29 August 2020|website=Hrvatski jezični portal|language=hr}}</ref> born 23 January 2002) is a Croatian professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Defender (association football)|defender]] for [[Bundesliga]] club [[RB Leipzig]] and the [[Croatia national football team|Croatia national team]]. Although primarily a [[Defender (association football)#Centre-back|centre-back]], he has often played as a [[Defender (association football)#Full-back|left-back]].

==Club career==
===Early career===
Gvardiol started playing football at the age of seven when his father, once an amateur player in his native [[Novigrad, Zadar County|Novigrad]], took him to [[NK Trešnjevka|Trešnjevka]]. While there, he was spotted by [[NK Lokomotiva|Lokomotiva]] and [[NK Zagreb|Zagreb]]; however, at the last minute he received an offer from [[GNK Dinamo Zagreb|Dinamo Zagreb]] which his family accepted.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|last=Juranović|first=Tomislav|date=13 September 2020|title=Ovo je zlatni dječak od 200 milijuna kuna: 'Ako do 20. godine ne postane Vatreni, netko mora u zatvor!'|url=https://sportske.jutarnji.hr/sn/nogomet/hnl/ovo-je-zlatni-djecak-od-200-milijuna-kuna-ako-do-20-godine-ne-postane-vatreni-netko-mora-u-zatvor-15018772|website=Sportske novosti|language=hr-hr}}</ref>

Initially, he played as a [[Defender (association football)#Full-back|left-back]] or a [[Midfielder#Central midfielder|central midfielder]] until [[GNK Dinamo Zagreb Academy|Dinamo academy]] coach [[Dalibor Poldrugač]] moved him to the [[Defender (association football)#Centre-back|centre-back]] position.
