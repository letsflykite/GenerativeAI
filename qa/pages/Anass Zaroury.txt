'''Anass Zaroury''' ({{lang-ar|أنس الزروري}}; born 7 November 2000) is a professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[winger (association football)|winger]] for [[Premier League]] club [[Burnley F.C.|Burnley]]. Born in Belgium, he plays for the [[Morocco national football team|Morocco national team]].

==Club career==
On 1 February 2021, Zaroury joined [[Belgian First Division A]] side [[R. Charleroi S.C.|Charleroi]] on a two-and-a-half-year deal, returning on loan to [[Lommel S.K.|Lommel]] until the end of the season.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.proximus.be/pickx/nl/2115932/anass-zaroury-versiert-transfer-naar-charleroi-maar-blijft-nog-even-in-lommel|title=Anass Zaroury versiert transfer naar Charleroi (maar blijft nog even in Lommel)|website=proximus.be|date=1 February 2021|accessdate=16 February 2021|language=Dutch}}</ref>

On 30 August 2022, Zaroury signed for [[EFL Championship]] club [[Burnley F.C.|Burnley]] for an undisclosed fee on a four-year deal.<ref> {{cite web|url=https://www.burnleyfootballclub.com/content/zaroury-is-a-claret|title=ZAROURY IS A CLARET|website=www.burnleyfootballclub.com|date=30 August 2022|accessdate=30 August 2022}}</ref> He scored his first goal for Burnley in a 4–0 win against [[Swansea City A.F.C.|Swansea City]] on 15 October 2022.<ref>{{cite news  |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/63158539 |title=Burnley 4-0 Swansea |publisher=[[BBC]] |date=15 October 2022 |access-date=17 October 2022}}</ref> Zaroury scored the second and third goals as Burnley beat Bournemouth in the 3rd round of the FA Cup in January 2023.<ref>{{cite news | url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/64167888 | title=Championship Burnley beat Bournemouth in thriller | work=BBC Sport }}</ref>

== International career ==
Born in Belgium, Zaroury is of Moroccan descent.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hbvl.be/cnt/dmf20201016_97816838|title=Anass Zaroury: in de tribune onder Maes, in de basis onder Manning|website=www.hbvl.be}}</ref> He is a former youth international for Belgium, having played with them up to the [[Belgium national under-21 football team|Belgium U21]]s. On 16 November 2022, Zaroury was officially invited to join the [[Morocco national football team|Moroccan national team]], during the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] in Qatar,<ref>{{Cite web |date=16 November 2022 |title=رسميا.. وليد الركراكي يوجه الدعوة إلى أنس زروري لتعويض أمين حارث في المونديال |url=https://www.hesport.com/رسميا-وليد-الركراكي-يوجه-الدعوة-إلى-أن-239539.html |access-date=16 November 2022 |website=هسبورت |language=ar}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |date=16 November 2022 |title=Burnley's boy wonder Anass Zaroury receives late call-up for the World Cup in Qatar |url=https://www.burnleyexpress.net/sport/football/burnleys-boy-wonder-anass-zaroury-who-contributed-to-the-demise-of-east-lancs-rivals-blackburn-rovers-at-the-weekend-receives-late-call-up-for-the-world-cup-in-qatar-3920048 |access-date=16 November 2022 |website=www.burnleyexpress.net |language=en}}</ref> in which he replaced [[Amine Harit]], who suffered an injury one week before the World Cup started.<ref>{{Cite news |title=Morocco's Harit set to miss World Cup after injury |language=en-GB |work=BBC Sport |url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/africa/63623256 |access-date=16 November 2022}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |date=14 November 2022 |title=Morocco's Harit and Poland goalkeeper Dragowski out of World Cup after late injuries |url=https://www.independent.co.uk/sport/football/world-cup/world-cup-injuries-amine-harit-bartlomiej-dragowski-b2224975.html |access-date=16 November 2022 |website=The Independent |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |date=14 November 2022 |title=Amine Harit to miss the World Cup following serious knee injury |url=https://en.hespress.com/52910-amine-harit-to-miss-the-world-cup-following-serious-knee-injury.html |access-date=16 November 2022 |website=HESPRESS English - Morocco News |language=en-US}}</ref> On 17 November, he made his international debut in a friendly match against [[Georgia national football team|Georgia]].<ref>{{cite tweet |user=BurnleyOfficial |title=Anass Zaroury made his international debut for Morocco in their friendly against Georgia last night |number=1593595653183180800 |date=18 November 2022 }}</ref>
