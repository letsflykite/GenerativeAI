'''Álvaro José Zamora Mata''' (born 9 March 2002) is a Costa Rican professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Midfielder (association football)|midfielder]] for [[Deportivo Saprissa]] and the [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica national team]].

==International career==
Zamora made his national team debut for Costa Rica in a friendly against [[South Korea national football team]] on 23 September 2022. He came in as a substitute for [[Gerson Torres]] at [[half-time]].<ref>{{cite web|date=23 September 2022|title=South Korea vs. Costa Rica|url=https://www.espn.com/soccer/commentary?gameId=652568|access-date=4 November 2022|work=[[ESPN]]}}</ref>
 
He was called up to the final 26-man [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica]] squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] in Qatar.<ref>{{cite web |date=3 November 2022 |title=Suárez eligió a sus 26 jugadores para la aventura mundialista en Catar |trans-title=Suarez chooses 26 players for the Qatar World Cup adventure |url=https://www.fedefutbol.com/2022/11/03/suarez-eligio-a-sus-26-jugadores-para-la-aventura-mundialista-en-catar/ |access-date=3 November 2022 |work=[[Costa Rican Football Federation|FCRF]] |language=es}}</ref>
