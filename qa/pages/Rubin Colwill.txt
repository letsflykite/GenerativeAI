'''Rubin James Colwill''' (born 27 April 2002) is a Welsh professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Winger (association football)|winger]] for {{English football updater|CardiffC}} club [[Cardiff City F.C.|Cardiff City]] and the [[Wales national football team|Wales national team]].

==Club career==
Colwill joined the youth academy of [[Cardiff City F.C.|Cardiff City]] when he was eight.<ref>{{cite web |title=Rubin Colwell player details |url=https://www.cardiffcityfc.co.uk/player/92 |publisher=Cardiff City F.C. |access-date=31 May 2021}}</ref> He made his professional debut with the club as a late substitute during a 3–1 [[EFL Championship]] win over [[Coventry City F.C.|Coventry City]] on 13 February 2021.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://uk.soccerway.com/matches/2021/02/13/england/championship/cardiff-city-fc/coventry-city-fc/3345588/|title=Cardiff City vs. Coventry City – 13 February 2021 – Soccerway|website=soccerway.com}}</ref> The following day, Colwill signed his first professional contract, alongside [[Isaak Davies]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://clwbpeldroed.org/2021/02/15/isaak-davies-rubin-colwill-cardiff-city/|title=Isaak Davies and Rubin Colwill sign new deals with Cardiff City|first=Martin|last=Townley|date=15 February 2021}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.walesonline.co.uk/sport/football/football-news/this-just-beginning-cardiff-city-19842685|title=The Cardiff starlet who wowed Mick McCarthy has just given Bluebirds huge boost|first=Glen|last=Williams|date=15 February 2021|website=WalesOnline}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url= https://www.cardiffcityfc.co.uk/news/rubin-colwill-isaak-davies-sign-new-city-deals |title= Rubin Colwill & Isaak Davies sign new City deals – Soccerway|publisher=Cardiff City F.C. |date=14 February 2021 |accessdate=15 February 2021}}</ref> Colwill's first league start for the club came in a 2–1 win over [[Wycombe Wanderers F.C.|Wycombe Wanderers]] on 24 April 2021, earning high praise from manager [[Mick McCarthy]] for his performance in the match. <ref>{{Cite web|url= https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/56781830 |title= Cardiff City 2 – 1 Wycombe Wanderers|publisher=BBC Sport |date=24 April 2021 |accessdate=25 April 2021}}</ref> On 12 September 2021, Colwill scored his first senior goals, scoring a brace in Cardiff's 2-1 victory against [[Nottingham Forest F.C.|Nottingham Forest]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/58451969 |title=Nottingham Forest 1–2 Cardiff City |publisher=BBC Sport |date=12 September 2021 |accessdate=14 September 2021}}</ref> On 6 February 2022, he scored Cardiff's only goal in a 3-1 loss to [[Liverpool F.C.|Liverpool]] in the FA Cup fourth round.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/60187991 |title=Liverpool 3-1 Cardiff |publisher=BBC Sport |date=6 February 2022 ==International career==
Colwill made his debut for the [[Wales national under-17 football team]] in a 2–2 draw against [[Belarus national under-17 football team|Belarus]] on 10 October 2018.<ref name="WalesU17">{{cite web |url= https://www.uefa.com/under17/match/2023728--belarus-vs-wales/ |title=Belarus 2–2 Wales |publisher=UEFAcom |author=|date=10 October 2018 |accessdate=13 February 2021 }}</ref> He was selected for the [[Wales national under-21 football team|Wales under-21 squad]] for a friendly match against [[Republic of Ireland national under-21 football team|Republic of Ireland]] on 25 March 2021 and made his debut for the side as a substitute during a 2–1 defeat.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/56539453 |title=Wales U21 1-2 Republic of Ireland U21: Two goals in a minute seal win |publisher=BBC Sport |last=Williams |first=Aled |date=26 March 2021 |accessdate=2 April 2021}}</ref>

On 30 May 2021, despite having never made an appearance for the senior squad, Colwill was selected by [[Rob Page]] for Wales' [[UEFA Euro 2020|Euro 2020]] squad.<ref>{{cite web |title=Euro 2020: Uncapped Rubin Colwill in Wales' European Championship squad |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/57301006 |publisher=BBC Sport |access-date=31 May 2021 |date=30 May 2021}}</ref> He made his senior national team debut on 2 June 2021 in a friendly against France, replacing [[Joe Morrell]] in the 83rd minute.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[ESPN]]|url=https://www.espn.com/soccer/lineups?gameId=601637|title=France v Wales game report|date=2 June 2021}}</ref>  Colwill scored his first international goal for Wales on 29 March 2022 in a 1-1 friendly match draw against [[Czech Republic national football team|Czech Republic]].<ref>https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/60874724 Colwill first Wales goal</ref>

In November 2022 he was named in the Wales squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] in [[Qatar]].<ref>[https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/63545011 2022 Wales World Cup squad]</ref> He came in as last substitute for injured [[Joe Allen]], at the 81st minute of the [[2022_FIFA_World_Cup_Group_B|third group game]] against [[England national football team|England]].<ref>[https://www.fifa.com/fifaplus/en/match-centre/match/17/255711/285063/400235454 Game's datas on FIFA official Website]</ref>
