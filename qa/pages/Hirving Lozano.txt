'''Hirving Rodrigo Lozano Bahena''' (born 30 July 1995) is a Mexican professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Midfielder#Winger|winger]] for {{Italian football updater|Napoli}} club [[S.S.C. Napoli|Napoli]] and the [[Mexico national football team|Mexico national team]]. He is popularly known by his nickname '''Chucky'''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.espnfc.us/story/2907349/Chucky-lozano-to-sign-for-manchester-united-after-olympics-father-in-law|title=Chucky Lozano to sign for Manchester United after Olympics - father-in-law|last=Rej|first=Arindam|date=4 July 2016|publisher=[[ESPN FC]]|access-date=9 November 2016}}</ref>

Lozano began his career with [[C.F. Pachuca|Pachuca]], winning the [[2015–16 Liga MX season#Torneo Clausura|2016 Clausura]] and the [[2016–17 CONCACAF Champions League]], playing in 152 games and scoring 44 goals for the team. In June 2017, he signed for Dutch side [[PSV Eindhoven]] and won the [[Eredivisie]] in his first season in the Netherlands, and also finished as the team's top scorer. In 2019, Lozano signed for Napoli for a reported €42 million, making him the most expensive [[Mexicans|Mexican]] player of all time.<ref name=":0" />

Participating with various youth teams, Lozano won the [[2015 CONCACAF U-20 Championship]] and played at the [[Football at the 2016 Summer Olympics – Men's tournament|2016 Summer Olympics]]. He made his senior international debut in February 2016 and represented the nation at the [[Copa América Centenario]], [[2017 FIFA Confederations Cup]], [[2018 FIFA World Cup]] and [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].

===PSV===
====2017–18 season====
On 19 June 2017, following a lengthy transfer saga which had Lozano linked to a number of European clubs, including [[Manchester United F.C.|Manchester United]], [[Manchester City F.C.|Manchester City]], [[S.L. Benfica|Benfica]] and [[AFC Ajax|Ajax]], it was announced that Dutch club [[PSV Eindhoven]] had secured the signing of Lozano on a six-year contract.<ref name="PSV">{{Cite magazine |title=Hirving Lozano joins PSV after lengthy transfer saga |first=Luis Miguel |last=Echegaray |magazine=Sports Illustrated |date=19 June 2017 |access-date=21 September 2018 |url= https://www.si.com/planet-futbol/2017/06/19/hirving-lozano-psv-transfer-pachuca-mexico}}</ref> He temporarily left the Mexican national side which was in Russia for the [[2017 FIFA Confederations Cup|Confederations Cup]] in order to secure the transfer. He became the fifth Mexican player to join PSV.<ref name="PSV" />

Lozano made his competitive debut for PSV in the [[UEFA Europa League|Europa League]] qualifiers against Croatian side [[NK Osijek|Osijek]], playing in both legs as they failed to make the group stage as a result of a 2–0 aggregate score.<ref>{{cite web|title=PSV y 'Chucky' Lozano se quedan fuera de la Europa League |url=https://www.sdpnoticias.com/deportes/2017/08/03/psv-y-chucky-lozano-se-quedan-fuera-de-la-europa-league |publisher=SDP Noticias |language=es |access-date=3 August 2017}}</ref> On 12 August, Lozano scored on his [[Eredivisie]] debut with PSV against [[AZ Alkmaar]]. He started and played 84 minutes in his team's 3–2 victory, receiving a standing ovation from the home fans as he was subbed off.<ref>{{cite web|title=Lozano brings fans to their feet; Hernandez struggles in Prem debut |url=http://www.espnfc.com/team/mexico/203/blog/post/3179450/chucky-lozano-brings-fans-to-their-feet-javier-hernandez-struggles-in-premier-league-debut |website=ESPNFC.com |publisher=ESPN |access-date=13 August 2017}}</ref> A week later, he scored once again and provided an assist in PSV's 4–0 win over [[NAC Breda]].<ref>{{cite web|title=PSV Eventually Overcome NAC Breda |url=https://www.football-oranje.com/psv-eventually-overcome-nac-breda/ |website=Football-Oranje |date=20 August 2017 |access-date=20 August 2017}}</ref> The following week, Lozano scored again in PSV's 2–0 victory against [[Roda JC]]; he became the first player to score in his first three games with PSV, and he was later named Eredivisie Player of the Month for the month of August.<ref name="potm aug">{{cite web|title=Chucky Lozano Named Eredivisie Player Of The Month After The Best Start In PSV History |url=http://the18.com/soccer-news/hirving-lozano-eredivisie-player-of-month |website=The18.com |access-date=6 September 2017}}</ref> On 10 September 2017, Lozano received his first red card for PSV in a 2–0 defeat against [[SC Heerenveen]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Video: Hirving Lozano weird red card vs Heerenveen!|url=http://wittyfutty.com/2017/09/video-hirving-lozano-weird-red-card-vs-heerenveen/|website=wittyfutty.com|access-date=10 September 2017}}</ref>

On 17 February 2018, Lozano received his second red card of the season, again during a league match against Heerenveen, for making contact with defender [[Lucas Woudenberg]]'s face. He was handed a three-match suspension which was ultimately upheld by the [[KNVB]] disciplinary committee after a failed appeal by PSV.<ref>{{cite web |title= Lozano suspended three matches after PSV red card |url= http://www.goal.com/en-us/news/hirving-lozano-suspended-three-matches-after-psv-red-card/786t19xyy8tl1wc2621xmzfhq |work=Goal|access-date= 23 February 2018}}</ref> Lozano made his return on 18 March, scoring the second goal in PSV's 3–0 victory over [[VVV Venlo]], his 14th goal of the season.<ref>{{cite web |title= Hirving Lozano, el regreso del 'terror' de un mexicano en el futbol de Holanda (FOTOS) |url= https://mundohispanico.com/noticias/deportes/hirving-lozano-el-regreso-del-terror-de-un-mexicano-en-el-futbol-de-holanda-fotos |publisher= Mundo Hispánico |language= es |access-date= 18 March 2018 |archive-date= 12 June 2018 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20180612140217/https://mundohispanico.com/noticias/deportes/hirving-lozano-el-regreso-del-terror-de-un-mexicano-en-el-futbol-de-holanda-fotos |url-status= dead }}</ref>

Lozano won the Eredivisie title with PSV following their 3–0 win over rivals [[AFC Ajax|Ajax]] on 15 April.<ref name="seal">{{cite news |title=PSV Eindhoven beat Ajax to seal title |url= https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/43776657 |publisher=BBC Sport |access-date=15 April 2018}}</ref> He ended the season as the team's highest scorer in the league with 17 league goals and providing 11 assists.<ref>{{cite web|title=The Coming of Chucky: Hirving Lozano and the Road to the Top|url=https://thesefootballtimes.co/2018/04/20/the-coming-of-chucky-hirving-lozano-and-the-road-to-the-top/|work=These Football Times|first=Charlie|last=Pritchard|date=20 April 2018|access-date=28 September 2018}}</ref>

Napoli|Napoli]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.sscnapoli.it/static/news/Napoli-confirm-signing-of-Hirving-Lozano-from-PSV-Eindhoven-18219.aspx |title=Napoli confirm signing of Hirving Lozano from PSV Eindhoven |publisher=S.S.C. ====2021–22 season====
Lozano scored his first goal of the [[2021–22 Serie A|season]] on 20 September, the fourth in a 4–0 victory over [[Udinese Calcio|Udinese]] at the [[Stadio Friuli]].<ref>{{cite web|title=El primero de muchos: golazo del 'Chucky' Lozano para el 4-0 del Napoli vs. Udinese [VIDEO]|url=https://depor.com/mexico/mexicanos-en-el-mundo/gol-hirving-lozano-en-el-napoli-vs-udinese-chucky-anoto-el-4-0-de-los-azules-por-la-serie-a-italiana-video-gol-chucky-lozano-hoy-mexico-mx-estados-unidos-usa-eeuu-nczd-noticia/|work=Depor.com|date=20 September 2021 |language=Spanish|access-date=21 September 2021}}</ref> It was his 60th goal in Europe, surpassing [[Nery Castillo]] as the fifth-highest scoring Mexican in Europe.<ref>{{cite web|last=Ramírez|first=Sergio|title=¡Superó a Nery Castillo! ==International career==
===Youth===
In December 2014, Lozano was called up by [[Sergio Almaguer]] to participate with the [[Mexico national under-20 football team|under-20]] side in the [[2015 CONCACAF U-20 Championship|CONCACAF U-20 Championship]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Sergio Almaguer dio a conocer a los convocados para el Premundial sub20|url=https://espndeportes.espn.com/noticias/nota/_/id/2261540/sergio-almaguer-dio-a-conocer-a-los-convocados-para-el-premundial-sub20|work=ESPN|date=23 December 2014|language=es}}</ref> In the first group stage match against [[Cuba national under-20 football team|Cuba]], he scored a brace and added four assists in Mexico's 9–1 win, placing them first in their group.<ref>{{cite web|title=Lozano instrumental in Mexico win|url=https://www.concacaf.com/en/under-20s-men/article/lozano-instrumental-in-mexico-win|website=Concacaf.com|publisher=CONCACAF|date=11 January 2015|access-date=15 October 2018}}</ref> He went on to score three more goals and notched another assist in the tournament. Mexico went on to win the tournament, defeating [[Panama national under-20 football team|Panama]] in a penalty shoot-out 4–2 following a 1–1 draw thus earning entry to the [[2015 FIFA U-20 World Cup]] in [[New Zealand]].<ref name="proclama">{{cite web|title=En penales, México se proclama campeón de Premundial sub 20|url=https://www.excelsior.com.mx/adrenalina/2015/01/25/1004548|work=Excelsior|date=25 January 2015|access-date=12 October 2018}}</ref> Finishing with five goals, he was proclaimed the Golden Boot winner along with United States midfielder [[Romain Gall]]; Lozano was also named in the tournament's Best XI.<ref name="TSG">{{cite web|title=TSG announces CU20 Best XI, Awards|url=https://www.concacaf.com/en/under-20s-men/article/tsg-announces-cu-20-best-xi-awards-91680|website=Concacaf.com|publisher=CONCACAF|first=Alejandro|last=Diaz|date=25 January 2015|access-date=15 October 2018}}</ref>

In May 2015, Lozano was then called up to participate in the U-20 World Cup,<ref>{{cite web|title=Almaguer define a sus guerreros para el Mundial Sub 20|url=https://www.excelsior.com.mx/adrenalina/2015/05/08/1023097|work=Excelsior|date=8 May 2015|access-date=12 October 2018}}</ref> where he would appear in all three group stage matches. Mexico would finish last in the group.<ref>{{cite web|title=México Sub 20, listo para el Mundial en Nueva Zelanda|url=http://www.milenio.com/deportes/mexico-sub-20-listo-mundial-zelanda|work=Milenio|date=8 May 2015|access-date=12 October 2018}}</ref>

On 18 September 2015, Lozano was selected by coach [[Raúl Gutiérrez]] to play in the [[2015 CONCACAF Men's Olympic Qualifying Championship|CONCACAF Olympic Qualifying Championship]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Lista definitiva de la Selección Mexicana Sub 22 para el Preolímpico|url=https://www.tudn.com/futbol/mexico/lista-definitiva-de-la-seleccion-mexicana-sub-22-para-el-preolimpico|work=Univision|date=18 September 2015}}</ref> He scored Mexico's second goal in the 2–0 semi-final win over [[Canada men's national under-23 soccer team|Canada]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Mexico tops Canada, will defend Olympic crown|url=https://www.concacaf.com/en/olympic-qualifying-men/article/mexico-tops-canada-will-defend-olympic-crown|website=Concacaf.com|first=Carlos|last=Salcedo|date=11 October 2015|access-date=10 October 2018|archive-date=11 October 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181011013746/https://www.concacaf.com/en/olympic-qualifying-men/article/mexico-tops-canada-will-defend-olympic-crown|url-status=dead}}</ref>
Mexico ultimately won the tournament after defeating [[Honduras national under-23 football team|Honduras]] 2–0 in the final,<ref name="win">{{cite web|title=Mexico 2, Honduras 0 |url=http://www.mlssoccer.com/post/2015/10/14/mexico-wrap-perfect-concacaf-olympic-qualifying-tournament-win-over-honduras |website=MLSsoccer.com |access-date=14 October 2015}}</ref> with Lozano being awarded the tournament's Golden Ball as well as being included in the tournament's Best XI.<ref name="awards"/>

On 7 July 2016, Lozano was named in the 18-man squad that would participate in that year's [[Football at the 2016 Summer Olympics|Summer Olympics]] in [[Rio de Janeiro]], Brazil.<ref>{{cite web|title=Definida la convocatoria del Tri para Juegos Olímpicos |url=http://www.mediotiempo.com/futbol/2016/07/07/definida-la-convocatoria-del-tri-para-juegos-olimpicos |work=Medio Tiempo |language=es |access-date=7 July 2016}}</ref> In Mexico's third group stage match against [[South Korea national under-23 football team|South Korea]] on 10 August, Lozano was sent off in stoppage time after shoving a Korean player. Mexico lost the match 1–0, finishing third in their group and were thus eliminated from the competition.<ref>{{cite web|title=South Korea vs. Mexico 2016: Final score 1-0, El Tri out of men's Olympic soccer tournament |url=https://www.sbnation.com/soccer/2016/8/10/12430078/mexico-south-korea-2016-results-olympics-soccer-score |website=SBNation.com |date=10 August 2016 |access-date=10 August 2016}}</ref>

On 8 June 2017, Lozano was named in Mexico's 23-man squad for the [[2017 FIFA Confederations Cup|FIFA Confederations Cup]] in Russia.<ref>{{cite web|title=Los 23 de Osorio para la Confederaciones |url=https://www.marca.com/claro-mx/futbol/seleccion-mx/2017/06/08/593936b2468aeb80498b46a4.html |work=Marca |date=8 June 2017 |access-date=10 October 2018}}</ref> In their final group stage match against the [[Russia national football team|hosts]], Lozano scored the second goal in a 2–1 win after chasing a long clearance from [[Héctor Herrera]] and beating goalkeeper [[Igor Akinfeev]] to the ball towards the edge of the box, allowing Lozano to nip in and head it into the open net.<ref>{{cite web|title=Hirving Lozano seals Mexico win to send Russia out of Confederations Cup |url=https://www.theguardian.com/football/2017/jun/24/mexico-russia-confederations-cup-hirving-lozano |work=The Guardian |date=24 June 2017 |access-date=24 June 2017}}</ref> His performance led him to be named [[Player of the match|man of the match]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Hirving Lozano: Budweiser Man of the Match (Spanish)|url=https://www.fifa.com/confederationscup/videos/hirving-lozano-budweiser-man-of-the-match-spanish-2898163|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200801172624/https://www.fifa.com/confederationscup/videos/hirving-lozano-budweiser-man-of-the-match-spanish-2898163|url-status=dead|archive-date=1 August 2020|publisher=FIFA|date=24 June 2017}}</ref>

On 4 June 2018, Lozano was named in Mexico's 23-man squad for the [[2018 FIFA World Cup|World Cup]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Convocatoria de la Selección Nacional de México |url=https://miseleccion.mx/convocatoria-la-seleccion-nacional-mexico-13/ |website=MiSeleccion.mx |language=es |access-date=4 June 2018}}</ref> On 17 June, he scored the lone goal, thanks to an assist by [[Javier Hernández]], in the opening [[2018 FIFA World Cup Group F|group]] match win over defending champions [[Germany national football team|Germany]],<ref>{{cite web|last= Ronay |first=Barney |title=Mexico's Hirving Lozano stuns world champions Germany for brilliant win |url= https://www.theguardian.com/football/2018/jun/17/germany-mexico-world-cup-match-report |work=The Guardian |date=17 June 2018 |access-date=17 June 2018}}</ref> where he was subsequently named FIFA's Man of the Match.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.fifa.com/worldcup/matches/match/300331502/#motm |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180612143900/https://www.fifa.com/worldcup/matches/match/300331502/#motm |url-status=dead |archive-date=12 June 2018 |title=Germany v Mexico – Man of the Match |publisher=FIFA |date=17 June 2018 |access-date=17 June 2018}}</ref> In Mexico's second match against [[South Korea national football team|South Korea]], Lozano provided the assist to Javier Hernández in scoring the second goal in their 2–1 win.<ref>{{cite web|title=South Korea vs Mexico, World Cup 2018: Does Barcelona transfer make sense for Hirving Lozano? Scouting report|url=https://www.independent.co.uk/sport/football/world-cup/mexico-vs-south-korea-scouting-report-hirving-lozano-barcelona-transfer-news-latest-update-a8413656.html|work=The Independent|first=Jack|last=Watson|access-date=25 June 2018}}</ref> Lozano also started in the final group fixture against [[Sweden national football team|Sweden]], and in the [[2018 FIFA World Cup knockout stage#Brazil vs Mexico|round-of-16]] match against [[Brazil national football team|Brazil]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.fifa.com/worldcup/players/player/386337/ |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180623113453/https://www.fifa.com/worldcup/players/player/386337/ |url-status=dead |archive-date=23 June 2018 |title=Hirving Lozano |publisher= FIFA |access-date=9 July 2018}}</ref>

Lozano was included in coach [[Gerardo Martino]]'s preliminary squad for the [[2019 CONCACAF Gold Cup]], however he was subsequently ruled out of the tournament after having not fully recovered from a knee injury suffered while playing with PSV.<ref>{{cite web|title=Mexico's Lozano out for summer's Gold Cup |url=http://www.espn.com/soccer/mexico/story/3862787/mexicos-lozano-out-for-summers-gold-cup?platform=amp |work=ESPN FC | date=27 May 2019 |access-date=27 May 2019}}</ref>

On 10 July 2021, Lozano suffered an injury to the head when he crashed into Trinidad and Tobago goalkeeper, [[Marvin Phillip]] who kneed Lozano on the head which also caused Lozano to have an eye gash and a neck injury and had to wear a cervical collar on the way to the hospital. It was confirmed that Lozano would miss out the rest of the [[2021 Gold Cup]] and would miss out around 4–6 weeks.

On 14 November 2022, Lozano was named in Mexico's 26-man squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup|World Cup]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Lista de convocados de la Selección Mexicana al Mundial de Qatar 2022|url=https://www.tvazteca.com/aztecadeportes/lista-oficial-convocados-seleccion-mexicana-mundial-qatar-2022|work=Tvazteca.com|first=Omar|last=Moro|date=14 November 2022|language=Spanish}}</ref>

==Player profile==
===Style of play===
{{quote box|width=33%|align=upright|quote="When you look at his playing style, without wanting to compare, you know, Lozano has what [[Lionel Messi|Messi]] also has – a centre of gravity low to the ground, the ball is usually quickly under control, very explosive and agile."|source=—[[Marcel Brands]], former Director of Football for PSV.<ref>{{cite web|title=Hirving Lozano scouting report: Verdict on whether the PSV star could cut it at Tottenham|url=https://www.football.london/tottenham-hotspur-fc/transfer-news/hirving-lozano-psv-spurs-transfers-15324389|website=Football.london|first=Alasdair|last=Gold|date=26 October 2018|access-date=29 October 2018}}</ref>}}

Mainly right-footed, Lozano is generally deployed on the [[Winger (association football)|left wing]], but can also play on the right or as a [[Forward (association football)#Centre-forward|central]] [[Striker (association football)|striker]].<ref name="PSV"/><ref>{{Cite web |title=Why Juventus must sign Hirving Lozano at all costs – and how he'd fit in |work=Calciomercato.com |date=23 June 2018 |access-date=21 September 2018 |url= https://www.calciomercato.com/en/news/why-juventus-must-sign-hirving-lozano-at-all-costs-and-how-he-d--31329}}</ref> A versatile and creative player, with good technique and an eye for goal, Lozano's shooting and [[Cross (football)|crossing]] abilities have produced good returns of goals and assists for club and country.<ref>{{Cite web |title=World Cup 2018: Young players to watch in Russia - including 'El Chucky', 'The Sergeant' & 'The Bull' |first=Chris |last=Bevan |publisher=BBC Sport |date=9 June 2018 |access-date=21 September 2018 |url= https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/44369212}}</ref><ref name="larcada">{{Cite news |title=Analysis {{!}} Chucky Lozano and Mexico's breakout young stars put the World Cup on notice |first=Albert |last=Larcada |newspaper=Washington Post |date=27 June 2018 |access-date=21 September 2018 |url= https://www.washingtonpost.com/news/fancy-stats/wp/2018/06/27/chucky-lozano-and-mexicos-breakout-young-stars-put-the-world-cup-on-notice/?noredirect=on}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.football-italia.net/141977/lozano-just-what-napoli-need |title=Lozano is just what Napoli need |publisher=Football Italia |last1=Nuzzo |first1=Massimo |date=11 August 2019 |access-date=6 October 2019 }}</ref> Lozano's style of play has been described as: "Playing on the left side of a 4–2–3–1 the vast majority of the time, right-footed Lozano has all the attributes of a classic [[inverted winger]]. Possessing great power in his right foot, he's often dangerous when shooting after cutting in from the left-hand side. [...] [He] is equally adept at creating chances as well as scoring them, with a strong left-foot he's able to deliver crosses after taking the ball down the line, although he elects to cut inside more often than choosing to go outside the opposition full-back. Lozano also has quite good creative vision, particularly for a 20 year-old."<ref>{{cite web|url=http://outsideoftheboot.com/2015/09/22/scout-report-hirving-lozano-pachucas-tricky-winger/|publisher=Outsideoftheboot|title=Hirving Lozano: Pachuca's tricky winger|first=Thomas|last=Harrison|date=22 September 2015}}</ref> He has also been described as being "[q]uick with or without the ball and possessing a powerful and accurate shot."<ref name="foot-ora">{{cite web|title=Top 50 Eredivisie U21 Stars To Watch 2017/18: Hirving Lozano. |url=http://www.football-oranje.com/top-50-eredivisie-u21-stars-watch-201718-hirving-lozano/ |website=Football-Oranje |access-date=11 August 2017}}</ref> Although more widely known for his offensive prowess, he also offers a high defensive work rate through his speed and constant pressing on the ball when not in possession, willing to chase down loose balls and often forcing defenders and goalkeepers to clear the ball long, thus allowing his team to win the ball back quickly, or even managing to steal the ball himself.<ref>{{cite web|title=Hirving Lozano – the man following in the footsteps of Luis Suarez|url=https://www.cricketsoccer.com/2018/01/13/hirving-lozano-man-following-footsteps-luis-suarez/|work=Cricketsoccer.com|first=David|last=Kraakman|date=13 January 2018|access-date=16 October 2018}}</ref><ref name="larcada"/><ref>{{cite web|title=Hirving Lozano better off at PSV than Barcelona - for now|url=http://www.goal.com/en-us/news/hirving-lozano-better-off-at-psv-than-barcelona-for-now/19ontv7uxzwlj1kllzgywfyn2a|work=Goal|first=Jon|last=Arnold|date=17 September 2018|access-date=28 September 2018}}</ref> As a result, Lozano was PSV's highest-pressing player during his stay at the Dutch club.<ref>{{cite web|title=Porto duo Hector Herrera, 'Tecatito' Corona shine for Mexico in Europe|url=http://www.espn.com/soccer/club/mexico/203/blog/post/3694271/porto-duo-hector-herreratecatito-corona-shine-for-mexico-in-europe|work=ESPN|first=Cesar|last=Hernandez|date=8 November 2018|access-date=9 November 2018}}</ref> His diminutive stature and agile playing style has been cited as the reason for being the victim of various fouls, at one point becoming the most-fouled-on player in various seasons of the Liga MX.<ref>{{cite web|title=Ni Neymar, ni Hazard... Nadie recibe tantas faltas como Hirving Lozano|url=https://www.marca.com/claro-mx/futbol/liga-mx/2017/03/04/58bae8eb22601d203b8b458c.html|work=Marca|language=es|first=Daniel|last=Panero|quote=Hirving Lozano se ha convertido en un auténtico 'punching ball' en México. El pasado Apertura recibió 100 faltas en apenas 17 jornadas, una cifra equiparable a la de Neymar en la Liga[.]|date=4 March 2017|access-date=15 November 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Pachuca's Hirving Lozano leaves on stretcher after injury vs. Tijuana|url=http://www.espn.com/soccer/mexican-liga-mx/story/3074235/pachucas-hirving-lozano-leaves-on-stretcher-after-injury-vs-tijuana|first=Tom|last=Marshall|work=ESPN|date=4 March 2017|access-date=15 November 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Hirving Lozano transfer: The clubs who could sign PSV's €40 million-rated Mexico star|url=https://www.goal.com/en-us/news/hirving-lozano-transfer-the-clubs-who-could-sign-psvs-mexico/y4xba5mu6xsh1lkalgxpiuqk1|work=Goal.com|first=Jon|last=Arnold|quote=While he was the victim of the most fouls in Liga MX in almost every season in which he played, Lozano still can have trouble coping with opponents who use physicality to body him out of games.|date=1 July 2018|access-date=15 November 2018}}</ref>

Lozano has been criticized for a lack of discipline: railing against authority during his time with Pachuca,<ref name="Sky">{{cite web|title=Hirving Lozano: The making of Mexico's breakout World Cup star|url=https://www.skysports.com/football/news/11096/11421113/hirving-lozano-the-making-of-mexicos-breakout-world-cup-star|work=Sky Sports|first=Nick|last=Wright|date=2 July 2018|access-date=10 October 2018}}</ref> being involved in on-field incidents with little provocation,<ref>{{cite web|title=Mexico's Hirving Lozano held back by lack of emotional control - Osorio|url=https://www.espn.com/soccer/mexico/story/3432659/mexicos-hirving-lozano-held-back-by-lack-of-emotional-control-juan-carlos-osorio|work=ESPN|first=Tom|last=Marshall|date=26 March 2018}}</ref> and picking up eight red cards despite at a young age: five with Pachuca, one with the Mexico U-23 team, and two red cards in his first season at PSV (tied with 2 other players for the most in the [[2017–18 Eredivisie#Discipline|2017–18 season]]).<ref>{{cite web|title=Al 'Chucky' Lozano le vuelve a salir lo diabólico|url=http://www.estadiodeportes.mx/futbol/liga-de-holanda/al-chucky-lozano-le-vuelve-a-salir-lo-diabolico-eredivise-holanda-expulsado-tarjeta-roja/|work=Estadiodeportes.mx|date=17 February 2018|access-date=9 November 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Hirving Lozano: smiling Mexico star who must learn to control his temper|url=https://www.theguardian.com/football/2018/jun/08/hirving-lozano-smiling-mexico-star-who-must-learn-to-control-his-temper|work=The Guardian|first=Manuel|last=Portillo|date=8 June 2018|access-date=28 September 2018}}</ref>

He has been compared to Uruguayan striker [[Luis Suárez]], in both playing style<ref>{{cite web|title=Veel vertrouwen in 'Luis Suarez' van PSV: "80 miljoen euro kost hij dan.."|url=https://www.voetbalprimeur.nl/nieuws/783418/veel-vertrouwen-in-luis-suarez-van-psv-80-miljoen-euro-kost-hij-dan-.html|trans-title=A lot of confidence in PSV's 'Luis Suarez': "It will cost him 80 million euros ..."|work=VoetbalPrimeur|language=Dutch|date=18 December 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Luis Suárez pone nombre a su sustituto en el Barça (y no es caro)|url=https://www.donbalon.com/noticia/detalle/76438/luis-suarez-pone-nombre-sustituto-barca-no-es-caro|trans-title=Luis Suárez names his substitute at Barça  (and he's not expensive)|work=[[Don Balón]]|first=Marcos|last=Rodríguez|language=Spanish|date=21 April 2018}}</ref> and personality.<ref>{{cite web|title='Lozano gaat te ver, net als Suárez ooit'|url=https://www.ad.nl/nederlands-voetbal/lozano-gaat-te-ver-net-als-suarez-ooit~a536f8d6/|trans-title='Lozano goes too far, just like Suárez did once'|work=[[Algemeen Dagblad|AD]]|first=Rik|last=Elfrink|language=Dutch|date=3 April 2018}}</ref> Lozano has cited [[Rafael Márquez]] and [[Damián Ariel Álvarez|Damián Álvarez]] as players he grew up idolizing.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://m.multimedios.com/deportes/futbol/pachuca/damian-idolo-del-chucky.html|title=Damián, el ídolo del 'Chucky'|publisher=Multimedios|language=es|date=3 August 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://somosinvictos.com/2016/03/28/curiosidades-preguntas-rapidas-para-chucky-lozano/|publisher=Invictos|title=Curiosidades: Preguntas Rápidas Para Chucky Lozano|language=es|date=28 March 2016}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Lozano has a younger brother named Bryan Mauricio who is also a footballer, playing as a midfielder for [[Club Universidad Nacional]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Hermano de 'Chucky' Lozano jugará con el primer equipo de Pumas|url=https://www.publimetro.com.mx/mx/deportes/2018/06/06/hermano-chucky-lozano-bryan-lozano-bahena-jugara-pumas-liga-mx.html|work=Publimetro|language=es|date=6 June 2018|access-date=18 September 2018}}</ref>

Lozano married his girlfriend Ana Obregón in 2014, whom he met while playing for Pachuca. The pair have two children; a daughter born in 2014, and a son born in 2017.<ref>{{cite web|title=¿Quién es la esposa de Hirving Lozano?|url=http://www.goal.com/es-cl/noticias/quien-es-la-esposa-de-hirving-lozano/5btl80aguo2716esl9l48zbrt|work=Goal|language=es|date=15 January 2018|access-date=18 September 2018}}</ref>

===Nickname===
Lozano is popularly known by his nick name ''[[Chucky (character)|Chucky]]'', after the doll of the [[Child's Play (franchise)|''Child's Play'']] horror film series. He reportedly got the moniker during his youth career, as he used to hide under team-mates' beds and scare them.<ref>{{cite web|title=Mexico's 'Chucky' haunts Uruguay|url=https://www.fifa.com/u20worldcup/news/y=2015/m=6/news=mexico-s-chucky-haunts-uruguay-2618183.html|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150604051804/http://www.fifa.com/u20worldcup/news/y=2015/m=6/news=mexico-s-chucky-haunts-uruguay-2618183.html|url-status=dead|archive-date=4 June 2015|publisher=FIFA|date=3 June 2015|access-date=18 September 2018}}</ref>
