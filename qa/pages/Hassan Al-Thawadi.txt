'''Hassan Al-Thawadi''' ({{lang-ar|حسن الذوادي}}) is a Qatari lawyer who was the Secretary General at Supreme Committee for Delivery and Legacy for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] Qatar Local Organizing Committee.
