'''Remko Jurian Pasveer''' (born 8 November 1983) is a Dutch professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Goalkeeper (association football)|goalkeeper]] for [[Eredivisie]] club [[AFC Ajax|Ajax]].

==Club career==
===Twente===
Pasveer started playing football at local [[Sportclub Enschede|SC Enschede]]. He then moved to [[FC Twente]], where he was reserve goalkeeper from 2003 to 2006, behind [[Cees Paauwe]] and [[Sander Boschker]]. 

===Heracles Almelo===
In the summer of 2006 he left for [[Heracles Almelo]], where he initially again had a role as backup goalkeeper, now behind [[Martin Pieckenhagen]]. In the 2008–09 and 2009–10 seasons, Pasveer was sent on loan to [[Go Ahead Eagles]], who at the time played in the second-tier [[Eerste Divisie]].<ref>{{cite news |title=Pasveer wijkt nog een jaar uit naar Go Ahead Eagles |url=https://www.vi.nl/nieuws/pasveer-wijkt-nog-een-jaar-uit-naar-go-ahead-eagles |work=Voetbal International |date=1 May 2009 |language=nl}}</ref> After a difficult start, he grew into a fan favourite in [[Deventer]]. For the 2010–11 season, Pasveer returned to Heracles, this time to in the role as starting goalkeeper. He remained as starter for four years, after which he decided not to sign a contract extension.<ref>{{cite news |title=Pasveer vertrekt bij Heracles, AZ laat Gudmundsson gaan |url=https://www.nu.nl/sport/3740429/pasveer-vertrekt-bij-heracles-az-laat-gudmundsson-gaan.html |work=NU |date=31 March 2014 |language=nl}}</ref>

[[File:PSV Eindhoven, Teamcamp Bad Erlach, July 2014 (175).jpg|thumb|left|200px|Remko Pasveer during [[PSV Eindhoven|PSV]]'s teamcamp in [[Bad Erlach]], Austria, July 2014]]

===PSV===
In the summer of 2014, Pasveer signed a three-year contract with [[PSV Eindhoven|PSV]], where he had to compete with first goalkeeper [[Jeroen Zoet]].<ref>{{cite news |title=Pasveer: 'Krijg bij PSV eerlijke kans op basisplaats' |url=https://www.vi.nl/nieuws/pasveer-krijg-bij-psv-eerlijke-kans-op-basisplaats |work=Voetbal International |date=26 June 2014 |language=nl}}</ref> He made his official debut for the [[Eindhoven]]-based club on 21 August 2014, during the first of two legs against Belarusian club [[FC Shakhtyor Soligorsk|Shakhtyor Soligorsk]] in the final qualifying round for the [[2014–15 UEFA Europa League]]. Pasveer played the entire match, which his club won 1–0 at home.<ref>{{cite news |title=Cocu: "Platform voor Pasveer om zich te laten zien" |url=https://www.fcupdate.nl/voetbalnieuws/261052/cocu-platform-voor-pasveer-om-zich-te-laten-zien/ |work=FCUpdate.nl |date=21 August 2014 |language=nl}}</ref> He played several more times that season in both [[KNVB Cup]] and Europa League matches. A week after PSV secured the [[Eredivisie]] title,<ref name="psv1415" /> Pasveer also made his league debut for the club in matchday 32 against [[SBV Excelsior|Excelsior]].<ref>{{cite news |title=PSV met Pasveer en Isimat-Mirin tegen Excelsior |url=https://www.nu.nl/voetbal/4038006/psv-met-pasveer-en-isimat-mirin-excelsior.html |work=NU |date=25 April 2015 |language=nl}}</ref> PSV triggered an option in his contract in May 2015 and thus committed him to the club until mid-2018.<ref>{{cite news |title=PSV licht optie op Pasveer: 'Spreekt vertrouwen uit' |url=https://www.vi.nl/nieuws/psv-licht-optie-op-pasveer-spreekt-vertrouwen-uit |work=Voetbal International |date=8 May 2015 |language=nl}}</ref> Pasveer won the national title with PSV for the second time in a row on 8 May 2016.<ref name="psv1516" /> The club started the last matchday of the season with as many points as rivals [[AFC Ajax|Ajax]], but with a goal difference of –6. PSV then won 1–3 at [[PEC Zwolle]] that day, while Ajax drew 1–1 at [[De Graafschap]].<ref>{{cite news |title=Ajax vergooit titel door misstap tegen De Graafschap |url=https://www.vi.nl/nieuws/ajax-vergooit-titel-door-misstap-tegen-de-graafschap |work=Voetbal International |date=8 May 2016 |language=nl}}</ref> He did not make a league appearance that season, but only played in the cup tournament. Pasveer made his [[UEFA Champions League]] debut on 1 November 2016. Due to an injury from Zoet, he a made a start in the 1–2 lost group match at home against [[FC Bayern Munich|Bayern Munich]].<ref>{{cite news |title=Pasveer kijkt uit naar Bayern München |url=https://www.rtlnieuws.nl/sport/voetbal/artikel/355661/pasveer-kijkt-uit-naar-bayern-munchen |work=RTL Nieuws |date=29 October 2016 |language=nl}}</ref> Pasveer made three league appearances during the 2016–17 season. Zoet, who was the starter that season, only conceded 23 goals in 31 appearances. With that, PSV had the statistically best defense in the Eredivisie that year, alongside the Ajax defense. The Eindhoven team finished third that season.

===Vitesse===
Pasveer signed with [[SBV Vitesse|Vitesse]] in July 2017.<ref>{{cite news |title=PSV-doelman Pasveer (33) tekent voor drie jaar bij Vitesse |url=https://www.nu.nl/voetbal/4817445/psv-doelman-pasveer-33-tekent-drie-jaar-bij-vitesse.html |work=NU |date=5 July 2017 |language=nl}}</ref> He made his debut on 5 August 2017 during the lost game for the [[Johan Cruyff Shield]] against [[Feyenoord]].<ref>{{cite news |last1=Krabbendam |first1=Martijn |title=Teleurgestelde Room meldt zich ziek, Pasveer keept in JC Schaal |url=https://www.vi.nl/nieuws/teleurgestelde-room-meldt-zich-ziek-pasveer-keept-in-jc-schaal |work=Voetbal International |date=3 August 2017 |language=nl}}</ref> His first year under head coach [[Leonid Slutsky (football coach)|Leonid Slutsky]] was difficult, but eventually he grew into a starter and [[captain (association football)|team captain]].<ref>{{cite news |title=Remko Pasveer nieuwe aanvoerder Vitesse |url=https://www.omroepgelderland.nl/nieuws/2465203/Remko-Pasveer-nieuwe-aanvoerder-Vitesse |work=Omroep Gelderland |date=12 September 2020 |language=NL}}</ref> In the 2019–20 season, he was voted "Player of the Year" by Vitesse fans.<ref>{{cite web |title=Remko Pasveer speler van het jaar seizoen 19{{!}}20 |url=https://vitesse.org/nieuws/remko-pasveer-speler-van-het-jaar-seizoen-197c20/ |website=Supportersvereniging Vitesse |language=nl |date=1 July 2020}}</ref>

===Ajax===
On 23 April 2021, it was announced that Pasveer had signed a two-year contract with [[AFC Ajax|Ajax]], joining the club on a free transfer from 1 July.<ref>{{cite web |title=Ajax signs Remko Pasveer |url=https://english.ajax.nl/articles/ajax-signs-remko-pasveer/ |website=english.ajax.nl |date=23 April 2021}}</ref> He made his debut for the club on 7 August in a 4–0 loss to PSV in the [[Johan Cruyff Shield]].<ref>{{cite news |title=PSV vernedert tiental Ajax in strijd om Johan Cruijff Schaal |url=https://www.nu.nl/voetbal/6150306/psv-vernedert-tiental-ajax-in-strijd-om-johan-cruijff-schaal.html |work=NU |date=7 August 2021 |language=nl}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Ajax vs. PSV - 7 August 2021 - Soccerway |url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2021/08/07/netherlands/super-cup/afc-ajax/psv-nv/3513238/ |website=int.soccerway.com |access-date=9 August 2021}}</ref> Due to suspensions and injuries for goalkeepers [[André Onana]] and [[Maarten Stekelenburg]], Pasveer stayed on as the starter in goal. On 19 October 2021 Pasveer earned a record as the first goalkeeper to stop three direct shots on goal from Norwegian striker [[Erling Haaland]] in a [[UEFA Champions League]] match. Pasveer went on to keep a clean sheet against [[Borussia Dortmund]] as the match ended a 4–0 win for Ajax.<ref>{{cite news |title=Pasveer ontpopt zich tot kwelgeest van Haaland (en pakt een record) |url=https://www.ad.nl/europees-voetbal/pasveer-ontpopt-zich-tot-kwelgeest-van-haaland-en-pakt-een-record~a08ea066/ |website=Algemeen Dagblad |access-date=20 October 2021}}</ref> Four days later, Pasveer kept a clean-sheet once more, in a regular season match, as Ajax defeated his former club, and [[AFC Ajax–PSV Eindhoven rivalry|rivals]] PSV Eindhoven 5–0 at home.<ref>{{cite news |title=Ajax neemt ook PSV de maat in topper (5-0) en verstevigt koppositie |url=https://nos.nl/artikel/2402982-ajax-neemt-ook-psv-de-maat-in-topper-5-0-en-verstevigt-koppositie |work=NOS |date=24 October 2021 |language=nl}}</ref> He conceded only 2 goals in the domestic league prior to matchday 12, played on 7 November 2021.<ref>{{cite web |title=Ajax resultaten - Voetbal, Nederland |url=https://www.flashscore.nl/team/ajax/8UOvIwnb/uitslagen/ |website=flashscore.nl |access-date=3 December 2021 |language=nl}}</ref> This proved to be a record, as no goalkeeper in the Eredivisie had conceded so few goals during the first 12 games before. This yielded an impressive average of 1 goal against every 4 matches, both at national and international level.<ref>{{cite web |title=Ajax - UCL - Matches |url=https://www.uefa.com/uefachampionsleague/clubs/50143--ajax/matches/ |website=UEFA.com |access-date=3 December 2021 |language=en}}</ref>

==International career==
Pasveer was part of the [[Netherlands national football team|Netherlands]] squad that won the [[2006 UEFA European Under-21 Championship]] in Portugal.<ref name="jong-oranje" />

On 22 September 2022, Pasveer made his debut for [[Netherlands national football team|the Netherlands]] as a starter against [[Poland national football team|Poland]] in a [[2022–23 UEFA Nations League A#Group 4|2022–23 UEFA Nations League]] match. At age 38, he was the second oldest player to ever debut for the Dutch national team, after [[Sander Boschker]] in 2010 (aged 39).<ref>{{cite web |date=22 September 2022 |title=Remko Pasveer is op 1-na-oudste debutant in Oranje ooit |url=https://sportnieuws.nl/voetbal/binnenland/remko-pasveer-is-op-1-na-oudste-debutant-in-oranje-ooit/ |access-date=25 September 2022 |website=Sportnieuws |language=nl-NL}}</ref>
