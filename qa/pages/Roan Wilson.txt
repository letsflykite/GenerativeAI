'''Roan Roberto Wilson Gordon''' (born 1 May 2002) is a Costa Rican professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[midfielder]] for Portuguese club [[Gil Vicente F.C.|Gil Vicente]] and the [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica national team]].

==Club career==
===Hometown club Limon FC===
Wilson began at his hometown club [[Limón F.C.|Limón]]. At 13 he bet his coach  a car that he would make it to the professional level.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/football/ng-interactive/2022/nov/16/world-cup-2022-player-profiles-qatar-match-ratings|title=World Cup 2022: A complete guide to all 831 players. Everything you need to know (and more) about every squad member in Qatar|website=The Guardian}}</ref> He became one of the young players to earn the most playing minutes at the club.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Brenes |first=Por Cristian |title=Solo 43 jóvenes futbolistas ticos logran pasar puerta abierta por el reglamento |url=https://www.nacion.com/puro-deporte/futbol-nacional/solo-43-jovenes-futbolistas-ticos-logran-pasar/EKTONFWOIFDFVP7NMSX6RC5MKE/story/ |access-date=17 September 2022 |website=La Nación |language=en}}</ref> However after financial mismanagement and relegation the club was unable to function at the same level.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Alvarado |first=Andrés |title=Limón se despide de la Primera División tras perder liguilla por el descenso ante Sporting |url=https://www.ameliarueda.com/nota/limon-desciende-tras-perder-liguilla-descenso-noticias |access-date=17 September 2022 |website=ameliarueda.com |language=es}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last=Mora |first=Sofia Barboza |date=12 February 2022 |title=El Caribe llora: Limón FC desapareció por grandes deudas |url=https://amprensa.com/2022/02/el-caribe-llora-limon-fc-desaparecio-por-grandes-deudas/ |access-date=17 September 2022 |website=AMPrensa.com |language=es}}</ref>

===Municipal Grecia===
Wilson was able to join [[Municipal Grecia]] on a free transfer in 2021. After a period of time to acclimate, such was Wilson’s form that he garnered interest from abroad, and a bid for his services from an unnamed Polish club was turned down in March 2022.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Roan Wilson, la joven promesa que Grecia pule y espera venderlo a Europa |url=https://everardoherrera.com/index.php/futbol-costa-rica-4/76-futbol-nacional/86461-roan-wilson-la-joven-promesa-que-grecia-pule-y-espera-venderlo-a-europa |access-date=17 September 2022 |website=everardoherrera.com}}</ref>

===Move to Portugal: Gil Vicente===
In January 2023 Wilson signed for Portuguese club [[Gil Vicente F.C.]] on a free transfer.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.record.pt/futebol/futebol-nacional/liga-bwin/gil-vicente/amp/oficial-roan-wilson-e-reforco-do-gil-vicente|title=Official: Roan Wilson joins Gil Vicente|website=Record.pt}}</ref>

==International career==
Born in Costa Rica, Wilson is of Jamaican descent.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rsqNSuRu2rQ|title=How many players of Jamaican Heritage will be at the World Cup?|via=www.youtube.com}}</ref> He was called up to the [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica national team]] for the crucial [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] intercontinental qualifying eliminator match against [[New Zealand national football team|New Zealand]] in May 2022, which Costa Rica ultimately won 1–0.<ref>{{Cite web |date=13 May 2022 |title=Roan Wilson, Juan Luis Pérez and Allen Guevara summoned in Sele for the playoff against New Zealand |url=https://everardoherrera.com/index.php/futbol-costa-rica-5/89178-roan-wilson-juan-luis-perez-y-allen-guevara-convocados-en-sele-de-cara-al-repechaje-ante-nueva-zelanda |website=everadoherrera.com}}</ref> He made his senior debut on 3 June 2022 in a match against [[Panama national football team|Panama]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Costa Rica inicia La Liga de Naciones con derrota en Panamá (2-0) |url=https://www.everardoherrera.com/index.php/futbol-costa-rica-5/89942-costa-rica-inicia-la-liga-de-naciones-con-derrota-en-panama-2-0 |access-date=17 September 2022 |website=www.everardoherrera.com}}</ref>
He was called up to the final 26-man [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica]] squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] in Qatar.<ref>{{cite web |date=3 November 2022 |title=Suárez eligió a sus 26 jugadores para la aventura mundialista en Catar |trans-title=Suarez chooses 26 players for the Qatar World Cup adventure |url=https://www.fedefutbol.com/2022/11/03/suarez-eligio-a-sus-26-jugadores-para-la-aventura-mundialista-en-catar/ |access-date=3 November 2022 |work=[[Costa Rican Football Federation|FCRF]] |language=es}}</ref> At the tournament, he played for a few minutes by entering as a substitute against [[Germany national football team|Germany]].<ref>{{cite news |last1=Monge |first1=Alejandro |title=1x1: Costa Rica sacó a sus mejores guerreros para hacer temblar al cuatro veces campeón del mundo |url=https://espndeportes.espn.com/futbol/costa-rica/nota/_/id/11311754/cost-rica-mejores-guerreros-temblar-cuatro-veces-campeon-mundo |access-date=2 December 2022 |work=ESPNdeportes.com |date=1 December 2022 |language=es}}</ref>
