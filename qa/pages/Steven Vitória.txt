'''Steven de Sousa Vitória''' (born 11 January 1987) is a Canadian professional [[association football|soccer]] player who plays as a [[centre-back]] for [[Primeira Liga]] club [[G.D. Chaves|Chaves]] and the [[Canada men's national soccer team|Canada national team]].

Having started out at [[FC Porto|Porto]], he went on to play for several clubs in Portugal in both the [[Primeira Liga]] and the [[Segunda Liga]], this including a one-year spell at [[S.L. Benfica|Benfica]]. He also competed professionally in the United States and Poland.

Vitória is a [[Canada men's national soccer team|Canadian international]], making his debut in 2016 at the age of 29, after previously representing Portugal at youth international level. He was part of the squads at the [[CONCACAF Gold Cup]] in [[2017 CONCACAF Gold Cup|2017]], [[2021 CONCACAF Gold Cup|2021]] and [[2023 CONCACAF Gold Cup|2023]], reaching the semi-finals in 2021, as well as at the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].

==Early life==
Born in [[Toronto]], [[Ontario]], Canada, to [[Portuguese people|Portuguese]] immigrants from the [[Azores]],<ref name=STAR>{{cite news|url=https://www.thestar.com/sports/2007/06/29/portuguese_star_happy_to_play_at_home.html|title=Portuguese star happy to play at home|newspaper=[[Toronto Star]]|first=Matthew|last=Chung|date=29 June 2007|access-date=5 February 2015}}</ref><ref name=CANADA>{{cite web|url=http://www.sportsnet.ca/soccer/2014-world-cup/csa-canada-soccer-steven-vitoria/|title=Steven Vitoria reconsiders Canada option|publisher=[[Sportsnet]]|first=Daniel|last=Fernandes|date=6 September 2012|access-date=24 April 2014}}</ref> Vitória played youth [[association football|soccer]] with the Sudbury Lions, CS Azzurri, Mississauga Falcons, Dixie SC, Kleinburg-Nobleton SC, and [[Glen Shields SC]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://canadasoccer.com/news/canada-soccer-announce-squad-for-fifa-world-cup-qatar-2022/|title=Canada Soccer announces squad for FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022|publisher=[[Canadian Soccer Association]]|date=12 November 2022|access-date=13 November 2022}}</ref> He joined the [[Woodbridge Strikers]] at the age of 17, being deployed mainly as a [[Forward (association football)#Striker|striker]]. He was approached to appear for [[Canada men's national soccer team|Canada]]'s youth national teams on multiple occasions after leaving the country,<ref name=STAR/> but declined each time.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.canadasoccer.com/mitchell-announces-u-20-squad-for-porto-international-tournament-p147179|title=Mitchell announces U-20 squad for Porto International Tournament|publisher=Canadian Soccer Association|date=5 April 2006|access-date=26 January 2016}}</ref>

==International career==
===Portugal===
Vitória represented [[Portugal national football team|Portugal]] at the [[2006 Lusophony Games]] in [[Macau]], the [[2006 UEFA European Under-19 Championship]] in [[Poland]]<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.record.xl.pt/Futebol/selecoes/interior.aspx?content_id=275561|title=Sub-19: Carlos Dinis escolhe 24 jogadores|trans-title=Under-19: Carlos Dinis chooses 24 players|newspaper=Record|language=pt|date=29 June 2006|access-date=24 April 2014}}</ref> and the [[2007 FIFA U-20 World Cup]] in his birth nation. In the latter tournament, he featured in the 2–1 group stage loss to [[Gambia national football team|Gambia]].<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.thestar.com/sports/soccer/2007/07/08/gambia_upsets_portugal_21.html|title=Gambia upsets Portugal 2–1|newspaper=Toronto Star|date=8 July 2007|access-date=19 November 2021}}</ref>

===Canada===
In September 2012, without having been [[Cap (sport)|capped]] at senior level, 25-year-old Vitória considered switching allegiance to [[Canada men's national soccer team|Canada]].<ref name=CANADA/> In January 2016, he accepted a call-up by the country for a [[Exhibition game|friendly]] against the [[United States men's national soccer team|United States]] on 5 February,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://canadasoccer.com/canada-selects-23-players-for-usa-friendly-in-february-p159103|title=Canada selects 23 players for USA friendly in February|publisher=Canadian Soccer Association|date=22 January 2016|access-date=22 January 2016}}</ref> and played the full 90 minutes in the 1–0 defeat at the [[Home Depot Center]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://canadasoccer.com/?gid=1294&t=csa_match|title=Canada vs USA – 2016-02-05|publisher=Canadian Soccer Association|date=6 February 2016|access-date=6 February 2016}}</ref> He scored his first goal on 6 October, helping to a 4–0 friendly win over [[Mauritania national football team|Mauritania]].<ref name=GOAL>{{cite web|url=https://matchcenter.mlssoccer.com/matchcenter/2016-10-06-canada-mens-national-team-vs-mauritania/recap|title=Canada 4, Mauritania 0 – International friendly recap|publisher=Major League Soccer|first=Alicia|last=Rodriguez|date=6 October 2016|access-date=28 June 2017}}</ref>

Vitória was named to the Canadian squads for the [[CONCACAF Gold Cup]] in [[2017 CONCACAF Gold Cup|2017]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mlssoccer.com/post/2017/06/27/canada-names-nine-mls-players-23-man-concacaf-gold-cup-roster|title=Canada names nine MLS players to 23-man CONCACAF Gold Cup roster|publisher=Major League Soccer|first=Dave|last=Zeitlin|date=27 June 2017|access-date=28 June 2017}}</ref> and [[2021 CONCACAF Gold Cup|2021]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.canadasoccer.com/news/canada-soccer-announces-2021-concacaf-gold-cup-roster/|title=Canada Soccer announces 2021 CONCACAF Gold Cup roster|publisher=Canadian Soccer Association|date=1 July 2021|access-date=1 July 2021}}</ref> In the latter tournament, he [[Captain (association football)|captained]] the side to the semi-final, where he was suspended for the 2–1 defeat to [[Mexico national football team|Mexico]] in [[Houston]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.cbc.ca/sports/soccer/gold-cup-canada-mexico-semifinal-recap-july-29-1.6123267|title=Canada loses heartbreaker to Mexico on late goal in tense Gold Cup semifinal|publisher=Canadian Broadcasting Corporation|date=30 July 2021|access-date=10 October 2022}}</ref>

In November 2022, Vitória was included in the squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://canpl.ca/article/canada-announces-26-man-squad-for-2022-fifa-world-cup-in-qatar|title=Canada announces 26-man squad for 2022 FIFA World Cup in Qatar|publisher=[[Canadian Premier League]]|first=Charlie|last=O'Connor-Clarke|date=13 November 2022|access-date=13 November 2022}}</ref> He made his debut in [[FIFA World Cup|the competition]] on the 23rd at the age of 35, in a 1–0 loss against [[Belgium national football team|Belgium]].<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.jn.pt/desporto/steven-vitoria-e-dificil-perder-assim-mereciamos-mais-15382905.html|title=Steven Vitória: "É difícil perder assim, merecíamos mais!"|trans-title=Steven Vitória: "It's tough to lose like this, we deserved more!"|newspaper=[[Jornal de Notícias]]|first=Bernardo|last=R. Monteiro|language=pt|date=24 November 2022|access-date=25 November 2022}}</ref>

In June 2023, Vitória was selected for the [[2023 CONCACAF Nations League Finals]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://canpl.ca/article/canada-canmnt-2023-concacaf-nations-league-final-roster|title=Former CPLers Loturi, Zator called up by Canada for 2023 Concacaf Nations League Finals|publisher=Canadian Premier League|first=Mitchell|last=Tierney|date=7 June 2023|access-date=12 June 2023}}</ref> Later that month, he made the final 23-man squad due to appear at the [[2023 CONCACAF Gold Cup|2023 Gold Cup]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://canpl.ca/article/canmnt-announces-final-23-man-squad-for-concacaf-gold-cup|title=CanMNT announces final 23-man squad for Concacaf Gold Cup|publisher=Canadian Premier League|first=Charlie|last=O'Connor-Clarke|date=19 June  2023|access-date=19 June 2023}}</ref>

==Style of play==
Vitória is known for his heading, marking and scoring abilities, especially on penalty kicks and [[Free kick (association football)|free kicks]].<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.ojogo.pt/futebol/1a-liga/benfica/noticias/steven-vitoria-e-o-segredo-dos-penaltis-3269346.html|title=Steven Vitória e o segredo dos penáltis|trans-title=Steven Vitória and the penalties' secret|newspaper=[[O Jogo]]|language=pt|date=16 June 2013|access-date=26 July 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://futebolfactory.com/2013/06/17/transfer-window-central-defender-steven-vitoria-signs-four-year-deal-with-benfica/|title=Transfer window: Central defender Steven Vitória signs four-year deal with Benfica|publisher=The Futebol Factory|date=17 June 2013|access-date=5 February 2015}}</ref>
