'''Alexander Hartmann Bah''' (born 9 December 1997) is a Danish professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[right-back]] for [[Primeira Liga]] club [[S.L. Benfica|Benfica]] and the [[Denmark national football team|Denmark national team]].

After coming through [[Næsby Boldklub|Næsby]]'s youth academy, he was promoted to the first team in 2016, and later represented fellow Danish side [[HB Køge]] and spent two years with [[SønderjyskE Fodbold|SønderjyskE]] in [[Danish Superliga]]. In January 2021, Bah joined [[Slavia Prague]], where he won the [[Czech First League]] and [[Czech Cup]] in 2021 and was named the [[Czech First League]]'s Defender of the Season in 2022, and then transferred to [[S.L. Benfica|Benfica]] for €8 million.

After representing Denmark at various youth levels, Bah was called up to the full international team for the first time in November 2020. He scored on his international debut against [[Sweden national football team|Sweden]], and went on to represent the side at the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].

==Early life==
Bah was born in [[Årslev]] on the island of [[Funen]], [[Denmark]],<ref name="nordschleswiger19">{{cite news |last1=Kragh Iversen |first1=Jens |title=Bah hat noch keine Eile |url=https://www.nordschleswiger.dk/de/sport-sonderjyske-fussball/bah-hat-noch-keine-eile |accessdate=12 November 2020 |work=[[Der Nordschleswiger]] |date=25 December 2019 |language=da}}</ref> and is of Danish and Gambian descent.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.dbold.dk/2016/09/18-aarige-bah/|title=18-årige Bah i HB Køge: Vil være stamspiller hurtigst muligt|first=Rasmus|last=Dalgaard|date=12 September 2016}}</ref> He later attended [[efterskole]] education at ISI Idrætsefterskole in [[Ikast]], where he also made appearances for local side [[Ikast FC|Ikast FS]].<ref name="isi">{{cite web |title=Det siger tidligere elever |url=https://www.isi.dk/efterskolen/linjeidraetter/fodbold |website=isi.dk |publisher=ISI Idrætsefterskole |accessdate=12 November 2020 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20200924084629/https://www.isi.dk/efterskolen/linjeidraetter/fodbold |archivedate=24 September 2020 |language=da |quote="Jeg valgte ISI på grund af skolens gode omdømme i forhold til at udvikle fodboldspillere og alle de gode faciliteter – både sportsligt og som efterskolelev."}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Ikast FS - Aabenraa BK |url=https://www.dbu.dk/resultater/kamp/991316%C2%A4202935/startopstilling |website=dbu.dk |publisher=[[Danish Football Association]] |accessdate=11 November 2020}}</ref>

==Club career==
===Early career===
Bah started his footballing career with local club Aarslev Boldklub,<ref name="aarslev">{{cite news |last1=Abildtrup |first1=Niels |title=Årslev stolt over Bahs debut |url=https://fyens.dk/artikel/%C3%A5rslev-stolt-over-bahs-debut |accessdate=11 November 2020 |work=Fyens Stiftstidende |date=21 August 2018 |language=da}}</ref> where he played for ten years before joining [[Boldklubben 1913|B 1913]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Alexander Bah: På vej mod store eventyr |url=https://www.naesbyboldklub.dk/klubben/klubnyt/nyheder-2021/alexander-bah-paa-vej-mod-store-eventyr/ |website=Næsby Boldklub |language=da |date=24 January 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last1=Abildtrup |first1=Niels |title=Gang i sagerne og sang i gaderne i fynsk fodbold - Dalum og OKS størst, mens OB på Ærø kæmper hårdt |url=https://faa.dk/artikel/gang-i-sagerne-og-sang-i-gaderne-i-fynsk-fodbold-dalum-og-oks-st%C3%B8rst-mens-ob-p%C3%A5-%C3%A6r%C3%B8-k%C3%A6mper-h%C3%A5rdt |access-date=19 December 2022 |work=Fyns Amts Avis |date=15 December 2022 |language=da}}</ref> He joined [[Næsby Boldklub]] three years later, in the spring of 2013, because the club according to Bah "[...] had a good reputation for developing young players".<ref name="interview-naesby">{{cite news |title=Interview med Alexander Bah |url=https://www.naesbyboldklub.dk/klubben/om-klubben/klubnyt/interview-med-alexander-bah/ |accessdate=11 November 2020 |work=[[Næsby Boldklub]] |date=31 March 2018 |language=da}}</ref> Furthermore, his youth coach Andreas Bech had also moved to Næsby Boldklub, which was also a significant factor in Bah's choice.<ref name="interview-naesby" /> After attending ISI Idrætsefterskole in [[Ikast]] in 2014–15, he returned to Næsby.<ref name="Career">{{cite news |title=Alexander Bah, um dinamarquês que vem de Praga para ser a nova solução do Benfica para um problema antigo |url=https://observador.pt/2022/06/07/alexander-bah-um-dinamarques-que-vem-de-praga-para-ser-a-nova-solucao-do-benfica-para-um-problema-antigo/ |accessdate=20 September 2020 |work=Observador |date=7 June 2022 |language=pt}}</ref>

===Næsby Boldklub===
Bah made his breakthrough on the first team of [[Næsby Boldklub]] in the spring of 2016.<ref name="interview-naesby" /> He started on the bench in the first three games of the season, where he impressed as a substitute.<ref name="interview-naesby" /> He made his first start in the fourth matchday of the spring against [[Boldklubben Avarta|Avarta]] on 2 April 2016, which Næsby won 4–0 at home at [[Næsby Stadium|ALPI Arena Næsby]], putting on a strong performance.<ref name="interview-naesby" /><ref>{{cite web |title=Næsby - Avarta, 2. Division Nedrykningsspil 2015/2016 |url=https://www.bold.dk/fodbold/kamp/naesby-vs-avarta/10507142/ |website=bold.dk |accessdate=12 November 2020}}</ref> He continued the season as a starter during the spring of 2016, where he made a total of six assists in 16 appearances in the third-tier [[Danish 2nd Division]].<ref>{{cite news |last1=Miles |first1=Peter |title=Næsby: Bah kan blive Superliga-spiller |url=https://www.bold.dk/fodbold/nyheder/naesby-bah-kan-blive-superliga-spiller/ |accessdate=12 November 2020 |work=bold.dk |date=10 August 2016 |language=da}}</ref><ref name="interview-naesby" />

===HB Køge===
In August 2016, it was announced that Bah moved to [[HB Køge]], where he signed a three-year contract.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Larsen |first1=Michael Patrick |title=HB Køge henter Bah i Næsby |url=https://www.dbold.dk/2016/08/hb-koege-henter-bah-naesby/ |accessdate=12 November 2020 |work=D'Bold |date=10 August 2016 |language=da}}</ref> He made his debut for the club in the [[Danish Cup]] as part of the starting lineup, where he played the first 60 minutes before being replaced by Ahmed Hassan in a 0–2 win over [[Karlslunde IF]] in the 1st round.<ref>{{cite news |title=Karlslunde 0-2 HB Køge |url=https://www.hbkoge.dk/fixture_and_result/karlslunde-hb-koege/ |accessdate=12 November 2020 |work=[[HB Køge]] |date=10 August 2016 |language=da}}</ref> In November 2017, he was named "Talent of the Year", also known as the "[[Danske Bank]] Award", for HB Køge in 2017.<ref name="Career"/>

===SønderjyskE===
On 7 August 2018, Bah signed a five-year contract with [[Danish Superliga]] club [[SønderjyskE Fodbold|SønderjyskE]].<ref>{{cite news |last1=Ravn |first1=Jacob |title=SønderjyskE henter profil i HB Køge |url=https://www.soenderjyske.dk/fodbold/soenderjyske-henter-profil-i-hb-koege/ |accessdate=12 November 2020 |work=[[SønderjyskE Fodbold]] |date=7 August 2018 |language=da}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last1=Møller Karlsen |first1=Nanna |title=SønderjyskE snupper Alexander Bah |url=https://www.bold.dk/fodbold/nyheder/soenderjyske-snupper-alexander-bah/ |accessdate=12 November 2020 |work=bold.dk |date=6 August 2018 |language=da}}</ref> He arrived at the club as a [[Midfielder#Winger|right winger]], but largely played as a [[Defender (association football)#Wing-back|right wing-back]] with great success, which, among other things, have contributed to SønderjyskE winning a [[Danish Cup]] title and Bah making his full international debut for the [[Denmark national football team|Denmark national team]]. He was among the three nominated for Superliga Player of the Month in November 2020, but saw the award go to [[Jesper Lindstrøm]] of [[Brøndby IF]].<ref>{{cite news |last1=Dehn |first1=Anders |title=Brøndby-bombere og Bah: Her er de nominerede til Superliga-pris |url=https://www.tipsbladet.dk/nyhed/superliga/broendby-bombere-og-bah-her-er-de-nominerede-til-superliga-pris |access-date=2 January 2021 |work=[[Tipsbladet]] |date=2 December 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201202155713/https://www.tipsbladet.dk/nyhed/superliga/broendby-bombere-og-bah-her-er-de-nominerede-til-superliga-pris |archive-date=2 December 2020 |language=da}}</ref>

===Slavia Prague===
On 5 January 2021, Bah signed for [[Czech First League]] club [[SK Slavia Prague|Slavia Prague]] on a four-and-a-half year contract.<ref>{{cite web |title=Slavii posílil dánský reprezentant Alexander Bah |url=https://www.slavia.cz/clanek.asp?id=Slavii-posilil-dansky-reprezentant-Alexander-Bah-18047 |website=slavia.cz |publisher=[[SK Slavia Prague]] |access-date=6 January 2021 |language=cs |date=5 January 2021}}</ref> In doing so, SønderjyskE received a club record fee reported to be around DKK 13 million.<ref>{{cite news |title=SønderjyskE sælger forsvarsspiller i rekordhandel |url=https://sport.tv2.dk/fodbold/2021-01-05-soenderjyske-saelger-forsvarsspiller-i-rekordhandel |access-date=6 January 2021 |work=[[TV 2 (Denmark)|TV 2]] |date=5 January 2021 |language=da}}</ref> He made his debut for the club as a starter on 16 January in a 3–1 win over [[SK Sigma Olomouc|Sigma Olomouc]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Happy for my debut and for important points |url=https://en.slavia.cz/clanek.asp?id=746 |website=en.slavia.cz |publisher=Slavia Prague |access-date=1 February 2021 |date=17 January 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Slavia po dvou penaltách porazila na úvod ligového jara Olomouc |url=https://www.ceskenoviny.cz/zpravy/slavia-po-dvou-penaltach-porazila-na-uvod-ligoveho-jara-olomouc/1983408 |access-date=1 February 2021 |work=České Noviny |date=16 January 2021 |language=cs}}</ref> Bah arrived at the club as a replacement for [[Vladimír Coufal]], who left the for English [[Premier League]] side [[West Ham United]], during the season, quickly became a starter in the right-back slot, after impressing his coach [[Jindřich Trpišovský]] for his defensive positioning and attacking play, helping his team win the [[Double (association football)|domestic double]] of the [[Czech First League]] and [[Czech Cup]].<ref>{{Cite web | title = He came, he saw, he conquers. How the Danish bachelor Bah settled in Slavia| website = iDNES.cz | url = https://www.idnes.cz/fotbal/prvni-liga/alexander-bah-slavia-praha-cesky-mistr.A210506_083826_fotbal_malu
|date= 6 May 2021 |access-date= 9 April 2022}}</ref><ref name="Career"/>

The following season, Bah was named the Czech First League's Defender of the Season, making 26 appearances and scoring three goals as Slavia Prague finished runners-up behind [[Viktoria Plzeň]], while also helping the club reached the quarter-finals of the inaugural [[2021–22 UEFA Europa Conference League|UEFA Europa Conference League]], where his team were eliminated, after losing 6–4 on aggregate to eventual finalists [[Feyenoord]].<ref>{{Cite web| title = Slavia - Feyenoord 1:3. Prague finish in KL, Mandous made a fatal mistake| website = iSport.cz| url = https://isport.blesk.cz/clanek/fotbal-evropske-pohary-konferencni-liga/412360/slavia-feyenoord-1-3-prazane-v-kl-konci-mandous-osudove-chyboval.html| language = cs| date = 3 June 2022}}</ref>

===Benfica===
On 7 June 2022, Bah signed a five-year contract with [[Primeira Liga]] side [[S.L. Benfica|Benfica]], for a reported fee of €8 million, joining former Slavia Prague teammate [[Petar Musa]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Alexander Bah is a Benfica player! |url=https://www.slbenfica.pt/en-us/agora/noticias/2022/06/07/futebol-benfica-alexander-bah-contratacao |website=S.L. Benfica |access-date=7 June 2022 |date=7 June 2022}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Águias Confirmam Contratação de Bah |trans-title=Eagles confirm Bah's signing |url=https://www.abola.pt/nnh/2022-06-07/benfica-aguias-confirmam-contratacao-de-bah/944507 |access-date=7 June 2022 |publisher=A Bola |date=7 June 2022 |language=pt}}</ref> He made his debut for the club on 5 August, replacing [[Gilberto (footballer, born 1993)|Gilberto]] in the 63rd minute and providing the assist for [[Rafa Silva]]'s goal in a 4–0 home win over [[F.C. Arouca|Arouca]] in the [[Primeira Liga]].<ref>{{cite web|date=5 August 2022|title=Rafa bisa frente ao Arouca|url=https://www.abola.pt/nnh/2022-08-05/benfica-rafa-bisa-frente-ao-arouca-video/951876|publisher=[[A Bola]]}}</ref>

==International career==
Bah represented [[Denmark national football team|Denmark]] at [[Denmark national under-20 football team|under-20]] and [[Denmark national under-21 football team|under-21]], for a total of 2 [[Cap (sport)|caps]].<ref name="SW">{{cite web |title=A. Bah - Profile |url=https://int.soccerway.com/players/alexander-hartmann-bah/454794/ |website=Soccerway |publisher=Perform Group |access-date=12 November 2020}}</ref>

On 9 November 2020, he was called up to [[Kasper Hjulmand]]'s senior squad for the [[Exhibition game|friendly]] against [[Sweden national football team|Sweden]] due to several cancellations from, among others, the Danish national team players playing in England, due to the [[COVID-19 pandemic in England|COVID-19 restrictions]], as well as a case of [[COVID-19]] in the squad, which had put several national team players in quarantine. He came on as a substitute at half-time during the match against Sweden and scored the final goal in a 2–0 win at [[Brøndby Stadium]] in his debut match, on 11 November 2020.<ref>{{cite news |title=Wind og Bah sikrer dansk 2-0 sejr over Sverige |url=https://dbu.dk/nyheder/2020/november/wind-og-bah-sikrer-dansk-2-0-sejr-over-sverige/ |accessdate=12 November 2020 |work=[[Danish Football Association]] |date=11 November 2020 |language=da |quote=Et flot dansk angreb med mange afleveringer – og hvor den svenske defensiv nærmest virkede rundtossede – var Wind, Mæhle og Damsgaard de afgørende spilstationer, inden debutanten Alexander Bah afsluttede angrebet med en stensikker scoring. Flot angreb, flot afslutning!}}</ref>

==Style of play==
Bah is mainly known for his speed, energy and offensive capabilities, as well as his technique, creativity and crossing ability. He is capable of playing as a full-back or winger on the right flank. He is an aggressive player who attacks spaces and seeks to break deep blocks with accurate passes, capable of receiving a pass from on the byline and finding the penalty spot with a pinpoint cross to allow his teammate to convert.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://breakingthelines.com/player-analysis/alexander-bah-the-answer-to-benficas-right-back-woes-2/|title=Alexander Bah: The Answer to Benfica's Right-Back Woes? |website=Breaking The Lines|date=22 June 2022}}</ref>
