'''Joel Robert Waterman''' (born January 24, 1996) is a Canadian [[Association football|soccer]] player who plays for [[CF Montréal]] of [[Major League Soccer]] and the [[Canada men's national soccer team|Canada national team]]. 

==Early life==
Waterman began playing soccer at age five with Aldergrove Youth SC.<ref name=canada>{{Canada Soccer player|id=13480}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.aldergrovesoccer.com/2019/07/04/former-aysc-player-joel-waterman-playing-against-vancouver-whitecaps-july-24/|title=Former AYSC Player Joel Waterman Playing Against Vancouver Whitecaps July 24|date=July 4, 2019|work=Aldergrove YSC}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.aldergrovestar.com/sports/aldergrove-entourage-swarms-bc-place-for-local-soccer-player-gone-pro/|title=Aldergrove entourage swarms BC Place for local player gone pro|date=July 31, 2019|work=[[Aldergrove Star]]|first=Sarah|last=Grochowski}}</ref> At age 12, he joined Langley United.<ref name=being>{{Cite web|url=https://gospartans.ca/news/2020/7/7/sparta-being-joel-waterman.aspx|work=[[Trinity Western Spartans]]|date=July 7, 2017|title=Being Joel|first=Mark|last=Janzen}}</ref> Afterwards, he played with [[Surrey United SC]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://aftn.ca/get-to-know-your-tss-rovers-joel-waterman/|title=Get To Know Your TSS Rovers: Joel Waterman|date=April 18, 2017|work=Away from the Numbers|first=Michael|last=McColl}}</ref>

When he was 13, he made the British Columbia provincial team.<ref name=being /> In 2013, he represented British Columbia at the [[2013 Canada Summer Games]]<ref name=being /> and also had a brief trial with the [[Vancouver Whitecaps Academy]], but did not make the squad.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.rds.ca/soccer/cfm/joel-waterman-la-constance-d-un-grand-neglige-1.16323070|title=Joel Waterman ou la constance d'un grand négligé|trans-title=Joel Waterman or the constancy of a great underdog|language=fr|first=Nicolas|last=Landry|date=October 22, 2022|work=[[Réseau des sports|RDS]]}}</ref>

when he was in Grade 11.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.aldergrovestar.com/sports/aldergrove-athlete-now-able-to-play-as-himself-in-favourite-childhood-video-game|title=Aldergrove pro athlete now ==Club career==
===Early career===
In 2016, Waterman played in the US-based [[USL League Two|Premier Development League]] side [[Kitsap Pumas]] and made eleven league appearances,<ref name=16pdl>{{cite web |title=Joel Waterman 2016 PDL Stats |url=https://www.uslleaguetwo.com/roster_players/12440583?subseason=278141 |publisher=[[USL League Two|Premier Development League]]}}</ref> while also appearing in two [[US Open Cup]] matches.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.lapresse.ca/sports/soccer/2020-01-14/l-impact-acquiert-le-defenseur-central-joel-waterman|title=L'Impact acquiert le défenseur central Joel Waterman|trans-title=The Impact acquires central defender Joel Waterman|language=fr|date=January 14, 2020|work=[[La Presse (Montréal)|La Presse]]}}</ref> In 2017, Waterman played with Vancouver-based [[TSS FC Rovers]] in the PDL, where he made eleven appearances.<ref name=17pdl>{{cite web |title=Joel Waterman 2017 PDL Stats|url=https://www.uslleaguetwo.com/roster_players/18033337?subseason=376352 |publisher=[[USL League Two|Premier Development League]]}}</ref> In 2018, Waterman played for [[Calgary Foothills FC|Calgary Foothills]] in the PDL, making fourteen league appearances and another three in the playoffs as Foothills won the PDL Championship.<ref name=18pdl>{{cite web |title=Joel Waterman 2018 PDL Stats|url=https://www.uslleaguetwo.com/roster_players/24767475 |publisher=[[USL League Two|Premier Development League]]}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=December 12, 2018|url=https://nsxinetwork.com/pdl-pedigree-with-a-cpl-destiny-newly-drafted-joel-waterman-looks-to-bring-leadership-to-cavalry-fc/|title=PDL pedigree with a CPL destiny: Newly drafted Joel Waterman looks to bring leadership to Cavalry FC|first=Jacob|last=Noseworthy|work=NSXI Network}}</ref>

Star]]|first=Sarah|last=Grochowski}}</ref> Waterman made his professional debut for Cavalry in their inaugural game against [[York9 FC]] on May 4.<ref>{{Cite match.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://theprovince.com/sports/soccer/mls/ballistic-ascent-of-aldergroves-joel-waterman-continues-with-canmnt-invite|title=Ballistic ascent of Aldergrove's Joel Waterman
