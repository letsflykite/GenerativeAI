'''Henry Josué Martín Mex''' (born 18 November 1992) is a Mexican professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Forward (association football)|forward]] for [[Liga MX]] club [[Club América|América]] and the [[Mexico national football team|Mexico national team]].

==Club career==
===Itzaes===
At age 15, Martín played one season in the [[Tercera División de México|third division]] with [[F.C. Itzaes|Itzaes]] during the Clausura 2008 season. He made 13 league appearances and scored 6 goals.

===Mérida===
Martín began his professional career in 2013 with his home town club [[Venados F.C.|Mérida]]. He made his professional debut with the first team on 13 July 2013 against Delfines del Carmen, where he was subbed in for José Luis Pineda in the final seconds of the match. He scored his first league goal on 24 August against [[Atlético Zacatepec|Zacatepec]] where the match ended 2–0.

===Tijuana===
Martín transferred to [[Club Tijuana]] for the Apertura 2014 season. He made his [[Liga MX]] debut with Tijuana against [[Club América|América]] on 26 July 2014, almost exactly one year after his career debut.<ref>{{cite web|title=Henry Martín recordó su debut con Tijuana contra América en el Estadio Azteca hace seis años en la Liga MX|url=https://americamonumental.bolavip.com/noticias/Henry-Martin-recordo-su-debut-con-Tijuana-contra-America-en-el-Estadio-Azteca-hace-seis-anos-en-la-Liga-MX-20200727-0006.html|work=America Monumental|date=27 July 2020|language=es}}</ref> He scored his first goal three days later in a [[Copa MX]] match against [[Atlético Zacatepec|Zacatepec]], where Tijuana won 3–1.

===América===
On 13 December 2017, Martín joined [[Club América]], reuniting with former Tijuana coach [[Miguel Herrera]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.clubamerica.com.mx/comunicados/bienvenido-henry-martin/|title=¡Bienvenido Henry Martín!|trans-title=Welcome Henry Martin!|language=es|date=13 December 2017|access-date=22 March 2018}}</ref> He also scored on his league debut with the club in a 1–0 away win against [[Querétaro F.C.|Querétaro]] on 7 January 2018.<ref>{{cite web|title=Henry Martín y su debut con gol en América: "Es un sueño llegar a un equipo grande y aprovecharlo"|url=https://www.tudn.com/futbol/liga-mx/henry-martin-y-su-debut-con-gol-en-america-es-un-sueno-llegar-a-un-equipo-grande-y-aprovecharlo-video|work=TUDN|date=9 January 2018}}</ref> On 3 February, Martín scored his first career [[hat-trick]] in a 5–1 win over [[Lobos BUAP]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Match Recap: Club América 5, Lobos BUAP 1|url=https://www.fmfstateofmind.com/2018/2/4/16970990/match-recap-club-america-5-lobos-buap-1|work=FMF State of Mind|date=4 February 2018}}</ref>

As América won the [[Clausura 2019 Copa MX]] [[Clausura 2019 Copa MX Final|final]] against [[FC Juárez|Juárez]], Martín finished as the tournament's top scorer with 5 goals.<ref>{{cite web|title=Henry Martín fue el máximo goleador de la Copa MX Clausura 2019|url=https://americamonumental.bolavip.com/copamx/Henry-Martin-fue-el-maximo-goleador-de-la-Copa-MX-Clausura-2019-20190411-0035.html}}</ref>

==International career==
On 30 August 2015, Martín received his first call-up by interim head coach [[Ricardo Ferretti]] to replace the injured [[Oribe Peralta]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Montes, Peralta out for Mexico friendlies|url=https://www.foxsports.com/stories/soccer/montes-peralta-out-for-mexico-friendlies|work=Fox Sports}}</ref> He earned his first cap with [[Mexico national football team|Mexico]] on 4 September in a friendly match against [[Trinidad and Tobago national football team|Trinidad and Tobago]], playing as starter and eventually subbed-out for [[Raúl Jiménez]] in the 59th minute of the match.<ref>{{cite web|title=Delantero Henry Martin causa baja del Tri por lesión, informa la FMF|date=5 September 2015|url=https://www.espn.com.mx/noticias/nota/_/id/2465493/delantero-henry-martin-causa-baja-del-tri-por-lesion-informa-la-fmf}}</ref>

On 11 October 2018, Martín scored his first goal for Mexico in their 3–2 win over [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Henry Martín se estrena con el Tri y pide sitio para el futuro|url=https://www.marca.com/claro-mx/futbol/seleccion-mx/2018/10/12/5bc09ff5e2704e68948b460f.html|work=Marca|date=12 October 2018}}</ref>

Martín was called up by [[Jaime Lozano]] as one of three over-age reinforcements for the [[2020 Summer Olympics]] in [[Tokyo]]. He won the [[bronze medal]] with the Olympic team.<ref>{{cite web|title=Selección Mexicana: La Convocatoria oficial de Jaime Lozano para los Juegos Olímpicos|url=https://www.soyfutbol.com/seleccionmexicana/Seleccion-Mexicana-La-Convocatoria-oficial-de-Jaime-Lozano-para-los-Juegos-Olimpicos-20210630-0042.html}}</ref>

In October 2022, Martín was named in Mexico's preliminary 31-man [[Mexico national football team|squad]] for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]], and in November, he was ultimately included in the final 26-man roster.<ref>{{cite web|title=Lista de convocados de la Selección Mexicana al Mundial de Qatar 2022|url=https://www.tvazteca.com/aztecadeportes/lista-oficial-convocados-seleccion-mexicana-mundial-qatar-2022|work=Tvazteca.com|first=Omar|last=Moro|date=14 November 2022|language=Spanish}}</ref> In Mexico's third match at the tournament on November 30, Martín scored a goal against [[Saudi Arabia national football team|Saudi Arabia]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://dknetwork.draftkings.com/2022/11/30/23486677/mexico-goal-videovs-saudi-arabia-henry-martin-world-cup-highlights-2022|first=Chinmay|last=Vaidya|title=Henry Martin scores Mexico’s first 2022 World Cup goal vs. Saudi Arabia [VIDEO]|date=November 30, 2022}}</ref>

In June 2023, Martín  was named to Mexico's 23-man squad for the [[2023 CONCACAF Gold Cup]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://en.as.com/soccer/mexico-national-team-squad-for-the-gold-cup-2023-selected-players-absences-n/|first=Roddy|last=Cons|title=Mexico national team squad for the Gold Cup 2023: selected players, absences...|date=June 20, 2023}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Henry's older brother, [[Freddy Martín|Freddy]], was also a professional footballer who played as a forward.<ref>{{cite news |date=15 January 2016 |title=Liga MX ofrece duelo de hermanos Yucatecos |newspaper=Sipse.com |trans-title=Liga MX offers duel of Yucateco brothers |url=https://sipse.com/deportes/hermanos-yucatecos-disputaran-partido-futbo-primera-division-187268.html |access-date=22 March 2018 |language=es|last1=Sipse |first1=Redacción }}</ref>
