'''Jesús Noel Valenzuela Sáez''' (born 24 November 1983) is a Venezuelan [[referee (association football)|football referee]] who is a listed as an international referee for [[FIFA]] since 2013.<ref>{{cite web |year=2021 |title=FIFA 2021 Refereeing International Lists |url=https://digitalhub.fifa.com/m/73018d1c158c1fd5/original/2021-FIFA-Refereeing-International-Lists.pdf |publisher=FIFA |access-date=30 January 2021}}</ref>

He made his officiating debut in the [[2011–12 Venezuelan Primera División season]].<ref name="Arbitro">{{cite web |url=https://www.livefutbol.com/ficha_arbitro/jesus-valenzuela/ |title=Jesús Valenzuela: Partidos como árbitro |website=livefutbol.com |access-date=6 July 2019 |language=es}}</ref><ref name="Partidos">{{cite web |url=https://www.livefutbol.com/arbitro/ven-primera-division-2011-2012-apertura/1/ |title=Venezuela: Primera División 2011/2012 Apertura: Árbitros |website=livefutbol.com |access-date=6 July 2019 |language=es}}</ref><ref name="PrimeraDivisión">{{cite web |url=https://www.livefutbol.com/ficha_arbitro/jesus-valenzuela/3/1/ven-primera-division-2011-2012-apertura/ |title=Jesús Valenzuela: Primera División 2011/2012 Apertura |website=livefutbol.com |access-date=6 July 2019 |language=es}}</ref>

On 14 December 2021, Valenzuela was named [[CONMEBOL]]'s best referee for 2021 by the [[IFFHS]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=IFFHS MEN'S CONTINENTAL AWARDS 2021 – CONMEBOL |url=https://www.iffhs.com/posts/1520 |date=14 December 2021 |access-date=18 December 2021 |publisher=IFFHS}}</ref>
