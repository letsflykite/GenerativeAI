'''Jan Seidel''' (born 10 October 1984) is a German [[Association football|football]] [[Referee (association football)|referee]].

Jan Seidel lives in [[Oberkrämer]]. He is a [[German Football Association|DFB]] referee for Grün-Weiß Brieselang. He has managed games in the [[Regionalliga Nord|North Regional league]] since the 2008–09 season, and he has managed the games in the [[3. Liga|3rd division]] from the 2009–10 season up until the 2011–12 season. He later specialized as an [[Assistant referee (association football)|assistant referee]].

As a linesman, Seidel has been involved in the [[2. Bundesliga]] games since the 2009/10 season and the [[Bundesliga]] games since the 2012–13 season. Since the 2016–17 season he has been a linesman of the [[UEFA Europa League]], and since the 2017–18 season, the [[UEFA Champions League]].

In April 2021, he was nominated along with [[Rafael Foltyn]] to be part of [[Daniel Siebert (referee)|Daniel Siebert]]’s refereeing team for the [[UEFA Euro 2020]] in June and July 2021.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.uefa.com/insideuefa/mediaservices/mediareleases/news/0268-12182cf1475f-825d23faf300-1000--euro-2020-referee-teams-appointed/ |title=Referee teams for UEFA EURO 2020 appointed |date=21 April 2021|publisher=UEFA |access-date=25 May 2022}}</ref>

In May 2022, he was once again nominated together with Rafael Foltyn to be part of Daniel Siebert's refereeing team for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] in November and December 2022.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.spiegel.de/sport/fussball/fussball-wm-in-katar-fifa-nominiert-erstmals-drei-schiedsrichterinnen-a-f45d93c5-1c34-4c0b-8c25-480a4f90f09a |title=Fifa nominiert erstmals drei Schiedsrichterinnen |date=19 May 2022|publisher=Spiegel |access-date=25 May 2022}}</ref>
