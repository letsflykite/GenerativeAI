'''Alphonso Boyle Davies''' (born November 2, 2000) is a Canadian professional [[Association football|soccer]]<!--Davies is a Canadian and the name of the sport in Canada is "soccer". MOS:TIES applies to the WP:LANGVAR used.--> player who plays as a [[Defender (association football)#Full-back|left-back]] or [[Midfielder#Winger|winger]] for {{German football updater|BaMunich}} club [[FC Bayern Munich|Bayern Munich]] and the [[Canada men's national soccer team|Canada national team]]. Considered one of the best full-backs in the world, Davies has earned the nickname "''The'' ''[[Roadrunner]]"'' for his exceptional pace, dribbling ability, and creativity.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Mithani|first=Priyank|date=January 8, 2022|title=5 most valuable full-backs in the world right now - January 2022|url=https://www.sportskeeda.com/football/5-valuable-full-backs-world-right-now-january-2022-cancelo-alexander-arnold|access-date=January 31, 2022|website=Sportskeeda|archive-date=January 31, 2022|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220131131750/https://www.sportskeeda.com/football/5-valuable-full-backs-world-right-now-january-2022-cancelo-alexander-arnold|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last1=McCambridge|first1=Ed|last2=published|first2=Mark White|date=October 15, 2021|title=Best left-backs in the world: the top 10, ranked|url=https://www.fourfourtwo.com/us/features/best-left-backs-world-ranked-alphonso-davies-david-alaba-ben-chilwell-andrew-robertson-jordi-alba|access-date=January 31, 2022|website=FourFourTwo|archive-date=June 24, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210624195657/https://www.fourfourtwo.com/us/features/best-left-backs-world-ranked-alphonso-davies-david-alaba-ben-chilwell-andrew-robertson-jordi-alba|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news |title=The best left-back in the world! Davies' rise has Bayern eyeing Champions League glory |url=https://www.goal.com/en-us/news/the-best-left-back-in-the-world-davies-rise-has-bayern/1rzjc5ncsuhxo1vb90oxqemolp |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211019112417/https://www.goal.com/en-us/news/the-best-left-back-in-the-world-davies-rise-has-bayern/1rzjc5ncsuhxo1vb90oxqemolp |archive-date=October 19, 2021 |access-date=June 16, 2021 |website=Goal (website)}}</ref>

Davies was the first player born in the 2000s to play in a [[Major League Soccer]] match. He joined Bayern in January 2019 from MLS side [[Vancouver Whitecaps FC]] on a contract lasting until 2023 for a then-record MLS transfer fee.<ref>{{Cite news |date=July 25, 2018 |title=FC Bayern verpflichtet Alphonso Davies bis 2023 |language=de |trans-title=Bayern commits Alphonso Davies until 2023 |work=FC Bayern Munich |url=https://fcbayern.com/de/news/2018/07/fc-bayern-verpflichtet-alphonso-davies-bis-2023 |url-status=live |access-date=July 25, 2018 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190329074442/https://fcbayern.com/de/news/2018/07/fc-bayern-verpflichtet-alphonso-davies-bis-2023 |archive-date=March 29, 2019}}</ref><ref name="MLS record" /> Davies was named the Bundesliga Rookie of the Season for [[2019–20 Bundesliga|2019–20]].<ref name="roty" /> In that season he also was part of the team winning the [[Treble (association football)|continental treble]] after Bayern won the Bundesliga, the [[2019–20 UEFA Champions League|UEFA Champions League]] and the [[2019–20 DFB-Pokal|DFB-Pokal]].

Born in Ghana to Liberian parents, Davies obtained Canadian citizenship in June 2017; he was until then a Liberian national. Later that month, he became the youngest player to appear for the Canada national team.<ref name="talent belies his youth">{{Cite web |last=Buhler |first=Andy |date=July 5, 2017 |title=Canadian soccer phenom Alphonso Davies' talent belies his youth |url=https://www.thestar.com/sports/soccer/2017/07/05/canadian-soccer-phenom-alphonso-davies-talent-belies-his-youth.html |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200125155057/https://www.thestar.com/sports/soccer/2017/07/05/canadian-soccer-phenom-alphonso-davies-talent-belies-his-youth.html |archive-date=January 25, 2020 |access-date=July 8, 2017 |website=The Star}}</ref> By scoring two goals in a [[2017 CONCACAF Gold Cup]] match against [[French Guiana national football team|French Guiana]], he became the youngest player to score for the team, the youngest to score at the [[CONCACAF Gold Cup]], and first player born in the 2000s to score at a top level international tournament.<ref name="becomes first">{{Cite news |last=Schuller |first=Rudi |title=Davies becomes first 2000s-born player to score at a top level international tournament |url=http://www.goal.com/en-us/news/588/gold-cup/2017/07/07/36915632/davies-becomes-first-2000s-born-player-to-score-at-a-top |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170718222931/http://www.goal.com/en-us/news/588/gold-cup/2017/07/07/36915632/davies-becomes-first-2000s-born-player-to-score-at-a-top |archive-date=July 18, 2017 |access-date=July 8, 2017 |work=Goal (website)}}</ref> He would go on to be named [[CONCACAF Awards#Player of the Year|CONCACAF Men's Player of the Year]] in [[2021 CONCACAF Awards|2021]] and [[2022 CONCACAF Awards|2022]].<ref name="CONCACAF POTY"/><ref name="CONCACAF POTY 2"/> Davies was called up to play in the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]], where he scored Canada's first ever World Cup goal. He is widely considered both the best player on and the face of the Canada national team.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Kloke |first=Joshua |date=March 22, 2022 |title=How Canada have continued their success without Alphonso Davies, who could be on the verge of becoming 'the most famous Canadian athlete' |url=https://theathletic.com/3202461/2022/03/22/how-canada-have-continued-their-success-without-alphonso-davies-who-could-be-on-the-verge-of-becoming-the-most-famous-canadian-athlete/ |access-date=June 10, 2022 |website=[[The Athletic]] |archive-date=June 10, 2022 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220610132752/https://theathletic.com/3202461/2022/03/22/how-canada-have-continued-their-success-without-alphonso-davies-who-could-be-on-the-verge-of-becoming-the-most-famous-canadian-athlete/ |url-status=live }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Callaghan|first=Joe|date=October 14, 2021|title=Alphonso Davies' Canadian moment has arrived. Davies is the first soccer player, and first Canadian, to become an ambassador for the United Nations refugee agency, [[United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees|UNHCR]].<ref name=unhcr>{{cite news |last=Stone |first=Simon |date=March 24, 2021 |title=Bayern Munich defender Alphonso Davies on his parents fleeing war and his efforts to help refugees |work=BBC Sport |url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/56507473 |url-status=live |access-date=March 24, 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210324111152/https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/56507473 |archive-date=March 24, 2021}}</ref>

In May 2022, Davies was called up to the Canada squad for two [[2022–23 CONCACAF Nations League|CONCACAF Nations League]] matches and a friendly.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://twitter.com/canadasocceren/status/1529540157539590145 |title=Canada Soccer's Men's National Team have announced their squad for the upcoming series of three international matches in June including the 2022-23 edition of Concacaf Nations League |access-date=May 29, 2022 |website=Twitter |language=en |archive-date=May 29, 2022 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220529114002/https://twitter.com/canadasocceren/status/1529540157539590145 |url-status=live }}</ref> In the June 9 game against [[Curaçao national football team|Curaçao]], he marked his return to international competition after his myocarditis-related absence with two goals in a 4–0 victory for Canada.<ref>{{Cite web |date=June 10, 2022 |last=Karstens-Smith |first=Gemma |title=Davies scores twice, Canada tops Curacao in CONCACAF Nations League play |url=https://www.cbc.ca/sports/soccer/canada-soccer-curaco-june-5-recap-1.6484080 |access-date=June 10, 2022 |website=[[CBC Sports]] |archive-date=June 10, 2022 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220610044435/https://www.cbc.ca/sports/soccer/canada-soccer-curaco-june-5-recap-1.6484080 |url-status=live }}</ref>

In November 2022, Davies was named to [[2022 FIFA World Cup squads#Canada|Canada's squad]] for the 2022 FIFA World Cup.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://canpl.ca/article/canada-announces-26-man-squad-for-2022-fifa-world-cup-in-qatar|date=November 13, 2022|title=Canada announces 26-man squad for 2022 FIFA World Cup in Qatar|work=[[Canadian Premier League]]|first=Charlie|last=O'Connor-Clarke|access-date=November 13, 2022|archive-date=November 13, 2022|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20221113160308/https://canpl.ca/article/canada-announces-26-man-squad-for-2022-fifa-world-cup-in-qatar|url-status=live}}</ref> In Canada's first match of the tournament against [[Belgium national football team|Belgium]] on November 23, he failed to score an early penalty in an eventual 1–0 loss, with [[Thibaut Courtois]] saving his shot.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mlssoccer.com/news/belgium-spoil-canada-s-world-cup-return-behind-thibaut-courtois-masterclass|title=Belgium spoil Canada's World Cup return behind Thibaut Courtois masterclass|publisher=www.mlssoccer.com|date=November 23, 2022|access-date=November 23, 2022|archive-date=November 23, 2022|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20221123223720/https://www.mlssoccer.com/news/belgium-spoil-canada-s-world-cup-return-behind-thibaut-courtois-masterclass|url-status=live}}</ref> Four days later, he scored Canada's first ever goal at the [[FIFA World Cup]] in the match against [[Croatia national football team|Croatia]]; however, Croatia came back to win 4–1, eliminating Canada from the tournament after two matches.<ref>{{Cite news |author=Bryan Murphy |title=Alphonso Davies World Cup goal for Canada vs Croatia is nation's first in men's FIFA World Cup history |url=https://www.sportingnews.com/us/soccer/news/alphonso-davies-world-cup-goal-canada-croatia-first-history/eeh1fmtqtmbcn9akthdj7lvn |website=The Sporting News |date=November 27, 2022 |access-date=November 27, 2022 |archive-date=November 27, 2022 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20221127165516/https://www.sportingnews.com/us/soccer/news/alphonso-davies-world-cup-goal-canada-croatia-first-history/eeh1fmtqtmbcn9akthdj7lvn |url-status=live }}</ref>

In June 2023, Davies was named to the final 23-man squad for Canada ahead of the [[2023 CONCACAF Nations League Finals]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Former CPLers Loturi, Zator called up by Canada for 2023 Concacaf Nations League Finals|date=June 7, 2023|url=https://canpl.ca/article/canada-canmnt-2023-concacaf-nations-league-final-roster|first=Mitchell|last=Tierney|work=[[Canadian Premier League]]}}</ref> Having missed the final months of the club season due to a hamstring injury, Davies did not start in the semi-final against Panama, but came off the bench as a substitute in the second half and scored the team's second goal in a 2–0 victory. Canada advanced to the tournament final against [[United States men's national soccer team|the United States]], their first tournament final in 23 years.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.cbc.ca/sports/soccer/soccer-canada-concacaf-nations-league-semifinal-panama-recap-1.6878600|title=Davies, David each score as Canada blanks Panama to reach CONCACAF Nations League final|website=[[CBC Sports]]|date=June 15, 2023|access-date=June 15, 2013}}</ref>

==Style of play==
Although mainly playing as an attacking full back on the left flank, Davies is capable of playing as a [[Defender (association football)#Wing-back|wing-back]], [[Midfielder#Wide midfielder|left midfielder]], or [[Midfielder#Winger|winger]] due to his explosive pace, [[Dribbling#Association football|dribbling]], creativity, and [[Cross (football)|crossing]] ability. He is considered to be a highly promising young player in the media.<ref name="Bayern Munich: Who is new signing Alphonso Davies?" /><ref>{{Cite web |date=August 15, 2018 |title=Scout Report: Alphonso Davies – Speedy Canadian Winger |url=http://outsideoftheboot.com/2018/08/15/scout-report-alphonso-davies-speedy-canadian-winger/ |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200124132545/http://outsideoftheboot.com/2018/08/15/scout-report-alphonso-davies-speedy-canadian-winger/ |archive-date=January 24, 2020 |access-date=August 14, 2020 |publisher=Outside of the Boot}}</ref>
