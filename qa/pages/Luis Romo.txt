'''Luis Francisco Romo Barrón''' (born 5 June 1995) is a Mexican professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Midfielder#Defensive midfielder|defensive midfielder]] for [[Liga MX]] club [[C.F. Monterrey|Monterrey]] and the [[Mexico national football team|Mexico national team]].

==Club career==
===Querétaro===
Born in [[Sinaloa]], Romo began his career playing for the youth squads of [[Cruz Azul]]. At the age of 15, Romo was cut by Cruz Azul, and eventually joined the youth squads of [[Querétaro F.C.|Querétaro]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Luis Romo, el niño 'desechado' de Cruz Azul que volvió para ser campeón|url=https://www.milenio.com/futbol/club-cruz-azul/luis-romo-desechado-cruz-azul-volvio-campeon|publisher=Milenio|language=Spanish|date=30 May 2021|access-date=21 June 2021}}</ref>

He made his [[Liga MX]] debut with Querétaro on 20 July 2018, in the team's opening match of the [[2018–19 Liga MX season|season]], against [[Atlas F.C.|Atlas]].<ref name="Queretaro">{{cite web|title=Con un valor duplicado, Luis Romo regresa a La Corregidora|url=https://espndeportes.espn.com/futbol/mexico/nota/_/id/7278384/luis-romo-regreso-corregidora-duplica-su-valor|publisher=ESPN|language=es|date=12 August 2020}}</ref> By the [[2019–20 Liga MX season#Torneo Apertura|2019 Apertura]], he was named club [[Captain (association football)|captain]].<ref name="Queretaro"/> It proved to be Romo's final campaign with Querétaro, playing in 17 matches and scoring three goals.<ref>{{cite web|title=Cruz Azul hizo oficial el fichaje de Luis Romo para el C2020|url=https://mexico.as.com/mexico/2020/01/02/futbol/1577971133_391261.html|website=as.com|date=2 January 2020 |language=Spanish|access-date=2 January 2020}}</ref>

===Cruz Azul===
In January 2020, he joined Cruz Azul, returning to the club he had played for as a youngster.<ref>{{cite web|title=Liga MX: Luis Romo, la sorpresa de Cruz Azul para el 2020|url=https://www.foxdeportes.com/liga-mx/story/liga-mx-luis-romo-la-sorpresa-de-cruz-azul-para-el-2020/|publisher=Fox Deportes|language=es|date=2 January 2020|first=Enrique|last=Gomez}}</ref> He made his league debut for the club on the 17th of the month in a 2–1 defeat to [[Atlético San Luis]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Razones de la derrota de Cruz Azul ante Atlético de San Luis|url=https://www.espn.com.mx/futbol/mexico/nota/_/id/6531938/razones-de-la-derrota-de-cruz-azul-ante-atletico-de-san-luis|publisher=ESPN|date=17 January 2020|language=es}}</ref> On 25 July, he scored his first goal for ''La Máquina'' in a 3–0 victory over [[Santos Laguna]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Luis Romo, la bujía en el esquema del Cruz Azul|url=https://espndeportes.espn.com/futbol/mexico/nota/_/id/7370535/luis-romo-siboldi-cruz-azul-pieza-importante|publisher=ESPN|language=es|date=1 September 2020}}</ref> On 3 December, he scored his first brace in Cruz Azul's 4–0 first leg victory over [[Club Universidad Nacional|Pumas UNAM]] in the [[Guardianes 2020 Liga MX final phase#Semi-finals|Guardianes 2020 semi-finals]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Luis Romo firma su doblete y le da forma a la goleada celeste ante Pumas|url=https://www.marca.com/claro-mx/futbol/liga-mx/2020/12/04/5fc9ad98e2704ef08c8b4594.html|publisher=Marca Claro|date=3 December 2020|language=Spanish}}</ref> He played in the return leg of the series which Cruz Azul lost 4–0, resulting in the team's elimination 4–4 on aggregate.<ref>{{cite web|title=Cruz Azul y una histórica eliminación: Pumas logró la hazaña, remontó un 4-0 y avanzó a la final del fútbol mexicano|date=7 December 2020 |url=https://www.lanacion.com.ar/deportes/futbol/futbol-mexico-cruz-azul-eliminado-pumas-semifinales-nid2533177/|publisher=La Nacion|language=Spanish|access-date=7 December 2020}}</ref>

Romo played an integral part in Cruz Azul's championship-winning [[2020–21 Liga MX season#Torneo Guardianes 2021|Guardianes 2021]] campaign. He played in 19 matches, scored three goals, and provided seven assists,<ref>{{cite web|title=Luis Romo es elegido como el mejor jugador del Guard1anes 2021|url=https://www.milenio.com/futbol/club-cruz-azul/luis-romo-cruz-azul-elegido-figura-guardianes-2021|publisher=Milenio|language=Spanish|date=10 June 2021|access-date=21 June 2021}}</ref> and was named the league's best player.<ref name=Mejor>{{cite web|title=Luis Romo ha sido elegido como el mejor jugador del Guardianes 2021|date=10 June 2021 |url=https://www.90min.com/es/posts/luis-romo-ha-sido-elegido-como-el-mejor-jugador-del-guardianes-2021}}</ref>

==International career==
Romo received his first call up to the [[Mexico national football team|senior national team]] by [[Gerardo Martino]], and made his debut on 19 November 2019 in the 2–1 [[2019–20 CONCACAF Nations League A|CONCACAF Nations League]] victory against [[Bermuda national football team|Bermuda]].<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[CONCACAF]]|url=https://www.concacafnationsleague.com/en/nations-league/game-detail/476585#tab_match=match-details|title=Mexico v Bermuda game report|date=19 November 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=How Mexico have turned things around under Gerardo "Tata" Martino in 2019|url=https://www.espn.com/soccer/mexico-mex/story/3994412/how-mexico-have-turned-things-around-under-gerardo-tata-martino-in-2019|publisher=ESPN|first=Tom|last=Marshall|date=20 November 2019}}</ref>

Romo was called up by [[Jaime Lozano]] as one of three over-age reinforcements for the [[2020 Summer Olympics]] in [[Tokyo]]. He won the [[bronze medal]] with the Olympic team.<ref>{{cite web|title=Luis Romo sería uno de los refuerzos de la Sub-23 para los Juegos Olímpicos|url=https://futbol.radioformula.com.mx/nacional/luis-romo-cruz-azul-seleccion-mexicana-refuerzo-juegos-olimpicos-fut/}}</ref>

In October 2022, Romo was named in Mexico's preliminary 31-man [[Mexico national football team|squad]] for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]], and in November, he was ultimately included in the final 26-man roster, but did not receive any minutes on the field during the tournament.<ref>{{cite web|title=Lista de convocados de la Selección Mexicana al Mundial de Qatar 2022|url=https://www.tvazteca.com/aztecadeportes/lista-oficial-convocados-seleccion-mexicana-mundial-qatar-2022|work=Tvazteca.com|first=Omar|last=Moro|date=14 November 2022|language=Spanish}}</ref>
