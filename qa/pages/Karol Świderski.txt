'''Karol Grzegorz Świderski''' (born 23 January 1997) is a Polish professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[striker (association football)|striker]] for [[Major League Soccer]] club [[Charlotte FC]] and the [[Poland national football team|Poland national team]].

==Club career==
===Jagiellonia Białystok===
Świderski began his career in the youth academy of Rawia Rawicz. From 2012 to 2014, he was in the sports school of [[UKS SMS Łódź]]. Then, in 2014, he went on to play for [[Ekstraklasa]] side [[Jagiellonia Białystok]], playing at first in their III Liga team and their youth academy. He eventually started playing exclusively in their first team in the 2016–17 season.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.gol24.pl/ekstraklasa/a/oficjalnie-patryk-tuszynski-i-karol-swiderski-pilkarzami-jagiellonii,9560386/ |title=Oficjalnie: Patryk Tuszyński i Karol Świderski piłkarzami Jagiellonii |language=pl |publisher=gol24.pl |date=20 June 2014 |access-date=5 November 2017}}</ref>

Świderski was only 17 when he made his debut in the Ekstraklasa for Jagiellonia on 23 August 2014, in a 3–1 loss against [[Śląsk Wrocław]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.90minut.pl/news/238/news2381509-T-Mobile-Ekstraklasa-Jagiellonia-1-3-Slask.html |title=T-Mobile Ekstraklasa: Jagiellonia 1-3 Śląsk |language=pl |publisher=90minut.pl |date=23 August 2014 |access-date=4 November 2017}}</ref> On 3 June 2015, in an away match, he scored his first professional goal against [[Pogoń Szczecin]] in the 80th minute, in a 3–1 win.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.90minut.pl/news/251/news2515724-T-Mobile-Ekstraklasa-Pogon-1-3-Jagiellonia-Jagiellonia-w-Lidze-Europy.html |title=T-Mobile Ekstraklasa: Pogoń 1-3 Jagiellonia. Jagiellonia w Lidze Europy |language=pl |publisher=90minut.pl |date=3 June 2015 |access-date=4 November 2017}}</ref>

Świderski's UEFA debut came on 2 July 2015, where he scored and won the game for Jagiellonia in a 1–0 win against [[FK Kruoja Pakruojis|Kruoja Pakruojis]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.90minut.pl/news/252/news2528384-Jagiellonia-zwycieza-w-koncowce.html |title=Jagiellonia zwycięża w końcówce |language=pl |publisher=90minut.pl |date=2 July 2015 |access-date=4 November 2017}}</ref> He also played in the reverse leg, which Jagiellonia won 8–0 and scored in the 4th minute.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.90minut.pl/news/253/news2531342-Pokruojeni.html |title=Pokruojeni |language=pl |publisher=90minut.pl |date=9 July 2015 |access-date=4 November 2017}}</ref>

===PAOK===
On 20 January 2019, PAOK have confirmed the signing of Świderski, penning a 3.5-year contract with the Polish forward on a €2 million transfer fee.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.contra.gr/Soccer/Hellas/Superleague/paok/swiderski-paok-prijovic.5417635.html |title=Ο Σβιντέρσκι είναι καλύτερος από τον Πρίγιοβιτς του '17 |language=el |publisher=www.contra.gr |date=20 January 2019}}</ref> He debuted for the club on 27 January 2019, being subbed in the 81st minute for [[Omar El Kaddouri]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://poranny.pl/karol-swiderski-zadebiutowal-w-greckiej-ekstraklasie-a-jego-paok-saloniki-wygral/ar/13840126 |title=Karol Świderski zadebiutował w greckiej ekstraklasie, a jego PAOK Saloniki wygrał |language=pl |publisher=poranny.pl |date=27 January 2019 |access-date=30 January 2019}}</ref> On 30 January 2019, Świderski's first goal came in the 87th minute, after coming off the bench for [[Dimitris Limnios]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://poranny.pl/karol-swiderski-strzelil-gola-a-paok-saloniki-wygral/ar/13849038 |title=Karol Świderski strzelił gola, a PAOK Saloniki wygrał!|language=pl |publisher=poranny.pl |date=30 January 2019 |access-date=30 January 2019}}</ref> On 18 February 2019, Świderski scored as a substitute in a 5–1 away win against [[Apollon Smyrni]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Απόλλων - ΠΑΟΚ 1-5: Καθάρισε σε 33' και καλπάζει για το πρωτάθλημα|url=https://www.sport24.gr/football/ellada/SuperLeague/apollwn-paok-1-5-katharise-se-33-kai-kalpazei-gia-to-prwtathlhma.5443531.html |publisher=www.sport24.gr |date=18 February 2019}}</ref> On 10 March 2019, he scored in a 3–0 home win against [[Atromitos F.C.|Atromitos]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sport24.gr/football/ellada/SuperLeague/paok-atromhtos-3-0-astamathtos-gia-to-ahtthto.5460304.html |title=ΠΑΟΚ - Ατρόμητος 3-0: Ασταμάτητος για το αήττητο! |publisher=Sport 24| language=el| date=10 March 2019| access-date=11 March 2019}}</ref> On 21 April 2019, he scored the final goal in a 5–0 defeat of [[Levadiakos F.C.|Levadiakos]] that confirmed PAOKs first league title for 34 years.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.agonasport.com/agonasport-allnews/five-star-paok-crowned-super-league-champions |title=Five-star PAOK crowned Super League champions |first=Graham |last=Wood |website=Agonasport |date=21 April 2019 |access-date=30 September 2019}}</ref>

On 25 August 2019, he scored his first goal of the [[2019–20 Superleague Greece|2019–20 season]] with a header, opening the score, in a 2–1 home win game against [[Panetolikos F.C.|Panetolikos]],<ref>{{cite news|title=ΠΑΟΚ - Παναιτωλικός 2-1: Έπαιξε όσο χρειαζόταν για το τρίποντο |url=https://www.sport24.gr/football/ellada/SuperLeague/paok-panaitwlikos-2-1-epaikse-oso-xreiazotan-gia-to-triponto.5583506.html |publisher=www.sport24.gr |date=25 August 2019}}</ref> and a week later, he scored again in a 2–1 home win against [[Panionios F.C.|Panionios]].<ref>{{cite news|title=ΠΑΟΚ - Πανιώνιος 2-1: Αγχωτική νίκη με Σβιντέρσκι |url=https://www.sport24.gr/football/ellada/SuperLeague/paok-paniwnios-2-1-agxwtikh-nikh-me-svinterski.5587856.html |publisher=www.sport24.gr |date=1 September 2019}}</ref> On 29 August, he scored after an assist from [[Rodrigo Soares]] as PAOK beat [[ŠK Slovan Bratislava]] in the [[UEFA Europa League|second leg of their Europa League play-off]] tie but were eliminated on away goals after the score finished 3–3 on aggregate.<ref>{{cite news|title=PAOK vs Slovan Bratislava 3-2 |url=https://www.skysports.com/football/paok-snika-vs-slovan/420125 |publisher=www.skysports.com |date=29 August 2019}}</ref> On 29 September 2019, he scored both PAOK's goals in a 2–2 draw city rivals [[AEK Athens F.C.|AEK Athens]].<ref>{{cite news|title=ΑΕΚ - ΠΑΟΚ 2-2: Η ντερμπάρα των Δικεφάλων δεν είχε νικητή |url=https://www.sport24.gr/football/ellada/SuperLeague/aek-paok-2-2-h-ntermpara-twn-dikefalwn-den-eixe-nikhth.5607775.html |publisher=www.sport24.gr |date=29 September 2019}}</ref> On 4 January 2020, the Polish forward gleefully slotted home the rebound after [[Julián Cuesta]] clumsily spilled a shot from [[Dimitris Pelkas]], to open the score in an away 4–2 loss against rivals [[Aris Thessaloniki F.C.|Aris]], as PAOK were finally beaten after 51 games in [[Superleague Greece|Superleague]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Άρης - ΠΑΟΚ 4-2: Του έσπασε το αήττητο με τεσσάρα |url=https://www.sport24.gr/football/ellada/SuperLeague/arhs-paok-4-2-toy-espase-to-ahtthto-me-tessara.5665367.html |publisher=www.sport24.gr |date=4 January 2020}}</ref> On 19 January 2020, Swiderski finished clinically with a low shot on the turn to open the score in a 3–1 home win game against [[Asteras Tripoli]].<ref>{{cite news|title=ΠΑΟΚ - Αστέρας 3-1: "Καθάρισε" o Δικέφαλος με την ποιότητά του |url=https://www.sport24.gr/football/ellada/SuperLeague/paok-asteras-3-1-katharise-o-dikefalos-me-thn-poiothta-toy.5671614.html |publisher=www.sport24.gr |date=19 January 2020}}</ref> On 12 February 2020, Świderski received a wonderful pass from [[Dimitris Pelkas]], and the Polish striker subsequently beat [[Sokratis Dioudis]] with a pinpoint attempt, helping reigning [[Greek Cup]] winners PAOK breezed through to the semi-finals of the Greek Cup, defeating Panathinaikos 1–0 in Athens to complete a 3–0 aggregate triumph.<ref>{{cite news|title=Παναθηναϊκός - ΠΑΟΚ 0-1: Τον πλήγωσε ένα δικό του παιδί |url=https://www.sport24.gr/football/ellada/GreekCup/panathhnaikos-paok-0-1-ton-plhgwse-ena-diko-toy-paidi.5681422.html |publisher=www.sport24.gr |date=12 February 2020}}</ref>

On 4 October 2020, he scored a brace in the last 3 minutes of the game sealing a triumphiant 3–0 home win against [[OFI Crete F.C.|OFI]].<ref>{{cite news|title=ΠΑΟΚ - ΟΦΗ 3-0: Επέστρεψε στις νίκες με τριάρα |url=https://www.sport24.gr/football/paok-ofi-3-0-epestrepse-stis-nikes-me-triara.9007565.html |publisher=www.sport24.gr |date=4 October 2020}}</ref> On 31 January 2021, he scored a brace in a 5–0 home win against [[Panetolikos F.C.|Panetolikos]].

On 19 January 2022, PAOK accepted [[MLS]] club [[Charlotte FC]]'s €5 million offer for Świderski. Świderski played in 28 matches [[2021-22 Super League Greece|this season]], scoring 4 goals and 3 assists. In his last match with the club, the Polish international striker entered the game as a late substitute, in the derby [[Greek Cup]] game against [[AEK Athens]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Liga grecka. ===Charlotte FC===
On 26 January 2022, [[Charlotte FC]] announced the signing of Świderski from PAOK to a Designated Player contract through the 2025 season with an option for 2026. The 25-year-old Polish international will occupy an international roster slot on the club’s inaugural roster.<ref>{{cite news|title=Η Σάρλοτ ανακοίνωσε την απόκτηση του Σβιντέρσκι |url=https://www.sport24.gr/football/i-sarlot-anakoinose-tin-apoktisi-toy-svinterski.9508163.html |publisher=www.sport24.gr |date=26 January 2022}}</ref>

==International career==
===U-18===
Świderski was chosen in March 2015 by the Polish U-18 national team coach Rafał Janas to play in the LFF U-18 Federation Cup. He made his debut in the winner's match against Georgia, which Poland won 1–0.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.90minut.pl/news/248/news2480724-Puchar-Federacji-LFF-U-18-Polska-1-0-Gruzja.html |title=Puchar Federacji LFF U-18: Polska 1-0 Gruzja |language=pl |publisher=90minut.pl |date=24 March 2015 |access-date=4 November 2017}}</ref> Świderski played again and scored his first goal in the 9th minute against Latvia, where they drew 1–1.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.90minut.pl/news/248/news2481090-Puchar-Federacji-LFF-U-18-Lotwa-1-1-Polska.html |title=Puchar Federacji LFF U-18: Łotwa 1-1 Polska |language=pl |publisher=90minut.pl |date=25 March 2015 |access-date=4 November 2017}}</ref>

===U-19===
Świderski made his debut for the U-19 team on 5 September 2015 in a friendly against Wales, which ended in a 1–1 draw.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.90minut.pl/news/255/news2559310-U-19-Walia-1-1-Polska.html |title=U-19: Walia 1-1 Polska |language=pl |publisher=90minut.pl |date=5 September 2015 |access-date=4 November 2017}}</ref> He played two friendlies against Slovenia, scoring both the goals in the first match, which ended in a 2–2 draw.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.90minut.pl/news/257/news2575593-U-19-Polska-2-2-Slowenia.html |title=U-19: Polska 2-2 Słowenia |language=pl |publisher=90minut.pl |date=10 October 2015 |access-date=4 November 2017}}</ref> He was successful in the U-19 European Championship qualifier in Cyprus against Bulgaria, Cyprus and Luxembourg, scoring 3 goals.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.uefa.com/under19/season=2016/standings/round=2000601/group=2003575/index.html |title=Group 3 |publisher=uefa.com |date=22 July 2016 |access-date=4 November 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.90minut.pl/news/258/news2582325-U-19-Kadra-na-turniej-eME.html |title=U-19: Kadra na turniej eME |language=pl |publisher=90minut.pl |date=26 October 2016 |access-date=4 November 2017}}</ref>

Karol Świderski czeka na szansę. Strzela i gra częściej niż Arkadiusz Milik oraz Krzysztof Piątek|language=pl |publisher=sport.tvp.pl |date=28 March 2021 |access-date=28 March 2021}}</ref> He was later included in the Polish squads for the [[UEFA Euro 2020]]<ref>{{cite news |url=https://pzpn.pl/reprezentacje/reprezentacja-a/aktualnosci/2021-05-17/paulo-sousa-oglosil-powolania-na-uefa-euro-2020 |title=Paulo Sousa ogłosił powołania na UEFA EURO 2020 |trans-title=Paulo Sousa announced appointments for UEFA Euro 2020 |work=[[Polish Football Association]] |date=17 May 2021 |access-date=17 May 2021 |language=pl}}</ref> and [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://pzpn.pl/reprezentacje/reprezentacja-a/aktualnosci/2022-11-10/czeslaw-michniewicz-oglosil-kadre-na-mundial-w-katarze |title=Czesław Michniewicz ogłosił kadrę na mundial w Katarze |trans-title=Czesław Michniewicz has announced the team for the World Cup in Qatar |publisher=[[Polish Football Association]] |date=10 November 2022 |access-date=10 November 2022 |language=pl}}</ref>
