'''Ángel Israel Mena Delgado''' (born 21 January 1988) is an Ecuadorian professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Forward (association football)#Winger|winger]] for [[Liga MX]] club [[Club León|León]] and the [[Ecuador national football team|Ecuador national team]].

==Club career==
===Emelec===
Mena had extended his contract until 2017 with [[Club Sport Emelec]]. He played in 129 games for the national championship and scored 25 goals, according to records of the Ecuadorian Football Federation (FEF).<ref name="Universo 2015">{{cite web | last=Universo | first=El | title=Ángel Mena seguirá en Emelec hasta el 2017 | website=El Universo | date=24 February 2015 | url=http://www.eluniverso.com/deportes/2015/02/24/nota/4588906/angel-mena-seguira-emelec-hasta-2017 | language=es | accessdate=7 March 2015}}</ref>

He debuted in the first division in 2007. He scored his first official goal on 16 March 2008 at the [[Club Deportivo Técnico Universitario|Técnico Universitario]].{{citation needed|date=March 2015}}

In 2010, he was loaned to [[Club Deportivo Cuenca|Deportivo Cuenca]]. In 2011, he returns to [[Club Sport Emelec]], and was champion in [[2013 Campeonato Ecuatoriano de Fútbol Serie A|2013]] and [[2014 Campeonato Ecuatoriano de Fútbol Serie A|2014]].{{citation needed|date=March 2015}}

After scoring 14 goals as Emelec won the 2014 championship, Mena extended his contract with the club for two years in February 2015.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=El Telegrafo|title=El 'Ángel' se queda en Emelec|url=http://www.telegrafo.com.ec/futbol-nacional/item/el-angel-se-queda-en-casa.html|language=Spanish|date=25 February 2015}}</ref> Mena scored the first goal in the 2014 Ecuadorian Serie A season final match against [[Barcelona SC]], winning 3–0.

===Cruz Azul===
In 2017 Mena joined Mexican side [[Cruz Azul]]. On 12 February Mena scored an impressive free-kick goal against [[Santos Laguna]] which earned Cruz Azul a 2–2 draw.<ref>{{cite web|title=Golazo de Ángel Mena con el Cruz Azul|date=13 February 2017|accessdate=17 March 2017|url=http://www.eluniverso.com/deportes/2017/02/13/nota/6046720/golazo-angel-mena-cruz-azul|publisher=[[El Universo]]}}</ref>

==International career==
In April 2014, being in Emelec, Mena was first called up to the Ecuador national football team, led by coach [[Reinaldo Rueda]], for a microcycle. Mena was called for Ecuador's provisional 30-man squad for [[World Cup 2014]] in Brazil.<ref name="ITV – Player, TV Guide, ITV, ITV2, catchup, X Factor, Vera, Coronation Street, Emmerdale 2014">{{cite web | title=Valencia named in Ecuador World Cup squad | website=ITV – Player, TV Guide, ITV, ITV2, catchup, X Factor, Vera, Coronation Street, Emmerdale | date=14 May 2014 | url=http://www.itv.com/worldcup/valencia-named-in-ecuador-world-cup-squad | accessdate=7 March 2015}}</ref>

Later in 2014, the interim coach of the Ecuador national team, [[Sixto Vizuete]] called up Mena to play friendly matches against [[Bolivia national football team|Bolivia]] and [[Brazil national football team|Brazil]].<ref name="juarango 2014">{{cite web | last=juarango | first=Juan Arango – | title=Ecuador makes callup for Bolivia and Brazil friendlies | website=Soccerly | date=22 August 2014 | url=http://soccerly.com/article/juarango/ecuador-makes-callup-for-bolivia-and-brazil-friendlies | accessdate=7 March 2015}}</ref> However, Mena suffered an injury that prevented him playing with the national team.{{citation needed|date=March 2015}}

Mena made his debut on 28 March 2015 in a match against [[Mexico national football team|Mexico]], coming in as a sub for [[Jefferson Montero]].

==Personal life==
Mena is married to Shirley Posligua with whom he has two daughters. He and his family are devout [[Christianity|Christian]]s. Mena is good friends with fellow footballer [[Joao Rojas (footballer, born 1989)|Joao Rojas]] with whom he shared a locker while at Emelec, the fact that Rojas played for Cruz Azul was a significant factor in motivating Mena to join the club.<ref>{{cite web|title=Ángel Mena alista su viaje a México|url=http://www.elcomercio.com/deportes/angelmena-viaje-mexico-cruzazul-futbol.html|accessdate=17 March 2017|language=Spanish|date=14 December 2016|publisher=[[El Comercio (Ecuador)|El Comercio]]}}</ref>
