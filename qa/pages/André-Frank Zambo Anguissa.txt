'''André-Frank Zambo Anguissa''' (born 16 November 1995) is a Cameroonian professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[midfielder]] for {{Italian football updater|Napoli}} club [[S.S.C. Napoli|Napoli]] and the [[Cameroon national football team|Cameroon national team]].<ref name="soccerway">{{cite web|url=https://int.soccerway.com/players/franck-zambo/394835/|title=Z. Anguissa |website=Soccerway |access-date=27 August 2018}}</ref> 

==Club career==
Zambo Anguissa joined [[Olympique de Marseille|Marseille]] in 2015 from [[Stade de Reims]]. He made his first team debut on 17 September 2015 in the [[UEFA Europa League]] against [[FC Groningen]].<ref name="soccerway2">{{cite web|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2015/09/17/europe/uefa-cup/stichting-fc-groningen/olympique-de-marseille/2147201/|title=Groningen vs. Marseille – 17 September 2015 |website=Soccerway |accessdate=24 September 2015}}</ref> Three days later, he made his [[Ligue 1]] debut against [[Olympique Lyonnais|Lyon]].<ref name="soccerway3">{{cite web|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2015/09/20/france/ligue-1/olympique-de-marseille/olympique-lyonnais/2045909/|title=Marseille vs. Lyon – 20 September 2015 |website=Soccerway |accessdate=24 September 2015}}</ref>

On 16 May 2018, he played in the [[2018 UEFA Europa League Final]] which Marseille lost against [[Atlético Madrid]] at the [[Parc Olympique Lyonnais]] in [[Décines-Charpieu]], [[Lyon Metropolis|Lyon]], [[France]].<ref name="host">{{cite web|url=http://www.uefa.com/uefaeuropaleague/news/newsid=2398004.html |title=Lyon to host 2018 UEFA Europa League Final |publisher=UEFA |date=9 December 2016 |accessdate=9 December 2016}}</ref>

===Fulham===
In August 2018, he was transferred to [[Fulham F.C.|Fulham]] from Marseille and was loaned to [[Villarreal CF|Villarreal]] in July 2019 following Fulham's relegation from the [[Premier League]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/49134505 |title=Andre-Frank Zambo Anguissa: Villarreal sign Fulham midfielder on loan |website=BBC Sport |date=26 July 2019 | accessdate=26 July 2019}}</ref> He made his Villarreal debut on 17 August, playing full 90 minutes in a 4–4 home draw against [[Granada CF]].<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.marca.com/en/football/spanish-football/cronica/2019/08/18/5d5885deca4741365e8b45aa.html|title=Villarreal and Granada draw in eight-goal thriller|newspaper=Marca|date=18 August 2019|accessdate=18 August 2019}}</ref>

On 31 August 2021, Anguissa signed a new three-year contract with the Championship club before heading out on loan to [[S.S.C. Napoli|Napoli]] for the [[2021–22 S.S.C. Napoli season|2021–22 season]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=Napoli formalizes the purchase of Anguissa with the formula of the loan with the right of redemption |url=https://www.sscnapoli.it/static/news/Il-Napoli-ufficializza-l-acquisto-di-Anguissa-con-la-formula-del-prestito-con-diritto-di-riscatto-23331.aspx|website=S.S.C. Napoli|date=31 August 2021|access-date=31 August 2021|language=it}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=André-Frank Zambo Anguissa is on route to becoming one of Europe's finest defensive midfielders |url=https://napoliseriea.com/andre-frank-zambo-anguissa-defensive-midfielders|website=Napoliseriea|date=25 October 2021|access-date=5 October 2021|language=en}}</ref>

===Napoli===
On 26 May 2022, Napoli purchased Anguissa's rights from Fulham.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=S.S.C. Napoli|url=https://www.sscnapoli.it/static/news/Il-Napoli-ufficializza-il-riscatto-di-Frank-Anguissa-dal-Fulham-25184.aspx|title=Il Napoli ufficializza il riscatto di Frank Anguissa dal Fulham|date=26 May 2022|accessdate=9 June 2022|language=it}}</ref> On 7 September, he scored his first [[UEFA Champions League|Champions League]] goal in a 4–1 win over [[Liverpool F.C.|Liverpool]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.uefa.com/uefachampionsleague/match/2035642--napoli-vs-liverpool/ |title=Napoli 4–1 Liverpool |website=UEFA |date=7 September 2022 }}</ref> On 1 October, he scored his first [[Serie A]] goals, by netting a brace in a 3–1 win over [[Torino F.C.|Torino]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.goal.com/en-us/match/napoli-v-torino/4s3pupioh6jqfj7c17po1g3dg |title=Napoli 3–1 Torino |website=Goal.com |date=1 October 2022 }}</ref>

==International career==
Zambo Anguissa made his international debut for the [[Cameroon national football team|Cameroon national team]] in a [[Exhibition game#Association football|friendly]] 1–0 win over [[Tunisia national football team|Tunisia]] on 24 March 2017.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://fecafoot-officiel.com/?p=7842 |title=Tunisie vs Cameroun : 23 lions au boulot à Diegem &#124; Federation Camerounaise de Football |website=fecafoot-officiel.com |access-date=15 January 2022 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170326140103/http://fecafoot-officiel.com/?p=7842 |archive-date=26 March 2017 |url-status=dead}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/39338538|title=Tunisia v Cameroon|website=BBC Sport |date=24 March 2017|accessdate=4 April 2020}}</ref>
