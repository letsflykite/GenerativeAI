'''Armel Bella-Kotchap''' (born 11 December 2001) is a German professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[centre-back]] for [[EFL Championship]] club [[Southampton F.C.|Southampton]] and the [[Germany national football team|Germany national team]].<ref name="worldfootball">{{cite web |url=https://www.worldfootball.net/player_summary/armel-bella-kotchap/ |title=Armel Bella-Kotchap |website=worldfootball.net |publisher=HEIM:SPIEL |access-date=23 May 2021}}</ref>

==Club career==

=== VfL Bochum ===
In July 2018, Bella-Kotchap joined [[VfL Bochum]] professional team [[2018–19 VfL Bochum season|2018–19]] summer training camp.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Tag 5 in Weiler: Neue Gesichter|language=German|date=10 July 2018|publisher=[[VfL Bochum]]|url=https://www.vfl-bochum.de/news/uebersicht/profis/tag-5-in-weiler-neue-gesichter/}}</ref> In March 2019, Bella-Kotchap signed his first professional contract with VfL Bochum.<ref name="contract">{{cite news |location=[[Bochum]] |url=https://www.vfl-bochum.de/news/uebersicht/profis/profivertraege-fuer-talentwerk-trio/ |title=Profiverträge für Talentwerk-Trio |trans-title=Professional contracts for Talentwerk trio |publisher=[[VfL Bochum]] |date=3 March 2019 |access-date=28 April 2019 |language=German |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190428112408/https://www.vfl-bochum.de/news/uebersicht/profis/profivertraege-fuer-talentwerk-trio/ |archive-date=28 April 2019}}</ref> He made his professional debut for Bochum in the [[2. Bundesliga]] on 28 April 2019, starting in the away match against [[FC Erzgebirge Aue|Erzgebirge Aue]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.worldfootball.net/report/2-bundesliga-2018-2019-erzgebirge-aue-vfl-bochum/ |title=Erzgebirge Aue – VfL Bochum -:- (2. Bundesliga 2018/2019, 31. Round) |website=worldfootball.net |publisher=HEIM:SPIEL |date=28 April 2019 |access-date=28 April 2019}}</ref>

=== Southampton ===
On 21 June 2022, Bella-Kotchap joined [[Southampton F.C.|Southampton]] on a four-year deal.<ref>{{Cite web |date=21 June 2022 |title=Bella-Kotchap becomes a Saint |url=https://www.southamptonfc.com/news/2022-06-21/announcement-armel-bella-kotchap-signing-southampton-football-club-21-june-2022 |access-date=21 June 2022 |website=Southampton FC}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Armel Bella-Kotchap: Southampton sign defender from Bundesliga club Bochum |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/61888603 |publisher=BBC Sport |access-date=21 June 2022 |date=21 June 2022}}</ref> Having established himself as a regular starter at centre-back,<ref name="SB22" /> Bella-Kotchap dislocated his shoulder in a collision during the 1–1 draw with [[West Ham United F.C.|West Ham United]] on 16 October. As a result, he was expected to be out of action for several weeks.<ref>{{cite web |title=Bella-Kotchap injury 'definitely a loss for us' |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/articles/cpr7ewyp4pyo |publisher=BBC Sport |access-date=18 October 2022 |date=18 October 2022}}</ref> He returned from injury on 6 November in a 1–4 defeat against [[Newcastle United F.C.|Newcastle United]].<ref>{{Cite web |last=Jurejko |first=Jonathan |date=6 November 2022 |title=Southampton 1–4 Newcastle United |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/63443730 |access-date=28 April 2023 |website=BBC Sport}}</ref> On 4 January 2023, Bella-Kotchap tweaked his knee during the first half of a 0–1 defeat against [[Nottingham Forest F.C.|Nottingham Forest]] and was expected to be injured for a matter of weeks.<ref>{{Cite web |last=House |first=Alfie |date=7 January 2023 |title=Bella-Kotchap injury severity confirmed + reason for Armstrong absence |url=https://www.dailyecho.co.uk/sport/23235504.bella-kotchap-injury-severity-confirmed-reason-armstrong-absence/ |access-date=28 April 2023 |website=Southern Daily Echo}}</ref> Bella-Kotchap dislocated his shoulder again during a 3–3 draw with [[Tottenham Hotspur F.C.|Tottenham Hotspur]].<ref>{{Cite web |last=House |first=Alfie |date=19 March 2023 |title=Bella-Kotchap waiting on specialist to learn extent of shoulder injury |url=https://www.dailyecho.co.uk/sport/23397212.bella-kotchap-waiting-specialist-learn-extent-shoulder-injury/ |access-date=28 April 2023 |website=Southern Daily Echo}}</ref>

==International career==
Bella-Kotchap began his youth international career with the [[Germany national under-18 football team|Germany under-18 team]], making his debut on 15 November 2018 in a [[Exhibition game|friendly]] match against [[Cyprus national under-18 football team|Cyprus]], which finished as a 1–0 win.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.worldfootball.net/report/u18-h-freundschaft-2018-november-zypern-deutschland/ |title=Cyprus – Germany 0:1 (U18 Friendlies 2018, November) |website=worldfootball.net |publisher=HEIM:SPIEL |date=15 November 2018 |access-date=28 April 2019}}</ref>

On 15 September 2022, Bella-Kotchap was included in the [[Germany national football team|German senior squad]] for the first time.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Southampton's Armel Bella-Kotchap earns first Germany call-up {{!}} Daily Echo |url=https://www.dailyecho.co.uk/sport/21604313.amp/ |access-date=15 September 2022 |website=www.dailyecho.co.uk}}</ref> He made his debut as a late substitute on 26 September in a 3–3 draw with [[England national football team|England]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Bella-Kotchap makes international bow |url=https://www.southamptonfc.com/news/2022-09-26/international-report-armel-bella-kotchap-england-germany-26-september-2022 |publisher=Southampton F.C. |access-date=27 September 2022 |date=26 September 2022}}</ref> In November 2022, he was selected in the German squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] in Qatar.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.dfb.de/news/detail/flick-beruft-moukoko-fuellkrug-und-goetze-in-wm-kader-245974/|title=Flick beruft Moukoko, Füllkrug und Götze in WM-Kader|publisher=[[German Football Association]]|date=10 November 2022|access-date=10 November 2022|language=de}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Bella-Kotchap was born in Paris, France,<ref name="contract"/> and is the son of former Cameroon international [[Cyrille Florent Bella]].<ref>{{cite news |location=[[Weiler-Simmerberg]] |url=https://www.vfl-bochum.de/news/uebersicht/profis/tag-5-in-weiler-neue-gesichter/ |title=Tag 5 in Weiler: Neue Gesichter |trans-title=Day 5 in Weiler: New faces |publisher=[[VfL Bochum]] |date=10 July 2018 |access-date=28 April 2019 |language=German |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190428111752/https://www.vfl-bochum.de/news/uebersicht/profis/tag-5-in-weiler-neue-gesichter/ |archive-date=28 April 2019}}</ref>
