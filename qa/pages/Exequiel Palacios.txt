'''Exequiel Alejandro Palacios''' ({{IPA-es|ekseˈkjel paˈla.sjos}}; born 5 October 1998) is an Argentine professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[midfielder]] for [[Bundesliga]] club [[Bayer 04 Leverkusen|Bayer Leverkusen]] and the [[Argentina national football team|Argentina national team]]. He was a member of the Argentina team that won the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].

==Club career==
Palacios is a youth exponent from [[Club Atlético River Plate]]. He made his [[Argentine Primera División|league debut]] on 8 November 2015 against [[Newell's Old Boys]].<ref name="soccerway2">{{cite web|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2015/11/08/argentina/primera-division/club-atletico-river-plate/newell-s-old-boys/1978582/|title=River Plate vs. Newell's Old Boys – 8 November 2015 – Soccerway|publisher=soccerway.com|access-date=2 December 2015}}</ref>

Palacios made his final appearance for his boyhood club in the final of the [[2018–19 Copa Argentina|Copa Argentina]] on 13 December 2019, playing 79 minutes of a 3–0 victory over [[Central Córdoba de Santiago del Estero|Central Córdoba]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.copaargentina.org/es/partido/3274_Central-Cordoba-SdE-vs-River.html|title=Central Córdoba (SdE) 0-3 River|website=Copa Argentina|date=13 December 2019|access-date=3 January 2020|language=es}}</ref>

=== Bayer Leverkusen ===
On 16 December 2019, [[Bayer 04 Leverkusen|Bayer Leverkusen]] announced that Palacios would join the club on 1 January 2020, signing a five-and-a-half year deal.<ref>{{cite web |title=Bayer 04 sign Exequiel Palacios |url=https://www.bayer04.de/en-us/news/bayer04/bayer-04-sign-exequiel-palacios |website=Bayer Leverkusen |access-date=16 December 2019 |date=16 December 2019}}</ref>

==International career==
Palacios made his international debut for [[Argentina national football team|Argentina]] on 8 September 2018 in a 3–0 international friendly against the [[Guatemala national football team]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://us.soccerway.com/matches/2018/09/08/world/friendlies/argentina/guatemala/2805437/|title = Argentina vs. Guatemala - 8 September 2018 - Soccerway}}</ref> 

On 12 November 2020, Palacios fractured a bone in his spine during a 1–1 draw with [[Paraguay national football team|Paraguay]] in a [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification (CONMEBOL)|World Cup qualifier]] and was expected be out for three months. Palacios sustained the injury in an aerial duel with [[Angel Romero (footballer)|Angel Romero]] in the first half of the contest at [[La Bombonera]] and was replaced by [[Giovani Lo Celso]], who took the corner from which [[Nicolás González (footballer, born 1998)|Nicolas Gonzalez]] headed home Argentina's equaliser.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.espn.com/soccer/fifa-world-cup-qualifying-conmebol/story/4234050/argentinas-palacios-suffers-fractured-spine-following-aerial-challenge|title=Argentina's Palacios suffers fractured spine following aerial challenge|website=ESPN|date=13 November 2020|access-date=13 November 2020}}</ref>

On 10 July 2021, Palacios appeared in Argentina's 1-0 victory over Brazil in winning the CONMEBOL Copa America.

On 18 December 2022, Palacios was an unused substitute in Argentina's victory over France in the final of the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]]. Palacios came on in the 74th minute in Argentina's 3-0 victory over Croatia in their semi-final match and in the 80th minute in their 2-1 victory over Australia in the Round of 16.  Palacios appeared in one of Argentina's three group stage matches, substituting on in the 69th minute in Argentina's second match against Mexico.

==Personal life==
Palacios initially was noticed by the media in August 2018 with his well publicized relationship with Instagram model Sol Pérez. It quickly became public though that around the same time Palacios and Pérez had started their relationship, Palacios had also begun dating amateur actress, singer, and dancer Juliana Orellano. Both Pérez and Orellano quickly ended their relationship with Palacios after finding out that he was dating the other. There was more drama however as on 21 August it became known that Palacios had been in relationship with a neighborhood friend Karen Gramajo, a manicurist, for four years.<ref name="ole">{{cite web|title=¿Otra amante de Exequiel Palacios?|url=https://www.ole.com.ar/fuera-de-juego/exequiel-palacios-sol-perez-juliana-orellano-novia-amante-botinera-jugador-river_0_2073992704.html|language=es|work=[[Olé (sports newspaper)|Olé.com]]|date=21 August 2018|access-date=23 August 2018}}</ref> All three women angrily took to social media to protest Palacios' disloyalty.<ref>{{cite web|title=Sol Pérez se olvidó de Exequiel Palacios y subió una foto infernal para que la extrañe|url=https://bolavip.com/america/Sol-Perez-se-olvido-de-Exequiel-Palacios-y-subio-una-foto-infernal-para-que-la-extrane-20180822-0046.html|language=es|date=23 August 2018|access-date=23 August 2018|work=BolaVip}}</ref>
