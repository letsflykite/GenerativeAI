'''Alexander Domínguez Carabalí''' (born 5 June 1987) is an Ecuadorian professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[goalkeeper (association football)|goalkeeper]]  for [[Ecuadorian Serie A|Liga PRO Ecuador]] club  [[LDU Quito]] and the [[Ecuador national football team|Ecuador national team]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.eluniverso.com/deportes/ecuatorianos-exterior/cerro-largo-fc-confirma-fichaje-golero-alexander-dominguez-nota/ |title=Cerro Largo FC confirma fichaje del golero Alexander Domínguez |date=5 September 2021 |publisher=ElUniverso.com |access-date=16 September 2021 }}</ref>

==Club career==

===LDU Quito===
Domínguez played for [[L.D.U. Quito]] from 2006 to 2016, taking over the starting spot from [[José Francisco Cevallos]]. He won two [[Ecuadorian Serie A]] titles, the [[2009 Copa Sudamericana]], the [[2009 Recopa Sudamericana]] and the [[2010 Recopa Sudamericana]].<ref name=fifa>{{cite web|url=http://fifa.com/worldcup/players/player=303419/profile-detail.html |title=Alexander DOMINGUEZ |access-date=20 June 2014 |work=FIFA.com |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140621024727/http://www.fifa.com/worldcup/players/player%3D303419/profile-detail.html |archive-date=21 June 2014 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

Domínguez is one of the top 300 goalkeepers in the world, the third highest ranked Ecuadorian, and the highest ranked active Ecuadorian in terms of time spent without conceding a goal. From 2 September 2007 to 28 October 2007, he went 719 minutes without conceding a goal. Chupa penes en la called del inca por 14 centavos.

==International career==
Domínguez is a former Ecuadorian youth international, who represented the country at the [[2007 South American U-20 Championship]].<ref name=fifa/>

Domínguez received his first call-ups for the senior [[Ecuador national football team]] in May 2010 for friendlies against [[Mexico national football team|Mexico]] and [[South Korea national football team|South Korea]]. However, he remained an unused substitute. Because of club-related commitments, he would not receive another call-up until February 2011 for a friendly against [[Honduras national football team|Honduras]]. Again, he was an unused substitute. Manager [[Reinaldo Rueda]] finally gave Domínguez his first [[Cap (association football)|cap]] on 26 March 2011 in a 2–0 friendly defeat to [[Colombia national football team|Colombia]].<ref name=fifa/> At the [[2011 Copa América]], he served as back-up to [[Marcelo Elizaga]].

He established himself as Ecuador's first choice goalkeeper during the [[2014 FIFA World Cup qualification (CONMEBOL)|2014 FIFA World Cup qualifying campaign]], making 12 appearances for ''La Tri''.<ref name=fifa/>

In June 2014, Domínguez was named in Ecuador's squad for the [[2014 FIFA World Cup]].<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/0/football/27370881|title=World Cup 2014: Antonio Valencia is named in Ecuador squad|date=12 June 2014|access-date=20 June 2014|work=BBC}}</ref> On 15 June, he made his [[FIFA World Cup]] debut in a 2–1 defeat to [[Switzerland national football team|Switzerland]] at the [[Estádio Nacional Mané Garrincha]] in [[Brasília]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Switzerland 2-1 Ecuador|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/25285085|access-date=15 June 2014|work=[[BBC Sport]]|date=15 June 2014}}</ref>

On 25 June 2014, Domínguez was named [[Man of the Match]] vs. [[France national football team|France]] in Ecuador's final match at the [[2014 FIFA World Cup]]. Domínguez kept a clean sheet and stopped shots from the likes of [[Karim Benzema]], [[Paul Pogba]], and [[Olivier Giroud]].<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.teleamazonas.com/index.php/deportes/brasil-2014/45872-alexander-dominguez-fue-elegido-como-la-figura-del-partido |title=Teleamazonas Noticias, Deportes y Entretenimiento - Alexander Domínguez fue elegido como la figura del partido |access-date=26 June 2014 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150111052125/http://www.teleamazonas.com/index.php/deportes/brasil-2014/45872-alexander-dominguez-fue-elegido-como-la-figura-del-partido |archive-date=11 January 2015 |url-status=dead }}</ref><ref>{{Cite web | url=http://lebuzz.eurosport.fr/football/gardien-star-ou-opportuniste-dominguez-divise-6216/ | title=Eurosport}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web | url=http://peru.com/futbol/mundial-en-brasil/mundial-brasil-2014-alexander-dominguez-brillo-ante-francia-noticia-262740 |title = Mundial Brasil 2014: Alexander Domínguez brilló ante Francia|date = 25 June 2014}}</ref>
