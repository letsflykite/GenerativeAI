'''Gregor Kobel''' (born 6 December 1997) is a Swiss professional [[footballer]] who plays as a [[Goalkeeper (association football)|goalkeeper]] for [[Bundesliga]] club [[Borussia Dortmund]] and the [[Switzerland national football team|Switzerland national team]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.worldfootball.net/player_summary/gregor-kobel/ |title=Gregor Kobel |website=worldfootball.net |publisher=HEIM:SPIEL |access-date=12 August 2017}}</ref>

==Club career==

=== Hoffenheim ===
Kobel made his professional debut for [[TSG 1899 Hoffenheim|1899 Hoffenheim]] on 12 August 2018, starting in the first round of the [[2017–18 DFB-Pokal]] against [[3. Liga]] side [[FC Rot-Weiß Erfurt|Rot-Weiß Erfurt]]. The match finished as a 1–0 away win for Hoffenheim.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.worldfootball.net/report/dfb-pokal-2017-2018-1-runde-rot-weiss-erfurt-1899-hoffenheim/ |title=Rot-Weiß Erfurt – 1899 Hoffenheim 0:1 (DFB-Pokal 2017/2018, 1. Round) |website=worldfootball.net |publisher=HEIM:SPIEL |date=12 August 2017 |access-date=18 August 2018}}</ref>

=== VfB Stuttgart ===
For the [[2019–20 2. Bundesliga|2019–20 season]] Kobel was loaned out to [[VfB Stuttgart]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.vfb.de/en/vfb/latest/news/professionals/2019/gregor-kobel-wechselt-zum-vfb-/|title=Gregor Kobel joins VfB|work=vfb.de|publisher=[[VfB Stuttgart]]|date=24 June 2019|accessdate=1 July 2019}}</ref> On 28 July 2020, Kobel moved permanently to VfB Stuttgart and signed a contract until June 2024.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.vfb.de/en/vfb/latest/news/professionals/2020/gregor-kobel-verpflichtung/|title=Gregor Kobel signs for VfB|work=vfb.de|publisher=[[VfB Stuttgart]]|date=28 July 2020|accessdate=28 July 2020}}</ref>

=== Borussia Dortmund ===
On 31 May 2021, Kobel signed a five-year contract with [[Borussia Dortmund]] ahead of the [[2021–22 Borussia Dortmund season|2021–22 season]]. The transfer fee paid to VfB Stuttgart was 15 million [[euro]]s.<ref>{{cite news|title=Borussia Dortmund sign Gregor Kobel from VfB Stuttgart|url=https://www.bundesliga.com/en/bundesliga/news/borussia-dortmund-sign-gregor-kobel-from-vfb-stuttgart-burki-hitz-16105|url-status=live|access-date=1 June 2021|website=[[Bundesliga]]}}</ref>

== International career ==
Kobel was a youth international for Switzerland.
He was called up to the [[Switzerland national football team|senior team]] for the first time on 2020.<ref>{{Cite web |date=6 November 2020 |title=Aufgebot der Nati - Petkovic setzt auf bewährte Kräfte |url=https://www.srf.ch/sport/fussball/nationalmannschaft/aufgebot-der-nati-petkovic-setzt-auf-bewaehrte-kraefte |access-date=6 July 2022 |website=Schweizer Radio und Fernsehen (SRF) |language=de}}</ref>
In 2021 the national team for the [[UEFA European Championship|2020 UEFA European Championship]], where the team created one of the main sensations of the tournament reaching the quarter-finals.<ref>https://www.uefa.com / uefaeuro-2020 / match / 2024485 - switzerland-vs-spain / lineups /? iv = true</ref>

He made his debut on 1 September 2021 in a friendly against [[Greece national football team|Greece]], a 2–1 victory at home.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[ESPN]]|url=https://www.espn.com/soccer/lineups?gameId=594176|title=Switzerland v Greece game report|date=1 September 2021}}</ref>

In 2021, he was called up to the national team for the [[UEFA European Championship|2020 UEFA European Championship]], where the team upset favorites [[France national football team|France]] en route to the quarter-finals, where they lost to [[Spain national football team|Spain]].<ref>{{Cite web |date=2021-06-28 |title=France 3-3 Switzerland (aet; pens 4-5): Euro 2020 last 16 – as it happened |url=http://www.theguardian.com/football/live/2021/jun/28/france-v-switzerland-euro-2020-last-16-score-updates-live |access-date=2022-12-09 |website=the Guardian |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |title=Switzerland vs. Spain - Football Match Summary - July 2, 2021 - ESPN |url=https://www.espn.com/soccer/match/_/gameId/560300 |access-date=2022-12-09 |website=ESPN.com |language=en}}</ref>
