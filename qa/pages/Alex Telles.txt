'''Alex Nicolao Telles''' ({{IPA-pt|aˈlɛks ˈtɛlis|br}}; born 15 December 1992) is a Brazilian professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[left-back]] for [[Premier League]] club [[Manchester United F.C.|Manchester United]] and the [[Brazil national football team|Brazil national team]].

Telles started his career at Brazilian [[Campeonato Brasileiro Série D|Série D]] side [[Esporte Clube Juventude|Juventude]], before being sold to [[Campeonato Brasileiro Série A|Série A]] side [[Grêmio Foot-Ball Porto Alegrense|Grêmio]] in 2013. That year, he was voted the best left-back in the league.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.cbf.com.br/futebol-brasileiro/noticias/campeonato-brasileiro-serie-a/os-melhores-do-campeonato-sao-homenageados-na-festa-da-premiacao-brasileirao-2013 |title=Os melhores do campeonato são homenageados na festa da Premiação Brasileirão 2013|trans-title=The best of the championship are honored in the awards party Brasileirão 2013 |publisher=Brazilian Football Confederation |access-date=13 November 2018 |language=pt |date=9 December 2013}}</ref> In January 2014, Telles signed with Turkish [[Süper Lig]] club [[Galatasaray S.K. (football)|Galatasaray]], winning various trophies including a league title, two [[Turkish Cup]]s and the [[Turkish Super Cup]], only to be loaned to [[Serie A]] team [[Inter Milan]] during the 2015–16 season. 

In July 2016, Telles was sold to [[Primeira Liga]] side [[FC Porto]], where he established himself as one of the best left-backs in the league. He made almost 200 appearances for Porto and won four trophies, including two league titles, one [[Taça de Portugal]] and one [[Supertaça Cândido de Oliveira]], as well as being named in the Primeira Liga Team of the Year for three consecutive seasons. Telles' performances sparked the interest of several European clubs, and in October 2020, he signed for Manchester United for an initial €18&nbsp;million (£15.4&nbsp;million).

Born in Brazil, Telles also holds [[Italian nationality law|Italian citizenship]], but ended up representing [[Brazil national football team|Brazil]] at international level. He made his senior international debut in 2019.

==Club career==
===Juventude and Grêmio===
Telles was born in [[Caxias do Sul]], a city in the Brazilian state of [[Rio Grande do Sul]]. At the age of eight, he began playing football with the neighbouring kids and was later enrolled to a youth academy of [[Esporte Clube Juventude]].

Telles began his professional career with Juventude, making his debut on 24 January 2011 against [[Esporte Clube São José|São José-PA]].<ref>[https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2011/01/24/brazil/gaucho-1/esporte-clube-sao-jose-porto-alegre/esporte-clube-juventude/1047629/ EC São José vs. Juventude 1–2]; Global Sports Media, 24 January 2011</ref> He scored his first goal on 20 August in a 1–1 home draw against [[Esporte Clube Cruzeiro|Cruzeiro]].<ref>[http://www.futebolinterior.com.br/clube/juventude-rs/191638+Juventude-RS_1_x_1_Cruzeiro-RS_-_Decepcao_alviverde_em_Caxias_do_Sul Juventude-RS 1 x 1 Cruzeiro-RS – Decepção alviverde em Caxias do Sul! (Juventude-RS 1–1 Cruzeiro-RS — ''Alviverde'' disappointment in Caxias do Sul!)]; Futebol Interior, 20 August 2011 {{in lang|pt}}</ref>

In December, Telles was transferred to [[Grêmio Foot-Ball Porto Alegrense|Grêmio]] after a partnership was established with Juventude.<ref>[http://esporte.uol.com.br/futebol/ultimas-noticias/2012/12/12/gremio-contrata-lateral-esquerdo-alex-telles-e-encaminha-parceria-com-o-juventude.htm Grêmio contrata lateral esquerdo Alex Telles e encaminha parceria com o Juventude (Grêmio signs left back Alex Telles and pushes partnership with Juventude forward)]; UOL Esporte, 12 December 2012 {{in lang|pt}}</ref> He made his Grêmio debut on 3 February 2013 against [[Sport Club Internacional|Internacional]].<ref>[http://globoesporte.globo.com/jogo/gaucho-2013/03-02-2013/internacional-gremio.html Deu a lógica: em Gre-Nal quente, Inter vence os reservas do Grêmio]; Globo Esporte, 3 February 2013 {{in lang|pt}}</ref> On 26 May, Telles made his [[Campeonato Brasileiro Série A|Série A]] debut, starting in a 2–0 home win against [[Clube Náutico Capibaribe|Náutico]].<ref>[http://globoesporte.globo.com/jogo/brasileirao2013/26-05-2013/gremio-nautico.html Grêmio cumpre promessa, joga bem e vence Náutico pelo Brasileirão]; Globo Esporte, 26 May 2013 {{in lang|pt}}</ref>

===Galatasaray===
On 22 January 2014, after long negotiations, Telles completed a move to Turkish club [[Galatasaray S.K. (football)|Galatasaray]] for €6&nbsp;million, signing a contract that will keep him with the club until 2018.<ref>[http://www.galatasaray.org/futbol/futbol_as/haber/19071.php Alex Telles Galatasaray'da &#91;Alex Telles to Galatasaray&#93;]; Galatasaray S.K., 22 January 2014 {{in lang|tr}}</ref> He made his debut two weeks later, starting in a 3–0 win against [[Tokatspor]] for [[2013–14 Turkish Cup|the campaign]]'s [[Turkish Cup]], and made his maiden [[Süper Lig]] debut in a 3–0 home victory against [[Eskişehirspor]] on 8 February 2014.<ref>[http://www2.galatasaray.org/futbol/turkiyekupasi/haber/19248.php Tokatspor 0–3 Galatasaray] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140221201504/http://www2.galatasaray.org/futbol/turkiyekupasi/haber/19248.php |date=21 February 2014 }}; Galatasaray S.K., 5 February 2014 {{in lang|tr}}</ref> Telles made his first assist for the club against [[Antalyaspor]] in a 2–2 away game on 17 February.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www2.galatasaray.org/futbol/superlig/haber/19348.php |title=Galatasaray vs Antalyaspor |publisher=Galatasaray S.K. |date=17 February 2014 |access-date=18 February 2014 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140227072312/http://www2.galatasaray.org/futbol/superlig/haber/19348.php |archive-date=27 February 2014}}</ref> He also scored his first goal for Galatasaray against [[Akhisar Belediyespor]] on 8 March in a 6–1 home win.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.hurriyet.com.tr/spor/futbol/25968388.asp |title=Telles ilk golünü attı [Telles's first goal] |newspaper=Hürriyet |date=8 March 2014|access-date=9 March 2014}}</ref>

In August 2015, there were rumours{{where|date=September 2015}} mentioning Telles as a good choice to fill the place made by the departure of left-back [[Filipe Luís]] at [[Chelsea F.C.|Chelsea]]. In response to the rumour, Galatasaray manager [[Hamza Hamzaoğlu]] said the club would only be tempted by an acceptable offer.{{citation needed|date=September 2015}}

====Loan to Inter Milan====
On 31 August 2015, Galatasaray agreed to let Telles join Italian club [[Inter Milan|Internazionale]] of [[Serie A]] on a one-year loan for a €1.3&nbsp;million fee, in addition to €250,000 if Inter qualify for the group stage of the [[2016–17 UEFA Champions League]].<ref name=KAP>{{cite web |url=http://www.kap.gov.tr/en/search/notice-results.aspx?id=464832 |title=Alex Nicolao Telles – Geçici Transfer |date=1 September 2015|access-date=5 November 2015 |publisher=Turkish Public Disclosure System (KAP) |language=tr}}</ref> The deal included a buyout option for €8.5&nbsp;million,<ref name=KAP/> letting Internazionale make the move permanent when the loan deal is finished.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.inter.it/en/news/48882 |title=ALEX TELLES IS A NERAZZURRI PLAYER |publisher=Inter Milan |date=31 August 2015|access-date=22 April 2018}}</ref> Telles reunited with Internazionale manager [[Roberto Mancini]] in Italy, who previously coached him at Galatasaray.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.espn.com/soccer/soccer-transfers/story/2591278/felipe-melo-adem-ljajic-alex-telles-sign-with-inter-milan |title=Felipe Melo, Adem Ljajic and Alex Telles complete Inter Milan moves |publisher=ESPN |date=1 September 2015|access-date=22 April 2018}}</ref>
[[File:Alex Telles 2018.jpg|thumb|315x315px|Telles playing for [[FC Porto|Porto]] in 2018]]
He made his debut for the club on 13 September in the [[2015–16 Serie A]] matchday 3 against city rivals [[A.C. Milan|Milan]], playing the entirety of the match as Inter won 1–0.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.sportmediaset.mediaset.it/calcio/inter/serie-a-inter-milan-1-0-guarin-decide-il-derby_1077027-201502a.shtml |title=Serie A: Inter-Milan 1–0, Guarin decide il derby|trans-title=Serie A: Inter-Milan 1–0, Guarin decides the derby |publisher=Sport Mediaset |language=it |date=13 September 2015|access-date=22 April 2018}}</ref>

===Porto===
On 12 July 2016, Portuguese side [[FC Porto]] announced the signing of Telles for a €6.5&nbsp;million fee. According to a Galatasaray press release, the [[Istanbul]] club reserved a further 10% of any added value in a future transfer deal.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://maisfutebol.iol.pt/liga/transferencias/oficial-alex-telles-no-fc-porto-por-6-5-milhoes-de-euros |title=OFICIAL: Alex Telles no FC Porto por 6,5 milhões de euros|trans-title=OFFICIAL: Alex Telles at FC Porto for 6.5 million euros |website=Maisfutebol |language=pt |date=12 July 2016|access-date=16 January 2019}}</ref> The following day, after signing a five-year contract, he stated: ''"I ambition titles, I ambition a very beautiful story. I have recently commented with my relatives that I do not want to come here in passing, I want to leave a legacy."''<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.record.pt/futebol/futebol-nacional/liga-nos/fc-porto/detalhe/alex-telles-nao-quero-vir-de-passagem-quero-deixar-um-legado |title=Alex Telles: "Não quero vir de passagem, quero deixar um legado"|trans-title=Alex Telles: «I do not want to come in passing, I want to leave a legacy» |newspaper=Record |language=pt |date=13 July 2016|access-date=16 January 2019}}</ref> On 12 August, in a 3–1 away win against [[Rio Ave F.C.|Rio Ave]], he marked his league debut and first competitive match with a poor performance and subsequent expulsion.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.record.pt/futebol/futebol-nacional/liga-nos/fc-porto/detalhe/alex-telles-com-estreia-azarada |title=Alex Telles com estreia azarada|trans-title=Alex Telles with unlucky debut |newspaper=Record |language=pt |date=12 August 2016|access-date=16 January 2019}}</ref> Despite the rough start, Telles gradually cemented himself in the team's starting eleven, scoring his first goal when facing rivals [[Boavista F.C.|Boavista]] in a 3–1 home victory at [[Estádio do Dragão]].<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.jn.pt/desporto/interior/f-c-porto-vence-boavista-com-reviravolta-5406078.html |title=F.C. Porto vence Boavista com reviravolta|trans-title=F.C. Porto wins Boavista with turnaround |newspaper=Jornal de Notícias |language=pt |date=23 September 2016|access-date=16 January 2019}}</ref>

====Loan to Sevilla====

==International career==
Although he was born in Brazil, Telles also has an Italian passport,<ref name="Passport">{{cite web |url=https://www.football-italia.net/92705/italy-ask-after-alex-telles |title=Italy ask after Alex Telles |website=Football Italia |date=9 October 2016|access-date=22 April 2018}}</ref> making him in the past eligible to represent [[Italy national football team|Italy national team]].<ref name="Passport"/> In October 2016, Telles said that he would "welcome" a call-up from the ''"Azzurri"'', adding that his great-grandparents are Italian and he feels Italian.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.espn.com/soccer/italy/story/2971439/porto-defender-alex-telles-open-to-representing-italy-instead-of-brazil |title=Porto defender Alex Telles open to representing Italy instead of Brazil |publisher=ESPN |first=Ben |last=Gladwell |date=13 October 2016|access-date=22 April 2018}}</ref> In March 2019, however, he received his first call-up to the [[Brazil national football team|Brazil national team]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Alex Telles substitui Filipe Luís nos amistosos de março |url=https://www.cbf.com.br/selecao-brasileira/noticias/selecao-masculina/alex-telles-substitui-filipe-luis-nos-amistosos-de-marco |publisher=Brazilian Football Confederation |access-date=16 March 2019 |date=15 March 2019}}</ref> He made his debut for Brazil on 23 March 2019 in a friendly against [[Panama national football team|Panama]], as a starter.<ref>{{cite web |publisher=ESPN |url=http://www.espn.com/soccer/match?gameId=537099 |title=Brazil v Panama game report |date=23 March 2019}}</ref>

On 7 November 2022, Telles was named in the squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Seleção Brasileira está convocada para a Copa do Mundo FIFA Qatar 2022|url=https://www.cbf.com.br/selecao-brasileira/noticias/selecao-masculina/selecao-brasileira-esta-convocada-para-a-copa-do-mundo-fifa-qatar-2022|publisher=[[Brazilian Football Confederation|CBF]]|access-date=7 November 2022|date=7 November 2022|language=pt}}</ref> He was ruled out for the rest of the competition after picking up a right knee injury in the [[2022 FIFA World Cup Group G#Cameroon vs Brazil|final group stage match]] against [[Cameroon national football team|Cameroon]].<ref>{{Cite web |last=Mintah |first=Yaw Loic |date=2022-12-03 |title=Qatar 2022: Gabriel Jesus and Alex Telles ruled out of tournament with injuries |url=https://citisportsonline.com/2022/12/03/qatar-2022-gabriel-jesus-and-alex-telles-ruled-out-of-tournament-with-injuries/ |access-date=2022-12-04 |website=Citi Sports Online |language=en-US}}</ref>

==Style of play==
Telles is an offensive [[full-back (association football)|full back]] and is described as a skilled, quick and [[Playmaker|playmaking]] defender.<ref>{{cite web |title=Roberto Carlos'tan Alex Telles yorumu |trans-title=Roberto Carlos speaks of Alex Telles |url=http://www.trtspor.com.tr/haber/futbol/spor-toto-super-lig/roberto-carlostan-alex-telles-yorumu-62582.html |language=tr |publisher=TRTspor |date=16 January 2014 |access-date=19 January 2014 }}</ref>
