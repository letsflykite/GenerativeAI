'''Jassem Gaber Abdulsallam''' ({{lang-ar|جاسم جابر عبد السلام}}; born 20 February 2002) is a [[Qatari]] professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Midfielder (association football)|midfielder]] for Qatari club [[Al-Arabi SC (Qatar)|Al-Arabi]] and the [[Qatar national football team]].<ref name="SW"/> He was selected for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].<ref>[https://www.fifa.com/fifaplus/en/articles/qatar-squad-announcement-fifa-world-cup-2022 Hosts Qatar announce 26-man World Cup squad] FIFA. Retrieved 13 November 2022.</ref>

==Career==
In January 2020, Gaber signed a four-year contract to remain with [[Al-Arabi SC (Qatar)|Al-Arabi]] until the 2024 season.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.kooora.com/?n=898806 |title=العربي يؤمن بقاء جاسم جابر حتى 2024 |trans-title=Al-Arabi believes that Jassem Gaber will remain until 2024 |website=Kooora.com |date=25 February 2020 |access-date=17 November 2022}}</ref>

In January 2022, Gaber was included in the [[International Federation of Football History & Statistics|IFFHS]] AFC Under-20 Team of the Year for 2021.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.iffhs.com/posts/1558 |title=IFFHS Men's Youth Continental Teams of the Year 2021 – AFC |publisher=[[International Federation of Football History & Statistics]] |date=3 January 2022 |access-date=17 November 2022}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last=Titaoui |first=Amer |url=https://www.royasport.com/?p=4008 |title=لاعب العربي جاسم جابر ضمن أفضل تشكيلة آسيوية شابة |trans-title=Al-Arabi player Jassem Jaber is among the best young Asian squad |work=Roya Sports |date=4 January 2022 |access-date=17 November 2022 |language=ar}}</ref>
