'''Dylan James Christopher Levitt''' (born 17 November 2000) is a Welsh professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[midfielder]] for [[Scottish Premiership]] club [[Hibernian F.C.|Hibernian]] and the [[Wales national football team|Wales national team]].

Levitt is a graduate of the [[Manchester United F.C. Reserves and Academy|Manchester United youth system]]. He made his senior debut in a [[UEFA Europa League]] match in November 2019, which was his only first-team appearance for [[Manchester United F.C.|Manchester United]]. He spent time on loan at [[Charlton Athletic F.C.|Charlton Athletic]], Croatian club [[NK Istra 1961|Istra 1961]] and Scottish club [[Dundee United F.C.|Dundee United]]. He left Manchester United in July 2022 and joined Dundee United on a permanent deal. Levitt moved to Hibernian a year later.

Levitt represented Wales at several youth levels, before making his senior international debut in a [[UEFA Nations League]] game against [[Finland national football team|Finland]] in September 2020. He was selected for the Wales squads that played in [[UEFA Euro 2020]] and the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].

==Club career==
===Manchester United===
Levitt joined the [[Manchester United F.C. Reserves and Academy|Manchester United youth system]] at the age of eight and signed his first professional contract with the club in April 2018.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Sands |first1=Katie |title=Dylan Levitt, the outstanding Man Utd teenager just handed his Wales debut by Ryan Giggs |url=https://www.walesonline.co.uk/sport/football/football-news/dylan-levitt-man-utd-wales-16346194 |work=WalesOnline |publisher=Media Wales |date=3 September 2020 |access-date=1 June 2021}}</ref> In November 2019, he signed a new contract with the club until June 2022.<ref>{{cite news |title=Dylan Levitt signs new contract with Manchester United |url=https://www.manchestereveningnews.co.uk/sport/football/dylan-levitt-man-utd-contract-17227972 |work=Manchester Evening News |date=8 November 2019 |access-date=1 June 2021}}</ref> He made his senior debut with United in a [[2019–20 UEFA Europa League|UEFA Europa League]] match against [[FC Astana|Astana]] on 28 November 2019.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.manutd.com/en/news/detail/match-report-astana-v-man-united-europa-league-28-november-2019 |first=Gemma|last=Thompson|title=FC Astana 2 Manchester United 1|publisher=Manchester United |date=28 November 2019 |access-date=28 November 2019}}</ref>

On 8 September 2020, Levitt was sent on a season-long loan to [[Charlton Athletic F.C.|Charlton Athletic]].<ref>{{cite news |title=Dylan Levitt: Charlton Athletic sign Manchester United midfielder on loan |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/54080226 |work= BBC Sport |date=8 September 2020 |access-date=1 June 2021}}</ref> On 8 January 2021, it was reported that Levitt's loan at Charlton Athletic had been cut short after he made only five first-team appearances at the [[EFL League One|League One]] club.<ref>{{cite news |title=Dylan Levitt: Wales midfielder recalled from Charlton by Manchester United |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/55594996 |work=BBC Sport |date=8 January 2021 |access-date=1 June 2021}}</ref>

On 15 February 2021, Levitt joined [[Croatian First Football League|Prva HNL]] side [[NK Istra 1961|Istra 1961]] on loan for the remainder of the [[2020–21 NK Istra 1961 season|2020–21 season]].<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/56075031 |title=Dylan Levitt: Manchester United loan Wales midfielder to Croatian club Istra |work=BBC Sport |date=15 February 2021 |accessdate=1 June 2021}}</ref> On 14 April, he was unexpectedly given a chance by coach [[Danijel Jumić]] in the [[2020–21 Croatian Football Cup|Croatian Cup]] semi-final against [[HNK Rijeka|Rijeka]], being named in the starting lineup. He played until 64th minute when he was replaced by [[Dino Halilović]], as Istra managed to pull off an upset 3[[2020–21 NK Istra 1961 season|–]]2 win and qualify for the [[2021 Croatian Football Cup Final|final]].<ref>{{Cite web|last=Matteoni|first=Robert|date=14 April 2021|title=Video: Istra srušila Riječane i ušla u povijesno finale Kupa, branitelj naslova eliminiran nakon drame|url=https://sportske.jutarnji.hr/sn/nogomet/hnl/video-istra-srusila-rijecane-i-usla-u-povijesno-finale-kupa-branitelj-naslova-eliminiran-nakon-drame-15065398|url-status=live|access-date=14 April 2021|website=Sportske novosti|language=hr-hr|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210414184315/https://sportske.jutarnji.hr/sn/nogomet/hnl/video-istra-srusila-rijecane-i-usla-u-povijesno-finale-kupa-branitelj-naslova-eliminiran-nakon-drame-15065398 |archive-date=14 April 2021 }}</ref>

===Dundee United===
On 20 August 2021, Levitt joined [[Scottish Premiership]] club [[Dundee United F.C.|Dundee United]] on a season-long loan.<ref>{{cite news |first=Steven |last=Railston |title=Manchester United's Dylan Levitt completes loan transfer to Dundee United |url=https://www.manchestereveningnews.co.uk/sport/football/transfer-news/dylan-levitt-man-united-loan-21362921 |work=Manchester Evening News |date=20 August 2021 |access-date=20 August 2021 }}</ref> After scoring six goals in 29 league and cup appearances for the Tangerines, including an extra-time winner against [[Kilmarnock F.C.|Kilmarnock]] in the fourth round of the [[2021–22 Scottish Cup]],<ref>{{cite news |first=Andrew |last=Southwick |title=Kilmarnock 1-2 Dundee United (AET): Dylan Levitt extra-time winner for visitors |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/60005929 |website=BBC Sport |date=22 January 2022 |accessdate=17 May 2022 }}</ref> and three in the final five league matches to help Dundee United qualify for European football, Levitt was voted the fans' player of the year.<ref>{{cite news |title=Dundee United Player of the Year Awards 2021/22 |url=https://www.dundeeunitedfc.co.uk/news/7319/DUNDEE-UNITED-PLAYER-OF-THE-YEAR-AWARDS-202122.html |website=dundeeunitedfc.co.uk |publisher=Dundee United FC |date=16 May 2022 |accessdate=17 May 2022 }}</ref> 

Levitt then joined Dundee United on a two-year deal in July 2022,<ref>{{Cite web |date=7 July 2022 |title=Dylan Levitt Joins Dundee United |url=https://www.dundeeunitedfc.co.uk/news/7365/DYLAN-LEVITT-JOINS-DUNDEE-UNITED.html |access-date=8 July 2022 |website=DundeeUnitedFC.co.uk |publisher=Dundee United}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Levitt completes permanent move|url=https://www.manutd.com/en/news/detail/man-utd-midfielder-dylan-levitt-rejoins-dundee-united-on-permanent-deal|website=ManUtd.com |publisher=Manchester United |date=8 July 2022 |access-date=8 July 2022 }}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Dundee Utd: Steven Fletcher & Dylan Levitt pen two-year deals at Premiership club |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/62080878 |website=BBC Sport |date=7 July 2022 |access-date=7 July 2022}}</ref> and later extended this contract by one year. United were relegated from the Premiership after the 2022&ndash;23 season, and in July 2023 the club accepted an offer of around £300,000 for him from [[Hibernian F.C.|Hibernian]].<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.edinburghnews.scotsman.com/sport/football/hibs/hibs-complete-signing-of-dylan-levitt-fee-paid-contract-length-lee-johnson-comments-4208541 |title=Hibs complete signing of Dylan Levitt - fee paid, contract length, Lee Johnson comments |first=Patrick |last=McPartlin |newspaper=Edinburgh Evening News |date=5 July 2023 |accessdate=5 July 2023}}</ref><ref name = hibs/>

===Hibernian===
Levitt signed a three-year contract with [[Hibernian F.C.|Hibernian]] on 5 July 2023.<ref name = hibs>{{cite web |url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/66111836 |title=Dylan Levitt: Hibernian sign Wales international from Dundee United |website=BBC Sport |date=5 July 2023 |accessdate=5 July 2023}}</ref>
