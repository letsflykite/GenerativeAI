'''Victor Enok Nelsson''' (born 14 October 1998) is a Danish professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[centre back]] for [[Süper Lig]] club [[Galatasaray S.K. (football)|Galatasaray]] and the [[Denmark national football team|Denmark national team]].

==Youth career==
Nelsson started playing football when he was 4 years old in Hornbæk IF, where his father also was coaching. Later, he got contacted by scouts from [[FC Nordsjælland]], and joined the club on 25 March 2010.<ref>{{ cite web | title = Fokus På: Victor Nelsson – Ét opkald: Fra Hornbæk til Farum | url = https://www.dbold.dk/2016/03/fokus-paa-victor-nelsson-et-opkald-fra-hornbaek-til-farum/ | publisher = dbold.dk | date = 21 March 2016 }}</ref>

==Club career==

===FC Nordsjælland===
Nelsson got his debut for [[FC Nordsjælland]] on 12 September 2016. Having started on the bench, he replaced [[Mathias Jensen]] in the 87nd minute in a 3–1 defeat against [[Aarhus Gymnastikforening|AGF]] in the [[Danish Superliga]].<ref>{{ cite web | title =AGF VS. NORDSJÆLLAND 3 - 1 | url = https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2016/09/12/denmark/superliga/agf-aarhus/fc-nordsjalland/2238321/ | publisher = soccerway.com | date = 12 September 2016 }}</ref> On 14 October 2016, Nelsson signed his first professional contract and was promoted to the first team squad.<ref>{{ cite web | title = Ung midtbanemand forlænger med FCN | url = http://www.bold.dk/fodbold/nyheder/ung-midtbanemand-forlaenger-med-fcn/ | publisher = bold.dk | date = 14 October 2016 }}</ref> He played 23 league games for FCN in the 2016–17 season. Nelsson played the first two league games at the midfield, but then he was retrained as a centre back.<ref>[http://www.bold.dk/fodbold/nyheder/victor-nelsson-er-klar-paa-ny-position/ Victor Nelsson er klar på ny position]‚ bold.dk, 31 July 2017</ref> He also became a regular starter from the 2017–18 season.

At the age of 19 and after a great period in the first half part of the 2017–18 season, Nelsson got called up to the [[Denmark League XI national football team|Denmark B national team]] by [[Åge Hareide]], so he could look him forward and consider to pick him to the A national team.<ref>[https://www.bold.dk/fodbold/nyheder/glad-fcn-stopper-ikke-nok-bare-at-vaere-med/ Glad FCN-stopper: Ikke nok bare at være med]‚ bold.dk, 12 December 2017</ref> In the winter break 2017–18, he changed his shirt number from 36 to 4.

===FC Copenhagen===
On 5 July 2019, Nelsson joined [[FC Copenhagen]] signing a contract until 2024.<ref>[https://www.fck.dk/nyhed/fck-henter-victor-nelsson/ FCK henter Victor Nelsson]‚ fck.dk, 5 July 2019</ref>

===Galatasaray===
[[Galatasaray S.K. (football)|Galatasaray]] announced on 11 August 2021 that it had reached an agreement with [[F.C. Copenhagen|Copenhagen]] for a transfer fee of €7 million to be paid within five years. In addition, according to the agreement, the unilateral release fee of the football player was determined as €25 million.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.galatasaray.org/haber/gs-sportif-a-s/victor-nelsson-galatasarayda/49135|title=Victor Nelsson Galatasaray'da!|publisher=Galatasaray S.K.|date=11 August 2021|access-date=11 August 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.galatasaray.org/haber/futbol/hos-geldin-victor-nelsson/49136|title=Hoş geldin Victor Nelsson!|publisher=Galatasaray S.K.|date=11 August 2021|access-date=11 August 2021}}</ref>

Nelsson became the champion in the [[Süper Lig]] in the [[2022–23 Süper Lig|2022–23]] season with the [[Galatasaray S.K. (football)|Galatasaray]] team. Defeating [[MKE Ankaragücü|Ankaragücü]] 4-1 away in the match played in the 36th week on 30 May 2023, Galatasaray secured the lead with 2 weeks before the end and won the 23rd championship in its history.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/65761517|title=Turkish Super Lig: Galatasaray win title for 23rd time after beating Ankaragucu 4-1|publisher=BBC Sport|date=May 30, 2023|access-date=May 31, 2023}}</ref>
