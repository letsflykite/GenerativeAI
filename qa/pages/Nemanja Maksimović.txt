'''Nemanja Maksimović''' ({{lang-sr-Cyrl|Немања Максимовић}}, {{IPA-sh|němaɲa maksǐːmoʋitɕ|pron}}; born 26 January 1995) is a Serbian professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[defensive midfielder]] for [[La Liga]] club [[Getafe CF|Getafe]] and the [[Serbia national football team|Serbia national team]].

==Club career==
===Early career===
Born in [[Banja Koviljača]], Maksimović played for [[Red Star Belgrade]]'s youth selection teams up until the under-19 level, but eventually had his contract terminated. After his display at the [[2013 UEFA European Under-19 Championship|2013 UEFA U19 Championship]], he signed with Slovenian side [[NK Domžale|Domžale]]. He made his professional debut on 18 October 2013 in a 2–2 draw with [[NK Zavrč|Zavrč]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Zavrč – Domžale 2:2 – Prva liga Telekom Slovenije |url=https://www.prvaliga.si/tekmovanja/default.asp?action=tekma&id_tekme=9560 |website=prvaliga.si |access-date=6 February 2020 |language=sl |date=18 October 2013}}</ref>

===Astana===
On 7 February 2015, Maksimović signed a two-and-a-half-year contract with [[FC Astana|Astana]] of the [[Kazakhstan Premier League]].<ref>{{cite web|script-title=ru:В ФК Астана - чемпион Европы-2013|url=http://fca.kz/rus/news/4211/|publisher=[[FC Astana]] official website|access-date=9 February 2015|language=ru|date=7 February 2015}}</ref> On 27 August 2015, Maksimović scored the equalizing goal against [[APOEL FC|APOEL]] in the second leg of the [[2015–16 UEFA Champions League qualifying phase and play-off round#Play-off round|2015–16 UEFA Champions League play-offs]], which qualified Astana to their first ever Champions League group stage campaign.<ref name="HotSportInt">{{cite web|url=http://fudbal.hotsport.rs/2017/05/13/hotsport-intervju-nemanja-maksimovic-sanjao-sam-da-igram-za-zvezdu-iskoristicu-sansu-u-valensiji-foto/|author=Petar Lazić|title=Hotsport.rs: ''HOTSPORT INTERVJU – NEMANJA MAKSIMOVIĆ: San mi se nije ostvario da igram za voljeni klub, a sada ću igrati protiv Mesija i Ronalda''|language=sr|date=13 May 2017|access-date=8 January 2018}}</ref>

===Valencia===
On 2 July 2017, [[Valencia CF|Valencia]] announced the signing of Maksimović on a contract until 2022.<ref>{{cite web|title=Maksimovic, new Valencia CF player until 2022|url=http://en.valenciacf.com/ver/71802/maksimovic--new-valencia-cf-player-until-2022.html|website=valenciacf.com|publisher=Valencia CF|access-date=3 July 2017|date=2 July 2017}}</ref> He made his debut for the club on 18 August, coming on as a late substitute for [[Carlos Soler (footballer)|Carlos Soler]] in a 1–0 [[La Liga]] home win against [[UD Las Palmas|Las Palmas]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.marca.com/eventos/marcador/futbol/2017_18/la-liga/jornada_1/val_pal/|title=Este Valencia pinta bien|trans-title=This Valencia look good|publisher=[[Marca (newspaper)|Marca]]|language=es|date=18 August 2017|access-date=18 August 2017}}</ref>

===Getafe===
On 16 July 2018, Maksimović signed with [[Getafe CF|Getafe]] until 2024.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.getafecf.com/Noticia/tabid/137/Article/5407/El-Getafe-se-hace-con-Maksimovic.aspx|title=	El Getafe se hace con Maksimovic|publisher=Getafe CF|trans-title=Getafe signs Maksimovic|language=es|date=16 July 2018|access-date=16 July 2018}}</ref>

==International career==
Maksimović played for the Serbia U19 team that won the [[2013 UEFA European Under-19 Championship]].<ref>[http://www.uefa.com/under19/season=2013/matches/round=2000317/match=2012711/postmatch/lineups/index.html] UEFA: Match Report - France 0-1 Serbia - 1 August 2013</ref> He was named to Serbia's U20 squad for the [[2015 FIFA U-20 World Cup]] in New Zealand, scoring the winner with two minutes of extra-time remaining in the final, beating [[Brazil national under-20 football team|Brazil]] 2–1 for Serbia's first international honour as an independent nation.<ref>{{cite news|title=Under-20 World Cup: Serbia beat Brazil in Auckland final|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/0/football/33210273|access-date=20 June 2015|publisher=[[BBC Sport]]|date=20 June 2015}}</ref>
Maksimović made his debut for [[Serbia national football team|Serbia]] on 23 March 2016, in a 1–0 defeat to [[Poland national football team|Poland]].<ref>{{cite web|script-title=ru:Максимович дебютировал за Сербию|url=http://www.sports.kz/news/maksimovich-debyutiroval-za-serbiyu|website=sports.kz|publisher=Sports.KZ|access-date=24 March 2016|language=ru|date=24 March 2016}}</ref> In May 2018 he was named in Serbia’s preliminary squad for the [[2018 FIFA World Cup]] in Russia, but he wasn't selected for the final squad.<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.fss.rs/index.php?id=2021&aid=13898 |script-title=sr:Један тим, срцем свим – Младен Крстајић одабрао |trans-title=One team, with all their hearts – Mladen Krstajić selects |website=FSS.rs |publisher=[[Football Association of Serbia]] |date=24 May 2018 |access-date=24 May 2018 |language=sr}}</ref>

In November 2022, he was selected in Serbia's squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] in Qatar.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://fss.rs/en/head-coach-dragan-stojkovic-announces-the-list-of-players-for-the-fifa-world-cup-2022-in-qatar/ |title=Head-coach Dragan Stojković announces the list of players for the FIFA World Cup 2022 in Qatar |publisher=[[Football Association of Serbia]] |date=11 November 2022 |access-date=11 November 2022}}</ref> He played in all three group stage matches, against [[Brazil national football team|Brazil]],<ref>{{cite news |url= https://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/sports/world-cup-2022/richarlison-brings-brazil-to-life-in-the-second-half-defeats-serbia-2-0/3971175/ |title=Richarlison Brings Brazil to Life in the Second Half, Defeats Serbia 2-0 |first=Kristen |last=Conti |newspaper=NBCNew York |location=New York |date=24 November 2022 |access-date=7 December 2022}}</ref> [[Cameroon national football team|Cameroon]],<ref>{{Cite news |last=Sky Sports |date=2022-11-28 |title=World Cup 2022 - Cameroon 3-3 Serbia: Vincent Aboubakar and Eric Maxim Choupo-Moting goals complete stunning comeback |language=en |work=Sky Sports |url=https://www.skysports.com/football/cameroon-vs-serbia/report/462988#:~:text=World%20Cup%202022%20%2D%20Cameroon%203,Moting%20goals%20complete%20stunning%20comeback&text=Cameroon%20kept%20alive%20their%20faint,with%20Serbia%20in%20Al%2DWakrah. |access-date=2022-11-28}}</ref> and [[Switzerland national football team|Switzerland]].<ref>{{cite news |url= https://www.theguardian.com/football/2022/dec/02/serbia-switzerland-world-cup-group-g-match-report |title=Switzerland advance and Serbia go out as Freuler finish settles stormy contest |first=Nick |last=Ames |newspaper=The Guardian|location=London |date=2 December 2022 |access-date=7 December 2022}}</ref> Serbia finished fourth in the group.<ref>{{cite news |url= https://soccer.nbcsports.com/2022/12/02/world-cup-2022-group-g-brazil-serbia-cameroon-switzerland-rankings-table-standings/ |title=World Cup 2022 Group G: Brazil, Serbia, Cameroon, Switzerland schedule, fixtures, rankings |first=Nicholas |last=Mendola |newspaper=NBC Sports|location=Lusail |date=2 December 2022 |access-date=7 December 2022}}</ref>
