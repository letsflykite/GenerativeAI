'''Jakub Piotr Kiwior''' (born 15 February 2000) is a Polish professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[centre-back]] or a [[left-back]] for [[Premier League]] club [[Arsenal F.C|Arsenal]] and the [[Poland national football team|Poland national team]].

Kiwior is a product of the [[R.S.C. Anderlecht]] academy. After being unable to break into the first team, he joined [[FK Železiarne Podbrezová]].

Kiwior represented Poland at the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Znamy szeroką kadrę reprezentacji Polski na mundial w Katarze! - Reprezentacja A |url=https://pzpn.pl/reprezentacje/reprezentacja-a/aktualnosci/2022-10-20/znamy-szeroka-kadre-reprezentacji-polski-na-mundial-w-katarze |access-date=20 October 2022 |website=PZPN - Łączy nas piłka |language=pl}}</ref>

==Club career==
===FK Železiarne Podbrezová===
Kiwior made his [[Slovak Super Liga|Fortuna Liga]] debut for [[FK Železiarne Podbrezová|Železiarne Podbrezová]] against [[FC Nitra|Nitra]] on 16 February 2019. Kiwior played the entirety of the 3–1 victory at [[Štadión pod Zoborom|pod Zoborom]].<ref>[https://ulk.futbalnet.sk/zapas/354271/ FC Nitra - FK Železiarne Podbrezová 1:3] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190217030300/https://ulk.futbalnet.sk/zapas/354271/ |date=17 February 2019 }} 16 February 2019, futbalnet.sk</ref>

===MŠK Žilina===
In August 2019, Kiwior's transfer to [[MŠK Žilina]] was announced.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Jakub Piotr Kiwior prestupuje do MŠK Žilina|url=http://www.zpfutbal.sk/jakub-piotr-kiwior-prestupuje-do-msk-zilina/|date=3 August 2019|website=FK Železiarne Podbrezová|language=sk-SK|access-date=12 May 2020}}</ref> He made his debut in the yellow jersey on 10 August 2019 during an [[Štadión pod Čebraťom|away]] fixture against [[MFK Ružomberok|Ružomberok]]. While Žilina grabbed a three-goal lead in the first half, Ružomberok scored two in the second period and hence Kiwior was brought on to secure the defence, as a replacement for [[Lukáš Jánošík]]. ''Šošoni'' managed to keep the 3–2 win and remained undefeated in the league.<ref>{{Cite web|title=MFK Ružomberok - MŠK Žilina {{!}} 2 - 3|url=https://ulk.futbalnet.sk/zapas/426346/|website=ulk.futbalnet.sk|language=sk|access-date=12 May 2020}}</ref>

Over a month later, in his second appearance, Kiwior had debuted in the starting-XI against [[FC ViOn Zlaté Moravce|ViOn Zlaté Moravce]]. While Žilina's defence remained unbeaten, the strikers had failed to score and then match concluded in a goal-less tie.<ref>{{Cite web|title=FC ViOn Zlaté Moravce - Vráble - MŠK Žilina {{!}} 0 - 0|url=https://ulk.futbalnet.sk/zapas/426372/|website=ulk.futbalnet.sk|language=sk|access-date=12 May 2020}}</ref> On 9 November Kiwior had recorded his first assist with Žilina. At [[Štadión pod Dubňom]], Žilina was behind Ružomberok by two goals in the second half. Kiwior, a first-half replacement, managed to find [[Ján Bernát]] with a pass and narrow the score. Still, Žilina lost 2–1.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Žilina vs. Ružomberok - 9 November 2019 - Soccerway|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2019/11/09/slovakia/super-liga/mk-ilina/mfk-ruomberok/3032662/|website=int.soccerway.com|access-date=12 May 2020}}</ref>

During the season, he also made sporadic appearances for the reserves competing in [[2. Liga (Slovakia)|2. Liga]].

During the [[COVID-19 pandemic in Slovakia|coronavirus pandemic]], as Žilina had entered liquidation to survive financially and had consequently released 17 players, Kiwior remained unaffected.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Zo dňa na deň sa mu rozpadol káder. Na likvidáciu MŠK Žilina reaguje aj tréner Pavol Staňo|url=https://sport.aktuality.sk/c/442052/fortuna-liga-zo-dna-na-den-sa-mu-rozpadol-kader-na-likvidaciu-msk-zilina-reaguje-aj-trener-pavol-stano/|last=s|first=SPORT SK, s r o & Ringier Axel Springer Slovakia a|website=Šport.sk|language=sk|access-date=12 May 2020}}</ref>

===Spezia Calcio===
[[File:Jakub Kiwior 2 Match Football Spezia Calcio x US Sassuolo Calcio Stade Alberto Picco - La Spezia (IT42) - 2022-08-27 - 2 (cropped).jpg|left|thumb|230x230px|Kiwior with [[Spezia Calcio|Spezia]] in 2022]]
On 31 August 2021, Kiwior signed for [[Serie A]] side [[Spezia Calcio|Spezia]] on a four-year deal.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.acspezia.com/it/news/ufficiale-jakub-kiwior-un-nuovo-giocatore-dello-spezia-calcio.22790.html |title=Ufficiale {{!}} Jakub Kiwior è un nuovo giocatore dello Spezia Calcio |work=Spezia Calcio |language=it |date=31 August 2021 }}</ref> He made his debut against [[Inter Milan]] on 1 December 2021 as a starter and gave away a penalty after handball that converted into goal by [[Lautaro Martínez]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://sport.tvp.pl/57227078/jakub-kiwior-w-meczu-inter-spezia-zadebiutowal-w-serie-a-kim-jest-reprezentant-polski-u21-sylwetka-kariera-wiek-kluby|title=Jakub Kiwior zadebiutował w Serie A przeciwko Interowi. Kim jest młody piłkarz Spezii?|website=sport.tvp.pl|language=pl|date=2 December 2021|access-date=8 May 2023}}</ref>

Kiwior often deployed as [[Midfielder|defensive midfielder]] by his former manager [[Thiago Motta]] because of his ability to posses and control the ball.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://totalfootballanalysis.com/article/jakub-kiwior-scout-report-tactical-analysis-tactics|title=Jakub Kiwior: Why he is wanted by teams across Europe|first=Marcel|last=Seifeddine|publisher=Total Football Analysis|date=2 September 2022|access-date=8 May 2023}}</ref>

===Arsenal===
On 23 January 2023, Kiwior moved to [[Arsenal F.C.|Arsenal]] on a long-term contract,<ref>{{cite web|publisher=Arsenal F.C.|url=https://www.arsenal.com/news/jakub-kiwior-joins-long-term-contract|title=Jakub Kiwior joins on long-term contract|date=23 January 2023|access-date=23 January 2023}}</ref> making his debut against [[Sporting CP|Sporting]] on 9 March 2023 in the [[Europa League]] last 16 first leg as Arsenal drew 2–2.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=The Guardian|url=https://www.theguardian.com/football/live/2023/mar/09/sporting-v-arsenal-europa-league-last-16-first-leg-live-score-updates|title=Sporting v Arsenal, Europa League Last 16 First Leg|first=Barry|last=Glendenning|date=9 March 2023|access-date=9 March 2023}}</ref> He made his [[Emirates Stadium|home]] and [[Premier League]] debut as a substitute in Arsenal's 4–1 win against [[Crystal Palace F.C.|Crystal Palace]] on 19 March 2023. He was handed his first start in a 3–1 win over [[Chelsea F.C.|Chelsea]] in early May after erratic performances from Arsenal back line since [[William Saliba]]'s injury. He then started in the next game against [[Newcastle United F.C.|Newcastle]], where a handball decision was overturned.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/64929886|title=Arsenal cruise past Palace to go eight points clear|first=Michael|last=Emons|publisher=BBC Sport|date=19 March 2023|access-date=19 March 2023}}</ref> He helped Arsenal have a clean sheet in that match and gained compliments for his performance.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://talksport.com/football/1417702/arsenal-fans-reaction-jakub-kiwior-newcastle-masterclass/|title=Arsenal fans left thinking the same thing as Jakub Kiwior puts in masterclass against Newcastle|first=Connor|last=Andrews|publisher=TalkSport|date=7 May 2023|access-date=8 May 2023}}</ref> On the final matchday of the 2022–23 season, he scored his first goal for Arsenal in a 5–0 win over [[Wolverhampton Wanderers F.C.|Wolverhampton Wanderers]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/65661810 |title=Arsenal thrash Wolves to finish season off in style|first=Steve|last=Sutcliffe|publisher=BBC Sport |date=28 May 2023|access-date=28 May 2023}}</ref>

==International career==
Kiwior made his debut as a starter for [[Poland national football team|Poland]] against [[Netherlands national football team|Netherlands]] in a [[2022–23 UEFA Nations League A#Group 4|2022–23 UEFA Nations League]] 2–2 away draw on 11 June 2022.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=soccerway.com|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2022/06/10/europe/uefa-nations-league/netherlands/poland/3710411/|title=Netherlands vs. Poland 2–2|date=11 June 2022|access-date=11 June 2023}}</ref> In November 2022, he was named in the 26-man squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] in Qatar. He started in all four of Poland's games during the tournament.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=soccerway.com|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2022/11/22/world/world-cup/mexico/poland/3749514/|title=Mexico vs. Poland 0–0|date=22 November 2022|access-date=11 June 2023}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|publisher=soccerway.com|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2022/11/26/world/world-cup/poland/saudi-arabia/3749516/|title=Poland vs. Saudi Arabia 2–0|date=26 November 2022|access-date=11 June 2023}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|publisher=soccerway.com|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2022/11/30/world/world-cup/poland/argentina/3749517/|title=Poland vs. Argentina 0–2|date=30 November 2022|access-date=11 June 2023}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|publisher=soccerway.com|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2022/12/04/world/world-cup/france/poland/3298977/|title=France vs. Poland 3–1|date=4 December 2022|access-date=11 June 2023}}</ref>

On 16 June 2023, Kiwior scored his first international goal in a [[Exhibition game|friendly match]] against [[Germany national football team|Germany]] which ended 1–0 for Poland.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.polskieradio.pl/395/7790/Artykul/3190790,poland-beat-germany-10-in-football-friendly |title=Poland beat Germany 1-0 in football friendly |website=polskieradio.pl |date=16 June 2023 |access-date=17 June 2023}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.reuters.com/sports/soccer/crisis-deepens-euro-2024-hosts-germany-with-1-0-loss-poland-2023-06-16/ |title=Crisis deepens for Euro 2024 hosts Germany with 1-0 loss to Poland |website=reuters.com |date=17 June 2023 |access-date=17 June 2023}}</ref>
