'''Renato Steffen''' (born 3 November 1991) is a Swiss professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Winger (association football)|left winger]] for [[Swiss Super League]] club [[FC Lugano|Lugano]] and the [[Switzerland national football team|Switzerland national team]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.footballdatabase.eu/football.joueurs.renato.steffen.173928.en.html|title=Renato Steffen - Wolfsburg - Stats|website=footballdatabase.eu|publisher=Football Database|access-date=12 April 2020}}</ref>

== Club career ==
=== Youth football ===
Steffen played his youth football with local club FC Erlinsbach. He played three years in the [[FC Aarau|Aarau]] U-14 and U-16 youth teams. He then transferred to SC Schöftland and spent another four years with them.

=== Solothurn and Thun ===
Steffen played amateur football in the [[1. Liga Classic]] for [[FC Solothurn|Solothurn]]. After one season he transferred to [[FC Thun|Thun]] in July 2012<ref name="bz 2012-10-20">{{cite news | first = Alexander | last = Wäfler | year = 2012 | url = http://www.bernerzeitung.ch/sport/fussball/Unerschrocken-in-einer-neuen-Welt/story/15328912 | title = Unerschrocken in einer neuen Welt | newspaper = Berner Zeitung | publisher = Bernerzeitung | access-date = 20 October 2012}}</ref> and made his professional debut on 2 September 2012 in the game against [[Servette FC]]. He scored 4 goals in 19 games for Thun in the [[2012–13 Swiss Super League]] that season.

=== Young Boys ===
At the end of that season Steffen was sold for €500.000 to [[BSC Young Boys|Young Boys]]. Here he signed a four-year deal in Bern.

=== Basel ===
During the winter break of the [[2015–16 Swiss Super League|2015–16 Super League]], on 12 January, [[FC Basel|Basel]] announced that Steffen had signed a four-and-a-half-year contract with them.<ref name="fcb-aktuell-2015/16-Steffen">{{cite web | last = FC Basel 1893 | author-link = | date = 12 January 2016 | url = https://www.fcb.ch/aktuell/news/1-mannschaft/2016/01/renato-steffen-wechselt-zum-fc-basel-1893 | title = Renato Steffen wechselt zum FC Basel 1893 | format = | work = Renato Steffen moves to FC Basel 1893 | publisher = FC Basel 1893 AG | language = de | access-date = 2022-11-16}}</ref> Steffen played his first team league debut for Basel on 7 February, coming in as a substitute in the 74th minute, in the home game in the [[St. Jakob-Park]] against [[FC Luzern|Luzern]]. He scored his first goal for his new club in the same game, it was the final goal of the match in the 85 minute and Basel won the game 3–0.
<ref name="fcb-achiv-2015/16-FCB-FCL">{{cite web | last = Verein "Basler Fussballarchiv” | author-link = | date = 7 February 2016 | url = https://www.fcb-archiv.ch/saison/spiele?command=detail&id=31043 | title = FC Basel - FC Luzern 3:0 (0:0) | format = | publisher = Verein "Basler Fussballarchiv” | access-date = 2022-11-16}}</ref>

Under trainer [[Urs Fischer (footballer)|Urs Fischer]] Steffen won the [[Swiss Super League]] championship with Basel at the end of their [[2015–16 FC Basel season|2015–16 season]] season.<ref name="SRF-2015/16-19-meistertite">{{cite web | first = | last = | author-link = | date = 30 April 2016 | url = https://www.srf.ch/sport/fussball/super-league/fc-basel-holt-den-19-meistertitel | title = FC Basel holt den 19. Meistertitel | format = | work = FC Basel wins their 19th championship title | publisher = SRF Zürich | language = de | access-date = 2022-11-16}}</ref><ref name="fcb 2016-04-30">{{cite web | first = Casper | last = Marti | year = 2016 | url = http://www.fcb.ch/de-CH/News/2016/04/30/FCB-Sion | title = Es ist vollbracht ! Der FCB ist zum 19. Mal Meister | publisher = FC Basel 1893 | access-date = 30 April 2016}}</ref> Again at the end of the [[2016–17 Swiss Super League|2016–17 Super League season]] Basel won the league title. For the club this was the eighth title in a row and their 20th championship title in total.<ref name="fcb 2017-06-02">{{cite web | first = Casper | last = Marti | year = 2017 | url = https://www.fcb.ch/de-CH/News/2017/06/02/Der-Saisonabschluss-im-Zeitraffer | title = Der Saisonabschluss im Zeitraffer | publisher = FC Basel 1893 | access-date = 2 June 2017}}</ref> They also won the [[Swiss Cup]] for the twelfth time that season, which meant they had won the double for the sixth time in the club's history.<ref name="schweiz am wochenende-20. Mal">{{cite web | first = Markus | last = Brütsch | author-link = | date = 29 April 2017 | url = https://www.watson.ch/sport/fussball/726807678-zwei-sterne-fuer-basel-der-fcb-gewinnt-zum-20-mal-die-meisterschaft-wie-es-dazu-kam | title = Zwei Sterne für Basel: Der FCB gewinnt zum 20. Mal die Meisterschaft – wie es dazu kam | format = | work = Two stars for Basel: FCB wins the championship for the 20th time – how it came about | publisher = schweiz am wochenende | language = de | access-date = 2022-11-16}}</ref>

During the winter break of their 2017–18 season Steffan left the club and signed for [[VfL Wolfsburg]].<ref name="fcb-aktuell-2017/18-Steffan">{{cite web | last = FC Basel 1893 | author-link = | date = 10 January 2018 | url = https://www.fcb.ch/aktuell/news/1-mannschaft/2018/01/renato-steffen-wechselt-zum-vfl-wolfsburg | title = Renato Steffen wechselt zum VfL Wolfsburg | format = | work = Renato Steffen is moving to VfL Wolfsburg | publisher = FC Basel 1893 AG | language = de | access-date = 2022-11-16}}</ref> During his two years with the club Steffan played a total of 112 games for Basel scoring a total of 23 goals. 61 of these games were in the [[Swiss Super League]], seven in the [[Swiss Cup]], 16 in the UEFA competitions (Champios League and Europa League) and 28 were friendly games. He scored 15 goal in the domestic league, two in the cup, two in the European games and the other four were scored during the test games.<ref name="fcb-achiv- Renato Steffen">{{cite web | last = Verein "Basler Fussballarchiv” | authorlink = | year = 2018 | url = https://www.fcb-archiv.ch/spieler?command=detail&id=8868 | title = Renato Steffen - FCB statistic | format = | publisher = Verein "Basler Fussballarchiv” | accessdate = 2022-11-16}}</ref>

=== Wolfsburg ===
On 10 January 2018, Steffen signed a three and a half year deal with [[VfL Wolfsburg]]. The transfer fee was reported as being €1.75 million.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.kicker.de/news/fussball/bundesliga/startseite/714577/artikel_alles-klar_renato-steffen-wird-ein-wolf.html|title=VfL bestätigt: Renato Steffen ist ein Wolf|trans-title=VfL confirms: Renato Steffen is a wolf|language=de|website=kicker.de|publisher=Kicker|date=10 January 2018|access-date=12 April 2020}}</ref> He scored the first Wolfsburg goal after the temporary cancellation of the [[Bundesliga]] because of the [[COVID-19 pandemic in Germany]], against [[FC Augsburg]] with a header from the edge of the box.

=== Lugano ===
On 30 August 2022, Steffen signed a three-year contract with [[FC Lugano|Lugano]].<ref>{{cite web|publisher=Lugano|url=https://www.fclugano.com/benvenuto-renato/|title=RENATO STEFFEN ALL'FC LUGANO FINO AL 2025|date=30 August 2022|access-date=30 August 2022|language=it}}</ref>

== International career ==
Steffen made his first appearance for [[Switzerland national football team|Switzerland]] on 9 October 2015, in a [[UEFA Euro 2016 qualifying|Euro 2016 qualifier]] against [[San Marino national football team|San Marino]]. He was subbed on in the 78th minute of a 7–0 victory.

In May 2019, he Steffen played in the [[2019 UEFA Nations League Finals]], where his team finished fourth.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/48191915|title = Pickford the hero in England shootout win|work = BBC Sport}}</ref> He then started for Switzerland against [[Germany national football team|Germany]] in a [[2020–21 UEFA Nations League]] match.

Steffen was forced to sit out of [[UEFA Euro 2020]], due to injury.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.football.ch/sfv/nationalteams/a-team/news/renato-steffen-verpasst-uefa-euro.aspx |title=Schweizerischer Fussballverband - Renato Steffen verpasst die UEFA EURO 2020 |website=www.football.ch |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211028172848/https://www.football.ch/sfv/nationalteams/a-team/news/renato-steffen-verpasst-uefa-euro.aspx |archive-date=28 October 2021}} </ref> Following the tournament, he returned to the national team and was frequently chosen in the starting lineup for [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification (UEFA)|2022 World Cup qualifying]] matches. On 12 October 2021, Steffen scored his first international goal for Switzerland against [[Lithuania national football team|Lithuania]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.kicker.de/renato-steffen/nationalspieler|title = Renato Steffen &#124; VFL Wolfsburg &#124; 2021/22 &#124; Spielerprofil}}</ref>

On 9 November 2022, he was announced as part of the final squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] in [[Qatar]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.football.ch/sfv/nationalteams/a-team/news/murat-yakin-gibt-schweizer-wm-aufgebot-bekannt.aspx |title=Murat Yakin gibt Schweizer WM-Aufgebot bekannt |trans-title=Murat Yakin announces Swiss World Cup squad |publisher=[[Swiss Football Association]] |date=9 November 2022 |access-date=9 November 2022 |language=de}}</ref> At the tournament itself he only had a brief appearance in their group game against [[Brazil national football team|Brazil]], coming on in the last fifteen minutes of the game. 

On 16 March 2023, he was included in Switzerland's squad for their initial matches of their upcoming [[UEFA Euro 2024 qualifying Group I|UEFA Euro qualifying]] campaign.<ref name="nati">{{cite web | author = | date = 2023-03-16 | url = https://www.football.ch/sfv/nationalteams/a-team/news/maenner-nationalteam-mit-neuen-kraeften-in-die-neue-kampagne.aspx | title = Männer-Nationalteam: Mit «neuen» Kräften in die neue Kampagne | format = | work = | publisher = [[Swiss Football Association|SFV]] | access-date = 2023-03-20}}</ref> On 25 March 2023, he scored a hat-trick in Switzerland's 5–0 win over [[Belarus national football team|Belarus]], in [[Novi Sad]], [[Serbia]].<ref>{{Cite web |last=UEFA.com |title=Belarus-Switzerland {{!}} European Qualifiers 2024 |url=https://www.uefa.com/european-qualifiers/match/2036307--belarus-vs-switzerland/ |access-date=2023-03-26 |website=UEFA.com |language=en}}</ref>
