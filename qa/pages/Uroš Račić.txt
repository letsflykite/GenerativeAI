'''Uroš Račić''' ({{lang-sr-cyr|Урош Рачић}}; born 17 March 1998) is a Serbian professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[defensive midfielder]] for [[La Liga]] club [[Valencia CF|Valencia]] and the [[Serbia national football team|Serbia national team]].

==Club career==
===Early years===
Born in [[Kraljevo]],<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.unijasprs.org.rs/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=3204|title=Близанци из "женске" улице|website=unijasprs.org.rs|language=sr|date=2 March 2009|access-date=14 May 2016}}</ref> Račić started playing football in local football academies "Bambi" and "Knjaz" along with his twin brother Bogdan. After three years paying with Altina football school in [[Zemun]], twins returned to home town and joined [[FK Sloga Kraljevo|Sloga Kraljevo]]. They also played with Sloboda Čačak before joined [[OFK Beograd]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.crvenazvezdafk.com/scc/vest/5516/Racic-Lune-mi-je-drugi-otac|title=Рачић - Луне ми је други отац|website=Red Star Belgrade official website|language=sr|date=5 January 2017|access-date=7 January 2017}}</ref> After two years he spent with youth team, Uroš was also licensed for the first team for the [[2015–16 Serbian SuperLiga|2015–16]] season.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.ofkbeograd.co.rs/index.php/os/os/16?tip=players&show=213&stat=1&detaljnostat=14|title=Uroš Račić|website=OFK Beograd official website|language=sr|access-date=3 March 2016}}</ref><ref name="srbijafudbal">{{cite web |url=http://www.srbijafudbal.com/crzvezda/racic_u.htm|title=Uroš Račić|website=srbijafudbal.com|language=sr|access-date=12 October 2016}}</ref> After he scored a goal for youth team in a match against [[Red Star Belgrade]] for 4:1 win, Uroš moved in opponent team with his twin brother and signed a three-year professional contract with [[Red Star Belgrade|Red Star]] on 13 March 2016.<ref name="racici">{{cite web |url=http://mondo.rs/a883890/Sport/Fudbal/Uros-i-Bogdan-Racic-OFK-u-FK-Crvena-zvezda.html|title=Ekspresno završeno: Račići potpisali za Zvezdu!|website=mondo.rs|language=sr|date=13 March 2016|access-date=14 May 2016}}</ref>

===Red Star Belgrade===
Račić made his debut with [[Red Star Belgrade]] on 8 May 2016 in a friendly match against [[Olympiacos F.C.|Olympiacos]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.crvenazvezdafk.com/scc/vest/4845/crvena-zvezda-olimpijakos-2-2-1-1|title=Црвена звезда - Олимпијакос 2:2 (1:1)|website=Red Star Belgrade official website|language=sr|date=8 May 2016|access-date=14 May 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.crvenazvezdafk.com/scc/vest/4848/uros-racic-velika-cast|title=Урош Рачић - Велика част|website=Red Star Belgrade official website|language=sr|date=9 May 2016|access-date=14 May 2016}}</ref> On 14 May 2016, he made his [[Serbian SuperLiga|SuperLiga]] debut in away match against [[FK Radnik Surdulica|Radnik Surdulica]], coming on as a substitute for [[Aleksandar Katai]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.crvenazvezdafk.com/scc/vest/4867/radnik-crvena-zvezda-1-4-1-2|title=Радник - Црвена звезда 1:4 (1:2)|website=Red Star Belgrade official website|language=sr|date=14 May 2016|access-date=14 May 2016}}</ref>

During the 2017 summer pre-season, Račić was presented as one of the club's top 10 youth prospects.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.alo.rs/top-10-zvezdinih-klinaca-za-buducnost/109373|title=ALO! PREDSTAVLJA Top 10 Zvezdinih klinaca za budućnost|website=[[Alo!]]|language=sr|date=31 May 2017|access-date=7 July 2017}}</ref> On 6 July 2017, Račić made his first continental appearance for the club, in the second leg of the [[2017–18 UEFA Europa League#First qualifying round|first qualifying round]] for the 2017–18 UEFA Europa League, in a 3–3 draw against [[Floriana F.C.|Floriana]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.crvenazvezdafk.com/scc/vest/6036/Florijana-Crvena-zvezda-3-3-1-1|title=Флоријана - Црвена звезда 3:3 (1:1)|website=Red Star Belgrade official website|language=sr|date=6 July 2017|access-date=7 July 2017}}</ref> Račić scored his first senior goal for [[Red Star Belgrade]] in a 3−1 victory against [[OFK Bačka Bačka Palanka|OFK Bačka]] on 13 August 2017.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.crvenazvezdafk.com/scc/vest/6171/Racic-Dugo-sam-sanjao-ovaj-trenutak|title=Рачић - Дуго сам сањао овај тренутак|website=Red Star Belgrade official website|language=sr|date=13 August 2017|access-date=13 August 2017}}</ref> On the last day of the 2017 summer transfer window, Račić extended his contract with Red Star to 30 June 2021.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://fss.rs/documents/fudbal/2017/Van_Br_19_2017.pdf|title="FUDBAL", vanredni broj 19/17, page 35|website=[[Football Association of Serbia]]|language=sr|date=31 August 2017|access-date=1 September 2017}}</ref> He also scored in a 2–1 victory over [[FK Mladost Lučani|Mladost Lučani]] in the 14th fixture of the Serbian SuperLiga campaign.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.mozzartsport.com/vesti/zvezda-spavala-i-prezivela-pesic/182416|title=Zvezda spavala i preživela! (VIDEO)|website=mozzartsport.com|language=sr|date=22 October 2017|access-date=22 October 2017}}</ref> His goal was proclaimed goal of the week in the domestic football competition, after which he won a wrist[[watch]] as a reward.<ref name="pogba">{{cite web |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lW1jNrLEmNc|title=Gol kola - Uroš Račić - Dodela sata 10. novembar 2017.|website=soskanal.net|language=sr|date=12 November 2017|access-date=11 January 2018}}</ref> On 5 May 2018, Račić was fouled for a [[Penalty kick (association football)|penalty kick]], which was scored by [[Dejan Joveljić]] in 4–0 home victory over [[FK Spartak Subotica|Spartak Subotica]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.crvenazvezdafk.com/scc/vest/7038/crvena-zvezda-spartak-4-0-3-0|title=Црвена звезда - Спартак 4:0 (3:0)|website=Red Star Belgrade official website|language=sr|date=5 May 2018|access-date=7 May 2018}}</ref> He scored and assisted teammate [[Nemanja Radonjić]] in the last season match against [[FK Voždovac|Voždovac]] on 19 May 2018.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.mozzartsport.com/fudbal/vesti/cas-je-vidis-cas-je-ne-vidis-radonjic-i-ben-preneli-zurku-na-teren-video/295704|title=Čas je vidiš, čas je ne vidiš: Radonjić i Ben preneli žurku na teren! (VIDEO)|website=mozzartsport.com|language=sr|date=19 May 2018|access-date=20 May 2018}}</ref>

===Valencia===
Račić joined Valencia in the summer of 2018 in a [[euro|€]]2.2 million transfer from Red Star Belgrade.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://rs.n1info.com/a394939/Sport-Klub/Fudbal/Gotovo-Racic-u-Valensiji-Zvezdi-2.2-miliona-evra.html|title=Gotovo: Račić u Valensiji, Zvezdi 2.2 miliona evra|website=[[N1 (television)|N1]]|language=sr|date=8 June 2018|access-date=13 June 2018}}</ref> On 13 June 2018, he signed a four-year contract,<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.valenciacf.com/ver/85015/uro%A1-ra%8Di%87-%80%9Cvenir-al-valencia-cf-es-cumplir-un-sue%C3%B1o%80.html|title=Uroš Račić: "Venir al Valencia CF es cumplir un sueño"|website=Valencia CF official website|language=es|date=13 June 2018|access-date=13 June 2018}}</ref> but was assigned to [[Valencia CF Mestalla|the B-team]] in [[Segunda División B]]. A fixed-rate clause of €100 million was inserted in his contract.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.valenciacf.com/ver/85016/comunicado-oficial-i-uro%A1-ra%8Di%87.html|title=Comunicado oficial I Uroš Račić|website=Valencia CF official website|language=es|date=13 June 2018|access-date=13 June 2018}}</ref>

====2017–18 season====
On 4 December 2018, Račić made his debut for Valencia in a 1–0 win over [[CD Ebro]] in the [[2018–19 Copa del Rey#Round of 32|Copa del Rey]].<ref name="MV">{{cite web|url=http://www.mozzartsport.com/fudbal/vesti/racic-debitovao-za-valensiju-mestalja-je-odusevljena-hocemo-jos-video/313367|work=Mozzart Sport|title=Račić debitovao za Valensiju, Mestalja je oduševljena: Hoćemo još!|language=sr|date=4 December 2018|access-date=11 December 2018}}</ref>

====Loans====
On 14 January 2019, Račić was loaned to [[Segunda División]] side [[CD Tenerife]] until the end of [[2018–19 Segunda División|the season]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://en.valenciacf.com/ver/94928/official-statement-i-uro%A1-ra%8Di%87.html|title=Official statement {{!}} Uroš Račić|publisher=Valencia CF|date=14 January 2019|access-date=7 March 2019}}</ref> He was then loaned to Portuguese team [[F.C. Famalicão|FC Famalicão]] in August 2019.<ref name="PF">{{cite web|url=https://mozzartsport.com/fudbal/vesti/uros-racic-u-famalisau/334457|author=Dejan Stanković|work=Mozzart Sport|title=''Uroš Račić u Famalisau''|language=sr|date=7 August 2019|access-date=6 September 2019}}</ref>

====2020–21 season====
On 13 September 2020, Račić came on as a substitute in Valencia's opening game of the [[2020–21 La Liga]] season home against [[Levante UD|Levante]], in a 4–2 win.

==Style of play==
{{quote box|width=33%|quote="Račić is most similar like [[Slaviša Jokanović]], the real [[Squad number (association football)|'six']]. He is a [[Midfielder#Defensive midfielder|defensive midfielder]] with an extraordinary jump, great game review and incredible intelligence. He will just demonstrate his quality in the future."|source=—[[Zvezdan Terzić]], general director of [[Red Star Belgrade]], December 2017<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.mozzartsport.com/vesti/zvezdan-terzic-na-sasvim-drugaciji-nacin-racic-je-novi-jokanovic-ne-bih-doveo-geja-u-zvezdu-ofarbacu-se-ako-budemo-u-finalu-lige/189383|title=Zvezdan Terzić, na sasvim drugačiji način: Račić je novi Jokanović, ne bih doveo geja u Zvezdu, ofarbaću se ako budemo u finalu Lige Evrope|work=mozzartsport.com|location=Belgrade|date=30 December 2017|access-date=2 January 2018}}</ref>}}
Standing at 6-foot-4-inches (1.93 m),<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.crvenazvezdafk.com/scc/igraci/prvi-tim/350/uros-racic|title=Урош Рачић|website=Red Star Belgrade official website|language=sr|access-date=14 May 2016}}</ref> Račić is a right-footed footballer.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://sport.blic.rs/fudbal/domaci-fudbal/grujicev-naslednik-uros-racic-za-blicsport-necu-pare-zelim-da-me-pamte-po-igrama-u/kyxx48x|title=GRUJIĆEV NASLEDNIK Uroš Račić za Blicsport: Neću pare, želim da me pamte po igrama u Zvezdi|website=[[Blic]]|language=sr|date=12 February 2017|access-date=2 January 2018}}</ref> He joined [[Red Star Belgrade]] as a successor to [[Marko Grujić]] and player who usually plays on [[Midfielder#Central midfielder|central midfield]] position, being capable of playing both attacking and defensive role equally.<ref name="racici"/> Račić also played as a [[Defender (association football)#Centre-back|centre-back]] in a friendly during the [[Miodrag Božović]] era.<ref name="raciccb">{{cite web |url=http://mondo.rs/a919249/Sport/Fudbal/Crvena-zvezda-Radomlje-3-1-David-Babunski-dvostruki-strelac-i-asistent.html|title=Pobeda Zvezde za kraj: Babunski se istakao|website=mondo.rs|language=sr|date=8 August 2016|access-date=10 September 2016}}</ref> In August 2017, Račić was linked with [[Juventus F.C.|Juventus]], when he was also compared with [[Sergej Milinković-Savić]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://mondo.rs/a1035879/Sport/Fudbal/Uros-Racic-iz-Crvene-zvezde-u-Juventus-za-6-miliona-evra.html|title=Juventus bi Račića za 6.000.000, ali ne ovog leta?|website=mondo.rs|language=sr|date=27 August 2017|access-date=2 January 2018}}</ref> Račić emphasized [[Paul Pogba]] as his idol.<ref name="pogba"/>

{{quote box|width=33%|align=left|quote="I saw him on the [[Eternal Derby (Serbia)|Eternal Derby]]. He is very tall, young and right-footed [[midfielder]], a typical [[Balkan]] player with good ball control."|source=—[[Predrag Mijatović]], former Yugoslav international footballer, June 2018<ref name="mijatovic">{{cite news |url=https://www.levante-emv.com/valencia-cf/2018/06/12/mijatovic-racic-talento-verde-competir/1730432.html|title=Mijatovic: "Racic tiene talento, pero está verde para competir en la Liga"|work=[[Levante-EMV]]|location=Valencia|date=12 June 2018|access-date=12 June 2018}}</ref>}}
When it was announced about Račić's signing with [[Valencia CF|Valencia]], former player of the club, [[Predrag Mijatović]], described Račić in Spanish media. He said he watched him against his former club, [[FK Partizan|Partizan]] and mentioned Račić's physical characteristics. He also described his mode of play, but emphasized a difference in quality between the [[Serbian SuperLiga]] and [[La Liga]].<ref name="mijatovic"/>
