==Format==
The twelve teams that advanced from the [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification – AFC second round|second round]] (the seven group winners excluding Qatar and the five best group runners-up) were divided into two groups of six teams to play home-and-away [[round-robin tournament|round-robin]] matches. The top two teams of each group qualified for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]], and the two third-placed teams advanced to the [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification – AFC fourth round|fourth round]]. World Cup hosts [[Qatar national football team|Qatar]] won Group E in the second round (which meant they advanced to the [[2023 AFC Asian Cup]] finals) but were not required to qualify for the World Cup.

== Draw and seeding ==
The draw for the third round was held on 1 July 2021 at 15:00 [[Time in Malaysia|MST]] ([[UTC+08:00|UTC+8]]), in [[Kuala Lumpur]], Malaysia.<ref name="draw">{{cite web |url=https://www.goal.com/en/news/2022-world-cup-asian-qualifiers-draw-iran-south-korea-japan/ngdfkugdgta213jabrop9yc82|title=2022 World Cup Asian qualifiers Draw: When will be the third round matches played?|website=Goal|date=1 July 2021}}</ref>

The seeding for the draw was based on a [[FIFA Men's World Rankings#Special releases|special release]] of the [[FIFA Men's World Rankings]] for Asian teams on 18 June 2021 (shown in parentheses below).<ref name="ranking">{{cite web |url=https://www.fifa.com/tournaments/mens/worldcup/qatar2022/news/asia-world-cup-qualifying-whats-next|title=Asia World Cup qualifying: What's next?|publisher=FIFA|date=18 June 2021}}</ref>

Each group contained one team from each of the six pots.
