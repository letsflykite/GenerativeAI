'''Nahuel Molina Lucero''' (born 6 April 1998) is an Argentine professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[right-back|right-back, right-midfielder or right-winger]] for [[La Liga]] club [[Atlético Madrid]] and the [[Argentina national football team|Argentina national team]].<ref name="soccerway">{{Soccerway|432521|access-date=19 April 2022}}</ref> He was a member of the Argentina team that won the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].

==Club career==
Molina is a youth export from [[Boca Juniors]]. On 18 February 2016, he made his first team debut in a league game against [[San Martín de San Juan]]. He played the full game.<ref name="soccerway2">{{cite web|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2016/02/18/argentina/primera-division/ca-san-martin-de-san-juan/boca-juniors/2182932/|title=San Martín San Juan vs. Boca Juniors - 18 February 2016 - Soccerway|publisher=soccerway.com|accessdate=20 February 2016}}</ref>

===Defensa y Justicia===
In January 2018, Molina was loaned out to [[Defensa y Justicia]] for the rest of 2018.<ref>[https://es.besoccer.com/noticia/molina-deja-boca-por-defensa-y-justicia-359225 Molina deja Boca por Defensa y Justicia], besoccer.com, 11 January 2018</ref> The next season he was loaned to [[Rosario Central]] where he found the continuity he needed to stand out and ended up returning to [[Boca Juniors]] at the start of 2019.

===Udinese Calcio===
On 15 September 2020, Molina joined [[Udinese Calcio]] on a free transfer until 2025.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.football-italia.net/158582/official-molina-joins-udinese |title=OFFICIAL: Molina joins Udinese |website=Football Italia |date=15 September 2020 }}</ref>

===Atlético Madrid===
On 28 July 2022, Molina joined [[Atlético Madrid]] on a five-year deal,<ref>{{Cite web |title=Club Atlético de Madrid - Welcome, Nahuel Molina! |url=https://en.atleticodemadrid.com/noticias/molina-is-now-a-red-white-player |access-date=28 July 2022 |website=Club Atlético de Madrid |language=en}}</ref> for a reported fee of €10 million and €5 million in variables.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Egea |first=Pablo |date=28 July 2022 |title=Nahuel Molina es nuevo jugador del Atlético |trans-title=Nahuel Molina is a new Atletico player |url=https://www.marca.com/futbol/atletico/2022/07/28/62d1960b268e3efd1d8b45c3.html |access-date=28 July 2022 |website=MARCA |language=es}}</ref>

==International career==
Molina made his debut for the [[Argentina national football team|Argentina national team]] on 3 June 2021 in a [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification (CONMEBOL)|World Cup qualifier]] against [[Chile national football team|Chile]]. He substituted [[Juan Foyth]] in the 81st minute.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=ESPN|url=https://www.espn.com/soccer/lineups?gameId=561064|title=Argentina v Chile game report|date=3 June 2021}}</ref>

Molina scored his first goal for Argentina on 9 December 2022, in their [[2022 FIFA World Cup|2022 World Cup]] quarter-final match against the [[Netherlands national football team|Netherlands]]. He previously assisted  to [[Alexis Mac Allister]] during their group stage match vs [[Poland national football team|Poland]]. He started six matches for Argentina as first choice right back scoring one goal and providing one assist throughout the tournament en route to become champions.
