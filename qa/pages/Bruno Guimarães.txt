'''Bruno Guimarães Rodriguez Moura''' (born 16 November 1997) is a Brazilian professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Midfielder#Midfielder midfielder|central-midfielder or defensive-midfielder]] for {{English football updater|NewcastU}} club [[Newcastle United F.C.|Newcastle United]] and the [[Brazil national football team|Brazil national team]].

==Club career==
===Early career===
Guimarães' was born in the [[Imperial de São Cristóvão|São Cristóvão]] neighborhood of [[Rio de Janeiro]]. His father was a taxi driver and his mother sold auto parts for motorcycles. His grandfather immigrated from Spain to Brazil, and through him Guimarães has Spanish citizenship.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.tvazteca.com/aztecadeportes/hijo-taxista-convocado-brasil-qatar-2022-es2|title=Es hijo de un taxista y fue convocado por Brasil para Qatar 2022|date=8 November 2022|website=Azteca Deportes}}</ref> Guimarães made his debut with [[Grêmio Osasco Audax Esporte Clube|Audax]].{{Citation needed|date=January 2022}} on 9 April 2015, aged just 17, by playing the last three minutes in a 2–1 [[Campeonato Paulista]] away win against [[Clube Atlético Bragantino|Bragantino]].{{Citation needed|date=July 2022}}

Guimarães was promoted to the main squad in the 2017 campaign after impressing in the year's [[Copa São Paulo de Futebol Júnior]].{{Citation needed|date=January 2022}}

===Athletico Paranaense===
On 11 May 2017, he joined [[Club Athletico Paranaense|Athletico Paranaense]] on loan until April 2018 and was initially assigned to the under-23 squad.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://globoesporte.globo.com/pr/futebol/times/atletico-pr/noticia/atletico-pr-confirma-contratacao-de-volante-bruno-guimaraes-do-audax.ghtml|title=Atlético-PR confirma contratação de volante Bruno Guimarães, do Audax|trans-title=Atlético-PR confirm signing of defensive midfielder Bruno Guimarães, from Audax|publisher=[[Globo Esporte]]|language=pt|date=11 May 2017|accessdate=6 November 2018}}</ref>

Guimarães made his [[Campeonato Brasileiro Série A|Série A]] debut on 17 June 2017, coming on as a second-half [[Substitute (association football)|substitute]] for [[Deivid Willian da Silva|Deivid]] in a 1–0 away defeat of [[Atlético Clube Goianiense|Atlético Goianiense]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://esporte.uol.com.br/futebol/campeonatos/brasileiro/serie-a/ultimas-noticias/2017/06/17/atletico-go-x-atletico-pr.htm|title=Atlético-PR faz gol no início, segura Atlético-GO e sai da zona da degola|trans-title=Atlético-PR score in the beginning, hold on against Atlético-GO and leave the relegation zone|publisher=[[Universo Online|UOL Esporte]]|language=pt|date=17 June 2017|accessdate=6 November 2018}}</ref> The following 1 March, he was bought outright and signed a contract until 2021.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://globoesporte.globo.com/pr/futebol/times/atletico-pr/noticia/atletico-pr-chega-a-um-acordo-com-audax-sp-e-contrata-bruno-guimaraes-ate-2020.ghtml|title=Atlético-PR chega a acordo com Audax-SP e contrata Bruno Guimarães até 2021|trans-title=Atlético-PR reach agreement with Audax-SP and sign Bruno Guimarães until 2021|publisher=Globo Esporte|language=pt|date=1 March 2018|accessdate=6 November 2018}}</ref>

Guimarães scored his first senior goal on 10 March 2018, netting his team's fourth in a 7–1 home routing of [[Rio Branco Sport Club|Rio Branco-PR]], for the year's [[Campeonato Paranaense]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.espn.com.br/futebol/artigo/_/id/4068052/atletico-pr-faz-7-a-1-no-rio-branco-e-assume-lideranca-do-grupo-b|title=Atlético-PR faz 7 a 1 no Rio Branco e assume liderança do Grupo B|trans-title=Atlético-PR make 7–1 over Rio Branco and take the leadership og Group B|publisher=[[ESPN Brasil]]|language=pt|date=10 March 2018|accessdate=6 November 2018}}</ref> He then became an undisputed starter for the main squad under new manager [[Tiago Nunes]]. He renewed his contract until 2023 on 5 February 2019.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://globoesporte.globo.com/pr/futebol/times/athletico-pr/noticia/athletico-renova-contrato-com-o-volante-bruno-guimaraes-ate-2023.ghtml|title=Athletico renova contrato com o volante Bruno Guimarães até 2023|trans-title=Athletico renew contract with defensive midfielder Bruno Guimarães until 2023|publisher=Globo Esporte|language=pt-BR|date=5 February 2019|accessdate=19 September 2019}}</ref>

===Lyon===
On 29 January 2020, Guimarães signed with [[Ligue 1]] side [[Olympique Lyonnais|Lyon]] on a four-and-a-half-year contract. The transfer fee paid to Athletico Paranaense was reported as €20 million, who also secured a 20% sell-on clause.<ref>{{cite web|date=30 January 2020|title=Transferts : Bruno Guimaraes officiellement à l'OL|trans-title=Transfers : Bruno Guimaraes officially at OL|url=https://www.lequipe.fr/Football/Actualites/Transferts-bruno-guimaraes-officiellement-a-l-ol/1104252|publisher=[[L'Équipe]]|language=fr-FR|accessdate=30 January 2020}}</ref>

===Newcastle United===
On 30 January 2022, Guimarães joined [[Premier League]] club [[Newcastle United F.C.|Newcastle United]] on a four-and-a-half-year contract for a reported fee of up to £40m. He made his debut on 8 February as a late substitute in a 3–1 win against [[Everton F.C.|Everton]].<ref name="ncldebut">{{cite news |last1=Magowan |first1=Alistair |title=Newcastle beat fellow strugglers Everton |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/60210236 |publisher=BBC Sport|access-date=9 February 2022}}</ref> He scored his first goal for Newcastle with a "powerful back-heel volley" in a 2–1 victory at [[Southampton F.C.|Southampton]] on 10 March, on his full debut game.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Howarth |first1=Matthew |title=Southampton 1–2 Newcastle United |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/59792555 |publisher=BBC Sport |access-date=11 March 2022 |date=10 March 2022}}</ref> He scored his first brace for Newcastle in a 2–1 victory against [[Leicester City F.C.|Leicester City]] on 17 April, with his second coming in the 95th minute, winning the game.<ref>{{cite web |title=Newcastle United 2–1 Leicester City |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/61055887 |publisher=BBC Sport |access-date=17 April 2022 |date=17 April 2022}}</ref> On the 31 December 2022, Newcastle United held a moment of respect for the passing of the Brazilian footballer [[Pele]].<ref name=pele2022/> In respect to Pele, Guimarães wore the Brazilian national shirt.<ref name=pele2022> {{cite news | last = Bennett| first=Gordon | title = Bruno Guimaraes' touching tribute to Brazil legend Pele ahead of Newcastle United vs Leeds | newspaper = Chronicle| location = Newcastle | language = English | date = 31 December 2022| url = https://www.chroniclelive.co.uk/sport/football/football-news/pele-brazil-bruno-guimaraes-newcastle-25870551}}</ref>

==International career==
Guimarães was eligible to represent Spain before earning his senior cap for Brazil.<ref name="www.planetsport.com">{{cite web |title= Why are Newcastle United so desperate to sign Lyon midfielder Bruno Guimaraes?|url=https://www.planetsport.com/soccer/news/why-newcastle-desperate-to-sign-lyon-midfielder-bruno-guimaraes |publisher=Planet Sport|accessdate=30 January 2022|date=30 January 2022}}</ref>

Guimarães featured for [[Brazil national under-23 football team|Brazil's under-23 team]] in the [[2020 CONMEBOL Pre-Olympic Tournament]].<ref name="cbf.com.br">{{cite web |title=Tite convoca a Seleção Brasileira para a estreia nas Eliminatórias |url=https://www.cbf.com.br/selecao-brasileira/noticias/selecao-masculina/tite-convoca-a-selecao-brasileira-para-a-estreia-nas-eliminatorias |publisher=[[Brazilian Football Confederation|CBF]] |accessdate=18 September 2020 |date=18 September 2020 |language=Portuguese}}</ref> On 17 June 2021, he was named in the squad for the [[Football at the 2020 Summer Olympics – Men's tournament|2020 Summer Olympics]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Seleção Olímpica é convocada para os Jogos Olímpicos de Tóquio 2020 |url=https://www.cbf.com.br/selecao-brasileira/jogos/olimpicos-de-toquio-2020/selecao-olimpica-e-convocada-para-os-jogos-olimpicos-de-toquio |publisher=[[Brazilian Football Confederation|CBF]] |access-date=17 June 2021 |date=17 June 2021}}</ref>

In September 2020, Guimarães was called up to the senior [[Brazil national football team|Brazil]] squad for [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification (CONMEBOL)|2022 FIFA World Cup qualification]] matches against [[Bolivia national football team|Bolivia]] and [[Peru national football team|Peru]] on 9 and 13 October 2020, respectively.<ref name="cbf.com.br"/> He had already been named in the Brazilian team call-up in March 2020, but that summoning was postponed in response to the COVID-19 pandemic.{{Citation needed|date=January 2022}} He debuted in a 2–0 away win over [[Uruguay national football team|Uruguay]] on 17 November 2020.{{Citation needed|date=January 2022}}

On 7 November 2022, Guimarães was named in the squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Seleção Brasileira está convocada para a Copa do Mundo FIFA Qatar 2022|url=https://www.cbf.com.br/selecao-brasileira/noticias/selecao-masculina/selecao-brasileira-esta-convocada-para-a-copa-do-mundo-fifa-qatar-2022|publisher=[[Brazilian Football Confederation|CBF]]|access-date=7 November 2022|date=7 November 2022|language=pt}}</ref>
