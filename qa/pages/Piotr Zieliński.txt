'''Piotr Sebastian Zieliński''' ({{IPA-pl|ˈpjɔtr ʑɛˈlij̃skʲi|-|Pl-Piotr Zieliński.ogg}}; born 20 May 1994) is a Polish professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[midfielder]] for {{Italian football updater|Napoli}} club [[S.S.C. Napoli|Napoli]] and the [[Poland national football team|Poland national team]]. During his senior career he has also played for [[Udinese Calcio|Udinese]] and [[Empoli F.C.|Empoli]].

Zieliński was selected to represent Poland at [[UEFA Euro 2016]] in France, as well as the [[2018 FIFA World Cup]] in Russia, the pan-European [[UEFA Euro 2020]], and the [[2022 FIFA World Cup|2022 World Cup]] in Qatar. He has two brothers who are also professional footballers – [[Paweł Zieliński|Paweł]] and Tomasz Zieliński.

==Club career==
===Early career===
Zieliński started his career at his local club, [[:pl:Orzeł Ząbkowice Śląskie|Orzeł Ząbkowice Śląskie]], where he was coached by his father.<ref name="weszlo">{{cite web|title=Skąd się wziął w Udinese Piotr Zieliński?|trans-title=How did Zielinski came to Udinese?|url=http://weszlo.com/2013/04/18/skad-sie-wzial-w-udinese-piotr-zielinski/|publisher=Weszło!|access-date=17 May 2016|language=pl|date=18 April 2013}}</ref> At the age of 14, he joined [[Zagłębie Lubin]], even though he was wanted by [[Bayer 04 Leverkusen|Bayer Leverkusen]] and [[Liverpool F.C.|Liverpool]] and also had trials at [[Feyenoord]] and [[SC Heerenveen|Hereenveen]].<ref name="weszlo"/> He rose through the ranks and started training with the first team at the age of 15, under [[Franciszek Smuda]].<ref name="weszlo"/>

At 17 he moved abroad to join [[Udinese Calcio]] after they scouted him on international youth tournament.<ref name="weszlo"/>

===Udinese===
Zieliński made his debut in [[Serie A]] on 2 November 2012 when he replaced [[Antonio Di Natale]] in 91st minute of a match against [[Cagliari Calcio|Cagliari]].<ref name=debiut/>

====Empoli (loan)====
In 2014, Zieliński joined [[Empoli F.C.|Empoli]] on loan.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.tuttosport.com/news/calcio/calciomercato/2014/09/01-679177/empoli_dall_udinese_zielinski_in_prestito/ |title=Empoli, dall'Udinese Zielinski in prestito |work=www.tuttosport.com |language=it |date=1 September 2014 |access-date=27 September 2020 }}</ref>

===Napoli===
[[File:FC Salzburg gegen SSC Napoli (Championsleague 3. Runde 23. Oktober 2019) 65.jpg|left|thumb|230x230px|Piotr Zieliński (right) in a [[UEFA Champions League]] match against [[FC Red Bull Salzburg|Red Bull Salzburg]], 2019.]]
On 4 August 2016, after months of transfer speculation, Zieliński moved from Udinese to Napoli for a reported €16 million.<ref>{{cite web|title=Official: Zielinski joins Napoli|url=https://www.football-italia.net/88797/official-zielinski-joins-napoli|website=Football Italia|access-date=23 May 2018|date=4 August 2016}}</ref> He scored his first goal with Partenopei's shirt on 3 December, in a 3–0 home win against [[Inter Milan]];<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.repubblica.it/sport/calcio/serie-a/2016/12/02/news/pagelle_napoli_inter-153330215/ |title=Le pagelle di Napoli-Inter: Zielinski dominatore, Perisic non pervenuto |work=La Repubblica |language=it |date=2 December 2016 |access-date=27 September 2020 }}</ref> he ended his first season with six goals. On 31 August 2020 he signed new contract with Napoli until June 2023. On 31 October 2021, not only did he decide the derby between [[U.S. Salernitana 1919]] and Napoli, scoring the only goal of the game, but with 32 goals he also became the second best Polish goal-scorer in Serie A's history (behind [[Arkadiusz Milik]]), beating [[Zbigniew Boniek]]'s record.<ref>{{cite web| url = https://www.goal.pl/serie-a/gol-zielinskiego-polak-wyprzedzil-legende-wideo/ |language = pl |title=Gol Zielińskiego! Polak wyprzedził legendę [WIDEO] |date = 31 October 2021 | access-date = 20 December 2021}}</ref> On 17 February 2022, he scored a goal for Napoli in a [[2021–22 UEFA Europa League|Europa League]] match against [[FC Barcelona|Barcelona]] which ended in a 1–1 draw.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/60406561 |title=Barcelona 1 1 Napoli | access-date = 18 February 2022}}</ref> On 7 September 2022, he scored two goals and had one assist in a [[2022–23 UEFA Champions League|Champions League]] match against [[Liverpool F.C.]], which concluded in a 4–1 victory for Napoli.<ref>{{Cite news |url= https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/62815794 |title=Liverpool thrashed by Napoli in Champions League |work=BBC Sport |access-date=8 September 2022}}</ref>

On 9 June, Napoli confirmed that Zieliński would depart from the club following the conclusion of the 2022–23 season.{{cn|date=June 2023}}

==International career==
[[File:Zieliński Poland.jpg|thumb|Zieliński at the [[2018 FIFA World Cup]] playing against [[Japan national football team|Japan]].]]
On 4 June 2013, Zieliński made his senior international debut for the [[Poland national football team|Poland national team]] in a friendly match against [[Liechtenstein national football team|Liechtenstein]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Skromna wygrana na pożegnanie Dudka|url=http://www.90minut.pl/news/213/news2131345-Skromna-wygrana-na-pozegnanie-Dudka.html|publisher=90minut.pl|language=pl}}</ref>

In May 2018, he was named in Poland’s preliminary 35-man squad for the [[2018 FIFA World Cup|2018 World Cup]] in Russia.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.goal.com/en-gb/amp/news/revealed-every-world-cup-2018-squad-23-man-preliminary-lists/oa0atsduflsv1nsf6oqk576rb|title=Revealed: Every World Cup 2018 squad - Final 23-man lists|website=goal.com|publisher=Goal|date=4 June 2018|access-date=1 April 2020}}</ref>

==Personal life==
On 15 June 2019, Zieliński married Laura Słowiak<ref>{{cite web| url = https://sportowefakty.wp.pl/pilka-nozna/827707/kolejny-slub-polskiego-pilkarza-piotr-zielinski-powiedzial-tak-laurze-slowiak |title=Kolejny ślub polskiego piłkarza! Piotr Zieliński powiedział "tak" Laurze Słowiak |date=15 June 2019 | access-date = 20 December 2021}}</ref> with whom he has a son, Maksymilian (born 2021).<ref>{{cite web| url = https://sport.onet.pl/ofsajd/piotr-zielinski-zostal-ojcem-jego-zona-laura-urodzila-syna-jest-pierwsze-zdjecie/yjs55by |title=Piotr Zieliński został ojcem. Pokazał pierwsze zdjęcia dziecka | access-date = 20 December 2021}}</ref> In 2021, he passed an [[Italian language]] exam at the B2 level at the [[University of Naples]] in an effort to obtain Italian citizenship.<ref>{{cite web| url = https://sport.tvp.pl/52527920/piotr-zielinski-ubiega-sie-o-wloskie-obywatelstwo |language = pl |title=Piotr Zieliński ubiega się o włoskie obywatelstwo |date = 27 February 2021 | access-date = 20 December 2021}}</ref>
