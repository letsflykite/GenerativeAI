'''Shojae Khalilzadeh''' ({{lang-fa|شجاع خلیل‌زاده}}; born 14 May 1989) is an [[Iran|Iranian]] professional [[Football player|footballer]] who plays as a [[centre-back]] for [[Persian Gulf Pro League]] club [[Tractor S.C.|Tractor]] and [[Iran national football team|Iran national team]]. He is known for his pace, artistic tackles and positional sense.<ref name=":15" >[https://archive.ph/20220128001513/https://www.sofascore.com/player/shoja-khalilzadeh/223340 Shoja Khalilzadeh] at SofaScore</ref>

Khalilzadeh starting his career in [[Kerman Province|Kerman]] where he moved to two clubs, first joining [[Mes Rafsanjan F.C.|Mes Rafsanjan]] and then signing with [[Mes Kerman F.C.|Mes Kerman]] in 2010. His performance earned him a transfer to [[Sepahan S.C.|Sepahan]] [[Isfahan]] in 2013 and spent four years with ''Talaei Pooshan'', which included a successful loan spell with [[Tractor S.C.|Tractor]] in [[Tabriz]]. In May 2017, Khalilzadeh joined [[Persepolis F.C.|Persepolis]] side that he helped turn into one of the strongest on the continent, before joining Qatari side [[Al-Rayyan SC|Al-Rayyan]] in October 2020.<ref name=":5" /><ref name=":12" />

==Club career==
===Early years in Mazandaran===
At the age of 16, Khalilzadeh started playing football with local youth teams for three years.<ref name=":3">{{cite news|title=شجاع خلیل زاده سوپر استار جدید فوتبال ایران|url=http://amirkolaamirkola.blogfa.com/post/72|publisher=امیرکلا شهر من|language=fa|date=19 April 2013}}</ref><ref>[http://www.mohammadghorbanian.blogfa.com/post/203 Shojae Khalilzadeh with Ahmad Mehdizadeh in 2007]</ref><ref>[https://www.instagram.com/p/B67p4Ctni7j/ His coach Mohammad Ghorbanian and his friend the player Ahmad Mehdizadeh]</ref>

Khalilzadeh began his professional career with [[Mes Rafsanjan F.C.|Mes Rafsanjan]] in 2008. In his second season, under Croatian coach [[Miroslav Blažević]], he reached with the team the semi-finals of the [[2011–12 Hazfi Cup]].<ref name="Blažević" >{{cite web |url=http://www.goal.com/iran/news/1825/%D8%A7%DB%8C%D8%B1%D8%A7%D9%86/2011/12/20/2810742/%D8%AE%D9%84%DB%8C%D9%84-%D8%B2%D8%A7%D8%AF%D9%87%D8%B9%D8%B0%D8%A7%D8%A8-%DA%A9%D8%B4%DB%8C%D8%AF%D9%85-%D8%A7%D9%85%D8%A7-%D8%A8%D8%B1%D9%86%D8%AF%D9%87-%D8%B4%D8%AF%DB |title=خلیل زاده:عذاب کشیدم اما برنده شدیم |publisher=Goal |date=20 December 2011 |archive-url=https://archive.today/20130124163415/http://www.goal.com/iran/news/1825/%D8%A7%DB%8C%D8%B1%D8%A7%D9%86/2011/12/20/2810742/%D8%AE%D9%84%DB%8C%D9%84-%D8%B2%D8%A7%D8%AF%D9%87%D8%B9%D8%B0%D8%A7%D8%A8-%DA%A9%D8%B4%DB%8C%D8%AF%D9%85-%D8%A7%D9%85%D8%A7-%D8%A8%D8%B1%D9%86%D8%AF%D9%87-%D8%B4%D8%AF%DB |archive-date=24 January 2013 |url-status=dead}}</ref> In June 2012, Khalilzadeh extended his contract for a third season a worth [[Iranian rial|R]]800m. ===Sepahan and Tractor===
In late June 2013, Khalilzadeh was presented as a player of Isfahani club [[Sepahan F.C.|Sepahan]] on a four-year contract.<ref>{{cite web |title=شجاع خلیل‌زاده به سپاهان پیوست/ مدافع سابق مس در راه اصفهان |url=https://www.mehrnews.com/news/2083697/شجاع-خلیل-زاده-به-سپاهان-پیوست-مدافع-سابق-مس-در-راه-اصفهان |language=fa |date=25 June 2013}}</ref> In his first season, under Croatian coach [[Zlatko Kranjčar]], he formed a partnership in the heart of the Sepahan defensive with the [[Captain (association football)|captain]] [[Hadi Aghily]].<ref>{{cite web |title=شجاع خلیل زاده : بدان و درک کن که هميشه در قلب هواداران فوتبال ايران وخصوصا سپاهاني‌ها خواهي ماند. |url=https://lenz.varzesh3.com/content/80677}}</ref><ref>[https://lenz.varzesh3.com/content/1996 Khalilzadeh with his teammates in Sepahan]</ref> He helped the team to won of the [[2014–15 Persian Gulf Pro League]].

Khalilzadeh was loaned to [[Tractor S.C.|Tractor]] to complete his mandatory military service.<ref name="Veisi" /> He was one of the best players under coach [[Amir Ghalenoei]], the club reached the top of their group at the [[2016 AFC Champions League group stage#Group C|2016 AFC Champions League]], outperforming the Saudi giant [[Al Hilal SFC|Al-Hilal]]. He returned to Sepahan at the end of the loan spell in July 2016.<ref>{{cite web |title=قرارداد شجاع خلیل‌زاده با سپاهان بالاخره نهایی شد |url=https://www.tasnimnews.com/fa/news/1395/04/20/1126211/قرارداد-شجاع-خلیل-زاده-با-سپاهان-بالاخره-نهایی-شد |language=fa}}</ref>

Sepahan coach [[Abdollah Veisi]] talking about efforts to move Khalilzadeh to Persepolis in the 2016–17 season.<ref name=":7" >[https://medal1.com/ابراز-امیداوری-شجاع-خلیل-زاده-از-حضور-در-جام-جهانی-13445/ 2 سالی دنبال خریدش بود و بالاخره هم توانست او را جذب كند].</ref>

===Persepolis===

===Al-Rayyan===
On 25 October 2020, [[Al-Rayyan SC|Al-Rayyan]] announced on the last day of the summer [[transfer window]] the signing of Khalilzadeh on a two-year contract a worth $700,000.<ref>{{cite web|title=Shoja' Khalilzadeh signs two-year contract with Al Rayyan|url=https://www.the-afc.com/news/afcsection/shoja-khalilzadeh-signs-two-year-contract-with-al-rayyan|publisher=AFC|date=24 October 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=شجاع خلیل زاده رسماً تصمیمش برای فصل آینده را فاش کرد/ بازگشت به پرسپولیس؟|url=https://www.khabarvarzeshi.com/news/311880/شجاع-خلیل-زاده-رسما-تصمیمش-برای-فصل-آینده-را-فاش-کرد-بازگشت|publisher=خبر ورزشی|date=18 June 2021|language=fa}}</ref> It was his first career stop outside of his home country and became the second Iranian to play for Al-Rayyan since 1980s, after his former coach [[Ebrahim Ghasempour|Ghasempour]].<ref>{{cite web |title=قاسمپور سرمربی تیم فوتبال مس کرمان شد/ "بلاژویچ" مدیرفنی نارنجی پوشان |url=https://www.mehrnews.com/news/1534192/قاسمپور-سرمربی-تیم-فوتبال-مس-کرمان-شد-بلاژویچ-مدیرفنی-نارنجی |language=fa |date=14 February 2012}}</ref> Under the coach [[Diego Aguirre|Aguirre]], he played a [[central defender]], and [[Gabriel Mercado|Mercado]] moved to the [[right-back]] in the following games. He scored his first goal with a side [[Volley (association football)|volley]] shot in a 2–1 victory against [[Qatar SC]] on 12 January 2021.<ref>{{cite tweet|user=AlkassDigital|number=1349001953590841346|title=Khalilzadeh goal helps Al-Rayyan beat Qatar SC|date=12 January 2021}}</ref> The following week, Khalilzadeh scored his second goal with a counter-attack in a 3–0 win against [[Al-Wakrah SC|Al-Wakrah]].<ref>{{cite tweet|user=QSL|number=1351553926022262787|title=Khalilzadeh second goal Al-Wakrah vs Al-Rayyan|date=19 January 2021}}</ref> As a defender and [[deep-lying playmaker]] behind the midfield,<ref name="TFA">{{cite web |url=https://totalfootballanalysis.com/analysis/finding-the-best-centre-backs-in-asia-data-analysis-statistics |title=Finding the best centre-backs in Asia – data analysis |work=Total Football Analysis Magazine |author=Mario Husillos Jr. |date=16 October 2021}}</ref> he played 26 games and scored three goals. Al-Rayyan finished third in the league. In late October 2021, Khalilzadeh extended his contract with the club until 2023.<ref>{{cite web|title=خلیل‌زاده یک سال دیگر در الریان ماندنی شد|url=https://www.tasnimnews.com/fa/news/1400/08/05/2597131/خلیل-زاده-یک-سال-دیگر-در-الریان-ماندنی-شد|publisher=[[Tasnim News Agency]]|date=27 October 2021|language=fa}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=واکنش شجاع به تصمیمش برای فصل آینده {{!}} کنعانی داغ بود آن حرف‌ها را زد! {{!}} منتقدان تیم ملی خودشان در زمین بازی کنند! |url=https://www.hamshahrionline.ir/news/672652/واکنش-شجاع-به-تصمیمش-برای-فصل-آینده-کنعانی-داغ-بود-آن-حرف-ها |work=همشهری آنلاین |date=27 April 2022 |language=fa}}</ref>

==International career==
Khalilzadeh was invited to [[Iran national football team|Iran national team]] in 2012 by [[Carlos Queiroz]]. He made his debut for the ''Team Melli'' in a 6–1 victory against [[Tajikistan national football team|Tajikistan national team]].<ref>[https://globalsportsarchive.com/match/soccer/2012-11-06/iran-vs-tajikistan/1330513/ International friendly match]</ref>

==Personal life==
