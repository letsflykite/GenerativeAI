'''Szymon Piotr Żurkowski''' (born 25 September 1997) is a Polish professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[midfielder]] for Italian {{Italian football updater|Spezia}} club [[Spezia Calcio|Spezia]] on loan from [[ACF Fiorentina|Fiorentina]]. He represents the [[Poland national football team|Poland national team]].

==Club career==
Żurkowski started his career with [[Gwarek Zabrze]].<ref>[https://weszlo.com/2017/10/19/zawsze-moglem-biegac-wiecej-niz-inni-165-km-sandecja-zrobilbym-jeszcze-polowke/ Zawsze mogłem biegać więcej niż inni. 16,5 km z Sandecją? Zrobiłbym jeszcze połówkę!] weszlo.com</ref>

In 2016 Żurkowski signed for [[Górnik Zabrze]].

On 28 January 2019, Żurkowski signed for [[ACF Fiorentina|Fiorentina]] a contract until 30 June 2023.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://twitter.com/acffiorentina/status/1089902381612056576|title=Szymon Zurkowski è viola Tutti i dettagli #NoiSiamoFirenze #InsiemeSiamoPiùForti|last=Fiorentina|first=A. C. F.|date=28 January 2019|website=@acffiorentina|language=it|access-date=28 January 2019}}</ref> He stayed at Górnik Zabrze on loan for the remainder of the 2018–19 season.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://m.tuttomercatoweb.com/serie-a/ufficiale-fiorentina-preso-szymon-zurkowski-dal-gornik-zabrze-1207472|access-date=28 January 2019|title=UFFICIALE: Fiorentina, preso Szymon Zurkowski dal Gornik Zabrze|language=Italian}}</ref>

On 30 January 2020, he joined [[Empoli F.C.|Empoli]] on loan.<ref>{{cite press release|publisher=[[Empoli F.C.|Empoli]]|url=https://empolifc.com/szymon-zurkowski-e-un-nuovo-calciatore-dellempoli/|title=Szymon Żurkowski è un nuovo calciatore dell'Empoli|date=30 January 2020|language=Italian}}</ref> On 1 September 2020, he return on loan to [[Empoli F.C.|Empoli]] until 30 June 2021.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://m.tuttomercatoweb.com/serie-b/ufficiale-empoli-presi-zurkowski-e-terzic-in-prestito-dalla-fiorentina-1427303|title=UFFICIALE: Empoli, presi Zurkowski e Terzic in prestito dalla Fiorentina}}</ref>

On 12 January 2023, Żurkowski joined [[Spezia Calcio|Spezia]] on loan, with an obligation to buy.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=Spezia|url=https://www.acspezia.com/en/news/official-szymon-zurkowski-is-a-new-spezia-player.24354.html|title=SZYMON ZURKOWSKI IS A NEW SPEZIA PLAYER|date=12 January 2023|access-date=17 January 2023}}</ref>

==International career==
===Under 21 team===
During the [[2019 UEFA European Under-21 Championship qualification]], from September 2017 to March 2018, Żurkowski played five of the [[Poland national under-21 football team|Polish U21]]'s six group games, scoring one goal. Poland sit top of [[2019 UEFA European Under-21 Championship qualification Group 3|Group 3]] with four games to follow.

===Senior team===
In May 2018, Żurkowski was named in the 35-man provisional squad for [[Poland national football team|Poland]] for the [[2018 FIFA World Cup]], however, was not included in the [[2018 FIFA World Cup squads#Poland|23-man squad]].<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.pzpn.pl/reprezentacje/reprezentacja-a/aktualnosci/2018-05-11/szeroki-sklad-reprezentacji-polski-na-mistrzostwa-swiata-2018|title=Szeroki skład rerpezentacji Polski na mistrzostwa świata 2018|newspaper=[[Polish Football Association|PZPN]]|date=11 May 2018|language=Polish }}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.thescore.com/ita_fed/news/1545594|title=Lewandowski, Serie A contingent lead 35-man provisional Poland squad|newspaper=[[theScore Inc.|The Score]]|date=May 2018 }}</ref>

Żurkowski debuted on 24 March 2022 for Poland in a friendly match as a starter against [[Scotland national football team|Scotland]].
