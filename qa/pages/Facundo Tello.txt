'''Facundo Raúl Tello Figueroa''' (born on 4 May 1982) is an Argentine [[soccer|football]] referee who is a listed international referee for [[FIFA]] since 2019.<ref>{{cite web|date=2021|title=FIFA list of referees|url=https://digitalhub.fifa.com/m/73018d1c158c1fd5/original/2021-FIFA-Refereeing-International-Lists.pdf|publisher=resources.fifa.com|accessdate=30 January 2021}}</ref> He has been officiating the highest division of Argentine football league since 2013.<ref>{{cite web|title=Debuta el árbitro Facundo Tello que pasó por Zona Sur|url=http://manifiestodefutbol.com/debuta-el-arbitro-facundo-tello-que-paso-por-zona-sur/|publisher=manifiestodelfutbol.com|date=15 May 2013|access-date=23 January 2018}}</ref> On 19 May 2022 FIFA announced the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]]'s referee list, where Tello was chosen.

==Biography==
Facundo Tello was born in [[Bahía Blanca]], in the [[Buenos Aires Province]] on 4 May 1982.

==Career==
Tello debuted in the highest category of Argentine football with only four matches in the second division, where he officiated the [[Godoy Cruz Antonio Tomba|Godoy Cruz]] and [[Club Atlético Vélez Sarsfield|Vélez Sarsfield]] match, on the fourteenth date of the 2013 Final Tournament.<ref>{{cite web|title=El árbitro bahiense Facundo Tello debutará en Primera|url=https://www.lanueva.com/nota/2013-5-14-19-35-0-el-arbitro-bahiense-facundo-tello-debutara-en-primera|publisher=La Nueva|date=14 May 2013|access-date=22 August 2018}}</ref>

Tello, who belongs to the Bahiense Association of Referees, made his first steps in tournaments of the Argentine Soccer Association from 2011, when he debuted in the then Argentine Tournament A.<ref>{{cite web|title=El bahiense Facundo Tello debutará arbitrando Argentino A|url=https://www.lanueva.com/nota/2011-1-19-16-46-0-el-bahiense-facundo-tello-debutara-arbitrando-argentino-a|publisher=La Nueva|date=19 January 2011|access-date=22 August 2018}}</ref>

As he was settled in the first division, Tello officiated the first summer superclassic of 2018, with the Millionaires victory 1–0.<ref>{{cite web|title=Facundo Tello será el árbitro del Superclásico en Mar del Plata|url=https://www.clarin.com/deportes/futbol/facundo-tello-arbitro-superclasico-mar-plata_0_BkiZFSRmz.html|publisher=Clarín|date=6 January 2018|access-date=22 August 2018}}</ref> That same year, he was summoned to officiate in the return final that defined the second promotion to the 2018-2019 Super League, between [[San Martín de Tucumán]] and [[Club Atlético Sarmiento|Sarmiento]] with a 5–1 victory for San Martín de Tucumán.<ref>{{cite web|title=Facundo Tello será el árbitro de la finalísima entre San Martín y Sarmiento|url=http://oncegol.com.ar/2018/05/31/nacional-b-facundo-tello-sera-el-arbitro-de-la-finalisima-entre-san-martin-y-sarmiento/|publisher=oncegol.com.ar|date=31 May 2018|access-date=22 August 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180822223908/http://oncegol.com.ar/2018/05/31/nacional-b-facundo-tello-sera-el-arbitro-de-la-finalisima-entre-san-martin-y-sarmiento/|archive-date=22 August 2018}}</ref>

In 2019, Tello entered the roster of international referees for FIFA as he was appointed by Conmebol as a 4th official for the South American U-20 Championship in [[Chile]] along with his colleagues Fernando Rapallini, Ezequiel Brailosky and Gabriel Chade.,<ref name="sudamericano">{{cite web|title=Facundo Tello se convirtió en árbitro internacional|url=https://viapais.com.ar/bahia-blanca/786686-facundo-tello-se-convirtio-en-arbitro-internacional/|publisher=Vía Bahía Blanca|date=2 January 2019|access-date=26 January 2019}}</ref> becoming the first Bahia collegiate to achieve this distinction.<ref name="sudamericano"/>

He was one of the appointed referees to officiate the [[2021 FIFA Arab Cup]],<ref>{{cite web|title=List of appointed Match Officials|url=https://digitalhub.fifa.com/m/c692b0b51863b43/original/FIFA-REFEREES-LIST-Fifa-Arab-Cup-2021-QAT_081021.pdf|website=FIFA.com|publisher=Fédération Internationale de Football Association}}</ref> as he was summoned to officiate the match between [[Jordan national football team|Jordan]] and [[Morocco national football team|Morocco]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=FIFA Arab Cup 2021™: Jordan - Morocco|url=https://www.fifa.com/tournaments/mens/arabcup/arabcup2021/match-center/400175818|access-date=5 December 2021|website=www.fifa.com|language=en}}</ref> On 6 November 2022, Tello refereed Argentina's Champions Trophy final between [[Boca Juniors]] and [[Racing Club de Avellaneda|Racing Club]], where he brandished ten red cards (seven red cards to Boca and three to Racing).<ref> {{Cite web|title=World Cup referee Facundo Tello dishes out 10 red cards in Argentina's Champions Trophy final|url=https://news.sky.com/story/world-cup-referee-facundo-tello-dishes-out-10-red-cards-in-argentinas-champions-trophy-final-12740823|access-date=7 November 2022|website=www.news.sky.com|language=en}}</ref>
