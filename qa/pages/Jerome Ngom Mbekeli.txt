'''Jerome Ngom Mbekeli''' (born 30 September 1998) is a [[Cameroon]]ian footballer who plays as a [[midfielder]] for [[FC Sheriff Tiraspol|Sheriff Tiraspol]] in the [[Moldovan Super Liga]].

==Career==
===Swope Park Rangers===
In January 2019, Mbekeli joined [[USL Championship]] club [[Swope Park Rangers]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sportingkc.com/news/swope-park-rangers-adds-internationals-ayyoub-allach-and-jerome-ngom-mbekeli|title=Swope Park Rangers adds internationals Ayyoub Allach and Jerome Ngom Mbekeli|first=Patrik|last=Bergabo|publisher=Sporting Kansas City|date=4 January 2019|accessdate=25 November 2022}}</ref> After scoring twice against [[Bethlehem Steel F.C. (2016)|Bethlehem Steel]], he was named to the USL Championship Team of the Week.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sportingkc.com/post/2019/04/09/jerome-ngom-mbekeli-nets-usl-championship-team-week-honors|title=Jerome Ngom Mbekeli nets USL Championship Team of the Week honors &#124; Sporting Kansas City|first=Sam|last=Kozvan|publisher=Sportingkc.com|date=9 April 2019|accessdate=20 March 2020}}</ref>

===Sheriff Tiraspol===
On 5 July 2023, [[FC Sheriff Tiraspol|Sheriff Tiraspol]] announced the singing of Ngom Mbekeli from [[S.K. Beveren|Beveren]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Добро пожаловать, Жером |url=http://www.fc-sheriff.com/ru/vse/dobro-pozhalovat-zherom/ |website=fc-sheriff.com |publisher=FC Sheriff Tiraspol |access-date=5 July 2023 |language=Russian |date=5 July 2023}}</ref>

===International===
Mbekeli was named to [[Cameroon national football team|Cameroon]]'s roster for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://sportnewsafrica.com/en/all-african-countries/qatar-2022-indomitable-lions-newboy-jerome-ngom-oozes-confidence/|title=Qatar 2022: Indomitable Lions newboy Jerome Ngom oozes confidence|work=Sport News Africa|date=18 November 2022|accessdate=25 November 2022}}</ref> He was one of the few amateur players at the tournament, making approximately the minimum wage in his home nation of Cameroon.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.volkskrant.nl/sport/voor-jerome-ngom-mbekeli-uit-kameroen-is-de-wk-selectie-een-lot-uit-de-loterij~b08db93a/|title=Voor Jerome Ngom Mbekeli uit Kameroen is de WK-selectie een lot uit de loterij|trans-title=For Jerome Ngom Mbekeli from Cameroon, the World Cup selection is a lottery ticket|language=Dutch|first=Bart|last=Vlietstra|work=de Volkskrant|date=18 November 2022|accessdate=25 November 2022}}</ref>
