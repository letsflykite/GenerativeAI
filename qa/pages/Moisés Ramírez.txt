'''Wellington Moisés Ramírez Preciado''' (born 9 September 2000) is an Ecuadorian [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Goalkeeper (association football)|goalkeeper]] for [[C.S.D. Independiente del Valle|Independiente del Valle]].

==Club career==
Born in [[Guayaquil]], Ramírez joined [[C.S.D. Independiente del Valle]]'s youth setup in 2016, from Toreros FC. He made his first team debut at the age of just 17 on 10 July 2018, starting in a 1–3 away loss against [[S.D. Aucas]].

On 30 January 2019, Ramírez moved abroad and joined [[Real Sociedad]] on loan until June; he was initially assigned to [[Real Sociedad B|the reserves]] in [[Segunda División B]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.realsociedad.eus/es/noticias/detalle/refuerzo-para-la-porteria-del-sanse|title=Refuerzo para la portería del Sanse|trans-title=Addition to the goal of ''Sanse''|publisher=Real Sociedad|language=es|date=30 January 2019|accessdate=20 September 2020}}</ref> His loan was extended until 31 December on 28 June, with a buyout clause,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.realsociedad.eus/es/noticias/detalle/ampliada-su-cesion|title=Ampliada su cesión|trans-title=Loan extended|publisher=Real Sociedad|language=es|date=28 June 2019|accessdate=20 September 2020}}</ref> which was declined on 10 December.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.realsociedad.eus/en/news/details/real-decline-ramirez-buy-out-option|title=Real decline Ramírez buy-out option|publisher=Real Sociedad|date=10 December 2019|accessdate=20 September 2020}}</ref>

Upon returning, Ramírez was assigned back at the main squad, being mainly a backup to [[Jorge Pinos]].

==International career==
He made his debut for the [[Ecuador national football team]] on 7 October 2021 in a [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification (CONMEBOL)|World Cup qualifier]] against [[Bolivia national football team|Bolivia]].<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[FIFA]]|url=https://www.fifa.com/tournaments/mens/worldcup/qatar2022/qualifiers/conmebol/match-center/400104609|title=Ecuador v Bolivia game report|date=7 October 2021}}</ref>
