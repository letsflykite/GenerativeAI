'''Arthur Nicolas Theate''' (born 25 May 2000) is a Belgian professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Defender (association football)|defender]] for {{French football updater|Rennes}} club [[Stade Rennais F.C.|Rennes]] and the [[Belgium national football team|Belgium national team]].

==Club career==
===Early years===
Theate played youth football for Alliance Melen-Micheroux, [[K.A.S. Eupen|Eupen]], [[K.R.C. Genk|Genk]] and [[Standard Liège]].<ref>{{cite news |title=Les 3 générations de footeux |url=https://www.laprovince.be/294099/article/2018-10-16/les-3-generations-de-footeux |access-date=15 August 2022 |work=La Province |date=16 October 2018 |language=fr}}</ref> Until reaching the under-14 level, he played as a [[striker (association football)|striker]] at Eupen. At Genk, he was repositioned as a [[left winger]] after the arrival of a new player, after which he moved to the position of [[left-back]] and then [[centre-back]].<ref>{{cite news |last1=Gautier |first1=Guillaume |title=Arthur Theate (KV Oostende): 'Bij Standard en Genk heb ik nooit echt een uitleg gekregen' |url=https://sportmagazine.knack.be/sport/voetbal-nationaal/arthur-theate-kv-oostende-bij-standard-en-genk-heb-ik-nooit-echt-een-uitleg-gekregen/article-interview-1655607.html?cookie_check=1660595867 |access-date=15 August 2022 |work=Sport/Voetbalmagazine |date=20 October 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210301145101/https://sportmagazine.knack.be/sport/voetbal-nationaal/arthur-theate-kv-oostende-bij-standard-en-genk-heb-ik-nooit-echt-een-uitleg-gekregen/article-interview-1655607.html |archive-date=1 March 2021 |language=nl}}</ref> During his time at the Genk youth academy, his future at times looked uncertain, and he was described as being physically tough, but not always refined.<ref name="arthur">{{cite news |last1=Geril |first1=Jürgen |title=Het sprookje van 'Koning' Arthur Theate: vorig jaar afgeschreven als profvoetballer, nu Rode Duivel |url=https://www.nieuwsblad.be/cnt/dmf20211109_96678402 |access-date=15 August 2022 |work=Het Nieuwsblad |date=10 November 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211220123928/https://www.nieuwsblad.be/cnt/dmf20211109_96678402 |archive-date=20 December 2021 |language=nl-BE}}</ref>

After three years playing for the Genk youth teams, he joined Standard Liège where he also struggled with regular [[midfielder]] [[Damjan Pavlović]] being played as left-back in the youth team to replace Theate.<ref name="arthur" /> He soon returned to Genk, where he trained with the first team during the [[2019–20 K.R.C. Genk season|2019–20]] pre-season under head coach [[Felice Mazzu]]. After this, however, Theate failed to make the cut and was demoted to the reserves. He returned to Standard in 2019, but barely a year after his return, Standard let him go again.<ref name="arthur" />

===Oostende===
In the summer of 2020, Theate saw no prospects of succeeding at Standard Liège, after which he was offered a trial with [[Belgian First Division A]] club [[K.V. Oostende|Oostende]]. After a week of practicing with the first team, he was able to convince German coach [[Alexander Blessin]] of his abilities. Blessin mainly noticed his explosiveness and power, ideal for his high five-man backline and ''[[Association football tactics#"Forward" versus "collapsing" defences|Gegenpressing]]'' tactics. Previously, Theate had not been able to convince [[Lommel S.K.|Lommel]] or [[Lierse Kempenzonen]] during trials.<ref name="arthur" />

In July 2020, he signed a three-year contract with an option with Oostende. Theate made his debut on 10 August 2020 in the Belgian First Division A against [[K Beerschot VA|Beerschot]], featuring in starting line-up.<ref>{{cite news |title=Oostende haalt jonge verdediger Arthur Theate in huis |url=https://sportmagazine.knack.be/sport/voetbal-nationaal/oostende-haalt-jonge-verdediger-arthur-theate-in-huis/article-news-1619755.html |access-date=15 August 2022 |work=Sport/Voetbalmagazine |date=13 July 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210117042944/https://sportmagazine.knack.be/sport/voetbal-nationaal/oostende-haalt-jonge-verdediger-arthur-theate-in-huis/article-news-1619755.html |archive-date=17 January 2021 |language=nl}}</ref> Together with [[Anton Tanghe]] he grew into the permanent centre-back duo in the Oostende defence. On 4 October 2020, he scored his first professional goal, heading home a cross against [[Royal Excel Mouscron]] putting his team in a good position ahead of their 3–0 victory.<ref>{{cite news |title=Oostende rekent thuis makkelijk af met Moeskroen en nestelt zich in de linkerkolom |url=https://www.hln.be/jupiler-pro-league/oostende-rekent-thuis-makkelijk-af-met-moeskroen-en-nestelt-zich-in-de-linkerkolom~add1d470/ |access-date=15 August 2022 |work=Het Laatste Nieuws |date=4 October 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210629021747/https://www.hln.be/jupiler-pro-league/oostende-rekent-thuis-makkelijk-af-met-moeskroen-en-nestelt-zich-in-de-linkerkolom~add1d470/ |archive-date=29 June 2021 |language=nl}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Het wonder van Arthur Theate: weggestuurd bij Genk en Standard, afgewezen door derdeklassers, nu Rode Duivel |url=https://www.nieuwsblad.be/cnt/dmf20211006_97557056 |access-date=15 August 2022 |work=Het Nieuwsblad |date=7 October 2021 |language=nl-BE}}</ref>

During the winter transfer period of the 2020–21 season, Oostende received an offer for Theate of €5 million from Italian [[Serie A]] club [[Bologna F.C. 1909|Bologna]], but the Belgian coastal club refused.<ref>{{cite news |title=KV Oostende weigerde Italiaans bod |url=https://www.nieuwsblad.be/cnt/dmf20210201_98147286 |access-date=15 August 2022 |work=Het Nieuwsblad |date=2 February 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210513201929/https://www.nieuwsblad.be/cnt/dmf20210201_98147286 |archive-date=13 May 2021 |language=nl-BE}}</ref> In his first season at Oostende, he managed to qualify for European play-offs, but eventually missed out. He missed only one game due to suspension and four matches due to injury, playing the entirety of the other 27 matches.

In the 2021–22 season, Theate did not make an appearance during the first two matchdays pending a possible transfer.<ref>{{cite news |title=KV Oostende vangt jackpot voor Arthur Theate, verdediger komt vanavond niet meer in actie |url=https://www.nieuwsblad.be/cnt/dmf20210730_95178724 |access-date=15 August 2022 |work=Het Nieuwsblad |date=30 July 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211103040941/https://www.nieuwsblad.be/cnt/dmf20210730_95178724 |archive-date=3 November 2021 |language=nl-BE}}</ref> On the third matchday of the season, he returned to the starting line-up against [[K.A.A. Gent|Gent]].<ref>{{cite news |last1=Van Assche |first1=Timmy |title=Arthur Theate na overwinning tegen AA Gent en eerste clean sheet: Bij KVO vormen we één familie |url=https://www.hln.be/sport-in-de-buurt/arthur-theate-na-overwinning-tegen-aa-gent-en-eerste-clean-sheet-bij-kvo-vormen-we-een-familie~a3f3fb4d/ |access-date=15 August 2022 |work=Het Laatste Nieuws |date=9 August 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211028201504/https://www.hln.be/sport-in-de-buurt/arthur-theate-na-overwinning-tegen-aa-gent-en-eerste-clean-sheet-bij-kvo-vormen-we-een-familie~a3f3fb4d/ |archive-date=28 October 2021 |language=nl-BE}}</ref> On 22 August 2021, he picked up a red card in his last game for Oostende against his former club Standard.<ref>{{cite news |title=Standard laat Sclessin intens vieren: Rouches kloppen KV Oostende en gaan voorlopig op kop |url=https://www.hln.be/sport/standard-laat-sclessin-intens-vieren-rouches-kloppen-kv-oostende-en-gaan-voorlopig-op-kop~aeb063cb/ |access-date=15 August 2022 |work=Het Laatste Nieuws |date=20 August 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211030090034/https://www.hln.be/sport/standard-laat-sclessin-intens-vieren-rouches-kloppen-kv-oostende-en-gaan-voorlopig-op-kop~aeb063cb/ |archive-date=30 October 2021 |language=nl-BE}}</ref> Theate played a total of 40 official matches for Oostende, in which he scored five goals.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Van den Broeck |first1=David |last2=Verbeeke |first2=Junior |last3=Van Genechten |first3=Vincent |title=Amper negen maanden prof, maar Arthur Theate al in het vizier van bondscoach Martinez: "Dat begrijp ik best" |url=https://www.nieuwsblad.be/cnt/dmf20210318_93688935 |work=Het Nieuwsblad |date=18 March 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210514171306/https://www.nieuwsblad.be/cnt/dmf20210318_93688935 |archive-date=14 May 2021 |language=nl-BE}}</ref>

===Bologna===
On 26 August 2021, Theate signed with [[Serie A]] club [[Bologna F.C. 1909|Bologna]] on initial season-long loan, with a subsequent obligation to buy.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=Bologna|url=https://www.bolognafc.it/en/arthur-theate-joins-bologna/|title=Arthur Theate joins Bologna|date=26 August 2021|access-date=2 July 2022}}</ref>

On 18 September 2021, Theate made his Bologna debut against defending champions [[Inter Milan]] on the fourth matchday of the season. Theate came on as a substitute in the 74th minute behind 6–0 and headed in the 6–1 final score twelve minutes later.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Heylen |first1=Jens |title=Bologna en scorende Theate lopen tegen oplawaai aan bij landskampioen Inter |url=https://www.nieuwsblad.be/cnt/dmf20210918_96933286 |access-date=19 November 2022 |work=Het Nieuwsblad |date=18 September 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211102214736/https://www.nieuwsblad.be/cnt/dmf20210918_96933286 |archive-date=2 November 2021 |language=nl-BE}}</ref> Bologna had started the season with a four-man defense, but after conceding twelve goals in three games, coach [[Siniša Mihajlović]] started the game against [[S.S. Lazio|Lazio]] on 3 October with five at the back with Theate as left-sided centre-back. This was also his first start for the club. Theate contributed with an assist and a goal in the 3–0 win.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Conings |first1=Wim |title=Straf: Belgische verdediger Theate (21) is bij eerste basisplek meteen goed voor goal én assist tegen Lazio |url=https://www.nieuwsblad.be/cnt/dmf20211003_94170087 |access-date=19 November 2022 |work=Het Nieuwsblad |date=3 October 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211003152808/https://www.nieuwsblad.be/cnt/dmf20211003_94170087 |archive-date=3 October 2021 |language=nl-BE}}</ref>

Theate impressed during his season at Bologna, making 31 league appearances in which he scored twice and provided one assist.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Hauspie |first1=Jan |title=Arthur Theate: Ik ben ongelooflijk gelukkig geweest in Oostende |url=https://www.demorgen.be/sport/arthur-theate-ik-ben-ongelooflijk-gelukkig-geweest-in-oostende~bff30a99/ |access-date=19 November 2022 |work=De Morgen |date=15 June 2022 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220703074821/https://www.demorgen.be/sport/arthur-theate-ik-ben-ongelooflijk-gelukkig-geweest-in-oostende~bff30a99/ |archive-date=3 July 2022 |language=nl-BE}}</ref>

==International career==
Theate received his first call-up to the [[Belgian national football team|senior Belgium squad]] for the [[2021 UEFA Nations League Finals]].<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/58864343 |title=Italy 2-1 Belgium |publisher=BBC |date=10 October 2021 |access-date=11 October 2021}}</ref> He made his debut on 16 November 2021 in a [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification – UEFA Group E|World Cup qualifier]] against [[Wales national football team|Wales]].<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[FIFA]]|url=https://www.fifa.com/tournaments/mens/worldcup/qatar2022/qualifiers/uefa/match-center/400155643|title=Wales v Belgium game report|date=16 November 2021|accessdate=30 November 2021}}</ref>

He was selected for national coach [[Roberto Martínez]]'s squad ahead of the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]],<ref>{{cite news |last1=Van Damme |first1=Michael |title=VIDEO. Arthur Theate volgt WK-selectie op massagetafel, Rennes-ploegmaats dolblij voor hem en Jérémy Doku |url=https://www.nieuwsblad.be/cnt/dmf20221110_95082050 |access-date=19 January 2023 |work=Het Nieuwsblad |date=10 November 2022 |language=nl-BE}}</ref> but failed to make an appearance as the ''Red Devils'' were knocked out of the tournament at the group stages for the first time since [[1998 FIFA World Cup|1998]].<ref>{{cite news |last1=Morse |first1=Ben |title=Belgium knocked out of World Cup after goalless draw with Croatia as head coach Roberto Martinez steps down |url=https://edition.cnn.com/2022/12/01/football/belgium-croatia-group-f-world-cup-spt-intl |access-date=19 January 2023 |work=CNN |date=1 December 2022}}</ref>
