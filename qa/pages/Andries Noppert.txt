'''Andries Noppert''' (born 7 April 1994) is a Dutch professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Goalkeeper (association football)|goalkeeper]] for [[Eredivisie]] club [[SC Heerenveen|Heerenveen]] and the [[Netherlands national football team|Netherlands national team]].<ref name = "SW">{{soccerway|andries-noppert/300862|access-date=3 February 2018}}</ref>

==Club career==
=== NAC Breda ===
Trained through the youth ranks of [[SC Heerenveen|Heerenveen]], Noppert made his professional debut in 2014 for [[NAC Breda]].<ref name = "WF"/>

=== Foggia ===
In January 2018, he was signed by Italian club Foggia.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.immediato.net/2018/01/30/un-gigante-per-il-foggia-arriva-il-portiere-andries-noppert-23-anni-e-oltre-2-metri-di-altezza/|title=Un gigante per il Foggia, arriva il portiere Andries Noppert: 23 anni e oltre 2 metri di altezza|date=30 January 2018|publisher=Immediato.net|language=it}}</ref> After traveling by car from the Netherlands to Italy, his car was stolen by the [[Foggia]]n [[Mafia]].<ref name="NOS">{{Cite web |url=https://nos.nl/artikel/2453672-de-foggia-episode-van-oranje-keeper-noppert-hij-rookte-als-een-schoorsteen |title=De Foggia-episode van Oranje-keeper Noppert: 'Hij rookte als een schoorsteen' |publisher=NOS |date=24 November 2022 |language=nl |access-date=24 November 2022}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.trouw.nl/sport/de-wonderlijke-carriere-van-keeper-andries-noppert-voor-mij-is-dit-ook-bizar~b02eb6c0/ |title=De wonderlijke carrière van keeper Andries Noppert |publisher=Trouw |date=20 November 2022 |language=nl |access-date=30 November 2022}}</ref> He made his debut for the club on 28 April, in a 3–1 loss to Cittadella.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://it.soccerway.com/matches/2018/04/28/italy/serie-b/as-cittadella/foggia-calcio/2560442/|title=Cittadella vs. Foggia 3 - 1|publisher=soccerway|date=|language=it|access-date=21 November 2022}}</ref> Three months later, the goalkeeper stopped a [[Penalty kick (association football)|penalty]] in the game against [[A.C. Carpi|Carpi]], which ended with a score of 2-0 for Foggia.<ref name="NOS" /> Over the course of a season and a half, he played 8 matches in [[Serie B]].<ref name="NOS" /> During his stay in Italy Noppert received the nickname ''Il Grattacielo'' ("The [[Skyscraper]]" in Italian) from the country's media due to his height.<ref name="NOS" />

=== Dordrecht ===
On 12 September 2019, Noppert signed with [[FC Dordrecht|Dordrecht]].<ref>{{cite press release|publisher=[[FC Dordrecht|Dordrecht]]|url=https://www.fcdordrecht.nl/2019/09/12/noppert-extra-dordtse-doelman/|title=Noppert extra Dordtse doelman|date=12 September 2019|language=Dutch}}</ref> After leaving the club he considered joining the police.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2022/12/02/sports/world-cup/usa-netherlands-goalkeepers.html|title=The Giant World Cup Rookie and an Enduring Dutch Mystery|first=Rory|last=Smith|work=The New York Times |date=2 December 2022|via=NYTimes.com}}</ref>

=== Go Ahead Eagles ===
In January 2021, Noppert signed with [[Go Ahead Eagles]] until the end of the season.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.ga-eagles.nl/andries-noppert-fantastisch-om-weer-met-team-op-veld-te-staan/|title=Andries Noppert: 'Fantastisch om weer met team op veld te staan'|date=22 January 2021}}</ref>

=== Heerenveen ===
Noppert returned to Heerenveen on 16 May 2022, signing a two-year deal.<ref>{{cite web |title=sc Heerenveen verwelkomt Noppert terug in Fryslân |url=https://www.sc-heerenveen.nl/nieuws/2022/sc-heerenveen-verwelkomt-noppert-terug-in-fryslan |website=sc Heerenveen |access-date=1 July 2022 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220516094643/https://www.sc-heerenveen.nl/nieuws/2022/sc-heerenveen-verwelkomt-noppert-terug-in-fryslan |archive-date=16 May 2022 |language=nl-NL |date=16 May 2022 |url-status=live}}</ref> It was his first season as a first-choice goalkeeper.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.fifa.com/fifaplus/en/articles/world-cup-qatar-2022-andries-noppert-from-rags-to-riches-benchwarmer-national-team-netherlands|title=Noppert: from benchwarmer to national team goalkeeper|website=FIFA|access-date=26 March 2023}}</ref> Noppert quickly impressed, so much so that Louis van Gaal called him up for the Netherlands squad for the World Cup on 11 November 2022,<ref>{{Cite web |title=Van Gaal maakt 26-koppige WK-selectie bekend |url=https://www.onsoranje.nl/nieuws/nederlands-elftal/80525/van-gaal-maakt-26-koppige-wk-selectie-bekend |access-date=31 May 2023 |website=www.onsoranje.nl}}</ref> where he also became first choice goalkeeper.<ref>{{Cite web |date=20 November 2022 |title='Noppert eerste doelman van Oranje op WK' |url=https://www.vi.nl/nieuws/noppert-eerste-doelman-van-oranje-op-wk |access-date=31 May 2023 |website=Voetbal International |language=nl}}</ref> Upon return, Noppert played 3 matches for Heerenveen before being injured during a warming up on 25 January 2023.<ref>{{Cite web |date=29 January 2023 |title=Doelman Noppert worstelt met 'vervelende blessure' |url=https://www.rtlnieuws.nl/sport/voetbal/artikel/5362373/doelman-noppert-worstelt-met-vervelende-blessure |access-date=31 May 2023 |website=RTL Nieuws |language=nl}}</ref> What first looked to be a minor injury became a major one causing him to be out of play for a then unknown amount of time. After being out for 4 months and having undergone surgery, Noppert returned to the pitch in Heerenveen’s last match of the season against Go Ahead Eagles. In which he kept a clean sheet and won 2-0, sending Heerenveen into European play-offs.<ref>{{Cite web |title=sc Heerenveen-Go Ahead Eagles (28-05-2023) - Voetbal International |url=https://www.vi.nl/wedstrijden/2023/05/28/nederland/eredivisie/sc-heerenveen-vs-go-ahead-eagles |access-date=31 May 2023 |website=www.vi.nl}}</ref>

==International career==
Noppert was called up to the [[Netherlands national football team|Netherlands national team]] in September 2022.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://lc.nl/sport/Andries-Noppert-van-SC-Heerenveen-geselecteerd-voor-Oranje-27930617.html|title=Andries Noppert van SC Heerenveen zit in definitieve selectie Oranje|website=Leeuwarder Courant}}</ref> In November 2022, he was included in the nation's 26-man squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] in [[Qatar]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.onsoranje.nl/nieuws/nederlands-elftal/80525/van-gaal-maakt-26-koppige-wk-selectie-bekend |title=Van Gaal maakt 26-koppige WK-selectie bekend |trans-title=Van Gaal announces 26-man World Cup squad |publisher=[[Royal Dutch Football Association]] |date=11 November 2022 |access-date=12 November 2022 |language=nl}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.goal.com/en-gb/news/who-is-andries-noppert-the-towering-dutch-goalkeeper-making-his-debut-with-the-netherlands-at-the-world-cup/bltf4827c39badc0ecb|title=Who is Andries Noppert? The towering Dutch goalkeeper making his debut with the Netherlands at the World Cup &#124; Goal.com|website=www.goal.com}}</ref> He discovered his inclusion in the squad on TV.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/football/2022/nov/30/i-found-out-i-was-going-by-tv-inside-andries-nopperts-world-cup-dream|title='I found out I was going by TV': inside Andries Noppert's World Cup dream|date=30 November 2022|website=the Guardian}}</ref>

Noppert made his international debut in his country’s opening match at the tournament against [[Senegal national football team|Senegal]], a 2–0 victory.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Sportstar |first=Team |date=21 November 2022 |title=LIVE Senegal vs Netherlands, FIFA World Cup: Gakpo, Noppert start for the Dutch, Qatar 2022 updates |url=https://sportstar.thehindu.com/football/fifa-world-cup/news/fifa-world-cup-live-streaming-senegal-vs-netherlands-info-sen-v-ned-score-qatar-2022-wc-updates/article66164696.ece |access-date=21 November 2022 |website=sportstar.thehindu.com |language=en}}</ref>
