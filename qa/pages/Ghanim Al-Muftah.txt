'''Ghanim Muhammad Al-Muftah''' ({{Lang-ar|غانم محمد المفتاح|Ghānim Muḥammad al-Muftāḥ}}, born 5 May 2002) is a [[YouTuber|Qatari YouTuber]] and philanthropist with [[caudal regression syndrome]]. In 2017, he was Qatar's youngest entrepreneur at 15.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web |date=2022-11-20 |title=Ghanim Al Muftah: The boy who stole the show in WC opening ceremony alongside Morgan Freeman |url=https://www.tbsnews.net/sports/ghanim-al-muftah-boy-who-stole-show-wc-opening-ceremony-alongside-morgan-freeman-535806 |access-date=2022-11-20 |website=The Business Standard |language=en}}</ref>

Ghanim Muhammad Al-Muftah was born in [[Doha]], Qatar on 5 May 2002,<ref>{{Cite web |title=فيديو {{!}} قصة غانم المفتاح الذي وُلد بنصف جسد وبات مصدراً عالمياً للإلهام {{!}} Goal.com |url=https://www.goal.com/ar/%D8%A3%D8%AE%D8%A8%D8%A7%D8%B1/%D9%81%D9%8A%D8%AF%D9%8A%D9%88-%D9%82%D8%B5%D8%A9-%D8%BA%D8%A7%D9%86%D9%85-%D8%A7%D9%84%D9%85%D9%81%D8%AA%D8%A7%D8%AD-%D8%A7%D9%84%D8%B0%D9%8A-%D9%88%D9%8F%D9%84%D8%AF-%D8%A8%D9%86%D8%B5%D9%81-%D8%AC%D8%B3%D8%AF-%D9%88%D8%A8%D8%A7%D8%AA-%D9%85%D8%B5%D8%AF%D8%B1%D8%A7%D9%8B-%D8%B9%D8%A7%D9%84%D9%85%D9%8A%D8%A7%D9%8B-%D9%84%D9%84%D8%A7%D9%95%D9%84%D9%87%D8%A7%D9%85/bltfaf9577fbdf704f6 |access-date=2022-11-27 |website=www.goal.com}}</ref> with [[Caudal regression syndrome|caudal Regression Syndrome]], a disease that affects the development of the lower spine. He has climbed [[Jebel Shams]].<ref name=":0" />
