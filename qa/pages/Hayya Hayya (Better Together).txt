"'''Hayya Hayya (Better Together)'''" is a song by American singer [[Trinidad Cardona]], Nigerian singer [[Davido]] and Qatari singer [[AISHA]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://music.apple.com/us/album/hayya-hayya-better-together-music-from-the-fifa-world/1616734969|title=Hayya Hayya (Better Together) [Music from the FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022 Official Soundtrack] – Single by Trinidad Cardona, Davido & AISHA on iTunes|work=iTunes Store (EU)|access-date=April 1, 2022}}</ref> It is the first single of the multi-song [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] [[FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022 Official Soundtrack|official soundtrack]]. The track was produced by [[RedOne]] and was released on April 1, 2022.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://african.business/2022/04/apo-newsfeed/trinidad-cardona-joins-davido-and-aisha-for-hayya-hayya-better-together-the-first-single-from-the-fifa-world-cup-qatar-2022tm-official-soundtrack/ |title=Trinidad Cardona joins Davido and Aisha for Hayya Hayya (Better Together) |date=April 1, 2022 |website=African.business }}</ref> The title contains the [[Arabic]] word ''hayyā'' (هيا), an interjection meaning "let's go!"<ref>{{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=23JyDwAAQBAJ&dq=%22let%27s+go%21%22+%D9%87%D9%8A%D8%A7&pg=PT201|title=Arabic For Dummies|first=Amine|last=Bouchentouf|date=September 27, 2018|publisher=John Wiley & Sons|isbn=9781119475484 |via=Google Books}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sbs.com.au/news/article/these-are-the-voices-behind-the-world-cup-song-that-football-fans-will-hear-over-and-over-again/s16hpjy2h|title=These are the voices behind the World Cup song that football fans will hear over and over again|website=SBS News}}</ref>

==Music video==
The music video for the song was released on April 1, 2022. It features [[Trinidad Cardona]], [[Davido]], AISHA and [[RedOne]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.fifa.com/tournaments/mens/worldcup/qatar2022/media-releases/origin1904-p.cxm.fifa.comtrinidad-cardona-joins-davido-and-aisha-for-hayya-hayya-better-together-the|title=FIFA World Cup™ Official Soundtrack kicks off with Hayya Hayya (Better Together)|website=www.fifa.com}}</ref>
