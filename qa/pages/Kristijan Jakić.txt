'''Kristijan Jakić''' (born 14 May 1997) is a Croatian professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[defensive midfielder]] for [[Bundesliga]] club [[Eintracht Frankfurt]] and the [[Croatia national football team|Croatia national team]].

== Career ==

=== Early career and RNK Split ===
A native of [[Runovići]], he started training at the age of 7,<ref>[http://www.dalmacijanews.hr/clanak/wr87-splitov-jaki-iz-runovica-volim-kaku-beskrajno-vjerujem-treneru-sablicu Splitov Jaki iz Runovića: Volim Kaku, beskrajno vjerujem treneru Sabliću] at DalmacijaNews</ref> at the local [[:hr:NK Mračaj Runović|Mračaj]].<ref>[http://www.dalmacijanews.hr/clanak/wr87-splitov-jaki-iz-runovica-volim-kaku-beskrajno-vjerujem-treneru-sablicu Splitov Jaki iz Runovića: Volim Kaku, beskrajno vjerujem treneru Sabliću] at DalmacijaNews</ref> Subsequently, he moved to the nearby, bigger [[NK Imotski|Imotski]], where he would spend four seasons,<ref>[http://imotskenovine.hr/nk-kamen-ivanbegovina-klub-kojem-favoriti-lome-zube/ NK Kamen Ivanbegovina: Klub na kojem favoriti "lome zube"] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190420033232/https://imotskenovine.hr/nk-kamen-ivanbegovina-klub-kojem-favoriti-lome-zube/ |date=20 April 2019 }} at Imotske Novine</ref> qualifying for the [[Croatian Academy Football League]] in 2013.<ref>[http://radioimotski.hr/naslovnica/sjajna-generacija-kadeta-nk-imotski-najbolji-vec-prodani-u-hajduk-i-split/ Sjajna generacija kadeta NK Imotski – najbolji već prodani u Hajduk i Split] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170517170002/http://radioimotski.hr/naslovnica/sjajna-generacija-kadeta-nk-imotski-najbolji-vec-prodani-u-hajduk-i-split/ |date=17 May 2017 }} at Radio Imotski</ref> He moved, however, to the [[RNK Split]] academy that summer. In 2015, he debuted for the [[Croatia national under-19 football team|Croatia U19 team]],<ref>[http://hns-cff.hr/players/139876/kristijan-jakic/ Kristijan Jakić] at Croatian Football Federation official page</ref> and the following and in December of the same year, for the RNK Split senior team, in a 1–1 home draw with [[NK Osijek|Osijek]], coming in the 66th minute for [[Miloš Vidović]].<ref>[https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2015/12/20/croatia/1-hnl/nk-split/nk-osijek/2042770/ Split-Osijek] at Soccerway</ref>

=== Istra 1961 and Lokomotiva ===
In the May 2017, following the relegation of RNK Split, Jakić moved to [[NK Lokomotiva|Lokomotiva]].<ref>[http://www.nklokomotiva.hr/vijest/kristijan-jakic-novi-je-igrac-lokomotive-483 Kristijan Jakić novi je igrač Lokomotive] at [[NK Lokomotiva]] official site</ref> Struggling during his first half-season at the club, Jakić requested a loan to [[NK Istra 1961|Istra 1961]] from club's director of football Božidar Šikić. He spent the second half of the [[2017–18 Croatian First Football League|2017–18 season]] in [[Pula]], where he regained form thanks to Istra coach [[Darko Raić-Sudar]]. He returned to Lokomotiva at the end of the season, where he turned into one of the best players during coach [[Goran Tomić]]'s tenure at the club.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|last=Olivari|first=Davorin|date=15 January 2021|title=Novi 'Brozović' otkrio: 'Dinamo je iznad svih u Hrvatskoj, a ja želim dostići vrh i zaigrati u Realu'|url=https://sportske.jutarnji.hr/sn/nogomet/hnl/novi-brozovic-otkrio-dinamo-je-iznad-svih-u-hrvatskoj-a-ja-zelim-dostici-vrh-i-zaigrati-u-realu-15043128|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=15 January 2021|website=Sportske novosti|language=hr-hr}}</ref>

=== Dinamo Zagreb ===
His good performances for Lokomotiva drew attention from Croatian giants [[GNK Dinamo Zagreb|Dinamo Zagreb]], who signed him for €1.2 million on 24 July 2020.<ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=24 July 2020|title=Kristijan Jakić potpisao za Dinamo!|url=https://www.gnkdinamo.hr/hr/Novosti/Clanak/kristijan-jakic-potpisao-za-dinamo|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=15 January 2021|website=GNK Dinamo|language=hr-HR}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Dobrinić|first=Damir|date=21 October 2020|title=Dinamo napravio 4. najbolji transfer u povijesti i zaradio deset puta više od Hajduka, rekord Lokosa!|url=https://sportske.jutarnji.hr/sn/nogomet/hnl/dinamo-napravio-4-najbolji-transfer-u-povijesti-i-zaradio-deset-puta-vise-od-hajduka-rekord-lokosa-15026974|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=15 January 2021|website=Sportske novosti|language=hr-hr}}</ref> He was forced to miss the beginning of the [[2020–21 GNK Dinamo Zagreb season|season]] due to [[Coronavirus disease 2019|COVID-19]] infection. He made his Dinamo debut on 12 September in a 2–1 victory over [[HNK Hajduk Split|Hajduk Split]].<ref name=":0" /> In his [[2020–21 UEFA Europa League|Europa League]] debut, in a goalless draw with [[Feyenoord]] on 22 October, he fouled [[Mark Diemers]] and caused a penalty, which Dinamo goalkeeper [[Dominik Livaković]] saved.<ref>{{Cite web|last1=Lipovac|first1=Nikola|last2=Mršnik|first2=Patrik|date=22 October 2020|title=Totalna ofenziva i drama protiv Feyenoorda: Dinamo s igračem više promašio pobjedu u zadnjim minutama|url=https://sportske.jutarnji.hr/sn/nogomet/europska-liga/totalna-ofenziva-i-drama-protiv-feyenoorda-dinamo-s-igracem-vise-promasio-pobjedu-u-zadnjim-minutama-15027179|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=15 January 2021|website=Sportske novosti|language=hr-hr}}</ref><ref name=":0" /> On 8 November, scored his first goal for Dinamo in a 5–0 routing of Istra 1961.<ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=8 November 2020|title=Razigrani Dinamo, pet golova, Jakić slavi prvijenac|url=https://gnkdinamo.hr/hr/Novosti/Clanak/razigrani-dinamo-pet-golova-jakic-slavi-prvijenac|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=15 January 2021|website=GNK Dinamo|language=hr-HR}}</ref> By the end of the first half-season, Jakić had already established himself as the important part of Dinamo's starting lineup.<ref name=":0" />

On 20 July 2021, in Dinamo's [[2021–22 UEFA Champions League qualifying phase and play-off round#Q2 CH1.1|Champions League second qualifying round]] against [[AC Omonia|Omonia]], Jakić assisted [[Lovro Majer]] and scored himself as Dinamo won 2–0.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Tolić|first=Josip|date=20 July 2021|title=Majer: Ma ovo bismo potpisali; Jakić: Teren baš nije kako treba|url=https://www.24sata.hr/sport/majer-ma-ovo-bismo-potpisali-jakic-teren-bas-nije-kako-treba-774836|url-status=live|access-date=27 August 2021|website=24sata|language=hr}}</ref> Ahead of Dinamo's [[2021–22 UEFA Champions League qualifying phase and play-off round#PO CH4.2|Champions League play-off]] against [[FC Sheriff Tiraspol|Sheriff Tiraspol]] on 25 August, Jakić was dropped from the squad, with coach [[Damir Krznar]] claiming he had injured his ankle in the 1–0 [[2021–22 Croatian First Football League|league]] victory over Lokomotiva the weekend before.<ref>{{Cite web|date=24 August 2021|title=Novi problemi u kadru Dinama uoči uzvrata sa Šerifom, ali Krznar ostaje optimist: "Možemo do Lige prvaka!"|url=https://gol.dnevnik.hr/clanak/rubrika/nogomet/novi-problemi-u-kadru-dinama-uoci-uzvrata-sa-serifom-ali-krznar-ostaje-optimist-mozemo-do-lige-prvaka---664062.html|url-status=live|access-date=27 August 2021|website=Gol.hr|language=hr}}</ref> However, ''[[Sportske novosti]]'' published that Jakić was removed from the squad as a disciplinary measure after he was dissatisfied by the club refusing [[Eintracht Frankfurt]]'s offer to buy him.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Ničota|first=Tomo|date=24 August 2021|title=Pomutnja u Dinamu: Prvotimac poručio da želi transfer i razljutio Krznara koji ga stavlja na 'led'!?|url=https://sportske.jutarnji.hr/sn/nogomet/liga-prvaka/pomutnja-u-dinamu-prvotimac-porucio-da-zeli-transfer-i-razljutio-krznara-koji-ga-stavlja-na-led-15097198|url-status=live|access-date=27 August 2021|website=Sportske novosti|language=hr-hr}}</ref> He was unexpectedly named in the starting lineup in the 29 August league derby against [[NK Osijek|Osijek]], and scored the opener as Dinamo won 2–0.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Đukarić|first=Gradimir|date=29 August 2021|title=VIDEO: Slavlje Dinama u Osijeku! Jakić se oprostio pogotkom, Livaković obranio penal, nesretni autogol...|url=https://sportske.jutarnji.hr/sn/nogomet/hnl/video-slavlje-dinama-u-osijeku-jakic-se-oprostio-pogotkom-livakovic-obranio-penal-nesretni-autogol-15098324|url-status=live|access-date=30 August 2021|website=Sportske novosti|language=hr-hr}}</ref>

== International career ==
Jakić earned his first call-up to the [[Croatia national football team|Croatia national team]] on 20 September 2021, when coach [[Zlatko Dalić]] called him up for the October [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification – UEFA Group H|World Cup qualifiers]] against [[Cyprus national football team|Cyprus]] and [[Slovakia national football team|Slovakia]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Dalić objavio popis za nastavak kvalifikacija: Pozvao mladog asa iz Bayerna, vratio Brekala, nema Rebića i Petkovića! |url=https://gol.dnevnik.hr/clanak/hrvatska-reprezentacija/zlatko-dalic-objavio-popis-za-nastavak-kvalifikacija-za-svjetsko-prvenstvo-vatrene-cekaju-cipar-i-slovacka---667392.html |website=Gol.hr |access-date=20 September 2021 |language=hr |date=20 September 2021}}</ref> He made his debut on 8 October against the former opponent, getting substituted on in the 84th minute of the 3–0 victory.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Bradovski|first=Mihaela|date=8 October 2021|title=Debitant Jakić: 'Trenutno proživljavam svoje najbolje nogometne trenutke'|url=https://sportnet.rtl.hr/vijesti/555587/nogomet-reprezentacija/debitant-jakic-trenutno-prozivljavam-svoje-najbolje-nogometne-trenutke/|url-status=live|access-date=9 October 2021|website=Sportnet|language=hr}}</ref>
