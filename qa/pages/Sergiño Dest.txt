'''Sergiño Gianni Dest''' (born November 3, 2000) is a professional [[Association football|soccer]] player who plays as a [[Full-back (association football)|full-back]] for [[La Liga]] club [[FC Barcelona|Barcelona]]. Born in the Netherlands, he represents the [[United States men's national soccer team|United States national team]].

==Early life==
Born in the Netherlands to a [[Surinamese Americans|Surinamese-American]] father and a Dutch mother,  Dest played for the youth academy of [[Almere City FC|Almere City]] until 2012, when he moved to the [[AFC Ajax|Ajax]] youth academy. Initially a [[Forward (association football)|forward]], he progressed through the ranks of the club until making the switch to a [[Defender (association football)#Full-back|full-back]].<ref name="barca-official" />

==Club career==
===Ajax===
Dest debuted for [[Jong Ajax]] on October 15, 2018, in a 2–1 loss against [[Jong PSV]]. Dest impressed over the course of the 2018–19 season, making 18 appearances in the Dutch [[2018–19 Eerste Divisie|Eerste Divisie]] and tallying one goal and two assists.<ref name="Debut" /> He also scored one goal and provided one assist in seven appearances in the prestigious [[2018–19 UEFA Youth League|UEFA Youth League]].<ref name="Debut" />

On July 27, 2019, Dest made his debut for the [[AFC Ajax|Ajax first team]] in an official game when he started the [[2019 Johan Cruyff Shield]] match against [[AFC Ajax–PSV Eindhoven rivalry|rivals]] [[PSV Eindhoven]].<ref name="Debut" /> Ajax won the match 2–0 and claimed the trophy.<ref name="Ajax vs. PSV 2 - 0">{{cite web |title=Ajax vs. PSV 2 - 0 |url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2019/07/27/netherlands/super-cup/afc-ajax/psv-nv/3020705/ |website=Soccerway.com |access-date=July 28, 2019 |archive-date=August 22, 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190822225301/https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2019/07/27/netherlands/super-cup/afc-ajax/psv-nv/3020705/ |url-status=live }}</ref> On August 10, 2019, Dest debuted in the [[Eredivisie]], replacing [[Noussair Mazraoui]] in the 54th minute of Ajax's 5–0 home win against [[FC Emmen]].<ref name="Ajax 5 - 0 Emmen">{{cite web |title=Ajax 5 - 0 Emmen |url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2019/08/10/netherlands/eredivisie/afc-ajax/fc-emmen/3032064/ |website=Soccerway.com |access-date=August 12, 2019 |archive-date=September 18, 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190918225515/https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2019/08/10/netherlands/eredivisie/afc-ajax/fc-emmen/3032064/ |url-status=live }}</ref> He also appeared as a substitute in qualifying matches for the [[UEFA Champions League]] against [[PAOK FC|PAOK]] and [[APOEL FC|APOEL]]. In September, Dest was officially promoted to the Ajax first-team squad. On September 17, 2019, he made his first start in the Champions League against [[Lille OSC]], a match which Ajax won 3–0.<ref>{{cite web |title=Ajax – LOSC 2020 History |url=https://www.uefa.com/uefachampionsleague/match/2027038--ajax-vs-losc/ |publisher=[[UEFA]] |access-date=October 1, 2020 |archive-date=August 1, 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200801173830/https://www.uefa.com/uefachampionsleague/match/2027038--ajax-vs-losc/ |url-status=live }}</ref> Dest finished his first senior season with 35 appearances in which he scored two goals—both in a [[KNVB Cup]] fixture against [[SC Telstar|Telstar]].<ref name="sw" /><ref>{{cite web |title=Watch: USMNT's Sergiño Dest Scores Two Goals for Ajax |url=https://www.si.com/soccer/2019/12/18/sergino-dest-goal-video-ajax-usmnt |publisher=[[Sports Illustrated]] |access-date=October 1, 2020 |date=December 18, 2019 |archive-date=December 1, 2020 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201201180911/https://www.si.com/soccer/2019/12/18/sergino-dest-goal-video-ajax-usmnt |url-status=live }}</ref>

==International career==
Dest has dual nationality making him eligible to play for either the United States or the Netherlands and ultimately chose to play for the United States' senior national team.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Dest chooses to play for US over Netherlands |url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/sports/soccer/2019/10/28/ajax-defender-dest-chooses-to-play-for-us-national-team/40452829/ |access-date=2022-11-14 |website=USA TODAY |language=en-US |archive-date=November 14, 2022 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20221114025916/https://www.usatoday.com/story/sports/soccer/2019/10/28/ajax-defender-dest-chooses-to-play-for-us-national-team/40452829/ |url-status=live }}</ref> Dest has represented the United States at youth and full international level. He played five games in total for the [[United States men's national under-17 soccer team|United States under-17 team]], including four appearances at the [[2017 FIFA U-17 World Cup|2017 FIFA Under-17 World Cup]].<ref name="u17u20stats">{{cite web |url=https://www.besoccer.com/player/s-dest-429346 |title=S. Dest |website=besoccer.com |access-date=August 30, 2022 |archive-date=August 30, 2022 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220830174811/https://www.besoccer.com/player/s-dest-429346 |url-status=live }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://americansoccernow.com/articles/sergino-dest-16-is-opening-eyes-at-the-u-17-world-cup|title=ASN: Sergino Dest, 16, Is Opening Eyes at the U-17 World Cup|website=americansoccernow.com|access-date=November 21, 2018|archive-date=September 25, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200925170743/http://americansoccernow.com/articles/sergino-dest-16-is-opening-eyes-at-the-u-17-world-cup/|url-status=live}}</ref> He represented the [[United States men's national under-20 soccer team|United States under-20 team]] at the [[2019 FIFA U-20 World Cup|2019 FIFA Under-20 World Cup]], where he played four matches.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.espn.com/soccer/team/squad/_/id/2833/united-states-u20|title=United States U20 Scoring Squad|website=ESPN|access-date=September 18, 2019|archive-date=September 25, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200925170739/https://www.espn.com/soccer/team/squad/_/id/2833/united-states-u20/|url-status=live}}</ref> In total, he made 12 appearances and scored one goal for the under-20 team.<ref name="u17u20stats"/>

Dest made his senior debut for the [[United States men's national soccer team|United States]] on September 6, 2019, starting and playing 68 minutes in a 3–0 friendly loss against [[Mexico national football team|Mexico]].<ref name="usseniordebut">{{Cite web|url=https://sports.yahoo.com/dest-us-debut-against-mexico-among-6-starters-004206850--sow.html|title=Dest debuts against Mexico as US makes 6 changes|website=sports.yahoo.com|access-date=September 10, 2019|archive-date=October 5, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191005084831/https://sports.yahoo.com/dest-us-debut-against-mexico-among-6-starters-004206850--sow.html|url-status=dead}}</ref> Dest chose to commit his international future to the United States at the senior level on October 28, 2019.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.mlssoccer.com/news/sergino-dest-chooses-united-states-over-netherlands|title=Sergino Dest chooses United States over Netherlands &#124; MLSSoccer.com|website=mlssoccer|access-date=September 19, 2021|archive-date=September 19, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210919002022/https://www.mlssoccer.com/news/sergino-dest-chooses-united-states-over-netherlands|url-status=live}}</ref> He was included in the United States' 26-man roster for the 2022 World Cup in Qatar.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Dest |first=Sergiño |title=A dream come true to be called up for my first World Cup! During the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]], Dest provided the assist for [[Christian Pulisic]]'s winning goal in a 1–0 victory over [[Iran national football team|Iran]], which sent the United States into the [[2022 FIFA World Cup knockout stage|Round of 16]]. In that next game against [[Netherlands national football team|Netherlands]] (his birth nation), he was culpable in leaving too much space for [[Daley Blind]] to score the second Dutch goal in a 3–1 defeat for the U.S.<ref>[https://www.mlssoccer.com/news/three-takeaways-as-usa-suffer-world-cup-exit-against-netherlands Three takeaways as USA suffer World Cup exit against Netherlands] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20221206011648/https://www.mlssoccer.com/news/three-takeaways-as-usa-suffer-world-cup-exit-against-netherlands |date=December 6, 2022 }}, Matthew Doyle, [[Major League Soccer]] website. ==Style of play==
Dest combines many key qualities needed in a full-back, including strong attacking prowess, solid defensive play, and being excellent in possession. He has the ability to take players on and his pace is a virtue going forward and defending.<ref name="barca-official" />
