'''Mikkel Krogh Damsgaard''' ({{IPA-da|ˈtɑmsˌkɒˀ}}; born 3 July 2000) is a Danish professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[winger (association football)|winger]] for [[Premier League]] club [[Brentford F.C.|Brentford]] and the [[Denmark national football team|Denmark national team]].

==Club career==
===Early career===
Damsgaard started playing football in his hometown [[Jyllinge]], where he played for eight years, with his father as his coach. During a game with Jyllinge FC, Damsgaard was spotted by a scout from [[FC Nordsjælland|Nordsjælland]], who was out actually looking for another player, but ended up contacting Damsgaard.<ref name="jyllinge">{{cite news |last1=Davidsen |first1=Martin |title=Podcast: Fra Jyllinge til Sampdoria - interview med Mikkel Damsgaard |url=https://www.playmaker.nu/home/podcast-mikkel-damsgaard-fortller-om-vejen-fra-brnefodbold-til-serie-a |access-date=2 January 2021 |work=playmaker.nu |date=2 September 2020 |language=Danish}}</ref> He then joined FC Nordsjælland as a U12 player on a youth contract until 2020.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Dalsgaard |first1=Rudi |title=FCN's målsluger uden målnæse |url=https://sn.dk/Sport-FAA/FCNs-maalsluger-uden-maalnaese/artikel/723116 |access-date=2 January 2021 |work=Sjællandske Nyheder |date=9 February 2018 |language=Danish}}</ref>

===Nordsjælland===
On 27 September 2017, Damsgaard made his official debut for [[FC Nordsjælland|Nordsjælland]] at the age of 17. He played the whole game against [[Vejgaard BK]] in a [[Danish Cup]] game, making one assist in a 4&ndash;0 win at [[Soffy Road]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Reaktioner fra sejren over Vejgaard |url=https://fcn.dk/2017/09/reaktioner-sejren-vejgaard/ |website=FC Nordsjælland |access-date=21 September 2020 |language=Danish |date=27 September 2017}}</ref>

He made his [[Danish Superliga]] debut for Nordsjælland on 26 November 2017 in a game against [[AC Horsens]] in shirt number 27.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=Soccerway|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2017/11/26/denmark/superliga/fc-nordsjalland/ac-horsens/2460427/|title=Game Report by Soccerway|date=26 November 2017}}</ref>

On 12 July 2018, Damsgaard made his European debut for [[FC Nordsjælland|Nordsjælland]] against [[Cliftonville F.C.|Cliftonville]] in a [[2018–19 UEFA Europa League|Europa League qualifier]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Europa League Qual (Sky Sports) |url=https://uk-sport-web.prod.oceanusorigin.com/football/cliftonville-vs-fc-nordsjaelland/teams/392411 |access-date=10 August 2022 |website=Sky Sports |language=en}}</ref>

===Sampdoria===
On 6 February 2020, it was confirmed that Damsgaard had signed a four-year contract with Italian [[Serie A]] club [[U.C. Sampdoria|Sampdoria]] with effect from 1 July 2020.<ref>{{cite web |title=Damsgaard blucerchiato dal 1° luglio, arriva dal Nordsjælland a titolo definitivo |url=https://www.sampdoria.it/damsgaard-blucerchiato-dal-1-luglio-arriva-dal-nordsjaelland-a-titolo-definitivo/ |website=U.C. Sampdoria |access-date=21 September 2020 |language=Italian |date=6 February 2020}}</ref> The transfer fee was estimated at €6.7 million ([[Danish krone|DKK]] 50 million).<ref>{{cite web |title=Medier: FCN får 50 millioner for Damsgaard |url=https://www.bold.dk/fodbold/nyheder/medier-fcn-faar-50-millioner-for-damsgaard/ |website=bold.dk |access-date=21 September 2020 |language=Danish |date=6 February 2020}}</ref> On 17 October 2020, he scored his first Serie A goal in a 3–0 victory against [[S.S. Lazio|Lazio]].<ref>{{Cite web|date=17 October 2020|title=Grande Sampdoria: Lazio schiantata con Quagliarella, Augello e Damsgaard|url=https://www.sampdoria.it/grande-sampdoria-lazio-schiantata-con-quagliarella-augello-e-damsgaard/|access-date=18 October 2020|website=U.C. Sampdoria|language=it-IT}}</ref>

===Brentford===
On 10 August 2022, Damsgaard joined English [[Premier League]] club [[Brentford F.C.|Brentford]] for a €15 million fee on a five-year deal.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Romano |first1=Fabrizio |title=Brentford snap up Mikkel Damsgaard from Sampdoria for €15m |url=https://www.theguardian.com/football/2022/aug/10/brentford-snap-up-mikkel-damsgaard-from-sampdoria-for-15m |website=The Guardian |access-date=10 August 2022 |date=10 August 2022 |url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Brentford sign Mikkel Damsgaard |url=https://www.brentfordfc.com/en/news/article/brentford-sign-mikkel-damsgaard |publisher=Brentford |access-date=10 August 2022 |date=10 August 2022 |url-status=live}}</ref>

==International career==
On 21 June 2021, Damsgaard scored a goal in a 4–1 win over [[Russia national football team|Russia]] in the [[UEFA Euro 2020]], to become the youngest Danish player to score at the [[UEFA European Championship|European Championship]], aged 20 years 353 days.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.uefa.com/uefaeuro-2020/match/2024469--russia-vs-denmark/postmatch/report/ |title=Russia 1-4 Denmark: Dazzling Danes storm into last 16 |website=UEFA |date=21 June 2021 }}</ref> In the Euro 2020 semi-finals, he scored the first goal against [[England national football team|England]] with the tournament's only freekick goal. However Denmark eventually lost 2–1 after extra-time.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.uefa.com/uefaeuro-2020/match/2024490--england-vs-denmark/ |title= England vs Denmark| website=UEFA |date=7 July 2021 }}</ref>
