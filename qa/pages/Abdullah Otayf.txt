'''Abdullah Ibrahim Yahya Otayf''' ({{lang-ar|عبد الله إبراهيم يحيى عطيف}}; born 3 August 1992) is a Saudi Arabian [[Association football|footballer]] who plays for [[Saudi Professional League|Saudi Pro League]] side [[Al-Ahli Saudi FC|Al-Ahli]] as a [[midfielder]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://int.soccerway.com/players/abdullah-otayf/197881/|title = Saudi Arabia - Abdullah Otayf - Profile with news, career statistics and history - Soccerway}}</ref>

==Club career==
Otayf started his career at [[Al-Shabab FC (Riyadh)|Al-Shabab]] before joining [[Portuguese Second Division]] side [[Louletano]] on 29 August 2012.<ref>{{cite web |title=صقر وعبدالله عطيف يوقعان رسمياً لنادي لوليتيانو البرتغالي |url=https://akhbaar24.argaam.com/article/detail/109815}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.zerozero.pt/jogador.php?id=207871&epoca_id=142|title=Abdullah Otayf :: Abdullah Ibrahim Otayf :: Al Hilal}}</ref>

On 12 February 2013, Otayf returned to Saudi Arabia and joined [[Al Hilal SFC|Al-Hilal]].<ref>{{cite web |title=عطيف ينتقل للهلال |url=https://arriyadiyah.com/416542}}</ref> On 10 April 2018, Otayf renewed his contract with Al-Hilal until the end of the 2020–21 season.<ref>{{cite web |title=رسمياً.. الهلال يجدد عقد عبدالله عطيف 3 أعوام |url=https://akhbaar24.argaam.com/article/detail/387437}}</ref> On 11 December 2020, Otayf renewed his contract with Al-Hilal once again, signing a two-season extension.<ref>{{cite web |title=الهلال يجدد عقد عبدالله عطيف |url=https://www.alarabiya.net/sport/saudi-sport/2020/12/12/%D8%A7%D9%84%D9%87%D9%84%D8%A7%D9%84-%D9%8A%D8%AC%D8%AF%D8%AF-%D8%B9%D9%82%D8%AF-%D8%B9%D8%A8%D8%AF%D8%A7%D9%84%D9%84%D9%87-%D8%B9%D8%B7%D9%8A%D9%81}}</ref>

On 8 March 2023, Otayf signed a pre-contract agreement with [[Al-Ahli Saudi FC|Al-Ahli]].<ref>{{cite web |title=أهلي جدة يتعاقد مع الدولي عبدالله عطيف |url=https://www.alarabiya.net/sport/saudi-sport/2023/03/08/%D8%A3%D9%87%D9%84%D9%8A-%D8%AC%D8%AF%D8%A9-%D9%8A%D8%AA%D8%B9%D8%A7%D9%82%D8%AF-%D9%85%D8%B9-%D8%A7%D9%84%D8%AF%D9%88%D9%84%D9%8A-%D8%B9%D8%A8%D8%AF%D8%A7%D9%84%D9%84%D9%87-%D8%B9%D8%B7%D9%8A%D9%81}}</ref> He officially joined the club following the conclusion of the 2022–23 season.

==International career==
He represented [[Saudi Arabia]] in 2011 U20 World Cup. He made his senior debut against [[Iran national football team|Iran]] on 9 December 2012 in the 2012 WAFF Championship.<ref>[https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2012/12/09/asia/west-asian-federation-championship/iran/saudi-arabia/1397072/ West Asian Federation Championship/Iran-Saudi Arabia] ''Soccerway''</ref> He scored his first goal against [[Yemen national football team|Yemen]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2012/12/12/asia/west-asian-federation-championship/yemen/saudi-arabia/1397075/|title = Yemen vs. Saudi Arabia - 12 December 2012 - Soccerway}}</ref> In that match he celebrated using a [[Sergio Busquets]] shirt<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.101greatgoals.com/blog/saudi-player-abdullah-otayf-celebrates-a-beauty-against-yemen-with-a-sergio-busquets-t-shirt/ |title=Saudi player Abdullah Otayf celebrates a beauty against Yemen with a Sergio Busquets t-shirt &#124; 101 Great Goals |access-date=3 April 2013 |archive-date=4 March 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304025814/http://www.101greatgoals.com/blog/saudi-player-abdullah-otayf-celebrates-a-beauty-against-yemen-with-a-sergio-busquets-t-shirt/ |url-status=dead }}</ref>

On 23 December 2013, Otayf was named in the squad taking part in the [[2013 WAFF Championship]].<ref>{{cite web |title=رياضي / المنتخب السعودي يصل للدوحة للمشاركة في بطولة غرب آسيا |url=https://sp.spa.gov.sa/viewfullstory.php?lang=ar&newsid=1181966}}</ref>

In May 2018 he was named in Saudi Arabia's preliminary [[Saudi Arabia national football team|squad]] for the [[2018 FIFA World Cup]] in Russia.<ref>{{cite web|title= World Cup 2018: All the confirmed squads for this summer's finals in Russia|url= https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/440833652018}}{{Dead link|date=September 2018 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}</ref> He was also included in the final squad.

Otayf was included in [[Saudi Arabia national football team|Saudi Arabia]]'s squad for the [[2019 AFC Asian Cup]] in the United Arab Emirates.<ref name="AFC">{{cite web |url=http://www.the-afc.com/afc/documents/PdfFiles/afc-asian-cup-uae-2019-complete-squad-lists |title=AFC Asian Cup UAE 2019 Complete Squad Lists |website=The-AFC.com |publisher=[[Asian Football Confederation]] |format=PDF |date=27 December 2018 |access-date=28 December 2018 |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181228174458/http://www.the-afc.com/afc/documents/PdfFiles/afc-asian-cup-uae-2019-complete-squad-lists |archive-date=28 December 2018}}</ref>

On 20 November 2019, Al-Rubaie was named in the squad for the [[24th Arabian Gulf Cup]].<ref>{{cite web |title=رينارد يعلن قائمة الأخضر لبطولة كأس الخليج الـ24 |url=https://www.saff.com.sa/news.php?id=138}}</ref> He won the best player award at the tournament.<ref>{{cite web |title=عبدالله عطيف أفضل لاعب في خليجي 24 |url=https://www.alarabiya.net/sport/saudi-sport/2019/12/08/%D8%B9%D8%A8%D8%AF%D8%A7%D9%84%D9%84%D9%87-%D8%B9%D8%B7%D9%8A%D9%81-%D8%A3%D9%81%D8%B6%D9%84-%D9%84%D8%A7%D8%B9%D8%A8-%D9%81%D9%8A-%D8%AE%D9%84%D9%8A%D8%AC%D9%8A-24}}</ref>

On 11 November 2022, Otayf was named in the squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.saff.com.sa/en/news.php?id=1932 |title=Renard announces the national team list for the World Cup 2022 in Qatar |publisher=[[Saudi Arabian Football Federation]] |date=11 November 2022 |access-date=12 November 2022}}</ref>
