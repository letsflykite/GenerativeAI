'''Abdulaziz Hatem Mohammed Abdullah''' ({{lang-ar|عبد العزيز حاتم}}; born 28 October 1990) is a Qatari professional [[association football|footballer]] of Sudanese descent who plays as a [[midfielder (football)|midfielder]] for [[Al-Rayyan SC|Al Rayyan]] and the [[Qatar national football team]].

==Club career==
Hatem began his professional career with [[Al-Arabi SC (Qatar)|Al-Arabi SC]] in 2007 and moved to [[Al-Gharafa SC]] in 2015. In July 2019 he joined Al-Rayyan SC.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.qsl.qa/en/al-rayyan-midfielder-abdulaziz-hatem-exclusive-interview-qslqa-ahead-their-qatar-clasico-against-al | title=Ahead Of Their Qatar Clasico Against Al Sadd | date= | accessdate=2022-11-30}}</ref>

==International career==
He made his debut for the [[Qatar national football team]] during the [[9th International Friendship Tournament]] held in December 2009.

Hatem participated for the [[Qatar national under-23 football team|Qatar olympic team]] in the [[2012 Summer Olympics|2012 Summer Olympic qualifiers]]. He received 2 yellow cards during the group stage, rendering him ineligible to compete for one game. Qatar, however, chose to play him against [[Oman national under-23 football team|Oman]], resulting in [[FIFA]] overturning the 1–1 draw to a 3–0 loss for Qatar. This meant that Qatar, who were undefeated in the group stage, would end up third in the group while Oman and South Korea went through to the next stage.<ref>[https://archive.today/20120717094126/http://www.al3nabi.com/news-4-38933.html بسبب مشاركة عبد العزيز حاتم , الأوليمبي القطري قد يخسر من عمان اداريا] {{in lang|ar}}</ref>
