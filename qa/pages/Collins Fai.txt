'''Collins Ngoran Fai''' (born 13 August 1992) is a Cameroonian professional [[association football|footballer]], who plays for Saudi Arabian club [[Al-Tai FC|Al-Tai]] and the [[Cameroon national football team|Cameroon national team]]. Mainly a [[right back]],<ref>{{Cite web|date=29 January 2022|title=🇨🇲 Fai Collins completes move to Al_Tai FC as he continues to impress at AFCON|url=https://www.kick442.com/%f0%9f%87%a8%f0%9f%87%b2-fai-collins-completes-move-to-al_tai-fc-as-he-continues-to-impress-at-afcon/|access-date=6 February 2022|website=KICK442|language=en-GB}}</ref> he can also operate as a [[left back]].

==Early life==
Collins Fai was born and raised in [[Bamenda]], a city of the [[Anglophone Cameroonian|English-speaking part of Cameroon]]. He spent his entire youth career at local FC Bamenda.

==Club career==
Aged 19, Fai joined the Cameroonian top team of Union Douala. 

In 2013, Collins Fai joined European football for the first time as he signed with the Romanian team of [[Dinamo București]].

In January 2016, Fai joined [[Standard Liège]] in [[Belgium]]. On 17 March 2018, Fai played as [[Standard Liège]] beat [[K.R.C. Genk|Genk]] 1–0 in extra time to win the [[2018 Belgian Cup Final]] and qualify for the [[UEFA Europa League]].<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://m.live.sporza.be/cm/lmc.mobile/m_mc_voetbal/Beker-van-Belgie-1718/MG_BVB_F/1.3162424 |title=Emond knikt Standard na verlengingen naar bekerwinst |access-date=23 April 2018 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180521203352/http://m.live.sporza.be/cm/lmc.mobile/m_mc_voetbal/Beker-van-Belgie-1718/MG_BVB_F/1.3162424 |archive-date=21 May 2018 |url-status=dead }}</ref> On 29 January 2022, Fai joined Saudi Arabian club [[Al-Tai FC|Al-Tai]].<ref>{{cite web |title=الطائي يتعاقد مع الظهير الأيمن الكاميروني كولينز فاي |url=https://www.alriyadh.com/1932186}}</ref>
