The [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] was an association football tournament took place in November and December 2022 involved 32 men's national teams from nations affiliated to the [[International Federation of Association Football]] (FIFA). The tournament was broadcast all over the world.

==Host broadcaster==
As with all FIFA World Cup matches since the [[2002 FIFA World Cup|2002 edition]], the production of the 'world television feed' will be by Host Broadcast Services (HBS), part of [[Infront Sports & Media]].<ref name=":0">{{cite news|last1=Kerschbaumer|first1=Ken|title=FIFA World Cup 2022: Overview of FIFA World Cup Match-Coverage Plans|url=https://www.sportsvideo.org/2022/11/18/fifa-world-cup-2022-overview-of-fifa-world-cup-match-coverage-plans/|access-date=10 December 2022|work=Sports Video Group|date=18 November 2022}}</ref> Matches will be captured in [[Ultra-high-definition television|Ultra-high-definition (UHD)]] and [[Hybrid Log Gamma|HLG]] [[High-dynamic-range television|High-dynamic-range (HDR)]], with [[5.1 surround sound]].<ref name=":0" /> The 'world feed' is then used by rights holding broadcasters around the world.
