'''Raphael Claus''' (born 6 September 1979 in [[Santa Bárbara d'Oeste]], [[Brazil]]) is a Brazilian [[Association football|football]] [[referee (association football)|referee]]. He is nominated as an official referee list of [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] in [[Qatar]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fifa.com/tournaments/mens/worldcup/qatar2022/media-releases/36-referees-69-assistant-referees-and-24-video-match-officials-appointed-for-fifa-world-cup-qatar-2022|title=36 referees, 69 assistant referees, and 24 video match officials appointed for FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022}}</ref>

==Professional==
Raphael Claus currently represents the [[Federação Paulista de Futebol]], and has been listed on the [[FIFA]] table since the beginning of 2015.<ref>{{cite web|URL=http://www.aagsp.com.br/pagina/23539/raphael-claus.html|title=Raphael Claus|publisher=Associação de Árbitros da Grande São Paulo|access-date=27 September 2015 |language=pt}}</ref> He refereed in several international competitions, such as the [[2021 Copa América]] and the [[2019 FIFA U-20 World Cup]].
