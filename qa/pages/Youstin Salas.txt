'''Youstin Delfín Salas Gómez''' (born 17 June 1996) is a Costa Rican professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[defensive midfielder]] for [[Liga FPD]] club [[Deportivo Saprissa|Saprissa]] and the [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica national team]].

== International career ==
Salas was called up to the final 26-man [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica]] squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] in Qatar.<ref>{{cite web |date=3 November 2022 |title=Suárez eligió a sus 26 jugadores para la aventura mundialista en Catar |trans-title=Suarez chooses 26 players for the Qatar World Cup adventure |url=https://www.fedefutbol.com/2022/11/03/suarez-eligio-a-sus-26-jugadores-para-la-aventura-mundialista-en-catar/ |access-date=3 November 2022 |work=[[Costa Rican Football Federation|FCRF]] |language=es}}</ref>
