This article relates the participation of [[Tunisia national football team|Tunisia]] during the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] organized in Qatar from 20 November to 18 December 2022.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Première étape vers le Qatar pour l’Afrique |url=https://www.fifa.com/fr/tournaments/mens/worldcup/qatar2022/news/origin1904-p.cxm.fifa.compremiere-etape-vers-le-qatar-pour-l-afrique |access-date=2023-04-07 |website=www.fifa.com |language=fr}}</ref> This is the country's sixth participation in the competition after 1978, 1998, 2002, 2006 and 2018.<ref>{{Cite web |title=La Tunisie qualifiée officiellement pour la phase finale de la Coupe du monde de football au Qatar_French.news.cn |url=http://french.news.cn/2022-03/30/c_1310534936.htm |access-date=2023-04-07 |website=french.news.cn |language=fr}}</ref> Tunisia meets [[France national football team|France]], [[Denmark national football team|Denmark]] and [[Australia men's national soccer team|Australia]] in [[2022 FIFA World Cup Group D|Group D]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Groupe D Coupe du Monde 2022 de Football - Pays Qualifiés |url=https://www.footmercato.net/international/coupe-du-monde/groupe/3717424938369062189-groupe-d |access-date=2023-04-07 |website=Foot Mercato : Info Transferts Football - Actu Foot Transfert |language=fr}}</ref>

For the sixth time, Tunisia failed to qualify for the second round of the World Cup, as Tunisia finished the tournament ranked 21st after a 0–0 draw with Denmark, a 0–1 defeat against Australia and a 1–0 victory over France.

== Qualification ==

=== Summary ===
{{main|2022 FIFA World Cup qualification (CAF)}}
Tunisia, which only has to intervene in the [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification – CAF second round|second round]], meets [[Mauritania national football team|Mauritania]], [[Equatorial Guinea national football team|Equatorial Guinea]] and [[Zambia national football team|Zambia]]. With four wins, a draw and a defeat, the third round is reached, but this is not effective until the day of the last match.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Men's Ranking: November 2021 |url=https://www.fifa.com/fifa-world-ranking/men?dateId=id13471 |access-date=2023-04-07 |website=www.fifa.com |language=en}}</ref> The [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification – CAF third round|third round]] is played over two legs and Tunisia take on Mali, the only third-round contender who has never qualified for a World Cup. In the first leg, they took the lead over [[Mali national football team|Mali]] in the 36th minute thanks to an own goal from [[Moussa Sissako]],<ref>{{Cite web |date=2022-03-25 |title=But contre son camp et carton rouge en moins de 5 minutes, la soirée cauchemar de Sissako lors de Mali-Tunisie |url=https://www.lefigaro.fr/sports/football/but-contre-son-camp-et-carton-rouge-en-moins-de-5-minutes-la-soiree-cauchemar-de-sissako-lors-de-mali-tunisie-20220325 |access-date=2023-04-07 |website=LEFIGARO |language=fr}}</ref> who received a red card four minutes later.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Rault |first=Mathieu |title=Mali-Tunisie : Moussa Sissako a fini les mains en sang |url=https://www.footmercato.net/a4718746111882148532-mali-tunisie-moussa-sissako-a-fini-les-mains-en-sang |access-date=2023-04-07 |website=Foot Mercato : Info Transferts Football - Actu Foot Transfert |language=fr}}</ref> The Tunisians finished 1–0, so a goalless draw in the second leg was enough for them to qualify.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Qualifications Coupe du monde 2022 : la Tunisie qualifiée pour le Mondial après son nul face au Mali |url=https://www.eurosport.com/geoblocking.shtml |access-date=2023-04-07 |website=www.eurosport.com}}</ref>

A total of 29 players are used; twenty of them are also in the squad for the [[2021 Africa Cup of Nations]], during which Tunisia are eliminated in the quarter-finals. Only [[Aïssa Laïdouni]] and [[Ali Maâloul]] have played all eight matches.<ref>{{Cite web |title=EN : La liste de Mondher Kebaier pour affronter la Libye et la Guinée Equatoriale |url=https://www.kawarji.com/actu-64765-en-la-liste-de-mondher-kebaier-pour-affronter-la-libye-et-la-guinee-equatoriale.html |access-date=2023-04-07 |website=www.kawarji.com}}</ref> [[Mohamed Ali Ben Romdhane]] plays seven games, but is not used in the first game. [[Farouk Ben Mustapha]] is the goalkeeper in all second round matches, but [[Bechir Ben Saïd]], who is making his Africa Cup debut, plays in the third round. [[Sebastian Tounekti]] (one game) makes his first international appearance in qualifying.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Malek |first=Fakhreddine Ben |date=2021-03-17 |title=Foot : Qui est Sébastien Tounekti, le nouvel attaquant de l’équipe nationale ? |url=https://sport.tunisienumerique.com/foot-qui-est-sebastien-tounekti-le-nouvel-attaquant-de-lequipe-nationale/ |access-date=2023-04-07 |website=Sport By TN |language=fr-FR}}</ref> The top scorer is captain [[Wahbi Khazri]] with three goals, all scored in the second round.<ref>{{Cite web |date=2021-10-14 |title=Mondial 2022 (Q) : le classement des meilleurs buteurs en Zone Afrique |url=https://www.afrik-foot.com/mondial-2022-q-le-classement-des-meilleurs-buteurs-en-zone-afrique |access-date=2023-04-07 |website=www.afrik-foot.com |language=fr-FR}}</ref> He received a second yellow card in the last match of the second round and was replaced by [[Youssef Msakni]] in the third round.<ref>{{cite web|access-date=30 August 2022|date=24 March 2022|language=fr|title=Barrages Coupe du Monde 2022 : "La force de la Tunisie, c'est le collectif" Jalel Kadri|url=https://africafootunited.com/barrages-coupe-du-monde-2022-la-force-de-la-tunisie-cest-le-collectif-jalel-kadri/|website=africafootunited.com}}<!-- auto-translated by Module:CS1 translator -->.</ref> In total, eight players have scored at least one goal, plus an own goal by a Malian player, which allows Tunisia to qualify for the World Cup.

=== Second Round ===
{{main|2022 FIFA World Cup qualification – CAF second round}}
The draw for the ten groups will be held on 21 January 2020 in [[Cairo]]. The teams are divided into four pots according to their FIFA ranking.<ref>{{Cite web |date=2019-12-26 |title=Afrique : les chapeaux des éliminatoires pour le Mondial 2022 sont connus ! |url=https://www.onzemondial.com/coupe-du-monde-2022/afrique-les-chapeaux-des-eliminatoires-pour-le-mondial-2022-sont-connus-205847 |access-date=2023-04-07 |website=Onze Mondial |language=fr}}</ref> The second round was supposed to start in March 2020,<ref>{{Cite web |last=Football |first=CAF-Confedération Africaine du |title=Cameroon 2021 qualifiers resume November, Qatar 2022 set for May 2021 |url=https://www.cafonline.com/news-center/news/cameroon-2021-qualifiers-resume-november-qatar-2022-set-for-may-2021 |access-date=2023-04-07 |website=CAFOnline.com |language=en}}</ref> but is postponed several times due to the [[COVID-19 pandemic in Africa|COVID-19 pandemic]] and does not start until September 2021.<ref>{{Cite web |title=L'Afrique avance en groupes vers le Qatar |url=https://www.fifa.com/fr/tournaments/mens/worldcup/qatar2022/news/origin1904-p.cxm.fifa.coml-afrique-avance-en-groupes-vers-qatar-2022 |access-date=2023-04-07 |website=www.fifa.com |language=fr}}</ref>

=== Third Round ===
{{main|2022 FIFA World Cup qualification – CAF third round}}
The matches are knockout round robin matches. The five winners of the confrontations are qualified for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup|2022 World Cup]] in Qatar. The draw for the third round, initially scheduled for 18 December takes place on 22 January 2022 in [[Douala]],<ref>{{Cite web |last=Football |first=CAF-Confedération Africaine du |title=Dates for FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022 African Qualifiers and TotalEnergies Africa Cup of Nations 2023 draws announced |url=https://www.cafonline.com/total-africa-cup-of-nations/news/dates-for-fifa-world-cup-qatar-2022-african-qualifiers-and-totalenergies-africa- |access-date=2023-04-07 |website=CAFOnline.com |language=en}}</ref> during the [[2021 Africa Cup of Nations]]. Mondher Kebaier is sacked following the elimination of Tunisia in the quarter of final at the African Cup of Nations and replaced by [[Jalel Kadri]].<ref>{{Cite web |last=Bouchet |first=Stevie G. |date=2022-02-02 |title=Mondher Kebaier limogé de son poste de sélectionneur de la Tunisie |url=https://www.foot-paul.com/coupe-du-monde-2022-news/kebaier-limoge-selectionneur-tunisie/ |access-date=2023-04-07 |website=foot-paul.com |language=fr-FR}}</ref> In the first leg at the [[Stade du 26 Mars]] in [[Bamako]], Tunisia won 1–0 thanks to an own goal by [[Moussa Sissako]] on pressure from Msakni.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Mali vs. Tunisia - 25 March 2022 - Soccerway |url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2022/03/25/africa/wc-qualifying-africa/mali/tunisia/3737782/ |access-date=2023-04-07 |website=int.soccerway.com}}</ref> As for the return match at the Hammadi Agrebi Stadium, in front of 50,000 spectators, it ended in a draw 0–0,<ref>{{Cite web |title=Tunisia vs. Mali - 29 March 2022 - Soccerway |url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2022/03/29/africa/wc-qualifying-africa/tunisia/mali/3737787/ |access-date=2023-04-07 |website=int.soccerway.com}}</ref> so that the team qualified for the World Cup for the sixth time in its history.<ref>{{Cite web |title=La Tunisie à la Coupe du monde 2022 - Aigles de Carthage |url=https://www.foot-paul.com/coupe-du-monde-2022/equipes/tunisie/ |access-date=2023-04-07 |website=foot-paul.com |language=fr-FR}}</ref>

== Preparation ==

=== Draw ===
The draw for the final phase of the 2022 World Cup takes place on 1 April 2022 at the Doha Exhibition Centre. Tunisia are assigned to Pot 3 and could be drawn against defending champions Brazil, France, Germany or hosts Qatar. Tunisia meet [[France national football team|France]], [[Denmark national football team|Denmark]] and [[Australia men's national soccer team|Australia]] in [[2022 FIFA World Cup Group D|Group D]].

The Tunisians haven't scored against any of these teams in their previous World Cup appearances. There have been four friendlies against France, two draws and two losses, and the Tunisians have faced the France B team twice at the Mediterranean Games, winning and losing once. They lost to Denmark at the [[Football at the 1960 Summer Olympics|1960 Summer Olympics]] and in preparation for the [[2002 FIFA World Cup|2002 World Cup]]; they also lost to Australia in a friendly match and won the [[2005 FIFA Confederations Cup|2005 Confederations Cup]].

{{main|2022 FIFA World Cup Group D}}
[[File:Stade_Education_City1.jpg|thumb|Tunisia v Denmark.]]
In the first match in [[2022 FIFA World Cup Group D|Group D]], [[Mohamed Dräger]] threatens the opponent's goal, then [[Issam Jebali]] dominates [[Kasper Schmeichel]], but the situation is prevented by an offside. In the 43rd minute, Jebali comes face to face with Schmeichel after hitting the goal and tries to beat the goalkeeper with a through shot, but he uses his thumbs and fends off the finish.<ref>{{Cite web |date=22 November 2022 |title=📝 Danemark – Tunisie : les tops et les flops ! |url=https://www.onzemondial.com/coupe-du-monde-2022/danemark-tunisie-les-tops-et-les-flops-807212 |access-date=2022-12-26 |website=Onze Mondial |language=fr}}</ref> [[Christian Eriksen]] then makes an attempt beyond the goal line, but [[Aymen Dahmen]] is illustrated with a save.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Dia |first=Jules |date=5 November 2022 |title=Mondial 2022 : Aymen Dahmen sera le gardien n°1 de la Tunisie |url=https://sportnewsafrica.com/breve/mondial-2022-aymen-dahmen-sera-le-gardien-n1-de-la-tunisie/ |access-date=2022-12-26 |website=Sport News Africa |language=fr-FR}}</ref> From the corner that follows, [[Andreas Cornelius]] wastes an opportunity by finding himself alone at the far post, but his header only seals the structure of the goals. Due to the subsequent dominance over the ball, despite their best efforts, the Danes could not find a solution against the Tunisia defence, and the match ended in a goalless draw.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Résultat Danemark – Tunisie (0–0) la 1re journée de Coupe du Monde 2022 Qatar 22/11 |url=https://www.footmercato.net/live/6802407250390377002-danemark-vs-tunisie |access-date=2022-12-26 |website=Foot Mercato : Info Transferts Football – Actu Foot Transfert |language=fr}}</ref> Thanks to his performance in this match, [[Aïssa Laïdouni]] receives the man of the match award.<ref>{{Cite web |date=22 November 2022 |title=Tunisie-Danemark : Aissa Aidouni élu homme du match |url=https://news.gnet.tn/tunisie-danemark-aissa-aidouni-elu-homme-du-match/ |access-date=2022-12-26 |website=Gnet news |language=fr-FR}}</ref>
[[File:Al_Janoub_Stadium.jpg|thumb|Tunisia v Australia.]]
In the second match, the team are led to a 1–0 loss against [[Australia men's national soccer team|Australia]],<ref>{{Cite web |title=Résultat Tunisie – Australie (0–1) la 2e journée de Coupe du Monde 2022 Qatar 26/11 |url=https://www.footmercato.net/live/5106660749969941024-tunisie-vs-australie |access-date=2022-12-26 |website=Foot Mercato : Info Transferts Football – Actu Foot Transfert |language=fr}}</ref> with the technical framework and the players receiving criticism due to the weakness of the midfield and the attack,<ref>{{Cite web |last=Wane |first=Amy |date=26 November 2022 |title=Mondial 2022 : Jalel Kadri prudent contre l'Australie |url=https://sportnewsafrica.com/breve/mondial-2022-jalel-kadri-prudent-contre-laustralie/ |access-date=2022-12-26 |website=Sport News Africa |language=fr-FR}}</ref> which reduces the chances of Tunisia to qualify for the round of 16. In the final game against world champions [[France national football team|France]], [[Wahbi Khazri]] put Tunisia ahead in the 58th minute with a low shot to the bottom right corner.<ref>{{Cite web |date=30 November 2022 |title=Tunisia 1–0 France: World Cup 2022 – as it happened |url=https://www.theguardian.com/football/live/2022/nov/30/tunisia-v-france-world-cup-2022-live-score-updates |access-date=2022-12-26 |website=the Guardian |language=en}}</ref> At this stage, Tunisia is in a position to qualify in the group. However, two minutes later, Australia took the lead against [[Denmark national football team|Denmark]] in the other match, which sent Tunisia out of the knockout stage.<ref>{{Cite news |title=Tunisia upset France but fail to qualify |language=en-GB |work=BBC Sport |url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/63725314 |access-date=2022-12-26}}</ref> Captain Khazri wins the Man of the Match award.<ref>{{Cite web |last=NY |first=Yusra |date=30 November 2022 |title=Tunisie-France : Déclaration de l'homme du match Wahbi Khazri (Vidéo) |url=https://kapitalis.com/tunisie/2022/11/30/tunisie-france-declaration-de-lhomme-du-match-wahbi-khazri-video/ |access-date=2022-12-26 |website=Kapitalis |language=fr-FR}}</ref> This is Tunisia's first victory against a European team in the World Cup, and the team have collected the most points (four points) in the group stage since their first appearance in [[1978 FIFA World Cup]] (three points). In this context, [[Wahbi Khazri]] is retiring from international retirement,<ref>{{Cite web |title=Tunisie : Wahbi Khazri met un terme à sa carrière internationale |url=https://www.footmercato.net/a4577726844384497424-tunisie-wahbi-khazri-met-un-terme-a-sa-carriere-internationale |access-date=2022-12-03 |website=Foot Mercato : Info Transferts Football – Actu Foot Transfert |language=fr}}</ref> after 74 games in which he scored 25 goals.

====Tunisia vs France====
The two teams had faced each other four times, most recently in 2010 friendly, a 1–1 draw.

[[Wahbi Khazri]] put Tunisia into the lead in the 58th minute with a low shot to the bottom right corner. At that stage, Tunisia was in a position to qualify from the group. However, two minutes later [[Australia men's national soccer team|Australia]] went in front against [[Denmark national football team|Denmark]] in the other match taking place at the same time, which put Tunisia outside of the qualifying positions.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/football/live/2022/nov/30/tunisia-v-france-world-cup-2022-live-score-updates|title=Tunisia 1 France 0: As it happened|date=30 November 2022|work=Guardian|accessdate=1 December 2022}}</ref> In added time, [[Antoine Griezmann]] scored to seemingly make it 1–1 with a volley but the goal was ruled out by VAR for offside. Australia went on to beat Denmark, which meant that Tunisia finished third in the group and failed to get into the knockout stage for the sixth consecutive World Cup.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/63725314|title=Tunisia 1 France 0|date=30 November 2022|work=BBC Sport|accessdate=1 December 2022}}</ref>

== Controversies ==

=== Threatening to be excluded ===
At the end of October 2022, an internal dispute concerning the Minister of Youth and Sports, Kamel Deguiche, and the president of the [[Tunisian Football Federation|federation]], Wadie Jary, threatens Tunisia's participation in the World Cup.<ref>{{Cite web |date=28 October 2022 |title=Coupe du monde 2022 : la Tunisie menacée d'exclusion par la Fifa, l'Italie pourrait être repêchée |url=https://www.lefigaro.fr/sports/football/coupe-du-monde/coupe-du-monde-2022-la-tunisie-menacee-d-exclusion-par-la-fifa-l-italie-pourrait-etre-repechee-20221028 |access-date=2022-11-08 |website=LEFIGARO |language=fr}}</ref> Indeed, the two men are known to hate each other and the first wishes to dissolve the federation to separate from the second.<ref>{{Cite web |date=28 October 2022 |title=Coupe du Monde : La Tunisie menacée d’exclusion du Mondial ! |url=https://www.onzemondial.com/coupe-du-monde-2022/coupe-du-monde-la-tunisie-menacee-d-exclusion-du-mondial-803462 |access-date=2022-11-08 |website=Onze Mondial |language=fr}}</ref> [[FIFA]] systematically penalizes cases of interference in the world of football, it therefore warns the Tunisian federation: by means of a letter, it warns that it reserves the right to exclude Tunisia from the competition in case of taking power over the federation by Deguiche.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Tunisie – Mondial 2022 : les Aigles de Carthage privés de Coupe du monde ? – Jeune Afrique |url=https://www.jeuneafrique.com/1389003/societe/tunisie-mondial-2022-les-aigles-de-carthage-prives-de-coupe-du-monde/ |access-date=2022-11-08 |website=JeuneAfrique.com |language=fr-FR}}</ref>

=== Allegations of interference in the squad ===
Dozens of news websites in Tunisia reported that coach [[Jalel Kadri]] was subjected to pressure during the formation of the Tunisian team's squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup|2022 World Cup]], according to the player [[Saad Bguir]], who was excluded from the final list and who was on the initial list.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Quel gardien pour la sélection: La polémique qui n'aurait jamais dû avoir lieu ! |url=https://www.espacemanager.com/quel-gardien-pour-la-selection-la-polemique-qui-naurait-jamais-du-avoir-lieu.html |access-date=2022-11-17 |website=Espace Manager |language=fr}}</ref> He also announced his international retirement through a phone call on the TV program Stade Plus on Carthage Plus, until the president of the [[Tunisian Football Federation]], Wadie Jary, left his position.<ref>{{Cite web |date=15 November 2022 |title=Saâd Bguir : "Plus jamais de sélection jusqu'au départ d'Al Jari" |url=https://news.gnet.tn/saad-bguir-plus-jamais-de-selection-jusquau-depart-dal-jari/ |access-date=2022-11-17 |website=Gnet news |language=fr-FR}}</ref> It started when [[Bilel Ifa]] was excluded from the list a day before the official announcement, and was later brought back after fans outraged on social media. On 14 November 2022, hundreds of fans greeted the team bus in [[Doha]] upon their arrival, but the president of the [[Tunisian Football Federation|Federation]] was insulted in the worst terms.

Also, the selection of four goalkeepers was under pressure from the [[Tunisian Football Federation]] to meet the wishes of certain teams on the financial level, FIFA, since the [[2010 FIFA World Cup]], paying a subsidy to each. team, of which at least one player has been called up in each edition of the competition. On 28 November 2022, former national team player [[Issam Jemâa]] said on [[Radio IFM]],<ref>{{Cite web |title=Avant France – Tunisie, Issam Jemaa fait des révélations choquantes sur la liste des 26 {{!}} Goal.com |url=https://www.goal.com/fr/news/avant-france-tunisie-issam-jemaa-fait-des-revelations-choquantes-sur-la-liste-des-26/blte982ebbd3e84128a |access-date=2022-11-30 |website=www.goal.com}}</ref> that TFF officials had sacrificed one of the players to call four goalkeepers into the final list, as the choice was on [[Bilel Ifa]], who returned to the team after the anger of the fans, [[Taha Yassine Khenissi]], who was medically examined for two hours, so they could find him injured or [[Seifeddine Jaziri]], who was called up due of financial transactions between him and the brother of the president of the TFF, Wajih Jary. In the final, [[Saad Bguir]] was abandoned.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Boureda |first=Gabriel |date=15 November 2022 |title=CdM 2022 : Un scandale au sein de la sélection tunisienne dans le groupe de la France ? |url=https://www.score.fr/football/articles/cdm-2022-un-scandale-au-sein-de-la-selection-tunisienne-dans-le-groupe-de-la-france/ |access-date=2022-11-30 |website=Score.fr |language=fr}}</ref>
