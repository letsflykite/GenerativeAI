'''Cody Mathès Gakpo''' (born 7 May 1999) is a Dutch professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Forward (association football)|forward]] for {{English football updater|Liverpoo}} club [[Liverpool F.C.|Liverpool]] and the [[Netherlands national football team|Netherlands national team]].
 
A [[PSV Eindhoven|PSV]] academy graduate, Gakpo made his first-team debut in February 2018. In the 2021–22 season, he won the [[Dutch Footballer of the Year]] award after scoring 21 goals in 47 games in all competitions. He signed for Liverpool in January 2023.

Gakpo played youth international football for the Netherlands from [[Netherlands national under-18 football team|under-18]] to [[Netherlands national under-21 football team|under-21]] level. He made his senior international debut in June 2021 at [[UEFA Euro 2020]].

==Early life and youth ==
Cody Mathès Gakpo<ref name="FIFA">{{cite web |url=https://fdp.fifa.org/assetspublic/ce44/pdf/SquadLists-English.pdf |title=Squad List: FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022: Netherlands (NED) |publisher=FIFA |page=20 |date=18 December 2022 |access-date=1 January 2023}}</ref> was born on 7 May 1999<ref name="FIFA"/> in [[Eindhoven]] and raised in the district of Stratum.<ref name="vi1">{{cite news |last1=Boeringa |first1=Reon |title=Cody Gakpo: van Stratum naar de spotlights |url=https://www.vi.nl/pro/overig/cody-gakpo-van-stratum-naar-de-spotlights/share/b03f3d1df7 |website=Voetbal International |date=9 October 2019 |language=nl}}</ref> His father was born in Togo and has Ghanaian ancestry, while his mother is Dutch.<ref name="ed1">{{cite news |last1=Elfrink |first1=Rik |title=Cody Gakpo leeft de droom van bijna elke Eindhovense jongen |url=https://www.ed.nl/psv/cody-gakpo-leeft-de-droom-van-bijna-elke-eindhovense-jongen~af693eb3/ |newspaper=Eindhovens Dagblad |date=1 June 2019 |language=nl}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Ghana coach reveals talks with Dutch players |url=https://www.football-oranje.com/ghana-coach-reveals-talks-with-dutch-players/ |website=Football Oranje |date=17 October 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=EXCLUSIVE: Ghanaian sensation Cody Gakpo extends contract with Dutch giants PSV Eindhoven |url=https://www.kickgh.com/more/ghanaians-abroad/7973-exclusive-ghanaian-sensation-cody-gakpo-extends-contract-with-dutch-giants-psv-eindhoven |website=Kickgh.com |date=12 September 2019}}</ref> In 2007, he moved to the youth academy of [[PSV Eindhoven|PSV]], where he then progressed through all of the youth teams.<ref name="ed1" />

==Club career==
===PSV===
====Jong PSV====
For the 2016–17 season Gakpo was part of the reserve team [[Jong PSV]] for the first time, but mainly featured in the U19 team. Gakpo made his professional debut in the [[Eerste Divisie]] for Jong PSV on 4 November 2016 in a game against [[Helmond Sport]].<ref>{{cite web |website=worldfootball.net |url=http://www.worldfootball.net/report/eerste-divisie-2016-2017-helmond-sport-jong-psv/ |title=Game Report |date=4 November 2016}}</ref> In the rest of the season he only made one more appearance for Jong PSV. After his production increased in the following season's U19 Eredivisie with seven goals and five assists in 13 league games, he was finally promoted to the reserves at the turn of the year. In his second appearance in the second-tier Eerste Divisie, on 19 January 2017 in a 3–2 away win against [[De Graafschap]], he scored a brace and made the assist for the third goal of his team.<ref>{{cite web |title=Gakpo bezorgt Jong PSV driepunter |url=https://www.psv.nl/nieuwspagina/artikel/gakpo-bezorgt-jong-psv-driepunter.htm |publisher=PSV |language=nl |date=19 January 2018}}</ref> For Jong PSV, he managed to score seven goals in the 2017–18 season, which he achieved in 12 league games.<ref name="wf">{{cite web |title=Cody Gakpo &raquo; Club matches |url=https://www.worldfootball.net/player_summary/cody-gakpo/2/ |website=worldfootball.net |access-date=17 August 2021}}</ref>

====First team====
Gakpo made his first team debut for PSV as an injury-time substitute in a 3–1 victory over Feyenoord on 25 February 2018.<ref>{{cite web |title=Geboren Eindhovenaar Cody Gakpo maakt officiële debuut in eerste elftal |url=https://psv.supporters.nl/nieuws/artikel/43977/Geboren-Eindhovenaar-Cody-Gakpo-maakt-offici%C3%ABle-debuut-in-eerste-elftal |website=PSV.supporters.nl |date=25 February 2018|access-date=9 August 2022}}</ref> In the 2018–19 season, Gakpo was able to further improve his performances. This was also noticed by first team coach, [[Mark van Bommel]]. He was selected several times for the matchday squad of the first team in the first half of the season. He would initially, however, remain an unused substitute.<ref name="wf" /> In the 5–1 home win against the [[Go Ahead Eagles]] on 3 December 2018, he scored the first hat-trick of his professional career.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Elfrink |first1=Rik |title=Jong PSV maakt met grootse Gakpo schroot van de aspiraties van Go Ahead Eagles |url=https://www.ed.nl/psv/jong-psv-maakt-met-grootse-gakpo-schroot-van-de-aspiraties-van-go-ahead-eagles~a430b9b3/ |newspaper=Eindhovens Dagblad |date=3 December 2018 |language=nl}}</ref> After he scored a goal and got an assist in the 5–2 home win against [[Almere City FC|Almere City]] on 21 December,<ref>{{cite news |last1=Velthoven |first1=Florian van |title=Gakpo en Piroe zijn nieuwe groeibriljantjes van PSV: 'Hoofdmacht halen is realistischer geworden' |url=https://www.omroepbrabant.nl/nieuws/2906328/gakpo-en-piroe-zijn-nieuwe-groeibriljantjes-van-psv-hoofdmacht-halen-is-realistischer-geworden |publisher=Omroep Brabant |date=21 December 2018 |language=nl}}</ref> he made his debut in the [[Eredivisie]] just one day later. In the 3–1 home win against [[AZ Alkmaar|AZ]], he was substituted on for [[Steven Bergwijn]] in the closing stages.<ref>{{cite web |title=Dutch Eredivisie |url=https://www.skysports.com/football/psv-vs-az-alkmaar/390340 |website=Sky Sports |access-date=17 August 2021}}</ref> He scored nine goals in ten league games for Jong PSV by the end of the year and was subsequently promoted to become a regular member of the first team.<ref name="wf" /> On 3 February 2019, he scored his first league goal in his second appearance in a 5–0 home win over [[Fortuna Sittard]] and also assisted another goal.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Beek |first1=Yvonne van |title=Aanvoerder De Jong trots op honderdste doelpunt: 'Fantastisch in vijf seizoenen PSV' |url=https://www.ed.nl/psv/aanvoerder-de-jong-trots-op-honderdste-doelpunt-fantastisch-in-vijf-seizoenen-psv~a6f9eec8/ |newspaper=Eindhovens Dagblad |date=3 February 2019 |language=nl}}</ref> By the end of the season he made 14 league appearances, but without being able to record another goal.<ref name="wf" />

In the 2019–20 season, he made his breakthrough in the Eredivisie and recorded seven goals and as many assists in 25 league games.<ref name="wf" />

On 13 September 2020, the first matchday of the [[2020–21 Eredivisie|2020–21 Eredivisie season]], Gakpo scored his first brace for PSV, contributing to a 3–1 win over [[FC Groningen|Groningen]].<ref>{{cite news |last1=Elfrink |first1=Rik |title=PSV pakt belangrijke uitzege in Groningen, met dank aan Cody Gakpo |url=https://www.bd.nl/psv/psv-pakt-belangrijke-uitzege-in-groningen-met-dank-aan-cody-gakpo~a4abfaf5/ |newspaper=Brabants Dagblad |date=13 September 2020 |language=nl}}</ref> He scored another brace on 24 September in the 5–1 victory in the [[UEFA Europa League]] qualifier against [[NŠ Mura]], allowing PSV to qualify for the play-off round.<ref>{{cite web |title=PSV cruise to a 5–1 win over NS Mura |url=https://www.psv.nl/english-psv/news/article/psv-cruise-to-a-5-1-win-over-ns-mura.htm |publisher=PSV |date=24 September 2020}}</ref> He was again decisive there, on scoring PSV's second goal in a 2–0 win over [[Rosenborg BK|Rosenborg]] on 1 October.<ref>{{cite web |title=Rosenborg BK – PSV |url=https://www.psv.nl/psv/wedstrijden/wedstrijd/rosenborg-bk-psv-01-10-2020.htm |publisher=PSV |language=nl |date=26 August 2014}}</ref> He finished the season with 29 appearances, in which he scored 11 goals.<ref name="wf" />

Gakpo scored the winning goal for PSV in a 2–1 win over [[AFC Ajax|Ajax]] in the [[2022 KNVB Cup Final]].<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://us.soccerway.com/matches/2022/04/17/netherlands/knvb-beker/psv-nv/afc-ajax/3761296/ |title=PSV VS. AJAX 2–1 |website=Soccerway |publisher=Perform Group |access-date=29 May 2022}}</ref> On 31 August 2022, he scored his first hat-trick in a 7–1 win over [[FC Volendam|Volendam]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.football-oranje.com/gakpo-stars-as-psv-hit-seven-past-volendam/ |title=Gakpo stars as PSV hit seven past Volendam |website=Football Oranje |date=31 August 2022}}</ref>

===Liverpool===
On 28 December 2022, Gakpo agreed to sign for [[Premier League]] club [[Liverpool F.C.|Liverpool]] once the transfer window opened on 1 January 2023.<ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.liverpoolfc.com/news/liverpool-reach-agreement-cody-gakpo-transfer |title=Liverpool reach agreement for Cody Gakpo transfer |publisher=Liverpool F.C. |date=28 December 2022 |access-date=28 December 2022}}</ref> He was reported by [[BBC Sport]] to have agreed a five-and-a-half-year contract, for a [[transfer fee]] of between £35.4 to £44.3&nbsp;million (€40 to €50&nbsp;million), which would be a record fee received by PSV.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/64104472 |title=Cody Gakpo: Liverpool reach agreement with PSV Eindhoven to sign forward |website=BBC Sport |date=28 December 2022 |access-date=28 December 2022}}</ref> He made his debut for the club as a starter in a 2–2 third-round [[FA Cup]] tie at [[Anfield]] against [[Wolverhampton Wanderers F.C.| Wolves]] on 7 January 2023.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/64168002|title=Liverpool 2-2 Wolverhampton Wanderers|last=Matthews|first=Callum|website=[[BBC Sport]]|date=7 January 2023|access-date=7 January 2023}}</ref> Gakpo scored his first goal for Liverpool in a 2–0 [[Merseyside derby]] league victory over [[Everton F.C.|Everton]] on 13 February.<ref>{{cite web |title=Liverpool 2–0 Everton |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/64533611 |website=[[BBC Sport]] |access-date=14 February 2023 |date=13 February 2023}}</ref>  He scored a brace of goals against Liverpool's [[Liverpool F.C.–Manchester United F.C. rivalry|arch rivals]] Manchester United on 5 March 2023 in a 7–0 victory.<ref>{{Citation |title=Liverpool hit SEVEN past Man United {{!}} Liverpool 7-0 Manchester United {{!}} Premier League Highlights |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Ih_HOGFO5tw |access-date=2023-03-05 |language=en}}</ref>

==International career==
[[File:Austria U-18 vs. Netherlands U-18 2017-03-23 (086).jpg|thumb|upright|Gakpo playing for the [[Netherlands national under-18 football team|Netherlands national under-18 team]] in 2017]]

Gakpo was eligible to play for the Netherlands, Ghana or Togo at international level.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.dailymailgh.com/netherlands-coach-eyes-ghanaian-youngster-gakpo/ |title=Netherlands coach eyes Ghanaian youngster Gakpo |website=Dailymailgh |date=14 August 2019|access-date=9 August 2022}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.ghanaweb.com/GhanaHomePage/SportsArchive/Ghanaian-striker-Cody-Gakpo-delighted-to-sign-new-long-term-deal-with-PSV-Eindhoven-1142882 |title=Ghanaian striker Cody Gakpo delighted to sign new long-term deal with PSV Eindhoven |website=GhanaWeb |date=29 December 2020|access-date=9 August 2022}}</ref> He played youth international football for the Netherlands at [[Netherlands national under-18 football team|under-18]],<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.onsoranje.nl/teams/185190/speler/362 |title=Cody Gakpo Jeugd mannen onder 18 |publisher=Royal Dutch Football Association |access-date=9 August 2022}}</ref> [[Netherlands national under-19 football team|under-19]],<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.onsoranje.nl/teams/185189/speler/362 |title=Cody Gakpo Jeugd mannen onder 19 |publisher=Royal Dutch Football Association |access-date=9 August 2022}}</ref> [[Netherlands national under-20 football team|under-20]]<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.onsoranje.nl/teams/206598/speler/362 |title=Cody Gakpo Jeugd mannen beloftenelftal |publisher=Royal Dutch Football Association |access-date=9 August 2022}}</ref> and [[Netherlands national under-21 football team|under-21]] levels.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.onsoranje.nl/teams/185185/speler/362 |title=Cody Gakpo Jong Oranje |publisher=Royal Dutch Football Association |access-date=9 August 2022}}</ref>

Gakpo was called up to the senior [[Netherlands national football team|Netherlands]] squad for [[UEFA Euro 2020]] and made his debut in the third match of the group stage against [[North Macedonia national football team|North Macedonia]] as a substitute in the 79th minute for [[Frenkie de Jong]].<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.onsoranje.nl/nieuws/nederlands-elftal/79017/frank-de-boer-maakt-ek-selectie-bekend |title=Frank de Boer maakt EK-selectie bekend |publisher=Royal Dutch Football Association |date=26 May 2021 |access-date=26 May 2021 |language=nl}}</ref> Thereby, he became the first Netherlands player to make his international debut at a European Championship since [[Martien Vreijsen]] in [[UEFA Euro 1980|1980]].<ref>{{cite news |title=Gakpo schrijft historie met Oranje-debuut |url=https://www.vi.nl/nieuws/gakpo-schrijft-historie-met-oranje-debuut |website=Voetbal International |date=21 June 2021 |language=nl}}</ref>

Gakpo was named in the squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] in Qatar and had an immediate impact, scoring in three consecutive group matches against [[Senegal national football team|Senegal]],<ref>{{Cite news |title=Late goals give Dutch victory over Senegal |website=BBC Sport |url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/63619381 |access-date=29 November 2022}}</ref> [[Ecuador national football team|Ecuador]],<ref>{{Cite news |title=Valencia scores as Ecuador draw with Netherlands |website=BBC Sport |url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/63672202 |access-date=29 November 2022}}</ref> and [[Qatar national football team|Qatar]].<ref>{{Cite news |title=Gakpo nets again as Dutch beat Qatar to top group |website=BBC Sport |url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/63711993 |access-date=29 November 2022}}</ref>

==Style of play==
Usually deployed on the [[Winger (association football)|left wing]], Gakpo often cuts inside on his right foot to move to a more central attacking position, and uses his speed and [[Dribbling (association football)|dribbling]] skills to take on defenders until he finds the space to make an attempt on goal.<ref name="vi1" />

==Personal life==
Gakpo is a [[Pentecostalism|Pentecostal]] Christian.<ref>{{cite news |date=8 December 2022 |title=Cody Gakpo: ‘I try to transfer the lessons from my church to others as best I can’ |language=en-us |work=Netherlands Posts English |url=https://netherlands.postsen.com/sports/112754/Cody-Gakpo-%E2%80%98I-try-to-transfer-the-lessons-from-my-church-to-others-as-best-I-can%E2%80%99.html |access-date=23 February 2023}}</ref> He has said, "I try to read the Bible every day, I pray every day, I like to go to church, and I read many books about the faith".<ref>{{cite web |last1=Wise |first1=Talia |title=Dutch Stars Depay, Gakpo Stand for Christ During FIFA World Cup: 'We Are Thankful to God' |date=9 December 2022 |url=https://www1.cbn.com/cbnnews/world/2022/december/dutch-stars-depay-gakpo-stand-for-christ-during-fifa-world-cup-we-are-thankful-to-god |access-date=29 December 2022}}</ref> During the 2022 FIFA World Cup, along with [[Memphis Depay]], he led a [[Bible study (Christianity)|Bible study]] for fifteen other Dutch teammates.<ref>{{cite news |date=9 December 2022 |title=Dutch Stars Depay, Gakpo Stand for Christ During FIFA World Cup: 'We Are Thankful to God' |url=https://www1.cbn.com/cbnnews/world/2022/december/dutch-stars-depay-gakpo-stand-for-christ-during-fifa-world-cup-we-are-thankful-to-god |access-date=27 March 2023 |website=CBN News}}</ref>
