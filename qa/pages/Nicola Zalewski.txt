'''Nicola Zalewski''' (born 23 January 2002) is a professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[midfielder]] for [[Serie A]] club [[A.S. Roma|Roma]]. Born and raised in Italy to Polish parents, he plays for the [[Poland national football team|Poland national team]] at international level.<ref>{{Soccerway|id=nicola-zalewski/572283|accessdate=}}</ref>

== Club career ==
Zalewski made his professional debut for [[A.S. Roma|Roma]] on 6 May 2021 in the [[2020–21 UEFA Europa League|Europa League]] [[2020–21 UEFA Europa League knockout phase|semi-final]] against [[Manchester United F.C.|Manchester United]], replacing [[Pedro (footballer, born 1987)|Pedro]] at the 76th minute and being instrumental to the winning goal, an own goal by [[Alex Telles]], seven minutes later.<ref>{{Cite web|date=6 May 2021|title=Cronaca Roma - Manchester Utd|url=https://corrieredellosport.it/live/partita/roma-manchester-utd-2197769/|url-status=live|access-date=6 May 2021|website=[[Corriere dello Sport]]|language=it}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=6 May 2021|title=Roma 3-2 Manchester United (agg: 5-8): Red Devils ease into final|url=https://www.uefa.com/uefaeuropaleague/match/2029907--roma-vs-man-united/postmatch/report/|url-status=live|access-date=9 May 2021|website=[[UEFA]].com}}</ref> He made his [[Serie A]] debut three days later, on 9 May, during a home game against [[F.C. Crotone|Crotone]].<ref>{{Cite web|date=9 May 2021|title=Roma-Crotone, problemi fisici per Darboe: Bove esordisce in prima squadra|url=https://www.ilromanista.eu/news/as-roma/91489/roma-crotone-problemi-fisici-per-darboe-bove-esordisce-in-prima-squadra|url-status=live|access-date=9 May 2021|website=Il Romanista|language=it}}</ref> On 29 December, Roma announced his contract had been extended until 2025.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Bove, Darboe e Zalewski rinnovano il proprio contratto con la Roma |url=https://www.asroma.com/it/notizie/2021/12/Bove-Darboe-e-Zalewski-rinnovano-il-proprio-contratto-con-la-Roma |access-date=28 April 2022 |website=www.asroma.com |language=it}}</ref> On 28 February 2022, he first played as a starter for Roma in their 1–0 against [[Spezia Calcio|Spezia]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Serie A, Spezia-Roma 0-1: giallorossi passano su rigore al 99' |url=https://www.sportmediaset.mediaset.it/calcio/roma/serie-a-spezia-roma-0-1-giallorossi-passano-su-rigore-al-99_46653274-202202k.shtml |access-date=28 April 2022 |website=Sportmediaset.it |language=IT}}</ref>

== International career ==
Zalewski, while born in Tivoli, [[Italian nationality law|Italy]], doesn't hold an Italian citizenship.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ojdxe8dGbzQ | title=Zalewski O Mourinho, Michniewiczu I Mundialu. "Włosi Chcieli Mnie W Swojej Kadrze" | website=[[YouTube]] }}</ref> He was first selected by the youth selections of the [[Poland national football team|Poland national team]] after being discovered by [[Zbigniew Boniek]], head of the [[Polish Football Association]] and former Roma player.<ref name="bio1">{{cite web |url=https://gianlucadimarzio.com/it/roma-chi-%C3%A8-nicola-Zalewski |language=Italian |accessdate=6 May 2021 |date=5 October 2019 |title=Roma, chi è Nicola Zalewski: polacco di Tivoli tifoso giallorosso |publisher=GianlucaDiMarzio.com}}</ref> He made his debut for the senior team on 5 September 2021, in the [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification (UEFA)|World Cup Qualifier]] against [[San Marino national football team|San Marino]] where he came on in 66th minute and assisted [[Adam Buksa]] in the 94th minute who made it 7–1 for Poland.<ref>{{cite web | url=https://www.uefa.com/european-qualifiers/match/2030879/ | title=San Marino-Poland &#124; European Qualifiers 2022 }}</ref>

== Personal life ==
Zalewski was born in [[Tivoli, Lazio|Tivoli]] to [[Poland|Polish]] parents and grew up in [[Poli, Lazio|Poli]].<ref name="bio1" /> He has a sister, Jessica, born in 1992.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Chi è Zalewski: alle origini del talento della Roma |url=https://corrieredellosport.it/news/calcio/serie-a/roma/2022/05/04-92469527/chi_zalewski_alle_origini_del_talento_della_roma |access-date=2023-02-02 |website=corrieredellosport.it |date=4 May 2022 |language=it}}</ref> His father, Krzysztof Zalewski, died of cancer on 24 September 2021.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.meczyki.pl/newsy/tragedia-reprezentanta-polski-zmarl-jego-ojciec-kilka-dni-temu-byl-najszczesliwszym-czlowiekiem-na-ziemi/174123-n|title=Tragedia reprezentanta Polski, zmarł jego ojciec.
