'''Nawaf Shaker Fayrouz Al-Abed''' ({{lang-ar|نواف شاكر فيروز العابد}}; born 26 January 1990) is a Saudi Arabian professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a winger for the [[Saudi Arabia national football team|Saudi Arabia national team]].

==Club career==
===Al-Hilal===

In 2009, Al-Hilal signed in Al-Abed from their youth academy. On 7 November, Nawaf scored the fastest goal in professional football against [[Al-Shoulla F.C.|Al-Shoulla]] in two seconds in the [[Prince Faisal bin Fahd League U-21|Prince Faisal U-21 Cup]],<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://latimesblogs.latimes.com/sports_blog/2009/11/soccer-fastest-goal-in-history.html|title=Nawaf Al Abed scores the fastest goal in soccer history -- two seconds!|date=9 November 2009}}</ref> but the match was cancelled due to Al-Hilal making 6 player which are more than 21 years old play in the match.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.aleqt.com/2009/11/11/article_299845.html|title=سحب نقاط الهلال .. و"فيفا" يرفض الاعتراف بالهدف الأسرع|date=11 November 2009|website=صحيفة الاقتصادية}}</ref>

On 9 March 2017, Al-Abed scored a third penalty goal against [[Ittihad FC|Ittihad]] and performed a celebration which made Ittihad fans furious. The Saudi Arabia FA banned him for two games,<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://sabq.org/RJ84vk|title=إيقاف عابد "الهلال" مباراتين ومنع مولد "الاتحاد" من النهائي|website=صحيفة سبق الإلكترونية}}</ref> but Al-Hilal appealed and the ban was lifted.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.ajel.sa/sports/1852651 |title=لجنة الاستئناف تكشف أسباب إلغاء عقوبة نواف العابد &#124; صحيفة عاجل الإلكترونية |access-date=22 June 2017 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170611131325/http://www.ajel.sa/sports/1852651 |archive-date=11 June 2017 |url-status=dead }}</ref>

===Al-Shabab===
On 25 October 2020, Al-Abed joined [[Al Shabab FC (Riyadh)|Al-Shabab]] on a three-year contract.<ref>{{cite web |title=نواف العابد شبابيًّا لـ3 مواسم.. قادمًا من الهلال |url=https://sabq.org/prTdWy}}</ref>

On 18 April 2023, Al-Abed was released by Al-Shabab.<ref>{{cite web |title=الشباب ينهي عقد نواف العابد بالتراضي |url=https://www.alarabiya.net/sport/saudi-sport/2023/04/18/%D8%A7%D9%84%D8%B4%D8%A8%D8%A7%D8%A8-%D9%8A%D9%86%D9%87%D9%8A-%D8%B9%D9%82%D8%AF-%D9%86%D9%88%D8%A7%D9%81-%D8%A7%D9%84%D8%B9%D8%A7%D8%A8%D8%AF-%D8%A8%D8%A7%D9%84%D8%AA%D8%B1%D8%A7%D8%B6%D9%8A}}</ref>

==International career==
Al-Abed represented the [[Saudi Arabia national football team]] when his country hosted the [[2014 Gulf Cup of Nations]]. His first international goal came in the quarter-final match with [[Yemen national football team|Yemen]], becoming the winning goal as the Saudis prevailed 1–0.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.saudigazette.com.sa/index.cfm/index.cfm?method=home.regcon&contentid=20141121225114|title=Abed thunderbolt puts KSA in semis|date=21 November 2014|access-date=18 January 2015|work=Saudi Gazette|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150118105357/http://www.saudigazette.com.sa/index.cfm/index.cfm?method=home.regcon&contentid=20141121225114|archive-date=18 January 2015}}</ref> Nawaf scored again in the semi-final as Saudi Arabia defeated the [[United Arab Emirates national football team|United Arab Emirates]] 3–2. The Green Falcons eventually finished as runners-up to [[Qatar national football team|Qatar]], losing 2–1 in the final at the [[King Fahd International Stadium]].<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.gulfcup.sa/en/matches.php?id=16|title=Saudi Arabia 1-2 Qatar|access-date=18 January 2015|work=Asian Football Confederation}}</ref>

Al-Abed was included in Saudi Arabia's squad for the [[2015 AFC Asian Cup]], scoring his third international goal in a 4–1 defeat of [[North Korea national football team|North Korea]] in the team's second group match.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/sports/soccer/2015/01/14/saudi-arabia-eliminates-north-korea-4-1-at-asia-cup/21737935/|title=Saudi Arabia eliminates North Korea 4-1 at Asia Cup|date=14 January 2015|access-date=18 January 2015|work=USA Today}}</ref>

In May 2018 he was named in Saudi Arabia's preliminary [[Saudi Arabia national football team|squad]] for the [[2018 World Cup]] in Russia.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/440833652018|title=World Cup 2018: All the confirmed squads for this summer's finals in Russia}}</ref>
