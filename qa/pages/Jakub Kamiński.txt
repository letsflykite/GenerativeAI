'''Jakub Kamiński''' (born 5 June 2002) is a Polish professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays for [[Bundesliga]] club [[VfL Wolfsburg]] and the [[Poland national football team|Poland national team]] as a [[Forward (association football)#Winger|left winger]].<ref>[https://weszlo.com/2020/03/03/pilkarski-akrobata-lecha-poznan-chcial-rzucic-pilke-talent-rozblysnal/ Piłkarski akrobata z Lecha Poznań. Chciał rzucić piłkę, ale jego talent rozbłysnął] weszlo.com</ref><ref>{{soccerway|jakub-kamiski/574689}}</ref>

==Club career==
===Lech Poznań===
As a junior, he started his career in [[Szombierki Bytom]], before joining [[Lech Poznań]]'s academy in 2015.<ref>{{cite web |title=Lech Poznań pierwsza drużyna - Jakub Kamiński |url=https://www.lechpoznan.pl/lech-poznan-pierwsza-druzyna,11,jakub-kaminski,133,zawodnik.html |publisher=[[Lech Poznań]] |access-date=1 July 2022 |language=pl}}</ref> Climbing through the ranks with junior squads and the [[Lech Poznań II|reserve team]], he was first included in the first team roster in early 2019 by [[Adam Nawałka]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Debiutanci w Belek: Jakub Kamiński |url=https://www.lechpoznan.pl/aktualnosci,2,debiutanci-w-belek-jakub-kaminski,32390.html |publisher=Lech Poznań |access-date=1 July 2022 |language=pl |date=27 January 2019}}</ref>

He made his [[Ekstraklasa]] debut on 20 September 2019 in a 1–1 home draw against [[Jagiellonia Białystok]], in the starting line-up.<ref>{{cite web |title=Lech Poznań. Jakub Kamiński o debiucie: Wyszło OK |url=https://sport.interia.pl/klub-lech-poznan/news-lech-poznan-jakub-kaminski-o-debiucie-wyszlo-ok,nId,3215568 |website=interia.pl |access-date=1 July 2022 |language=pl |date=21 September 2019}}</ref> His first league goal came in a 3–3 away draw against [[Zagłębie Lubin]] on 6 June 2020. On 16 September 2020, he scored his first goal in European competitions against [[Hammarby Fotboll|Hammarby IF]] in a 0–3 [[2020–21 UEFA Europa League qualifying phase and play-off round|Europa League qualifying round]] away win. Following a successful [[2021–22 Lech Poznań season|2021–22 campaign]], which saw Lech win the championship on the club's 100th anniversary, he was voted Ekstraklasa's Young Player of the Season.<ref name=Awards>{{cite web |url=https://www.ekstraklasa.org/aktualnosci/3492 |title=Nagrodzono gwiazdy sezonu w PKO Bank Polski Ekstraklasie |publisher=Ekstraklasa |date=23 May 2022 |access-date=29 May 2022 |language=pl |archive-date=1 June 2022 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220601074930/https://www.ekstraklasa.org/aktualnosci/3492/ |url-status=dead }}</ref>

===Wolfsburg===
On 10 January 2022, Kamiński signed a five-year contract with [[Bundesliga]] club [[VfL Wolfsburg]], effective from 1 July 2022.<ref>{{cite web |title=Wolves sign Kaminski |url=https://www.vfl-wolfsburg.de/en/newsdetails/news-detail/detail/news/wolves-sign-kaminski |publisher=[[VfL Wolfsburg]] |access-date=1 July 2022 |language=en |date=10 January 2022}}</ref>

==International career==
He made his debut for the [[Poland national football team]] on 5 September 2021 in a [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification – UEFA Group I|World Cup qualifier]] against [[San Marino national football team|San Marino]], a 7–1 away victory. He started and played the whole match.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[FIFA]]|url=https://www.fifa.com/tournaments/mens/worldcup/qatar2022/qualifiers/uefa/match-center/400155539|title=San Marino v Poland game report|date=5 September 2021}}</ref>
