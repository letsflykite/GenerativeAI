'''Francisco Javier Calvo Quesada''' (born 8 July 1992) is a Costa Rican professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Defender (association football)#Centre Defender|centre-back]] for [[Konyaspor]] and  the [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica national team]].

==Early life==
After starting with [[Deportivo Saprissa]] as a youth player from 2005 to 2010, Calvo moved to the United States to study at [[San Jacinto College]] after getting a scholarship and played football at university level during that period.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.nacion.com/puro-deporte/futbol-nacional/francisco-calvo-se-abre-su-camino/VOGRIM65MNDIPNMB3JJTTFNOSY/story/|title=Francisco Calvo se abre su camino|website=Lanacion.com|access-date=8 February 2022}}</ref>

==Club career==
On his return to Costa Rica in 2011, Calvo played for [[C.S. Herediano|Herediano]] before a stint at [[A.D. Municipal Pérez Zeledón|Pérez Zeledón]] where he scored 5 goals in 21 matches.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://wvw.aldia.cr/nacion/futbol/unafut/2012/invierno/jugador_7_348.html |title=Francisco Calvo statistics |website= |access-date=8 February 2022 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140523012016/http://wvw.aldia.cr/nacion/futbol/unafut/2012/invierno/jugador_7_348.html |archive-date=23 May 2014 |url-status=dead}}</ref> In January 2013 he moved abroad to play for then Danish champions [[FC Nordsjælland|Nordsjælland]]<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.nacion.com/puro-deporte/futbol-internacional/francisco-calvo-firmo-con-el-campeon-danes/2XUYGHUZP5BGHO7JVZJI4POCJ4/story/|title=Francisco Calvo firmó con el campeón danés|website=Lanacion.com|access-date=8 February 2022}}</ref> but he returned to Herediano in summer 2013.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.nacion.com/puro-deporte/futbol-nacional/francisco-calvo-esta-cerca-de-regresar-al-herediano/NHUEZWKBIZDMRFMOZQQEN4XVG4/story/|title=Francisco Calvo está cerca de regresar al Herediano|website=Lanacion.com|access-date=8 February 2022}}</ref> In January 2015, Calvo joined [[Santos de Guápiles F.C.|Santos de Guápiles]] as a loan.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.nacion.com/puro-deporte/futbol-nacional/francisco-calvo-firmo-con-saprissa-a-partir-del-invierno-2015/MIJB5M5XNJHM3MRWXJ7XT5H4FQ/story/|title=Francisco Calvo firmó con Saprissa a partir del Invierno 2015|website=Lanacion.com|access-date=8 February 2022}}</ref>

In late May, Francisco joined Saprissa. In his first season with Saprissa, he appeared in 39 matches and scored five goals. The following season, he continued to be a set-piece threat as he scored 9 goals in 24 matches playing as a defender.

On 27 December 2016, Calvo joined [[Major League Soccer]] side [[Minnesota United FC]].<ref>{{cite web|title=MNUFC Signs Dynamic Defender Francisco Calvo |url=http://www.mnufc.com/post/2016/12/27/mnufc-signs-dynamic-defender-francisco-calvo |publisher=Minnesota United FC |date=27 December 2016 |access-date=27 December 2016}}</ref>
In 2018, Calvo was selected as an MLS All-Star, becoming the first Minnesota United player in club history to receive the honor.{{citation needed|date=January 2019}}

On 3 May 2019, Calvo was traded to [[Chicago Fire FC|Chicago Fire]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Minnesota United trades Francisco Calvo to Chicago for cash |url=http://www.startribune.com/minnesota-united-trades-francisco-calvo-to-chicago-for-cash/509431061/ |publisher=Star Tribune |date=3 May 2019 |access-date=3 May 2019}}</ref> Following the 2021 season, Calvo's contract option was declined by Chicago.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.chicagofirefc.com/news/chicago-fire-fc-announce-2021-roster-decisions|title=Chicago Fire FC Announce 2021 Roster Decisions &#124; Chicago Fire FC|website=Chicagofirefc.com}}</ref>

On 26 January 2022, Calvo signed as a free agent with [[San Jose Earthquakes]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sjearthquakes.com/news/news-earthquakes-sign-costa-rica-national-team-defender-francisco-calvo|title=NEWS: Earthquakes Sign Costa Rica National Team Defender Francisco Calvo &#124; San Jose Earthquakes|website=Sjearthquakes.com|access-date=8 February 2022}}</ref> On 5 March 2022, Calvo scored two late set piece goals for a ten-man San Jose team to salvage a 3–3 draw at home against the Columbus Crew. This performance led to him being named in the MLS Team of the Week.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sjearthquakes.com/news/news-francisco-calvo-selected-to-mls-team-of-the-week|title= NEWS: Francisco Calvo Selected to MLS Team of the Week|website=Sjearthquakes.com|access-date=8 March 2022}}</ref> He now plays at Konyaspor in the Turkish Süper Lig.

==International career==
On 29 May 2011, Calvo made his debut with the [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica senior team]] in [[San José, Costa Rica|San José]] in a friendly game against [[Nigeria national football team|Nigeria]].<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.chron.com/neighborhood/lakehouston/sports/article/San-Jac-star-plays-national-soccer-for-Costa-Rica-9376896.php|title=San Jac star plays national soccer for Costa Rica|website=[[Houston Chronicle]]|publisher=[[Hearst Communications]]|access-date=18 March 2023|date=22 June 2011}}</ref> He was also part of the Costa Rica squad at the [[2011 FIFA U-20 World Cup]].

Calvo was also a reserve call-up for Costa Rica at the [[2011 CONCACAF Gold Cup]],<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://fedefutbol.org/2011/05/30/una-nueva-figura-sustituira-a-gamboa/ |title=Una nueva figura sustituirá a Gamboa &#124; Federación Costarricense de Fútbol |access-date=10 July 2011 |archive-date=5 July 2011 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110705013810/http://fedefutbol.org/2011/05/30/una-nueva-figura-sustituira-a-gamboa/ |url-status=dead }}</ref> and later impressed senior head coach [[Ricardo La Volpe]] to win him a call-up for the Costa Rica squad at the [[2011 Copa América]]. He made his debut in the competition in a game against [[Colombia national football team|Colombia]], which ended in a 1–0 loss, and played in a 2–0 win against [[Bolivia national football team|Bolivia]].<ref>[http://yashinquesada.navegalo.com/index.php?option=com_content&view=article&id=8736:francisco-calvo-qse-viene-argentina-que-es-el-partido-mas-bonitos-para-nosotrosq&catid=81:la-noticia-del-dia&Itemid=173] {{Dead link|date=December 2019|bot=InternetArchiveBot|fix-attempted=yes}}</ref> also Francisco played [[Argentina national football team|Argentina]] in a 3–0 loss.

In May 2018, Calvo was named in Costa Rica’s 23-man squad for the [[2018 FIFA World Cup]] in Russia.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.goal.com/en/amp/news/costa-rica-names-23-man-world-cup-squad-featuring-keylor/1bw2iz8pkma5614bzushqyyo7g|title=Keylor Navas & six MLS players named in Costa Rica's 23-man World Cup squad|website=Goal.com|date=14 May 2018|access-date=24 March 2020}}</ref> He later reprised the same role at the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] in Qatar.
