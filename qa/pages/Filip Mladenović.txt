'''Filip Mladenović''' ({{Lang-sr-cyr|Филип Mлaдeнoвић}}; born 15 August 1991) is a Serbian professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Defender (association football)#Full-back|left-back]] for [[Super League Greece]] club [[Panathinaikos F.C.|Panathinaikos]], and the [[Serbia national football team|Serbia national team]].

==Club career==
===Borac Čačak===
He became a member of the [[Borac Čačak|Borac senior team]] in the [[2010–11 Serbian SuperLiga|2010–11]] season, making 18 league appearances that season. He played as [[left-back]].

===Red Star Belgrade===
Mladenović signed a four-year contract for [[Red Star Belgrade]] on 17 December 2011.<ref>[http://www.sportske.net/vest/domaci-fudbal/mladenovic-potpisao-za-zvezdu-60877.html] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304041752/http://www.sportske.net/vest/domaci-fudbal/mladenovic-potpisao-za-zvezdu-60877.html|date=4 March 2016}} Sportske: ''Mladenović potpisao za Zvezdu''. {{in lang|sr}} 17 December 2011</ref> During the summer of 2013, Red Star participated in the [[Uhrencup]] in [[Switzerland]], where they lost the final against [[FC Basel]] with a score of 2–1. Mladenović scored for Red Star and was voted best player of the game, for which he was supposed to get a watch as a prize, but he rejected it out of disappointment with the loss.<ref>[http://sport.blic.rs/Fudbal/Domaci-fudbal/236173/Mladenovic-Sudije-nas-pokrale-sat-namenjen-meni-neka-daju-njima] Blic Sport: N. Todorović. Blic Sport: ''Mladenović: Sudije nas pokrale, sat namenjen meni neka daju njima'' {{in lang|sr}} 9 July 2013. Retrieved 11 November 2015.</ref> By the end of 2013, Red Star's financial situation became very unstable as players and staff were not getting paid after several months of waiting. In spite of his frequent scoring and lanky frame, Mladenović was played mostly as a left back under coach [[Ricardo Sá Pinto]], who said that Mladenović's playing style reminded him of [[Fábio Coentrão]].<ref name="B92">{{cite web|url=http://www.b92.net/sport/vesti.php?yyyy=2013&mm=04&dd=10&nav_id=703851|author=B92|title=SP: Dejo kao Zizu, Filip = Fabio|date=10 April 2013|access-date=11 November 2015|author-link=B92}}</ref> An exodus began when Sá Pinto announced in an emotional press conference when he said that he could no longer work at Red Star due to instabilities in the team's administration. On 5 October 2013, Mladenović filed a request to have his contract terminated with Red Star.<ref>[http://www.novosti.rs/vesti/sport.294.html:461475-Mladenovic-povlaci-tuzbu-protiv-Zvezde] Novosti: ''Mladenović povlači tužbu protiv Zvezde?'' {{in lang|sr}} 25 October 2013. Retrieved 11 November 2015.</ref> The Arbitration Committee in the [[Football Association of Serbia|FSS]] ruled in Mladenović's favor on 23 November, giving him the status of a [[free agent]].<ref>[http://www.sportske.net/vest/domaci-fudbal/mladenovic-slobodan-ali-igra-za-zvezdu-155420.html] MIHAJLO VIDOJEVIĆ. Sportske: ''Mladenović slobodan ali igra za Zvezdu''. {{in lang|sr}} 23 November 2013. Retrieved 11 November 2015.</ref> After the ruling on Mladenović and [[Marko Vešović]], coach [[Slaviša Stojanovič]] took both players off of the first team, but legally were still members of Red Star until the winter 2013–14 transfer window.<ref>[http://www.sportske.net/vest/domaci-fudbal/zvezda-vesovic-i-mladenovic-otpisani-156390.html] DARKO MITROVIĆ. Sportske: ''Zvezda – Vešović i Mladenović otpisani''. {{in lang|sr}} 30 November 2013. Retrieved 11 November 2015.</ref>

===BATE Borisov===
On 23 February 2014, Serbian news portal ''[[B92]]'' reported that Mladenović signed for Belarusian team [[FC BATE Borisov|BATE Borisov]] with a three-year contract.<ref>[http://www.b92.net/sport/fudbal/vesti.php?yyyy=2014&mm=02&dd=23&nav_id=815613] B92: ''Filip Mladenović u BATE Borisovu''. {{in lang|sr}} 23 February 2014. Retrieved 11 November 2015.</ref> He made his debut for BATE in the [[2014 Belarusian Super Cup]] final against [[FC Minsk]], and went on to score a goal 20 minutes into the game.<ref>[http://www.sportske.net/vest/medjunarodni-fudbal/filip-mladenovic-doneo-trofej-bate-borisovu-170817.html] DARKO MITROVIĆ. Sportske: ''Filip Mladenović doneo trofej BATE Borisovu!'' {{in lang|sr}} 15 March 2014. Retrieved 11 November 2015.</ref> On 29 September 2015, Mladenović scored two goals for BATE in the [[2015–16 UEFA Champions League group stage#Group E|2015–16 UEFA Champions League Group E]] against [[A.S. Roma|Roma]].<ref>[https://www.theguardian.com/football/2015/sep/29/bate-borisov-roma-champions-league-match-report] ''[[The Guardian]]: Belarusians Bate Borisov survive Roma fightback to claim famous win.'' 29 September 2015. Retrieved 11 November 2015.</ref> Thanks to his breakthrough game, Mladenović was listed on UEFA.com's Champions League "Team of the Week" on 1 October 2015.<ref>[http://sport.blic.rs/Fudbal/Evropski-fudbal/280408/U-DRUSTVU-NAJBOLJIH-Mladenovic-u-timu-kola-Lige-sampiona] B. Vinulović. Blic: U DRUŠTVU NAJBOLJIH: Mladenović u timu kola Lige šampiona! {{in lang|sr}} 1 October 2015. Retrieved 11 November 2015.</ref> Later that month, [[AC Milan]] was reported to show interest with their coach [[Siniša Mihajlović]] having known Mladenović from coaching the Serbia national team.<ref>[http://www.mozzartsport.com/vesti/miha-zeli-srbina-u-timu-milan-nudi-2-000-000-evra-za-filipa-mladenovica/118488] Mozzart: ''Miha želi Srbina u timu: Milan nudi 2.000.000 evra za Filipa Mladenovića'' {{in lang|sr}} 19 October 2015. Retrieved 11 November 2015.</ref>

===1. FC Köln===
On 5 January 2016, Mladenović signed a three and a half-year contract with [[1. FC Köln]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=FC holt Filip Mladenovic|trans-title=FC signs Filip Mladenovic|language=de|date=5 January 2016|access-date=5 January 2016|publisher=[[1. FC Köln]]|url=https://www.fc-koeln.de/de/fc-info/news/detailseite/details/fc-holt-filip-mladenovic/}}</ref>

=== Legia Warsaw ===
On 1 July 2020, he signed a three-year contract with the Poland [[Ekstraklasa]] side [[Legia Warsaw]]. He scored his maiden for Legia on 14 August 2020 in his debut in a 6–1 [[Polish Cup]] match victory against [[GKS Bełchatów]].<ref>{{Cite web |last=Gawroński |first=Piotr |date=2020-08-14 |title=Debiut i pierwszy gol Filipa Mladenovicia |url=https://legia.net/news/debiut-i-pierwszy-gol-filipa-mladenovicia/78233 |access-date=2023-06-12 |website=legia.net |language=pl}}</ref> He scored his first league goal for Legia on 2 November 2020 in a 3–0 win against [[Warta Poznań]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Pierwszy ligowy gol Mladenovicia w Legii! {{!}} #WARLEG 0:3 {{!}} Filip Mladenović wpisał się na listę strzelców w #Ekstraklasa po raz pierwszy w barwach Legia Warszawa! #WARLEG 0:3 |url=https://hi-in.facebook.com/Ekstraklasa.org/videos/pierwszy-ligowy-gol-mladenovicia-w-legii-warleg-03/660100528022475/ |access-date=2023-06-12 |website=www.facebook.com |language=pl}}</ref> On 2 May 2023, he won the [[Polish Cup]], defeating [[Raków Częstochowa]] in the final at the [[Stadion Narodowy|National Stadium]], before being sanctioned by the Disciplinary Committee of the [[Polish Football Association]] on 5 May 2023 for post-match behavior with a three-month disqualification covering all games (including outside [[Poland]]) and a financial penalty of PLN 120,000.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Wąsowski |first=Maciej |date=2023-05-05 |title=Znamy kary dla zawodników za finał Pucharu Polski. Najmocniej ukarany Mladenović |url=https://weszlo.com/2023/05/05/komisja-dyscyplinarna-pzpn-filip-mladenovic-final-pucharu-polski-legia-rakow/ |access-date=2023-06-12 |website=weszlo.com |language=pl-PL}}</ref> His contract terminated after the 2022–23 season.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Już wiadomo, gdzie zagra Filip Mladenović. Wszystko potwierdzone |url=https://przegladsportowy.onet.pl/pilka-nozna/ekstraklasa/juz-wiadomo-gdzie-zagra-filip-mladenovic-wszystko-potwierdzone/5640mdl |access-date=2023-06-12 |website=Przegląd Sportowy Onet |language=pl}}</ref>

===Panathinaikos===
On 12 June 2023, Greek side [[Panathinaikos F.C.|Panathinaikos]] announced the signing of Mladenović on a two-year deal.<ref>{{Cite web |date=2023-06-12 |title=Στον Παναθηναϊκό ο Μλαντένοβιτς |url=https://www.pao.gr/transfers/ston-panathinaiko-o-mlantenovits/ |access-date=2023-06-12 |publisher=[[Panathinaikos F.C.|Panathinaikos]] |language=el}}</ref>

==International career==
Mladenović made his debut for the [[Serbia national football team]] under coach Siniša Mihajlović on 31 May 2012, in a 2–0 loss against [[France national football team|France]].<ref>[http://www.novosti.rs/vesti/sport.294.html:382700-Mladenovic-Ovo-mi-je-najlepsi-deo-zivota] Vecernje Novosti (Serbian): Mladenović: Ovo mi je najlepši deo života</ref>

In November 2022, he was selected in Serbia's squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] in Qatar.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://fss.rs/en/head-coach-dragan-stojkovic-announces-the-list-of-players-for-the-fifa-world-cup-2022-in-qatar/ |title=Head-coach Dragan Stojković announces the list of players for the FIFA World Cup 2022 in Qatar |publisher=[[Football Association of Serbia]] |date=11 November 2022 |access-date=11 November 2022}}</ref> He played in a group stage match against [[Brazil national football team|Brazil]].<ref>{{cite news |url= https://www.nbcnewyork.com/news/sports/world-cup-2022/richarlison-brings-brazil-to-life-in-the-second-half-defeats-serbia-2-0/3971175/ |title=Richarlison Brings Brazil to Life in the Second Half, Defeats Serbia 2-0 |first=Kristen |last=Conti |newspaper=NBCNew York |location=New York |date=24 November 2022 |access-date=7 December 2022}}</ref>
