'''Walid Cheddira''' ({{lang-ar|وليد شديرة}}; born 22 January 1998) is a professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[forward (association football)|forward]] for {{Italian football updater|Bari}} club [[S.S.C. Bari|Bari]]. Born in Italy, he plays for the [[Morocco national football team|Morocco national team]].

== Club career ==
Cheddira spent the first four seasons of his senior career in the lower, non-professional tiers of Italian football.<ref>{{TuttoCalciatori|Cheddira_Walid}}</ref>

On 10 July 2019, Cheddira signed a three-year contract with [[Serie A]] club [[Parma Calcio 1913|Parma]],<ref>{{cite press release|publisher=[[Parma Calcio 1913|Parma]]|url=http://parmacalcio1913.com/luca-zamparo-e-walid-cheddira-sono-giocatori-del-parma-emmanuele-matino-in-prestito-alla-cavese?lang=en|title=LUCA ZAMPARO AND WALID CHEDDIRA JOIN PARMA, EMMANUELE MATINO SIGNS FOR CAVESE ON-LOAN|date=10 July 2019}}</ref> who loaned him to [[S.S. Arezzo|Arezzo]] in [[Serie C]] on 22 July.<ref>{{cite press release |publisher=[[S.S. Arezzo|Arezzo]] |url=http://www.ssarezzo.it/2019/07/22/cheddira-e-madonna-vestono-lamaranto/ |title=Cheddira e Madonna vestono l'amaranto |date=22 July 2019 |language=Italian}}</ref> He made his professional Serie C debut on 25 August, as a starter in a game against [[Calcio Lecco 1912|Lecco]].<ref>{{cite web|publisher=Soccerway|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2019/08/25/italy/serie-c1/ac-arezzo/calcio-lecco-1912/3108756/|title=Arezzo v Lecco game report|date=25 August 2019}}</ref> After half a season, Parma sent Cheddira on loan to Lecco on 31 January 2020,<ref>{{cite press release|publisher=[[Calcio Lecco 1912|Lecco]]|url=http://www.calciolecco1912.com/cheddira-nuovo-giocatore-calcio-lecco-1912/|title=Comunicato Ufficiale: Walid Cheddira nuovo giocatore della Calcio Lecco 1912|date=31 January 2020|language=Italian}}</ref> and to [[Mantova 1911 S.S.D.|Mantova]] on 4 September.<ref>{{cite press release |publisher=[[Mantova 1911 S.S.D.|Mantova]] |url=https://www.mantova1911.club/notizia.php?id=206 |title=SI ARRICCHISCE L'ATTACCO BIANCOROSSO: WALID CHEDDIRA È UN NUOVO GIOCATORE DEL MANTOVA |date=4 September 2020 |language=Italian}}</ref>

On 18 July 2021, Cheddira joined [[S.S.C. Bari|Bari]] on loan,<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[S.S.C. Bari|Bari]]|url=https://www.sscalciobari.it/it/news/2042-benvenuto-walid-cheddira/|title=Benvenuto Walid Cheddira !|date=18 July 2021|language=it}}</ref> who made the deal permanent on 30 June 2022, signing a contract until June 2025.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=Bari|url=https://www.sscalciobari.it/it/news/3022-bentornato-walid-walo-cheddira/|title=Bentornato Walid 'Walo' Cheddira !|date=30 June 2022|access-date=30 June 2022|language=it}}</ref>

== International career ==
Cheddira was born in Italy and is of [[Moroccans in Italy|Moroccan]] descent.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Trentini |first=Narciso |date=29 May 2022 |title=Walid Chedira, il giovane calciatore marocchino che ha sfondato a Bari, in Italia |url=https://www.gossipitaliano.net/walid-chedira-il-giovane-calciatore-marocchino-che-ha-sfondato-a-bari-in-italia/ |access-date=8 August 2022 |website=Gossip Italiano |language=it}}</ref> He was called up to play for the [[Morocco national football team|Morocco national team]] for two [[Exhibition game|friendlies]] against [[Chile national football team|Chile]] and [[Paraguay national football team|Paraguay]] on 23 and 27 September 2022, respectively;<ref>{{Cite web |last=Franceschi |first=Giovanni De |date=13 September 2022 |title=Cheddira convocato dalla Nazionale sogna il Mondiale in Qatar "Un onore, una gioia immensa" |url=https://www.cronachemaceratesi.it/2022/09/13/cheddira-convocato-dalla-nazionale-sogna-il-mondiale-in-quatar-un-onore-una-gioia-immensa/1674727/ |access-date=17 September 2022 |website=Cronache Maceratesi |language=it}}</ref> he made his debut against Chile as a second-half substitute in a 2–0 win.<ref>{{Cite web |date=23 September 2022 |title=المنتخب الوطني يفوز وديا على تشيلي بهدفين نظيفين في أول اختبار لوليد الركراكي |url=https://www.cawalisse.com/171256.html |access-date=24 September 2022 |website=Cawalisse {{!}} كواليس اليوم |language=ar}}</ref>

On 10 November 2022, Cheddira was named in Morocco's 26-man squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] in Qatar.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Morocco World Cup 2022 squad: Who's in and who's out? |url=https://www.goal.com/en-bh/lists/morocco-2022-world-cup-squad-hakimi-ziyech-mazraoui-qatar/blta36257fc4feeb66a |access-date=10 November 2022 |website=Goal}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |date=10 November 2022 |title=Moroccan coach unveils list of 26 Atlas Lions in 2022 World Cup |url=https://en.hespress.com/52697-moroccan-coach-unveils-list-of-26-atlas-lions-in-2022-world-cup.html |access-date=10 November 2022 |website=HESPRESS English}}</ref> He made his competition debut as an 82nd-minute substitute in the round of 16 against [[Spain national football team|Spain]], a [[Penalty shoot-out (association football)|penalty shoot-out]] victory for Morocco following a 0–0 draw after extra time.<ref>{{Cite web |date=6 December 2022 |title=Morocco vs Spain, Live Score, Lineups, Head to Head, Match Timeline, Stats, Result, Venue, FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022 |url=https://www.indiatimes.com/news/sports/morocco-vs-spain-live-score-lineups-head-to-head-match-timeline-stats-result-venue-fifa-world-cup-qatar-2022-586500.html |access-date=10 December 2022 |website=Indiatimes.com}}</ref> In the quarter-finals against [[Portugal national football team|Portugal]], Cheddira came on in the 65th minute, and was sent off for two yellow cards in stoppage time. Morocco won 1–0.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Gleeson |first=Mark |date=10 December 2022 |title=Morocco write World Cup history as they reach semi-finals |url=https://www.reuters.com/lifestyle/sports/morocco-beat-portugal-go-through-world-cup-semi-final-2022-12-10/ |access-date=10 December 2022 |website=[[Reuters]]}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |date=10 December 2022 |title=Morocco vs Portugal |url=https://onefootball.com/en/match/2282202 |access-date=10 December 2022 |website=[[OneFootball]]}}</ref>
