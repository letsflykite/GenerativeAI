'''Axel Wilson Arthur Disasi Mhakinis Belho''' (born 11 March 1998) is a French professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Defender (association football)|defender]] for [[Ligue 1]] club [[AS Monaco FC|Monaco]] and the [[France national football team|France national team]].

==Club career==
=== Early career ===
A youth academy product of [[Paris FC]], Disasi made his professional debut for the club in a 1–0 loss to [[RC Lens|Lens]] on 11 December 2016.{{Citation needed|date=February 2023}} He was later transferred to [[Stade de Reims|Reims]] in 2016.{{Citation needed|date=February 2023}}

=== Monaco ===
On 7 August 2020, Disasi completed a transfer from Reims to [[AS Monaco FC|Monaco]] for a fee of €13 million. He signed a five-year contract with the club.<ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=7 August 2020|title=Former Arsenal target Disasi joins Monaco in €13m deal|url=https://www.goal.com/en/news/former-arsenal-target-disasi-joins-monaco-in-13m-deal/pdf5wh5c0mmx1n1eri9dgqdag|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210416132524/https://www.goal.com/en/news/former-arsenal-target-disasi-joins-monaco-in-13m-deal/pdf5wh5c0mmx1n1eri9dgqdag |archive-date=16 April 2021 |access-date=7 August 2020|website=goal.com}}</ref> On 23 August, Disasi made his debut in a [[Ligue 1]] match against his former club Reims. He scored a goal as the match ended 2-2.<ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=23 August 2020|title=Monaco salvage draw against Reims in Kovac's first game in charge|url=http://www.espn.com/soccer/report?gameId=573271|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200825001719/https://www.espn.com/soccer/report?gameId=573271 |archive-date=25 August 2020 |access-date=23 August 2020|website=ESPN}}</ref>

==International career==
Born in France, Disasi is of [[DR Congo|Congolese]] and Angolan descent<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.congo-discovery.net/2017/07/14/rdc-football-gros-plan-sur-les-disasi/|title = RDC – Football : Gros plan sur les DISASI}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.leparisien.fr/espace-premium/sports/le-paris-fc-lance-un-lyceen-de-17-ans-11-12-2015-5359917.php|title=Le Paris FC lance un lycéen de 17 ans|date=11 December 2015|publisher=}}</ref> and was called up to the [[DR Congo national under-20 football team|DR Congo U20s]] for the [[2017 Jeux de la Francophonie]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.adiac-congo.com/content/jeux-de-la-francophonie-eric-tshibasu-retient-vingt-et-un-leopards-football-u20-de-la-rdc|title=Jeux de la Francophonie : Éric Tshibasu retient vingt et un Léopards football U20 de la RDC - adiac-congo.com : toute l'actualité du Bassin du Congo|website=www.adiac-congo.com}}</ref> 

Disasi was a youth international for France, and on 14 November 2022, was called up to [[France national football team|France]]’s [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] squad, replacing the injured [[Presnel Kimpembe]].<ref>{{Cite web |date=2022-11-14 |title=Thuram, Disasi added to France World Cup squad |url=https://www.espn.co.uk/football/france-fra/story/4802847/marcus-thuram-and-axel-disasi-added-to-france-world-cup-squad-as-presnel-kimpembe-ruled-out |access-date=2022-11-14 |website=ESPN.com |language=en}}</ref> On 30 November, he made his debut in a 1–0 group stage loss to [[Tunisia national football team|Tunisia]], becoming the first French international debutant during a World Cup since 1966.<ref>{{Cite web |title=FIFA World Cup 2022: France lose to Tunisia to wrap up group as Deschamps tests Camavinga at left back |url=https://www.cbssports.com/soccer/news/fifa-world-cup-2022-france-lose-to-tunisia-to-wrap-up-group-as-deschamps-tests-camavinga-at-left-back/ |access-date=2022-12-01 |website=CBSSports.com |language=en}}</ref> Disasi would go on to play in France's 3–1 win over [[Poland national football team|Poland]] in the round of 16 on 4 December and the defeat on [[Penalty shoot-out (association football)|penalties]] following a 3–3 draw against [[Argentina national football team|Argentina]] in [[2022 FIFA World Cup final|the final]] on 18 December.<ref>{{Cite web |date=4 December 2022 |title=France vs. Poland |url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2022/12/04/world/world-cup/france/poland/3298977/ |access-date=15 January 2023 |website=Soccerway}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |date=18 December 2022 |title=Argentina vs. France |url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2022/12/18/world/world-cup/argentina/france/3298990/ |access-date=15 January 2023 |website=Soccerway}}</ref>
