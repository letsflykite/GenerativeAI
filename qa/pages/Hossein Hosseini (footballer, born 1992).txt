'''Seyed Hossein Hosseini''' ({{lang-fa|سید حسین حسینی}}; born 30 June 1992) is an Iranian professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Goalkeeper (association football)|goalkeeper]] for [[Persian Gulf Pro League]] club [[Esteghlal F.C.|Esteghlal]] and the [[Iran national football team|Iran national team]].

==Club career==

===2010–2012: Early career and debut with Bargh Shiraz===
Hosseini started his career with [[Bargh Shiraz]]. He mostly benched and made just two league appearances in two seasons.

===Esteghlal===
As summer 2012, Hosseini joined [[Esteghlal F.C.|Esteghlal]] signing a three-year contract.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://abieteh.com/%D8%AF%D8%B1%D9%88%D8%A7%D8%B2%D9%87%E2%80%8C%D8%A8%D8%A7%D9%86-%D8%AA%DB%8C%D9%85-%D9%85%D9%84%DB%8C-%D8%B2%DB%8C%D8%B1-22-%D8%B3%D8%A7%D9%84-%D8%A8%D9%87-%D8%A7%D8%B3%D8%AA%D9%82%D9%84%D8%A7%D9%84/ |title=دروازه‌بان تیم ملی زیر 22 سال به استقلال پیوست – سایت هواداران باشگاه استقلال تهران |trans-title= Iran U22 goalkeeper joined Esteghlal|publisher=Abieteh.com |date=20 June 2014 |accessdate=14 June 2015}}</ref> Later in an interview with [[Navad]], he revealed that he declined a huge offer from [[Persepolis F.C.|Persepolis]] to join the team he supported during his childhood.<ref name="Navad interview">{{cite web|url=http://90tv.ir/video/435944/گفتگو-با-حسین-حسینی-نود-25-دی |title=گفتگو با حسین حسینی |trans-title= Conversation with Hossein Hosseini |publisher=[[Navad]] |language=Persian|accessdate=16 January 2018 |date=16 January 2018}}</ref> This claim was later confirmed by [[Mohammad Rouyanian]] who was Persepolis' chairman at the time.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.varzesh3.com/news/1498102/تایید-پپیشنهاد-حسین-حسینی-از-پرسپولیس|title=تایید پیشنهاد حسین حسینی از پرسپولیس |trans-title= Hosseini's offer from Persepolis was confirmed|publisher=Varzesh3.com|language=Persian|accessdate=16 January 2018 |date=16 January 2018}}</ref>

On 9 July 2013, he made his debut in a friendly against [[FC Dynamo Kyiv|Dynamo Kyiv]] in which he conceded a goal as Esteghlal beat their opponent 2–1.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.khabaronline.ir/detail/302794/sport/premiere-league |title=استقلال 2- دینامو کیف 1/ آبی ها قهرمان جام یوفا را بردند/مجیدی گل زد |trans-title=Esteghlal 2- Dynamo Kyiv 1/The Blues beat the Uefa cup winners/ Majidi scored|language=Persian |publisher=Khabar Online |accessdate=27 December 2017 |date=9 July 2013}}</ref> His first competitive appearance for [[Esteghlal F.C.|Esteghlal]] was in [[2013–14 Hazfi Cup|Hazfi Cup]] against [[Caspian Qazvin F.C.|Caspian Qazvin]] on 10 December 2013.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.asriran.com/fa/news/308432/استقلال-کاسپين-را-برد-و-حریف-ملوان-شد |title=استقلال کاسپين را برد و حریف ملوان شد |language=Persian |publisher=Asriran.com |date=10 December 2013 |accessdate=14 July 2015}}</ref>

====2014–16: Loan to Malavan====
Hosseini joined [[Malavan F.C.|Malavan]] in summer 2014 to complete his mandatory military service.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.varzesh3.com/news.do?itemid=1148988 |title=درواز‌ه‌بان استقلال به ملوان پیوست &#124; ورزش 3 |publisher=Varzesh3.com |date= |accessdate=14 June 2015}}</ref> He made his professional debut in [[2014–15 Iran Pro League]] in 1–0 loss against [[Rah Ahan]] on 25 August 2014.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://iranleague.ir/MatchDetails/6291 |title=لیگ برتر : راه آهن سورينت – ملوان بندر انزلي |publisher=Iranleague.ir |date=25 August 2014 |accessdate=14 September 2015}}</ref>

====2016–17: Return to Esteghlal====
After finishing his mandatory service, Hosseini returned to Esteghlal<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.varzesh3.com/news/1331694/بازگشت-سید-حسین-حسینی-به-استقلال |title=بازگشت سید حسین حسینی به استقلال |language=Persian |publisher=Varzesh3.com |accessdate=27 December 2017 |date=9 July 2013}}</ref> as backup for [[Mehdi Rahmati]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.varzesh3.com/news/1334257/ترکیب-دروازه%E2%80%8Cبان%E2%80%8Cهای-استقلال-مشخص-شد |title=ترکیب دروازه‌بان‌های استقلال مشخص شد |language=Persian |publisher=Varzesh3.com |accessdate=9 January 2018 |date=17 June 2016}}</ref> After Rahmati was sidelined for Esteghlal's opening match of the [[Persian Gulf Pro League]] season against [[Naft Tehran F.C.|Naft Tehran]] due to injury, Hosseini started the match and made his season debut.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.varzesh3.com/news/1343864 |title=استقلال 1 – نفت 1؛ منصوریان از خودی‌ها خورد! |language=Persian |website=Varzesh3.com |accessdate=9 January 2018 |date=25 July 2016}}</ref> He finished the season with 8 matches.

[[File:Hosseini2017.jpg|thumb|200px|right|Hosseini in his record-breaking match against [[Saipa F.C.|Saipa]] in January 2018]]
At the beginning of the season [[Mehdi Rahmati]] was the first choice keeper, however after failing to win four games out of the first five matches of the season, manager [[Alireza Mansourian]] decided to start Hosseini ahead of Rahmati. Hosseini made his season debut in the league on 15 September against [[F.C. Pars Jonoubi Jam|Pars Jonoubi Jam]] in which he conceded 2 goals.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.varzesh3.com/news/1461290/پارس-جنوبی-2-استقلال-0؛-20011-نفر-علیه-منصوریان! |title=پارس جنوبی 2 – استقلال 20 September, in a league match against [[Zob Ahan Esfahan F.C.|Zob Ahan]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.varzesh3.com/news/1462456/کاپیتانی-جالب-حسینی-در-استقلال |title=کاپیتانی جالب حسینی در استقلال |language=Persian |publisher=Varzesh3.com |accessdate=9 January 2018 |date=20 September 2017}}</ref> He kept his first clean sheet of the season in a 0–0 draw against [[Foolad F.C.|Foolad]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.varzesh3.com/news/1467110/گزارش-زنده-استقلال-0-فولاد-0 |title= استقلال 0 ـ فولاد 0؛ بدون موقعیت بدون گل |language=Persian |publisher=Varzesh3.com |accessdate=31 December 2017 |date=13 October 2017}}</ref> After keeping a clean sheet on 5 January 2018, he surpassed [[Alireza Beiranvand]]'s record of 721 minutes without conceding a league goal in [[Persian Gulf Pro League]] in a 0–0 draw against [[Tractor Sazi F.C.|Tractor Sazi]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.varzesh3.com/news/1495532/گزارش-زنده-تراکتورسازی-0-استقلال-0 |title=تراکتور 0 – استقلال 0؛ همه چیز تحت تاثیر رکورد |language=Persian |publisher=Varzesh3.com |accessdate=5 January 2018 |date=5 January 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.varzesh3.com/news/1495537/حسینی-رکورد-بسته-نگه-داشتن-دروازه-را-شکست|title=حسینی رکورد بسته نگه داشتن دروازه را شکست|trans-title= Hosseini broke clean sheet record|publisher=Varzesh3.com|language=Persian|date=5 January 2018|accessdate=5 January 2018}}</ref> On 12 January, he surpassed the record for consecutive minutes in Iranian top division league set by [[Bahram Mavaddat]] in 1976 by 28 minutes against [[Saipa F.C.|Saipa]];<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.varzesh3.com/news/1497037/گزارش-بازی-استقلال-سایپا-22-10-96 |title= استقلال 1 – سایپا 1؛ آبی، نارنجی، تماشایی |language=Persian |publisher=Varzesh3.com |accessdate=12 January 2018 |date=12 January 2018}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.varzesh3.com/news/1497156/حسینی-رکوردار-کلین-شیت-در-تاریخ-فوتبال-ایران-شد|title=حسینی رکوردار کلین شیت در تاریخ فوتبال ایران شد|trans-title= Hosseini broke clean sheet record in [[Reza Asadi]] finally ended his goalless streak by beating him in the 85th minute of the same match.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.varzesh3.com/news/1497169/پایان-رکورد-حسینی-در-کلین-شیت|title= پایان رکورد حسینی در کلین شیت |trans-title= The end of Hosseini' clean sheet record|publisher=Varzesh3.com|language=Persian|accessdate=12 January 2018 |date=12 January 2018}}</ref>
His record is broken by [[Payam Niazmand]] in October 2019. His performance earned him [[Navad]]'s player of the month award at the end of January.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://90tv.ir/video/439184/سید-حسینحسینی-بهترین-بازیکن-لیگ-برتر-در-ماه-دی-نود-16-بهمن|title=سید حسین حسینی بهترین بازیکن لیگ برتر در ماه دی (نود ۱۷ بهمن)|trans-title= Seyed Hossein Hosseini, league's player of the month award|publisher=[[Navad]]|language=Persian|accessdate=5 February 2018 |date=5 February 2018}}</ref> On 24 February, Hosseini picked up a yellow card for time wasting in a match against [[Foolad F.C.|Foolad]], which ruled him out of the [[Tehran derby]] the following week.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.varzesh3.com/news/1507306/حسین-حسینی-دربی-را-از-دست-داد|title=رحمتی دروازه‌بان استقلال در دربی شد|trans-title= Rahmati became the first choice goalkeeper of the derby|publisher= Varzesh3.com |language=Persian|accessdate=2 March 2018 |date=24 February 2018}}</ref>

==International career==
Hosseini represented [[Iran national under-17 football team|Iran U17]] at the [[2009 FIFA U-17 World Cup]] in Nigeria.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fifa.com/mm/document/tournament/competition/01/11/93/31/fu17_2009_squadlists.pdf |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20091123034530/http://www.fifa.com/mm/document/tournament/competition/01/11/93/31/fu17_2009_squadlists.pdf |url-status=dead |archive-date=23 November 2009 |title=FIFA U-17 World Cup Nigeria 2009 :List of Players |publisher=Fifa.com |accessdate=14 June 2015}}</ref> He also represented [[Iran national under-20 football team|Iran U20]].

He was called to join [[Iran national football team|Iran]]'s training camp by [[Carlos Queiroz]] on 3 November 2017.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.varzesh3.com/news/1471828/استقلالی-ها-اردوی-تیم-ملی-را-تسخیر-کردند |title=استقلالی ها اردوی تیم ملی را تسخیر کردند! |language=Persian |publisher=Varzesh3.com |accessdate=27 December 2017 |date=3 November 2017}}</ref> However he did not make it into the final squad.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.varzesh3.com/news/1472193/قلم-قرمز-کی-روش-روی-نام-4-بازیکن-استقلال |title=قلم قرمز کی روش روی نام 4 بازیکن استقلال |language=Persian |publisher=Varzesh3.com |accessdate=27 December 2017 |date=5 November 2017}}</ref> On 17 March 2018, he made his official debut as a substitute for [[Hamed Lak]] at half-time against [[Sierra Leone national football team|Sierra Leone]], which Iran won 4–0 with Hosseini keeping a clean sheet.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.tarafdari.com/خبر/933697/ایران-4-0-سیرالئون؛-تمرین-گلزنی%D9%90-تیم-ملی-مقابل-یک-تیم-محلی|title=ایران 4 – 0 سیرالئون؛ تمرین گلزنیِ تیم ملی مقابل یک تیم محلی|trans-title= Iran 4 – 0 Sierra Leone|publisher= Tarafdari |language=Persian|accessdate=17 March 2018|date=17 March 2018}}</ref> In May 2018 he was named in Iran's preliminary [[Iran national football team|squad]] for the [[2018 World Cup]] in [[Russia]].<ref>{{cite web|title= World Cup 2018: All the confirmed squads for this summer's finals in Russia|url= https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/440833652018}}</ref> He did not make the final 23.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://ifpnews.com/exclusive/carlos-queiroz-names-provisional-iran-squad-for-fifa-world-cup-2018/ |title=Carlos Queiroz Names Provisional Iran Squad for FIFA World Cup 2018 |website=IFPNews.com|date=20 May 2018 }}</ref>

Hosseini was called again to join the [[Iran national football team]] ([[Team Melli]]) by [[Dragan Skočić]] on 18 March 2022 for playing against South Korea and Lebanon on 24 and 29 March respectively .<ref>{{Cite web |title=Team Melli Squad list announced. |date=18 March 2022 |url=https://www.teammelli.com/team-melli-squad-list-announced/ |access-date=1 April 2022 |language=en-US}}</ref>

In the 2022 FIFA World Cup during Iran's match against England, Hosseini was brought on as a [[Substitute (association football)#Concussion substitute|concussion substitute]] after starting goalkeeper [[Alireza Beiranvand]] collided with a teammate, defender [[Majid Hosseini]]. This was the first use of a dedicated concussion substitute during a World Cup.<ref>{{cite web |last=Evans |first=Ethan |url=https://www.londonworld.com/sport/football/fifa-world-cup-2022-concussion-rules-for-qatar-tournament-after-alireza-beiranvand-substitution-vs-england-3926173 |title=FIFA World Cup 2022: Concussion rules for Qatar tournament – after Alireza Beiranvand substitution vs England |work=LondonWorld |date=21 November 2022 |access-date=21 November 2022}}</ref> Beiranvand had also broken his nose. The match ended with Iran scoring 2 goals and conceding 6.<ref>https://www.washingtonpost.com/sports/soccer/iran-goalkeeper-clashes-heads-with-teammate-at-world-cup/2022/11/21/97cd3f8c-69a3-11ed-8619-0b92f0565592_story.html</ref>

==Personal life==
Hosseini got married in May 2017. He named [[Manuel Neuer]] and [[Mehdi Rahmati]] as his role models.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://taptarinha.com/205-عکس-سید-حسین-حسینی-و-همسرش-بیوگرافی-در/ |title=عکس همسر سید حسین حسینی + بیوگرافی سید حسین حسینی |language=Persian |accessdate=15 January 2018}}</ref> Since childhood, he has supported Esteghlal.<ref name="Navad interview"/>
