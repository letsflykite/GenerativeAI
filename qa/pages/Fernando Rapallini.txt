'''Fernando Andrés Rapallini''' (born 28 April 1978) is an Argentine [[Association football|football]] referee who officiates in the [[Argentine Primera División]]. He has been a [[FIFA]] referee since 2014.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://resources.fifa.com/image/upload/2021-fifa-list-of-international-match-officials.pdf?cloudid=qy9ohgx0ugqacius6mc1 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210108124231/https://resources.fifa.com/image/upload/2021-fifa-list-of-international-match-officials.pdf?cloudid=qy9ohgx0ugqacius6mc1 |url-status=dead |archive-date=8 January 2021 |title=2021 Refereeing International Lists |website=FIFA.com |publisher=[[FIFA|Fédération Internationale de Football Association]] |access-date=22 April 2021}}</ref> 

==Refereeing career==
In 2011, Rapallini began officiating in the [[Argentine Primera División]]. His first match as referee was on 19 June 2011 between [[Godoy Cruz Antonio Tomba|Godoy Cruz]] and [[All Boys]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.worldfootball.net/report/primera-division-2010-2011-clausura-cd-godoy-cruz-all-boys/ |title=Argentina » Primera División 2010/2011 Clausura » 19. Round » Godoy Cruz – All Boys 1:0 |website=WorldFootball.net |access-date=22 April 2021}}</ref> In 2014, he was put on the [[FIFA International Referees List|FIFA referees list]]. He officiated his first senior international match on 5 June 2015 between [[Chile national football team|Chile]] and [[El Salvador national football team|El Salvador]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.worldfootball.net/report/freundschaft-2015-juni-chile-el-salvador/ |title=Friendlies 2015 » June » Chile – El Salvador 1:0 |website=WorldFootball.net |date=5 June 2015 |access-date=22 April 2021}}</ref>

|website=WorldFootball.net |date=1 February 2014 |access-date=22 April 2021}}</ref> [[2017 Copa Argentina Final]],<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.worldfootball.net/report/copa-argentina-2017-finale-river-plate-atletico-tucuman/ |title=Argentina » Copa Argentina 2017 » Final » River Plate – Atlético Tucumán 2:1 |website=WorldFootball.net |date=9 December 2017 |access-date=22 April 2021}}</ref> [[2018 Supercopa Argentina]] and [[2019 Trofeo de Campeones de la Superliga Argentina]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.tycsports.com/nota/supercopa-argentina/2019/04/25/supercopa-argentina-rapallini-el-arbitro-de-boca-central.html |title=Supercopa Argentina: Rapallini, el árbitro de Boca-Central |publisher=TyC Sports |date=25 April 2019 |language=Spanish}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.tycsports.com/racing-club/fernando-rapallini-el-arbitro-para-racing-tigre-20191203.html of the [[2015 Recopa Sudamericana]] between Argentine clubs [[San Lorenzo de Almagro|San Lorenzo]] and [[Club Atlético River Plate|River Plate]],<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.conmebol.com/es/16092015-1925/repaso-fueron-designados-los-arbitros-para-la-recopa|title=Repaso: Fueron designados los árbitros para la Rapallini was selected as a match official for the [[2015 South American U-17 Championship]] in Paraguay,<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.conmebol.com/es/arbitros-convocados-para-el-sudamericano-sub-17-paraguay-2015|title=Árbitros convocados para el Sudamericano Sub-17 - Paraguay 2015|publisher=CONMEBOL.com|date=10 February 2015|language=es}}</ref> as well as the [[2017 South American U-17 Championship]] and [[2019 South American U-20 Championship]], both in Chile.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.conmebol.com/es/convocados-los-arbitros-para-el-sudamericano-sub-17-chile-2017|title=Convocados los árbitros para el Sudamericano Sub-17, Chile 2017|publisher=CONMEBOL|date=17 January 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.worldfootball.net/referee_summary/fernando-rapallini/3/1/u20-h-campeonato-sudamericano-2019-chile/ |title=Fernando Rapallini » Campeonato Sudamericano 2019 Chile |website=WorldFootball.net |access-date=22 April 2021}}</ref> In March 2019, he was selected a referee for both the [[2019 FIFA U-20 World Cup]] in Poland and the [[2019 Copa América]] in Brazil.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fifa.com/u20worldcup/news/match-officials-for-the-fifa-u-20-world-cup-poland-2019-appointed|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190326191742/https://www.fifa.com/u20worldcup/news/match-officials-for-the-fifa-u-20-world-cup-poland-2019-appointed|url-status=dead|archive-date=26 March 2019|title=Match officials for the FIFA U-20 World Cup Poland 2019 appointed|publisher=FIFA.com|date=26 March 2017}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://digitalhub.fifa.com/m/728311cd6a4bcde1/original/kvqoxni6cxaddiqqpuhi-pdf.pdf|title=List of Appointed Match Officials FIFA U-20 World Cup Poland 2019|publisher=FIFA.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.conmebol.com/es/arbitros-convocados-para-la-conmebol-copa-america-brasil-2019|title=Árbitros convocados para la CONMEBOL Copa América - Brasil 2019|publisher=CONMEBOL.com|date=21 March 2019}}</ref>

As part of a referee exchange programme between [[CONMEBOL]] and [[UEFA]], Rapallini was selected on 21 April 2021 as a referee for [[UEFA Euro 2020]], to be held across Europe in June and July 2021.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://editorial.uefa.com/resources/0268-121825575945-25e91e643100-1000/referees_a4_vertical.pdf |title=Referees and assistant referees |work=[[UEFA]] |date=21 April 2021 |access-date=21 April 2021}}</ref> This marked the first time a South American referee had been selected to officiate at the [[UEFA European Championship]].<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.uefa.com/insideuefa/mediaservices/mediareleases/news/0268-12182cf1475f-825d23faf300-1000/ |title=Referee teams for UEFA EURO 2020 appointed |work=[[UEFA]] |date=21 April 2021 |access-date=21 April 2021}}</ref>
At the group stage he was the referee of the [[Ukraine national football team|Ukraine]]-[[North Macedonia national football team|North Macedonia]] game (17 June 2021) at the [[Arena Națională|National Arena]] in [[Bucharest]], and of the [[Croatia national football team|Croatia]]-[[Scotland national football team|Scotland]] clash (22 June 2021) at [[Glasgow|Glasgow's]] [[Hampden Park]], as well as the 1/16 game France-Switzerland (28 June 2021) at the [[Arena Națională|National Arena]] in [[Bucharest]].
