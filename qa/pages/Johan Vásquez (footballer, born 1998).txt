'''Johan Felipe Vásquez Ibarra''' (born 22 October 1998) is a Mexican professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[centre-back]] for {{Italian football updater|Genoa}} club [[Genoa CFC|Genoa]] and the [[Mexico national football team|Mexico national team]].

==Club career==
After a couple of years in Pumas UNAM academy, Vásquez first joined [[Tigres UANL|Tigres UANL youth academy]] in 2015. He then briefly transferred to Poblado Miguel Alemán FC in 2016 before settling in [[Cimarrones de Sonora]], successfully going through the ranks until reaching the first team and making his professional debut in the [[Ascenso MX]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Johan Vásquez|url=https://ligamx.net/cancha/jugador/111222/eyJpZENsdWIiOiAxNH0=/johan-felipe-vasquez-ibarra|website=ligamx.net|accessdate=2 September 2017|language=es-MX}}</ref>

In April 2018, Vásquez joined [[C.F. Monterrey|Monterrey]] on a two-year loan deal with the option to buy.<ref>{{cite web|title=Johan Vásquez, nuevo jugador de Rayados|date=18 April 2018|url=https://www.multimedios.com/deportes/futbol/rayados/johan-vasquez-nuevo-jugador-rayados.html}}</ref> He was eventually purchased by Monterrey for [[US$]]2 million, making him the most expensive transfer between the top two levels of Mexican football.<ref>{{cite web|title=Cimarrones de Sonora, una cantera para la Liga MX|date=20 May 2021|url=https://www.futboltotal.com.mx/futbol-mexicano/liga-mx/cimarrones-de-sonora-una-cantera-para-la-liga-mx/2021/05}}</ref>

In December 2019, Vásquez joined [[Club Universidad Nacional|UNAM]] on loan with the option to buy.<ref>{{cite web|title=Johan Vásquez llegará a Pumas UNAM con un préstamo a dos años con opción a compra|url=https://dalepumas.bolavip.com/ligamx/Johan-Vasquez-llegara-a-Pumas-UNAM-con-un-prestamo-a-dos-anos-con-opcion-a-compra-20191223-0005.html}}</ref> Two months later, following impressive performances, 50 percent of his rights were purchased by UNAM.<ref>{{cite web|title=Pumas habría comprado parte de la carta de Johan Vásquez|url=https://www.soyfutbol.com/ligas/Pumas-habria-comprado-parte-de-la-carta-de-Johan-Vasquez-20200221-0032.html}}</ref>

On 16 August 2021, Vásquez joined [[Serie A]] club [[Genoa C.F.C.|Genoa]] on a season-long loan with an obligation to buy after one played match.<ref>{{cite news|title=Johan Vásquez es oficialmente nuevo jugador del Genoa de la Serie A.|url=https://www.marca.com/claro-mx/futbol/mexicanos-mundo/2021/08/16/611a6b70e2704e4a7f8b45bc.html|publisher=Marca Claro|date=16 August 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Genoa loan Vásquez from UNAM Pumas - Purchase option after one game|url=https://www.transfermarkt.us/genoa-loan-vasquez-from-unam-pumas-purchase-option-after-one-game/view/news/391120}}</ref> On 17 October, Vásquez made his debut with the team in a league match against [[U.S. Sassuolo Calcio|Sassuolo]], scoring the equalizing goal for his team for a 2–2 draw.<ref>{{cite web|title=Johan Vásquez, el 'nómada' del futbol mexicano que cumple su sueño en Italia al debutar con un gol|date=18 October 2021 |url=https://espndeportes.espn.com/futbol/italia/nota/_/id/9374440/johan-vasquez-gol-debut-genoa-nomada-liga-mx-historia}}</ref>

On 18 June 2022, Vásquez moved to recently promoted [[U.S. Cremonese|Cremonese]] on loan with an option to buy.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=Cremonese|url=https://www.uscremonese.it/johan-vasquez-e-un-nuovo-giocatore-della-cremonese/|title=JOHAN VASQUEZ È UN NUOVO GIOCATORE DELLA CREMONESE|date=18 June 2022|access-date=20 June 2022|language=it}}</ref>

==International career==
===Youth===
Vásquez began his international career with the [[Mexico national under-23 football team|under-23]] side, featuring at the [[Football at the 2019 Pan American Games – Men's tournament|2019 Pan American Games]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://wrsd.lima2019.pe/PAG2019/resPAG2019/pdf/PAG2019/FBL/PAG2019_FBL_C33_FBLMTEAM11------------TEAM01------C_MEX.pdf|title=Football Men – Mexico Team Roster|publisher=Lima 2019|date=29 July 2019|access-date=25 August 2020|archive-date=29 July 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190729232226/https://wrsd.lima2019.pe/PAG2019/resPAG2019/pdf/PAG2019/FBL/PAG2019_FBL_C33_FBLMTEAM11------------TEAM01------C_MEX.pdf|url-status=dead}}</ref> where Mexico won the bronze medal.<ref>{{cite web|title=México rescató el Bronce en Panamericanos tras vencer a Uruguay con muchos apuros|url=https://www.mediotiempo.com/deportes-olimpicos/juegos-panamericanos/mexico-1-0-uruguay-bronce-tricolor-lima-2019-cronica-video}}</ref> He also participated at the [[2020 CONCACAF Men's Olympic Qualifying Championship|2020 CONCACAF Olympic Qualifying Championship]], scoring one goal in five appearances, where Mexico won the competition. He was included in the tournament's Best XI.<ref name=CONCACAF>{{cite web|title=Ellos son los 4 mexicanos que encabezan el 11 ideal de Concacaf del Preolímpico 2021|url=https://soyreferee.com/futbolinternacional/Ellos-son-los-4-mexicanos-que-encabezan-el-11-ideal-de-Concacaf-del-Preolimpico-2021-20210331-0016.html}}</ref> Vásquez was subsequently called up to participate in the [[2020 Summer Olympics]]. He won the [[bronze medal]] with the Olympic team.<ref>{{cite web|title=Revelan lista oficial de la Selección Sub-23 que acudirá a Tokio 2020|date=5 July 2021|url=https://espndeportes.espn.com/futbol/selecciones/nota/_/id/8854207/mexico-sub23-convocatoria-jaime-lozano-tokio-2020}}</ref>

===Senior===
On 2 October 2019, Vásquez made his [[Mexico national football team|senior national team]] debut in a friendly against [[Trinidad & Tobago national football team|Trinidad & Tobago]]. He came on as a substitute replacing [[Paolo Yrizar]] in the 63rd minute in Mexico's 2–0 win.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[ESPN]]|url=https://www.espn.com/soccer/lineups?gameId=557649|title=Mexico v Trinidad & Tobago game report|date=2 October 2019}}</ref>

In October 2022, Vásquez was named in Mexico's preliminary 31-man [[Mexico national football team|squad]] for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]], and in November, he was ultimately included in the final 26-man roster, but did not receive any minutes on the field during the tournament.<ref>{{cite web|title=Lista de convocados de la Selección Mexicana al Mundial de Qatar 2022|url=https://www.tvazteca.com/aztecadeportes/lista-oficial-convocados-seleccion-mexicana-mundial-qatar-2022|work=Tvazteca.com|first=Omar|last=Moro|date=14 November 2022|language=Spanish}}</ref>
