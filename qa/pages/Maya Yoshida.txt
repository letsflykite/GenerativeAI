{{nihongo|'''Maya Yoshida'''|吉田 麻也|Yoshida Maya|born 24 August 1988}} is a Japanese professional [[footballer]] who plays as a [[Defender (association football)#Centre-back|centre-back]] and captains the [[Japan national football team|Japan national team]].

==Club career==
===Nagoya Grampus===
Born in [[Nagasaki]], Japan, Yoshida began playing football at Nanling FC in his second year at elementary school.<ref name="Yoshida Brother"/><ref name="Yoshida Brother2"/> His older brother, Honami, played an important role in starting his football career when he searched on the internet for [[Nagoya Grampus]]'s U-15 team and this led Yoshida to join the U-15 side.<ref name="Yoshida Brother">{{cite web|url=https://www.nagasaki-np.co.jp/sports/2008/gorin/kikaku1/05.html|title=サッカー男子 吉田麻也（名古屋グランパス）|publisher=Nagasaki|language=ja|date=22 July 2008|access-date=5 April 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080801190827/https://www.nagasaki-np.co.jp/sports/2008/gorin/kikaku1/05.html|archive-date=1 August 2008|url-status=dead}}</ref> Once Yoshida has joined, Yoshida relocated to Nagoya to be close at Nagoya Grampus and attended Toyota High School.<ref name="Yoshida Brother"/><ref name="Yoshida Brother2"/><ref>{{cite news|title=サッカー吉田麻也選手、高校時代から一目置かれる存在|url=https://www.nikkei.com/article/DGXNASFD04009_U2A800C1CN8000/|publisher=Nikkei|language=ja|date=5 August 2012|access-date=5 April 2020}}</ref>

first team regular for the side, playing in the centre–back position.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://nagoya-grampus.jp/backnumber/result/2007/0526/nagare.html|title=2007
