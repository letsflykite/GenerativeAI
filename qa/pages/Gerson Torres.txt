'''Gerson Torres Barrantes''' (born 28 August 1997) is a Costa Rican professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Midfielder (association football)|right midfielder]] for [[Liga FPD]] club [[Club Sport Herediano|Herediano]] and the [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica national team]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://ligamx.net/cancha/jugador/124167/gerson-torres-barrantes|title= Gerson Torres Barrantes|publisher= LIGA BBVA Bancomer MX |website= LigaMX.net |date=13 March 2017 |accessdate= 22 September 2017}}</ref>

==Career==
===Club career===
====Youth career====
Torres joined Belen's youth academy in 2012. He continued through [[Belén F.C.|Belén Youth Academy]] successfully going through U-17. Until finally reaching the first team, [[Gerardo Ureña]] being the coach promoting torres to first team when he was just 17 years of age.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://m.excelsior.com.mx/adrenalina/2017/01/25/1142030/amp|title=Conoce a… Gerson torres|publisher=Excelsior.com.mx|accessdate=22 September 2017}}</ref>

====Club America====
Torres had slowly worked his way up from the U-20 team up to the first team where he made appearances in the [[Copa MX]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.nacion.com/m/deportes/futbol-costa-rica/Gerson_Torres-Herediano-America_de_Mexico_0_1606639366.html |title=Gerson Torres: la llave de un destino cercado para los futbolistas ticos|publisher= JOSE PABLO ALFARO ROJAS|website= Nacion.com|date=19 March 2017 |accessdate= 22 September 2017}}</ref> Torres was set to make his [[Liga MX]] debut on 19 March 2017 against UNAM however due to league regulation America was notified that Torres was ineligible to play for the first team due to being registered as a foreigner on the U-20 team.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.mediotiempo.com/futbol/2017/03/19/por-reglamento-america-no-pudo-alinear-a-sub-20-costarricense |title=America Sub-20|publisher= Edgar Malagon & Juan Manuel Teran |website= MedioTiempo.com|date=19 March 2017 |accessdate= 22 September 2017}}</ref>

====Necaxa====
Following his year at America, Torres signed a 5-year extension with Herediano.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.nacion.com/puro-deporte/legionarios/gerson-torres-jugara-a-prestamo-en-el-necaxa-de/2G2J3Z5S6NCCLITSES5NZR6L6Q/story/|title = Herediano firma por cinco años a Gerson Torres y lo presta al Necaxa por el 2018}}</ref> Torres is set to be on loan for the 2018 year at [[Club Necaxa|Necaxa]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://espndeportes.espn.com/futbol/mexico/nota/_/id/3808830/necaxa-anuncia-a-gerson-torres-despues-de-draft|title=Necaxa anuncia a Torres después de Draft|date=20 December 2017}}</ref>

===International career===
He made his international debut on 15 January 2017, in a 3–0 friendly win over [[Belize national football team|Belize]]. His first goal came as a stoppage-time header to defeat [[Honduras national football team|Honduras]] on 16 November 2021, as part of [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification – CONCACAF third round|CONCACAF's third round of qualification]] for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Torres' last-gasp goal delivers dramatic win for Costa Rica |url=https://www.concacaf.com/world-cup-qualifying-men/article/torres-last-gasp-goal-delivers-dramatic-win-for-costa-rica/ |website=Concacaf |access-date=8 April 2022 |date=15 November 2021}}</ref> Such a goal was highly crucial for Costa Rica, as it represented a watershed moment between a very difficult first half of the Octagonal for Costa Rica and a near-perfect second half that saw the team reaching the [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification (inter-confederation play-offs)|intercontinental play-off]].<ref>{{cite news |last1=Alvarado |first1=Sergio |title=Luis Fernando Suárez recobra la sonrisa al cierre de la eliminatoria |url=https://www.lateja.cr/deportes/luis-fernando-suarez-recobra-la-sonrisa-al-cierre/QXTN6BZQPZHSLHYAEDC2OLBGBU/story/ |access-date=8 April 2022 |work=La Teja |date=19 March 2022 |language=es}}</ref>
