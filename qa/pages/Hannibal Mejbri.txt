'''Hannibal Mejbri''' ({{lang-ar|حَنِّبَعْل المجبري}}; born 21 January 2003) is a professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[midfielder]] for {{English football updater|ManchesU}} club [[Manchester United F.C.|Manchester United]] and the [[Tunisia national football team|Tunisia national team]].

Mejbri joined the [[Manchester United F.C. Reserves and Academy|Manchester United youth system]] in 2019 from [[AS Monaco FC|AS Monaco]]. He had previously spent time at the [[INF Clairefontaine|Clairefontaine]] academy. He made his senior debut for the club in a Premier League game in May 2021. Mejbri spent the 2022–23 season [[loan (association football)|on loan]] to [[EFL Championship|Championship]] club [[Birmingham City F.C.|Birmingham City]]; he was a regular in the side, and scored his first senior goal.

Born in France to Tunisian parents, Mejbri represented his birth country at [[France national under-16 football team|under-16]] and [[France national under-17 football team|under-17]] levels. He made his senior international debut for Tunisia in 2021.

==Club career==

===Early career===
Despite interest from English clubs, Mejbri had a short spell with [[Athletic Club de Boulogne-Billancourt]], before joining [[AS Monaco FC|AS Monaco]] in 2018 for a fee of €1&nbsp;million.<ref name="leparisien" /> Although initially impressed by Monaco's youth development,<ref name="leparisien" /> Mejbri became disillusioned by the Monégasque club within a year of signing, with his parents claiming that the Ligue 1 side had breached contract agreements.<ref name="MEN1">{{cite web|url=https://www.manchestereveningnews.co.uk/sport/football/transfer-news/man-united-hannibal-mejbri-wiki-16713567|title=Who is Hannibal Mejbri? Profile of Manchester United new signing|first=Charlotte|last=Duncker|date=11 August 2019|access-date=30 June 2020|website=manchestereveningnews.co.uk}}</ref> 

In 2019, he was being tracked by clubs across Europe, including the German, French and Spanish champions, [[FC Bayern Munich|Bayern Munich]],<ref name="MEN1" /> [[Paris Saint-Germain F.C.|Paris Saint-Germain]] and [[FC Barcelona|Barcelona]] respectively.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://bleacherreport.com/articles/2849237-manchester-united-confirm-signing-of-hannibal-mejbri-from-monaco|title=Manchester United Confirm Signing of Hannibal Mejbri from Monaco|first=Matt|last=Jones|date=11 August 2019|access-date=30 June 2020|website=bleacherreport.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.skysports.com/football/news/11667/11774734/manchester-united-confident-of-signing-hannibal-mejbri-from-monaco|title=Manchester United confident of signing Hannibal Mejbri from Monaco|date=1 August 2019|access-date=30 June 2020|website=skysports.com}}</ref> Mejbri said that he was experiencing the "dark side of football" prior to signing with Manchester United and that he had not played football for nearly four months. Before signing, he was back home in Quartier des Amandiers and was training on his own with his sibling.<ref>{{Citation |title=1 vs 1 : Hannibal Mejbri « Avant de signer à Manchester, j'avais arrêté le foot pendant 4 mois » |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DkdOR3ExwKQ&t=803s |access-date=27 November 2022 }}</ref> 

===Manchester United===
On 11 August 2019, [[Premier League]] side Manchester United announced on their website that they had reached an agreement with Monaco to sign Mejbri, with the youngster reportedly rejecting moves to other English clubs.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.manutd.com/en/news/detail/man-utd-academy-deals-for-two-u23s-and-five-u18s|title=United accounce new academy deals|first=Adam|last=Marshall|date=11 August 2019|access-date=30 June 2020|website=manutd.com}}</ref> The fee paid by the Manchester club was believed to be around €5&nbsp;million, possibly rising to €10&nbsp;million in add-ons.<ref name="MEN1" /><ref name="Standard1">{{cite web|url=https://www.standard.co.uk/sport/football/manchesterunited/who-is-hannibal-mejbri-man-utd-new-signing-16-from-as-monaco-a4210756.html|title=Who is Hannibal Mejbri? Man Utd's new 16-year-old signing from AS Monaco|date=11 August 2019|access-date=30 June 2020|website=standard.co.uk}}</ref>

Mejbri settled quickly into Manchester United's youth teams, progressing to the under-23 squad despite still being 17.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.espn.com/soccer/manchester-united/story/4085483/forget-sideshow-bob-sneersmanchester-uniteds-mejbri-wants-last-laugh|title=Forget 'Sideshow Bob' sneers, Manchester United's Mejbri wants last laugh|first=Rob|last=Dawson|date=18 April 2020|access-date=30 June 2020|website=espn.com}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://thepeoplesperson.com/2020/04/07/hannibal-mejbri-why-manchester-united-fans-are-so-excited-213828/|title=Hannibal Mejbri – why Manchester United fans are so excited|date=7 April 2020|access-date=30 June 2020|website=thepeoplesperson.com}}</ref> Mejbri made his debut playing for the [[Manchester United F.C. Reserves and Academy|Manchester United U21]] side against [[Salford City F.C.|Salford City]] in the [[2020–21 EFL Trophy]] on 9 September 2020.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.manutd.com/en/news/detail/salford-city-0-man-utd-6-match-report-in-the-efl-trophy|title=Manchester United Under-21s hit six at Salford |first=Michael |last=Plant|date=9 September 2020|access-date=9 September 2020|website=ManUtd.com}}</ref> He signed a new contract with United in March 2021.<ref>{{cite news |title=Hannibal commits future to Manchester United by signing new long-term contract |url=https://www.manchestereveningnews.co.uk/sport/football/football-news/hannibal-manchester-united-new-contract-20174401 |work=Manchester Evening News |date=15 March 2021|access-date=28 May 2021}}</ref> On 20 May 2021, he won the [[Manchester United F.C. Reserves and Academy#Players of the Year|Denzil Haroun Reserve Player of the Year]] award.<ref>{{cite news |title=Hannibal Mejbri named Manchester United Under-23s Player of the Year |url=https://www.manchestereveningnews.co.uk/sport/football/football-news/manchester-united-hannibal-mejbri-award-20643956 |work=Manchester Evening News |date=20 May 2021|access-date=28 May 2021}}</ref> made his senior debut three days later in a 2–1 win over [[Wolverhampton Wanderers F.C.|Wolverhampton Wanderers]] in the final game of the [[2020–21 Premier League|Premier League]] season; he came on to replace [[Juan Mata]] in the 82nd minute.<ref>{{cite news|first=Gary|last=Rose |title=Wolves 1-2 Manchester United: Nuno Espirito Santo beaten in final game |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/57066741|work=BBC Sport|date=23 May 2021|access-date=23 May 2021}}</ref>

====Loan to Birmingham City====
On 29 August 2022, Mejbri joined [[EFL Championship|Championship]] club [[Birmingham City F.C.|Birmingham City]] on loan for the [[2022–23 Birmingham City F.C. season|2022–23 season]].<ref>{{cite web |title=Hannibal loan to Championship club|url=https://www.manutd.com/en/news/detail/man-utd-midfielder-hannibal-signs-for-birmingham-city-on-loan|website=ManUtd.com|publisher=Manchester United|date=29 August 2022|access-date=29 August 2022}}</ref>  On 10 February 2023, he scored his first senior goal, against [[West Bromwich Albion]], with a surprise free-kick.<ref>{{cite web |last1=Scott |first1=Ged |title=Birmingham City 2–0 West Bromwich Albion |url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/64507487 |website=BBC Sport |access-date=11 February 2023}}</ref>

==International career==

===France===
Mejbri made 12 appearances for France at [[France national under-16 football team|under-16]] level and three appearances at [[France national under-17 football team|under-17]] level.<ref name="fff"/>

He was first called for France at under-16 level in September 2018. He made his debut on 25 September 2018, in a 3–2 defeat to [[Denmark national under-17 football team|Denmark]]. He scored a goal on 22 April 2019, in a 4–0 win against [[Ivory Coast national under-17 football team|Ivory Coast]].{{cn|date=April 2023}} In the same year, he was called up for the first time to the under-17 national team.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Duncker |first=Charlotte |date=2019-08-11 |title=Who is Hannibal Mejbri? Profile of Manchester United new signing |url=https://www.manchestereveningnews.co.uk/sport/football/transfer-news/man-utd-hannibal-mejbri-wiki-16713567 |access-date=2023-04-02 |website=Manchester Evening News |language=en}}</ref> He scored two goals on his debut for the team on 22 October 2019, in an 8–0 win against [[Gibraltar national under-17 football team|Gibraltar]] in [[2020 UEFA European Under-17 Championship qualification]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=France - Gibraltar 8:0 |url=https://www.flashscore.com/match/Ol4EroJ9/#/match-summary/match-summary |access-date=2023-04-03 |website=www.flashscore.com |language=en}}</ref>

===Tunisia===
[[File:Phase de match Tunisie - Mali.jpg|thumb|Mejbri playing for Tunisia against Mali at the [[2021 Africa Cup of Nations]].]]
In May 2021, Mejbri was called up to the [[Tunisia national football team|Tunisia national team]] for the first time,<ref name="MEN">{{cite web |last=Duncker |first=Charlotte |date=11 August 2019 |title=Who is Hannibal Mejbri? Man Utd's new 16-year-old signing from AS Monaco |url=https://www.standard.co.uk/sport/football/manchesterunited/who-is-hannibal-mejbri-man-utd-new-signing-16-from-as-monaco-a4210756.html |access-date=30 June 2020 |website=standard.co.uk}}</ref>

He was selected for the [[2021 FIFA Arab Cup]], making his first appearance against [[Mauritania national football team|Mauritania]] in the first group match and being named [[Player of the match|man of the match]] in the third group match against the [[United Arab Emirates national football team|United Arab Emirates]].<ref>{{cite news |last=Marshall |first=Adam |date=28 May 2021 |title=Hannibal's international future confirmed |website=ManUtd.com |publisher=Manchester United |url=https://www.manutd.com/en/news/detail/hannibal-named-in-tunisia-squad-for-the-first-time |accessdate=28 May 2021}}</ref> He was once again named man of the match in the semi-final against [[Egypt national football team|Egypt]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Match Report of Tunisia vs Congo DR - 2021-06-05 - FIFA Friendlies - Global Sports Archive |url=https://globalsportsarchive.com/match/soccer/2021-06-05/tunisia-vs-congo-dr/2376878/ |website=globalsportsarchive.com}}</ref> He participated in almost all (88 minutes) of the regular time of the Arab Cup final,<ref>{{cite web |last=Stephen |first=Killen |date=17 December 2021 |title=Hannibal Mejbri wants Arab Cup displays to kickstart Manchester United career |url=https://www.manchestereveningnews.co.uk/sport/football/football-news/hannibal-man-united-arab-cup-22497566.amp |access-date=18 December 2021 |website=manchestereveningnews.co.uk}}</ref> before being replaced by [[Mohamed Ali Ben Romdhane]].<ref name=":0">{{Cite web |last=Findlater |first=James |date=18 December 2021 |title=Manchester United starlet Hannibal Mejbri suffers Arab Cup final heartbreak |url=https://www.manchestereveningnews.co.uk/sport/football/football-news/manchester-united-hannibal-mejbri-tunisia-22507937 |access-date=18 December 2021 |website=Manchester Evening News}}</ref>

He was twice named African revelation of the year at the Africa d'Or awards, in 2021 and 2022.<ref>{{Cite web |title=Africa d’Or 2021: Hannibal Mejbri élu révélation africaine de l’année |url=https://www.espacemanager.com/africa-dor-2021-hannibal-mejbri-elu-revelation-africaine-de-lannee.html |access-date=2023-04-02 |website=Espace Manager |language=fr}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web |last=Boubaker |first=Saber Ben |date=2022-07-31 |title=Prix Africa d’Or : Hannibal Mejbri élu révélation africaine 2022 |url=https://sport.tunisienumerique.com/prix-africa-dor-hannibal-mejbri-elu-revelation-africaine-2022/ |access-date=2023-04-02 |website=Sport By TN |language=fr-FR}}</ref> On 14 November 2022, he was selected by [[Jalel Kadri]] for Tunisia's squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Khazri, Talbi et Abdi dans la liste de la Tunisie pour la Coupe du monde |url=https://www.lequipe.fr/Football/Actualites/Khazri-talbi-et-abdi-dans-la-liste-de-la-tunisie-pour-la-coupe-du-monde/1364820 |access-date=2023-04-02 |website=L'Équipe |language=fr}}</ref>
