The '''[[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification]] [[Asian Football Confederation|AFC]]–[[CONMEBOL]] play-off''' was a single-leg match between the winner of the [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification – AFC fourth round|AFC fourth round]] play-off, [[Australia men's national soccer team|Australia]], and the fifth-placed team from [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification (CONMEBOL)|CONMEBOL]], [[Peru national football team|Peru]]. The match was played on 13 June 2022 at the [[Ahmad bin Ali Stadium]] in [[Al Rayyan]], Qatar. Australia won 5–4 on [[Penalty shoot-out (association football)|penalties]] following a 0–0 draw after [[Overtime (sports)#Association football|extra time]] to qualify for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] in Qatar.

==Background==
The draw for the inter-confederation play-off fixtures was held on 26 November 2021.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fifa.com/media-releases/draw-date-set-for-new-look-fifa-world-cup-qatar-2022-tm-intercontinental|title=Draw date set for new-look FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022 intercontinental play-offs|publisher=FIFA|date=19 November 2021}}</ref>

The match was the second meeting between Australia and Peru.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.11v11.com/teams/australia/tab/opposingTeams/opposition/Peru/ |title=Australia national football team: record v Peru |work=11v11.com |access-date=13 June 2022}}</ref> The sides previously met four years earlier in the [[2018 FIFA World Cup Group C]], with Peru winning 2–0 when ''La Blanquirroja'' was already eliminated.<ref>{{cite web |last=Creek |first=Stephen |title=Guerrero stars as Gareca's men make proud exit |url=http://www.goal.com/en/news/8/main/2018/06/26/45870652/australia-0-peru-2-guerrero-stars-as-garecas-men-make-proud |website=Goal.com |date=26 June 2018}}</ref> Before the match, most football commentators lauded Peru as the better team and had low expectations of Australia, which struggled in the Asian qualifiers.

==Match==

===Summary===
The match ended as a scoreless draw. In the 120th minute, Australian goalkeeper [[Mathew Ryan]] was subbed off for [[Andrew Redmayne]]. During the penalty shoot-out, Redmayne gained notoriety for his dance-like movements, which he used to confuse the Peruvian players.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Summerscales |first1=Robert |title=Dancing Goalkeeper Andrew Redmayne Sends Australia To World Cup In Penalty Shootout Win Over Peru |url=https://www.si.com/fannation/soccer/futbol/news/dancing-goalkeeper-andrew-redmayne-sends-australia-to-world-cup |access-date=14 June 2022 |work=Futbol on FanNation |date=13 June 2022 |language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Un show: el héroe de Australia y sus insólitos movimientos en los penales |url=https://www.ole.com.ar/mundial/mundial-qatar-202/arquero-australia-peru-redmayne-penales_0_JfIL760twM.html |access-date=14 June 2022 |work=[[Olé (sports newspaper)|Olé]] |date=13 June 2022 |language=es}}</ref> This strategy succeeded, as Redmayne stopped the decisive penalty from [[Alex Valera]] diving low to his right-hand side.<ref>{{cite web|url= https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/61771218|title= Australia 0 Peru 0 (3-4 on pens)|date=13 June 2022|work=BBC Sport|accessdate=14 June 2022}}</ref>

[[Graham Arnold]]'s decision to sub Redmayne in evoked the [[2014 FIFA World Cup knockout stage#Netherlands vs Costa Rica|2014 FIFA World Cup quarter-final match]] between [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica]] and the [[Netherlands national football team|Netherlands]], when Dutch coach [[Louis van Gaal]] subbed [[Jasper Cillessen]] off for [[Tim Krul]] in the 120th minute, as Krul successfully stopped two penalties to eliminate Costa Rica.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Castillo |first1=Luis Felipe |title=Perú sufre de un «Tim Krul» que apagó a más de 15 mil incas que vieron a Australia clasificar |url=https://amprensa.com/2022/06/peru-sufre-de-un-tim-krul-que-apago-a-mas-de-15-mil-incas-que-vieron-a-australia-clasificar/ |access-date=14 June 2022 |work=AMPrensa.com |date=13 June 2022 |language=es}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |last1=Guzmán |first1=Marcel |title=Australia y Países Bajos, con porteros claves para triunfar en penaltis |url=https://www.marca.com/claro-mx/futbol/mundial/2022/06/13/62a7b00622601d8b0e8b45b2.html |access-date=14 June 2022 |work=MARCA |date=13 June 2022 |language=es-MX}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=Andrew Redmayne is de Tim Krul van Australië: Socceroos dankzij keeper na strafschoppen naar WK |url=https://www.ad.nl/buitenlands-voetbal/andrew-redmayne-is-de-tim-krul-van-australie-socceroos-dankzij-keeper-na-strafschoppen-naar-wk~a48424e5/ |access-date=14 June 2022 |work=[[Algemeen Dagblad]] |date=13 June 2022 |language=nl}}</ref>
