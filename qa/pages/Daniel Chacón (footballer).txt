'''Daniel Alonso Chacón Salas''' (born 11 April 2001) is a Costa Rican professional [[association football|footballer]] for [[Liga Deportiva Alajuelense|Alajuelense]] on loan from [[Colorado Rapids]], and the [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica national team]].

==Club career==
Chacón joined up with [[C.S. Cartaginés]] as a youngster and made his senior debut for the club on 19 November 2017 as a 16 year-old.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://everardoherrera.com/index.php/75-futbol-costa-rica-2/36527-perez-zeledon-cayo-0-2-ante-cartagines-y-dependen-de-si-mismos-para-clasificar|title= Cartaginés derrotó 2–0 a un desconocido Pérez Zeledón que deberá esperar para clasificar|website=everadoherrera.com}}</ref> He scored a first goal for the club on 27 September 2020 against [[Saprissa]] in a 2–1 victory.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://everardoherrera.com/index.php/73-futbol-costa-rica/68170-cartagines-saprissa-promerica|title= Cartaginés has the measure set to Saprissa and beats him 2–1|website=everadoherrera.com}}</ref> In 2020 he was linked with a move to Portuguese side [[G.D. Estoril Praia]] however the transfer fell through.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://amprensa.com/2020/09/joven-del-cartagines-tendra-que-esperar-para-cumplir-sueno-europeo/|title=Young man from Cartaginés ==International career==
Chacón represented his country at the [[2017 FIFA U-17 World Cup]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.fedefutbol.com/2017/09/18/los-21-infantiles-mundial-la-india/|title=The 21 children for the World Cup in India|website=fedefutbol.com}}</ref> He made his debut for the senior Costa Rica side at 20 years-old, appearing as a substitute on 20 January 2022 during a 0–0 draw against [[Mexico national football team|Mexico]]. He made his first start for the national team on 30 March 2022 against the USA.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.fedefutbol.com/2022/03/30/la-sele-cierra-la-octogonal-con-paso-de-gigante/|title= La Sele closes the octagonal with a giant step|website=fedefutbol.com}}</ref> On 14 June 2022, Chacon appeared as a substitute in the match where his country sealed qualification for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] by beating [[New Zealand national football team|New Zealand]] 1–0  to win the intercontinental playoff held in Qatar.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.fedefutbol.com/2022/06/14/con-el-espiritu-de-todo-un-pais-estamos-en-el-mundial/|title= With the spirit of an entire country, we are in the World Cup!|website=fedefutbol.com}}</ref>
