'''Adam Rhys Davies''' (born 17 July 1992) is a professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as [[Goalkeeper (association football)|goalkeeper]] for {{English football updater|SheffieU}} club [[Sheffield United F.C.|Sheffield United]]. Born in Germany, he represents the [[Wales national football team|Wales national team]].

Davies began his career with [[Sheffield Wednesday F.C.|Sheffield Wednesday]] after previously playing in the [[Everton F.C. Reserves and Academy|Everton Academy]]. He did not make any appearances for Wednesday and left for [[Barnsley F.C.|Barnsley]] in June 2014. At [[Oakwell]] Davies experienced promotion from [[EFL League One|League One]] in [[2015–16 Barnsley F.C. season|2015–16]], relegation from the [[EFL Championship|Championship]] in [[2017–18 Barnsley F.C. season|2017–18]] and promotion again in [[2018–19 Barnsley F.C. season|2018–19]]. In June 2019 Davies joined [[Stoke City F.C.|Stoke City]].
