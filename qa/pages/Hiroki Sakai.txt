{{nihongo|'''Hiroki Sakai'''|酒井 宏樹|Sakai Hiroki|born 12 April 1990}} is a Japanese professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Defender (association football)#Full-back|right back]] for [[J1 League]] club [[Urawa Red Diamonds]] and the [[Japan national football team|Japan national team]].

==Club career==
===Kashiwa Reysol===
Born in [[Kashiwa, Chiba]], Japan, Sakai was introduced to football by his two older brothers and joined a school football team when he was the third grade of elementary school.<ref name="Sakai1">{{cite news |script-title=ja:『試合に出られなくても腐らないことが大事』酒井 宏樹（柏レイソル）|url=https://www.sakaiku.jp/column/interview/2011/001193.html|publisher=Sakaiku Official Website|language=ja|date=3 November 2011|access-date=19 June 2018}}</ref> He initially played as a striker before converting to a full back, where he has played since.<ref name="Sakai1"/> Sakai spent 10 years with the school football team before joining [[Kashiwa Reysol]] U15 side in 2003.<ref>{{cite news |script-title=ja:酒井宏「お前だけ応援」工藤の言葉背負う|url=https://www.nikkansports.com/brazil2014/news/p-sc-tp0-20140519-1303210.html|publisher=Nikkan Sports|language=ja|date=19 May 2014|access-date=19 June 2018}}</ref>

Sakai progressed through the ranks of the Kashiwa Reysol and learned his guidance under [[Tatsuma Yoshida]].<ref>{{cite news |script-title=ja:【全文掲載】酒井宏樹が魅せる、アーリークロスの原点【鈴木潤】|url=http://news.livedoor.com/article/detail/6685945/|publisher=Live Door News|language=ja|date=23 June 2012|access-date=19 June 2018}}<br>{{cite news |script-title=ja:史上初の「兄弟対決」が示した明るい未来 柏レイソルのクラブコンセプト|url=https://sports.yahoo.co.jp/column/detail/201209100014-spnavi|publisher=Sports Navi|language=ja|date=1 September 2012|access-date=19 June 2018}}</ref> Eventually, he worked his way through to the U16 and U18 side.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://blog.reysol.co.jp/news/2006/009506.html|script-title=ja:U-16日本代表フランス遠征メンバーに柏レイソルU-18から4選手|publisher=Kashiwa Reysol|language=ja|date=4 April 2006|access-date=19 June 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180609154154/http://blog.reysol.co.jp/news/2006/009506.html|archive-date=9 June 2018|url-status=dead}}<br>{{cite web|url=http://blog.reysol.co.jp/news/2007/008510.html|script-title=ja:7/6?8/13 練習グラウンドへの通行経路変更のお知らせ|publisher=Kashiwa Reysol|language=ja|date=28 June 2007|access-date=19 June 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180609160128/http://blog.reysol.co.jp/news/2007/008510.html|archive-date=9 June 2018|url-status=dead}}<br>{{cite news|script-title=ja:柏ユース比嘉ら５人が来季トップ昇格|url=https://www.nikkansports.com/soccer/news/f-sc-tp0-20081125-433418.html|publisher=Nikkan Sports|language=ja|date=25 November 2008|access-date=19 June 2018}}<br>{{cite web|url=https://www.jleague.jp/jsgoal_archive/official/detail.php?press_code=00077654|script-title=ja:オフィシャルニュース|publisher=J. In May 2014, Sakai was named in Japan's preliminary squad for the [[2014 FIFA World Cup]] in Brazil.<ref>{{cite news |script-title=ja:日本のＷ杯予備登録選手をフライング発表|url=https://www.nikkansports.com/brazil2014/news/p-sc-tp0-20140510-1298516.html|publisher=Nikkan Sports|language=ja|date=10 May 2014|access-date=19 June 2018}}</ref> In the end, he made it to the final cut for the 23-man squad.<ref>{{cite news |script-title=ja:大久保、斎藤ら選出 Ｗ杯メンバー一覧|url=https://www.nikkansports.com/brazil2014/news/f-sc-tp0-20140512-1299866.html|publisher=Nikkan Sports|language=ja|date=12 May 2014|access-date=19 June 2018}}</ref> However, Sakai made no appearances in the World Cup, as he spent the rest of the campaign on the substitute bench.<ref>{{cite news |script-title=ja:酒井宏が古巣柏で汗「１年１年が大事」|url=https://www.nikkansports.com/soccer/japan/news/p-sc-tp2-20140712-1332936.html|publisher=Nikkan Sports|language=es|date=12 July 2014|access-date=19 June 2018}}</ref> After the tournament ended, Sakai made his first appearance for the side in three months on 5 September 2014, in a 2–0 loss against [[Uruguay national football team|Uruguay]].<ref>{{cite news |script-title=ja:日本対ウルグアイ。新スタイルの意図は見えたが、攻守で課題。チームレベルとしてもゼロからのスタート【どこよりも早い採点】|url=https://www.footballchannel.jp/2014/09/05/post48679/|publisher=Football Channel Website|language=ja|date=5 September 2014|access-date=19 June 2018}}</ref> However, he was absent from the [[2015 AFC Asian Cup|AFC Asian Cup]] tournament under a new management of [[Javier Aguirre]].<ref>{{cite news|title=Kiyotake fehlt 96 zum Rückrundenstart|url=http://www.kicker.de/news/fussball/bundesliga/startseite/617549/artikel_kiyotake-fehlt-96-zum-rueckrundenstart.html|publisher=kicker.de|language=de|date=15 December 2014|access-date=19 June 2018}}</ref>

After Uchida's absent in recent matches of 2015, Sakai began to make an impact to keep his first team place for the national side.<ref>{{cite news|title=Defender Sakai aims to make impact in Uchida's In 2014, Sakai revealed that he married a non-celebrity woman and described her as supportive of his football career.<ref>{{cite news |script-title=ja:日本代表ＤＦ酒井宏樹が一般女性と結婚|url=https://www.daily.co.jp/newsflash/soccer/2014/08/24/0007268208.shtml|publisher=Daily Sport Online|language=ja|date=24 August 2014|access-date=19 June 2018}}<br>{{cite news |script-title=ja:酒井宏樹が結婚！「勝てて伝えることができてよかった」|url=https://www.sponichi.co.jp/soccer/news/2014/08/24/kiji/K20140824008802790.html|publisher=Sponichi|language=ja|date=24 August 2014|access-date=19 June 2018}}</ref> It was announced in November 2014 that he would become a father and his wife gave birth to a baby girl in January 2015.<ref>{{cite news |script-title=ja:酒井宏がパパに「ハノーバー中が喜び」独紙|url=https://www.nikkansports.com/soccer/world/news/f-sc-tp3-20141106-1392779.html|publisher=Nikkan Sports|language=ja|date=6 November 2014|access-date=19 June 2018}}<br>{{cite news |script-title=ja:ハノーバー酒井宏がパパに|url=https://www.nikkansports.com/soccer/world/news/p-sc-tp3-20141107-1392923.html|publisher=Nikkan Sports|language=ja|date=7 November 2014|access-date=19 June 2018}}<br>{{cite news |script-title=ja:ハノーファー酒井宏に第一子が誕生|url=https://www.footballchannel.jp/2015/01/14/post65915/|publisher=Football Channel|language=ja|date=14 January 2015|access-date=19 June 2018}}</ref>
