'''Simon Thorup Kjær''' (born 26 March 1989) is a Danish professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[centre-back]] for {{Italian football updater|Milan}} club [[A.C. Milan|AC Milan]] and [[Captain (association football)|captains]] the [[Denmark national football team|Denmark national team]].

Kjær was named 2007 [[Danish Football Player of the Year#Young Players of the Year|Danish under-19 talent of the year]] and 2009 Danish Talent of the Year. He has played over 120 matches for Denmark, including participation at the [[FIFA World Cup]] in [[2010 FIFA World Cup|2010]], [[2018 FIFA World Cup|2018]] and [[2022 FIFA World Cup|2022]], and the [[UEFA European Championship]] in [[UEFA Euro 2012|2012]] and [[UEFA Euro 2020|2020]].

On 15 July 2020, Kjær signed a permanent deal with Milan until 30 June 2022.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.acmilan.com/it/news/articoli/media/2020-07-15/comunicato-ufficiale-simon-kjaer|access-date=15 July 2020|title=COMUNICATO UFFICIALE: SIMON KJÆR|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20200715165233/https://www.acmilan.com/it/news/articoli/media/2020-07-15/comunicato-ufficiale-simon-kjaer|archivedate=12 September 2020|url-status=live}}</ref> On 11 March 2021, Kjær scored his first goal for the Rossoneri in a 1–1 away draw against [[Manchester United F.C.|Manchester United]] in the first leg of the [[2020–21 UEFA Europa League knockout phase#Round of 16|Europa League round of 16]] tie.<ref>{{cite news|last=Stone|first=Simon|title=Manchester United 1-1 AC Milan: AC Milan late equaliser denies United|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/56351824 |access-date=11 March 2021 |work=BBC Sport |date=11 March 2021}}</ref>

On 27 October 2021, he extended his contract with Milan until 2024.<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.acmilan.com/en/news/articles/media/2021-10-27/official-statement-simon-kjaer | title = OFFICIAL STATEMENT: SIMON KJÆR | website = AC Milan | date = 27 October 2021 | access-date = 4 December 2021}}</ref>

On 1 December 2021, in the opening minutes of the Serie A away game against [[Genoa C.F.C.|Genoa]], Kjær sustained an injury to his left knee and had to be stretchered off the pitch. After surgery on the anterior cruciate ligament, he was ruled out for six months.<ref>{{cite web | url = https://theathletic.com/news/ac-milan-defender-simon-kjaer-to-miss-six-months-after-acl-surgery/c0jeHoroGLK4/ | title = AC Milan defender Simon Kjaer to miss six months after ACL surgery | website = The Athletic | date = 3 December 2021 | access-date = 4 December 2021 | first = Luke | last = Bosher}}</ref>

On 22 May 2022, Kjaer won the Serie A when AC Milan beat Sassuolo at the Stadio Città del Tricolore. This was his first major trophy (both domestic and international).
