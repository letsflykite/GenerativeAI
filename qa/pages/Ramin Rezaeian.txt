'''Ramin Rezaeian Semeskandi''' ({{lang-fa|رامین رضائیان سمسکندی}}; born 21 March 1990) is an Iranian professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Defender (association football)|defender]] for [[Persian Gulf Pro League]] club [[Sepahan S.C.|Sepahan]] and the [[Iran national football team|Iran national team]].

He started his career as a [[defender (association football)#Full-back|right-back]], but he often plays as a [[midfielder#Winger|winger]]. Rezaeian was [[Carlos Queiroz]]'s first choice on the right side of the defense of the [[Iran national football team|Iran men's national team]] in the [[2018 FIFA World Cup qualification]] matches.

==Early life==
Rezaeian was born in [[Semes Kandeh-ye Olya]], a village in [[Sari County]], [[Mazandaran Province|Mazanderan]].<ref>{{cite news |url=http://www.iribnews.ir/fa/news/2152320/ملی-پوشان-مازندرانی-در-پی-درخشش-در-جام-جهانی-فوتبال |title=ملی پوشان مازندرانی در پی درخشش در جام جهانی فوتبال |trans-title=Mazanderani national players looking for a shine at the Football World Cup |language=fa |access-date=5 October 2018 |work=IRIB News}}</ref>

==Club career==
===Saba Qom===
Rezaeian played his entire career for [[Saba Qom F.C.|Saba Qom]] before signing for [[Rah Ahan Tehran F.C.|Rah Ahan]] in 2013.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.iplstats.com/playersteam12.htm |title=Iranian Premier League Stats: 2018-2019 Season |work=IPLstats.com |access-date=5 October 2018}}</ref> He made 98 appearances for the club scoring seven goals.

===Rah Ahan===
Rezaeian signed a three–year with the [[Tehran]]-based club [[Rah Ahan Tehran F.C.|Rah Ahan]] in 2013. After several good performances in the [[2014–15 Persian Gulf Pro League|2014–15]] season, Rezaeian was recognized as one of the best fullbacks in the league and was called up to the national team. On 3 April 2015, Rezaeian scored his team's first goal in a 2–2 draw against [[Persepolis F.C.|Persepolis]]. Two weeks later, on 17 April, he scored a brace in a 2–2 draw against [[Esteghlal Khuzestan F.C.|Esteghlal Khuzestan]].

===Persepolis===
[[File:Esteghlal Edges Past Persepolis 3-2 to Claim Tehran Derby-33.jpg|thumb|Rezaeian (right) playing in [[Tehran Derby|Tehran derby]] in 2017]]
Rezaeian joined [[Persepolis F.C.|Persepolis]] on 27 June 2015 with a two-year contract.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://fc-perspolis.com/Contents/2755/بازیکن-تیم-ملی-فوتبال-ایران-سرخپوش-شد |title=بازیکن تیم ملی فوتبال ایران سرخپوش شد |trans-title=Iran national team player became a red-wearer |language=fa |work=FC-Perspolis.com |access-date=5 October 2018}}</ref> He scored his first goal for Persepolis in a 2–0 victory against [[Siah Jamegan F.C.|Siah Jamegan]]. On 26 December 2015, Rezaeian scored a 30-yard free kick against [[Padideh F.C.|Padideh]] in stoppage time to earn them a 2–2 draw. He also scored the third goal for Persepolis in the [[Tehran derby]] against [[Esteghlal F.C.]] on 15 April 2016. Persepolis won that derby 4-2. After initially signing a contract with Turkish [[Süper Lig]] club [[Çaykur Rizespor|Rizespor]] in the summer of 2016,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://footballitarin.com/link_page.php?id=238442|title=فوتبالی‌ترین - پاسخ منفی رضاییان به پیشنهاد تیم ترکیه‌ای|website=footballitarin.com}}</ref> Rezaeian cancelled his contract and re–joined [[Persepolis F.C.|Persepolis]].

After an altercation with teammate [[Ali Alipour]] during a training camp before an [[AFC Champions League]] match, Rezaeian was told to train by himself. After a few weeks, his contract was eventually terminated with Persepolis and he was released.

===Oostende===
After rejecting an offer from Tehran rivals [[Esteghlal F.C.]], Rezaeian joined Belgian club [[K.V. Oostende]] on 9 July 2017 with a two-year contract after a successful trial period at the club. Ramin made his debut on 27 July 2017 in a 3rd round [[UEFA Europa League|Europa League]] qualifier against French club [[Olympique de Marseille]]. Oostende lost the match 4–2 and Rezaeian played the full 90 minutes.

===Al Shahania===

On 6 November 2018, Rezaeian joined [[Qatar Stars League]] side [[Al-Shahania SC|Al Shahania]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.tasnimnews.com/en/news/2018/11/06/1869971/qatar-s-al-shahania-signs-iranian-ramin-rezaeian|title=Qatar's Al Shahania Signs Iranian Ramin Rezaeian|website=tasnimnews.com|date=6 November 2018|access-date=6 November 2018}}</ref> In his first match with the club, on 8 November, Ramin scored a free kick goal in his team's 5–1 loss to [[Al-Duhail SC|Al Duhail]]. On 23 November, in his second match with his club, Ramin assisted his team's only goal in a 1–0 win against [[Al-Kharitiyath]].

===Al-Duhail===

On 22 August 2020, Rezaeian signed a two year contract with [[Qatar Stars League]] champion, [[Al-Duhail SC|Al-Duhail]].<ref>{{Cite web |date=23 August 2020 |title=Al-Duhail signs Iranian winger Ramin Rezaeian |url=https://en.mehrnews.com/news/162614/Al-Duhail-signs-Iranian-winger-Ramin-Rezaeian |access-date=10 July 2022 |website=Mehr News Agency |language=en}}</ref>

===Al-Sailiya===

After the engagement of the new trainer from Al-Duhail, Rezaein was loaned to Al-Sailiya.<ref>{{Cite web |date=1 January 2021 |title=Ramin Rezaeian joins Al Sailiya club |url=https://www.tehrantimes.com/news/456446/Ramin-Rezaeian-joins-Al-Sailiya-club |access-date=10 July 2022 |website=Tehran Times |language=en}}</ref>

=== Persepolis ===
On 12 February 2022, Rezaeian signed a contract with [[Persian Gulf Pro League]] champions [[Persepolis F.C.|Persepolis]].<ref>{{Cite web|title=رسمی؛ رامین رضاییان به پرسپولیس پیوست (عکس)|url=https://www.varzesh3.com/news/1810368|access-date=12 February 2022|website=ورزش سه|language=fa}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=رامین رضاییان رسماً به پرسپولیس برگشت- اخبار فوتبال ایران - اخبار ورزشی تسنیم {{!}} Tasnim|url=https://www.tasnimnews.com/fa/news/1400/11/23/2661808|access-date=12 February 2022|website=خبرگزاری تسنیم {{!}} Tasnim|language=fa}}</ref>

=== Sepahan ===
On 15 July 2022, Rezaeian signed a contract with Persian Gulf Pro League side [[Sepahan S.C.|Sepahan]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=رضاییان به سپاهان پیوست (عکس) |url=https://www.varzesh3.com/news/1833674/%D8%B1%D8%B6%D8%A7%DB%8C%DB%8C%D8%A7%D9%86-%D8%A8%D9%87-%D8%B3%D9%BE%D8%A7%D9%87%D8%A7%D9%86-%D9%BE%DB%8C%D9%88%D8%B3%D8%AA-%D8%B9%DA%A9%D8%B3 |access-date=15 July 2022 |website=ورزش سه |language=fa}}</ref>

==International career==
[[File:Iran and Portugal match at the FIFA World Cup 2018 6.jpg|thumb|Rezaeian (in white) at the [[2018 FIFA World Cup]] ([[Iran national football team|Iran]]–[[Portugal national football team|Portugal]] match)]]
He was called into [[Iran national football team|Iran]]'s [[2015 AFC Asian Cup]] squad on 30 December 2014 by [[Carlos Queiroz]].<ref name="2015acsquad">{{cite web |url=http://www.afcasiancup.com/news/en/nekounam-headlines-iranian-squad/10ppu97hz8lxf1ik1ux49b1ve0 |title=Queiroz Names Team Melli Squad |access-date=30 December 2014 |work=AFCAsianCup.com}}</ref> He made his debut against [[Iraq]] in a friendly on 4 January 2015. He also played a friendly match against [[Sweden national football team|Sweden]]. Rezaeian scored his first international goal on 17 November 2015 in a 6–0 victory against [[Guam national football team|Guam]]. In May 2018, he was named in [[Iran national football team|Iran's preliminary squad]] for the [[2018 FIFA World Cup]] in Russia.<ref>{{cite news |work=BBC |date=4 June 2018 |access-date=5 October 2018 |title=World Cup 2018: All the confirmed squads for this summer's finals in Russia |url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/44083365}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news |title=Queiroz names Iran World Cup squad |language=en-GB |work=BBC Sport |url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/44357568 |access-date=10 July 2022}}</ref>

===2021===

Despite his good performances, he was not invited to the national team.

== Style of play ==
Rezaeian has a high-level skill in long and fast starts.<ref>{{Cite web |last=اسپورت |first=آی |title=تفاوت سبک محرمی و رضاییان: بازی در فضا |url=http://isport.ir/fa/content/9b1d8907401d |access-date=10 July 2022 |website=آی اسپورت |language=fa}}</ref> He has a high skill in long passes and [[Corner kick|corner]] and [[Free kick (association football)|free kicks]]. He also has good scoring skills.<ref>{{Cite web |date=16 February 2022 |title=ببینید {{!}} آنالیز جذاب عملکرد رضاییان؛ چرا جذب رامین برای پرسپولیس حیاتی بود؟ |url=https://www.khabaronline.ir/news/1603475/ببینید-آنالیز-جذاب-عملکرد-رضاییان-چرا-جذب-رامین-برای-پرسپولیس |access-date=10 July 2022 |website=خبرآنلاین |language=fa}}</ref>

[[BBC Persian]] wrote about him: "On the right side, Ramin Rezaeian is the key player of [[Branko Ivanković|Branko]]. He is considered Branko's savior with his scoring power in difficult situations."<ref>{{Cite web |date=1 February 2016 |title=لیگ برتر فوتبال ایران؛ جدال کروات‌ها در ورزشگاه آزادی |url=https://www.bbc.com/persian/sport/2016/02/160201_l51_ipl_football_week18 |access-date=10 July 2022 |website=BBC News فارسی |language=fa}}</ref>
