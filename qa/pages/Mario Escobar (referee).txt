'''Mario Alberto Escobar Toca''' (born on 19 September 1986) is a Guatemalan [[soccer|football]] referee who is a listed international referee for FIFA since 2013.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://digitalhub.fifa.com/m/73018d1c158c1fd5/original/2021-FIFA-Refereeing-International-Lists.pdf|title=FIFA list of referees |publisher=resources.fifa.com |date=2021 |accessdate=30 January 2021}}</ref> He is also one of the referees for the [[Liga Nacional de Fútbol de Guatemala]].

On 15 May 2019, Escobar was officially selected as a referee for the [[2019 CONCACAF Gold Cup]] in [[Costa Rica]], [[Jamaica]], and the [[United States]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Match Officials Appointed for 2019 Concacaf Gold Cup|url=https://www.goldcup.org/en/article/match-officials-appointed-for-2019-concacaf-gold-cup|access-date=17 May 2019|website=www.goldcup.org}}</ref> He was summoned to officiate the [[2019 CONCACAF Gold Cup Final]] between [[Mexico national football team|Mexico]] and the [[United States men's national soccer team|United States]], and additionally refereed the [[2020 CONCACAF Champions League Final]] between [[Tigres UANL|UANL]] and [[Los Angeles FC]].

Escobar was also one of the appointed referees of the [[2019 FIFA U-17 World Cup]] in [[Brazil]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Designados los árbitros de la Copa Mundial Sub-17 de la FIFA Brasil 2019|url=https://es.fifa.com/u17worldcup/news/designados-los-arbitros-de-la-copa-mundial-sub-17-de-la-fifa-brasil-2019|access-date=30 October 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=List of appointed FIFA Match Officials|url=https://digitalhub.fifa.com/m/d55c867c1432bac/original/kkg9qf8zjbwh9vopioqf-pdf.pdf|language=en|access-date=30 October 2019}}</ref>
