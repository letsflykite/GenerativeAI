'''Seyed Majid Hosseini''' ({{lang-fa|سید مجید حسینی}}; born 20 June 1996) is an Iranian professional [[Association Football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Defender (association football)|defender]] for Turkish club [[Kayserispor]] and the [[Iran national football team|Iran national team]].<ref>[http://au.eurosport.com/football/majid-hosseini_prs372204/person.shtml eurosport]</ref>

Hosseini made his full international debut in 2018 and represented [[Iran national football team|Iran]] at the [[2018 FIFA World Cup]], [[2019 AFC Asian Cup]] and [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].

==Club career==
===Esteghlal===
Hosseini joined the first team of [[Esteghlal F.C.|Esteghlal]] in 2014 and made his first appearance for the club on 17 April 2015 as an 18-year-old. He spent a year on loan at [[Rah Ahan]], before returning to Esteghlal and earning a spot in the first eleven. Hosseini was named to the [[Persian Gulf Pro League]] team of the season for 2017–18.

===Trabzonspor===
On 30 July 2018, Hosseini officially [[Süper Lig]] side [[Trabzonspor]] on a three-year contract joining his compatriot [[Vahid Amiri]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.trabzonspor.org.tr/tr/haberler/guncel-haberler/majid-hosseiniyle-sozlesme-imzaladik-7-30-2018|title=Majid Hosseini'yle sözleşme imzaladık|trans-title=Majid Hosseini signed a contract.|language=Turkish|publisher=Trabzonspor|date=30 July 2018|accessdate=31 July 2018}}</ref> He was given the number 5 shirt, the same number he wore in [[Esteghlal F.C.|Esteghlal]] the previous season.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.tarafdari.com/node/1039869|title=شماره 5 باشگاه ترابزون اسپور به مجید حسینی رسید|trans-title=Trabzonspor's number 5 was given to Majid Hosseini|language=Persian|publisher=Tarafdari|date=30 July 2018|accessdate=31 July 2018}}</ref>

=== Kayserispor ===
On 28 July 2021, Hosseini signed for [[Süper Lig]] side [[Kayserispor]].<ref>{{Cite web |title=Kayserispor'un Yeni Transferi Majid Hosseini Oldu. |url=https://www.kayserispor.org/transferler/kayserispor-un-yeni-transferi-majid-hosseini-oldu |access-date=2023-04-13 |website=KAYSERİSPOR.ORG |language=tr}}</ref> 

==International career==
[[File:Iran and Portugal match at the FIFA World Cup 2018 7.jpg|thumb|Hosseini defending against [[Cristiano Ronaldo]] in the [[2018 FIFA World Cup]].]]
Hosseini made his international debut against [[Uzbekistan national football team|Uzbekistan]] on 19 May 2018. In June 2018, he was named in Iran's [[Iran national football team|squad]] for the [[2018 FIFA World Cup|2018 World Cup]] in Russia.<ref>{{Cite news |url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/44357568 |title=World Cup 2018: Iran head coach Carlos Queiroz names final squad|date=4 June 2018|website=BBC Sport|access-date=9 June 2018|language=en-GB}}</ref> After teammate [[Rouzbeh Cheshmi]] was injured in training, Hosseini started as centre back in Iran's two remaining group stage matches, playing every minute in the team's 0–1 loss to [[Spain national football team|Spain]] and 1–1 draw with [[Portugal national football team|Portugal]]. While playing against [[England national football team|England]] in the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]], Hosseini collided with teammate [[Alireza Beiranvand]] breaking Beiranvand's nose. England won the game 6-2.<ref>{{Cite web |date=2022-11-21 |title='Utter disgrace': Iran goalkeeper allowed to play on at World Cup after head clash |url=https://www.theguardian.com/football/2022/nov/21/iran-goalkeeper-head-clash-headway-world-cup |access-date=2022-11-23 |website=the Guardian |language=en}}</ref>
