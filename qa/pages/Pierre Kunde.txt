'''Pierre Kunde Malong''' (born 26 July 1995) is a Cameroonian professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays for [[Olympiacos F.C.|Olympiacos]], and the [[Cameroon national football team|Cameroon national team]] as a [[Midfielder#Central midfielder|central midfielder]].

==Club career==
Born in [[Limbe, Cameroon|Limbe]], Malong started his senior career in Spain with [[Alcobendas CF]]; he made his senior debut for the club on 11 November 2012 by starting in a 2–0 ''Regional Preferente'' home win against EF Periso.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.intersoccermadrid.com/en/pierre-kunde/282-one-of-ours-pierre-in-alcobendas-cf-team|title=One of ours, Pierre, in Alcobendas CF team|publisher=Inter Soccer Madrid|date=12 November 2012|accessdate=28 July 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180713201650/http://www.intersoccermadrid.com/en/pierre-kunde/282-one-of-ours-pierre-in-alcobendas-cf-team|archive-date=13 July 2018|url-status=dead}}</ref> His first goal came the following 3 March, in a 2–0 success at Atlético Leones de Castilla.

In 2013 Kunde moved to [[Atlético Madrid]], returning to youth football.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.intersoccermadrid.com/en/pierre-kunde/280-pierre-debuts-with-atletico-madrid-youth-honor-division-which-will-play-the-youth-european-champions-qjohansson-cupq|title=Pierre debuts with Atletico Madrid Youth Honor Division, which will play the Youth European Champions "Johansson Cup"|publisher=Inter Soccer Madrid|date=28 August 2013|accessdate=28 July 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180713202947/http://www.intersoccermadrid.com/en/pierre-kunde/280-pierre-debuts-with-atletico-madrid-youth-honor-division-which-will-play-the-youth-european-champions-qjohansson-cupq|archive-date=13 July 2018|url-status=dead}}</ref> The following year, he was promoted to [[Atlético Madrid B|the reserves]] in [[Segunda División B]],<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.atleticodemadrid.com/noticias/pierre-espero-marcar-muchos-goles-para-ayudar-al-equipo|title=Pierre: "Espero marcar muchos goles para ayudar al equipo"|trans-title=Pierre: "I hope to score many goals to help the team"|publisher=Atlético Madrid|language=es|date=4 March 2015|accessdate=29 July 2017}}</ref> immediately becoming a regular starter but suffering relegation in [[2014–15 Segunda División B|his first season]].

On 21 July 2016, Kunde was loaned to [[Extremadura UD]] for one year, still in the third division.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.hoy.es/deportes/futbol/liga-segunda-b/201607/21/pierre-kunde-fall-nuevos-20160721000944-v.html|title=Pierre Kunde y Fall, nuevos futbolistas del Extremadura|trans-title=Pierre Kunde and Fall, new footballers of Extremadura|publisher=Hoy|language=es|date=21 July 2016|accessdate=28 July 2017}}</ref> He scored a career-best 11 goals during [[2016–17 Segunda División B|the campaign]], with braces against [[San Fernando CD]] (3–1 away win), [[Linares Deportivo]] (3–1 away win) and [[Atlético Mancha Real]] (2–1 away win).<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.vavel.com/es/futbol/segunda-b/segunda-division-b-grupo-4/783060-hasta-pronto-pierre.html|title=Hasta pronto, Pierre|trans-title=See you soon, Pierre|publisher=[[Vavel]]|language=es|date=28 April 2017|accessdate=28 July 2017}}</ref>

On 25 July 2017, Kunde joined [[Granada CF]], [[2016–17 La Liga|freshly relegated]] to [[Segunda División]], on a one-year loan deal.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.granadacf.es/en/new/official-pierre-kunde-joins-granada-cf-on-loan|title=Official {{!}} Pierre Kunde joins Granada CF on loan|publisher=Granada CF|date=25 July 2017|accessdate=28 July 2017}}</ref> He made his professional debut on 20 August, coming on a late [[Substitute (association football)|substitute]] for [[Javier Espinosa]] in a 0–0 home draw against [[Albacete Balompié]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.marca.com/futbol/segunda-division/2017/08/20/5999d95922601dd7428b459b.html|title='Vuelta al cole' sin puntería|trans-title='Back to school' without aim|publisher=[[Marca (newspaper)|Marca]]|language=es|date=20 August 2017|accessdate=20 August 2017}}</ref>

Kunde scored his first professional goal on 8 October 2017, netting the last in a 2–0 home defeat of [[CD Lugo]]. The following 22 April, he scored a brace in a 3–3 home draw against [[Cultural y Deportiva Leonesa]].

On 6 July 2018, Kunde was transferred to [[Bundesliga]] side [[1. FSV Mainz 05]] and signed a four-year contract.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mainz05.de/en/news/news-detail-aktuelles/news/kunde-becomes-a-05er/|title=Kunde becomes a 05er|publisher=[[1. FSV Mainz 05]]|date=6 July 2018|accessdate=6 July 2018}}</ref>

On 22 June 2021, Kunde sign a three-year contract with Greek giants [[Olympiacos]] after undergoing a medical. The Greek side will reportedly be paying a little less than €2 million for the 25-year-old international, whose contract with Mainz expires in 2022. <ref>{{cite web|url=https://sportnewsafrica.com/en/article-mercato/mainz-cameroonian-midfielder-pierre-kunde-signs-for-olympiakos/|title=Mainz: Cameroonian midfielder Pierre Kunde signs for Olympiakos|publisher=sportnewsafrica.com |date=22 June 2021}}</ref>

==International career==
Kunde represented [[Cameroon national football team|Cameroon]] at under-18 and [[Cameroon national under-23 football team|under-23 levels]], the latter in the [[Football at the 2017 Islamic Solidarity Games|2017 Islamic Solidarity Games]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.camer-sport.be/6711/7/AZ/10/azerbaadjan-la-salection-des-lions-u-23-a-baku-avec-un-seul-gardien-de-but-azerbaijan.html|title=La sélection des Lions U-23 à Baku avec un seul gardien de but|trans-title=The Lions under-23 to Baku with only one goalkeeper|publisher=Camer-Sport|language=fr|date=3 May 2017|accessdate=28 July 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180613234231/http://www.camer-sport.be/6711/7/AZ/10/azerbaadjan-la-salection-des-lions-u-23-a-baku-avec-un-seul-gardien-de-but-azerbaijan.html|archive-date=13 June 2018|url-status=dead}}</ref> He made his full international debut on 27 May 2018, starting in a 0–1 [[Exhibition match|friendly]] loss against [[Burkina Faso national football team|Burkina Faso]].
