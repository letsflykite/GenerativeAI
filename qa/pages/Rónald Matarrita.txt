'''Rónald Alberto Matarrita Ulate''' (born 9 July 1994) is a Costa Rican professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[left-back]] for [[Super League Greece|Greek Super League]] club [[Aris Thessaloniki F.C.|Aris FC]] and the [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica national team]].

==Club career==

===New York City FC===
Matarrita was signed to [[New York City FC]] from [[Liga Deportiva Alajuelense|Alajuelense]] on 20 January 2016 for an undisclosed fee.<ref>{{cite web|last=Araos|first=Christian|url=http://www.empireofsoccer.com/alajeulense-transfer-matarrita-43899/|title=Alajeulense confirm transfer of Matarrita to NYCFC|date=19 January 2016|work=Empire of Soccer|access-date=16 November 2016|archive-date=28 June 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180628233701/http://www.empireofsoccer.com/alajeulense-transfer-matarrita-43899/|url-status=dead}}</ref> Matarrita made his MLS debut against the [[Chicago Fire FC|Chicago Fire]] on 6 March 2016.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.nycfc.com/post/2016/03/06/new-york-city-fc-chicago-fire-recap|title=New York City FC at Chicago Fire: Recap|website=nycfc.com|publisher=New York City Football Club|access-date=27 June 2016}}</ref> He scored his first goal for New York City during a 2–0 victory over the [[Seattle Sounders FC|Seattle Sounders]] on 25 June 2016.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://web.yesnetwork.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20160625&content_id=186208718&fext=.jsp&vkey=news_milb|title=Frank Lampard and Ronald Matarrita score, New York City FC beats Sounders, 2-0|date=25 June 2016|work=yesnetwork.com|access-date=27 June 2016|archive-date=2 April 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190402112515/http://web.yesnetwork.com/news/article.jsp?ymd=20160625&content_id=186208718&fext=.jsp&vkey=news_milb|url-status=dead}}</ref> After a strong first season, he was named New York City's Defensive Player of the Year for 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nycfc.com/post/2016/11/15/matarrita-named-defensive-player-2016|title=Matarrita Named Defensive Player of 2016|website=nycfc.com|publisher=New York City Football Club|date=15 November 2016|access-date=20 March 2020}}</ref>

===FC Cincinnati===
On 29 December 2020, Matarrita was traded to [[FC Cincinnati]] in exchange for $500,000 of General Allocation Money.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.fccincinnati.com/post/2020/12/29/fcc-acquire-costa-rica-international-defender-ronald-matarrita|title = FCC acquire Costa Rica international defender Ronald Matarrita &#124; FC Cincinnati}}</ref> Matarrita was released by Cincinnati following their 2022 season.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.fccincinnati.com/news/fc-cincinnati-announce-roster-decisions-ahead-of-2023-season|title=FC Cincinnati announce roster decisions ahead of 2023 season &#124; FC Cincinnati|website=fccincinnati}}</ref>

===Aris===
On 20 June 2023, Matarrita joined [[Super League Greece|Superleague Greece]] club [[Aris F.C.|Aris]] on a three-year contract. <ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.sport24.gr/football/aris-metagrafes-anakoinose-kai-ton-matarita-poy-ypegrapse-gia-tria-chronia.10086884.html |title=Άρης, μεταγραφές: Ανακοίνωσε και τον Ματαρίτα που υπέγραψε για τρία χρόνια |publisher=www.sport24.gr |date=20 June 2023}}</ref>

==International career==
Matarrita has represented [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica]] at under-17, under-20, under-22, and senior levels. He made his senior debut on 5 September 2015 in a friendly against [[Brazil national football team|Brazil]].<ref>{{cite web|last=Butler|first=Dylan|url=http://www.mlssoccer.com/post/2016/02/16/10-things-about-ronald-matarrita-new-york-city-fcs-costa-rican-international-talks|title=10 Things about Ronald Matarrita|publisher=mlssoccer.com|access-date=19 September 2016}}</ref> He scored his first goal for Costa Rica 6 September 2016, in a 3–1 win over [[Panama national football team|Panama]], in a [[2018 FIFA World Cup qualification – CONCACAF fourth round|World Cup qualification]] match.<ref>{{cite web|last=Krumholtz|first=Michael|url=http://www.ticotimes.net/2016/09/06/costa-rica-la-sele-group-b-qualifying-panama|title=La Sele pummels Panama 3-1 to win World Cup qualifying group|date=7 September 2016|publisher=The Tico Times|access-date=19 September 2016}}</ref> Matarrita was called up for the [[2018 FIFA World Cup]], but was ruled out of the tournament three days before Costa Rica's debut due to a harmstring injury.<ref>{{cite news |last=Chinchilla |first=Roy |url=https://www.fedefutbol.com/2018/06/14/matarrita-fuera-llega-kenner-al-mundial/ |title=Matarrita fuera, llega Kenner al Mundial |trans-title=Matarrita out, Kenner arrives at the World Cup |website=FEDEFUTBOL.com |publisher=[[Costa Rican Football Federation]] |date=14 June 2018 |access-date=15 June 2018 |language=es}}</ref>
