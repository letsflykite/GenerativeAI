'''Predrag Rajković''' ({{lang-sr-cyr|Предраг Рајковић}}, {{IPA-sh|prêdraːɡ rǎːjkoʋitɕ|pron}}; born 31 October 1995) is a Serbian professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Goalkeeper (association football)|goalkeeper]] for [[La Liga]] club [[RCD Mallorca|Mallorca]] and the [[Serbia national football team|Serbia national team]].

==Club career==
===Jagodina===
Born in [[Negotin]], he started training when he was 7 years old with his father Saša, who is also a former goalkeeper of [[FK Hajduk Veljko|Hajduk Veljko]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.novinezavicaj.com/intervjui-kat/796-predrag-rajkovic-golman-fudbalske-reprezentacije-srbijenegotinac-na-krovu-evrope|title=PREDRAG RAJKOVIĆ, GOLMAN FUDBALSKE REPREZENTACIJE SRBIJE NEGOTINAC NA KROVU EVROPE|website=ZAVIČAJ narodne novine|language=sr|date=22 August 2013|access-date=14 June 2015|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304121748/http://www.novinezavicaj.com/intervjui-kat/796-predrag-rajkovic-golman-fudbalske-reprezentacije-srbijenegotinac-na-krovu-evrope|archive-date=4 March 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.zurnal.rs/fudbal/zvezda/11508/zurnal-u-negotinu-sa-predragom-rajkovicem-zvezdin-hajduk-rajko|script-title=sr:Журнал у Неготину са Предрагом Рајковићем: Звездин хајдук Рајко|website=[[Sportski žurnal]]|language=sr|date=2 January 2015|access-date=14 June 2015}}</ref> He was with his hometown club until 2009, when he moved to [[FK Jagodina|Jagodina]]. For next 4 years, he played for youth teams of [[FK Jagodina|Jagodina]], and was with first team since [[2011–12 Serbian SuperLiga|2011–12]] season, and was licensed with jersey number 45.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.footballsquads.co.uk/serbia/2011-2012/superl/jagodina.htm|title=FK Jagodina 2011–12|website=footballsquads.co.uk|access-date=14 June 2015}}</ref> Next season, [[Igor Bondžulić|Bondžulić]] changed his squad number to 11 again, so Rajković took 1. Although Bondžulić was the first choice, Rajković mainly was his reserve in front of competitors [[Petar Jokić (footballer)|Petar Jokić]] and [[Stevica Zdravković]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.footballsquads.co.uk/serbia/2012-2013/superl/jagodina.htm|title=FK Jagodina 2012–13|website=footballsquads.co.uk|access-date=14 June 2015}}</ref> He made his professional debut for [[FK Jagodina|Jagodina]] on 9 March 2013 in a [[Serbian SuperLiga]] match versus [[FK Partizan|Partizan]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.sr.partizan.rs/59150-59150/|title=Jagodina – Partizan 0:1 (0:1)|website=FK Partizan official website|language=sr|date=9 March 2013|access-date=14 June 2015}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.kladionicasoccer.com/vest/?slug=intervju-predrag-rajkovic|title=INTERVJU: PREDRAG RAJKOVIĆ, ČUVAR MREŽE CRVENE ZVEZDE I MLADE REPREZENTACIJE SRBIJE|website=Soccer kladionica|language=sr|access-date=14 June 2015}}</ref> Until the end of [[2012–13 Serbian SuperLiga|2012–13]] season, he made one more match against [[FK Smederevo|Smederevo]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.superliga.rs/index.php/izvestaji-2012-13/1533-27-kolo-smederevo-jagodina#.VX1an_ntmqY|title=27.kolo SMEDEREVO – JAGODINA|website=Serbian SuperLiga official website|language=sr|date=11 May 2013|access-date=14 June 2015}}</ref> After [[Igor Bondžulić|Bondžulić]]'s departure, [[Anđelko Đuričić]] joined Jagodina. After first fixture and lose against [[FK Napredak Kruševac|Napredak Kruševac]], [[Mladen Dodić]] decided to give Rajković a chance, because of his merits in winning [[2013 UEFA European Under-19 Championship]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.jelenfootball.com/vest/33279/Dodic-Nece-nam-manjkati-hrabrosti-da-ubacimo-Rajkovica.html|title=DODIĆ: NEĆE NAM MANJKATI HRABROSTI DA UBACIMO RAJKOVIĆA|website=jelenfootball.com|language=sr|date=16 August 2013|access-date=14 June 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150629055646/http://www.jelenfootball.com/vest/33279/Dodic-Nece-nam-manjkati-hrabrosti-da-ubacimo-Rajkovica.html|archive-date=29 June 2015|url-status=dead}}</ref> But, after 2 appearances in new season, Rajković left to [[Red Star Belgrade]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.alo.rs/sport/fudbal/rajkovic-na-cetiri-gogic-na-tri-godine/29071|title=Rajković na četiri, Gogić na tri godine!|website=[[Alo!]]|language=sr|date=28 August 2013|access-date=14 June 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150629173936/http://www.alo.rs/sport/fudbal/rajkovic-na-cetiri-gogic-na-tri-godine/29071|archive-date=29 June 2015|url-status=dead}}</ref> Jagodina should receive 30 percent of his next transfer.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.zurnal.rs/fudbal/jelen-super-liga/17459/jagodini-od-transfera-predraga-rajkovica-30-odsto|script-title=sr:Јагодини од трансфера Предрага Рајковића 30 одсто|website=[[Sportski žurnal]]|language=sr|date=22 April 2015|access-date=14 June 2015}}</ref>

===Red Star Belgrade===
====2013–14 season====
He signed a four-year contract with [[Red Star Belgrade]] on 28 August 2013, and took the jersey number 95, according to year of his birth.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.crvenazvezdafk.com/vesti/konferencija/7779/Gogi%C4%87+i+Rajkovi%C4%87+nova+poja%C4%8Danja.html|script-title=sr:Гогић и Рајковић нова појачања|website=Red Star Belgrade official website|language=sr|date=28 August 2015|access-date=14 June 2015|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150629035526/http://www.crvenazvezdafk.com/vesti/konferencija/7779/Gogi%C4%87+i+Rajkovi%C4%87+nova+poja%C4%8Danja.html|archive-date=29 June 2015}}</ref> He acted as a backup to [[Boban Bajković]], and fought for a place on the bench with [[Miloš Vesić]]. He made his official debut for [[Red Star Belgrade|Red Star]] in last fixture against [[FK Vojvodina|Vojvodina]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.crvenazvezdafk.com/en/vesti/izvestaji/8808/Vojvodina+-+Crvena+zvezda+3%3A3+(0%3A1).html|title=Vojvodina – Crvena zvezda 3:3 (0:1)|website=Red Star Belgrade official website|date=29 May 2014|access-date=14 June 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150629022949/http://www.crvenazvezdafk.com/en/vesti/izvestaji/8808/Vojvodina+-+Crvena+zvezda+3%3A3+(0%3A1).html|archive-date=29 June 2015|url-status=dead}}</ref>

====2014–15 season====
After [[Boban Bajković|Bajković]]'s and [[Miloš Vesić|Vesić]]'s departures, Rajković stayed with a year younger [[Filip Manojlović]] and [[Marko Trkulja]] who returned from [[FK Spartak Subotica|Spartak Subotica]], where he was on loan. Club management decided to sign a more experienced goalkeeper, and [[Damir Kahriman]] joined [[Red Star Belgrade|Red Star]]. But Kahriman couldn't play at the beginning of the season because of administrative problems, so Rajković started the season as a first choice. After [[Nenad Milijaš|Milijaš]]'s departure, [[Nikola Mijailović (footballer)|Mijailović]]'s suspension, [[Aleksandar Luković|Luković]]'s injury, and [[Darko Lazović|Lazović]]'s conflict with management, coach [[Nenad Lalatović]] decided to give the captain's armband to Rajković.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.mozzartsport.com/vesti/nova-afera-traka-na-marakani-lazovic-ponovo-razalovan-predrag-rajkovic-novi-kapiten-zvezde/97196|title=Nova afera "traka" na Marakani: Lazović ponovo ražalovan, Predrag Rajković novi kapiten Zvezde!|website=mozzartsport.com|language=sr|date=14 February 2015|access-date=14 June 2015}}</ref> He had 16 clean sheets in 28 league appearances, and was selected as the best goalkeeper in [[2014–15 Serbian SuperLiga|2014–15]] season, chosen by other players.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.b92.net/sport/fudbal/vesti.php?yyyy=2015&mm=05&dd=26&nav_id=996482|title=JSL najboljih XI: Dominacija Čukaričkog|website=[[B92]]|language=sr|date=26 May 2015|access-date=14 June 2015}}</ref>

====2015–16 season====
On 29 June 2015, Rajković signed a new three-year contract with Red Star, after days of speculation about his potential transfer away from the club.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.mojacrvenazvezda.net/110919/2015/06/28/rajkovic-u-ponedeljak-potpisuje-novi-ugovor-sa-zvezdom/|title=Rajković u ponedeljak potpisuje novi ugovor!|website=MojaCrvenazvezda|language=sr|date=28 June 2015|access-date=29 June 2015}}</ref> After winning [[2015 FIFA U-20 World Cup]] in [[New Zealand]] and the award for the best goalkeeper of the tournament, many European clubs wanted Rajković, including French [[Ligue 1]] club [[Olympique Lyonnais|Lyon]], Belgian side [[Club Brugge K.V.|Club Brugge]] and according to some reports Italian giant [[Serie A]] club [[A.C. Milan]]. The [[Red Star Belgrade|Crvena zvezda]] captain was also a top target for [[Paris Saint Germain]] along with [[Kevin Trapp]], who signed for Les Parisiens at the end. Rajković was the best member in team during both matches against [[FC Kairat|Kairat]] in the [[2015–16 UEFA Europa League qualifying phase and play-off round#First qualifying round|first qualifying round]] of the [[UEFA Europa League|Europa League]]. Rajković kept the team from suffering a deeper loss with some incredible saves. The coach of [[FC Kairat|Kairat]], [[Vladimír Weiss (footballer, born 1964)|Vladimír Weiss]], said after the game that Rajković is one of the best goalkeepers in Europe.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.b92.net/sport/fudbal/vesti.php?yyyy=2015&mm=07&dd=09&nav_id=1013841|title=Rajković: Ne tražim alibi, biće bolje|website=[[B92]]|language=sr|date=9 July 2015|access-date=9 July 2015}}</ref> After great saves in first rounds of the [[Serbian SuperLiga]], despite Zvezda's poor start, Turkish champion [[Galatasaray S.K. (football)|Galatasaray]] declared interest in him.

Predrag Rajković was one of the contestants at [[2018 FIFA World Cup]] qualifying draw, which took place at the [[Konstantinovsky Palace]] in [[Strelna]], [[Saint Petersburg]] on 25 July 2015.

===Maccabi Tel Aviv===
[[File:Chelsea Vs Maccabi Tel-Aviv (21481917562).jpg|thumb|300px|Rajković for Maccabi in a [[2015–16 UEFA Champions League|UEFA Champions League]] match against [[Chelsea F.C.|Chelsea]] in 2015]]
On 29 August 2015, Rajković signed a five-year contract with [[Maccabi Tel Aviv F.C.|Maccabi Tel Aviv]]. The transfer fee was €3 million.<ref>{{cite news | script-title=he:פרדראג ראיקוביץ' חתם במכבי ת"א ל-5 שנים | trans-title = Predrag Rajković signed for five years with Maccabi |url=http://www.ynet.co.il/articles/0,7340,L-4695572,00.html|newspaper=[[ynet]] | date=29 August 2015 | language = he| last1 = צנציפר | first1 = נדב }}</ref> On 30 August, Rajković made his [[Israeli Premier League]] debut in a 1–0 victory over [[Hapoel Be'er Sheva F.C.|Hapoel Be'er Sheva]] during the second league match of the season.<ref>{{cite web|title=1–0 for Maccabi against Hapoel Be'er Sheva | date=30 August 2015 |url=http://one.co.il/Article/15-16/1,1,3,0/256435.html?ref=hp|publisher=[[ONE (website)|ONE]]|access-date=30 August 2015|language=he}}</ref> Rajković made 43 appearances in all competitions, including [[Israeli Premier League]], [[Israel State Cup|State Cup]], [[Toto Cup]] in which he did not play and [[UEFA Champions League]]. He noted 21 clean sheets at total until the end of 2015–16 season.<ref name="soccerway"/> Rajković also continued playing as the first choice [[Goalkeeper (association football)|goalkeeper]] in the [[2016–17 Israeli Premier League]] season. During the first half-season, he also made 14 appearances in the [[UEFA Europa League]], which the club ended at third place in group stage. Rajković made an assist to [[Viðar Örn Kjartansson]] in the [[Israel State Cup|State Cup]] match against [[Hapoel Bnei Lod F.C.|Hapoel Bnei Lod]] played on 7 January 2017, saving a shot from the opponent's previous attack.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.mozzartsport.com/vesti/rajko-brani-kao-da-nije-normalan-i-jos-asistira-video/156045|title=Rajko brani kao da nije normalan! I još asistira! (VIDEO)|website=mozzartsport.com|language=sr|date=9 January 2017|access-date=9 January 2017}}</ref> In May same year, Rajković was elected for the club's player of the season in an inner club event.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.rts.rs/page/sport/ci/story/31/fudbal/2724109/predrag-rajkovic-najbolji-igrac-makabija.html|script-title=sr:Предраг Рајковић најбољи играч Макабија|website=[[Radio Television of Serbia]]|language=sr|date=5 May 2017|access-date=5 May 2017}}</ref> On 14 December 2017, Rajković won the [[Toto Cup]] trophy with Maccabi, beating [[Hapoel Be'er Sheva F.C.|Hapoel Be'er Sheva]] in [[2017–18 Toto Cup Al#Final|final]] match of the competition.<ref name="oneil">{{cite web |url=http://www.one.co.il/Article/17-18/1,667,0,0/303547.html?ref=hp|script-title=he:בפעם החמישית: מכבי ת"א זכתה בגביע הטוטו|website=[[ONE (website)|ONE]]|language=he|date=14 December 2017|access-date=17 December 2017}}</ref>

=== Reims ===
On 23 July 2019, Rajković signed a four-year contract with [[Stade de Reims]]. The transfer fee was €3 million. Rajkovic was brought in for the outgoing [[Édouard Mendy|Edouard Mendy]], who was on his way to [[Stade Rennais F.C.|Stade Rennais]] after a summer of heavy speculation on his future. [[Édouard Mendy|Mendy]] had been hugely impressive in his first season in [[Ligue 1]], keeping 14 [[Shutout|clean sheets]] bettered only by [[Olympique Lyonnais|Lyon's]] [[Anthony Lopes]] and [[OGC Nice|Nice]]'s [[Walter Daniel Benítez|Walter Benitez]]. In his first season in the [[Champagne (province)|Champagne region]], Rajković will have youth academy product [[Nicolas Lemaître|Nicolas Lemaitre]] as a backup. Rajković made his debut on 10 August 2019 in a 2–0 away win against [[Olympique de Marseille|Marseille]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sofascore.com/player/predrag-rajkovic/188107|title=Predrag Rajković Stade de Reims videos, transfer history and stats - SofaScore|website=www.sofascore.com|access-date=17 January 2020}}</ref>

=== Mallorca ===

On 22 July 2022, Rajković signed for [[La Liga]] club [[RCD Mallorca|Mallorca]] on a four-year contract. The transfer fee paid to Reims was of €4.5 million, with a sell-on clause included in the deal as well.<ref>{{Cite web |date=22 July 2022 |title=Transferts : Predrag Rajkovic quitte Reims pour le Real Majorque (officiel) |url=https://www.lequipe.fr/Football/Actualites/Transferts-predrag-rajkovic-quitte-reims-pour-le-real-majorque-officiel/1344939 |access-date=23 July 2022 |website=[[L'Équipe]] |lang=fr}}</ref>

==International career==
===Youth===
Rajković was a member of the Serbia U-16 and [[Serbia national under-17 football team|Serbia U-17]] national teams.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.supersport.rs/fudbal/Nacionalni/16068/predrag-rajkovic-cim-oborim-rekord-naci-cu-i-devojku.html|title=PREDRAG RAJKOVIĆ: Čim oborim rekord, naći ću i devojku!|website=supersport.rs|language=sr|date=30 September 2014|access-date=14 June 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150710221554/http://www.supersport.rs/fudbal/Nacionalni/16068/predrag-rajkovic-cim-oborim-rekord-naci-cu-i-devojku.html|archive-date=10 July 2015|url-status=dead}}</ref> Rajković was one of the most deserving players for winning the [[2013 UEFA European Under-19 Championship]], when he saved goal many times with great interventions and stopped penalties.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.b92.net/sport/fudbal/vesti.php?yyyy=2013&mm=08&dd=02&nav_id=738758|title=Zapamtite Rajkovića i Mitrovića|website=[[B92]]|language=sr|date=2 August 2013|access-date=14 June 2015}}</ref> Next year, he also was one of the best, but Serbia lost against [[Portugal national under-19 football team|Portugal]] in the semi-final after penalties.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.telegraf.rs/sport/1170790-evropsko-prvenstvo-srbija-portugalija|title=SRBIJA NIJE IMALA SREĆE: Omladinci posle penala ispali u polufinalu EP! (FOTO) (VIDEO)|website=telegraf.rs|language=sr|date=28 July 2014|access-date=14 June 2015}}</ref> Rajković lead the team in the [[2015 FIFA U-20 World Cup]] with the captain's armband. After group stage and first place, Serbia beat Hungary after extra time. In the quarter-final, Rajković saved 2 penalties and missed one in a penalty shootout against the USA. After [[Nemanja Maksimović|Maksimović]]'s goal, Serbia advanced to the next round.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://sport.blic.rs/Fudbal/Svetski-fudbal/274046/TAKO-SE-BORI-ZA-SRBIJU-Orlici-dramaticnim-penalima-do-polufinala-SP|title=TAKO SE BORI ZA SRBIJU "Orlići" dramatičnim penalima do polufinala SP!|website=[[Blic]] Sport|language=sr|date=14 June 2015|access-date=14 June 2015}}</ref> In the final, Rajković was one of the most important men on the field, keeping the result with incredible saves. The captain of [[Serbia national under-20 football team]] won the nomination for the best goalkeeper in the competition.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.rts.rs/page/sport/sr/story/36/Fudbal/1954482/Rajkovi%C4%87+najbolji+golman+Svetskog+prvenstva%21.html|title=Rajković najbolji golman Svetskog prvenstva!|website=[[Radio Television of Serbia]]|language=sr|date=20 June 2015|access-date=20 June 2015}}</ref> [[Radovan Ćurčić]], coach of [[Serbia national under-21 football team|Serbia U-21]], called him for a friendly match against [[Israel national under-21 football team|Israel]], played on 6 February 2013.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://035info.rs/predrag-rajkovic-u-mladoj-reprezentaciji|title=Predrag Rajković u mladoj reprezentaciji|website=035info.rs|language=sr|date=31 January 2013|access-date=14 June 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150620181341/http://035info.rs/predrag-rajkovic-u-mladoj-reprezentaciji/|archive-date=20 June 2015|url-status=dead}}</ref>

===Senior===
Rajković was called in [[Serbia national football team|Serbia national team]] under coach [[Siniša Mihajlović]].<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.negotin.rs/vest-mladi-golman-iz-negotina-pozvan-na-pripreme-a-reprezentacije-1740.htm|title=Млади голман из Неготинa позван на припреме "А" репрезентације|website=negotin.rs|language=sr|date=24 May 2013|access-date=14 June 2015}}</ref> He debuted for the senior team on 7 August 2013 against Colombia.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.b92.net/sport/fudbal/vesti.php?yyyy=2013&mm=08&dd=14&nav_id=742761|title=Srbija izgubila od Kolumbije – 1:0|website=[[B92]]|language=sr|date=14 August 2013|access-date=14 June 2015}}</ref>

In June 2018 he was selected in Serbia’s squad for the [[2018 FIFA World Cup|2018 World Cup]],<ref>{{cite web | url = https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/44083365 | title = World Cup 2018: All the confirmed squads for this summer's finals in Russia | publisher = BBC Sport | date = 4 June 2018 | access-date = 25 June 2018}}</ref> but he did not make any appearances there as he was backup to [[Vladimir Stojković]].

In November 2022, he was selected in Serbia's squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] in Qatar,<ref>{{cite web |url=https://fss.rs/en/head-coach-dragan-stojkovic-announces-the-list-of-players-for-the-fifa-world-cup-2022-in-qatar/ |title=Head-coach Dragan Stojković announces the list of players for the FIFA World Cup 2022 in Qatar |publisher=[[Football Association of Serbia]] |date=11 November 2022 |access-date=11 November 2022}}</ref> but he didn't make any appearance there, as [[Vanja Milinković-Savić]] was the first-choice goalkeeper.

==Personal life==
On 8 August 2018, Rajković married his Serbian girlfriend Ana Cakić. Predrag picked his teammate from the Serbia national team, [[Saša Zdjelar]], for his best man on the wedding. They have two sons together.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.kurir.rs/sport/fudbal/3098117/rajkovic-se-zakleo-na-vecnu-ljubav-golman-reprezentacije-srbije-rekao-sudbonosno-da-ani-cakic-foto|title=RAJKOVIĆ SE ZAKLEO NA VEČNU LJUBAV: Golman reprezentacije Srbije rekao sudbonosno DA Ani Cakić (FOTO)|website=[[Kurir]]|language=sr|date=6 August 2018|access-date=28 August 2018}}</ref>
