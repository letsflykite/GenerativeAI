==Club career==
===Early career===
Majer was born in [[Zagreb]] in 1998. His father Egon hails from [[Preko]] on the [[List of islands of Croatia|island]] of [[Ugljan]].<ref>{{cite web |last1=Gjergja |first1=Ivica |title="Uvijek ƒáu doƒái, znam odakle sam krenuo"! |url=https://zadarski.slobodnadalmacija.hr/zadar/sport/uvijek-cu-doci-znam-odakle-sam-krenuo-606974 |website=Zadarski |access-date=27 January 2021 |language=hr-hr |date=3 June 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |last1=Juri≈°iƒá |first1=Predrag |title=TE≈ΩAK UDARAC ZA DINAMO Najveƒái talent ne ≈æeli karijeru nastaviti u Maksimiru: Otkrivamo detalje velikog transfera u Serie A koji je na pomolu |url=https://sportske.jutarnji.hr/sn/nogomet/tezak-udarac-za-dinamo-najveci-talent-ne-zeli-karijeru-nastaviti-u-maksimiru-otkrivamo-detalje-velikog-transfera-u-serie-a-koji-je-na-pomolu-7459971 |website=Sportske novosti |access-date=27 January 2021 |language=hr-hr |date=11 June 2018}}</ref> Majer started his football career with the local team [[GNK Dinamo Zagreb|Dinamo Zagreb]]. He left the team at the age of 12. In 2013, Majer joined [[NK Lokomotiva|Lokomotiva]] youth academy.

===Lokomotiva===
In 2016, Majer was called up to the Lokomotiva first team. On 30 June 2016, Majer made his senior team debut in the [[2016‚Äì17 UEFA Europa League qualifying phase and play-off round#First qualifying round|UEFA Europa League qualifying phase]] against [[UE Santa Coloma|Santa Coloma]] at [[Estadi Comunal d'Andorra la Vella|Estadi Comunal]], replacing [[Eros Grezda]] in the 76th minute by coach [[Mario Tokiƒá]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.uefa.com/uefaeuropaleague/season=2017/matches/round=2000788/match=2019992/index.html|title=UEFA Europa League 2017 - UE Santa Coloma-Lokomotiva Zagreb ‚Äì UEFA.com|website=uefa.com|access-date=7 January 2016}}</ref> On 17 July 2016, he made his [[Croatian First Football League|Prva HNL]] debut in the matchday 1 against Dinamo Zagreb, replacing [[Ivan Fioliƒá]] in the 55th minute of the 3‚Äì1 loss. On 25 October 2016, he scored in the [[2016‚Äì17 Croatian Football Cup#Second round|Croatian Football Cup]] in the 69th minute of the second round against [[NK Vinogradar|Vinogradar]]. His goal led Lokomotiva to advance to the quarter-finals.

On 10 March, Majer scored a brace in a 4‚Äì1 away win over Dinamo Zagreb, which was a historic first win for Lokomotiva over Dinamo, with the club winning after 28 attempts.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.24sata.hr/sport/uzivo-dinamo-nakon-debakla-u-rijeci-docekuje-svoju-sestru-564004|title=I ovo se dogodilo!
