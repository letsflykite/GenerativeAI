'''Bartosz Bereszyński''' (born 12 July 1992) is a Polish professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[right-back]] for [[Serie B]] club [[U.C. Sampdoria|Sampdoria]] and the [[Poland national football team|Poland national team]].

==Club career==
[[File:Bartosz Bereszynski Superpuchar.jpg|200px|thumb|Bereszyński with [[Legia Warsaw]] in 2015]]

Born in [[Poznań]], Bereszyński played youth football with TPS Winogrady Poznań, Poznaniak Poznań, [[Warta Poznań]] and [[Lech Poznań]].<ref name = "90minut">{{90minut|16214}}</ref> He played senior football with [[Lech Poznań]], [[Warta Poznań]] and [[Legia Warsaw]].<ref name = "90minut"/><ref name = "SW">{{Soccerway|bartosz-bereszynski/123106}}</ref> While with Lech Poznań, he played in the UEFA Europa League qualifying rounds.<ref>{{UEFA player|250020213}}</ref> He was one of the very few players to have moved voluntarily from Lech to Legia, who are fierce rivals,<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://gloswielkopolski.pl/bereszynski-nie-jest-pierwszy-wszystkie-transfery-na-linii-lech-legia-zdjecia/ar/742603|title = Bereszyński nie jest pierwszy. Wszystkie transfery na linii Lech - Legia &#91;ZDJĘCIA&#93;|date = 18 January 2013}}</ref> was nicknamed "[[Judas]]"<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.przegladsportowy.pl/pilka-nozna/pko-ekstraklasa/legia-warszawa/bartosz-bereszynski-historia-przejscia-z-lecha-poznan-do-legii-warszawa/33htrqe|title = Bereszyński już nie dostaje wiadomości ze słowem "Judasz"|date = 4 April 2013}}</ref> and received a violent reaction from the Lech fans.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://sport.se.pl/pilka-nozna/ekstraklasa/bartosz-bereszynski-po-meczu-lech-legia-kibice-rzucali-we-mnie-czym-popadnie-aa-RXs8-knC6-ey1F.html|title = Bartosz Bereszyński po meczu Lech - Legia: Kibice rzucali we mnie czym popadnie}}</ref>

Bereszyński was sent off in the last round of the [[2013–14 UEFA Europa League]] group stage,<ref>{{cite web|title=Match details|url=http://www.uefa.com/uefaeuropaleague/season=2014/matches/round=2000469/match=2012614/index.html|website=UEFA|access-date=8 August 2014}}</ref> and was suspended for the next three European matches. He did not feature in the second round nor the first leg of the 3rd qualification round in the [[2014–15 UEFA Champions League]]. However, as his club failed to include him in their squad registration for the second round matches, he had not finished his suspension when he came on as a sub in the 86th minute in the return leg against [[Celtic F.C.]] in the third round. Legia won the match 2–0, and 6–1 on aggregate. However, after considering the mistake, UEFA set the result to a 3–0 win for Celtic. This made the Scottish team winners on the [[away goals rule]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/0/football/28701951|title=Celtic reinstated to Champions League|publisher=BBC Sport|date=8 August 2014}}</ref>

In January 2017, Bereszyński moved to Italy, signing a four-year deal with [[U.C. Sampdoria|Sampdoria]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Oficjalnie: Bereszyński piłkarzem Sampdorii Genua!|url=http://www.przegladsportowy.pl/pilka-nozna/ligi-zagraniczne,bartosz-bereszynski-przeszedl-z-legii-warszawa-do-sampdorii-genua,artykul,747724,1,461.html|website=PrzegladSportowy.pl|access-date=4 January 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171004190900/http://www.przegladsportowy.pl/pilka-nozna/ligi-zagraniczne,bartosz-bereszynski-przeszedl-z-legii-warszawa-do-sampdorii-genua,artykul,747724,1,461.html|archive-date=4 October 2017|url-status=dead}}</ref>

On 7 January 2023, [[S.S.C. Napoli|Napoli]] announced the signing of Bereszyński on loan with a purchase option.<ref>{{cite web |title=IL NAPOLI UFFICIALIZZA L'ACQUISIZIONE DI BERESZYNSKI DALLA SAMPDORIA CON LA FORMULA DEL PRESTITO CON DIRITTO DI RISCATTO |url=https://sscnapoli.it/il-napoli-ufficializza-lacquisizione-di-bereszynski-dalla-sampdoria-con-la-formula-del-prestito-con-diritto-di-riscatto/ |publisher=[[S.S.C. Napoli|Napoli]] |language=it |date=7 January 2023}}</ref>

==International career==
Having previously represented Poland at youth level, in the [[Poland national under-19 football team|under-19]],<ref>{{cite web |title=eME U-19: Polska 1-3 Włochy |url=http://www.90minut.pl/news/157/news1578450-eME-U-19-Polska-1-3-Wlochy.html |website=90minut.pl |access-date=21 June 2023 |language=pl |date=26 May 2011}}</ref> [[Poland national under-20 football team|under-20]],<ref>{{cite web |url = http://archive.is/Jf2tS |title = Turniej Czterech Narodów: Niemcy – Polska 4:2 |publisher = [[Polski Związek Piłki Nożnej]] |date = 2 September 2011 |access-date = 21 June 2023 |language = pl}}</ref> and [[Poland national under-21 football team|under-21]] teams,<ref>{{cite web |title=Kadra Dorny ograła Litwę. Dwa gole Stępińskiego |url=https://archive.is/HTDa3 |publisher=Polski Związek Piłki Nożnej |access-date=21 June 2023 |language=pl |date=23 March 2013}}</ref> Bereszyński made his debut for the [[Poland national football team|senior national team]] against [[Liechtenstein national football team|Liechtenstein]] on 4 June 2013.<ref name = "NFT">{{NFT player|52489}}</ref>

In June 2018 he was named in Poland's squad for the [[2018 FIFA World Cup]] in Russia.<ref>{{cite news |last1=Wilson |first1=Sam |title=2018 World Cup team profiles: Poland boast potent frontline |url=https://www.stuff.co.nz/sport/football/world-game/104403419/2018-world-cup-team-profiles-poland |access-date=11 November 2022 |work=Stuff |date=10 June 2018}}</ref>

==Personal life==
On 14 March 2020, Bereszyński tested positive for [[COVID-19]], following its [[COVID-19 pandemic in Italy|pandemic in Italy]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.football-italia.net/151183/bereszynski-i-have-coronavirus|title=Official: Bereszynski: 'I have Coronavirus'|publisher=Football Italia|date=14 March 2020|access-date=14 March 2020}}</ref> His father [[Przemysław Bereszyński]] was also a professional football player<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.90minut.pl/kariera.php?id=113|title = Przemysław Bereszyński profile |work= 90minut.pl}}</ref> and currently a professional coach.<ref>{{cite web|url= https://www.przegladsportowy.pl/magazyn-przegladu-sportowego/patryk-wolski-od-nadziei-lecha-poznan-do-trenera-klasy-a/b8t1pzc | work = [[Przegląd Sportowy]] | title=BYŁ NADZIEJĄ LECHA POZNAŃ, ZOSTAŁ TRENEREM DRUŻYNY Z KLASY A. W WIEKU 22 LAT! |date= 16 February 2016 | author= Marcin Stus}}</ref>
