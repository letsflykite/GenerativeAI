'''César Arturo Ramos Palazuelos''' (born 5 December 1983) is a Mexican professional [[Association football|football]] [[Referee (association football)|referee]].<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://worldreferee.com/site/copy.php?linkType=referee&contextType=bio&linkID=8292 |title=Profile |access-date=27 March 2015 |archive-date=8 October 2017 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171008130248/http://worldreferee.com/site/copy.php?linkType=referee&contextType=bio&linkID=8292 |url-status=dead }}</ref> He has been a full international for [[FIFA]] since 2014.<ref>FIFA. [https://web.archive.org/web/20070624115119/http://www.fifa.com/associations/association=mex/footballofficials/referees/peoplekind=ref.html "Mexico: Referees"].</ref> He refereed matches in [[CONCACAF Champions League]], in the [[2018 FIFA World Cup]] in the [[2019 AFC Asian Cup]], and in the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].

==Refereeing career==
Ramos made his debut on 28 October 2006, in a [[Ascenso MX|Primera División A]] match between [[Zacatepec]] and [[Santos Laguna]] 1a. 'A'.<ref name="Liga MX"/> Ramos made his [[Liga MX|First Division]] debut as a fourth official on 15 January 2011, in a match between [[San Luis F.C.|San Luis]] and [[Puebla F.C.|Puebla]] in [[San Luis Potosí, San Luis Potosí|San Luis Potosí]].<ref>[http://www.ligamx.net/estadistica/tp/eyJ0aXR1bG9UYWJsYSI6IiBUUCAtIEPJU0FSIEFSVFVSTyBSQU1PUyBQQUxBWlVFTE9TICIsIiBwc1RpcG9Fc3RkIjoiQXJidCIsImlkVGVtcG9yYWRhIjoiNjEiLCJpZFRvcm5lbyI6IjIiLCJpZEFmbGREcnZkIjoiNTY4ODkiLCJpZERpdmlzaW9uIjoiOSIsImlkWm9uYUdycHMiOiIwIiwiRmFzZSI6IjEifQ== Liga MX]</ref> Later that year, Ramos made his First Division debut as a central referee in a match between [[C.F. Monterrey|Monterrey]] and [[Club Tijuana|Tijuana]] in [[Monterrey]].<ref>[http://www.ligamx.net/estadistica/central/eyJ0aXR1bG9UYWJsYSI6IiBDZW50cmFsIC0gQ8lTQVIgQVJUVVJPIFJBTU9TIFBBTEFaVUVMT1MgIiwiIHBzVGlwb0VzdGQiOiJBcmJ0IiwiaWRUZW1wb3JhZGEiOiI2MiIsImlkVG9ybmVvIjoiMSIsImlkQWZsZERydmQiOiI1Njg4OSIsImlkRGl2aXNpb24iOiI5IiwiaWRab25hR3JwcyI6IjAiLCJGYXNlIjoiMSJ9 Liga MX]</ref>

The first player Ramos booked in the First Division was [[Fernando Arce]] while the first player he sent off was [[Mariano Trujillo]] after he booked him twice in 15 minutes.<ref>[http://www.ligamx.net/cancha/informeArbitral/4695/eyJpZFRlbXBvcmFkYSI6IjYyIiwiaWRUb3JuZW8iOiIxIiwiaWREaXZpc2lvbiI6IjkiLCJpZENsdWJMb2NhbCI6IjE0IiwiaWRDbHViVmlzaXRhIjoiNSJ9 MTY-TIJ]</ref><ref>[http://www.ligamx.net/cancha/informeArbitral/19070/eyJpZFRlbXBvcmFkYSI6IjYzIiwiaWRUb3JuZW8iOiIxIiwiaWREaXZpc2lvbiI6IjEiLCJpZENsdWJMb2NhbCI6IjMiLCJpZENsdWJWaXNpdGEiOiI4In0= ATL-CHI]</ref>

Ramos refereed the [[2017 FIFA Club World Cup Final]] between [[Real Madrid]] and [[Gremio]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.futboltotal.com.mx/futbol-internacional/mundial-de-clubes/cesar-arturo-ramos-designado-fifa-mundial-ce-clubes/2017/11 |title=César Arturo Ramos, designado por FIFA para Mundial ce Clubes |website=www.futboltotal.com.mx |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171107235402/https://www.futboltotal.com.mx/futbol-internacional/mundial-de-clubes/cesar-arturo-ramos-designado-fifa-mundial-ce-clubes/2017/11 |archive-date=7 November 2017}} </ref>

Ramos was the only Mexican center referee assigned to referee at the [[2018 FIFA World Cup]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.record.com.mx/futbol-futbol-nacional-liga-mx/cesar-arturo-ramos-representara-mexico-en-rusia-2018|title=César Arturo Ramos representará a México en Rusia 2018|date=17 November 2017}}</ref>

In 2019, Ramos participated in a referee exchange program between the [[Asian Football Confederation|AFC]] and [[CONCACAF]], where Ramos would referee in the [[2019 AFC Asian Cup]] and [[Abdulrahman Al-Jassim]] of Qatar would referee at the [[2019 CONCACAF Gold Cup]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.the-afc.com/competitions/afc-asian-cup/latest/news/largest-ever-cast-of-match-officials-appointed-for-uae-2019|title=Largest-ever cast of match officials appointed for UAE 2019|website=www.the-afc.com|language=en-GB|access-date=4 July 2019}}</ref><ref>
{{Cite web|url=https://www.goldcup.org/en/article/match-officials-appointed-for-2019-concacaf-gold-cup|title=Match Officials Appointed for 2019 Concacaf Gold Cup|website=www.goldcup.org|language=en|access-date=4 July 2019|archive-date=17 May 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190517153005/https://www.goldcup.org/en/article/match-officials-appointed-for-2019-concacaf-gold-cup|url-status=dead}}</ref>

Ramos refereed the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]] semi-final match between [[2022_FIFA_World_Cup_knockout_stage#France_vs_Morocco|France and Morocco]] on 14 December.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.worldfootball.net/referee_summary/cesar-ramos_4/1/1/|title=César Ramos – Matches as referee|website=worldfootball.net}}</ref>
