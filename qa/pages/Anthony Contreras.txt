'''Anthony Daniel Contreras Enríquez''' (born 29 January 2000) is a Costa Rican [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Forward (association football)|forward]] for [[C.S. Herediano|Herediano]] and the [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rica national team]].

==Club career==
===Herediano===
Contreras joined [[C.S. Herediano]] in 2015, after he was spotted by a scout in a game for a club in [[Zapote]], at the age of 15.<ref>[http://municipalgrecia.com/noticias/220-anthony-contreras-se-convierte-en-uno-de-los-goleadores-de-las-panteras Anthony Contreras, se convierte en uno de los goleadores de las panteras], municipalgrecia.com, 5 June 2020</ref><ref>[https://www.nacion.com/puro-deporte/futbol-nacional/de-las-mejengas-de-pavas-surgio-el-artillero-a/ZWQ2VNFN6FBONMTM6674D2OB2E/story/ De las mejengas de Pavas surgió el artillero...], nacion.com, 21 July 2020</ref> He then went on a trial at the club and then joined the club permanently. Before that, Contreras had never played for a club.

23 months after joining Herediano, on 15 January 2017, he got his official debut for the club in the [[Liga FPD]] against [[Belén F.C.|Belén FC]] at the age of 16.<ref>[https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2017/01/15/costa-rica/primera-division/belen/cs-herediano/2384642/ BELÉN VS. HEREDIANO  1 - 1], soccerway.com, 15 January 2017</ref> Contreras started on the bench, coming on as a substitute for Heyreel Saravia in the 90th minute. This game was his only appearance in the 2017-18 season. In the following season, he only made one appearance, while he made zero in the 2018-19 season. To gain some experience, he was loaned out to [[La U Universitarios]] in the summer 2019 for the rest of the year, where he played 20 games as scored six goals.

On 13 December 2019 it was confirmed, that Contreras would be playing the rest of the 2019-20 season on loan for [[Municipal Grecia]], as well as six other players that Grecia had loaned from Herediano.<ref>[http://www.mariosegura.com/grecia-ratifica-refuerzos-rojiamarillos/ Grecia ratifica refuerzos rojiamarillos], mariosegura.com, 13 December 2019</ref><ref>[https://www.deportescr.net/2019/12/futbol/grecia-anuncio-7-prestamos-del-herediano/ Grecia anunció 7 préstamos del Herediano], deportescr.net, 13 December 2019</ref> Contreras played 15 games and scores six goals for the club.

Herediano announced in June 2020, that Contreras had returned to the club.<ref>[https://herediano.com/contreras-regresa-al-herediano/ Contreras regresa al Herediano], herediano.com, 23 June 2020</ref><ref>[https://yashinquesada.com/herediano-confirma-dos-llegadas-para-el-proximo-campeonato/ Herediano confirma dos llegadas para el próximo campeonato], yashinquesada.com, 23 June 2020</ref> Contreras participated in the teams pre-season and was also on the bench for a cup game in August 2020. However, on 15 September 2020, he was sent out on loan for the third time, this time to [[Guadalupe F.C.|Guadalupe FC]] for the rest of 2020.<ref>[https://sensaciondeportiva.com/herediano-cede-a-anthony-contreras-a-guadalupe-fc/ Herediano cede a Anthony Contreras a Guadalupe FC], sensaciondeportiva.com, 15 September 2020</ref>

Returning from loan in January 2021, Contreras became a regular player for Herediano and made 26 league appearances from January to June 2021. However, on 21 June 2021, Contreras was loaned out to AD Guanacasteca for the rest of 2021.<ref>[https://www.crhoy.com/deportes/otro-mas-herediano-envia-un-tercer-jugador-a-guanacasteca/ Otro más: Herediano envía un tercer jugador a Guanacasteca], crhoy.com, 21 June 2021</ref>

==International career==
In November 2019, Contreras was called up for the [[Costa Rica national football team|Costa Rican national team]]'s pre-squad.<ref>[http://greciadeportes.com/en/noticias/80-municipalgrecia/256-richard-steven-y-anthony-contreras-fueron-convocados-a-la-seleccion-nacional Richard Steven y Anthony Contreras fueron convocados a la selección nacional], greciadeportes.com, 1 November 2019</ref><ref>[https://laagendacr.com/jovenes-figuras-esperan-una-oportunidad-con-la-seleccion/ Jóvenes figuras esperan una oportunidad con la selección], laagendacr.com, 6 November 2019</ref>

Contreras was also a part of the [[Costa Rica national under-23 football team|Costa Rican U23 national team]] that was called up for the [[2020 CONCACAF Men's Olympic Qualifying Championship]] in March 2020.<ref>[https://www.columbia.co.cr/index.php/deportes/seleccion-nacional/14162-la-prelista-de-convocados-de-la-sub23-para-el-eliminatorio-olimpico La prelista de convocados de la Sub23 para el eliminatorio olímpico], columbia.co.cr, 3 March 2020</ref>

Contreras made his debut for the senior [[Costa Rica national football team]] on 12 November 2021 in a [[2022 FIFA World Cup qualification – CONCACAF third round|World Cup qualifier]] against [[Canada men's national soccer team|Canada]].<ref>{{cite web|publisher=[[FIFA]]|url=https://www.fifa.com/tournaments/mens/worldcup/qatar2022/qualifiers/concacaf/match-center/400180514|title=Canada v Costa Rica game report|date=12 November 2021|accessdate=18 November 2021}}</ref> Contreras scored his first goal for the national team on 27 March 2022 during a 2022 FIFA World Cup qualifier against [[El Salvador national football team|El Salvador]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://us.soccerway.com/matches/2022/03/27/nc-america/wc-qualifying-concacaf/el-salvador/costa-rica/3517535/|title=EL SALVADOR VS. COSTA RICA 1 - 2|website=Soccerway|access-date=28 March 2022}}</ref>
