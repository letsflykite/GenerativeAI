'''Zeno Koen Debast''' (born 24 October 2003) is a Belgian professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[centre-back]] for [[Belgian Pro League]] club [[R.S.C. Anderlecht|Anderlecht]] and the [[Belgium national football team|Belgium national team]].

==Club career==
Debast is a youth product of [[R.S.C. Anderlecht|Anderlecht]], signing his first contract in October 2019. He made his professional debut on 2 May 2021, in a 2–2 [[Belgian First Division A]] draw against [[Club Brugge]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://uk.soccerway.com/matches/2021/05/02/belgium/pro-league/club-brugge-kv/royal-sporting-club-anderlecht/3496939/|title=Club Brugge vs. Anderlecht - 24 October 2021 - Soccerway|website=soccerway.com}}</ref>

==International career==
In September 2022 Debast received his first call-up to the senior [[Belgium national football team|Belgian squad]] for the [[UEFA Nations League]] matches against [[Wales national football team|Wales]] and [[Netherlands national football team|Netherlands]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.sportstar.thehindu.com/football/belgium-squad-nations-league-defender-zeno-debast-gets-maiden-call-up/article65899273.ece/|title=Belgium calls up teenage defender Zeno Debast for Nations League|website=sportstar.com}}</ref>
