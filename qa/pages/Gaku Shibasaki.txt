{{nihongo|'''Gaku Shibasaki'''|柴崎 岳|Shibasaki Gaku|born 28 May 1992}} is a Japanese professional [[association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Midfielder (association football)#Central midfielder|midfielder]] for the [[Japan national football team|Japanese national team]].<ref>[http://www.japantimes.co.jp/sports/2017/01/31/soccer/shibasaki-wont-land-deal-las-palmas-poised-join-spanish-second-division-team/#.WJESPhsrKUk Shibasaki won't land deal with Las Palmas, poised to join Spanish second-division team] Japan Times.</ref>

==Club career==

===Early career===

==Personal life==
Shibasaki has two older brothers, whom one of them got him into football.<ref name="Shibasaki Interview 2"/><ref name="Shibasaki Interview 1"/> Growing up, Shibasaki said in an interview that his ambition is to become a footballer, having no interests in anything but football.<ref>{{cite news|title=見据える先は『100年後』。柴崎岳が語った原点と、日本が目指すべき未来:独占インタビュー|url=https://web.gekisaka.jp/news/detail/?277415-277415-fl|publisher=Web Gekisaka|language=ja|date=19 June 2019|access-date=18 April 2020}}</ref> He also idolised [[Alessandro Del Piero]], [[Francesco Totti]] and [[Hidetoshi Nakata]].<ref name="Shibasaki Interview 2">{{cite news|title=柴崎岳が明かす憧れの選手は?デポルティボ地元紙で語る「日本人で言うと...」|url=https://web.gekisaka.jp/news/detail/?286428-286428-fl|publisher=Web Gekisaka|language=ja|date=29 September 2019|access-date=18 April 2020}}<br>{{cite news|title=デポルティボ柴崎岳に現地紙インタビュー。「ア・コルーニャは青森に似ている」|url=https://www.footballchannel.jp/2019/09/28/post340277/|publisher=Football Channel|language=ja|date=28 September 2019|access-date=18 April 2020}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|title=Gaku: "El Leganés me ha dado la oportunidad de volver a empezar"|url=https://www.marca.com/futbol/leganes/2020/11/06/5fa433be46163fee128b460c.html|website=Marca|language=es|date=6 November 2020|access-date=10 August 2021}}</ref> When asked in an interview on how does his improve his concentration before the game, Shibasaki said: "First of all, I start by creating my own world.
