'''Christopher Maurice Wooh''' (born 18 September 2001) is a professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Defender (association football)|defender]] for [[Ligue 1]] club [[Stade Rennais F.C.|Rennes]]. Born in France, he plays for the [[Cameroon national football team|Cameroon national team]].

==Club career==

=== Nancy ===
Wooh is a youth product of Puiseux Louvres, [[US Chantilly|Chantilly]] and [[AS Nancy|Nancy]]. He made his professional debut with Nancy in a 1–0 [[Coupe de France]] loss to [[FC Sochaux-Montbéliard|Sochaux]] on 20 January 2021.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.estrepublicain.fr/sport/2021/01/01/as-nancy-lorraine-la-fiche-de-christopher-wooh|title=Football . AS Nancy Lorraine : Christopher Wooh|website=www.estrepublicain.fr}}</ref>

=== Lens ===
On 1 June 2021, Wooh agreed to a four-year contract with [[Ligue 1]] club [[RC Lens|Lens]], effective from 1 July.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Transferts : Lens trouve un accord avec Christopher Wooh (Nancy)|url=https://www.lequipe.fr/Football/Actualites/Transferts-lens-trouve-un-accord-avec-christopher-wooh-nancy/1257955|access-date=11 June 2021|website=L'Équipe|language=fr}}</ref>

=== Rennes ===
On 31 August 2022, Wooh signed to a four-year contract with [[Stade Rennais F.C.|Rennes]].

==International career==
Born in France, Wooh holds French and Cameroonese nationalities.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.unfp.org/joueur/christopher-wooh-22226/|title=Christopher Wooh |website=www.unfp.org}}</ref> In 2022, he was selected by [[Rigobert Song]] to represent the [[Cameroon national football team|Cameroon national team]] for the first time in his career, for the upcoming matchups of the Africa Cup 2023. He debuted with them in a 1–0 [[2023 Africa Cup of Nations qualification]] win over [[Burundi national football team|Burundi]] on 9 June 2022.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2022/06/08/africa/africa-cup-of-nations-qualification/burundi/cameroon/3782984/|title=Burundi vs. Cameroon - 9 June 2022|website=Soccerway}}</ref>
