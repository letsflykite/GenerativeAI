'''Ardon Jashari''' (born 30 July 2002) is a Swiss [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[midfielder]] for [[FC Luzern]] and the [[Switzerland national football team|Switzerland national team]].

==Club career==
Jashari made his professional debut with [[FC Luzern|Luzern]] in a 2–1 [[Swiss Super League]] win over [[FC Zürich]] on 31 July 2020.<ref name="Soccerway 2020">{{cite web | title=Luzern vs. Zürich – 31 July 2020 | website=Soccerway | date=31 July 2020 | url=https://ca.soccerway.com/matches/2020/07/31/switzerland/super-league/fc-luzern/fc-zurich/3037986/ | access-date=16 August 2020}}</ref>

==International career==
Jashari made his debut for the [[Switzerland national football team]] on 27 September 2022 in a [[2022–23 UEFA Nations League A|Nations League]] game against the [[Czech Republic national football team|Czech Republic]], substituting [[Remo Freuler]] in added time.<ref>{{cite web|publisher=UEFA|url=https://www.uefa.com/uefanationsleague/match/2034565/|title=Switzerland v Chechia game report|date=27 September 2022|access-date=6 October 2022}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Jashari was born in Switzerland, and is of [[ethnic Albanian]] descent from [[North Macedonia]].<ref name="Fakti Ditor 2018">{{cite web | title=Ardon Jashari-talenti nga Kumanova që po pushton futbollin zviceran | website=Fakti Ditor | date=31 May 2018 | url=http://www.faktiditor.ch/ardon-jashari-talenti-nga-kumanova-qe-po-pushton-futbollin-zviceran/ | language=sq | access-date=16 August 2020}}</ref>
