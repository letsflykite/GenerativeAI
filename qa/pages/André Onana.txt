'''André Onana Onana''' (born 2 April 1996) is a Cameroonian professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Goalkeeper (association football)|goalkeeper]] for {{Italian football updater|Internazionale}} club [[Inter Milan]].

Onana joined [[FC Barcelona|Barcelona]]'s youth system in 2010. He signed for [[AFC Ajax|Ajax]] in 2015, where he made 214 appearances and won three [[Eredivisie]] titles. Onana joined Inter Milan on a free transfer in July 2022.

At international level, Onana made 34 appearances for the [[Cameroon national football team|Cameroon national team]] from 2016 to 2022.

==Club career==
===Youth career===
Born in Nkol Ngok,<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.theguardian.com/football/2021/dec/30/andre-onana-on-the-error-that-led-to-a-nine-month-ban-40mg-can-destroy-a-career |title=André Onana on the error that led to a nine-month ban: '40mg can destroy a career' |website=theguardian.com |date=30 December 2021 |access-date=29 June 2023}}</ref> Onana joined [[FC Barcelona|Barcelona]] from the [[Samuel Eto'o]] Academy at the age of 14 in 2010.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.sport.es/en/news/mercado-fichajes/barcelona-line-up-possible-move-for-ajax-goalkeeper-onana-7920970 |title=Barcelona line up possible move for Ajax goalkeeper Onana |website=sport.es |date=8 April 2020 |access-date=29 June 2023}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=https://theathletic.com/1751222/2020/04/21/andre-onana-ajax-barcelona-spurs-chelsea-psg/ |title=The story of Andre Onana — one of Europe’s most wanted goalkeepers |website=theathletic.com |date=21 April 2020 |access-date=29 June 2023}}</ref> Because he was under 18 and a non-EU citizen, Onana had to go on loan in the 2012–13 season to the youth team of [[UE Cornellà|Cornellà]], before being loaned again the next season to the first-team of Vista Alegre.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.mundodeportivo.com/20130701/mundo-barsa/onana-cedido-vista-alegre_54377112273.html|title=Onana, cedido al Vista Alegre|date=1 July 2013|access-date=30 November 2022|newspaper=[[Mundo Deportivo]]|language=spanish|archive-date=30 November 2022|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20221130072519/https://www.mundodeportivo.com/20130701/mundo-barsa/onana-cedido-vista-alegre_54377112273.html|url-status=live}}</ref>

Onana said he took his wife's medicine by mistake and Ajax joined him in appealing against the decision.<ref>{{cite news |title=Ajax goalkeeper Andre Onana given 12-month doping ban |url=https://theathletic.com/news/andre-onana-ajax-doping-drugs-ban/kLF4Juxgst7j |access-date=5 February 2021 |work=The Athletic |date=5 February 2021 |archive-date=5 February 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210205105005/https://theathletic.com/news/andre-onana-ajax-doping-drugs-ban/kLF4Juxgst7j |url-status=live }}</ref> The ban was reduced to nine months by the [[Court of Arbitration for Sport]] in June.<ref>{{cite news |title=Andre Onana: Cameroon and Ajax keeper has doping ban reduced |url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/africa/57427001 |access-date=10 June 2021 |work=BBC Sport |date=10 June 2021 |archive-date=10 June 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210610203224/https://www.bbc.com/sport/africa/57427001 |url-status=live }}</ref>

In January 2022, he was linked with a transfer to Italian club [[Inter Milan]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.eurosport.co.uk/football/transfer-window/2007/ajax-goalkeeper-andre-onana-undergoing-inter-milan-medical-ahead-of-summer-move-to-azzurri_sto8692230/story.shtml|title=Ajax goalkeeper Andre Onana undergoing Inter Milan medical ahead of summer move to Serie A - reports|date=4 January 2022|website=Eurosport.co.uk}}</ref> In May 2022, he announced that he was leaving Ajax.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://twitter.com/AndreyOnana/status/1529510935697702915/photo/2|title=Thanks for all @AFCAjax|website=Twitter.com|access-date=4 June 2022|archive-date=3 June 2022|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220603084846/https://twitter.com/andreyonana/status/1529510935697702915/photo/2|url-status=live}}</ref> Onana spent seven and a half years with Ajax, making 214 appearances in all competitions during his stint.<ref name=InterBBC />

===Inter Milan===
On 1 July 2022, [[Inter Milan]] officially confirmed Onana's signing on a free transfer on a five-year contract.<ref name=InterBBC>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/africa/62011991|title=Inter Milan sign Cameroon keeper Onana from Ajax|via=www.bbc.co.uk|access-date=3 July 2022|archive-date=3 July 2022|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220703082113/https://www.bbc.com/sport/africa/62011991|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|publisher=Inter Milan|url=https://www.inter.it/it/news/2022/07/01/andre-onana-inter.html|title=ANDRÉ ONANA È UN NUOVO CALCIATORE DELL'INTER|date=1 July 2022|access-date=1 July 2022|language=it|archive-date=1 July 2022|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220701142037/https://www.inter.it/it/news/2022/07/01/andre-onana-inter.html|url-status=live}}</ref>

In July 2023, he entered into discussions with [[Manchester United F.C.|Manchester United]] regarding a transfer.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/66130771|title=Man Utd hope to conclude deal for Inter's Onana|via=www.bbc.co.uk}}</ref>

==Style of play==
Onana is a [[Goalkeeper (association football)#Sweeper-keeper|sweeper-keeper]] who has strong distribution skills.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://football-italia.net/inters-onana-bringing-back-sweeper-keeper-role-for-cameroon-at-world-cup/ |title=Inter's Onana bringing back sweeper keeper role for Cameroon at World Cup |website=football-italia.net |date=24 November 2022 |access-date=29 June 2023}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |url=https://inews.co.uk/sport/football/andre-onana-man-utd-summer-david-de-gea-jordan-pickford-2426940 |title=Why Man Utd should sign Andre Onana this summer – and forget David de Gea clone Jordan Pickford |website=inews.co.uk |date=22 June 2023 |access-date=29 June 2023}}</ref> He regularly comes out of his box to help build-up play and is known for his confidence on the ball and range of passing.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://theathletic.com/4613839/2023/06/19/andre-onana-manchester-united/ |title=Why Andre Onana would be a perfect transfer for Manchester United and Ten Hag |website=theathletic.com |date=19 June 2023 |access-date=29 June 2023}}</ref> In Cameroon's opening 2022 World Cup game against Switzerland, Onana had more touches of the ball outside of the box (26) than any goalkeeper in a World Cup match since records began in 1966.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.beinsports.com/en-au/articles/song-confirms-onana-dropped-for-discipline-re |title=Song confirms Onana dropped for discipline reasons |website=beinsports.com |date=28 November 2022 |access-date=29 June 2023}}</ref> He was described as resembling a "holding midfielder at times" in UEFA's Technical Observers' report on the 2022–23 Champions League.<ref>{{cite news |url=https://www.uefa.com/uefachampionsleague/news/0282-185d3205b744-6aab6b8b75c4-1000--uefa-champions-league-technical-talking-points/ |title=UEFA Champions League technical talking points |website=uefa.com |date=28 June 2023 |access-date=29 June 2023}}</ref>

==Personal life==
Onana's cousin, [[Fabrice Ondoa]], also plays as a [[Goalkeeper (association football)|goalkeeper]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.sport.es/es/noticias/barca/onana-ondoa-dulce-momento-dos-primos-que-formaron-barca-5768039|title=Onana-Ondoa: El dulce momento de dos primos que se formaron en el Barça|trans-title=Onana-Ondoa: The sweet moment of two cousins who formed in Barça|language=Spanish|date=26 January 2017|accessdate=26 January 2017|publisher=[[Sport (Spanish newspaper)|Sport]]|archive-date=30 January 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170130093759/http://www.sport.es/es/noticias/barca/onana-ondoa-dulce-momento-dos-primos-que-formaron-barca-5768039|url-status=live}}</ref>

In May 2019, Onana spoke out about being a black goalkeeper, saying they had to work harder than their white counterparts due to misconceptions about them making "mistakes".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/48147087|title=Cameroon's Andre Onana: 'Black keepers have to work harder'|date=6 May 2019|publisher=BBC Sport|access-date=7 May 2019|archive-date=7 May 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190507065021/https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/football/48147087|url-status=live}}</ref>
