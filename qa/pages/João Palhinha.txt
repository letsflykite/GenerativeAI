'''João Maria Lobo Alves Palhinha Gonçalves''' (born 9 July 1995) is a Portuguese professional [[Association football|footballer]] who plays for {{English football updater|Fulham}} club [[Fulham F.C.|Fulham]] and the [[Portugal national football team|Portugal national team]] as a [[Midfielder#Defensive midfielder|defensive midfielder]].

He came through at [[Sporting CP]], first appearing with the first team in 2017 and also having loans at [[Moreirense F.C.|Moreirense]], [[C.F. Os Belenenses|Belenenses]] and [[S.C. Braga|Braga]]. After returning he became a starter, notably winning the [[2020–21 Primeira Liga]]. In July 2022, he signed with Fulham for £20 million.

Palhinha made his full debut for [[Portugal national football team|Portugal]] in 2021. He was part of the squads at [[UEFA Euro 2020]] and the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].

==Club career==
===Sporting CP===
Born in [[Lisbon]], Palhinha joined local [[Sporting CP]]'s youth system in 2012, aged 17.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.record.pt/especial/detalhe/junior-palhinha-assina-contrato-com-o-sporting-790840|title=Júnior Palhinha assina contrato com o Sporting|trans-title=Junior Palhinha signs contract with Sporting|newspaper=[[Record (newspaper)|Record]]|language=pt|date=27 November 2012|access-date=4 July 2022}}</ref> He played his first game as a professional with [[Sporting CP B|the B team]], coming on as a late [[Substitute (association football)|substitute]] for fellow youth graduate [[Iuri Medeiros]] in a 3−2 home win against [[Portimonense S.C.|Portimonense]] in the [[Segunda Liga]], on 3 February 2014;<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.record.xl.pt/futebol/nacional/liga-honra/detalhe/sporting-b-portimonense-3-2-leoes-voltam-aos-triunfos-866263.html|title=Sporting B-Portimonense, 3−2: Leões voltam aos triunfos|trans-title=Sporting B-Portimonense, 3−2: Lions return to winning ways|newspaper=Record|language=pt|date=3 February 2014|access-date=3 August 2015|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150924152927/http://www.record.xl.pt/futebol/nacional/liga-honra/detalhe/sporting-b-portimonense-3-2-leoes-voltam-aos-triunfos-866263.html|archive-date=24 September 2015}}</ref> it was one of only two appearances during [[2013–14 Segunda Liga|the season]].

Still in the second division, Palhinha scored his first senior goal on 4 April 2015, his header contributing to a 3−1 home victory over [[Leixões S.C.|Leixões]].<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.record.xl.pt/futebol/nacional/liga-honra/detalhe/sporting-b-leixoes-3-1-quarto-triunfo-seguido-para-os-leoes-940703.html|title=Sporting B-Leixões, 3−1: Quarto triunfo seguido para os leões|trans-title=Sporting B-Leixões, 3−1: Fourth win in a row for the lions|newspaper=Record|language=pt|date=4 April 2015|access-date=3 August 2015|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150924152922/http://www.record.xl.pt/futebol/nacional/liga-honra/detalhe/sporting-b-leixoes-3-1-quarto-triunfo-seguido-para-os-leoes-940703.html|archive-date=24 September 2015}}</ref> From July 2015 to January 2017 he was loaned to [[Primeira Liga]] clubs [[Moreirense F.C.|Moreirense]]<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.ojogo.pt/futebol/1a-liga/sporting/noticias/interior/sporting-empresta-palhinha-ao-moreirense-4726756.html|title=Sporting empresta Palhinha ao Moreirense|trans-title=Sporting loan Palhinha to Moreirense|newspaper=[[O Jogo]]|language=pt|date=12 August 2015|access-date=28 August 2017}}</ref> and [[C.F. Os Belenenses|Belenenses]], respectively,<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.maisfutebol.iol.pt/geral/09-08-2016/sporting-joao-palhinha-emprestado-ao-belenenses|title=Sporting: João Palhinha emprestado ao Belenenses|trans-title=Sporting: João Palhinha loaned to Belenenses|publisher=Mais Futebol|first=Maria|last=Gomes de Andrade|language=pt|date=9 August 2016|access-date=28 August 2017}}</ref> making his debut in the competition whilst with the former in a 0−2 home loss to [[F.C. Arouca|Arouca]] where he played 22 minutes from the bench,<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.dn.pt/desporto/futebol-nacional/arouca-estreiase-na-i-liga-triunfo-em-casa-do-moreirense-4732599.html|title=Arouca estreia-se na I Liga triunfo em casa do Moreirense|trans-title=Arouca make I League debut with win at Moreirense|newspaper=[[Diário de Notícias]]|language=pt|date=16 August 2015|access-date=26 April 2022}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.record.pt/futebol/futebol-nacional/liga-bwin/moreirense/detalhe/joao-palhinha-direto-a-estreia|title=João Palhinha direto à estreia|trans-title=João Palhinha headed straight for debut|newspaper=Record|language=pt|date=17 August 2015|access-date=26 April 2022}}</ref> and netting for the first time while at the service of the latter against [[Vitória S.C.|Vitória de Guimarães]] (1−1 away draw, 90 minutes played).<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fpf.pt/pt/News/Todas-as-not%C3%ADcias/Not%C3%ADcia/news/8500|title=Vitória de Guimarães e Belenenses dividem pontos|trans-title=Vitória de Guimarães and Belenenses share points|publisher=[[Portuguese Football Federation]]|language=pt|date=17 September 2016|access-date=28 August 2017}}</ref>

Subsequently, returning to the [[Estádio José Alvalade]],<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.record.pt/futebol/futebol-nacional/liga-nos/sporting/detalhe/palhinha-regresso-agora-a-casa.html|title=Palhinha: "Regresso agora a casa"|trans-title=Palhinha: "I return home now"|newspaper=Record|first=Luís|last=Miroto Simões|language=pt|date=4 January 2017|access-date=28 August 2017}}</ref> Palhinha made his competitive debut with the first team on 21 January 2017, starting in a 2−2 draw at [[C.S. Marítimo|Marítimo]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://rr.sapo.pt/noticia/74010/maritimo_sporting|title=Leão agrava crise|trans-title=Lion worsens crisis|publisher=[[Rádio Renascença]]|first=Carlos|last=Calaveiras|language=pt|date=21 January 2017|access-date=28 August 2017}}</ref> He scored his first goals for them on 12 October of the same year, netting twice in a [[2017–18 Taça de Portugal|4–2 away win]] over [[A.R.C. Oleiros|Oleiros]] in the [[Taça de Portugal]].<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.ojogo.pt/futebol/1a-liga/sporting/noticias/interior/palhinha-defrontamos-o-oleiros-como-defrontamos-o-barcelona-ou-o-fc-porto-8839183.html|title=Palhinha: "Defrontámos o Oleiros tal como defrontámos o Barcelona ou o FC Porto"|trans-title=Palhinha: "We played Oleiros like we played Barcelona or FC Porto"|newspaper=O Jogo|language=pt|date=12 October 2017|access-date=13 October 2017}}</ref>

Palhinha spent the following two seasons on loan to [[S.C. Braga|Braga]].<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.dn.pt/desportos/interior/palhinha-emprestado-ao-sp-braga-ate-2020-9689092.html|title=Palhinha emprestado ao Sp. Braga until 2020|newspaper=Diário de Notícias|first=David|last=Pereira|language=pt|date=7 August 2018|access-date=31 August 2019}}</ref> He appeared in 76 competitive matches during his spell,<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.ojogo.pt/futebol/1a-liga/sporting/noticias/sporting-vai-resistindo-as-propostas-por-palhinha-confira-o-ponto-da-situacao-12528513.html|title=Sporting vai resistindo às propostas por Palhinha: confira o ponto da situação|trans-title=Sporting resist offers for Palhinha as it can: here's how it stands|newspaper=O Jogo|first1=Duarte|last1=Tornesi|first2=Rafael|last2=Toucedo|language=pt|date=17 August 2020|access-date=15 September 2020}}</ref> helping to a third-place finish in [[2019–20 Primeira Liga|2019–20]]<ref>{{cite web|url=https://maisfutebol.iol.pt/jogo/aominuto/braga/porto/13583396|title=Sp. Braga-FC Porto, 2–1 (crónica)|trans-title=Sp. Braga-FC Porto, 2–1 (match report)|publisher=Mais Futebol|first=Bruno José|last=Ferreira|language=pt|date=25 July 2020|access-date=15 September 2020}}</ref> and notably scoring the game's only goal against [[S.L. Benfica|Benfica]] for the club's first win at that opposition in 65 years.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.jn.pt/desporto/golo-de-joao-palhinha-quebra-jejum-de-65-anos-frente-ao-benfica--11826067.html|title=Golo de João Palhinha quebra jejum de 65 anos frente ao Benfica|trans-title=João Palhinha goal breaks 65-year fast against Benfica|newspaper=[[Jornal de Notícias]]|language=pt|date=15 February 2020|access-date=15 September 2020}}</ref>

Having started the [[2020–21 Primeira Liga|2020–21 campaign]] training on his own in the midst of negotiations with [[Al Nassr FC|Al Nassr]] and [[PFC CSKA Moscow|CSKA Moscow]], Palhinha was eventually reintegrated into Sporting's main squad<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.record.pt/futebol/futebol-nacional/liga-bwin/sporting/detalhe/joao-palhinha-reintegrado-no-plantel-do-sporting-conheca-os-motivos|title=João Palhinha reintegrado no plantel do Sporting: conheça os motivos|trans-title=João Palhinha reinstated in Sporting's squad: know the reasons|newspaper=Record|language=pt|date=2 September 2020|access-date=13 October 2021}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.record.pt/futebol/futebol-nacional/liga-bwin/sporting/detalhe/cska-moscovo-prepara-novo-ataque-a-palhinha|title=CSKA Moscovo prepara novo ataque a Palhinha|trans-title=CSKA Moscovo with new offensive for Palhinha|newspaper=Record|first1=João|last1=Soares Ribeiro|first2=Vítor|last2=Almeida Gonçalves|language=pt|date=24 September 2020|access-date=13 October 2021}}</ref> and ended up contributing 32 appearances for the champions,<ref name=PL>{{cite web|url=https://pt.uefa.com/uefachampionsleague/news/0269-1240dc179f19-ea808f855085-1000--sporting-e-campeao-portugues/|title=Sporting é campeão de Portugal ao fim de 19 anos|trans-title=Sporting are champions of Portugal after 19 years|publisher=UEFA|language=pt|date=11 May 2021|access-date=21 May 2021}}</ref><ref name=TSF>{{cite web|url=https://www.tsf.pt/desporto/joao-palhinha-do-sporting-no-melhor-onze-da-ultima-edicao-da-i-liga-13814732.html|title=João Palhinha, do Sporting, no melhor onze da última edição da I Liga|trans-title=João Palhinha, of Sporting, in the All-Star XI of I League's last edition|publisher=[[TSF (radio station)|TSF]]|language=pt|date=8 June 2021|access-date=19 October 2021}}</ref> with a goal in the 2–0 home defeat of [[F.C. Paços de Ferreira|Paços de Ferreira]];<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.dn.pt/desporto/ate-na-monotonia-este-sporting-brilha-13356223.html|title=Até na monotonia este Sporting brilha|trans-title=This Sporting can even shine in dullness|newspaper=Diário de Notícias|first=António Pedro|last=Pereira|language=pt|date=15 February 2021|access-date=19 October 2021}}</ref> consequently, he was named in the [[2020–21 Primeira Liga#Annual awards|Primeira Liga Team of the Year]] alongside five of his teammates.<ref name=TSF/>

In [[2021–22 Primeira Liga|2021–22]], still under manager [[Rúben Amorim]], Palhinha began facing stiff competition from newly-signed [[Manuel Ugarte (footballer)|Manuel Ugarte]].<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.jn.pt/desporto/ruben-amorim-palhinha-estava-confortavel-e-ja-nao-esta-14420187.html|title=Ruben Amorim: "Palhinha estava confortável e já não está"|trans-title=Ruben Amorim: "Palhinha was comfortable but not anymore"|newspaper=Jornal de Notícias|first=Bernardo|last=Monteiro|language=pt|date=17 December 2021|access-date=4 July 2022}}</ref><ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.record.pt/futebol/futebol-nacional/liga-bwin/sporting/detalhe/ugarte-imprescindivel-no-sporting-amorim-agarra-uruguaio|title=Ugarte imprescindível no Sporting: Amorim 'agarra' uruguaio|trans-title=Sporting cannot do without Ugarte: Amorim 'gets hold' of Uruguayan|newspaper=Record|first=Filipe|last=Balreira|language=pt|date=3 June 2022|access-date=4 July 2022}}</ref> He played 95 official games during his spell, scoring seven times.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://desporto.sapo.pt/futebol/primeira-liga/artigos/palhinha-confirma-adeus-ao-sporting-vou-para-a-melhor-liga-do-mundo|title=Palhinha confirma adeus ao Sporting: "Vou para a melhor Liga do mundo"|trans-title=Palhinha confirms Sporting goodbye: "I'm going to the best League in the world"|publisher=SAPO|language=pt|date=2 July 2022|access-date=4 July 2022}}</ref>

===Fulham===
On 4 July 2022, Palhinha signed a five-year contract with [[Fulham F.C.|Fulham]] for £20 million.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fulhamfc.com/news/2022/july/04/fulham-sign-palhinha/|title=Fulham sign Palhinha|publisher=Fulham F.C.|first=Geoff|last=Pruce|date=4 July 2022|access-date=4 July 2022}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.skysports.com/football/news/11681/12645493/joao-palhinha-fulham-confirm-20m-signing-of-midfielder-from-sporting-lisbon-on-five-year-contract|title=Joao Palhinha: Fulham confirm £20m signing of midfielder from Sporting Lisbon on five-year contract|publisher=[[Sky Sports]]|date=4 July 2022|access-date=4 July 2022}}</ref> He made his [[Premier League]] debut on 6 August, playing the entire 2–2 home draw against [[Liverpool F.C.|Liverpool]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/62358041|title=Fulham 2–2 Liverpool|publisher=[[BBC Sport]]|first=Neil|last=Johnston|date=6 August 2022|access-date=6 August 2022}}</ref> He scored his first goal 14 days later, helping the hosts to beat [[Brentford F.C.|Brentford]] 3–2;<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/football/62529373|title=Fulham 3–2 Brentford|publisher=BBC Sport|first=Mantej|last=Mann|date=20 August 2022|access-date=20 August 2022}}</ref> he was his team's Player of the Season with 68% of the votes, to runner-up [[Bernd Leno]]'s 17%.<ref name=POTS>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.com/sport/articles/cq5qrj9e18ro|title=Your Fulham player of the season is...|publisher=BBC Sport|date=1 June 2023|access-date=26 June 2023}}</ref>
