'''Michael Steveen Estrada Martínez''' (born 7 April 1996) is an Ecuadorian [[Association football|footballer]] who plays as a [[Forward (association football)|forward]] for [[Bulgaria|Bulgarian]] club [[PFC CSKA Sofia|CSKA Sofia]], on loan from [[Deportivo Toluca F.C.|Toluca]], and the [[Ecuador national football team|Ecuador national team]].

==Career==
===Club===
Estrada started his career with [[C.S.D. Macará|Macará]], making his senior debut in the [[Ecuadorian Serie A]] on 10 March 2013 in a 1–0 loss against [[Barcelona S.C.|Barcelona]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Barcelona 1–0 Macará|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2013/03/10/ecuador/primera-a/barcelona-sporting-club-guayaquil/club-social-y-deportivo-macara/1438442/|website=Soccerway|date=10 March 2013|accessdate=24 December 2016}}</ref>
After three seasons with Macara's first team, Estrada joined [[C.D. El Nacional|El Nacional]]. He made his debut on 7 February 2016, scoring in a 2–1 win over Barcelona.<ref>{{cite web|title=El Nacional 2–1 Barcelona|url=https://int.soccerway.com/matches/2016/02/07/ecuador/primera-a/cd-el-nacional/barcelona-sporting-club-guayaquil/2191521/|website=Int.soccerway.com|date=7 February 2016|access-date=24 December 2016}}</ref>

In December 2019, Estrada joined [[Liga MX]] side [[Deportivo Toluca F.C.|Toluca]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Hay tercer refuerzo|url=https://www.tolucafc.com/noticia/2019-12-17/hay-tercer-refuerzoo|publisher=Toluca FC|date=12 December 2019|access-date=14 December 2019|language=es}}</ref>

On 8 February 2022, Estrada moved to [[Major League Soccer]] side [[D.C. United]] on a season-long loan.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.dcunited.com/news/d-c-united-sign-ecuadorian-international-forward|title=D.C. United Sign Ecuador Forward Michael Estrada on a Season-long Loan |website=Dcunited.com|access-date=28 February 2022}}</ref> Estrada debuted for United's 2022 season opener against [[Charlotte FC]] on 26 February, scoring 2 goals in the 3–0 win.<ref>{{Cite web |last=Quillen |first=Ian Nicholas |date=26 February 2022 |title=Recap: DC United 3, Charlotte FC 0 |url=https://www.mlssoccer.com/news/recap-dc-united-3-charlotte-fc-0 |access-date=27 February 2022 |website=MLS}}</ref> In August, Estrada and the club mutually terminated the loan, and he returned to Toluca after scoring four goals in 16 league appearances.<ref>{{Cite web|date=16 August 2022|title=Michael Estrada exits DC United, returns to Toluca|url=https://www.mlssoccer.com/news/michael-estrada-exits-dc-united-returns-to-toluca|access-date=20 November 2022|website=MLS}}</ref>

Shortly thereafter, Estrada joined [[Cruz Azul]] on a year-long loan with an option to buy.<ref>{{cite web |url=https://espndeportes.espn.com/futbol/mexico/nota/_/id/10754073/cruz-azul-michael-estrada-sera-nuevo-refuerzo-de-la-maquina|title=Cruz Azul: Michael Estrada será nuevo refuerzo de la Máquina|trans-title=Cruz Azul: Michael Estrada will be a new reinforcement of the Machine|work=[[ESPN Deportes]]|date=10 August 2022|access-date=20 November 2022|language=es}}</ref> He made his debut on 17 August in a 2–1 defeat to [[Club Tijuana|Tijuana]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.benditofutbol.com/legionarios/michael-estrada-debuto-cruz-azul.html|title=Michael Estrada debutó con el Cruz Azul|trans-title=Michael Estrada debuted with Cruz Azul|first=Alexis|last=Sinchire|work=Bendito Futbol|date=17 August 2022|access-date=20 November 2022|language=es}}</ref>

===International===
On 14 November, Estrada was named in Ecuador's 26-man squad for the [[2022 FIFA World Cup]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://espndeportes.espn.com/futbol/mundial/nota/_/id/11228629/ecuador-lista-convocados-qatar-2022-mundial-copa-mundo |title=Ecuador, el último en entregar la lista de los 26 jugadores convocados para la Copa del Mundo de Qatar 2022 |trans-title=Ecuador, the last to deliver the list of the 26 players summoned for the Qatar World Cup 2022 |work=[[ESPN Deportes]] |date=14 November 2022 |access-date=15 November 2022 |language=es}}</ref> He would start in the opening match of the group stage, a 2–0 win over [[Qatar national football team|Qatar]].
